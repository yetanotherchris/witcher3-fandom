Beard and Hairstyle Set

**Beard and Hairstyle Set** is one of the 16 [free DLCs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It was released with [Alternate look for Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alternative_look_for_Yennefer "Alternative look for Yennefer") on May 20, 2015. There are a number of [barbers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barber "Barber") available in various locations. 

## Official Statement

    _Customize[Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to your liking with this amazing set of beards and hairstyles._

## Notes

  * The April 2014 joke was later added in with the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion as the Queen's Pageboy. It can be obtained from [Jean-Louis Ludovic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jean-Louis_Ludovic "Jean-Louis Ludovic") after [Of Sheers and a Witcher I Sing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Sheers_and_a_Witcher_I_Sing "Of Sheers and a Witcher I Sing").
  * If you select either "clean shaven" or "full beard, trimmed short," Geralt's facial hair will eventually grow into a full, thick beard. However, if you choose one of the other facial hair styles, his facial hair will not grow and the chosen facial hair style will remain indefinitely.
