**New Finisher Animations** is one of the 16 [free DLCs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ released on 22 July 2015. 

## Official Statement

_Slay your foes in new brutal and spectacular ways with this visceral and adrenaline-pumping DLC!_
