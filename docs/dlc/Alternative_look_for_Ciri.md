# Alternative look for Ciri

[![Ciri wearing alternative outfit](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d9/Alternateciri.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20150720121127)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d9/Alternateciri.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150720121127) [![Ciri wearing alternative outfit](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d9/Alternateciri.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20150720121127)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d9/Alternateciri.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150720121127) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Alternateciri.jpg)

Ciri wearing alternative outfit

**Alternate look for Ciri** is a new outfit for [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") inspired by the latest fashion in [Zerrikania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikania "Zerrikania").[1] It is one of the 16 [free DLCs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ released on July 19, 2015. The outfit can be disabled or enabled in the Downloadable Content tab of the main menu. 

## Official statement

    _Mix things up with this new and finely crafted outfit for Ciri._
