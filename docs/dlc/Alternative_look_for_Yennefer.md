# Alternative look for Yennefer

[![Yennefer in alternative outfit](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/1/16/Yennefer_alternate_outfit.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20170601223358)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/1/16/Yennefer_alternate_outfit.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20170601223358) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Yennefer_alternate_outfit.jpg)

Yennefer in alternative outfit

**Alternative look for Yennefer** adds a new outfit for [Yennefer of Vengerberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"). It is one of the 16 [free DLCs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , released on May 29, 2015, along with [Contract: Missing Miners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Missing_Miners "Contract: Missing Miners"). 

The outfit can be disabled in the Downloadable Content tab of the main menu. 

## Official Statement

    _Check out this entirely new look for the mighty[sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorceress "Sorceress") Yennefer of Vengerberg!_
