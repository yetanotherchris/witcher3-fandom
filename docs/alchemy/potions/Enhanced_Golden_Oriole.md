## Item ID

Golden Oriole 2

**Enhanced Golden Oriole** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being an upgraded version of [Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golden_Oriole "Golden Oriole"). Four of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. 

It grants the exact same poison immunity effect as the basic potion, but for twice as long.
