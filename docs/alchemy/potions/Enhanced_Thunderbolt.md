**Enhanced Thunderbolt** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being an upgraded version of [Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thunderbolt "Thunderbolt"). Four of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot.
