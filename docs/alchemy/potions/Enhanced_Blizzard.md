### Effect(s)

Whenever you slay an enemy, time slows for a short period. Extended duration.  
30% Slowdown (since [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"))  
55% Slowdown (before [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"))

**Enhanced Blizzard** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being an upgraded version of [Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blizzard "Blizzard"). Two of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot.
