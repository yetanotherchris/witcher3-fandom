_"Throughout his life, the[mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") [Petri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Petri "Petri") sought a way to strengthen his [magical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") powers. He failed in this endeavor, but several interesting potions came about through his attempts. One of these, known as Petri's Philter, is useful to [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") with magical skills. Witchers who prefer hand-to-hand combat rarely use Petri's Philter, however, especially since it is highly [toxic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_character_statistics#Toxicity "The Witcher character statistics") and thus renders the use of any other potions impossible."_

**Petri's Philter** increases the intensity of all the [witcher Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign").

This potion can be crafted if Geralt possesses the [alchemical formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formula:_Petri%27s_philtre "Formula: Petri's philtre"). 

### Journal entry

    _This potion, created by a student of a famous mage of the first[Conclave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brotherhood_of_Sorcerers "Brotherhood of Sorcerers"), is weaker than [Stammelford's Philtre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stammelford%27s_Philtre "Stammelford's Philtre"), but has no side effects. Petri's Philter increases the intensity of witcher Signs – the impact of the [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard"), the defenses of the [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen"), the effectiveness of the [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden"), and so on._

    _The potion increases Toxicity very markedly when imbibed._
    _It is recommended for witchers who rely more heavily on magic than on their[swords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), and before fights against [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_monsters "The Witcher 2 monsters") vulnerable to Signs._
