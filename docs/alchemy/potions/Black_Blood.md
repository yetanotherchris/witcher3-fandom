    _"In the distant past,[mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") working with [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") developed this potion specifically for use in fighting cemetery and crypt dwellers that drink the blood or eat the innards of their still living victims. Witchers use Black Blood unwillingly because the potion only works when a [monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") begins to feast on their body. Their transmuted blood proves to be a deadly drink."_

**Black Blood** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_potions "The Witcher potions") in _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ that turns the imbiber's blood into poison; blood becomes lethal to monsters which drink it. 

Its [journey entry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_journal "The Witcher journal") can be obtained by reading [The Book of the Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Book_of_the_Golden_Oriole "The Book of the Golden Oriole").

## Item ID

Black Blood 1

**Black Blood** returns as a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in the third game. Three of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. 

It returns 15% of the damage dealt by [vampires](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire") and [necrophages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage "Necrophage") back onto the [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster").
