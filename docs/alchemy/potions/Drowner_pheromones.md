## Tips

  * The pheromones will turn all [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") benign, as in they won't even be marked as enemies.
  * Only works on regular drowners, and not their [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowned_dead "Drowned dead") and [mucknixer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mucknixer "Mucknixer") variations.
  * The effect will be removed if any attempt to attack drowners is made.
  * The effect will also be removed if Geralt enters a body of water, which apparently will "wash off" the pheromones. 
    * This appears to happen even if the water is shallow like a puddle.
  * It is possible to blow up a drowner nest without the need to fight the drowners, while under the effect.
