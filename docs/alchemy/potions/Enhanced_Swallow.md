## Tips

  * During [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen") council meeting, if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") chooses to use the laboratory to make potions (also the more profitable choice for rewards) instead of traps, he will receive ready-made [Superior Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Swallow "Superior Swallow"). This not only saves on [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"), but also keeps the non-superior, allowing to have multiple Swallow potions on hand. Thus, it may be preferable to not upgrade the enhanced version.
