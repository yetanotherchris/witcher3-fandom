## Item ID

White Raffards Decoction 2

**Enhanced White Raffard's Decotion** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being an upgraded version of [White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "White Raffard's Decoction"). Two these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot.
