_"There is no bird more beautiful than the swallow, the harbinger of spring. Even the dark[mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") who developed the formula for [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")' potions appreciated the charm of this bird, lending its name to the potion that accelerates regeneration of a mutated organism."_

**Swallow** accelerates the regeneration of [Vitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_character_statistics "The Witcher character statistics"). 

### Source

  * [The Book of the Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Book_of_the_Swallow "The Book of the Swallow")
  * [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") receives this potion from [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") in the Prologue during the [Defending Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Defending_Kaer_Morhen "Defending Kaer Morhen") quest.
  * There are three swallow potions in the wardrobe in the [witchers' laboratory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witchers%27_laboratory "Witchers' laboratory") in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") in the Prologue.
  * Two more in a crate in the [Evening hall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Evening_hall "Evening hall") on the second floor of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen").

Geralt begins the game with four Swallow potions as well as the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formula:_Swallow "Formula: Swallow"). 

### Journal entry

    _Symbolizing spring and rejuvenation, the swallow lent its name to this potion that accelerates the rate at which wounds scab over and heal. As a universal brew, it is good on many occasions and additionally produces no side effects._
    _The witcher can use the Swallow potion to balance out some of the undesirable effects of potions like[Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thunderbolt "Thunderbolt"), [Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maribor_Forest_\(potion\) "Maribor Forest \(potion\)"), [Tiara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tiara "Tiara") and [Stammelford's Philtre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stammelford%27s_Philtre "Stammelford's Philtre")._
    _The potion increase Toxicity slightly when imbibed._
    _It is recommended before long fights. Slow but consistent[Vitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitality "Vitality") regeneration will provide a significant advantage over foes. Witchers unsure of the dangers they might face in a given battle should take Swallow._
