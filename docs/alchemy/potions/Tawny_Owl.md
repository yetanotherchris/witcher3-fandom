_"In preparation for an all-night vigil at the side of a cursed man, or before a battle which is certain to be prolonged, a witcher mixes a dose of Tawny Owl potion to boost his endurance."_

**Tawny Owl** significantly increases [Endurance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endurance "Endurance") regeneration; it is particularly valued by witchers who often use [Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") in combat.

### Journal entry

    _Tawny Owl speeds up the regeneration of[Vigor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vigor "Vigor"). After consuming it, the witcher can parry monsters' blows and cast [Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signs "Signs") more often. The potion is weaker than [Lapwing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lapwing "Lapwing"), but generates no side effects. It can also be used to alleviate the side effects of [Gadwall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gadwall "Gadwall")._
    _Potion moderately increases Toxicity when imbibed._
    _The potion is recommended for witchers who specialize in casting[Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signs "Signs") and those who fight monsters that employ strong attacks which must be parried. Its low toxicity and virtual lack of side effects make it ideal for combining with other potions._

## Notes

  * While this potion is part of the starting equipment, the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manuscript_page:_Tawny_Owl "Manuscript page: Tawny Owl") for it is not. It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased, but there will be no use for it unless the potion is discarded.
  * In a [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") Geralt will get another copy of this potion upon arriving in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), if he has lost or upgraded the basic one. This effectively allows have two version, but it's not possible to upgrade the new basic copy unless the previously upgraded version is discarded first, as the alchemy crafting screen simply doesn't show lower-tier versions of already obtained items.
