## Item ID

Full Moon 2

**Enhanced Full Moon** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being an upgraded version of [Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Full_Moon "Full Moon"). Four of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. 

It adds 650 (1100 in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +")) to maximum [Vitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitality "Vitality") for 90 seconds.
