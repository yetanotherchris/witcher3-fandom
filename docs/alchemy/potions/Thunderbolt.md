# Thunderbolt

**Thunderbolt** is a potion in _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ , _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ , and _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_.

### Base

[top quality base](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Potion_bases "Potion bases")

### Effect(s)

Damage +100%  
Parry -50%  
Dodge -50%

### Toxicity

High

### Duration

8 hours

_"Witchers take this potion before fighting strong, heavily armored opponents. Imbibing Thunderbolt causes witchers to enter into a battle trance. While in this state, witchers attack more efficiently and cause greater damage, while at the same time neglecting their own defense and becoming an easier target."_

**Thunderbolt** potion increases damage inflicted on opponents by 100%, but reduces dodging and parrying efficiency by 50% each. 

### Source

### Journal entry

    _The Thunderbolt potion augments muscle power and thus damage dealt using a sword. As simple as it is effective, the brew is a more powerful version of the[Rook](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rook "Rook") potion._
    _Unfortunately, Thunderbolt may cause weaker muscle fibers to rupture, decreasing both Vitality and its regeneration. These side effects can be alleviated by taking the[Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swallow "Swallow") potion._
    _Potion significantly increases Toxicity when imbibed._
    _This potion is recommended before fighting particularly resilient or heavily armored monsters._

## Item ID

Thunderbolt 1

**Thunderbolt** returns as a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in the third game. Three of these potions can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. 

The recipe for Thunderbolt is received from [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir"), along with all necessary ingredients to make it, right before fighting the [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests"), [The Beast of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beast_of_White_Orchard "The Beast of White Orchard"). The potion can also be received ready-made after [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen") council meeting, if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") chooses to use the laboratory for potions.
