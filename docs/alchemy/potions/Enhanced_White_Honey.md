**Enhanced White Honey** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being an upgraded version of [White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Honey "White Honey"). Two of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot.

## Notes

  * Enhanced White Honey was originally meant to "reduce all active potion effects' duration by 50%" instead of canceling, which would've been way too easy to abuse (and it's not even the superior version). This is the likely reason why all three versions of the potion only differ in the count of "charges" they have.
