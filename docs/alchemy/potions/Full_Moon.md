_"Full Moon is an all-purpose potion. Witchers imbibe it before combat when they are not certain what kind of opponent awaits them. The downside to Full Moon is its high toxicity."_

**Full Moon** potion significantly increases maximum [Vitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_character_statistics "The Witcher character statistics"). Specifically, it doubles the maximum total vitality. 

### Source

  * [The Book of the Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Book_of_the_Full_Moon "The Book of the Full Moon")
  * Receiving this potion from the druids in Chapter V, during "[The Green March](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Green_March "The Green March")" will unlock the formula if you did not have it already.

### Notes

  * Due to its long duration, one can take this potion and then meditate for 1 hour to remove all toxicity.

**Full Moon** returns as a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in the third game. Three of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. 

It adds 300 (600 in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +")) to maximum [Vitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitality "Vitality") for 60 seconds.
