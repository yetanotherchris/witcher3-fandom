### Toxicity

### Duration

_"According to an old tale, this potion was first made by[dryads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dryad "Dryad") from the forest of [Brokilon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brokilon "Brokilon"). The formula was obtained by the [druids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") of [Maribor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maribor_\(city\) "Maribor \(city\)") Forest, who then passed it on to their brethren in other corners of the world. The formula also reached [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), where the practical-minded witchers began producing the potion using ingredients obtained from monster carcasses."_

**Maribor Forest** significantly increases maximum [Endurance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_character_statistics "The Witcher character statistics"); it is predominantly used by [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") trained in the use of [Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign"). It used to double maximum endurance in the original game (100%), however as of the Enhanced Edition it only increases maximum endurance by 50%. 

### Source

  * [Formula for Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formula_for_Maribor_Forest "Formula for Maribor Forest")
  * [Gramps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gramps "Gramps"), if Geralt passes through the Three Islands region (northwest of the [Clay Pits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clay_Pits "Clay Pits")) with him before bringing him to the Statue of Melitele.
  * [Elder druid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_druid "Elder druid") in the [Druids' grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_grove "Druids' grove").

### Notes

  * Drinking this potion during combat gives very little benefit as it raises your maximum [Endurance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endurance "Endurance") limit but does not actually increase the current amount of [Endurance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endurance "Endurance") you have. It is best used before combat so your [Endurance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endurance "Endurance") has time to fully recover to the new maximum limit.
  * This is spelled "Marribor Forest" in the Formula list while resting in-game.

### Journal entry

    _Maribor Forest considerably boosts[Vigor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vigor "Vigor"). After consuming it, the witcher tirelessly parries blows and can cast many [Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signs "Signs") in a row._
    _It is, however, a devastating brew that causes minor twitching. It reduces[Vitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitality "Vitality") and the accuracy of special attacks, such as those causing [Poisoning](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Poison "Poison"), [Incineration](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Incineration "Incineration"), or [Bleeding](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bleeding "Bleeding"). Its side effects can be alleviated by consuming the [Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(potion\) "Wolf \(potion\)") and [Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swallow "Swallow") potions._
    _Potion causes serious intoxication when imbibed._
    _This potion is used by witchers who specialize in casting[Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signs "Signs") and by those preparing to fight monsters with strong attacks that must be parried._
