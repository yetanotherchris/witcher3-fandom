**Reinald's Philter** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

The formula can be learned during [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow") if one finds and reads [Reinald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald "Reinald")'s [second letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Second_letter_from_Reinald "Second letter from Reinald").

There are actually two versions of this item, with the quest item one (ID: "Signs Enhancement") serving as a placeholder preventing the player from consuming the real one (ID: ReinaldsPhiltre, having 0 ![Tw3 weight brown](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/98/Tw3_weight_brown.svg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/13?cb=20180420180200) like a regular potion) before the scripted decision point in the quest. The actual effects, +15% [Sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") intensity and +10% stamina regeneration in combat, are only listed on the real potion, which is added and consumed during the cutscenes, and thus never visible to the player.
