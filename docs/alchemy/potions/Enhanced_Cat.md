## Item ID

Cat 2

**Enhanced Cat** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being an upgraded version of [Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(potion\) "Cat \(potion\)"). Four of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. 

This potion doubles the duration of the basic one from 60 seconds to 120 seconds. It also provides an immunity to hypnosis in addition to the standard effect of night-vision. The "next-gen" [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)") version also adds 2% higher critical hit chance than the lower-tier potion.
