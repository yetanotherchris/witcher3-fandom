_"White Honey strongly stimulates the production of purifying enzymes in witchers' mutated bodies. Thus, it frees their bodies of the effects of toxicity, though it simultaneously nullifies the beneficial effects of any potions previously consumed. White Honey does not alleviate the effects of common venoms and poisons."_

**White Honey** reduces [Toxicity](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toxicity "Toxicity") to zero and cancels the effects of other potions. 

### Source

  * [The Book of the Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Book_of_the_Tawny_Owl "The Book of the Tawny Owl")
  * Receiving this potion from the druids in Chapter V, during "[The Green March](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Green_March "The Green March")" will unlock the formula if you did not have it already.
  * A crate in the [West hall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/West_hall "West hall"), western most room on the second floor of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen").

### Notes

  * White honey is useful in situations where you don't have readily access to a fire, but urgently need to drink more potions - typically in-between intense combat. However, since it also removes existing potions effects, it is best used together with short-duration or instant effect potions, such as [Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blizzard "Blizzard") or [White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "White Raffard's Decoction").
  * Giving White honey to the [baker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baker "Baker") in [Murky Waters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Murky_Waters "Murky Waters") will earn you a [sugardoll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_food_and_drink "The Witcher food and drink").
  * If White Honey contains a [Secondary Substance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Additional_substances "Additional substances") the effect WILL be applied and not be removed unless you drink another White Honey after. So for example a [Rubedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rubedo "Rubedo") dominant White Honey can be used as a weak albeit completely non-toxic healing potion. Also since White Honey is often used when intending to drink other [potions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_potions "The Witcher potions") it is a good idea to create [Albedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albedo "Albedo") dominant White Honey, as while being non-toxic it will not benefit from the effect itself it will however lower the toxicity of the potions you intend to drink right after.

### Notes

  * Unlike other potions, this potion starts with a single charge at the basic version, increases to two charges at the enhanced version, and then goes up to five charges at the superior version, without any special effects.
  * Due to the minimal required ingredients, this is usually one of the first potions that can be made.
