## Item ID

Trial Potion Kit

**The Decoctions of the Grasses** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used on [Uma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Uma "Uma") to break the curse that binds him. [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") provides the formulae for it so [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can brew them for her. The three decoctions are called Mother's Tears, Wildrye Juice, and Speargrass Sap.

## Notes

  * At some point during the game's development, this was not a single item but the three individual ones, still seen administered as such during the quest. Remnants of recipes for them, as well as for two more, _"Feather Grass"_ and _"Fescue Grass"_ , were still present in the initial release. 
    * Additionally, since the forktail spinal fluid is obtained by slaying a [forktail](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail "Forktail") during a quest, and it is known that [manticores](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manticore "Manticore") and [bruxae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bruxa "Bruxa") were also planned to appear, it stands to reason that the other two unique ingredients should've been gathered in the same manner.
