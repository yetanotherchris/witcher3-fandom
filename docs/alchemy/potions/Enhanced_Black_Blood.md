## Item ID

Black Blood 2

**Enhanced Black Blood** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being an upgraded version of [Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Blood "Black Blood"). Four of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. 

The upgrade confers a knock-back effect on [vampires](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire") and [necrophages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage "Necrophage"), in addition to a higher 20% of damage returned to them.
