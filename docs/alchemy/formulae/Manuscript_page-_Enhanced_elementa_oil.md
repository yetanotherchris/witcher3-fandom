## Item ID

Recipe for Magicals Oil 2

**Manuscript page: Enhanced elementa oil** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [enhanced elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_elementa_oil "Enhanced elementa oil"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Eternal Fire Priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)") in [Electors' Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Electors%27_Square "Electors' Square")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
