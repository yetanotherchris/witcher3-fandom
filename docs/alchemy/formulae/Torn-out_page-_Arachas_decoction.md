## Item ID

Recipe for Mutagen 2

**Torn-out page: Arachas decoction** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [arachas decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas_decoction "Arachas decoction"). 

It can be obtained by reading [The Illustrated Atlas of Insectoids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Illustrated_Atlas_of_Insectoids "The Illustrated Atlas of Insectoids"), found at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Herbalist \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Heddel\) "Herbalist \(Heddel\)") in [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") except [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

## Notes

  * This formula was supposed to be found along with [Manuscript page: Beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manuscript_page:_Beast_oil "Manuscript page: Beast oil") in [the Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar")'s hut. The chest that has it, by the door, is still there but looting it is explicitly disabled in the game files.
