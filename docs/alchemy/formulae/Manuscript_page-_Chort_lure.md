## Item ID

Recipe for Czart Lure

**Manuscript page: Chort lure** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to brew [chort lure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort_lure "Chort lure"). 

It can be automatically obtained during [Contract: Mysterious Tracks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Mysterious_Tracks "Contract: Mysterious Tracks"), or purchased from [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") or the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Lindenvale\) "Merchant \(Lindenvale\)") in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale").
