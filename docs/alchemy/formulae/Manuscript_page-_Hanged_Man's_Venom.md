## Item ID

Recipe for Hanged Man Venom 1

**Manuscript page: Hanged Man's Venom** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Hanged Man's Venom"). 

It is guaranteed to appear in tinderboxes in the basements at [Keira Metz's hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz%27s_hut "Keira Metz's hut") and in [Mowshurst](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mowshurst "Mowshurst"), right next to the corpse where [Out of the Frying Pan, into the Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Out_of_the_Frying_Pan,_into_the_Fire "Out of the Frying Pan, into the Fire") starts. It can also be found at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")

In the unlikely event that it hasn't been obtained until that point, it can also be given as a reward from [Eskel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eskel "Eskel") for beating him in a [horse race](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse_racing "Horse racing") at the end of [To Bait a Forktail...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/To_Bait_a_Forktail... "To Bait a Forktail...")
