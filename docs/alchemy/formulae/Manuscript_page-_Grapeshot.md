## Item ID

Recipe for Grapeshot 1

**Manuscript page: Grapeshot** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grapeshot "Grapeshot"). 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") already has this formula at the start of [Lilac and Gooseberries](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lilac_and_Gooseberries "Lilac and Gooseberries"). 

## Notes

  * While part of the starting equipment, if that wasn't the case, this formula would be available at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") starting at level 5, and would also be sold by [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") and [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton").
