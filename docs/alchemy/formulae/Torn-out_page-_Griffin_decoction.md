## Item ID

Recipe for Mutagen 27

**Torn-out page: Griffin decoction** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [griffin decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_decoction "Griffin decoction"). 

It can be obtained by reading [Heraldic Animals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heraldic_Animals_\(book\) "Heraldic Animals \(book\)"), found at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Bram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bram "Bram") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard")
  * [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira") in White Orchard
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Herbalist \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Heddel\) "Herbalist \(Heddel\)") in [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") except [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
