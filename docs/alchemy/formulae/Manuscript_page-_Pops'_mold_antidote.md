## Item ID

Recipe for Pops Antidote

**Manuscript page: Pops' mold antidote** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [pops' mold antidote](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pops%27_mold_antidote "Pops' mold antidote"). 

It is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dijkstra "Dijkstra") during the quest [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"), so that Geralt may replenish the antidote. 

## Associated quest

  * [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure")

## Notes

  * If a [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") is started, this formula is lost and must be relearned, however, the pops' mold antidote itself remains thus can still be drank and refilled as usual.
