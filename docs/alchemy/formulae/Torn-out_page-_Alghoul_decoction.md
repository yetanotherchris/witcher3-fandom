## Item ID

Recipe for Mutagen 15

**Torn-out page: Alghoul decoction** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [alghoul decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul_decoction "Alghoul decoction"). 

It can be obtained by reading [Ghouls and Alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghouls_and_Alghouls "Ghouls and Alghouls"), found at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kvalheim_Isle\) "Herbalist \(Kvalheim Isle\)") in [Kvalheim Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kvalheim_Isle "Kvalheim Isle"), south of [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare")
  * [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Master Topical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philibert_Topical "Philibert Topical") in his [hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Topical%27s_hut "Master Topical's hut") northwest of [Vikk Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vikk_Watchtower "Vikk Watchtower") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") except [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
