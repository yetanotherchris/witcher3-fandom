## Item ID

Recipe for White Gull 1

**Manuscript page: White Gull** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [White Gull](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Gull "White Gull"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), as regular random loot, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All base game [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") and the one in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Barrengarth\) "Herbalist \(Barrengarth\)") in [Barrengarth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barrengarth "Barrengarth") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Master Topical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philibert_Topical "Philibert Topical") in his [hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Topical%27s_hut "Master Topical's hut") northwest of [Vikk Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vikk_Watchtower "Vikk Watchtower") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
