## Item ID

Recipe for Bear Pheromone Potion 1

**Manuscript page: Bear pheromones** is is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is used to brew [bear pheromones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_pheromones "Bear pheromones"). 

Both the formula and potion and exist in the game files and are fully functional, but don't appear during gameplay and cannot be obtained without the use of console commands or mods.
