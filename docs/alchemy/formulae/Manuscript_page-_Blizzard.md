## Item ID

Recipe for Blizzard 1

**Manuscript page: Blizzard** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blizzard "Blizzard"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") starting at level 8, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in liberated [abandoned sites](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Cunny_of_the_Goose\) "Herbalist \(Cunny of the Goose\)") outside the [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") inn
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in a shop in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") at Gedyneith
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Barrengarth\) "Herbalist \(Barrengarth\)") in [Barrengarth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barrengarth "Barrengarth") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * All herbalists in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
