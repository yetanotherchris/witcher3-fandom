## Item ID

Recipe for Beast Oil 1

**Manuscript page: Beast oil** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beast_oil "Beast oil"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Eternal Fire Priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)") in [Electors' Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Electors%27_Square "Electors' Square")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")

## Notes

  * This formula was supposed to be found along with [Torn-out page: Arachas decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Arachas_decoction "Torn-out page: Arachas decoction") in [the Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar")'s hut. The chest that has it, by the door, is still there but looting it is explicitly disabled in the game files.
