## Item ID

Recipe for Albedo

**Manuscript page: Albedo** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Albedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albedo "Albedo"). 

It is guaranteed to appear in the following places: 

  * Two chests under the [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") tents at the [battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_\(White_Orchard\) "Battlefield \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), just feet away from the starting point
  * Tinderbox in [Keira Metz's hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz%27s_hut "Keira Metz's hut") basement
  * Chest inside the [Isolated Shack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Shack "Isolated Shack") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")
  * Chest in the hut southwest of [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto") (next to the third [Deadly Crossing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deadly_Crossing "Deadly Crossing"))
  * Three treasure chests inside [Hindhold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindhold "Hindhold"), as well as a tinderbox behind the [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") [nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") at the top
  * Chest outside the hut across the river east of [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy") (near where [Spooked Mare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spooked_Mare "Spooked Mare") starts)
  * Two chests on the lake isle south of [Honeyfill Meadworks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks")
  * Chest in the small alcove under the stairs from [Bridge to Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bridge_to_Kaer_Trolde "Bridge to Kaer Trolde") to the terraces
  * Chest on the platform directly north of the [Abandoned Sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Sawmill_\(location\) "Abandoned Sawmill \(location\)") signpost
  * Tinderbox in the [rock troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rock_troll "Rock troll")'s lake cave during the [Monster Slayer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_Slayer "Monster Slayer") quest

It can also be found at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Eternal Fire Priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)") in [Electors' Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Electors%27_Square "Electors' Square")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Perfumery\) "Merchant \(Perfumery\)") in the [Perfumery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perfumery "Perfumery") in [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Chuchote_Cave\) "Wine Merchant \(Chuchote Cave\)") at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Dun_Tynne_Hillside\) "Wine Merchant \(Dun Tynne Hillside\)") at [Dun Tynne Hillside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Hillside "Dun Tynne Hillside") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

## Notes

  * This formula can actually be found in two more locations, which however are unreachable - two chests inside [Mislav](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mislav "Mislav")'s hut in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), which is always locked, and the big chest atop the western tower at [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen")'s entrance.
