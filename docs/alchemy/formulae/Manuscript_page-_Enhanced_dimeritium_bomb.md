## Item ID

Recipe for Dwimeritium Bomb 2

**Manuscript page: Enhanced dimeritium bomb** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Enhanced Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dimeritium_bomb "Enhanced Dimeritium bomb"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") starting at level 10, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kaer_Trolde\) "Herbalist \(Kaer Trolde\)") in [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") at Gedyneith
  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") and [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
