## Item ID

Recipe for Samum 1

**Manuscript page: Samum** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Samum "Samum"). 

This formula is part of the starting equipment, which becomes accessible upon arrival in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

## Notes

  * Despite being part of the starting equipment, if it was possible for [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to not have it, this formula would also be present in some [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith")' stores, and would appear at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") plus as guaranteed loot at the [water hag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag") [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") southwest of the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard").
