## Item ID

Recipe for Thunderbolt 1

**Manuscript page: Thunderbolt** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thunderbolt "Thunderbolt"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") starting at level 3, but if that hasn't happened yet, it will be received from [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") right before fighting the [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests"), [The Beast of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beast_of_White_Orchard "The Beast of White Orchard"). 

## Notes

  * Vesemir giving the formula effectively makes it impossible to purchase, as all [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") who sell it are inaccessible before that point of the main story. However, it technically is offered by [Otto Bamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber"), [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar"), and all [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") except [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri").
