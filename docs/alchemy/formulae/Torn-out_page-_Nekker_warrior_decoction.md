## Item ID

Recipe for Mutagen 25

**Torn-out page: Nekker warrior decoction** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [nekker warrior decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior_decoction "Nekker warrior decoction"). 

It can be obtained by reading [The World Underground](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_World_Underground "The World Underground") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Herbalist \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Heddel\) "Herbalist \(Heddel\)") in [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist#Toussaint "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") except [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

## Notes

  * This is the only non-quest recipe in the game that must be purchased, one way or another, as the book The World Underground is also only available as a purchase.
