## Item ID

Recipe for Mutagen 22

**Torn-out page: Ancient leshen decoction** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [ancient leshen decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_leshen_decoction "Ancient leshen decoction"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Druids%27_Camp\) "Herbalist \(Druids' Camp\)") in the [Druids' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_Camp "Druids' Camp")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Ruined_Inn\) "Herbalist \(Ruined Inn\)") at the [Ruined Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_Inn "Ruined Inn")
  * [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") except [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can also be obtained by reading [The Heart of the Wilds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heart_of_the_Wilds "The Heart of the Wilds"), but the book never appears during gameplay.
