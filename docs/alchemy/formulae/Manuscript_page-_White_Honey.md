## Item ID

Recipe for White Honey 1

**Manuscript page: White Honey** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Honey "White Honey"). 

You already possess this recipe at the start of [Lilac and Gooseberries](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lilac_and_Gooseberries "Lilac and Gooseberries"). 

## Notes

  * Unlike other [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") formulae that are part of the starting equipment, this one would also technically become available at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") starting at level 15, around which superior versions of other regular potions appear.
