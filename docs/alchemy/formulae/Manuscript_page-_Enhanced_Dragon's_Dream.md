## Item ID

Recipe for Dragons Dream 2

**Manuscript page: Enhanced Dragon's Dream** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Enhanced Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dragon%27s_Dream "Enhanced Dragon's Dream"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") starting at level 5, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in a shop in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kaer_Trolde\) "Herbalist \(Kaer Trolde\)") in [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde")
  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
