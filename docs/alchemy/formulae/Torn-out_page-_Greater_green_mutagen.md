Recipe for Greater mutagen green

**Torn-out page: Greater green mutagen** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [greater green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_green_mutagen "Greater green mutagen"). 

This formula is part of the starting equipment, which becomes accessible upon arrival in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard").
