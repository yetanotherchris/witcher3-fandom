## Item ID

Recipe for Trial Potion Kit

**Formula: The Decoctions of the Grasses** is an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") during the main quest [Va Fail, Elaine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Va_Fail,_Elaine "Va Fail, Elaine") so he can brew [the Decoctions of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Decoctions_of_the_Grasses "The Decoctions of the Grasses").
