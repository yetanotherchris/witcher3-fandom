## Item ID

Mandrake cordial

**Mandrake cordial** is considered an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and thus can't be consumed like regular drinks. It's needed to craft [white gull](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_gull "White gull"). 

In the base game it must be purchased, but with the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion it can also be found as random loot in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint").
