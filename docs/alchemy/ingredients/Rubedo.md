# Rubedo

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Rubedo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/a8/Substances_Small_Rubedo.png/revision/latest?cb=20170711143917)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/a8/Substances_Small_Rubedo.png/revision/latest?cb=20170711143917 "Rubedo")Rubedo

**Rubedo** is an additional, or secondary substance which is present in some alchemical ingredients in addition to the primary substance. All alchemical [ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_ingredients "The Witcher ingredients") contain one of six basic substances: [aether](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aether "Aether"), [hydragenum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hydragenum "Hydragenum"), [quebrith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quebrith "Quebrith"), [rebis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rebis "Rebis"), [vermilion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vermilion "Vermilion"), and [vitriol](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitriol "Vitriol"). There are also three additional substances: [albedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albedo "Albedo"), [nigredo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nigredo "Nigredo"), and rubedo. Any given ingredient may contain only a single basic substance and potentially one additional, or secondary substance.

## Item ID

Rubedo

Unlike previous games, **Rubedo** is not simply part of other [alchemical ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_beast_oil "Superior beast oil")
  * [Superior cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_cursed_oil "Superior cursed oil")
  * [Lesser red mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_red_mutagen "Lesser red mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Red mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Red_mutagen "Red mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Greater red mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_red_mutagen "Greater red mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Petri%27s_Philter "Superior Petri's Philter")
  * [Pink armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pink_armor_dye "Pink armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Red armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Red_armor_dye "Red armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

One dose is always dropped by [Hubert Rejk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk") if he is killed during [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins"), but otherwise in the base game it must be crafted. In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be purchased from [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Perfumery\) "Merchant \(Perfumery\)") at the [Perfumery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perfumery "Perfumery")
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Chuchote_Cave\) "Wine Merchant \(Chuchote Cave\)") at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave"), after completing his quest
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Duchaton_Crest\) "Wine Merchant \(Duchaton Crest\)") at [Dun Tynne Hillside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Hillside "Dun Tynne Hillside"), after completing his quest

## Trivia

  * In alchemy, the _rubedo_ means "coagulation" of the matter, and is the last step [Magnum opus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magnum_opus_\(alchemy\) "wikipedia:Magnum opus \(alchemy\)") process.
