## Item ID

Harpy feathers

**Harpy feathers** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster feather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_feather "Monster feather"). 

It is a random drop from [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy"), and guaranteed from their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") and [erynias](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia "Erynia") who guard them. In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be found as regular random loot in the [Witch of Lynx Crag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_of_Lynx_Crag "Witch of Lynx Crag")'s hut during [A Knight's Tales](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Knight%27s_Tales "A Knight's Tales").
