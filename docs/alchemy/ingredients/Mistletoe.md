## Item ID

Mistletoe

**Mistletoe** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Albedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")
  * [Superior cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_cursed_oil "Superior cursed oil")
  * [Enhanced Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dragon%27s_Dream "Enhanced Dragon's Dream")
  * [Superior Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Full_Moon "Superior Full Moon")
  * [Nightwraith decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith_decoction "Nightwraith decoction")
  * [Rebis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rebis "Rebis")
  * [Relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Relict_oil "Relict oil")
  * [Rubedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rubedo "Rubedo")
  * [Enhanced specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_specter_oil "Enhanced specter oil")
  * [Superior specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_specter_oil "Superior specter oil")
  * [White armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_armor_dye "White armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(White_Orchard_sawmill\) "Merchant \(White Orchard sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(White_Orchard\) "Sawmill \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard")
  * Generic [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Boatmakers%27_Hut\) "Merchant \(Boatmakers' Hut\)") at the [Boatmakers' Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boatmakers%27_Hut "Boatmakers' Hut")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Border_Post\) "Merchant \(Border Post\)") southeast of the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(fishing_village\) "Merchant \(fishing village\)") in [fishing village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(fishing_village\) "Abandoned site \(fishing village\)") east of [Stonecutters' Settlement](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stonecutters%27_Settlement "Stonecutters' Settlement")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(campsite\) "Merchant \(campsite\)") in the [campsite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(campsite\) "Abandoned site \(campsite\)") west of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Mire_Landing\) "Merchant \(Mire Landing\)") at [Mire Landing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mire_Landing "Mire Landing") (Cestersover)
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Mulbrydale_crossroads\) "Merchant \(Mulbrydale crossroads\)") at a crossroads between [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale") and [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Bald_Mountain\) "Merchant \(Bald Mountain\)") by the bonfire at [Road to Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Road_to_Bald_Mountain "Road to Bald Mountain")

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Crane_Cape\) "Merchant \(Crane Cape\)") at [Crane Cape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crane_Cape "Crane Cape") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)

It is quite rare as a plant, but one place where it can easily be farmed is the graveyard by the Pellar's hut.
