## Item ID

Specter dust

**Specter dust** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [powdered monster tissue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Powdered_monster_tissue "Powdered monster tissue"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Petri%27s_Philter "Petri's Philter")
  * [Enhanced Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Petri%27s_Philter "Enhanced Petri's Philter")

It is a random drop from all [specter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") class [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"), with significantly higher chance to drop from [nightwraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith") and [beann'shies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Banshee "Banshee"), and can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Yoana](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yoana "Yoana") in [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Mohrin_Village\) "Merchant \(Mohrin Village\)") in [Mohrin Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mohrin_Village "Mohrin Village") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
