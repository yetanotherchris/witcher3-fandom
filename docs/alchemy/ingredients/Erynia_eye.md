## Item ID

Erynie eye

**Erynia eye** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_eye "Monster eye") and [monster feather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_feather "Monster feather"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_hybrid_oil "Enhanced hybrid oil")
  * [Superior hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_hybrid_oil "Superior hybrid oil")

It is a guaranteed drop from [erynias](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia "Erynia"), except those who guard [harpy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"). They are most often encountered throughout [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), like north of [Redgill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redgill "Redgill"), while a few live in [Kaer Morhen valley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen_valley "Kaer Morhen valley").
