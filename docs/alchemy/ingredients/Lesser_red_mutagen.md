## Item ID

Lesser mutagen red

**Lesser red mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is used to craft [red mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Red_mutagen "Red mutagen"), [alghoul decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul_decoction "Alghoul decoction") and [reliever's decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reliever%27s_decoction "Reliever's decoction"). 

They can also be used to augment [abilities](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities") but are not as effective as their regular or [greater](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_red_mutagen "Greater red mutagen") counterparts. When placed in the diamond-shaped slot in the character panel, they give +5% to attack power, which increases by 5% up to a max of +20% if all 3 skills attached to it are red. 

Like all [regular mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens"), they drop from many creatures, but are rarer than the other two colors and generally only dropped by stronger, rarer monsters such as: 

  * [Alghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul")
  * [Devourer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devourer "Devourer")
  * [Drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowned_dead "Drowned dead")
  * [Foglet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fogler "Fogler")
  * [Fiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend")
  * [Nekker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker")
  * [Nekker warrior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior "Nekker warrior")
  * [Arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas "Arachas") variations
  * [Arachnomorph](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachnomorph "Arachnomorph") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Botchling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Botchling "Botchling")
  * [Draconids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Draconid "Draconid")
  * [Ekimmara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekimma "Ekimma")
  * [Endrega](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega "Endrega") variations
  * [Katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan")

Sometimes it can be dropped by [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy"), [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul"), as well as [monster nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") "bosses" regardless of species.

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
