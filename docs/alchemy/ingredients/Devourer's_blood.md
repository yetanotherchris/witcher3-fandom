## Item ID

Greater Rotfiend blood

**Devourer's blood** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Superior Hanged Man's Venom")
  * [Superior necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_necrophage_oil "Superior necrophage oil")
  * [Superior relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_relict_oil "Superior relict oil")

It is a guaranteed drop from [devourers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devourer "Devourer"), except those who guard [rotfiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend "Rotfiend") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"). 

## Notes

  * There is an abandoned town called [Toderas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toderas "Toderas") northwest of [Benek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Benek "Benek") and northeast of [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor"), forming a triangle, where you find a pack of looters arguing. After you complete the event, however you wish to do so, simply fast travel somewhere else and then back to Toderas and there should be 4-5 devourers spawned along with possibly an [alghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul").
