**Drowner brain tissue** is obtained from [drowner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") carcasses. 

### Source

  * In [Chapter I](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chapters "Chapters"), an "Old townswoman" will tell Geralt a tale about drowned dead in exchange for food. This conversation results in journal entries for [Drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") _(not Drowned Dead,)_ Drowner Brain Tissue, [Cadaverine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cadaverine "Cadaverine"), and [Ginatz's Acid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ginatz%27s_Acid "Ginatz's Acid").
  * [Swamp Monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_Monsters "Swamp Monsters")

they also tend to make any man hornier than a succubus 

### Notes

  * Geralt can loot the bodies of drowners for cadaverine and ginatz's acid before he has a Bestiary entry for drowners or ingredient entries for those items. However, drowner remains will not contain drowner brain tissue until Geralt has the journal entries.
  * You will need three of these for the [Reverend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reverend_\(Outskirts\) "Reverend \(Outskirts\)") in [Chapter I](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chapters "Chapters").

## Item ID

Drowner brain

**Drowner brain** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that can be dismantled into [monster brain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_brain "Monster brain") and [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Drowner pheromones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner_pheromones "Drowner pheromones")
  * [Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swallow "Swallow")
  * [Enhanced Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Swallow "Enhanced Swallow")

It is a random drop from [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"), their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowned_dead "Drowned dead"), and [mucknixers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mucknixer "Mucknixer"). A few can also be found as guaranteeed regular loot, on a [witch hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter")'s corpse by a drowner nest on the swampy coastline far west of [the Orphans of Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog"), and in the cave during [Contract: Muire D'yaeblen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Muire_D%27yaeblen "Contract: Muire D'yaeblen"). 

### Notes

  * Drowner brain was meant to also be used in [nekker pheromones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_pheromones "Nekker pheromones"), but the potion was never implemented into the game.
