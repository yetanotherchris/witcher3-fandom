## Item ID

White myrtle

**White myrtle petals** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Arachas decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas_decoction "Arachas decoction")
  * [Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blizzard "Blizzard")
  * [Enhanced Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Blizzard "Enhanced Blizzard")
  * [Superior Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Blizzard "Superior Blizzard")
  * [Dwarven spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarven_spirit "Dwarven spirit")
  * [Ekimmara decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekimmara_decoction "Ekimmara decoction")
  * [Hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hybrid_oil "Hybrid oil")
  * [Enhanced hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_hybrid_oil "Enhanced hybrid oil")
  * [Superior hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_hybrid_oil "Superior hybrid oil")
  * [Pops' mold antidote](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pops%27_mold_antidote "Pops' mold antidote")
  * [Enhanced Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Swallow "Enhanced Swallow")
  * [Superior Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Swallow "Superior Swallow")
  * [Enhanced White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_White_Honey "Enhanced White Honey")
  * [Superior White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_White_Honey "Superior White Honey")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, as reward for letting the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/John_Geermer "John Geermer") go but keeping the [locked strongbox](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Locked_strongbox "Locked strongbox") during [Precious Cargo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Precious_Cargo "Precious Cargo"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):
