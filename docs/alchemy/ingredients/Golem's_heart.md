## Item ID

Golem heart

**Golem's heart** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_heart "Monster heart") and [monster essence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_essence "Monster essence"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blizzard "Blizzard")
  * [Enhanced Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Blizzard "Enhanced Blizzard")

It is a guaranteed drop from [Fire Elementals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Elemental "Fire Elemental") and [golems](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem").
