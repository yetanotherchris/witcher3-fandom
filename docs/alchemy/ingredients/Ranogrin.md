## Item ID

Ranogrin

**Ranogrin** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Golden_Oriole "Superior Golden Oriole")
  * [Greater green mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_green_mutagen "Greater green mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Hydragenum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hydragenum "Hydragenum")
  * [Insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid_Oil "Insectoid Oil")
  * [Enhanced insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_insectoid_oil "Enhanced insectoid oil")
  * [Nekker warrior decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior_decoction "Nekker warrior decoction")
  * [Enhanced ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_ogroid_oil "Enhanced ogroid oil")
  * [Superior ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_ogroid_oil "Superior ogroid oil")
  * [Superior Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Petri%27s_Philter "Superior Petri's Philter")
  * [Rebis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rebis "Rebis")
  * [Vitriol](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitriol "Vitriol")
  * [Yellow armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yellow_armor_dye "Yellow armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
