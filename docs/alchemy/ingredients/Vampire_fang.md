## Item ID

Vampire fang

**Vampire fang** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster tooth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_tooth "Monster tooth") and [vampire saliva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire_saliva "Vampire saliva"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from [alps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alp "Alp"), [garkains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Garkain "Garkain"), [fleders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fleder "Fleder"), and randomly dropped by all other [vampires](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire").
