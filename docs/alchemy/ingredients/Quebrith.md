## Item ID

Quebrith

Unlike previous games, **Quebrith** is not simply part of other [alchemical ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Full_Moon "Superior Full Moon")
  * [Superior Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Golden_Oriole "Superior Golden Oriole")
  * [Superior Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Superior Hanged Man's Venom")
  * [Superior Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Northern_Wind "Superior Northern Wind")
  * [Superior Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Thunderbolt "Superior Thunderbolt")

One dose is a guaranteed drop from the [katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan") encountered during [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), but otherwise in the base game it must be crafted. In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be purchased from [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Perfumery\) "Merchant \(Perfumery\)") at the [Perfumery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perfumery "Perfumery")
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Chuchote_Cave\) "Wine Merchant \(Chuchote Cave\)") at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave"), after completing his quest
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Duchaton_Crest\) "Wine Merchant \(Duchaton Crest\)") at [Dun Tynne Hillside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Hillside "Dun Tynne Hillside"), after completing his quest

## Trivia

  * Quebrith, or _kebrith_ , is the alchemic definition of [sulfur](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sulfur "Sulfur") given by [Paracelsus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paracelsus "wikipedia:Paracelsus").
