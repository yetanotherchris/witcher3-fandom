## Item ID

Lesser mutagen blue

**Lesser blue mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to craft [blue mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_mutagen "Blue mutagen") and [reliever's decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reliever%27s_decoction "Reliever's decoction"). They can also be used to augment [abilities](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities"), but are not as effective as their regular or [greater counterparts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_blue_mutagen "Greater blue mutagen"). When placed in the diamond-shaped slot in the character panel, they give +5% to [sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") intensity, which increases by 5 up to a max of +20% if all 3 skills attached to it are blue. 

Like all [regular mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they drop from many organic monsters. 

## _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion

With _Blood and Wine_ , the player can now craft these with other ingredients.

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
