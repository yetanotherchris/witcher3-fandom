## Item ID

Dog tallow

**Dog tallow** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft many [blade oils](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_oils "The Witcher 3 oils"): 

  * [Beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beast_oil "Beast oil")
  * [Cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cursed_oil "Cursed oil")
  * [Draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Draconid_oil "Draconid oil")
  * [Elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa_oil "Elementa oil")
  * [Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Hanged Man's Venom")
  * [Hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hybrid_oil "Hybrid oil")
  * [Insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid_oil "Insectoid oil")
  * [Necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage_oil "Necrophage oil")
  * [Relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Relict_oil "Relict oil")
  * [Vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire_oil "Vampire oil")

It is a random drop from all [dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog") and [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)"), though it also appears as regular random loot. It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")
  * All herbalists in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
