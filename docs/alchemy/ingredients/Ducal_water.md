## Item ID

Ducal water

**Ducal water** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Wind "Northern Wind")
  * [Enhanced Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Northern_Wind "Enhanced Northern Wind")
  * [Superior Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Northern_Wind "Superior Northern Wind")
  * [Vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire_oil "Vampire oil")

It can be found as regular random loot, random drops from bilge and [water hags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") except the [Eternal Fire Priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)")
  * All base game [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Putrid_Grove\) "Merchant \(Putrid Grove\)") in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Barrengarth\) "Herbalist \(Barrengarth\)") in [Barrengarth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barrengarth "Barrengarth") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Master Topical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philibert_Topical "Philibert Topical") in his [hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Topical%27s_hut "Master Topical's hut") northwest of [Vikk Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vikk_Watchtower "Vikk Watchtower") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") in her [home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri%27s_Hermitage "Pinastri's Hermitage") northeast of [Dun Tynne Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Crossroads "Dun Tynne Crossroads") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
