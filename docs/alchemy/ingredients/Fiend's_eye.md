## Item ID

Fiend eye

**Fiend's eye** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_eye "Monster eye") and [monster hair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hair "Monster hair"). 

It is a random drop from all [fiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend"). In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be found as regular random loot in the [Witch of Lynx Crag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_of_Lynx_Crag "Witch of Lynx Crag")'s hut during [A Knight's Tales](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Knight%27s_Tales "A Knight's Tales").
