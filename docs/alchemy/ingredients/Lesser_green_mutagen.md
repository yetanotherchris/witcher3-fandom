## Item ID

Lesser mutagen green

**Lesser green mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is be used to craft [green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Green_mutagen "Green mutagen") and [reliever's decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reliever%27s_decoction "Reliever's decoction"). They can also be used to augment [abilities](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities"), but are not as effective as their regular or [greater](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_green_mutagen "Greater green mutagen") counterparts. When placed in the diamond-shaped slot in the character panel, they give +50 to vitality, which increases by 50 up to a max of +200 if all 3 skills attached to it are green. 

Like all [regular mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they drop from many organic monsters. 

## _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion

With _Blood and Wine_ , the player can now craft these with other ingredients.
