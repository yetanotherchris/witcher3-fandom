## Item ID

Lamia lock of hair

**Lock of lamia hair** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster hair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hair "Monster hair"). 

It is a random drop from all [ekhidnae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna "Ekhidna"), except those who guard [siren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest").
