## Item ID

Harpy talon

**Harpy talons** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster claw](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_claw "Monster claw"). 

It is a random drop from [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") and their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and guaranteed from [erynias](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia "Erynia") who guard them.
