## Item ID

Nostrix

**Nostrix** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Black_Blood "Superior Black Blood")
  * [Enhanced Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dancing_Star "Enhanced Dancing Star")
  * [Superior Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dancing_Star "Superior Dancing Star")
  * [Superior draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_draconid_oil "Superior draconid oil")
  * [Superior elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_elementa_oil "Superior elementa oil")
  * [Hydragenum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hydragenum "Hydragenum")
  * [Quebrith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quebrith "Quebrith")
  * [Wraith decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith_decoction "Wraith decoction")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):

  * Generic [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
