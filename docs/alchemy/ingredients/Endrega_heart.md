## Item ID

Endriag heart

**Endrega heart** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_heart "Monster heart"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Devil%27s_Puffball "Enhanced Devil's Puffball")
  * [Superior Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Devil%27s_Puffball "Superior Devil's Puffball")
  * [Enhanced insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_insectoid_oil "Enhanced insectoid oil")
  * [Superior insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_insectoid_oil "Superior insectoid oil")

It is a random drop from all [endregas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega "Endrega"), and guaranteed from their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest").
