## Item ID

Werewolf saliva

**Werewolf saliva** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster saliva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_saliva "Monster saliva"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from [werewolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") and [ulfhedinn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulfhedinn "Ulfhedinn").
