**Manticore poison gland** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is used to craft [The Decoctions of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Decoctions_of_the_Grasses "The Decoctions of the Grasses") during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests"), [Va Fail, Elaine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Va_Fail,_Elaine "Va Fail, Elaine").
