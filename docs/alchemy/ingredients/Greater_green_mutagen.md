## Item ID

Greater mutagen green

**Greater green mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to augment [abilities](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities") and are more effective than their [lesser](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_green_mutagen "Lesser green mutagen") and [regular](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Green_mutagen "Green mutagen") counterparts. When placed in the diamond-shaped slot in the character panel, they give +150 to vitality, which increases by 150 up to a max of +600 if all 3 skills attached to it are green. 

Like all greater [mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be crafted outside of the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 

## _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion

With _Blood and Wine_ , the player can now craft these with other ingredients that don't include green mutagens. A couple of them can also be found as non-monster loot during the quests [Turn and Face the Strange](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Turn_and_Face_the_Strange "Turn and Face the Strange") and [La Cage au Fou](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/La_Cage_au_Fou "La Cage au Fou").

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
