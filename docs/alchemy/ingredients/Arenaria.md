## Item ID

Arenaria

**Arenaria** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_draconid_oil "Enhanced draconid oil")
  * [Superior draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_draconid_oil "Superior draconid oil")
  * [Fiend decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend_decoction "Fiend decoction")
  * [Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Hanged Man's Venom")
  * [Enhanced Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Enhanced Hanged Man's Venom")
  * [Katakan decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan_decoction "Katakan decoction")
  * [Enhanced necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_necrophage_oil "Enhanced necrophage oil")
  * [Superior necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_necrophage_oil "Superior necrophage oil")
  * [Superior ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_ogroid_oil "Superior ogroid oil")
  * [Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Petri%27s_Philter "Petri's Philter")
  * [Enhanced Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Petri%27s_Philter "Enhanced Petri's Philter")
  * [Superior Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Petri%27s_Philter "Superior Petri's Philter")
  * [Specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Specter_oil "Specter oil")
  * [Enhanced specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_specter_oil "Enhanced specter oil")
  * [White armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_armor_dye "White armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [White Gull](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Seagull "White Seagull")

It can be found as random loot, guaranteed in the chest during [A Dog's Life](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dog%27s_Life "A Dog's Life"), gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Gildorf\) "Merchant \(Gildorf\)") in the [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shop_\(Gildorf\) "Shop \(Gildorf\)") in [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")

Arenaria plants in the base game can be hard to find outside of [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), where it is most easily farmed in the fields between the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(White_Orchard\) "Sawmill \(White Orchard\)") and [Woesong Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Woesong_Bridge "Woesong Bridge"). It is much more prevalent in the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion.
