## Item ID

Longrube

**Longrube** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Aether](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aether "Aether")
  * [Doppler decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler_decoction "Doppler decoction")
  * [Enhanced Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Grapeshot "Enhanced Grapeshot")
  * [Superior Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Grapeshot "Superior Grapeshot")
  * [Grave hag decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grave_hag_decoction "Grave hag decoction")
  * [Enhanced ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_ogroid_oil "Enhanced ogroid oil")
  * [Orange armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orange_armor_dye "Orange armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Petri%27s_Philter "Superior Petri's Philter")
  * [Quebrith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quebrith "Quebrith")
  * [Superior specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_specter_oil "Superior specter oil")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):
