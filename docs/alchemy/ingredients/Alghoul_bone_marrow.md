## Item ID

Alghoul bone marrow

**Alghoul bone marrow** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster bone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_bone "Monster bone") and [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Alghoul decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul_decoction "Alghoul decoction")
  * [Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maribor_Forest_\(potion\) "Maribor Forest \(potion\)")

It is a guaranteed drop from [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul") and [ghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest").
