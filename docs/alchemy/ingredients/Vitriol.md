## Item ID

Vitriol

Unlike previous games, **Vitriol** is not simply part of other [alchemical ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is its own item. It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Blue armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_armor_dye "Blue armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Lesser blue mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_blue_mutagen "Lesser blue mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Blue mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_mutagen "Blue mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Greater blue mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_blue_mutagen "Greater blue mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Swallow "Superior Swallow")
  * [Superior White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_White_Honey "Superior White Honey")
  * [Turquoise armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Turquoise_armor_dye "Turquoise armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

Unlike other alchemical substances of its kind, while it is still necessary to craft early in the game, Vitriol can also be reliably found more than once: 

  * In 5 crates at [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen")'s main hall, appearing at the start of [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen") (each crate has 50% chance to have vitriol, but you'll know them for always having [balisse fruit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Balisse_fruit "Balisse fruit"), [berbercane fruit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berbercane_fruit "Berbercane fruit"), [crow's eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_eye "Crow's eye") and [white myrtle petals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_myrtle_petals "White myrtle petals"))
  * In the medic's tent at the [Tourney Grounds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tourney_Grounds "Tourney Grounds") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint")

In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be purchased from [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Perfumery\) "Merchant \(Perfumery\)") at the [Perfumery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perfumery "Perfumery")
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Chuchote_Cave\) "Wine Merchant \(Chuchote Cave\)") at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave"), after completing his quest
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Duchaton_Crest\) "Wine Merchant \(Duchaton Crest\)") at [Dun Tynne Hillside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Hillside "Dun Tynne Hillside"), after completing his quest

## Trivia

  * Vitriol is an archaic name for sulfate. The name comes from Latin "vitreolum", which means "glassy".
