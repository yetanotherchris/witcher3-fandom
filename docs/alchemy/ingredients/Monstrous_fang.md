## Item ID

Hag teeth

**Monstrous fang** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster teeth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_tooth "Monster tooth") and [monster saliva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_saliva "Monster saliva"). 

It is a random drop from [grave hags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grave_hag "Grave hag").
