## Item ID

Bison Grass

**Bison grass** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_beast_oil "Enhanced beast oil")
  * [Superior beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_beast_oil "Superior beast oil")
  * [Green armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Green_armor_dye "Green armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_hybrid_oil "Superior hybrid oil")
  * [Superior White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "Superior White Raffard's Decoction")

Unlike most herbs, it can't be found growing anywhere in the game, and is extremely rare as random loot, making it almost certain that some has to be bought in order to obtain all the necessary doses. It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in a shop in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [Otto Bamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber") at the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut") northeast of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kaer_Trolde\) "Herbalist \(Kaer Trolde\)") in [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
