The stems of the **bryonia** are used in alchemy. It is a plant which grows in wastelands. 

### Source

  * After completing the [Daily Bread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Daily_Bread "Daily Bread") quest, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can give any meat food to the [elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") in and around the cave. Any elf inside the cave at night will unlock [Han](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Han "Han") and [mandrake root](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mandrake_root "Mandrake root") in return. The elf who stays outside of the cave even at night will give entries for [Honeysuckle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeysuckle "Honeysuckle") and Bryonia instead.
  * One of many "Old peasant women" in [Murky Waters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Murky_Waters "Murky Waters") will give Geralt entries for [Verbena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Verbena "Verbena"), [Hop umbels](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hop_umbels "Hop umbels"), and finally Bryonia in exchange for "something sweet" _(Candy and Sugardolls work)_ each time.

  * [Mottle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mottle "Mottle") outside [Vergen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vergen "Vergen")
  * [Sambor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sambor "Sambor") in the [camp followers' encampment](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Camp_followers%27_encampment "Camp followers' encampment")
  * [Myron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myron "Myron") in the [Kaedweni camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedweni_camp "Kaedweni camp")

## Item ID

Bryonia

**Bryonia** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [The Decoctions of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Decoctions_of_the_Grasses "The Decoctions of the Grasses")
  * [Enhanced draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_draconid_oil "Enhanced draconid oil")
  * [Superior draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_draconid_oil "Superior draconid oil")
  * [Enhanced Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dragon%27s_Dream "Enhanced Dragon's Dream")
  * [Superior Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dragon%27s_Dream "Superior Dragon's Dream")
  * [Enhanced hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_hybrid_oil "Enhanced hybrid oil")
  * [Forktail decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail_decoction "Forktail decoction")
  * [Griffin decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_decoction "Griffin decoction")
  * [Superior Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Thunderbolt "Superior Thunderbolt")
  * [Enhanced White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "Enhanced White Raffard's Decoction")
  * [Superior White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "Superior White Raffard's Decoction")
  * [Vermilion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vermilion "Vermilion")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")

## Trivia

  * [Bryonia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bryony "wikipedia:Bryony") has 12 species under its name in the real world. Herbal medicine has found uses for some of them.
