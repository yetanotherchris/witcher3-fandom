## Item ID

Phosphorus

**Phosphorus** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Brown armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brown_armor_dye "Brown armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Enhanced Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dancing_Star "Enhanced Dancing Star")
  * [Superior Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dancing_Star "Superior Dancing Star")
  * [Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon%27s_Dream_\(bomb\) "Dragon's Dream \(bomb\)")
  * [Enhanced Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dragon%27s_Dream "Enhanced Dragon's Dream")
  * [Superior Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dragon%27s_Dream "Superior Dragon's Dream")
  * [Enhanced Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Samum "Enhanced Samum")
  * [Superior Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Samum "Superior Samum")

It can be found as regular random loot, random drop from [tangalores](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fogler "Fogler"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") except the [Eternal Fire Priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") outside of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Putrid_Grove\) "Merchant \(Putrid Grove\)") in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove")
  * [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") in her [home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri%27s_Hermitage "Pinastri's Hermitage") northeast of [Dun Tynne Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Crossroads "Dun Tynne Crossroads") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
