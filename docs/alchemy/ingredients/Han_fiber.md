## Item ID

Han

**Han fiber** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is used to craft the following items: 

  * [Aether](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aether "Aether")
  * [Superior Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Black_Blood "Superior Black Blood")
  * [Doppler decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler_decoction "Doppler decoction")
  * [Superior Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Golden_Oriole "Superior Golden Oriole")
  * [Enhanced Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Enhanced Hanged Man's Venom")
  * [Nigredo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nigredo "Nigredo")
  * [Orange armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orange_armor_dye "Orange armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Rubedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rubedo "Rubedo")
  * [Vermilion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vermilion "Vermilion")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, received as reward for completing [Practicum in Advanced Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Practicum_in_Advanced_Alchemy "Practicum in Advanced Alchemy"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except the ones in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
