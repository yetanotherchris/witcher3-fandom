## Item ID

Drowned dead tongue

**Drowner tongue** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster saliva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_saliva "Monster saliva") and [monster tongue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_tongue "Monster tongue"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Killer Whale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Killer_Whale "Killer Whale")
  * [Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maribor_Forest_\(potion\) "Maribor Forest \(potion\)")
  * [Enhanced Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Maribor_Forest "Enhanced Maribor Forest")

It is a random drop from [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"), [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"), [mucknixers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mucknixer "Mucknixer"), and guaranteed from their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest").
