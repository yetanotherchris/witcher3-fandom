## Item ID

Alchemists powder

**Alchemists' powder** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft several [bombs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs"): 

  * [Superior Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dancing_Star "Superior Dancing Star")
  * [Superior Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Devil%27s_Puffball "Superior Devil's Puffball")
  * [Superior Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dimeritium_bomb "Superior Dimeritium bomb")
  * [Superior Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dragon%27s_Dream "Superior Dragon's Dream")
  * [Superior Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Grapeshot "Superior Grapeshot")
  * [Superior Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Moon_Dust "Superior Moon Dust")
  * [Superior Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Samum "Superior Samum")

It can be found as random loot or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") outside of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Putrid_Grove\) "Merchant \(Putrid Grove\)") in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove")
  * [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") in her [home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri%27s_Hermitage "Pinastri's Hermitage") northeast of [Dun Tynne Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Crossroads "Dun Tynne Crossroads") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
