**Green mold** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_ingredients "The Witcher 2 ingredients") and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_quest_items "The Witcher 2 quest items") which can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_merchants "The Witcher 2 merchants"): 

  * [Anezka](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anezka "Anezka") in [Lobinden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lobinden "Lobinden")
  * [Myron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myron "Myron") in the [Kaedweni camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedweni_camp "Kaedweni camp")
  * [Marcus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus "Marcus") in [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne")
  * [The Incredible Lockhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Incredible_Lockhart "The Incredible Lockhart") in Loc Muinne

### Notes

  * This plant is required, along with [wolf's aloe leaves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf%27s_aloe_leaves "Wolf's aloe leaves") and [white myrtle petals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_myrtle_petals "White myrtle petals"), for the tattoo removal spell during the quest [Hung Over](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hung_Over "Hung Over"), which can be performed by [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss "Triss") or [Síle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sheala_de_Tancarville "Sheala de Tancarville") in Chapter I, or by [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart") in Chapter II.
