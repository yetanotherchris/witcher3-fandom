## Item ID

Arachas eyes

**Arachas eyes** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). They are not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_eye "Monster eye"). However, unless one is desperate for monster eyes (unlikely), it makes the wisest economic sense to sell these to an [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") rather than dismantle them. 

They are a random drop from all [arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas "Arachas"), [armored arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armored_arachas "Armored arachas"), and [venomous arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Venomous_arachas "Venomous arachas").
