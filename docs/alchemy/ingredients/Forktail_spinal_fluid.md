# Forktail spinal fluid

## Item ID

q401_forktail_brain

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") harvests one bottle of forktail spinal fluid during the quest [To Bait a Forktail...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/To_Bait_a_Forktail... "To Bait a Forktail..."). The item is automatically added to the inventory during the dialogue with [Eskel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eskel "Eskel") after killing the [forktail](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail "Forktail").

## Item ID

q401_trial_key_ingredient_a

This bottle of forktail spinal fluid is used to make [the Decoctions of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Decoctions_of_the_Grasses "The Decoctions of the Grasses") during the quest [Va Fail, Elaine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Va_Fail,_Elaine "Va Fail, Elaine"). It can be looted from a chest in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), along with the other necessary ingredients, after [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") tells Geralt to brew the decoctions.
