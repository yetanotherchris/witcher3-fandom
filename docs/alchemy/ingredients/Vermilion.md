## Item ID

Vermilion

Unlike previous games, **Vermilion** is not simply part of other [alchemical ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Maribor_Forest "Superior Maribor Forest")
  * [Orange armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orange_armor_dye "Orange armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_vampire_oil "Superior vampire oil")
  * [Superior White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "Superior White Raffard's Decoction")

In the base game it must be crafted, but in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion it can also be purchased from [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Perfumery\) "Merchant \(Perfumery\)") at the [Perfumery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perfumery "Perfumery")
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Chuchote_Cave\) "Wine Merchant \(Chuchote Cave\)") at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave"), after completing his quest
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Duchaton_Crest\) "Wine Merchant \(Duchaton Crest\)") at [Dun Tynne Hillside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Hillside "Dun Tynne Hillside"), after completing his quest
