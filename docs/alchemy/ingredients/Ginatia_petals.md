**Ginatia petals** – Ginatia are the flower petals of a common bush. This herb is plentiful in Chapters III and IV, as well as in [The Price of Neutrality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Price_of_Neutrality "The Price of Neutrality"). 

### Source

  * [Plants of Barren Lands](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Plants_of_Barren_Lands "Plants of Barren Lands")
  * [The Druid's Herbarium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Druid%27s_Herbarium "The Druid's Herbarium")
  * School of the Wolf cave in Kaer Morhen
  * An "Old peasant woman" around the village in [Murky Waters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Murky_Waters "Murky Waters") _(the one wearing a hat like the[Clerks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clerks "Clerks")' and the female Innkeeper's)_ will mention Dandelion wanted her to tell Geralt about local herbs. She will give entries for Ginatia, [Honeysuckle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeysuckle "Honeysuckle"), and [Berbercane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berbercane_fruit "Berbercane fruit"). She may not appear in the village until the [Daily Bread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Daily_Bread "Daily Bread") quest is complete.

## Item ID

Ginatia petals

**Ginatia petals** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Ancient leshen decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_leshen_decoction "Ancient leshen decoction")
  * [Blue mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_mutagen "Blue mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Greater blue mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_blue_mutagen "Greater blue mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Enhanced Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Devil%27s_Puffball "Enhanced Devil's Puffball")
  * [Superior Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Devil%27s_Puffball "Superior Devil's Puffball")
  * [Enhanced Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dimeritium_bomb "Enhanced Dimeritium bomb")
  * [Enhanced hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_hybrid_oil "Enhanced hybrid oil")
  * [Superior hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_hybrid_oil "Superior hybrid oil")
  * [Noonwraith decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith_decoction "Noonwraith decoction")

  * [Enhanced ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_ogroid_oil "Enhanced ogroid oil")
  * [Pink armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pink_armor_dye "Pink armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
