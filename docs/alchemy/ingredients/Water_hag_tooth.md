## Item ID

Water Hag teeth

**Water hag tooth** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster tooth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_tooth "Monster tooth") and [venom extract](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Venom_extract "Venom extract"). It is needed to craft [enhanced relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_relict_oil "Enhanced relict oil"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from all bilge and [water hags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag").
