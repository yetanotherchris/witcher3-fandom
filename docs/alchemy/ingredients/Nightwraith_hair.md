## Item ID

Nightwraiths hair

**Nightwraith hair** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster hair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hair "Monster hair"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from all [nightwraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith"). In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be found as regular random loot in the [Witch of Lynx Crag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_of_Lynx_Crag "Witch of Lynx Crag")'s hut during [A Knight's Tales](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Knight%27s_Tales "A Knight's Tales").
