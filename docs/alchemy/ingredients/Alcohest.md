## Item ID

Alcohest

**Alcohest** is considered an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and thus can't be consumed like regular drinks. It is the default alcohol used to replenish [potions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") and [bombs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs") during [meditation](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meditation "Meditation"), and is needed to needed to brew all enhanced potions: 

  * [Enhanced Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Black_Blood "Enhanced Black Blood")
  * [Enhanced Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Blizzard "Enhanced Blizzard")
  * [Enhanced Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Cat "Enhanced Cat")
  * [Enhanced Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Full_Moon "Enhanced Full Moon")
  * [Enhanced Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Golden_Oriole "Enhanced Golden Oriole")
  * [Enhanced Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Maribor_Forest "Enhanced Maribor Forest")
  * [Enhanced Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Petri%27s_Philter "Enhanced Petri's Philter")
  * [Enhanced Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Swallow "Enhanced Swallow")
  * [Enhanced Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Tawny_Owl "Enhanced Tawny Owl")
  * [Enhanced Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Thunderbolt "Enhanced Thunderbolt")
  * [Enhanced White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_White_Honey "Enhanced White Honey")
  * [Enhanced White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "Enhanced White Raffard's Decoction")
