## Item ID

Ekimma epidermis

**Ekimmara hide** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hide "Monster hide"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_cursed_oil "Enhanced cursed oil")
  * [Superior cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_cursed_oil "Superior cursed oil")

It is a guaranteed drop from all [ekimmaras](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekimma "Ekimma"), of which however there are only 3 in the entire game, all appearing during quests: 

  * In the mill during [Following the Thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Following_the_Thread_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Following the Thread \(The Witcher 3\)"), the loot from which is easily missable
  * In [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto") during [Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear")
  * [Sarasti](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarasti "Sarasti") during [Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Mystery_of_the_Byways_Murders "Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders")
