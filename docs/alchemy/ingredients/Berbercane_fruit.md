**Berbercane** is plentiful throughout most of the game. According to an old woman in the [Outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts "Outskirts"), the fruit assuages the insane. 

### Source

  * [Field Plants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Field_Plants "Field Plants")
  * This is one of the possible entries acquired from the [Old Women](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/People_of_the_Outskirts "People of the Outskirts") in the [Outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts "Outskirts") in exchange for food during Chapter I.
  * An "Old peasant woman" around the village in [Murky Waters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Murky_Waters "Murky Waters") _(the one wearing a hat like the[Clerks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clerks "Clerks")' and the female Innkeeper's)_ will mention Dandelion wanted her to tell Geralt about local herbs. She will give entries for [Ginatia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ginatia_petals "Ginatia petals"), [Honeysuckle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeysuckle "Honeysuckle"), and Berbercane. She may not appear in the village until the [Daily Bread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Daily_Bread "Daily Bread") quest is complete.

## Item ID

Berbercane fruit

**Berbercane fruit** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(potion\) "Cat \(potion\)")
  * [Enhanced Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Cat "Enhanced Cat")
  * [Superior Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Cat "Superior Cat")
  * [Ekhidna decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna_decoction "Ekhidna decoction")
  * [Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maribor_Forest_\(potion\) "Maribor Forest \(potion\)")
  * [Enhanced Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Maribor_Forest "Enhanced Maribor Forest")
  * [Superior Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Maribor_Forest "Superior Maribor Forest")
  * [Red armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Red_armor_dye "Red armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Swallow "Superior Swallow")
  * [Water hag decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag_decoction "Water hag decoction")

It can be found as both random, guaranteed during quests in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") keep, gathered from plants, received from [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira") if she is talked to about [Claer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claer "Claer") after completing [Contract: Devil by the Well](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Devil_by_the_Well "Contract: Devil by the Well"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist")

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(White_Orchard_sawmill\) "Merchant \(White Orchard sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(White_Orchard\) "Sawmill \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard")
  * Generic [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
