## Item ID

Greater mutagen blue

**Greater blue mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is used to augment [abilities](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities") and are more effective than their [lesser](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_blue_mutagen "Lesser blue mutagen") and [regular](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_mutagen "Blue mutagen") counterparts. When placed in the diamond-shaped slot in the character panel, they give +10% to [sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") intensity, which increases by 10 up to a max of +40% if all 3 skills attached to it are blue. 

Like all greater [mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be crafted outside of the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 

## _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion

With _Blood and Wine_ , the player can now craft these with other ingredients that don't include blue mutagens. One can also be found as non-monster loot during the quest [Turn and Face the Strange](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Turn_and_Face_the_Strange "Turn and Face the Strange").

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
