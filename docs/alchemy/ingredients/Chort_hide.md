## Item ID

Czart hide

**Chort hide** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hide "Monster hide") and [monster hair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hair "Monster hair"). 

It is a random drop from all [chorts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort "Chort").
