# Nigredo

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Nigredo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/1/16/Substances_Small_Nigredo.png/revision/latest?cb=20170711142829)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/1/16/Substances_Small_Nigredo.png/revision/latest?cb=20170711142829 "Nigredo")Nigredo

**Nigredo** is an additional, or secondary substance, which is present in some alchemical ingredients in addition to the primary substance. All alchemical [ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_ingredients "The Witcher ingredients") contain one of six basic substances: [aether](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aether "Aether"), [hydragenum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hydragenum "Hydragenum"), [quebrith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quebrith "Quebrith"), [rebis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rebis "Rebis"), [vermilion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vermilion "Vermilion"), and [vitriol](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitriol "Vitriol"). There are also three additional substances: [albedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albedo "Albedo"), nigredo, and [rubedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rubedo "Rubedo"). Any given ingredient may contain only a single basic substance and potentially one additional, or secondary substance.

## Item ID

Nigredo

Unlike previous games, **Nigredo** is not simply part of other [alchemical ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Black armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_armor_dye "Black armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dancing_Star "Superior Dancing Star")
  * [Superior Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dimeritium_bomb "Superior Dimeritium bomb")
  * [Superior Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Grapeshot "Superior Grapeshot")
  * [Superior Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Moon_Dust "Superior Moon Dust")
  * [Superior relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_relict_oil "Superior relict oil")
  * [Ursine silver sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_silver_sword_-_enhanced "Ursine silver sword - enhanced")
  * [Legendary Ursine silver sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_silver_sword_-_enhanced "Legendary Ursine silver sword - enhanced")

It is a guaranteed drop from most regular [katakans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan"), but that only amounts to two of them (during [A Tome Entombed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Tome_Entombed "A Tome Entombed") and at [Kaer Gelen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Gelen "Kaer Gelen")) in the base game, so for the most part it has to be crafted. In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be purchased from [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Perfumery\) "Merchant \(Perfumery\)") at the [Perfumery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perfumery "Perfumery")
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Chuchote_Cave\) "Wine Merchant \(Chuchote Cave\)") at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave"), after completing his quest
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Duchaton_Crest\) "Wine Merchant \(Duchaton Crest\)") at [Dun Tynne Hillside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Hillside "Dun Tynne Hillside"), after completing his quest

### Notes

  * It is possible to loot all 5 doses necessary for bombs and superior relict oil, but one has to wait all the way to [The Night of Long Fangs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Night_of_Long_Fangs "The Night of Long Fangs"), when a few more katakans will spawn around [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair"). However, as that is one of the last main quests in the entire game, this isn't a viable strategy for a standard single playthrough.

## Trivia

  * In alchemy, the _nigredo_ means "putrefaction" or "decomposition" of the matter, and is the first step [Magnum opus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magnum_opus_\(alchemy\) "wikipedia:Magnum opus \(alchemy\)") process.
