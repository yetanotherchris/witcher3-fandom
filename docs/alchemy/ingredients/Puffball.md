## Item ID

Pigskin puffball

**Puffball** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Aether](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aether "Aether")
  * [Enhanced beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_beast_oil "Enhanced beast oil")
  * [Superior beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_beast_oil "Superior beast oil")
  * [Brown armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brown_armor_dye "Brown armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Chort decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort_decoction "Chort decoction")
  * [Enhanced cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_cursed_oil "Enhanced cursed oil")
  * [Superior Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dimeritium_bomb "Superior Dimeritium bomb")
  * [Drowner pheromones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner_pheromones "Drowner pheromones")
  * [Elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa_oil "Elementa oil")
  * [Enhanced elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_elementa_oil "Enhanced elementa oil")
  * [Enhanced insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_insectoid_oil "Enhanced insectoid oil")
  * [Superior insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_insectoid_oil "Superior insectoid oil")
  * [Quebrith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quebrith "Quebrith")
  * [Rubedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rubedo "Rubedo")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") and the ones in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
