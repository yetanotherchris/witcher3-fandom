# Sewant mushrooms

**Sewant mushrooms** are a fungi that grow in sewers and caves. The [Ofieri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofir "Ofir") people believe that these mushrooms are [cursed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Curse "Curse").[1]

## Item ID

Sewant mushrooms

**Sewant mushrooms** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Blood "Black Blood")
  * [Enhanced Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Black_Blood "Enhanced Black Blood")
  * [Superior Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Black_Blood "Superior Black Blood")
  * [Superior Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Blizzard "Superior Blizzard")
  * [Enhanced Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dancing_Star "Enhanced Dancing Star")
  * [Superior Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dancing_Star "Superior Dancing Star")
  * [Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Puffball "Devil's Puffball")
  * [Enhanced Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Devil%27s_Puffball "Enhanced Devil's Puffball")
  * [Superior Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Devil%27s_Puffball "Superior Devil's Puffball")
  * [Drowner pheromones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner_pheromones "Drowner pheromones")
  * [Gray armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gray_armor_dye "Gray armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Nightwraith decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith_decoction "Nightwraith decoction")
  * [Shaelmaar bait](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shaelmaar_bait "Shaelmaar bait") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Tawny_Owl "Superior Tawny Owl")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, received as reward for completing [The Path of Warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Path_of_Warriors "The Path of Warriors"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar") and the ones in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
