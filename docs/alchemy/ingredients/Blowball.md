## Item ID

Blowbill

**Blowball** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Archgriffin decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin_decoction "Archgriffin decoction")
  * [Basilisk decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk_decoction "Basilisk decoction")
  * [Enhanced Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dimeritium_bomb "Enhanced Dimeritium bomb")
  * [Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golden_Oriole "Golden Oriole")
  * [Enhanced Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Golden_Oriole "Enhanced Golden Oriole")
  * [Superior Golden Oriole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Golden_Oriole "Superior Golden Oriole")
  * [Foglet decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foglet_decoction "Foglet decoction")
  * [Enhanced Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Grapeshot "Enhanced Grapeshot")
  * [Enhanced Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Moon_Dust "Enhanced Moon Dust")
  * [Superior Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Moon_Dust "Superior Moon Dust")
  * [Necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage_oil "Necrophage oil")
  * [Enhanced necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_necrophage_oil "Enhanced necrophage oil")

  * [Red mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Red_mutagen "Red mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
