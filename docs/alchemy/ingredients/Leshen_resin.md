## Item ID

Leshy resin

**Leshen resin** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [resin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Resin "Resin") and [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from all [leshens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshy "Leshy") and [spriggans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spriggan "Spriggan").
