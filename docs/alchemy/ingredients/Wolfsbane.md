# Wolfsbane

**Wolfsbane** , also known as the "queen of poisons" and "monk's hood",[1] is used in many [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") potions and [alchemic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemy "Alchemy") brews.

## Item ID

Wolfsbane

**Wolfsbane** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Cleansing mixture](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cleansing_mixture "Cleansing mixture") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cursed_oil "Cursed oil")
  * [Enhanced cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_cursed_oil "Enhanced cursed oil")
  * [Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Full_Moon "Full Moon")
  * [Enhanced Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Full_Moon "Enhanced Full Moon")
  * [Superior Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Full_Moon "Superior Full Moon")
  * [Purple armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Purple_armor_dye "Purple armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Enhanced Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Tawny_Owl "Enhanced Tawny Owl")
  * [Superior Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Tawny_Owl "Superior Tawny Owl")
  * [Enhanced vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_vampire_oil "Enhanced vampire oil")
  * [Superior vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_vampire_oil "Superior vampire oil")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except the ones in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
