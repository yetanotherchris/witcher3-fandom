## Item ID

Fogling teeth

**Foglet teeth** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster teeth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_tooth "Monster tooth"), and are needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Samum "Enhanced Samum")
  * [Superior Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Samum "Superior Samum")

They are a guaranteed drop from [foglets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fogler "Fogler").
