## Item ID

Saltpetre

**Saltpeter** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dancing_Star "Dancing Star")
  * [Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Puffball "Devil's Puffball")
  * [Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dimeritium_bomb "Dimeritium bomb")
  * [Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon%27s_Dream_\(bomb\) "Dragon's Dream \(bomb\)")
  * [Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grapeshot "Grapeshot")
  * [Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moon_Dust "Moon Dust")
  * [Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Wind "Northern Wind")
  * [Superior Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Northern_Wind "Superior Northern Wind")
  * [Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Samum "Samum")
  * [Zerrikanian powder](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_powder "Zerrikanian powder")

It can be found as both random and guaranteed loot, received as part of quest rewards, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") except the [Freya Priestess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya_Priestess "Freya Priestess")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")
  * All herbalists in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Cunny_of_the_Goose\) "Herbalist \(Cunny of the Goose\)") outside the [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") inn
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Putrid_Grove\) "Merchant \(Putrid Grove\)") in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove")
  * [Otto Bamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber") at the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut") northeast of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Barrengarth\) "Herbalist \(Barrengarth\)") in [Barrengarth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barrengarth "Barrengarth") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Master Topical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philibert_Topical "Philibert Topical") in his [hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Topical%27s_hut "Master Topical's hut") northwest of [Vikk Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vikk_Watchtower "Vikk Watchtower") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") in her [home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri%27s_Hermitage "Pinastri's Hermitage") northeast of [Dun Tynne Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Crossroads "Dun Tynne Crossroads") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

2 × saltpeter is always found in a big chest at the [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)") in White Orchard, 4-5 more in the big chest near the [grindstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grindstone "Grindstone") at the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison") (soldiers will consider is stealing though), and 10 in the room with the [gargoyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gargoyle "Gargoyle") during [Wandering in the Dark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_in_the_Dark "Wandering in the Dark"). 4 can be received from [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira") if she is talked to about [Claer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claer "Claer") after completing [Contract: Devil by the Well](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Devil_by_the_Well "Contract: Devil by the Well"), and 5 for completing [Practicum in Advanced Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Practicum_in_Advanced_Alchemy "Practicum in Advanced Alchemy").
