## Item ID

Water essence

**Water essence** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster essence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_essence "Monster essence"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(potion\) "Cat \(potion\)")
  * [Enhanced Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Cat "Enhanced Cat")

It can occasionally be found as regular random loot, but is primarily a random drop from [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"), [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren"), [ekhidnae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna "Ekhidna"), bilge and [water hags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag"), [archespores](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archespore "Archespore"), and archespore [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"). 

Water essence can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):
