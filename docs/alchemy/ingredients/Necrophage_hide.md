## Item ID

Necrophage skin

**Necrophage hide** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into a [monster hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hide "Monster hide") and [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). 

It is a random drop from the [botchling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Botchling "Botchling"), [devourers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devourer "Devourer"), [rotfiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend "Rotfiend") and their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and [scurvers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scurver "Scurver").
