## Item ID

Nekker heart

**Nekker heart** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that can be dismantled into [monster heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_heart "Monster heart") and [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "White Raffard's Decoction")
  * [Enhanced White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "Enhanced White Raffard's Decoction")

It is a random drop from [nekkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker"), and guaranteed from their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") and [nekker warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior "Nekker warrior").
