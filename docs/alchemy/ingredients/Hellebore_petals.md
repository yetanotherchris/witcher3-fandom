# Hellebore petals

**Hellebore** is a [plant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Plants_in_the_novels "Plants in the novels") which, when mixed with belladonna, hemlock, [sulfur](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sulfur "Sulfur"), pitch and tacks, make a poisonous concoction.[1]

## Item ID

Hellebore petals

**Hellebore petals** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Black_Blood "Enhanced Black Blood")
  * [Superior Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Black_Blood "Superior Black Blood")
  * [Enhanced Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dancing_Star "Enhanced Dancing Star")
  * [Fiend decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend_decoction "Fiend decoction")
  * [Greater red mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_red_mutagen "Greater red mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Maribor_Forest "Superior Maribor Forest")
  * [Nigredo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nigredo "Nigredo")
  * [Pops' mold antidote](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pops%27_mold_antidote "Pops' mold antidote")
  * [Purple armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Purple_armor_dye "Purple armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Samum "Superior Samum")
  * [Superior White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_White_Honey "Superior White Honey")
  * [Vitriol](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitriol "Vitriol")
