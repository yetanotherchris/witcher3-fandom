## Item ID

Bear fat

**Bear fat** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft many [blade oils](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_oils "The Witcher 3 oils"): 

  * [Enhanced beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_beast_oil "Enhanced beast oil")
  * [Enhanced cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_cursed_oil "Enhanced cursed oil")
  * [Enhanced draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_draconid_oil "Enhanced draconid oil")
  * [Enhanced elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_elementa_oil "Enhanced elementa oil")
  * [Enhanced Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Enhanced Hanged Man's Venom")
  * [Enhanced hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_hybrid_oil "Enhanced hybrid oil")
  * [Enhanced insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_insectoid_oil "Enhanced insectoid oil")
  * [Enhanced necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_necrophage_oil "Enhanced necrophage oil")
  * [Ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ogroid_oil "Ogroid oil")
  * [Enhanced ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_ogroid_oil "Enhanced ogroid oil")
  * [Enhanced relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_relict_oil "Enhanced relict oil")
  * [Specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Specter_oil "Specter oil")
  * [Enhanced specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_specter_oil "Enhanced specter oil")
  * [Enhanced vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_vampire_oil "Enhanced vampire oil")

It is a random drop from all [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") and [berserkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werebear "Werebear"), though it also appears as regular random loot. It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")
  * All herbalists in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Cunny_of_the_Goose\) "Herbalist \(Cunny of the Goose\)") outside the [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") inn
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
