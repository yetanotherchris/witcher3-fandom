## Item ID

Vampire saliva

**Vampire saliva** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster saliva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_saliva "Monster saliva"). 

It is dropped by all [vampires](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire"), at random from most, but guaranteed from [alps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alp "Alp"), [garkains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Garkain "Garkain"), and [fleders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fleder "Fleder"). It can also be dismantled from [vampire fangs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire_fang "Vampire fang").
