## Item ID

Crystalized essence

**Crystalized essence** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster essence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_essence "Monster essence"). 

It can be found as both regular random loot and from [nekker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Alchemist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist_\(Crematory\) "Alchemist \(Crematory\)") in the [crematory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crematory "Crematory") near [Oxenfurt Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Gate "Oxenfurt Gate")
  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Lindenvale\) "Blacksmith \(Lindenvale\)") in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale")
  * [Freya Priestess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya_Priestess "Freya Priestess") at the [temple to Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_of_Freyja "Temple of Freyja") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") in the roadside camp south of [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Kaer_Trolde_Harbor\) "Merchant \(Kaer Trolde Harbor\)") in [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Drummond_sawmill\) "Merchant \(Drummond sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(Drummond\) "Sawmill \(Drummond\)") south of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on Hindarsfjall
  * [Wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Yoana](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yoana "Yoana") in [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch")

It is also guaranteed on a few occasions during quests: 

  * Awarded for slaying the [hym](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hym "Hym") during [Possession](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Possession_\(quest\) "Possession \(quest\)")
  * Dropped by at least one of the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones") during [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(quest\) "Bald Mountain \(quest\)")
  * Loot from skulls attached to rock walls in the poisoned valley during [Through Time and Space](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Through_Time_and_Space "Through Time and Space")
