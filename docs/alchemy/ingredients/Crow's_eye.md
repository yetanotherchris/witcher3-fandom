## Item ID

Crows eye

**Crow's eye** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Albedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")
  * [Black armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_armor_dye "Black armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Chort lure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort_lure "Chort lure")
  * [Cockatrice decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice_decoction "Cockatrice decoction")
  * [Enhanced Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Full_Moon "Enhanced Full Moon")
  * [Superior Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Full_Moon "Superior Full Moon")
  * [Enhanced Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Grapeshot "Enhanced Grapeshot")
  * [Enhanced Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Maribor_Forest "Enhanced Maribor Forest")
  * [Superior Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Maribor_Forest "Superior Maribor Forest")
  * [Nigredo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nigredo "Nigredo")
  * [Rubedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rubedo "Rubedo")
  * [Superior Swallow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Swallow "Superior Swallow")
  * [Troll decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll_decoction "Troll decoction")

It can be found as random loot, guaranteed loot during quests in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") keep, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except the ones in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Silverton\) "Merchant \(Silverton\)") in a shop in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
