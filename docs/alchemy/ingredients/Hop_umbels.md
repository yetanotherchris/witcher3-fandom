# Hop umbels

**Hop umbels** are flowers whose pollen emits a strong aura against [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic").[1]

## Item ID

Hop umbels

**Hop umbels** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Grapeshot "Superior Grapeshot")
  * [Enhanced Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Moon_Dust "Enhanced Moon Dust")
  * [Superior Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Moon_Dust "Superior Moon Dust")
  * [Enhanced relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_relict_oil "Enhanced relict oil")
  * [Superior relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_relict_oil "Superior relict oil")
  * [Werewolf decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf_decoction "Werewolf decoction")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except the ones in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
