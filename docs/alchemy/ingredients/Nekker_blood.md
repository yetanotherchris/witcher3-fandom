## Item ID

Nekker blood

**Nekker blood** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed for anything but can be dismantled into [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). 

It is a random drop from [nekkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker"), their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and [nekker warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior "Nekker warrior"). In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be found as regular random loot in the [Witch of Lynx Crag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_of_Lynx_Crag "Witch of Lynx Crag")'s hut during [A Knight's Tales](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Knight%27s_Tales "A Knight's Tales").
