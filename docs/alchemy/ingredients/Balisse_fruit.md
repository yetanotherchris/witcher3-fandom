**Balisse fruit** is an edible berry characterized by subtle [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") resonance. The fruit is apparently poisonous unless soaked in alcohol first, after which it can be used for medicines. 

### Source

  * [Field Plants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Field_Plants "Field Plants")
  * This is one of the possible entries acquired from the [Old Women](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/People_of_the_Outskirts "People of the Outskirts") in the [Outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts "Outskirts") in exchange for food during Chapter I.
  * Pick as many as you need in Chapter I, as this fruit becomes rather scarce in Chapters II and III.

### _[The Price of Neutrality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Price_of_Neutrality "The Price of Neutrality")_ premium module

  * In the module, this is known (and labelled) as _Balissa_.

## Item ID

Balisse fruit

**Balisse fruit** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Alcohest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alcohest "Alcohest")
  * [Enhanced cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_cursed_oil "Enhanced cursed oil")
  * [Earth elemental decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_elemental_decoction "Earth elemental decoction")
  * [Killer Whale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Killer_Whale "Killer Whale")
  * [Nigredo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nigredo "Nigredo")
  * [Superior White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_White_Honey "Superior White Honey")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Shrine\) "Herbalist \(Shrine\)") at the [abandoned](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") roadside shrine in White Orchard
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), if she is recruited for the [battle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(White_Orchard_sawmill\) "Merchant \(White Orchard sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(White_Orchard\) "Sawmill \(White Orchard\)") in White Orchard
  * [Wandering merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(White_Orchard\) "Wandering merchant \(White Orchard\)") during the [epilogue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)") in White Orchard
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

Balisse fruit should actually be offered by way more merchants, including but not limited to every single herbalist, but is artificially restricted from doing so on the [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")/[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") maps. The limitation applies whenever more than 3 of it are supposed to be present, and the likely intended logic is that any larger stack would just be reduced to that, but instead gets outright removed. This is also the reason why Keira only sells balisse fruit in Kaer Morhen and not while she's in Velen. 

More merchants can also theoretically offer it by virtue of "randomly" selecting amounts lower than 3, but due to a separate randomization bug, that never seems to happen either: 

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith"), after [Practicum in Advanced Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Practicum_in_Advanced_Alchemy "Practicum in Advanced Alchemy")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kaer_Trolde\) "Herbalist \(Kaer Trolde\)") in [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kvalheim_Isle\) "Herbalist \(Kvalheim Isle\)") in [Kvalheim Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kvalheim_Isle "Kvalheim Isle") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in Novigrad's [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market")
  * [Otto Bamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber") at the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut") northeast of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
  * [Wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Pontar\) "Wandering merchant \(Pontar\)") around Velen and Novigrad, plus many generic merchants who use the same store
  * [Master Topical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philibert_Topical "Philibert Topical") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)

Balisse fruit can be found: 

  * Across the street from the merchant next to the fast travel sign in White Orchard, in a meadow with a cow.
  * On the slopes of the hills below the lighthouse of Eldberg.

### Notes

  * The described limitation is part of a wider concept to induce scarcity of some goods in the game, which was never fully developed, and unfortunately wasn't cleaned-up either. The mechanic actually applies to everything that can be considered a stack of a single item, not just merchant stores. It is sheer luck that balisse fruit almost never appears in quantities larger than 3 elsewhere, and the only other "item" affected are [orens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren") in than 100.
