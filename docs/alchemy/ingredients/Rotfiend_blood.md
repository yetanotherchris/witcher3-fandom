## Item ID

Rotfiend blood

**Rotfiend blood** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Cleansing mixture](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cleansing_mixture "Cleansing mixture") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Enhanced necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_necrophage_oil "Enhanced necrophage oil")

It is a guaranteed drop from [rotfiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend "Rotfiend"), their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") and [devourers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devourer "Devourer") who guard them, and [scurvers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scurver "Scurver") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. It can also be purchased from [Otto Bamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber") at the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut") northeast of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt").
