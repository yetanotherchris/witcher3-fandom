## Item ID

Ribleaf

**Ribleaf** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Archgriffin decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin_decoction "Archgriffin decoction")
  * [The Decoctions of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Decoctions_of_the_Grasses "The Decoctions of the Grasses")
  * [Ekhidna decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna_decoction "Ekhidna decoction")
  * [Green armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Green_armor_dye "Green armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Maribor Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Maribor_Forest "Superior Maribor Forest")
  * [Enhanced ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_ogroid_oil "Enhanced ogroid oil")
  * [Superior ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_ogroid_oil "Superior ogroid oil")
  * [Enhanced vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_vampire_oil "Enhanced vampire oil")
  * [White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "White Raffard's Decoction")
  * [Enhanced White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "Enhanced White Raffard's Decoction")
  * [Superior White Raffard's Decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_White_Raffard%27s_Decoction "Superior White Raffard's Decoction")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, randomly dropped by bilge and [water hags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")

Some is also always present in the quest containers during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests"), [Va Fail, Elaine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Va_Fail,_Elaine "Va Fail, Elaine").
