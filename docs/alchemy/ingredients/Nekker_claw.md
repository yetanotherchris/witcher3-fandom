## Item ID

Nekker claw

**Nekker claw** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster claw](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_claw "Monster claw"). 

It is a random drop from [nekkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker"), their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and [nekker warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior "Nekker warrior").
