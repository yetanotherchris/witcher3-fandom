These are harvested from dead [basilisks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk"). The skin of these lizards is all the rage in the more fashion conscious circles of [Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir"). The experience points for the basilisk kill is worth it alone. This is sadly not the case in _[The Price of Neutrality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Price_of_Neutrality "The Price of Neutrality")_ premium module. Although they are skinnable items, these hides have no alchemical value whatsoever.

Basilisk plate

**Basilisk hide** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into a [monster carapace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_carapace "Monster carapace") (the base game one) and [chitinous shell](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chitinous_shell "Chitinous shell"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from all [basilisks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk").
