## Item ID

Mutagen blue

**Blue mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to craft [greater blue mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_blue_mutagen "Greater blue mutagen"). Like all mid tier [regular mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they do not drop from creatures outside of _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. They can also be used to augment [abilities](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities"), but are not as effective as their greater counterparts. When placed in the diamond-shaped slot in the character panel, they give +7% to [sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") intensity, which increases by 7 up to a max of +28% if all 3 skills attached to it are blue. 

## _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion

With _Blood and Wine_ , the player can now craft these with other ingredients that don't include lesser blue mutagens.

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
