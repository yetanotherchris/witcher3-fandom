## Item ID

Lunar shards

**Lunar shards** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_armor "Enhanced Feline armor")
  * [Enhanced legendary Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_legendary_Feline_armor "Enhanced legendary Feline armor")

It can be found as regular random loot, randomly dropped by all [nightwraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith") and [beann'shies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Banshee "Banshee"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") except the [Eternal Fire Priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") outside of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Putrid_Grove\) "Merchant \(Putrid Grove\)") in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove")
  * [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") in her [home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri%27s_Hermitage "Pinastri's Hermitage") northeast of [Dun Tynne Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Crossroads "Dun Tynne Crossroads") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

Lunar shards can also be obtained by dismantling [Zireael armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zireael_armor "Zireael armor"), or either of the [Feline](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") armors that are crafted with it.
