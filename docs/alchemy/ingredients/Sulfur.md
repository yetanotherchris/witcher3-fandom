## Item ID

Sulfur

**Sulfur** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dancing_Star "Dancing Star")
  * [Enhanced Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dancing_Star "Enhanced Dancing Star")
  * [Superior Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dancing_Star "Superior Dancing Star")
  * [Superior Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Grapeshot "Superior Grapeshot")
  * [Greater red mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_red_mutagen "Greater red mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Enhanced Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Moon_Dust "Enhanced Moon Dust")
  * [Superior Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Moon_Dust "Superior Moon Dust")
  * [Yellow armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yellow_armor_dye "Yellow armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Zerrikanian powder](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_powder "Zerrikanian powder")

It can be found as regular random loot, random drops from [hounds of the Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt "Hound of the Wild Hunt") and [sandcrabs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sandcrab "Sandcrab"), received as reward for completing the secondary quest [Practicum in Advanced Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Practicum_in_Advanced_Alchemy "Practicum in Advanced Alchemy"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") except the [Eternal Fire Priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") outside of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
