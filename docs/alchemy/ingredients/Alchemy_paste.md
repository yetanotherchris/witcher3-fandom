## Item ID

Alchemical paste

**Alchemy paste** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Master's weapon repair kit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master%27s_weapon_repair_kit "Master's weapon repair kit")
  * [Superior beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_beast_oil "Superior beast oil")
  * [Superior cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_cursed_oil "Superior cursed oil")
  * [Superior draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_draconid_oil "Superior draconid oil")
  * [Superior elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_elementa_oil "Superior elementa oil")
  * [Superior Hanged Man's Venom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Hanged_Man%27s_Venom "Superior Hanged Man's Venom")
  * [Superior hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_hybrid_oil "Superior hybrid oil")
  * [Superior insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_insectoid_oil "Superior insectoid oil")
  * [Superior necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_necrophage_oil "Superior necrophage oil")
  * [Superior ogroid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_ogroid_oil "Superior ogroid oil")
  * [Superior relict oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_relict_oil "Superior relict oil")
  * [Superior specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_specter_oil "Superior specter oil")
  * [Superior vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_vampire_oil "Superior vampire oil")

It can be found as random loot or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") outside of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Lindenvale\) "Merchant \(Lindenvale\)") in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Putrid_Grove\) "Merchant \(Putrid Grove\)") in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove")
  * [Pinastri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri "Pinastri") in her [home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pinastri%27s_Hermitage "Pinastri's Hermitage") northeast of [Dun Tynne Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Crossroads "Dun Tynne Crossroads") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
