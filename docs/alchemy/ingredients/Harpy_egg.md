## Item ID

Harpy egg

**Harpy eggs** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster egg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_egg "Monster egg") and [monster feather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_feather "Monster feather"). 

It is a random drop from [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") and guaranteed from their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest").
