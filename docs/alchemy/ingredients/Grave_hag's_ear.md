## Item ID

Grave Hag ear

**Grave hag's ear** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything and cannot be dismantled, and thus can only be sold for profit. 

It is only dropped by [grave hags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grave_hag "Grave hag"), one of the [crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"), and the [Wicked Witch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wicked_Witch "Wicked Witch"). 

## Notes

  * Grave hag's ear was originally meant to be dismantled into [monster ears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_ear "Monster ear"), but the relevant part of its item definition is commented-out.
