## Item ID

Arachas venom

**Arachas venom** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [venom extract](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Venom_extract "Venom extract"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tawny_Owl "Tawny Owl")
  * [Enhanced Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Tawny_Owl "Enhanced Tawny Owl")

It can only be obtained as random loot from [arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas "Arachas"), and guaranteed from their [venomous](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Venomous_arachas "Venomous arachas") variety. It is never dropped by the [armored](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armored_arachas "Armored arachas") kind. 

## Notes

  * Only 1 dose is really needed as the Tawny Owl potion is part of the starting equipment. The only way to brew the basic potion is by first finding or buying the [recipe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manuscript_page:_Tawny_Owl "Manuscript page: Tawny Owl") and then discarding the potion.
  * There is a respawning level 11 arachas west-northwest of [Toderas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toderas "Toderas") (closer to [Mowshurst](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mowshurst "Mowshurst")) that is easy to farm.
