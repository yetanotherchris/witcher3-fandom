## Item ID

Gryphon feathers

**Griffin feathers** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster feathers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_feather "Monster feather") and [powdered monster tissue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Powdered_monster_tissue "Powdered monster tissue"). 

They're a random drop fom all [griffins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") and [archgriffins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin "Archgriffin").
