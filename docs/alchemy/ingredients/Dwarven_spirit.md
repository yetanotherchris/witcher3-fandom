## Item ID

Dwarven spirit

**Dwarven spirit** is considered an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and thus can't be consumed like regular drinks. It can be used to replenish [potions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") and [bombs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs") during [meditation](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meditation "Meditation") if no [alcohest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alcohest "Alcohest") is available, and is needed to craft all [mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") decoctions, base-level potions, and a couple of [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") ones.
