## Item ID

Cyclops eye

**Cyclops' eye** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into a [monster eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_eye "Monster eye") and [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). 

It is a random (but very high chance) drop from [cyclopses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops"), and the [Cloud Giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cloud_Giant "Cloud Giant") during [Beyond Hill and Dale...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beyond_Hill_and_Dale... "Beyond Hill and Dale...")
