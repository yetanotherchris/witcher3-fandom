**Honeysuckle** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_ingredients "The Witcher ingredients") which grows wild throughout the game. 

### Source

  * [Plants of Barren Lands](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Plants_of_Barren_Lands "Plants of Barren Lands")
  * After completing the [Daily Bread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Daily_Bread "Daily Bread") quest, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can give any meat food to the [elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") in and around the cave. The elf who stays outside of the cave even at night will give entries for honeysuckle and [bryonia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bryonia "Bryonia") instead.
  * An "old peasant woman" around the village in [Murky Waters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Murky_Waters "Murky Waters") _(the one wearing a hat like the[Clerks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clerks "Clerks")' and the female Innkeeper's)_ will mention [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") wanted her to tell Geralt about local herbs. She will give entries for [ginatia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ginatia_petals "Ginatia petals"), honeysuckle, and [berbercane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berbercane_fruit "Berbercane fruit"). She may not appear in the village until the Daily Bread quest is completed.

### Notes

  * A random paramedic in [Old Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Vizima "Old Vizima") asks for honeysuckle or [hop umbels](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hop_umbels "Hop umbels") in [Chapter V,](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_storyline "The Witcher storyline") in return she gives the journal entries for [Ifrits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ifrit "Ifrit") and [Pyrite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pyrite "Pyrite").

## Item ID

Honeysuckle

**Honeysuckle** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Ancient leshen decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_leshen_decoction "Ancient leshen decoction")

  * [Enhanced elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_elementa_oil "Enhanced elementa oil")
  * [Superior elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_elementa_oil "Superior elementa oil")
  * [Enhanced insectoid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_insectoid_oil "Enhanced insectoid oil")
  * [Enhanced Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Moon_Dust "Enhanced Moon Dust")
  * [Pink armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pink_armor_dye "Pink armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
