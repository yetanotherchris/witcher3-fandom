## Item ID

Buckthorn

**Buckthorn** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Alghoul decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul_decoction "Alghoul decoction")
  * [Brown armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brown_armor_dye "Brown armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Enhanced elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_elementa_oil "Enhanced elementa oil")
  * [Killer Whale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Killer_Whale "Killer Whale")
  * [Superior Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Blizzard "Superior Blizzard")
  * [Enhanced Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Petri%27s_Philter "Enhanced Petri's Philter")
  * [Superior Petri's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Petri%27s_Philter "Superior Petri's Philter")

It can only be found underwater, on ocean floors, lakebeds and riverbeds.
