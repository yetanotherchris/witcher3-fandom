**Calcium equum** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_ingredients "The Witcher ingredients") that the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_merchants "The Witcher merchants") will buy: 

  * [Kalkstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kalkstein "Kalkstein") in the [Nonhuman district](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman_district "Nonhuman district")

### [Journal Entry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_journal "The Witcher journal") source

  * [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert") (during the [A Potion for Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Potion_for_Triss "A Potion for Triss") quest in the [prologue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_storyline "The Witcher storyline"))
  * [A Small Book of Minerals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Small_Book_of_Minerals "A Small Book of Minerals")
  * [The Great Book of Minerals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Book_of_Minerals "The Great Book of Minerals")

### Notes

  * This is one of the first ingredients [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") learns about when he arrives at [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") in the prologue. It is found in a chest in the evening hall on the second floor.
  * It is necessary for the brewing of [A Potion for Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Potion_for_Triss "A Potion for Triss").
  * It is commonly referred to as 'horse lime'.
  * As long as you don't have calcium equum in your inventory, the chest's supply can be endless. Just continue to open and close the chest without removing the contents: with each iteration the total amount of calcium equum in chest will have increased by one. Only remove the contents when you have reached the desired amount.

## Item ID

Calcium equum

**Calcium equum** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft several [bombs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs"): 

  * [Enhanced Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Devil%27s_Puffball "Enhanced Devil's Puffball")
  * [Superior Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Devil%27s_Puffball "Superior Devil's Puffball")
  * [Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grapeshot "Grapeshot")
  * [Enhanced Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Grapeshot "Enhanced Grapeshot")
  * [Superior Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Grapeshot "Superior Grapeshot")

It can be found as regular random loot, randomly dropped by one of the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones") and the [Wicked Witch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wicked_Witch "Wicked Witch"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") except the [Eternal Fire Priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")
  * All herbalists in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Cunny_of_the_Goose\) "Herbalist \(Cunny of the Goose\)") outside the [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") inn
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
