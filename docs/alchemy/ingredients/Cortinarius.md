## Item ID

Cortinarius

**Cortinarius** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Cat "Enhanced Cat")
  * [Superior Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Cat "Superior Cat")
  * [Chort decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort_decoction "Chort decoction")
  * [Grave hag decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grave_hag_decoction "Grave hag decoction")
  * [Enhanced necrophage oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_necrophage_oil "Enhanced necrophage oil")
  * [Red armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Red_armor_dye "Red armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thunderbolt "Thunderbolt")
  * [Enhanced Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Thunderbolt "Enhanced Thunderbolt")
  * [Superior Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Thunderbolt "Superior Thunderbolt")
  * [Enhanced vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_vampire_oil "Enhanced vampire oil")
  * [Superior vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_vampire_oil "Superior vampire oil")

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, received from [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira") if she is talked to about [Claer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claer "Claer") after completing [Contract: Devil by the Well](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Devil_by_the_Well "Contract: Devil by the Well"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):
