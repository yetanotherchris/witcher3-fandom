## Item ID

Alghoul claw

**Alghoul claw** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster claw](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_claw "Monster claw"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul"), except those who guard [ghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest").
