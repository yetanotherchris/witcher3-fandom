## Item ID

mh107_fiend_dung

**Fiend dung** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , that is required make [chort lure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort_lure "Chort lure") during the quest [Contract: Mysterious Tracks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Mysterious_Tracks "Contract: Mysterious Tracks"). 

As such, it is a guaranteed loot item during that quest. However, it is also a random drop from all [fiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend").
