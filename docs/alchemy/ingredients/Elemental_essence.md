## Item ID

Elemental essence

**Elemental essence** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed for anything but can be dismantled into [monster essence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_essence "Monster essence"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from the [ice troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_troll "Ice troll") in [Clan Tordarroch Forge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tordarroch_Forge "Clan Tordarroch Forge"), [ice giants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant") during [Tedd Deireadh, The Final Age](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tedd_Deireadh,_The_Final_Age "Tedd Deireadh, The Final Age"), and random drop from most [elementa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa "Elementa") [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"): 

  * [Earth Elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_Elemental "Earth Elemental")
  * [Fire Elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Elemental "Fire Elemental")
  * [Gargoyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gargoyle "Gargoyle")
  * [Ice Elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_Elemental "Ice Elemental")
  * [Therazane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Therazane "Therazane")
  * [Golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem") at [Olevald Clearing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olevald_Clearing "Olevald Clearing") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Moreau's Golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moreau%27s_Golem "Moreau's Golem") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
