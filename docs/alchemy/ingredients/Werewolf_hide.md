## Item ID

Werewolf pelt

**Werewolf hide** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into a [monster hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hide "Monster hide") and [monster hair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hair "Monster hair"). 

It is a random drop from [werewolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") and [ulfhedinn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulfhedinn "Ulfhedinn").
