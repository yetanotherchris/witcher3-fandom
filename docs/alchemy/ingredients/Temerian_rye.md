## Item ID

Temerian Rye

**Temerian rye** is considered an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , and thus can't be consumed like regular drinks. It is not needed to craft anything, but can be used to replenish [potions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") and [bombs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs") during [meditation](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meditation "Meditation"). 

It can be found as random loot, guaranteed in the reward chest for [Temerian Valuables](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Valuables "Temerian Valuables"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Claywich\) "Merchant \(Claywich\)") in [Claywich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claywich "Claywich")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Downwarren\) "Merchant \(Downwarren\)") in [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren")
  * [Innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(Cunny_of_the_Goose\) "Innkeep \(Cunny of the Goose\)") in the [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose")
  * [Innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(The_Golden_Sturgeon\) "Innkeep \(The Golden Sturgeon\)") in [The Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon")
  * [Innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(The_Kingfisher\) "Innkeep \(The Kingfisher\)") ([Olivier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olivier "Olivier")'s replacement) in [The Kingfisher Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn")
  * [Innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(The_Nowhere_Inn\) "Innkeep \(The Nowhere Inn\)") in [The Nowhere Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nowhere_Inn "The Nowhere Inn")
  * [Innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(Seven_Cats_Inn\) "Innkeep \(Seven Cats Inn\)") in the [Seven Cats Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Seven_Cats_Inn "Seven Cats Inn")
  * [Stjepan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stjepan "Stjepan") in [The Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Alchemy "The Alchemy") in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")
  * [Innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(Svorlag\) "Innkeep \(Svorlag\)") in [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag") (cheapest)
