## Item ID

Moleyarrow

**Moleyarrow** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_draconid_oil "Enhanced draconid oil")
  * [Enhanced elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_elementa_oil "Enhanced elementa oil")
  * [Forktail decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail_decoction "Forktail decoction")
  * [Leshen decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshen_decoction "Leshen decoction")
  * [Orange armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orange_armor_dye "Orange armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Cat "Superior Cat")
  * [Wyvern decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern_decoction "Wyvern decoction")

## Notes

  * It was meant to also be used in [nekker pheromones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_pheromones "Nekker pheromones"), but the potion was never implemented into the game.
  * Moleyarrow can be found on the bottom of the sea bed in the [Novigrad Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks"). This is strange, seeing as moleyarrow does not appear to be an aqueous plant. Whether this was intentional or a bug is unknown.
