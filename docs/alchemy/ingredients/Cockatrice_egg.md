## Item ID

Cockatrice egg

**Cockatrice egg** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into a [monster egg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_egg "Monster egg") and [chitinous shell](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chitinous_shell "Chitinous shell"). 

It is a guaranteed drop from all [cockatrices](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice "Cockatrice").
