**Celandine** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_ingredients "The Witcher ingredients") which can be obtained from [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert"), during the [A Potion for Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Potion_for_Triss "A Potion for Triss") quest in the [Prologue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_storyline#Prologue_\(Kaer_Morhen\) "The Witcher storyline").

### Notes

  * [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") gives [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") one portion of celandine to make a [Potion for Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Potion_for_Triss "Potion for Triss") during the Prologue.
  * Five leaves of celandine are needed for [Shani](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shani "Shani") during the quest [Old Friend of Mine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Friend_of_Mine "Old Friend of Mine") in [Chapter II](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_storyline#Chapter_II_\(Temple_Quarter_of_Vizima\) "The Witcher storyline").

**Celandine** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items:

  * [Green mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Green_mutagen "Green mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

## Notes

  * Celandine is known in the books as "swallow's herb", or "herba zireael" in [Elder Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Speech "Elder Speech"). This is also the meaning in Czech and Polish.
  * In _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , during the secondary quest [Brave Fools Die Young](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brave_Fools_Die_Young "Brave Fools Die Young"), Geralt can give celandine to a man who has broken his leg, claiming it will dull the pain.
