## Item ID

Mutagen green

**Green mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is used to craft [greater green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_green_mutagen "Greater green mutagen"). Like all mid tier [regular mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they do not drop from creatures outside of _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. They can also be used to augment [abilities](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities"), but are not as effective as their greater counterparts. When placed in the diamond-shaped slot in the character panel, they give +100 to vitality, which increases by 100 up to a max of +400 if all 3 skills attached to it are green. 

## _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion

With _Blood and Wine_ , the player can now craft these with other ingredients that don't include lesser green mutagens.

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
