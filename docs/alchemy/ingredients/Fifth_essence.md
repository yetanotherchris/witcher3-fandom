## Item ID

Fifth essence

**Fifth Essence** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_vampire_oil "Enhanced vampire oil")
  * [Superior vampire oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_vampire_oil "Superior vampire oil")
  * [Mastercrafted Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Griffin_armor "Mastercrafted Griffin armor")
  * [Mastercrafted legendary Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_legendary_Griffin_armor "Mastercrafted legendary Griffin armor")
  * [Griffin silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_silver_sword "Griffin silver sword")
  * [Legendary Griffin silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Griffin_silver_sword "Legendary Griffin silver sword")
  * [Mastercrafted Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Wolven_armor "Mastercrafted Wolven armor")
  * [Mastercrafted legendary Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_legendary_Wolven_armor "Mastercrafted legendary Wolven armor")
  * [Wolven silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_silver_sword "Wolven silver sword")
  * [Legendary Wolven silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Wolven_silver_sword "Legendary Wolven silver sword")
  * [Mastercrafted forgotten Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_forgotten_Wolven_armor "Mastercrafted forgotten Wolven armor")
  * [Mastercrafted legendary forgotten Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_legendary_forgotten_Wolven_armor "Mastercrafted legendary forgotten Wolven armor")

It can be found as regular random loot, randomly dropped by [Earth Elementals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_Elemental "Earth Elemental"), [fiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend"), [foglets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fogler "Fogler"), and [Moreau's Golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moreau%27s_Golem "Moreau's Golem"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") outside of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * All base game [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer"), except [Fergus Graem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fergus_Graem "Fergus Graem") and those in [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik")
  * All base game [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") and the rescued one in [Fayrlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fayrlund "Fayrlund")
  * Armors and weapons [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")

  * The fifth essence, or element, rounds out the puzzle of the [classical elements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Classical_element "wikipedia:Classical element").
  * The word "quintessential" is derived from the Latin phrase for "fifth essence".
