# Albedo

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Albedo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7f/Substances_Small_Albedo.png/revision/latest?cb=20170711143847)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7f/Substances_Small_Albedo.png/revision/latest?cb=20170711143847 "Albedo")Albedo

**Albedo** is an additional, or secondary substance which is present in some alchemical ingredients in addition to the primary substance. All alchemical [ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_ingredients "The Witcher ingredients") contain one of six basic substances: [aether](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aether "Aether"), [hydragenum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hydragenum "Hydragenum"), [quebrith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quebrith "Quebrith"), [rebis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rebis "Rebis"), [vermilion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vermilion "Vermilion"), and [vitriol](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitriol "Vitriol"). There are also three additional substances: albedo, [nigredo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nigredo "Nigredo"), and [rubedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rubedo "Rubedo"). Any given ingredient may contain only a single basic substance and potentially one additional, or secondary substance. 

[![potions](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/76/Journal_potion.png/revision/latest?cb=20170723033558)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/76/Journal_potion.png/revision/latest?cb=20170723033558 "potions")Albedo [potions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_potions "The Witcher potions"): If all ingredients used to create a given potion contain albedo as an additional substance, the created potion and any subsequently imbibed potions for 1 hour have reduced [toxicity](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_character_statistics "The Witcher character statistics") by 1 scale. Meaning if the potion has High toxicity it is reduced to Medium toxicity. Potions with Low toxicity like [Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(potion\) "Cat \(potion\)") and [Wives' Tears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wives%27_Tears "Wives' Tears") become Non-toxic just like [White Honey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Honey "White Honey").

## Item ID

Albedo

Unlike previous games, **Albedo** is not simply part of other [alchemical ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_draconid_oil "Superior draconid oil")
  * [Superior elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_elementa_oil "Superior elementa oil")
  * [Superior hybrid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_hybrid_oil "Superior hybrid oil")
  * [Ursine silver sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_silver_sword_-_superior "Ursine silver sword - superior")
  * [Legendary Ursine silver sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_silver_sword_-_superior "Legendary Ursine silver sword - superior")
  * [White armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_armor_dye "White armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

In the base game it must be crafted, but in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion it can also be purchased from [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Perfumery\) "Merchant \(Perfumery\)") at the [Perfumery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perfumery "Perfumery")
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Chuchote_Cave\) "Wine Merchant \(Chuchote Cave\)") at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave"), after completing his quest
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Duchaton_Crest\) "Wine Merchant \(Duchaton Crest\)") at [Dun Tynne Hillside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Hillside "Dun Tynne Hillside"), after completing his quest

## Trivia

  * In alchemy, the _albedo_ means "purification" of the matter, and is the second step [Magnum opus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magnum_opus_\(alchemy\) "wikipedia:Magnum opus \(alchemy\)") process.
