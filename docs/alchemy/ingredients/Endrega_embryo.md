## Item ID

Endriag embryo

**Endrega embryo** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that can be dismantled into [monster egg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_egg "Monster egg") and [monster essence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_essence "Monster essence"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thunderbolt "Thunderbolt")
  * [Enhanced Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Thunderbolt "Enhanced Thunderbolt")

It is a random drop from [endrega workers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_worker "Endrega worker"), and guaranteed from their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") and [endrega warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_warrior "Endrega warrior") who guard them.
