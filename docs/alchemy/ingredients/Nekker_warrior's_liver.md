## Item ID

Nekker warrior liver

**Nekker warrior's liver** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster liver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_liver "Monster liver") and [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Enhanced draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_draconid_oil "Enhanced draconid oil")
  * [Superior draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_draconid_oil "Superior draconid oil")

It is a guaranteed drop from all [nekker warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior "Nekker warrior"), and can be purchased from [Otto Bamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber") at the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut") north of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt").
