## Item ID

Mahakam Spirit

**Mahakaman spirit** is considered an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and thus can't be consumed like regular drinks. It's needed to craft [dwarven spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarven_spirit "Dwarven spirit").

## Notes

  * It is the cheapest alcohol that can be used to replenish [potions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") and [bombs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs"), thus in the unlikely event of running out of strong alcohol, it is the most cost efficient to buy this one.
  * Innkeeps in Skellige sell alcohol at the cheapest prices, but there's one exception in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion: Hugo Monnart who buys and sells everything at base item values. Prices listed in the infobox don't include him.
