## Item ID

Stammelfords dust

**Stammelford's Dust** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Black armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_armor_dye "Black armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Enhanced Dancing Star](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dancing_Star "Enhanced Dancing Star")
  * [Enhanced Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Devil%27s_Puffball "Enhanced Devil's Puffball")
  * [Enhanced Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dimeritium_bomb "Enhanced Dimeritium bomb")
  * [Enhanced Dragon's Dream](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dragon%27s_Dream "Enhanced Dragon's Dream")
  * [Enhanced Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Grapeshot "Enhanced Grapeshot")
  * [Enhanced Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Moon_Dust "Enhanced Moon Dust")
  * [Enhanced Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Northern_Wind "Enhanced Northern Wind")
  * [Enhanced Samum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Samum "Enhanced Samum")

It can be found as random loot or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist")
  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")
  * All herbalists in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") except [Yolar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yolar "Yolar")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Cunny_of_the_Goose\) "Herbalist \(Cunny of the Goose\)") outside the [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") inn
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Gildorf\) "Merchant \(Gildorf\)") in the [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shop_\(Gildorf\) "Shop \(Gildorf\)") in [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Putrid_Grove\) "Merchant \(Putrid Grove\)") in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove")
  * [Otto Bamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber") at the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut") northeast of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")
  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Barrengarth\) "Herbalist \(Barrengarth\)") in [Barrengarth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barrengarth "Barrengarth") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
