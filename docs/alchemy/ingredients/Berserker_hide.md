## Item ID

Berserker pelt

**Berserker hide** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [monster hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hide "Monster hide") and [monster saliva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_saliva "Monster saliva"). 

It is a random drop from [berserkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werebear "Werebear"), of which there are only few encountered during [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"), making the ingredient very rare.
