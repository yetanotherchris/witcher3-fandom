## Item ID

Wraith essence

**Essence of wraith** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster essence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_essence "Monster essence"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior elementa oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_elementa_oil "Superior elementa oil")
  * [Reliever's decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reliever%27s_decoction "Reliever's decoction")
  * [Enhanced specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_specter_oil "Enhanced specter oil")
  * [Superior specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_specter_oil "Superior specter oil")

It is a guaranteed drop from the [penitent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Penitent "Penitent") and regular [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"), and random drop from all [noonwraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith "Noonwraith"), [nightwraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith"), and [beann'shies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Banshee "Banshee").
