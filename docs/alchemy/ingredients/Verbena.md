## Item ID

Verbena

**Verbena** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Aether](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aether "Aether")
  * [Hydragenum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hydragenum "Hydragenum")
  * [Katakan decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan_decoction "Katakan decoction")
  * [Superior Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Full_Moon "Superior Full Moon")
  * [Enhanced Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Northern_Wind "Enhanced Northern Wind")
  * [Superior Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Northern_Wind "Superior Northern Wind")
  * [Quebrith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quebrith "Quebrith")
  * [Rebis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rebis "Rebis")
  * [Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tawny_Owl "Tawny Owl")
  * [Enhanced Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Tawny_Owl "Enhanced Tawny Owl")
  * [Superior Tawny Owl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Tawny_Owl "Superior Tawny Owl")
  * [Superior Thunderbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Thunderbolt "Superior Thunderbolt")
  * [Vermilion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vermilion "Vermilion")
  * [White armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_armor_dye "White armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can be found as random loot, gathered from plants, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") except the ones in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel")
  * [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist") at [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith")
  * [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") in the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")
