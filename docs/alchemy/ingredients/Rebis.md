## Item ID

Rebis

Unlike previous games, **Rebis** is not simply part of other [alchemical ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Black_Blood "Superior Black Blood")
  * [Superior Blizzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Blizzard "Superior Blizzard")
  * [Superior Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Devil%27s_Puffball "Superior Devil's Puffball")
  * [Green armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Green_armor_dye "Green armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Lesser green mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_green_mutagen "Lesser green mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Green mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Green_mutagen "Green mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Greater green mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_green_mutagen "Greater green mutagen") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior specter oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_specter_oil "Superior specter oil")
  * [Ursine silver sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Ursine silver sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Legendary Ursine silver sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Legendary Ursine silver sword - mastercrafted")

In the base game it must be crafted, but in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion one dose can be found during [Scavenger Hunt: Grandmaster Wolven Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Grandmaster_Wolven_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Grandmaster Wolven Gear"), and it can also be purchased from [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Perfumery\) "Merchant \(Perfumery\)") at the [Perfumery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perfumery "Perfumery")
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Chuchote_Cave\) "Wine Merchant \(Chuchote Cave\)") at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave"), after completing his quest
  * [Wine Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wine_Merchant_\(Duchaton_Crest\) "Wine Merchant \(Duchaton Crest\)") at [Dun Tynne Hillside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dun_Tynne_Hillside "Dun Tynne Hillside"), after completing his quest
