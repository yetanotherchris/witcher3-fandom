## Item ID

Cockatrice maw

**Cockatrice stomach** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster saliva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_saliva "Monster saliva") and [monster stomach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_stomach "Monster stomach"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_beast_oil "Superior beast oil")
  * [Enhanced draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_draconid_oil "Enhanced draconid oil")
  * [Superior draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_draconid_oil "Superior draconid oil")

It is a guaranteed drop from [cockatrices](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice "Cockatrice"), of which there are four in the game, one encountered during a [contract quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_contracts "The Witcher 3 contracts") and three respawning in the wild: 

  * [Shrieker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shrieker "Shrieker") during [Contract: Shrieker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Shrieker "Contract: Shrieker")
  * Southwest of [Yantra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yantra "Yantra"), between the lake and the seacoast
  * North of [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn"), on the first larger island leading to [Eldberg Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eldberg_Lighthouse "Eldberg Lighthouse")
  * Northeast of [Fornhala](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fornhala "Fornhala"), in the swamp

As such, it is a naturally rare ingredient, but all three required can be obtained in a single playthrough. 

## Notes

  * Prior to [Patch 1.20](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.20_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.20 \(The Witcher 3\)") (released just before the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion), cockatrice stomach could be dismantled into 1-2 × [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood") and 1 × [monster saliva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_saliva "Monster saliva") instead.
