# Beggartick blossoms

The petals of **beggartick blossoms** , a common field plant with big red flowers, are used in the production of [fisstech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fisstech "Fisstech"). The blossoms of this swamp plant are poisonous and hallucinogenic, but in small doses it can sooth pain.[1]

## Item ID

Beggartick blossoms

  * [Albedo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albedo "Albedo")

  * [Red armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Red_armor_dye "Red armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

  * [Werewolf decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf_decoction "Werewolf decoction")
