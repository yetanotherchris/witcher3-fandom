## Item ID

Ghoul blood

**Ghoul's blood** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Blood "Black Blood")
  * [Enhanced Black Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Black_Blood "Enhanced Black Blood")

It is a guaranteed drop from [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul"), their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul") that guard them. In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also occasionally be found as random loot.
