**Letter to Tamara** is a letter in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , found in [Tamara Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger")'s room, usually during the quest [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters"). 

The letter hints at the mistreatment Tamara and her [mother](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anna_Strenger "Anna Strenger") suffered at the hands of their [father and husband](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") respectively. It is one three clues [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") finds in Tamara's room that help tell the story of their disappearance.
