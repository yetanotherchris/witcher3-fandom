## Item ID

mq1060_second_letter

**Second letter from Reinald** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It's found near [Reinald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald "Reinald")'s box of ingredients for making his own potion, [Reinald's Philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald%27s_Philter "Reinald's Philter"), during the secondary quest [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow"). 

## Journal entry

    _Dear O._
    _The whoresons! They locked me in! First they ask for aid, then, without hesitation, pen me in like a rabid mutt at the first sign of trouble. They wouldn't even hear my report, thought me a[specter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Specter "Specter") trying to deceive them. Fuck that [cult](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") and their "common good" horseshit. How can I help the common good if I can't get the herbs I need? Speaking of, the [sewant mushrooms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sewant_mushrooms "Sewant mushrooms") won't do the trick. I already checked. Turns out the worst-case scenario is true. I'm not sick after all - I'm possessed._
    _The specter has fully fused into my body. Not even[Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") has an effect. If only I had the right ingredients, I could brew my philter, intensify my [signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") and expel the filth with a combination of [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") and [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard"). But as I said, the cocksuckers won't fucking let me!_
    _Guess you'll be reading these letters after all... I'll be waiting deep in the caverns. Make the potion but DO NOT COME NEAR ME UNTIL YOU DRINK IT. And don't substitute any ingredients or the[red miasmal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Red_Miasmal "Red Miasmal") will possess you too._
    _Strange... "miasmal"? What is that? How do I know it?_
    _[Dwarven spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarven_spirit "Dwarven spirit")  
[Celandine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Celandine "Celandine") (not sewant mushrooms!)  
Contents of the chest_
    _Reinald_
