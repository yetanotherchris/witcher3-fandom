## Item ID

mq1050_notice

**Careful! There's a Swindler About!** is a [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Oxenfurt\) "Notice board \(Oxenfurt\)") and, when read, begins the secondary quest [The Truth is in the Stars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Truth_is_in_the_Stars "The Truth is in the Stars"). 

## Journal entry

    _Attention, countrymen!_
    _Don't get taken in by the platitudes and false prophecies dealt in by a certain so-called '[soothsayer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Sage "Old Sage")' dwelling in [Benkelham](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Benek "Benek")! This man calls himself a [Sage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sage "Sage"), but knows as much about divining the future as any toothless village hag, and all he truly cares about is extracting coin from the unwary! Don't let him cheat you, it'll only encourage him!_
    _-Felippe Cannalia, fifth-year student in the[Faculty of Medicine and Herbology](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy_faculties "Oxenfurt Academy faculties") at [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")_
