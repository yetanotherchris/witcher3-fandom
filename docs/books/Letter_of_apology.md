## Item ID

th1009_sorry_note

**Letter of apology** is an item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , added by [Patch 1.12.1](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.12.1_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.12.1 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It is found on a skeleton at the [old signal tower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_signal_tower "Old signal tower") along with a sword, [Pang of Conscience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pang_of_Conscience "Pang of Conscience"). 

## Journal entry

    _[illegible fragment] ...we all make mistakes, for to err is human. The key is to learn from one's errors. If something breaks, it must be fixed. And if our mistake harms another, the injured party must be recompensed for his frazzled nerves and lost time._
    _We thus apologize and ask for forgiveness. Evil is not what we do!  
C. de P.R._

## Notes

  * The letter is an Easter egg that serves as an apology from [CD PROJEKT RED](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/CD_PROJEKT_RED "CD PROJEKT RED") for a notorious bug which prevents players from obtaining the diagrams for the base version of the [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear"), most notably on the console ports.
