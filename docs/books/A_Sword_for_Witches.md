**A Sword for Witches** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that represents a sort of a manifesto for the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter"). 

It can be found as random loot, usually on bookshelves, throughout the entire game. Due to a randomization but, a copy of it always appears in a small crate inside the house at [Freya's garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindar "Hindar"), in the same room where [Morkvarg's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg%27s_journal "Morkvarg's journal") is during [In Wolf's Clothing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_Wolf%27s_Clothing "In Wolf's Clothing"). 

## Journal entry

    _Who are the hunters, you ask? Folk like you and yours, I reply. The decent kind, haters of lies, doers of good. The kind who live according to the gods' laws, and nature's laws, too. Those disgusted by the machinations of[witches](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch "Witch"), [magickers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") and [nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman"). All that separates us from common folk is that we've the courage to take up arms, to defend our lands from evil, to slice out the gangrene that eats us from within._
    _We haven't a leader. We haven't forts or land. Though gods-fearing[Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") supports us with his gold at times, we've not sworn him nor any other ruler any oath. We serve only the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire"), and we listen only to our own conscience._
    _Who can join us? Any who is right of soul and sound of body. You can find us in every larger city in the[North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/North "North"). We will give you board, lodging and a weapon. We will explain how to spot the tell-tale signs of evil - birthmarks in strange and arcane shapes, smooth skin on a matron aged more than thirty springs, and black [cats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(creature\) "Cat \(creature\)") kept in the yard, to name but a few. We will show you how to defend yourself from witchcraft, how to tame and snuff out [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") elements with [dimeritium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dimeritium "Dimeritium"). We will instruct you how to squeeze the sinners' darkest secrets out of them with a hot iron, and how to grant them cleansing death with the help of sacred fire._

## Trivia

  * The book title is likely a reference to [Malleus Maleficarum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malleus_Maleficarum), also known as _Hammer of Witches_ and in one instance alternately translated as _Hammer for Sorceresses_ \- a real life anti-witch treatise.
