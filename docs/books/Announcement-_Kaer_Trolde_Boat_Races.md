**Announcement: Kaer Trolde Boat Races** is an unseen notice in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can only be obtained via the console. The notice was meant to promote boat racing, which was cut from the final release of the game.
