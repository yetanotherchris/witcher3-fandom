## Item ID

lw_bm6_merchant_note

**Crumpled note** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is supposed to be found along with a treasure containing [diagram: Tracker's gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Tracker%27s_gauntlets "Diagram: Tracker's gauntlets") somewhere on [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)"). However, the container that has it doesn't appear during gameplay. As its contents refer to a non-existent [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") nearby, as well as [rats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rat "Rat") being the most dangerous thing, it was likely related to the plague questline that was cut from the base game. 

## Journal entry

    _Who gets rich off war? That's easy - mercenaries. Those whoresons fatten themselves on the misfortune of others. And do they ever think to share these spoils? Or even buy a round at the inn? Or toss a fellow a[crown](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")? Naye, sir, why, of course not! A [cow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cow "Cow")'d milk itself before that would happen! Or a [pig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pig "Pig") would cut off a slice of its own hocks and slap it on the griddle._
    _But as for me, I'm not the type to wait around for alms. I slipped into their camp yesternight, took whatever caught my eye and split. They'll never get me here. First of all, because I wiped all my tracks. Second, word is a griffin's hereabouts somewhere, so they'll most like be too scared to venture close. Of course, in truth there's no griffin nor any other beast more dangerous than a rat in these parts. It's all just the sort of rubbish old hags babble about to waste time while drawing[water](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water "Water")._
