**Letter from Frann** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the first floor of the [Rosemary and Thyme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme"). 

## Journal entry

    _When will you return to[Cidaris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cidaris_\(city\) "Cidaris \(city\)")? I hope it will be soon – for I miss you terribly. Waiting to see you again literally pains me. If you are to come, send me a message beforehand. I'll ask my mistress for a day off._
