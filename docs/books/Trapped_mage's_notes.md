## Item ID

**Trapped mage's notes** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found in one of the prison cells at [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")'s [Deireadh prison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deireadh_prison "Deireadh prison") during the main quest [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape"). 

## Journal entry

    _Dear Lizzy!_
    _I will be leaving here soon. I bought off a guard with a few potions and the promise of a reward once I am secure in my freedom. Prepare yourself, we will have to flee - pack only what is absolutely necessary. Take bandages and ointment - they have not treated me too kindly. But do not fear, another day or two and we will be together. The guard swore on his mother's grave that he will get me out of here. Wait for me._
    _Your Bernard_
