## Item ID

Draconides vol 1

**A Few Remarks on Basilisks and Cockatrices** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When first read, it adds [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries for [basilisks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk") and [cockatrices](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice "Cockatrice"), as well as the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Basilisk_decoction "Torn-out page: Basilisk decoction") for [basilisk decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk_decoction "Basilisk decoction") and the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Cockatrice_decoction "Torn-out page: Cockatrice decoction") for [cockatrice decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice_decoction "Cockatrice decoction"). 

It can be purchased from [Marcus T.K. Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson") or the [book merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Merchant \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"), and should also be available as random loot, but due to a randomization bug never appears as such. 

## Journal entry

    _Why've you got such big eyes, Grandmum? asked the little girl with worry. "Shriek! Shriek!" replied the basilisk. "Grandmum, why do you say 'Shriek! Shriek!'?" asked the little girl with surprise. The basilisk didn't reply, but instead clamped its toothy, venomous maw over the curious little girl and swallowed her up._
    _Luckily, a[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") by the name of [Eskel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eskel "Eskel") was wandering these lands in search of a [zeugl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zeugl "Zeugl"). The witcher was experienced and so knew basilisks only turn people to stone in fairy tales. He also knew it is best to avoid their venom._
    _Eskel had noticed the[monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") had recently eaten, and before anyone could say, "Ready, steady, go!" he threw himself at the beast and cut it open with his [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword"). The basilisk's stomach opened up and the witcher saw the half-digested foolish little girl inside._
