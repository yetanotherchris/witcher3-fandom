## Item ID

Ogres vol 2

**An Ancient Ballad** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that, when read, provides [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries for [cyclopses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops") and [ice giants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant"). 

It should be available as random loot, but due to a randomization bug doesn't appear anywhere during gameplay and can only be obtained through console commands. 

## Journal entry

    _They say 'neath the arms, of the[giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giant "Giant") of ice_
    _grew man-child and maid together_
    _And foot with foot, did the wise one fashion_
    _A son that six head bore._
    _Eighth anwer me well, if wise thou art called,_
    _If thou knowest it, Vafthruthnir, now:_
    _What farthest back, dost thou bear in mind?_
    _For wide is thy wisdom, giant!_
    _Winters unmeasured, ere earth was made_
    _Was the birth of Bergelmir_
    _This first knew I well, when the giant wise_
    _In a boat of old was borne._
    _Ninth answer me well, if wise thou art called_
    _If thou knowest it, Vafthruthnir, now:_
    _Whence comes the wind, that fares o'er the waves_
    _Yet never itself is seen?_
    _In an eagle's guise, at the end of heaven_
    _[Hraesvelg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hr%C3%A4svelg "Hräsvelg") sits, they say_
    _And from his wings, does the wind comes forth_
    _To move o'er the world of men._
