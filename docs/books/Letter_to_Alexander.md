## Item ID

q104_aleksander_letter

**Letter to Alexander** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted off a bed in [Keira Metz's hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz%27s_hut "Keira Metz's hut") near [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse").
