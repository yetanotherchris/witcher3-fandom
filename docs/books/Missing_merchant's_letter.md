**Missing merchant's letter** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and can be looted from a body near the cart containing the [Package for Keira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Package_for_Keira "Package for Keira"). 

## Journal entry

    _Volker,_
    _We've hit gold, old friend. Not only have we sold all of our stock – we have sold it for three times its cost! Our customer wants it all – wine from[Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair") (everything from Beaujolais Nouveau to the oldest vintages, white, red, rosé... you name it), catoblepas meat, pickled beef, pepper, cinnamon, cloves... I'm telling you, old friend, everything!_
    _There's one catch, however: we must deliver the goods to[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), to a village called [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). I know, I know – dangerous, what with the war and all, but think of the coin! I've managed to secure a pass that will get you across the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)"). Then it's just a hop, skip and a jump and you're there. You'll be fine!_
    _Klaus  
P.S. We'll split the proceeds 50/50._
