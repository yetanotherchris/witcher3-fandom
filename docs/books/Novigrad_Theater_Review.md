**Novigrad Theater Review** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Its contents are still present in the game files, but aren't attached to an [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") item definition. A related cut content book also exists in the game files as "[History of the Theater of Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/History_of_the_Theater_of_Novigrad "History of the Theater of Novigrad")". 

## Journal entry

    _In his latest work, entitled The Mentor, Cornelius presents the theater-going public with the story of a certain Dorant, a godless ruffian and defiler of virginal innocence who with all manner of tricks and despicable deceptions seduces one Clarissa, watched over by the great guardian of youth Gerontius, who in the end convinces Dorant of the error of his ways and forces him to marry the disgraced Clarissa._
