## Item ID

q301_drawing_oven

**Drawing of an oven** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the main quest [Novigrad Dreaming](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Dreaming "Novigrad Dreaming"), the [godling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Godling "Godling") [Sarah](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarah "Sarah") will pin this to the attic door, after you discover the [rag doll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rag_doll "Rag doll") in the crib. Finding this second clue directs [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to the basement, to find the depicted oven.
