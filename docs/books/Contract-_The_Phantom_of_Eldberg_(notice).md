## Item ID

mh207_contract

**Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg** is a [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Arinbjorn\) "Notice board \(Arinbjorn\)") and, when read, begins [Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Phantom_of_Eldberg "Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg"). 

## Journal entry

    _If you got this far, clearly you know how to read, so read the rest, and read it well._
    _A[wraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith")'s sprung up on [Eldberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eldberg_Lighthouse "Eldberg Lighthouse") and covered the whole isle in fog and put out the light in our lighthouse. If you're afraid to face a ghost in battle, stay far from Eldberg, for there you'll fund neither gold nor glory, only death. But if you've faced such phantoms before, then know that I, [Jorund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jorund "Jorund") of Arinbjorn, will pay my own [gold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") to you if you drive off the spirits from our clan's territories._
