## Item ID

gp_prologue_bandit_note02

**Inexpertly written letter** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It was supposed to be found in the [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") northwest of [Cackler Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cackler_Bridge "Cackler Bridge") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), in the treasure chest for [Dirty Funds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dirty_Funds "Dirty Funds") or on top of it. Some data in the game files suggests that it should appear there if the camp is empty, which however is never the case as the [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") will occupy the camp after it is cleared of deserters. 

## Journal entry

    _You gotta give up this madness, Morgen. Mum's beside herself, saying that's not becoming of a[Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria"), stealing from innocent folk. Bad enough you deserting the [army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Army "Temerian Army") like that, but to take to robbing... And how're you even sure you aren't hurting our own by accident? What happens if you and your mates make a mistake? You ever think about that? No, you didn't, ever ever since you were a lad you always acted first, thought later - or never. But you ain't a lad no more, so put an end to this reckless adventure and head home._

## Notes

  * The contents of this letter actually appear in the journal entry for the [Dirty Funds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dirty_Funds "Dirty Funds") quite often, though under unclear conditions.
