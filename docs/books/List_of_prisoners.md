**List of prisoners** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the [prison warden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prison_Warden "Prison Warden")'s desk during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests"), [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape"). 

Many of the names on the list are references to various moderators on CD PROJEKT RED's forums. 

## Journal entry

    _Prisoners of[Deireadh](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deireadh_prison "Deireadh prison")  
Page 258_
    _Blaix de Villmon  
Imprisoned the 3rd of [Birke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Birke "Birke") for use of [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic").  
Admitted no guilt despite exhaustive interrogation.  
Died during questioning on the 7th of [Blathe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blathe "Blathe")._
    _[Arthur de Vleester](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arthur_de_Vleester "Arthur de Vleester")  
Imprisoned the 5th of Birke for use of magic.  
Admitted his guilt the 10th of Birke.  
Sentenced to death by pyre.  
Burned at the stake the 11th of Birke._
    _[Margarita Laux-Antille](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margarita_Laux-Antille "Margarita Laux-Antille")  
Imprisoned the 6th of Birke for use of magic.  
Has admitted no guilt.  
Recommendation: enhanced interrogation._
    _Keth_[1]_  
Imprisoned the 6th of Birke for illicit soothsaying.  
Has admitted no guilt.  
Sentenced to death by pyre._
    _Undomiel_[2]_  
[Elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") witch of the forests, imprisoned the 6th of Birke.  
Sentenced to death by pyre for cursing the residents of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt").  
Has admitted no guilt._
    _"Zelona"_[3]_  
Sorceress, true name unknown.  
Imprisoned the 7th of Birke for use of magic and aiding other fugitives.  
Sentenced to death by pyre._
    _Shipek_[4]_  
Imprisoned the 7th of Birke for printing and distributing pamphlets slandering the[Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") nation.  
Admitted his guilt.  
Sentenced to death by hanging._
    _Vatt_[5]_  
[Dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf"), imprisoned the 7th of Birke for tearing down wanted posters of witches.  
Admitted his guilt.  
Sentenced to lose his right hand._
    _Gregski_[6]_  
Imprisoned the 8th of Birke for harboring fugitives.  
Admitted his guilt.  
Sentenced to death by hanging._
    _"Hunt"_[7]_  
True name unknown. Imprisoned the 8th of Birke for pestering elegant dames.  
Sentenced to one month in a dark cell._
    _Dona_[8]_  
Imprisoned the 8th of Birke for chiromancy.  
Has admitted no guilt.  
Recommendation: enhanced interrogation._
    _"Dragonbird"_[9]_  
True name unknown. Imprisoned the 9th of Birke.  
Accused of practicing numerology, admitted her guilt.  
Sentenced to death by pyre._
    _Second Child_[10]_  
Elven diviner, crazed, imprisoned the 9th of Birke for inciting discord and hatred for the[Church](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire").  
Appears comatose, uncommunicative - possible ruse.  
Recommendation: outlast, wait for response._
    _Corylea_[11]_  
Imprisoned the 10th of Birke for allegiance to[Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael").  
Admitted her guilt using crude language.  
Sentenced to death by hanging._
    _"Highland Tommy"_[12]_  
Refuses to reveal true name. Imprisoned the 11th of Birke for assault sword in hand.  
Admitted his guilt.  
Sentenced to death by decapitation._
    _"The Seeker"_[13]_  
True name unknown. Imprisoned the 11th of Birke on suspicion of[spying](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spy "Spy").  
Recommendation: enhanced interrogation using hot irons.  
Imperative: learn identity of prisoner's operator._
    _Man of N'vah_[14]_  
Imprisoned the 12th of Birke.  
Convicted for many bold home and shop burglaries.  
Has begun strike to protest his innocence.  
Recommendation: let him starve._
    _Petra Silie_[15]_  
Imprisoned the 12th of Birke for practice of herbalism and publication of banned utterances.  
Admitted her guilt.  
Sentenced to death by pyre._
    _Sidspyker_[16]_  
Imprisoned the 13th of Birke - denounced by neighbor for use of magic.  
Acquitted of charges and released._
    _"Reptile Man"_[17]_  
True name unknown. Imprisoned the 13th of Birke for engaging in many bloody rows.  
Remains unconscious due to sustained head injuries.  
Recommendation: hospitalize._
    _"Sardukhar"_[18]_  
[Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"), true name unknown, member of the Elite Palace Guard of the [emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperator "Imperator") of Nilfgaard._[19]_Imprisoned the 13th of Birke as enemy of the Realm.  
Held under close watch pending arrival of military intelligence personnel._
    _Nars_[20]_  
[Halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") from [Knotweed Meadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Knotweed_Meadow "Knotweed Meadow"). Imprisoned the 14th of Birke for tax evasion and extortion.  
Has admitted no guilt.  
Recommendation: enhanced interrogation._
    _Momotek_[21]_  
Imprisoned the 14th of Birke for possession and use of magic amulets. Weak of mind.  
Recommendation: fit to assist in guarding other convicts.  
Please note: not to be trusted with sharp implements._
