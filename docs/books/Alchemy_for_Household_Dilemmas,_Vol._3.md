**Alchemy for Household Dilemmas, Vol. 3** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which was meant to be found in the library during the secondary quest [The Tower Outta Nowheres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Tower_Outta_Nowheres "The Tower Outta Nowheres"), where it can still be examined and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has a unique line for it, but it cannot be picked up. 

Its contents are still present in the game files, but were never attached to an [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") item definition. 

## Journal entry

    _This method positively shines whenever unexpected guests drop by and one's larder is empty. In such emergencies, prepare a copious container – a vat, a barrel, a bucket – anything large and watertight. Next fill it three-fourths of the way full with water (it is strongly advised not to exceed this amount, for the substance will boil as an alchemical side effect, which could lead a portion of it to spill out if the container is overfilled). The next step is to add hillobarthus aurensis extract, two drops for every liter of water, and then let the mixture sit for fifteen minutes. After that time has passed, apply a reverse secondary filtration formula, which by now you should be well familiar with._
