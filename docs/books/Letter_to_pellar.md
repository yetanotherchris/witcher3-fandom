**Letter to pellar** is a note in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in [the Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar")'s hut near [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough"). 

## Journal entry

    _I asked, I threatened, and yet you did nothing. Now I can only beg – hold Forefathers' Eve. The situation is treacherous, I know. The[witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") prey on human poverty and pay informants well, but it is not fitting to condemn our dead to eternal wandering. If needs be, I will take it all on myself. Claim responsibility. I'd rather die than carry on my conscience dead souls who will never know comfort._
    _[Bolko](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolko "Bolko"), Ealdorman of [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse")_
