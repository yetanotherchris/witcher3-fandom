## Item ID

q303_note_for_ciri

This **Mysterious note** can be found at [Margrave Henckel's house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margrave_Henckel%27s_house "Margrave Henckel's house"), usually during the main quest [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"). It provides a clue to solving the wine bottles "puzzle", which opens the secret door. However, despite glowing like a quest item and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") having scripted comments when it is picked up, it is technically not a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and not required to solve the puzzle. 

### Journal entry

    _The wine from[your](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") birth year - it's simply brilliant. Startling bouquet. You absolutely must try it. But afterwards, please place the bottle back where you found it._
    _With love from[D](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dudu "Dudu")_

### Associated quest

  * [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure")

### Notes

  * The puzzle in question was originally intended to be more elaborate than just moving the [bottle of wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bottle_of_wine "Bottle of wine") from where it is found to only one possible slot. See the video below.

## Item ID

lw_sk41_prison_island_note

This **Mysterious note** can be found on the body of one of the pirates who can be killed at the very end of [Kaer Almhult](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Almhult "Kaer Almhult")'s dungeon. It is related to the nearby unnamed hidden treasure marker. 

### Journal entry

    _Keep an eye out for castaways. Lone travelers. Drunk warriors. Bring them to me, to the cellar. The Ancient One craves blood._
    _Ph'nglui mglw'nafh Cthulhu R'lyeh wgah'nagl fhtagn._[1]
