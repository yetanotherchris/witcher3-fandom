## Item ID

**Aeramas' notes** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is found on [Aeramas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aeramas "Aeramas")' desk, along with a [bull figurine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bull_figurine "Bull figurine") during [Of Dairy and Darkness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Dairy_and_Darkness "Of Dairy and Darkness"). 

## Journal entry

    _The vision stabilized and, lo and behold, I saw as if through a moonlit haze the Four Princes, each clad in armor and astride a[horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse"). The first rode a steed the color of fog, the second of sulfur, the third black as the abyss, and the fourth – a pony._
    _And then I beheld a fountain, a clear spring, but the Princes did not drink of it, for it was forbidden._
    _And the a Bird of Paradise, shining in ruby and gold, flew above them and alit upon the branch of a tree. And the tree had arms numbering a thousand and forty._
    _"Draw from the source," said the Bird, but they pointed to the Sun's bloody tears and did not draw._
    _And then a pure Virgin appeared with bared breast, exposing her modest bosoms, ruddy and firm like ripe linden berries._
    _Then my nose became accustomed to the aroma and the vision passed._
    _Tomorrow I shall try to unseal that well-aged sample from Genno Myvort. It is said to have a refined, deep scent with slightly nutty aftertastes. Well. We shall see._
