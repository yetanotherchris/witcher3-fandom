## Item ID

Beasts vol 1

**Fauna of the Northern Realms, Volume 1** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When first read, it provides the [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries for [dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog") and [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)"). 

It should be available as random loot, both in the base game and in [vintners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vintner "Vintner")' camps [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), but due to a randomization bug can only and always be found on two adjacent bookshelves at the [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") library. It can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Halfling merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Marcus T.K. Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson") in his [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Books_and_Scrolls "Books and Scrolls") on [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * Books [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Merchant \(Hierarch Square\)") in Hierarch Square

## Journal entry

    _What a[wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)") is, every man knows. Who hasn't heard them howling at nights or seen their tracks in the snow come morning? Who hasn't felt a cold chill run down his spine when he catches sight of a pack of five of twenty of them hunting in the woods?_
    _These beautiful animals are treated by men as enemies - and not without reason, for they attack our flocks and will even kill our[horses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") during times of deep frost. Yet they are noble [beasts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_beasts "The Witcher 3 beasts"), endowed with great intelligence and displaying a strength and independence that no man can help but admire._
