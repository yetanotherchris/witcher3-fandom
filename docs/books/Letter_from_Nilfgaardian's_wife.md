## Item ID

mq1015_hang_man_note

**Letter from Nilfgaardian's wife** is a note in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is only retrievable if the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") deserter is killed during the quest [Lynch Mob](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lynch_Mob "Lynch Mob"), after which it can be looted off his hanged body. 

## Journal entry

    _Dilvyn,_
    _I lost my father and brother in the[last Nordling war](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Second_Nilfgaard_War "Second Nilfgaard War"). Please, come back to me, for I'm afraid I'll lose my husband, too, and our little Beatrys will lose her father._
    _I know the punishment for desertion. But I also know some men have made it home unscathed. We'll hide at Iffan's farm, you'll wait out the war in peace there._
    _I pray for you every day._
    _Alveen_
