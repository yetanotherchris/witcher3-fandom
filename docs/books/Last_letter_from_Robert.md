## Item ID

mq1060_letter_from_bob

**Last letter from Robert** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It only appears if the [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit") becomes a refugee camp/hospital, after completion of [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow") (i.e. if the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") deacon isn't killed by [Reinald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald "Reinald")). It can then be found in the first wall-side tent immediately to the right after entering the gates. 

## Journal entry

    _Son,_
    _I hope you're well and this letter finds you. Please make sure your sister and her little sprouts know that I'm all right. The Black Ones' attack didn't prove as fierce as it must have appeared to you from the outside looking in, though that's not to say they were altogether gentle. I was fortunate to flee a fortnight after[Belleteyn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Belleteyn "Belleteyn"). Still, before escape seemed possible, for the first time in my life, I sat haunted by the thought I may never see any of you again._
    _You know your old man is as tough and stubborn as they come, and I'm sure I'll manage from here on just fine. But you and your sister are grown now - you know life can, and often will, turns us from the paths we set for ourselves._
    _I had hoped to reunite with you in[Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"), but chances of that grow slimmer every day. These damned Nilfgaardian bureaucrats refuse to let anyone cross the border. I've already arranged to set out tomorrow for [Aedirn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aedirn "Aedirn") with a band of dwarves. They claim to know another route, safe and swift. Gods willing, perhaps I'll even arrive first and welcome you myself. Yet, if fate does decide to lead me elsewhere, at least I shall have good company and a new adventure ahead of me. One unlike any other before._
    _I intended to keep this letter brief, so I shall end it here with only one request - should anything happen to me, do not let the world change you. Stay true to yourselves as the good folk you are and always take care of each other._
    _Until we meet in[Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rivia "Rivia")._
    _Your father, Robert S._
