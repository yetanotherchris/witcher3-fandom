**Scoia'tael note** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found at an unmarked [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") camp by the road leading south from [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") forest [Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Novigrad\) "Crossroads \(Novigrad\)"), and it refers to the [Person(s) in distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_distress "Person\(s\) in distress") directly north from the camp. 

## Journal entry

    _[Dh'oine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") have captured [Cerbin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael_Crossroads\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael Crossroads\)"). They keep him on a leash, like a [dog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog"). Two bandits guard him constantly, so I cannot attack, or else they might hurt him... And I have no intention of negotiating with dh'oine._
    _But Cerbin has a good head on his shoulders. The dh'oine force him to drink alcohol, make him sing. He then insults them or calls out to us in[Elder Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Speech "Elder Speech"). His voice carries over the swamps, so we hear every word. We know these humans' routine. We know when they change watches. What weapons they have. I take note of it all. When I know enough, I shall strike. Quickly. When they are not expecting - and where it will hurt the most._
