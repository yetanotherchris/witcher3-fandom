## Item ID

faroe_map

**The Lonesome World Guide to Faroe** is a readable item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When read, it marks the [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken") [fast travel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") signpost and [harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harbor "Harbor") on the map, which however are automatically obtained when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") arrives on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") during the quest [Destination: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige"). 

It can be purchased from the following [The Witcher 3 merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") at the roadside camp between the [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") [crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Crossroads \(Ard Skellig\)") and [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Kaer_Trolde_Harbor\) "Merchant \(Kaer Trolde Harbor\)") at Kaer Trolde Harbor
  * [Wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Drummond_sawmill\) "Merchant \(Drummond sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(Drummond\) "Sawmill \(Drummond\)") south of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund"), after liberating it
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fayrlund\) "Merchant \(Fayrlund\)") in [Fayrlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fayrlund "Fayrlund")

## Journal entry

    _The most southerly-thrusting outcrop of the Skellige Archipelago is the wild and untamed isle of[Faroe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle"). Visiting here is a must for experienced travelers undeterred by unexplored wilderness and nature at its reddest in tooth and claw. Which is not to say that Faroe is uninhabited - just that its vibrant, primeval landscapes, not its [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") settlements, constitute its primary attraction._
    _Acting as sole exception to this, and providing a wonderful treat to adventure-seeking travelers, is the port of Harviken, the home village to[Clan Dimun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Dimun "Clan Dimun"). With a bit of luck one might find oneself there when the clan's warriors return from their overseas raids and divide their plunder. A days-long feast is thrown in the warriors' honor afterwards - providing an excellent opportunity to sample local delicacies and observe local customs. For more on this, see the "Feasts and Festivals" chapter._
