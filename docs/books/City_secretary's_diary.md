**City secretary's diary** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in the [Ice Plains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_Plains "Ice Plains"), the world destroyed by the [White Frost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Frost "White Frost"), on a skeleton in the lighthouse. 

## Journal entry

    _The 27th of[Blathe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blathe "Blathe")_
    _We have tried everything,[Magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") rituals do not work, the gods cannot be moved by sacrifices, nor do the methods of scholars show results. All we have done was for naught. The cold is more and more overwhelming, and snow falls constantly. We no longer know what to do._
    _The 17th of[Feainn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feainn "Feainn")_
    _At the mayor's request I went to the[mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") of Tor Gleavi'ch to ask how to arrest the changes to our climate. Sadly, they, too, are powerless. They claim the frost comes from some opening between the worlds, though I do not know if I understand them properly. One way or another, the snows do not relent._
    _The 35th of[Feainn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feainn "Feainn")_
    _It grows worse every day. The mayor has ordered everyone who can to gather in the lighthouse. We are to bring warm clothing, supplies and fuel. He hopes together we will find survival easier. People walk there slowly, bundles of wood scavenged from furniture strapped to their backs. In this weather even a meager fire is sweeter than honey._
    _The 6th of[Lammas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lammas "Lammas")_
    _Most who once lived in this city are now dead. The few who might live still cannot be reached - snow has buried every home. Only a few chimneys stick out in the whiteness. We are almost out of fuel and food. I feel that we will not make it much longer._
