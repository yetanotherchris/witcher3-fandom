## Item ID

q302_casino_register

**Income and Outlays** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found at [Whoreson Junior's casino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_casino "Whoreson Junior's casino"), usually during the main quest [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"). Despite being associated with the quest however, it doesn't serve a functional purpose.
