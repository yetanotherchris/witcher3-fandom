**Heroes of Skellige: Sove** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It describes the story of [Sove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sove "Sove"), the legendary founder of [Clan Brokvar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Brokvar "Clan Brokvar"). 

It can only be purchased from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Svorlag\) "Merchant \(Svorlag\)") in [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag"). 

## Journal entry

    _Legend states that Clan Brokvar, based on the isle of[Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog"), was founded by Sove. This hero took as his wife the lovely yet extremely quarrelsome [Ulula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulula "Ulula"). He tried countless ways to tame her, but no matter what lengths he went to, he could not get along with her. Finally he sailed to [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") to meditate in [Hindar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindar "Hindar"), the sacred grove. [Hemdall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall "Hemdall") and [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") took pity on him and gave him the formula for a potion that would grant him Understanding. They also instructed him to take a new approach, do something he had never done before, to resolve his conflicts with his wife. In order to gather the required ingredients, Sove had to kill a [monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"), climb the highest mountain in the isles and dive into the deepest undersea chasm. Then he prepared the potion, quaffed it and, searching for another approach to the conflict, began meditating while hanging from the tree_Irminsul _by[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] one leg. In this manner he achieved enlightenment, and returned to Spikeroog to live with Ulula in peace and harmony to the end of their days._
