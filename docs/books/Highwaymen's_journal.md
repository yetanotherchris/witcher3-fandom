## Item ID

mq2003_bandit_journal

**Highwaymen's journal** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on one of the bandit's bodies during the unmarked secondary quest [Yustianna Disturbed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna_Disturbed "Yustianna Disturbed"), and another copy is in a chest at the same location. It doesn't do anything, but it may've been part of some [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") along with a [key to treasure chamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Key_to_treasure_chamber "Key to treasure chamber").
