**The Lonesome World Guide to Hindarsfjall** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that, when read, marks the [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") [fast travel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") signpost and [harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harbor "Harbor") on the map. 

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") at the roadside camp between the [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") [crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Crossroads \(Ard Skellig\)") and [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Kaer_Trolde_Harbor\) "Merchant \(Kaer Trolde Harbor\)") at Kaer Trolde Harbor
  * [Wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Drummond_sawmill\) "Merchant \(Drummond sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(Drummond\) "Sawmill \(Drummond\)") south of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund"), after liberating it
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fyresdal\) "Merchant \(Fyresdal\)") in [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal")

## Journal entry

    _The smallest of the Skellige Isles is also the birthplace of the islander's culture. Skelligers believe themselves direct descendants of[Otkell](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otkell "Otkell"), a legendary hero who was the first practitioner of the cult of [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") in the isles. He erected a beautiful [temple](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_of_Freyja "Temple of Freyja") in honor of the goddess on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") (sadly the temple has been closed to visitors in recent years due to concerns about the possible theft of the famous diamond, [Brisingamen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brisingamen "Brisingamen"), ornamenting the goddess' statue). Otkell also constructed a [fantastic garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindar "Hindar") near the temple - this can still be visited upon making prior arrangements with the [priestesses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priest "Priest")' mother superior._
    _An enticing option for those wishing to visit Hindarsfjall is the package excursion offered by the congregation of Freya worshippers in[Poviss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Poviss "Poviss"). Thanks to a longstanding agreement with the isle's priestesses, they can offer tours of all the local highlights at a very reasonable price. This is by far the best option for families with children or the elderly._
