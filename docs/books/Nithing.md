## Item ID

mq2030_nithing

**Nithing** is a [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found on [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Rannvaig\) "Notice board \(Rannvaig\)") and, when read, begins the secondary quest [The Nithing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nithing "The Nithing"). 

## Journal entry

    _I'm asking for help from any who can give it. Someone has cast a cruel and treacherous[curse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Curse "Curse") on my line for which I cannot find a remedy. If you've skill in undoing such charms, or even if you have heard of a worthy cunning man or pellar who has such a gift, seek me out in my house near [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig") and rescue me from this misfortune. I shan't spare the [coin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") in recompense._
    _–[Lothar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lothar_\(Tjalve%27s_father\) "Lothar \(Tjalve's father\)")_
