**Contract: Missing Soldiers** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Journal entry

    _Let it be known that the[Imperial Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") is in need of a man who knows the area - a hunter or a scout, for example - to help us find a missing patrol._
    _It is possible the soldiers fell prey to a[monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"). If this suspicion is confirmed, anyone delivering the beast's head will be paid a reward by the undersigned. Address any and all questions to the same individual._
    _Glory to the[Emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperator "Imperator")!_
    _-[Milan Noran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Milan_Noran "Milan Noran"), commander of the division stationed in [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton")_
