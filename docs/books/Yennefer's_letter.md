## Item ID

q001_letter_from_yenn

**Yennefer's Letter** is document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

You start the game with this letter, and in this opening scene [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has the dialogue option to let [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") read the note looking for clues about where to find [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"). The [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")' search for Yen during the entirety of the prologue of the game is spurred by this letter. 

## Journal entry

    _Dear friend,_
    _Forgive me for not asking about your health or how you have been these last years. Time is very short._
    _I have important news. We must meet, and soon. Ride to[Willoughby](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Willoughby "Willoughby"), near [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), and don't spare the [horses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") \- while I do eagerly await our reunion, I won't be able to wait, eagerly or otherwise, very long._
    _Your dear friend,_
    _Yennefer._
    _P.S. I still have the[unicorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unicorn_\(creature\) "Unicorn \(creature\)")._
