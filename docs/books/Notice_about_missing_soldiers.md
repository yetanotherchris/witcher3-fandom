**Notice about missing soldiers** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be used to start [Contract: Patrol Gone Missing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Patrol_Gone_Missing "Contract: Patrol Gone Missing") but isn't the only way to start it. 

## Journal entry

    _Announcement:_
    _Soldiers in the service of His Imperial Majesty[Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis") went missing while performing their duties. A reward shall be paid to anyone who assists in finding them. For more information see the [quartermaster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tavar_Eggebracht "Tavar Eggebracht") at the [Army Group 'Center' camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")._
