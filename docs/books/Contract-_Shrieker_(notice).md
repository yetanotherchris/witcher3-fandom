## Item ID

mh101_contract

**Contract: Shrieker** is a notice board posting and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is found on [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Notice board \(Crow's Perch\)"). 

## Journal entry

    _To Whom It May Concern:  
Let it hereby be known that whoever kills the [shrieker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shrieker "Shrieker"), the monster that's wove its nest near Crow's Perch and has taken to killing both men and beasts, will be given a sizable reward. Take heed that she's a dreadful dangerous creature and killing it'll take a trained fighter, not just a pack of peasants with pitchforks.  
-[Chet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chet "Chet") at Crow's Perch_
