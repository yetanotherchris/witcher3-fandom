## Item ID

**Letter to Gaetan** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found, along with [Teigr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Teigr "Teigr") and 250 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), in [Gaetan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gaetan "Gaetan")'s stash if he's spared during the DLC secondary quest [Take What You Want](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Take_What_You_Want "Take What You Want"). 

## Journal entry

    _Gaetan_
    _It's over. Soldiers have taken the[school](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Cat "School of the Cat"). They killed [Axel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axel_\(witcher\) "Axel \(witcher\)") and [Cedric](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cedric_\(witcher\) "Cedric \(witcher\)"). As for [Schrödinger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Schr%C3%B6dinger "Schrödinger"), well, I can't say for sure - might be alive, might be dead. Bounties have been put on your head and mine. Avoid cities and the high roads. Don't attract any attention._
    _-[Joël](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jo%C3%ABl "Joël")_

  * The letter makes a reference to [Schrödinger's cat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schr%C3%B6dinger%27s_cat "wikipedia:Schrödinger's cat"), a thought experiment which considers a cat that may be simultaneously alive and dead.
