## Item ID

q302_dijkstras_notes

**Information from SD** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

Obviously written by [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"), this document is somehow linked to the main quest [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"). Its cryptic nature leaves plenty of room for speculation, but it's unclear if it's just another fluff note or part of something more significant. 

## Journal entry

    _Lesson Summary_
    _The[little bastard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley") washes his golden [goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Goose "Goose"). Plucked almost half her feathers in the last month alone._
    _The sharp lad met with the little bastard in private for a chat on some unknown matter. Afterwards Sharpie retired to a fluffed coop, where he ripped up two cages and killed three[hens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chicken "Chicken"). Chicken wrangler didn't raise a fuss._
    _White haired[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") by the name of [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has appeared in town. Sniffing around. Careful - the [Fireminder-in-Chief](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Menge "Caleb Menge")'s got his eye on him._
    _Our old pal[Little Boy Blue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche "Vernon Roche")'s raised a [new tent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Partisan_Hideout "Temerian Partisan Hideout") outside town. Trying to train farm pups and street strays the tricks his old [striped hounds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_Stripes "Blue Stripes") used to do. Next week we'll know for sure where he gets his doggy snacks, but it's clear the [strict man](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s eagles could have use for such tricks._
    _City broom masters intend to sweep up any and all sparklers, bubblers and stick wavers. The holy tooter received a secret shopping list. Don't know from whom. We suspect the[Fireminders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") and the [Wartseekers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") are involved._
