## Item ID

q001_academic_book

**Unfinished book** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , written by [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy") scholar [Aldert Geert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aldert_Geert "Aldert Geert"), before he was hanged by an unknown faction. 

It can be found beneath the [Hanged Man's Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Tree "Hanged Man's Tree") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), and as of [Patch 1.05](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.05_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.05 \(The Witcher 3\)"), the [Zoltan Chivay card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay_\(gwent_card\) "Zoltan Chivay \(gwent card\)") is coupled with the book, if it wasn't previously won in a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") game with Aldert in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

## Journal entry

    _It is widely known that "historia magistra vitae est," or in the[Common Tongue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Speech "Common Speech"), "history is life's teacher." Her students, however, have heretofore preferred to partake of her lectures from afar, namely amidst the quiet stacks of university libraries. They knew her teachings solely from the accounts of third parties, accounts inevitably colored by these individual prejudices or imperfect memories. I, by contrast, have decided the time has come to break with this convenient yet harmful practice and begin to draw knowledge from the source, writing history where it is made, on the front lines of [conflict](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III"). Many will say this way madness lies, and that my venture will indubitably end in my untimely death. To them I say with pride, "audentes fortuna iuvat!" - or in the Commo_
