## Item ID

lw_cp36_note

**Witch hunter's notes** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It was presumably part of cut content where the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") reach [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), similarly to a [letter from Keira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_Keira "Letter from Keira"), and should've appeared somewhere in the [Crow's Perch region](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch_\(region\) "Crow's Perch \(region\)"). However, it is unknown exactly where this was supposed to be found, whether on an already dead witch hunter or one that must be killed first, and the only location associated with the "cp36" ID remaining in the game files is the [forktail](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail "Forktail") [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") southwest of [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale"), where a regular [Nilfgaardian soldier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army")'s corpse is seen instead.
