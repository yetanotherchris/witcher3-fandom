**Sage's notes** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be looted from [Lara Dorren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lara_Dorren "Lara Dorren")'s shrine during the quest [Magic Lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Lamp_\(quest\) "Magic Lamp \(quest\)"). 

## Journal entry

    _Day 3275. Final conclusions regarding the[lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_lamp_\(quest_item\) "Magic lamp \(quest item\)")._
    _My experiences with the[magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") lamp unequivocally confirm that by using it active centers of condensed spiritual energy can be coaxed into contact and can communicate a limited set of the being's last memories._
    _[Lara Dorren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lara_Dorren "Lara Dorren")'s remains, however, emit entirely inert spiritual energy despite the violent conditions of her death, which should have strengthened the desired tendency._
    _Lara remains silent._
    _At this point further research on lamps enabling contact with the dead seem entirely useless for my purposes._
