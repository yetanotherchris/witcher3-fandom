## Item ID

Insectoids vol 1

**Wondrous World of Insectoids** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It provides [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries of all three kinds of [endregas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega "Endrega"): [drones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_drone "Endrega drone"), [warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_warrior "Endrega warrior") and [workers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_worker "Endrega worker"). 

It should be available as random loot, but due to a randomization bug can only and always be found in a chest upstairs at the [Deireadh prison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deireadh_prison "Deireadh prison"), accessible during the main quest, [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape"). It can also be purchased from [Marcus T.K. Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson") or the books [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Merchant \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). 

## Journal entry

    _One is hard-pressed to think of a creature more hard-working and better organized than the[endrega](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega "Endrega"). Ants, bees and termites are no match for them in this regard. Endregas best even [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") when it comes to foresightedness and industry._
    _In fact, the human race could benefit a great deal from following their example, resigning from the primitive and obsolete social structures of feudalism and adopting instead a form of arachnocommunism._
    _In arachnocommunism, every individual has a specific place and performs a specific labor for the common good. The roles are as follows: worker, soldier and[queen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_queen "Endrega queen"). Each comrade contributes to the necessary daily toil according to his special abilities, and takes from the common fund of wealth according to his special needs._

## Notes

  * While at [Ermion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mousesack "Mousesack")'s laboratory during the main quest [The King is Dead – Long Live the King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_King_is_Dead_%E2%80%93_Long_Live_the_King "The King is Dead – Long Live the King"), [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") has dialogue mentioning this book, as evidently present among Ermion's things found there. However, despite a number of other books found at the place, this one can't be seen or looted by [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia").
