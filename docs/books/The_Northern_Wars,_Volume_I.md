**The Northern Wars, Volume I** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Its contents are still present in the game files, but were never attached to an [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") item definition. 

## Journal entry

    _Responsibility for the outbreak of the[first war](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_I "Northern War I") ([1263](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1263 "Timeline/Games")-[1264](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1264 "Timeline/Games")) lies wholly with the Temerian Special Forces. Intercepted documents clearly show that [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") intended to annex [Cintra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cintra "Cintra") and moved in this direction, dealing a brazen blow to the balance of power that the [Empire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") could not abide. Only by moving its forces into Cintra and establishing order there could the Empire preempt the Temerians' destructive actions and safeguard peace. Sadly, the [North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"), stubborn and short-sighted as ever, chose armed conflict over reasonable compromise._
    _During this conflict the forces of the Northern Realms perpetrated unpardonable atrocities, for which reprisals came first with the[Battle of the Marnadal Valley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Marnadal "Battle of Marnadal") and then with the [Cintrian Incident](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slaughter_of_Cintra "Slaughter of Cintra"). Our Imperial Majesty, [Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis"), expressed his sympathies in a letter to the Cintrian court, adding that he hoped such horrors would never need to be repeated._
    _Once Cintra was taken, Nilfgaard considered the situation in the North stabilized, yet the Northern Realms fought on, bringing the situation to a head with the[clash near Sodden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Sodden_Hill "Battle of Sodden Hill"). Nordling propaganda depicts this battle as the turning point of the war. In truth it had no political significance whatever. Shortly afterwards an armistice was signed, rendering Cintra a vassal of the Empire of Nilfgaard for all eternity._
