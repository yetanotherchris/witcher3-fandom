## Item ID

q304_ambasador_letter

**Diplomatic report** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found on a desk inside the small office at the [Var Attre Villa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Var_Attre_Villa "Var Attre Villa"), usually during the main quest [Broken Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broken_Flowers "Broken Flowers"). 

## Journal entry

    _To: His Imperial Majesty[Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis"), [Emperor of Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperator "Imperator")_
    _Since the time of my last report, remarkably swift progress has been made and sentiment in the the city has crystallized. If anyone previously believed the ongoing war did not concern the free city, not a single resident now doubts the further course of the conflict will be determined within its walls. Our agents have confirmed earlier reports contending that King[Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") has important plans for [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and lately we have observed an increase in his agents' activity as well as the spread of propaganda. His prime objective seems to be seizure of the wealth held by the Church of the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire"). Yet though [Hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch "Hierarch") [Hemmelfart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemmelfart "Hemmelfart") officially rules the city, true power is in the hands of local [crime lords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four"). I recommend the emperor's agents focus further activity in that direction._
    _[Henry var Attre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henry_var_Attre "Henry var Attre")_
    _by decree of His Imperial Majesty_
    _Ambassador of the[Empire of Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") in the Free City of Novigrad_
