**Holy Tome of the Eternal Fire** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is very uncommon as random loot, but is guaranteed in a few places associated with [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire")-related quests: 

  * Near the house entrance at the [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence"), where the books are being burned
  * In the room where [Sweet Nettie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sweet_Nettie "Sweet Nettie") is found during [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins")
  * In the underground library during [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never")

    _The Fire illuminates the darkness. The Evil that in shadows lurks will be revealed in the Fire's harsh light and perish in its smoldering embers. Whosever shall seek to hide his guilt and lecherous works in darkness shall stand naked before the devouring flames._
