## Item ID

hr200_notice

**Race for Modron Freya's Glory** is a [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Larvik\) "Notice board \(Larvik\)") and, when read, begins the secondary quest [The Heroes' Pursuits: For the Goddess' Glory!](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_For_the_Goddess%27_Glory! "The Heroes' Pursuits: For the Goddess' Glory!"). 

## Journal entry

    _Looking to worship[Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") and prove your courage at the same time? Join the annual Heroes' Pursuits! The [priestesses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priest "Priest") and our goddess herself will shower you with gold and blessings._
    _Let it be known that not everyone who takes part in our[race](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse_racing "Horse racing") will reach the finish line in one piece. So if you've a pregnant wife, unfinished business or debts you're honor-bound to repay, think twice before straddling your [mount](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse")._
    _– Sister[Svana](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svana "Svana")_
