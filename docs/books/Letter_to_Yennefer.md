**Letter to Yennefer** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be looted from the room that [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") discuss [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") during [Imperial Audience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Audience "Imperial Audience"). 

## Journal entry

    _Yennefer, my dear friend,_
    _Thank you for your letter. Forgive me for not answering your earlier attempt to reach me via[megascope](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Megascope "Megascope"). I am trying to limit my [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") communication to the absolute minimum. One never knows who's listening, don't you agree?_
    _I am delighted you have found a position at our gracious emperor's court and wish you the best of luck in the search for his daughter. It is good to know that[Emhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis")'s intentions for her have become more, how shall I put it, mundane. Perhaps in these circumstances an agreement regarding the [Lodge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses") will prove possible after all?_
    _In response to your first question, I can state beyond all doubt that Ciri has not appeared anywhere south of the[Yaruga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yaruga "Yaruga"). Believe me, I could recognize her magic signature in my sleep._
    _I have not had any contact with[Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") for a long time. I only know that things in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") have taken an ill turn. She mentioned something earlier about fleeing to [Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir_and_Poviss "Kovir and Poviss"), but I am afraid that in the current political climate that amounts to an impossible daydream._
    _I hope I will soon be able to join you in[Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"). First, however, I must take care of some unfortunate yet urgent matters in [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair")._
    _With my fondest regards,_
    _[Fringilla Vigo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fringilla_Vigo "Fringilla Vigo")_
    _P.S. Thank you for the news about Geralt. He always seems to land on his feet, doesn't he?_
