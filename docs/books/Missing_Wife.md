**Missing Wife** is a notice board posting and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , needed to start the quest [Wild at Heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_at_Heart "Wild at Heart"). 

## Journal entry

    _Good people, take pity and hear my plea._
    _My wife,[Hanna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanna "Hanna"), she's missing. A few days ago she went into the woods and hasn't yet returned. I'm near out of my wits with worry and will pay any price to the man who brings her back to me, or at least tells me where to look for her._
    _[Niellen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Niellen "Niellen"), hunter from [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")_
