**Note from Father Edgar** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It can be found in the [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit") mine system, in a small room opposite of where the [second letter from Reinald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Second_letter_from_Reinald "Second letter from Reinald") is. 

## Journal entry

    _The matter is settled, as promised. Riots will no longer be a concern. But next time do reconsider before lecturing me on the[Church](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire")'s ethics. If we had not exacted punishment upon the uprising's leaders, the ungrateful wretches would already have us hanged. And after murdering us, they would undoubtedly carry the rot to every surrounding village, blanketing [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") in the scarlet fog. Remember - in the face of great sin, committing a lesser may prove the choice most moral._
    _Before we leave, draft up a tidy missive. Only be sure to omit any remarks concerning the "nail remedy". Mention of torture puts the prior in a melancholic humor from which he is a chore to lift._
    _Father Edgar_
