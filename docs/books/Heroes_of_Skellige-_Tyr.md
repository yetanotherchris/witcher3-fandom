**Heroes of Skellige: Tyr** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that describes the heroic deeds of [Tyr Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tyr "Tyr"). 

It can only be purchased from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Svorlag\) "Merchant \(Svorlag\)") in [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag"). 

## Journal entry

    _[Clan Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tuirseach "Clan Tuirseach"), master of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"), was founded by a hero named Tyr. Legend has it that to take possession of the land promised him by [Hemdall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall "Hemdall"), his father, Tyr first had to defeat a great and powerful [bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") named [Yngvar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yngvar "Yngvar"). It was well known that no mortal had ever survived an encounter with Yngvar, so Tyr decided to prepare properly for the fight. He ventured into the deepest cave in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and with the fire of the bowels of the earth as his furnace, he forged a blade so fine it could slice a hair lengthwise and so strong it could fell a hundred-year-old tree in one blow and not be dulled. [Heulyn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heulyn "Heulyn"), his mother, engraved protective runes upon the sword by the first light of dawn so that it would protect Tyr from the bear's attacks. Yngvar was defeated, Tyr became master of An Skellig, and his descendants are one of the wealthiest and most powerful clans in the Isles to this day._
