**Imperial Edict Number 283** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It should be available as random loot on some bookshelves, particularly in [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire")-related locations. However, likely due to oversight, only 2 such bookshelves were ever placed, not anywhere near [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_\(people\) "Nilfgaardian \(people\)"), and due to a randomization bug they always select another specific book. Thus, it is only obtainable through console commands. 

## Journal entry

    _Let it be known that anyone suspected of aiding or abetting[Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") rebels will answer before a summary military tribunal. Taking down the bodies of rebels executed by hanging is considered aid to the rebellion. By imperial decree, bandits thus punished are to serve as a warning to other rabble-rousers, and so their corpses are to hang at the roadside until they drop of their own accord to fertilize the once Temerian, now [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soil for the glory of the [emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor")._

## Notes

  * Two similar books, titled "Imperial Decree Number 296" and "Nilfgaardian orders", also exist in the game files, but have empty contents.
