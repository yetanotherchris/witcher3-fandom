## Item ID

lw_cp14_bandit_note

These **Highwayman's notes** can be looted off a man's body at the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") northeast of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley"). 

### Journal entry

    _And they say crime doesn't pay! Ha, never heard greater fucking rubbish in my life. It pays like fucking mad! We took so much loot from that old sod that we won't drink it all up in a year's time, not even if we down[Mahakaman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mahakam "Mahakam") spirit like it's water and chase it with dry wine from [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair")! Some might blather nonsense about the [gods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Religion "Religion") punishing us and whatnot, but I'm too old to believe in fairy tales. Well, next stop - [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")! We'll sell what we took and live like [kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King")!_

## Item ID

lw_tm12_bandit_note

This version is supposed to be dropped by the leader of the [Bandits' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandits%27_Camp "Bandits' Camp") (east of [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton")), but whether intentionally or due to a bug, he only drops the [bandits' key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandits%27_key "Bandits' key") to release a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Claywich\) "Merchant \(Claywich\)") from [Claywich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claywich "Claywich"). 

### Journal entry

    _The[baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") said we'd die of hunger in a week if we left his service. That he'd already looted all the peasants in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") bare, and there weren't nothing left for us to steal. Well, he might've been right about that... but there's still the peasants themselves, and in [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") they're fungible goods. We've already broken bread with a fellow who rounds up men for the Black Ones. We threw him a few hayseeds we'd found wandering the woods - he paid top coin for each and told us to keep them coming._
    _So we're preparing a manhunt the likes of which Velen's never seen. Like any proper hunting party, we've split into two groups. We're camping out here, while the rest are west of[Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). We'll go at the same time, from two sides, so none can run away._
    _Funny, aren't they - life's surprises. When we were with the baron, we thought the peasants worthless dung. And here it turns out each one's a bill of exchange, just waiting to be turned in for gold._
