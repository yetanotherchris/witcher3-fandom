## Item ID

Magical Monsters vol 2

**Of Fire and Stone** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When read, which adds [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries for [earth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_Elemental "Earth Elemental"), [fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Elemental "Fire Elemental"), [ice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_Elemental "Ice Elemental") elementals, and [hounds of the Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt "Hound of the Wild Hunt"), as well as the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Earth_elemental_decoction "Torn-out page: Earth elemental decoction") for [earth elemental decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_elemental_decoction "Earth elemental decoction"). 

It should be available as random loot in both the base game and the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, but due to a randomization bug it only appears as such on bookshelves in houses around [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), and can only be purchased from the [Ducal Camerlengo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rafael_de_Surmann "Rafael de Surmann") in [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair"). Thus, it is rather useless as none of the above-mentioned [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") can be encountered in Toussaint. 

## Journal entry

    _Sphera Mundi is divided into four[Elemental Planes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elemental_plane "Elemental plane"): the Plane of [Earth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth "Earth"), the Plane of [Water](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water "Water"), the Plane of [Air](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Air "Air") and the Plane of [Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire "Fire"). Earth and Water together form our [Planet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World"), around which circulates Air. Above Air stretches the Aether - Fiery Air, or in short, Fire. Past Fire lie the Subtle Sidereal Heavens known collectively as Firmamentum, which are of a spherical nature. That is where the Erratica Sydera, drifting stars, and Fixa Sydera, fixed stars, reside._
    _The Elemental Planes are inhabited by creatures which can be summoned by use of[magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic"). They are: [gnomes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnome "Gnome"), [sylphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sylph "Sylph"), salamanders, and ondynates, all of which belong to the category commonly known as [elementa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa "Elementa") ("elementum" in the singular)._
