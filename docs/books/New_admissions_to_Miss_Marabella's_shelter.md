In the year [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") had to make the choice of killing [the Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree") or spare it. If Geralt chose to spare the spirit after meeting the orphans, the spirit will keep its end of the bargain, later confirmed by finding this document on a desk at Miss [Marabella](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marabella "Marabella")'s [School for Tots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marabella%27s_School_for_Tots "Marabella's School for Tots") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), where the children themselves can also be seen around.
