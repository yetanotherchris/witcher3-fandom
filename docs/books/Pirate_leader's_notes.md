**Pirate leader's notes** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is dropped by the [Pirate Leader](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate_Leader_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Pirate Leader \(Spitfire Bluff\)") at the [pirate base](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate_base_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Pirate base \(Spitfire Bluff\)") camp southwest of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp") and northwest of [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle"). While it's not necessary for anything, its contents refer to other events and locations in the [Spitfire Bluff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spitfire_Bluff "Spitfire Bluff") area, at some of which letters addressed to the Pirate Leader can be found. 

## Journal entry

    _Nothing quite like war! The[Redanians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") and [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_\(people\) "Nilfgaardian \(people\)") are at each other's throats, and no one pays a whit of attention to us. We can rob, raid and kill till we get bored of it - which'll never happen._
    _Usually I never set ashore near populated places, but this time we had no choice. Our boat started taking on water after the last storm, we had to drag her onto dry land for some repairs. We were ready for a fight when we stepped onto the beach, but no one even tried to chase us off. Sometimes some of the[baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s blowhands come to us, but taking care of them's no trouble._
    _Since we're not in any particular hurry, I've decided to build us a new vessel instead of patching up the old dinghy. Why not? Best take advantage of the turmoil while it lasts. I hear there's a[boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") nearby - that'll do just fine. And while we wait for a new ship, I'll send men out to look [around](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks") for [Queen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen "Queen") [Zuleyka](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zuleyka "Zuleyka")'s famous [treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen_Zuleyka%27s_Treasure "Queen Zuleyka's Treasure"). If it's worth a tenth of what I've heard, we'll all be rich. Well, I will, at least._
