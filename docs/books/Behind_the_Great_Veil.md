## Item ID

Specters vol 2

**Behind the Great Veil** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that, when read, provides [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries for [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") and [hyms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hym "Hym"), as well as the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Wraith_decoction "Torn-out page: Wraith decoction") for [wraith decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith_decoction "Wraith decoction"). 

It should be available as random loot, but due to a randomization bug can only and always be found in a chest inside the library during [The Tower Outta Nowheres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Tower_Outta_Nowheres "The Tower Outta Nowheres"). 

## Journal entry

    _Of the many[demons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Demon "Demon") which reside in the outer spheres, hyms are among the most dangerous. Other demons usually must take on concrete living form - be it of man or [beast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_beasts "The Witcher 3 beasts"). Hyms, on the other hand, appear in our [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World") in their own immaterial form, which is invisible to all save their chosen victim._
    _Hyms feed on fear, regret, a sense of guilt - in a word, on negative emotions, and since they are eternally hungry, they provoke these emotions in the[humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") they torment. These demons cannot be exorcized. A man a hym takes as a victim is lost and will inevitably succumb to madness, and then death._
