**After the White Frost** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is found in the [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Mysterious_Elf "The Mysterious Elf")'s laboratory inside the ruins northwest of [Byways](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Byways "Byways"). 

## Journal entry

    _Spiring ash down dreamy hills  
past sparkling waters, [Tir ná Lia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tir_n%C3%A1_Lia "Tir ná Lia") drifts  
slowly, softly, silently,  
into oblivion._
    _[Sage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sage "Sage") lips blacken, frosted bones quiver._
    _Before an ice-bound eye can blink  
in distant skies I will watch your descent._
