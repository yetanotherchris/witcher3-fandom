## Item ID

lw_cp14_child_note

**Sodden note** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be looted off a child's body at the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") northeast of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley"). 

## Journal entry

    _Dear Vessy,_
    _We couldn't afford to pay for ourselves and Patrick, so we've sent the young lad to you. I know things're tough your way, too, that you've three tykes of your own – so know that if I didn't have to, I'd not ask this of you. But here in[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")... well, you wouldn't believe it if I told you._
    _Please take care of Patrick like your own son. We'll come back for him soon as the war's over. A thousand thanks._
    _Hanna_
    _P.S. Patrick's got a sack of coin in his bags – enough to pay his guide what we agreed on. You shouldn't have to add anything of your own._
    _P.P.S. Patrick's afraid of the dark. If you can, leave a candle burning by his bed._
