## Item ID

mh306_contract

**Contract: Haunted House** is a [notice board posting](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ which can be picked up at the following notice boards: 

  * [Notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Notice board \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * [Notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Gildorf\) "Notice board \(Gildorf\)") in [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf")

Reading it adds the [Contract: Doors Slamming Shut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Doors_Slamming_Shut "Contract: Doors Slamming Shut") to your journal. 

## Journal entry

    _I seek a[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") of unsullied reputation (documentation regarding prior achievements and written references preferred) who will undertake the task of combing the residence on the outskirts of the city recently acquired by the undersigned, [Count](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count "Count") [Kurt Dysart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kurt_Dysart "Kurt Dysart"), in order to find and drive off or kill [the creature](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarah "Sarah") which is haunting it. A sizable reward is guaranteed._
    _Sincerely,  
-Kurt Dysart, Count of [Anchor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anchor "Anchor") by writ of [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest") (temporarily in residence at [the Kingfisher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn"))_
