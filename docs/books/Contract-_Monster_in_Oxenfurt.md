## Item ID

mh304_contract

**Contract: Monster in Oxenfurt** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and found on [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Oxenfurt\) "Notice board \(Oxenfurt\)"). Reading it starts [Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Oxenfurt_Drunk "Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk"). 

## Journal entry

    _By orders of our most magnanimous rule,[King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"), any itinerant monster slayer reading this notice is obliged to abandon any currently-unresolved contracts immediately and devote himself to tracking and slaying this monster. The beast in question has befouled the cradle of [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") intellect, the city within whose territory lies the [oldest of academies of learning](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"), namely: [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). The swordsman who puts an end to its criminal doings will be rewarded with the King's full generosity._
    _-[Commander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nikolas_Friedman "Nikolas Friedman") of the Oxenfurt City Regiment_
