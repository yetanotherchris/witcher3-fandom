## Item ID

lore_elven_sages

**Elven Sages** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , typically found in elven ruins. 

Guaranteed copies of it can be found on a bench in [Keira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")'s illusory underground chamber during [Hunting a Witch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hunting_a_Witch "Hunting a Witch"), in the underground library during [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), and in Mad [Kiyan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kiyan "Kiyan")'s room during [Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear"). It can also be purchased from the [Scoia'tael merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael\)") south of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

## Journal entry

    _Many think "[Aen Saevherne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Saevherne "Aen Saevherne")" - meaning Sage - is simply the [Elder Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Speech "Elder Speech") term for [sorcerer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcerer "Sorcerer"). Yet, to equate a Sage with a common practitioner of [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") is akin to considering a newly-drafted private, hay sticking out of his breeches, a [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")'s equal simply because both wield swords._
    _A Sage wields magic, true, and does so with greater skill than even the most accomplished human mages. The great renown and regard in which they are held, however, comes not from this skill but from the other, truly rare abilities they possess._
    _Nothing is a secret for an[elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") sage. They see both past and future as easily as the present. They have mastery over all the arcana of magic as well as every domain of scholarship. They are the only ones who understand the nature and operation of the so-called [Elder Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Blood "Elder Blood") and the gene of [Lara Dorren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lara_Dorren "Lara Dorren") \- and they guard this knowledge jealously._
    _How numerous are the Elven Sages? In all my life I have met only one,[Ida Emean](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ida_Emean_aep_Sivney "Ida Emean aep Sivney") of the [Blue Mountains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_Mountains "Blue Mountains"). She might possibly be the last representative of this mysterious caste. It is true I have encountered mention of a [male elf who might also belong to it](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Avallac%27h "Avallac'h"), but as of this writing I have been unable to confirm this._
