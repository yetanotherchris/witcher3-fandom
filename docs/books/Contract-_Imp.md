**Contract: Imp** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Journal entry

    _On behalf of the[Merchants' Guild](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guild_of_Merchants "Guild of Merchants"), I hereby announce the following:_
    _A particularly bold beast - an[imp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Janne "Janne"), to our thinking - has taken to thieving in the market near the city [gates](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tretogor_Gate "Tretogor Gate"). Though it has not yet harmed any [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human"), this [monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") has caused a great deal of irrecoverable financial loss.  
Whoever tracks down and kills this imp shall receive a hefty reward - one free of all taxes, in accordance with our honorable [city](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") councilmen's decree numbered 1271/c/VII/10._
    _Do not tarry in providing us assistance, good people, for retail trade is the backbone of a healthy economy, meaning the financial well-being of the city's merchants lies in all of your best interests._
    _-[Sylvester Amello](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sylvester_Amello "Sylvester Amello"), Merchant_
