## Item ID

Hybrid Monsters vol 2

**Heraldic Animals** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that when read adds a [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entry for [griffins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)"), as well as [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Archgriffin_decoction "Torn-out page: Archgriffin decoction") for [archgriffin decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin_decoction "Archgriffin decoction") and [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Griffin_decoction "Torn-out page: Griffin decoction") for [griffin decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_decoction "Griffin decoction"). 

However, as the bestiary entry is automatically obtained when [Bram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bram "Bram") is rescued during one of the very first scenes in the game, it is really only useful for the decoction formulae. 

The book can only be purchased from the [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kaer_Trolde\) "Herbalist \(Kaer Trolde\)") in [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor"). It should also be available as random loot, but due to a randomization bug doesn't appear as such. 

## Journal entry

    _Of all the heraldic beasts, lions and griffins are the most noble and pure. Let us speak first of griffins, for the creatures from which the armorialists draw inspiration combine elements of both eagles and lions, and thus contain within them both rulers of the Animal Kingdom._
    _Griffins are brave hunters and the most chivalrous of creatures known to man. Before they snap down from the heavens upon their prey like a divine whip, they let out a long, piercing shriek in warning, so that the victim may prepare itself for the imminent attack. Griffins do not fear to attack armed men - in fact, they prefer such prey, for defeating a[knight](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Knight "Knight") flatters their pride. They even attack [fiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend"), the terror of the [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") wilderness, so honorable is their nature._

## Notes

  * This book was supposed to appear as guaranteed loot in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, on a skeleton on a cliff just under where the griffin is encountered during [A Portrait of the Witcher as an Old Man](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Portrait_of_the_Witcher_as_an_Old_Man "A Portrait of the Witcher as an Old Man"), but for unknown reasons the container is disabled for looting.
