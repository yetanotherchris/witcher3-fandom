**Gwent: Looking for New Players** is a document and [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It initially only appears on the notice board at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). However, as of [Patch 1.31](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.31_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.31 \(The Witcher 3\)"), if one hasn't started [Gwent: Velen Players](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent:_Velen_Players "Gwent: Velen Players") yet before the end of [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters"), it will also appear on the notice board at [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). 

## Journal entry

    _The Sharpers, a[gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") playing society based at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), is looking for new members - and opponents._
    _Experience preferred, but we will also school newcomers to the game (but not return any coin lost during training matches)._
    _For more information see the club's chairman,[Phillip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger"), known as the Bloody Baron._
