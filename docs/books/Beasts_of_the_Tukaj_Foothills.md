## Item ID

Relict Monsters vol 1

**Beasts of the Tukaj Foothills** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that, when read, adds [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries for [chorts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort "Chort") and [fiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend"), as well as [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Chort_decoction "Torn-out page: Chort decoction") for [chort decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort_decoction "Chort decoction") and [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Fiend_decoction "Torn-out page: Fiend decoction") for [fiend decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend_decoction "Fiend decoction"). 

It can be purchased from [Marcus T.K. Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson") in his [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Books_and_Scrolls "Books and Scrolls") on [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"), and should be available as random loot, but due to a randomization bug never appears as such. 

## Journal entry

    _The peasants of the[Tukaj Foothills](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tukaj_Foothills "Tukaj Foothills") say fiends are born of [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic"). For at times [witches](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch "Witch") cast spells on [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") causing them to mount [boars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_boar "Wild boar"), and the resultant offspring is neither boar nor bear, but has the makings of a fiend._
    _Usually such boar piglets die of hunger, but they are at times found and taken in by witches. Raised on enchanted milk, they grow in strength, and soon enough the milk is no longer enough for them. Now fiends, they start hunting and kill their victims with the ferocity of a boar and the strength of a bear. Yet they remain ever true to their mothers, meaning the witches. The witches protect them from danger, answer their every summons and aid them in all things._
