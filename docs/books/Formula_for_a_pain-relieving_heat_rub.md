**Formula for a pain-relieving heat rub** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found on a table inside a hut in [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"), the one with a [grindstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grindstone "Grindstone") in front. 

## Journal entry

    _For lumbago, rheumatism of the back or witchshot._
    _Grind two handfuls of Harpagophytum procumbens, called devil's claw by peasants, with[feline](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(creature\) "Cat \(creature\)") suet. Add dried and crushed arnica, mint and camphor. Heat resultant mixture before application. Use three times a day._
    _After treatment, scrub back with fresh water, lest it reek._

## Notes

  * It's likely [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") is the one who prescribed this for the intended recipient.

## Trivia

  * Most of the formula's contents are in fact real. [Harpagophytum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harpagophytum "wikipedia:Harpagophytum") is a plant native to southern Africa, indeed often called "devil's claw", known to be used for back pain relief in folk medicine.
