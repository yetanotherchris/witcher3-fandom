## Item ID

lw_sb2_sunken_ships_note

This version is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") book, found at the hidden treasure marker at [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks"). Finding it starts the treasure hunt quest [Queen Zuleyka's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen_Zuleyka%27s_Treasure "Queen Zuleyka's Treasure"). 

Despite what its original caption (no longer used) says, it is not found on any of the pirates, but as regular loot on the ground. This is the result of some [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") around the quest, where more clues than just this one note were intended to be required. 

### Journal entry

    _Yesterday we found a silver basin and a ruby brooch on the bottom. Means Queen Zuleyka's treasure really is somewhere around here. Maybe in the hold of one of the wrecked ships off the west coast - but we can't check them at the moment, waves are too high. We'll have to wait till the seas calm. We're pitching camp._

## Item ID

lw_sb3_camps_note

This one can be found at the [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp"), on a mattress under the northwestern corner of the tent. However, it can only be retrieved before clearing the site from bandits, after which the notes disappear along with an [unsent letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(boatyard_pirates\) "Unsent letter") nearby. 

After the liberation, a different [unsent letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(boatyard_workers\) "Unsent letter") can be found at the site. 

### Journal entry

    _What a beaute of a ship we're gonna have! She's going to be as yar as they come. True, that masthead the shipwright carved looks more like a sea cow than a buxom siren, but that's a minor detail._
    _Question is, will the lads be eager to set back out to sea? Some have made a nice little home in a hut nearby. I half expect to turn around and see them planting turnips and milking goats. Each according to his own, I guess - but it's a pirate's life for me._

## Item ID

lw_sb12_pirate_note

This version is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book that can only be obtained via console commands. 

It was intended to be found at the roadside [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") north-northeast of the Coast of Wrecks signpost in eastern Velen, whether on one of the corpses laying around or to be dropped by one of the deserters there. It's unclear if it has been intentionally removed or wasn't included due to oversight. 

### Journal entry

    _I know it ain't normal for a pirate to complain about violence and plundering... but for fuck's sake! There's no stopping these soldiers![Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") must've tossed them out of his [castle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") because they were too much, even for him. Not a day goes by they don't rob or beat somebody. We've cut down three of them already, but that hasn't discouraged the rest one jot. Gonna have to wipe them out or change hideouts, because frankly I didn't become a pirate to be the one getting assaulted._
