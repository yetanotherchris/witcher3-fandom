## Item ID

Poems of Gonzal de Verceo

**The Collected Verse of Gonzal de Verceo** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

One guaranteed copy of this book is found in a chest at the [Royal Palace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Palace_\(Vizima\) "Royal Palace \(Vizima\)") in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), in the room where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") meet during the quest [Imperial Audience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Audience "Imperial Audience"). Multiple copies are also found in the underground library during the quest [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), where [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") has a unique voice line mentioning the book. 

In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can be found as random loot inside rich houses around [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair"), and near people having picnics, suggesting that it is a popular leisurely read among Toussaintous. 

## Journal entry

    _'Love  
To love is to build a house of cards, or play a game of chess  
But one wrong word or ill-thought move  
And you must start it all afresh._
    _Tide  
Whenever I watch the tide recede  
Cold coils of fear grip round my heart.  
Will the seas sneak back, calm and sure  
In the dark of night as they have before?  
Or will they stay on distant shores  
Leaving crushed shells and washed-up dreams  
As memory of surfs of yore?_

## Trivia

  * The name "Gonzal de Verceo" is a reference to the 14th century Castillian poet, [Gonzalo de Berceo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gonzalo_de_Berceo "wikipedia:Gonzalo de Berceo").
