## Item ID

q103_tamara_prayer

**A prayer** is a note in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found in the basement of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can find this note next to a shrine to the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire"), usually during the main quest [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters"), but it can be picked up later if missed. It is written by [Tamara Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger"), showing her distain for her [father](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger"), and gives further details to her disappearance. 

## Journal entry

    _Eternal Fire, which lighteth our hearts and giveth us light,  
Heat us with Thy warmth,  
Dry our tears,  
Burn our foes,  
Embraces our friends in Thy care._
    _Give my[mother](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anna_Strenger "Anna Strenger") health,_
    _Punish my father,_
    _Help me fulfill my destiny._
