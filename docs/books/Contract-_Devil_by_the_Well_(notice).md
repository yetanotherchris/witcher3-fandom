## Item ID

**Contract: Devil by the Well** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When first read, it adds the quest [Contract: Devil by the Well](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Devil_by_the_Well "Contract: Devil by the Well") to the journal. 

## Journal entry

    _Good folk,_
    _I know there's a war on and every man's got trouble enough of his own, but perhaps there's one of you who could help a father in need._
    _You all surely know the well in the[ruined village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Village_\(White_Orchard\) "Abandoned Village \(White Orchard\)"), and the [devil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil_by_the_Well "Devil by the Well") that guards it with a jealous fury - and if you don't know, well, come ask and I'll tell you all about it._
    _Whoever drives that monster away from the well will get a fat purse full of[gold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). Just don't tarry, for it's an urgent matter._
    _–[Odolan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Odolan "Odolan")_
