**Mechanism Start-Up Information** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Its contents are still present in the game files, but were never attached to an [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") item. Only the contents of the journal entry are actually in the game files, and the title is conjectured from the first line. 

The way it is written makes this book possibly related to [The Tower Outta Nowheres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Tower_Outta_Nowheres "The Tower Outta Nowheres") quest, from which items such as gold token and multiple versions of [golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem") hearts, including a "charged one", have indeed also been cut. While highly speculative, the pun where "GOG" currently stands for [Gottfried's Omni-opening Grimoire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gottfried%27s_Omni-opening_Grimoire "Gottfried's Omni-opening Grimoire") may've originally been something like "Golem operating guide" as the title of this book. 

## Journal entry

    _"Mechanism Start-Up Information"_
    _Instructions for operating Golem Version 3.70_
    _Using the golem involves but two steps:  
1\. Insert a token.  
2\. Press the service button and state your command._
    _If your Golem fails to operate, you may request a refund or replacement from Munro Burch. Before you do so, however, please check the following:_ :
    _1\. Is the Golem's heart charged?  
2\. Is the module you wish to use installed in your Golem?  
3\. Have you inserted a token?_
    _Please note! Under no circumstances should you remove the Golem's safety lock. Removing the safety lock carries the risk of crippling injury or even death._
