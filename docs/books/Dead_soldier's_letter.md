**Dead soldier's letter** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , the contents of which point out the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") on the beach beneath the [Ransacked Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ransacked_Village "Ransacked Village"). 

It can be found at the [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") [battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_\(White_Orchard\) "Battlefield \(White Orchard\)"), north of the [Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(White_Orchard\) "Crossroads \(White Orchard\)"). It is on the ground among the many corpses, in pretty much the middle of the battlefield near a [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") banner and what looks like a burned-down structure of some kind, though one too tiny to be a building. 

## Journal entry

    _Gustav, I've got something to ask of you. I've come into the possession of quite the stash of valuables. Don't ask how - modesty prevents me from trumpeting the heroic deeds I performed in the fight for our beloved[fatherland](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria"). At any rate there's so damned much of it that there's no way in any hell I'll carry it back on my own, so I've put everything in a chest and buried it on the beach, at the base of the cliff with the two burned-out huts on it. You know, we'd go crayfish hunting there, remember?_
    _So here's what you've got to do: grab a wheelbarrow, throw a shovel in it and take it to the beach - but make sure no one sees you. We'll load the treasure and sell it all in[Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") \- and split the earnings fifty-fifty, like family should._
    _See you later. And remember - not a word to anyone!_
    _Johenn_
