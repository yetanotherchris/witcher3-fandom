## Item ID

lore_polymorphism

**Polymorphy** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in [Morkvarg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg "Morkvarg")'s cave lair during the secondary quest [In Wolf's Clothing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_Wolf%27s_Clothing "In Wolf's Clothing") and should also be available as random loot. However, due to a randomization bug, it only appears in one more place where it is guaranteed - the bedside chest with [journal from the Moldavie Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Journal_from_the_Moldavie_Residence "Journal from the Moldavie Residence") during [Contract: Doors Slamming Shut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Doors_Slamming_Shut "Contract: Doors Slamming Shut"). 

## Journal entry

    _To change form - to shape one's matter as clay - this is no novice's trick, no flickering glowlight or[rat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rat "Rat")-killing blue bolt. Only those with bowels soaked in oceans of [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") arcana - and few are born with bowels so absorbent - can hope to master it._
    _There are exceptions, of course.[Dragons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon "Dragon"), as is well known, possess such a talent innately. With no need for study they can, guided by some otherworldly intuition, change between [humanoid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") and reptilian form. As for the other races, [higher vampires](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Higher_vampire "Higher vampire") (e.g..[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] [bruxae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bruxa "Bruxa")) are known to flicker between states, yet given the understandable difficulties of conducting research in this area, we do not know if this transition constitutes an act of [polymorphy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Polymorphism "Polymorphism")._
    _The human race is, obviously, much more accessible to scholars of polymorphy. Their years of study have borne fruit in the form of a set of methods and guidelines for the identification, nurture and deployment of this talent. The most accomplished result of the careful application of these methods -[Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart")._
