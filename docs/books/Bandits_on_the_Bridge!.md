## Item ID

mq1043_notice

**Bandits on the Bridge!** is a [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't seem to be found during gameplay. 

## Journal entry

    _To travelers and native folk, to women, children and elders, to men in their prime and those barely starting to get fuzz under their beaks, to anyone who might be passing by and knows how to read - read closely and tell those who don't!_
    _A band of scoundrels and thieves has waylaid the bridge south of[Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") and won't let anyone passed who doesn't pay them a toll! So be on the lookout if you're planning on passing through there, and if you can, wend your road over another bridge!_
