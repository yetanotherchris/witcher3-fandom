**Letter from a pirate leader** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted from the fishing hut at the [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Abandoned site \(Spitfire Bluff\)") west of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). It must, however, be retrieved before clearing the area of bandits, for after the area is liberated, this item disappears and is replaced by a different [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_\(The_Witcher_3\)#Letter_\(Hangman's_Alley\) "Letter \(The Witcher 3\)"). The easiest way to get this is to lure the last pirate inside the hut, and then quickly loot the letter just as the site liberation scene phases in. 

## Journal entry

    _Barnim,_
    _We've taken the shipyard. Shipwright didn't want to cooperate at first, but I knocked a few of his teeth out and he changed his mind. But I could break his ribs and put a torch to his feet, and he still wouldn't be able to build us a boat out of sand and reeds, so you'll have to go south and bring back the following:_
    _\--a yew for the mast, forty feet, straight as an arrow_
    _\--five thick oaks to cut into boards_
    _\--three barrels of pitch_
    _\--a hundred feet of thick rope._
    _I want to sail out on another raid as soon as possible, so don't bitch and moan, just grab your axes and get to work. Or else I'll try my some of my methods of persuasion on you, too._

## Notes

  * This letter is penned by the [Pirate Leader](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate_Leader_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Pirate Leader \(Spitfire Bluff\)") at the [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate_base_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Pirate base \(Spitfire Bluff\)") northwest of [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle"). Yet, the orders described are for the pirates to go south from there, not the northern shore of [Spitfire Bluff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spitfire_Bluff "Spitfire Bluff") where this is found.
