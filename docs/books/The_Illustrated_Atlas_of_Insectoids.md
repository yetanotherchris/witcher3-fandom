## Item ID

Insectoids vol 2

_**The Illustrated Atlas of Insectoids**_ is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When first read, it provides [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries for [arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas "Arachas"), [armored arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armored_arachas "Armored arachas") and [venomous arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Venomous_arachas "Venomous arachas"), as well as the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Arachas_decoction "Torn-out page: Arachas decoction") for [arachas decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas_decoction "Arachas decoction"). 

It should be available as random loot, but due to a randomization bug can only and always be found in two chests: in the [Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lighthouse "Lighthouse") southwest of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and by the beds in the large hall at [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") keep. 

## Journal entry

    _[Arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas "Arachas"). [Monst](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"). of the —>Brachyoarachindaesubg., subtype—>viperhexapoda. Lives mainly in boggy terrain, but also in caves and abandoned mines. Hierarchical caste struct., w/ highly spec. members. master of —>mimicry, many specimens highly poisonous. Invasive species, immigrated from the south. Must be fought with silver, ideally freshly coated with [insectoid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid "Insectoid") —>[oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid_Oil "Insectoid Oil")._

## Notes

  * An earlier version of this book, titled "Illustrated Atlas of Insectoids - From Arachas to Zerrikanian Xanthogramma", also exists in the game files but with empty contents. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") was even [supposed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") to have a unique comment when finding it, suggesting that it was to be found during a quest or otherwise in a scripted location.
