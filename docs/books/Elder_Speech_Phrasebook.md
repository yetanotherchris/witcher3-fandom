**Elder Speech Phrasebook** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Its contents are still present in the game files, but were never attached to an [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") item definition. 

## Journal entry

    _Traveling_
    _N'aen aespar a me – Don't shoot me.  
M'aespar que va'en, ell'ea? – Are you going to shoot me, or what?  
Caelm, evellienn! – Calm down, everyone!  
Gar'ean! N'te va! – Watch out! Don't go in there!  
Glaeddyv vort! – Drop your sword!_
    _On a romantic outing_
    _Ess've vort shaente aen... – Sing me a song about...  
Va'en aesledde, ell'ea? – We'll go for a sleigh ride, all right?  
Elaine tedd a'taeghane – Lovely weather we're having.  
Caen me a'baethe? – Give me a kiss?  
N'te dice'en – Don't say anything._
    _Shopping_
    _Mir'me vara – Show me your goods.  
Naev'de aen tvedeane – Ten a dozen._
