## Item ID

spikeroog_map

**The Lonesome World Guide to Spikeroog** is a readable item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When read, it marks the [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag") [fast travel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") signpost and [harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harbor "Harbor") on the map, which however is automatically obtained when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") arrives on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") during the quest [Destination: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige"). 

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") at the roadside camp between the [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") [crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Crossroads \(Ard Skellig\)") and [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Kaer_Trolde_Harbor\) "Merchant \(Kaer Trolde Harbor\)") at Kaer Trolde Harbor
  * [Wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Drummond_sawmill\) "Merchant \(Drummond sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(Drummond\) "Sawmill \(Drummond\)") south of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund"), after liberating it
  * [Ilge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ilge "Ilge") (Innkeep) in [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn")

## Journal entry

    _Though[Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog") is not the most frequently-visited of the Skellige Isles, one cannot deny it has a certain inescapable charm. Particularly bewitching are the snow-capped peaks towering over its southern and western shores._
    _Spikeroog will surely please lovers of the martial arts. The northern portion of the isle is[home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hov "Hov") to a famed arena where local warriors test their strength in no-holds-barred bouts. South of the arena lies the village of Svorlag, home to [Clan Brokvar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Brokvar "Clan Brokvar"). Located on a picturesque bay, Svorlag is particularly lovely to visit in the summer and around the feasts of [Belleteyn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Belleteyn "Belleteyn"). South of Svorlag sprawls a primeval wilderness that serves as Clan Brokvar's chief hunting grounds._
    _Visiting the southern portions of the isle should be reserved only for extremely experienced travelers, for they are completely wild, inhospitable and deprived of all human settlements. The area is crawling with dangerous creatures of all kinds (described in more detail in the "Fauna and Flora" section). One should also show particular caution when traveling near the caves along the isle's coast._
