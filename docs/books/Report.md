## Item ID

q311_aen_elle_notes

This **report** , written by [Inglor Helyanwë](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Inglor_Helyanw%C3%AB "Inglor Helyanwë"), can be found on a fountain in the [Palace of Awakening](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Palace_of_Awakening "Palace of Awakening") in [Tir ná Lia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tir_n%C3%A1_Lia "Tir ná Lia"), during the quest [Through Time and Space](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Through_Time_and_Space "Through Time and Space"). 

### Journal entry

    _Despite the significant measures already undertaken, the situation in the outer regions of Tilath na Viell, Tilath na Lia and Tilath na Crob've provinces has not improved. The[Sages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Saevherne "Aen Saevherne") sent to handle the matter in the field have merely succeeded in delaying the advance of [the White Frost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_White_Frost "The White Frost"), which slowly yet surely engulfs more and more territory. We must accept that Tilath na Buhne is now completely lost - the Sages did not reach it in time._
    _I recommended the swift evacuation of the endangered provinces and a strengthening and radicalization of measures intended to seize[Aen Seidhe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Seidhe "Aen Seidhe") territory. Otherwise i must ask to be freed of responsibility for the provinces under my jurisdiction._
    _Inglor Helyanwë,[Viceroy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viceroy "Viceroy") of the Western Shore_
