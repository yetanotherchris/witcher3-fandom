# Nilfgaardian's journal

There are two documents named **Nilfgaardian's journal** in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Depending on who wins the [third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III"), one of the two versions can be found during the "Witcher ending" of the epilogue quest, [Something Ends, Something Begins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)"), at the unmarked [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") camp by the road leading southwest of [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard").

### Journal entry

    _Maintaining discipline in the ranks grows harder by the day — hunger, pestilence, dysentery and word of the[Redanians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army")' approach has wreaked havoc. Second [Lieutenant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Military_ranks "Military ranks") von Dubb put eight soldiers into confinement for subversive activities and spreading enemy propaganda. This raised morale at once, because it meant an immediate increase in biscuit rations._
    _Vague stories of Redanian cruelty towards prisoners of war circulate around camp. Supposedly they flay men alive as skillfully as if they were removing peas from a pod. Compared to these tales Second Lieutenant von Dubb's morning motivational talks seem pale indeed._
    _It is in just such situations that imperial officers must shine as examples and categorically deny such idiotic rumors. I believe, however, that this is having the exact opposite effect of the one desired._

## Item ID

q505_nilf_diary_won1

This version can be found if [Emhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis") wins the war (i.e. if Geralt sided with [Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche "Vernon Roche") and [Thaler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thaler_\(spy\) "Thaler \(spy\)") during Reason of State), on top of the large crate immediately next to the iron chest in the camp. However, its container is invisible and doesn't glow with [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses") \- it is still lootable, but one must know to stand right next to it for the loot tooltip to appear. 

### Journal entry

    _The last muster before marching out lasted three hours. Second Lieutenant von Dubb once again told us of the[Sun Empire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire")'s great triumph and the intricate politics of victory that required leaving [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") to the Temerians. He made it clear that abandoning the occupied lands was essential to maintaining the empire's territorial integrity, explaining it all in great detail, as if wanting to quell any doubts right from the start._
    _Of course, no one intends to ask any questions. Even the densest man in the camp can see through that twaddle._
