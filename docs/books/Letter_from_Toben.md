## Item ID

mq1036_refugee_letter

**Letter from Toben** is a letter in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's only found if one saved [John Verdun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/John_Verdun "John Verdun") during the unmarked quest, [At the Mercy of Strangers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/At_the_Mercy_of_Strangers "At the Mercy of Strangers"), in which case John will hunt down the refugees to a camp and kill them. If this happens, this letter can be found near the back of the camp. 

## Journal entry

    _Dear Mother,_
    _Everything has gone very well so far. I still have a considerable ways to go to the city, but as soon as I get there I will dispatch this letter and set about writing another._
    _I miss you and Father horribly. I am being extremely careful with the coin you gave me. I have not spent hardly a thing. Once I find employment, I will set aside an amount every week, and send you my savings regularly._
    _Tell Yolanda I love her greatly and will send for her soon. To come and be my wife, of course, I spoke with a certain[Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") who told me there are more merchants in the city than ants in the woods, and that scribes are always wanted. I believe he spoke the truth._
    _Your Toben._
    _Give Fido a good belly rub for me._
