## Item ID

mq2052_contract

**Witcher Needed!** is a document and [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Fyresdal\) "Notice board \(Fyresdal\)"), and when read begins [Contract: Skellige's Most Wanted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Skellige%27s_Most_Wanted "Contract: Skellige's Most Wanted"). 

## Journal entry

    _I - the writer of this notice - seek the help of a[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). The work on offer's tough, true, but for a witcher, it should be a breeze. Time's short, so don't tarry. Any interested witchers, look for me outside the inn in Fyresdal._
    _Uggo, son of Olaf_
