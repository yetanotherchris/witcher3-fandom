## Item ID

Gwent Almanac

**A Miraculous Guide to Gwent** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ added as part of [Patch 1.20](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.20_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.20 \(The Witcher 3\)"). It displays the number of [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") cards that may still be won or looted from [unique players](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players"), or purchased in each of the base game's [regions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_locations "The Witcher 3 locations"), as well as the number of cards that may still be won from generic [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") or craftsmen anywhere in the base game's world. This book may effectively be used as a counter to gauge progress of the quest [Collect 'Em All](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Collect_%27Em_All "Collect 'Em All"), but it does not provide specific information about the locations of cards beyond the region in which they can be found. 

It may be obtained by winning at gwent against [Aldert Geert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aldert_Geert "Aldert Geert") at any time after arriving at the [White Orchard inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_inn "White Orchard inn"), but before completing [Lilac and Gooseberries](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lilac_and_Gooseberries "Lilac and Gooseberries"). After the completion of said quest, the book may only be obtained by purchasing it from the [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shop_\(Gildorf\) "Shop \(Gildorf\)") in the [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), which region becomes accessible only after completing the first objective of the [main story](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests") quest [The Nilfgaardian Connection](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nilfgaardian_Connection "The Nilfgaardian Connection"). 

## Journal entry

    _"The tome you hold in your hands, commissioned by the Most Gracious[Duke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Duke "Duke") de Berry, shall make use of [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") most arcane to display which gwent cards are currently missing from your collection. You need but open it and repeat in your mind "How 'bout a round of gwent?" and the following shall appear:_

[The Skellige Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"): 28

[Royal Palace in Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Palace_in_Vizima "Royal Palace in Vizima"): 1

Number of new cards which may still be won from players of no particular renown or skill:: 47_

## Notes

  * _Important:_ Even though the book mentions 47 cards won from random players there are actually 51. The 4 additional cards are the weather cards. 
    * The reason they are not listed is most likely because the base deck already contains 2 of each of them while the guide only lists cards that were not yet obtained.
  * The numbers listed above are the initial values. As gwent cards in a particular region are acquired, the value for that region will decrease.
  * Once the counter of remaining cards for a particular region reaches zero, that line disappears from the book.
  * The single card assigned to Kaer Morhen belongs to [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert") and can actually be obtained prior to going there by playing him when he's at [The Nowhere Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nowhere_Inn "The Nowhere Inn") in Novigrad.

## Bugs

  * Some cards that are listed in the Guide as "Velen" are actually located in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). For example, cards won from merchants in north-eastern part of Novigrad and cards bought from [The Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon")'s innkeeper.
