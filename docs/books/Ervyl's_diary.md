**Ervyl's diary** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be looted from a house in the [Ice Plains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_Plains "Ice Plains") during the main quest [Through Time and Space](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Through_Time_and_Space "Through Time and Space"). 

## Journal entry

    _The 30th of[Birke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Birke "Birke")_
    _This time of year the flowers should be in full bloom, but instead snow covers all. Spring's clearly going to be late. Food will be dear soon, that's certain._
    _The 12th of[Blathe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blathe "Blathe")_
    _It's colder and colder. Snow has been falling non-stop for 37 days now. Folk don't even bother clearing it anymore. There are no more streets, only tunnels dug through the snow._
    _The 21st of[Feainn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feainn "Feainn")_
    _The snow never stops falling. Our larder is bare. At least we can melt snow to slake our thirst - though I don't know how long our wood will last. For now, we're alive._
