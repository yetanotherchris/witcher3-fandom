    _Some devilry's taken hold of our fields, something neither spirit nor phantom. Old Wil swears on his mother's grave it's like to be[Jenny o' the Woods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jenny_o%27_the_Woods "Jenny o' the Woods").
