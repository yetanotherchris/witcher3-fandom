**Redania: A Power on the Rise** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It should appear as random loot in some treasure chests and two bookshelves in [Dijkstra's bathhouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund%27s_Bathhouse "Sigismund's Bathhouse"). However, due to a randomization bug none of the possible containers ever select it, and it can only be obtained through console commands. 

## Journal entry

    _[Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") is a kingdom belonging to the so-called [League of Northern States](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"). It is located between the [Buina](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Buina "Buina") and [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)") rivers, bordered by [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") to the south and [Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir") and [Caingorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caingorn "Caingorn") to the north. To its east lies [Kaedwen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedwen "Kaedwen"), and to its southeast - [Aedirn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aedirn "Aedirn"). Its capital is [Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tretogor "Tretogor"), yet the land's most famous cities are [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), home to the enclave of culture and learning that is the [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"), and [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), which, while a free city, stands on Redanian soil._
    _The cult of the nobility runs strong in Redania. The social stratum possesses more privileges here than in any nearby realm. Great differences in wealth exist among the gentry, but Redanian law cares for birth not property, making a well-born beggar the legal equal of the richest magnate. Those of high birth and great wealth draw the latter primarily from the grain trade and pedigreed[horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") husbandry. This last the Redanians excel at, outdoing all other nations - Redanian steeds are unsurpassed. The Redanian [cavalry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cavalry "Cavalry") is thus among the best formations of its kind in the [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World"), and with the country's nobility filling its ranks it is the heart of the Redanian [armed forces](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army"). As a consequence of the nobility's privileged position, the peasantry is treated worse here than in neighboring realms, and only the king's iron fist keeps them from rebelling._
    _The Redanian army consists of thirty-five thousand armed men, which number includes a core of four thousand elite[knights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Knight "Knight"). Soon after the outbreak of the [Third War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III") against [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"), Redania's forces were further supplemented with Temerian and Kaedweni regiments, as well as with [mercenary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mercenary "Mercenary") troops from [Poviss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Poviss "Poviss") and Kovir. Indeed, one can now accurately speak of a united army of the North led by King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")._
    _The Redanian treasury relies on the profitable trade with Novigrad to fill its coffers, with wheat as the chief staple crop and main commodity. Redania has it in abundance, thus the country is oft called the granary of the North, its landscape consisting almost entirely of rolling waves of grain. Yet despite the land's natural bounty, Redania's economy has been in crisis since war with Nilfgaard broke out once more. The tumult and desperation has led in recent days to the cult of the[Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") gaining more and more adherents._
