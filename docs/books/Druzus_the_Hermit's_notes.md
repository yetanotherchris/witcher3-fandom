**Druzus the Hermit's notes** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ which can be found in a hut during [A Dog's Life](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dog%27s_Life "A Dog's Life"). 

## Journal entry

    _For years and years I succeeded in living a quiet life, far from the stink and cry of the big city._
    _When I entered this voluntary exile, I left the whole world behind and hoped it would do the same to me. Yet lately I have noticed someone observing me. Times are unsettled. War turns men into wild beasts. I suspect is is not a friendly neighbor keeping an eye on me._
    _[Barylka](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barylka "Barylka"), the only companion in my solitude, stands by the door at night and barks. What has happened to Alexis, Hector, Gizmo, Dex? Hunger drives men to do the worst – perhaps bandits now prowl the ares? Fools. They know I have nothing,_
    _Tomorrow I will attempt to explain everything to them._
