**Contract on... Gods Know What On!** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Journal entry

    _Good Folk!_
    _Walking the woods recently I spied some odd tracks and couldn't for the life of me figure out what left them, even though I've many a hunt under my belt. You know me, I'm not one to make a fuss, but I can't help but reckon they bode ill and we all should be on guard. And if anyone's seen something or figures he can identify the tracks, come see me. There's a reward._
    _-[Huntsman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hunter_\(Lindenvale\) "Hunter \(Lindenvale\)") of [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale")_
