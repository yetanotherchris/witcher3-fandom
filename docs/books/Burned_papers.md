## Item ID

mq0004_burnt_papers

**Burned papers** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be looted from the hut during [A Frying Pan, Spick and Span](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Frying_Pan,_Spick_and_Span "A Frying Pan, Spick and Span"). 

Its contents refer to plans potentially revealed later during [A Deadly Plot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Deadly_Plot "A Deadly Plot") and, also becoming obvious later, it was written by [Thaler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thaler_\(spy\) "Thaler \(spy\)").
