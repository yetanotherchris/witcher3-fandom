**Fauna of the Northern Realms, Volume 2** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When first read, it provides the [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entry for [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"). 

It should be available as random loot in both the base game and the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, but due to a randomization bug it only and always appears in one single place in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") \- a small bundle container at the [Duchaton Crest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Duchaton_Crest "Duchaton Crest") camp, on the opposite side of the big chest under the tent. 

## Journal entry

    _Bears dwell in the wildernesses of the[North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"), in its high-mountain regions and in areas gripped with eternal frost. These animals can even be found where the [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") eye sees nothing but ice and snow - for they are skilled omnivorous hunters and gatherers always able to find nourishment of some sort, even in the most hostile conditions._
    _Bears are best avoided. While they are not usually aggressive, it is always a good policy to stay out of the way of any massive and fast-moving animals, in particular ones equipped with sharp claws and teeth._
