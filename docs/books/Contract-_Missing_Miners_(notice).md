**Contract: Missing Miners** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Journal entry

    _It'll not come as news to most, but let me repeat for the ignorant: a few of our lads went looking for ore in the mountains and never returned. They must've run into trouble, and since those brave warriors couldn't handle it themselves, it must've been serious trouble indeed. That's why I'm looking for a brave man to go up and suss out what's become of them. Whoever does so will get[gold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") to match his glory._
    _-[Gjarr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gjarr "Gjarr"), village elder of [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare")_
