**Dirt-smeared letter** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It is related to a battle between [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") and pirates that they were intended to have a conflict with. Data for almost all of it is still in the game files, largely unused but a few things can still be seen during gameplay - a poster with a "call to arms" type of message can still be seen in [Holmstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein "Holmstein"), Drummond guards appear at the [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") to the northeast after it is cleared, and there are two [drakkars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drakkar "Drakkar") on the nearby shore - all remnants from the same larger event or quest. The battle would've taken place between the drakkars and the bandit camp and the "living world" around Holmstein would change significantly after its end, with related chatter by the locals, celebratory posters, and [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") infesting the battlefield. 

This particular letter was supposed to appear in the middle of three southern huts at Holmstein, presumably after the battle. Another one written by a pirate was also supposed to be found, on the beach between the two ships, but was either never added in or erased after the content in question was scrapped.
