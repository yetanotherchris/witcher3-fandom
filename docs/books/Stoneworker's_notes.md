## Item ID

mq1014_old_mine_journal

**Stoneworker's notes** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is part of the [Looters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Looters "Looters") (2nd group) quest, found in a chest near the mine entrance, but doesn't do anything. 

## Journal entry

    _13 VII 1271_
    _[Vimme Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vimme_Vivaldi "Vimme Vivaldi")'s order: twenty blocks measuring 40 x 70 x 30, three 55 x 60 x 40. _
    _Note: uniform stone, no inclusions, to be used for bank counters._
    _Order for the[Vegelbuds' residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence"): five slabs measuring 120 x 80 x 5. Note: polished surface. For decorative purposes._
    _14 VII 1271_
    _Order fulfilled. Collection arranged for 18 VIII 1271. Payment to be delivered in species._
    _To purchase: tracer x 3, wooden hammer x 5, leveler x 2, chisel x 4._
    _18 VIII 1271_
    _[Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") has crossed the border. Jorge did not come to retrieve his order. I've nothing with which to pay the boys for their work. They say they're gathering their tools and going to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")._
    _20 VIII 1271_
    _The road to Novigrad is closed. Blockades along the[Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)"). Redanians on one side, Nilfgaard on the other. The horizon burns at night. Pillars of smoke can be seen during the day. We'll barricade ourselves inside. Others want to join us, but we haven't enough food so we must send them away._
    _21 VIII 1271_
    _The[Redanians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") are ordering us to open the gates. They want to station a garrison in the quarry. No mention of what will become of us, and war rages all around. They say a major battle is brewing. We're staying put. The Redanian division's small, no siege equipment, and our palisade's strong. Perhaps they will give up and leave._
