## Item ID

**The Lodge of Sorceresses** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be bought from [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz"). 

It should also be available as random loot in approximately 15 locations, but due to a randomization bug, the containers there always select a different book to appear. 

## Journal entry

    _Just as warty slime-slathered[toads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Frog "Frog") croak out into the fetid swamp in search of kindred company, so too do [sorceresses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") seek their own kind, driven to gather and scheme against all that is good and worthy. The coven gathering the worst of them - the knab of those utterly depraved and swollen thick with the pus of [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") \- was the [Lodge of Sorceresses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses")._
    _The first[fiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend") of this Lodge were [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart"), Lady of [Montecalvo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Montecalvo "Montecalvo"), [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") of [Maribor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maribor_\(city\) "Maribor \(city\)"), [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") of [Carreras](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carreras "Carreras"), [Margarita Laux-Antille](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margarita_Laux-Antille "Margarita Laux-Antille"), [Síle de Tansarville](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/S%C3%ADle_de_Tansarville "Síle de Tansarville") of [Creyden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Creyden_\(city\) "Creyden \(city\)"), [Sabrina Glevissig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sabrina_Glevissig "Sabrina Glevissig") of [Ard Carraigh](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Carraigh "Ard Carraigh"), [Fringilla Vigo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fringilla_Vigo "Fringilla Vigo") of [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair"), [Francesca Findabair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francesca_Findabair "Francesca Findabair") and [Ida Emean](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ida_Emean_aep_Sivney "Ida Emean aep Sivney"), [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") women, [Assire var Anahid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Assire_var_Anahid "Assire var Anahid") and [Yennefer of Vengerberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")._
    _On the summit of[Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") they gathered, witches all, to swear fealty to their [devils](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil "Devil") and [demons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Demon "Demon"), their masters and lovers, and to promise to bring doom upon those living in peace in the [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"). 'Twas they who stood behind the murders of [Demavend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Demavend_III "Demavend III"), [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") of [Aedirn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aedirn "Aedirn"), [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest"), King of [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") and many other noble men besides. 'Twas they who sent [plague](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Catriona_plague "Catriona plague") and pox against decent, [god-fearing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Religion "Religion") folk._
    _Yet take comfort, dear reader, for their deeds shall not go unpunished. Our gracious leader[Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"), fifth of his name, saw through their lies and swore their deaths, deaths as inevitable as they shall be painful._
