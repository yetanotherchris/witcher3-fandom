## Item ID

mq1013_notice

**Witcher wanted!** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can start the secondary quest [Witcher Wannabe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Wannabe "Witcher Wannabe"). 

It only appears on notice boards some time after completing [Contract: The Merry Widow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Merry_Widow "Contract: The Merry Widow"). 

## Journal entry

    _During the time it takes to complete the contract, said witcher'll will have to right to room and board in the ealdorman's hut (costs deducted from final pay)._
