## Item ID

flotsam_experiment

**Witcher experimentations - cont.** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is a letter penned by [Farid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farid "Farid") and addressed to [Gaspar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gaspar "Gaspar") that discusses their long-term experiment on [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") in [Flotsam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flotsam "Flotsam") during _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_. The letter is only obtainable if a [saved game import](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Saved_game_import "Saved game import") was carried over from the second game, in which Geralt has to have agreed to the experiment by drinking their unknown substance. With those conditions satisfied, the letter can be found at the base of a statue outside the main entrance to [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"). 

## Journal entry

    _5/20/[1271](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1271 "Timeline/Games")_
    _My Dear Gaspar,_
    _We cannot rest on our laurels. True, we managed to convince the[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") to drink the concoction we had brewed without much difficulty, but now we must gather information. Somehow I doubt this Gerard, or whatever his name was, truly will truly keep an experiment diary, even if by some miracle he is literate. And without hard data, the [Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")'s governors will never renew our grant - believe me, I've been down that road before!_
    _To keep one step ahead of events, I've already taken appropriate action. To be precise, while in Flotsam I hired a spy who will follow our witcher's every move and observe his deeds, especially those performed in the alcove. If our calculations are correct, within a year's time his sterility will be reversed - proof of which fact should come quickly in the form of expanding female bellies, if the rumors about him contain even a grain of truth._
    _The spy's wage is costly, true, but I'm convinced this investment will yield dividends. I believe a potion which restores the ability to reproduce to those who have lost it due to magic or alchemic experiments will be in high demand - and earn us a fortune._
    _Vivat Academia,  
–Farid_
    _P.S. Just remember - not a peep about this around the department, or the ethics committee will flay us alive._
    _P.P.S. I've received the spy's first report. The witcher is headed to[Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne"). Perhaps it's a good time to visit?_
