## Item ID

q310_explorer_note

**Discarded letter** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable though console commands. 

It was supposed to be found in the [Oxenfurt sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_sewers "Oxenfurt sewers") during [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape") quest, but was never put in any container there. 

## Journal entry

    _To whomever finds this letter!_
    _My name is Grimmer Toug and I am a treasure hunter. I am trapped in the caves underneath[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") \- I entered through one of the city wells, but the cord broke and I cannot go out that way. I placed this letter in the current of the underground river, banking on the hope that it joins the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)") at some point. I beg anyone who finds this plea to come to my aid!_
    _Grimmer Toug_
