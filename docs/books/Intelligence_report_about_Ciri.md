## Item ID

lore_inteligence_report_about_ciri

**Intelligence report about Ciri** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It appears to be the basis of what [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") summarizes to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") during the main quest [Imperial Audience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Audience "Imperial Audience"). Thus it likely should've appeared in the same [Royal Palace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Palace_\(Vizima\) "Royal Palace \(Vizima\)") room, but there are no clues as to whether it was intentionally removed or if it was never placed due to oversight.
