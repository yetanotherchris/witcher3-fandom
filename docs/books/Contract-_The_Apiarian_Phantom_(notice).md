## Item ID

**Contract: The Apiarian Phantom** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Journal entry

    _Calling all[bounty-hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter"), trackers and [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")!_
    _Misfortune has fallen on[Honeyfill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks") and the apiary which for five generations has provided a livelihood for the entire Meiersdorf family! The apiarian phantom has descended upon us and has begun to destroy our hives, slay our bees and wreak general havoc all around! I shall be grateful for any help you can provide and guarantee a reward awaits you for work well done._
    _-[Holoferens Meiersdorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holofernes_Meiersdorf "Holofernes Meiersdorf") & Sons_

## Associated quest

## Notes

  * Interestingly, this item isn't added to Geralt's inventory when picked up from the notice board, and so it can only be read directly from it. However, unlike other notice board postings with similar behavior, this one can be obtained as an item through console commands.
