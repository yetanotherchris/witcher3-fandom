## Item ID

q210_avallach_notes_02

**Caranthir's Progress** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , written by [Avallac'h](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Avallac%27h "Avallac'h"). 

It can be found on top of a cabinet in the first room after entering Avallac'h's laboratory, during the quest [Child of the Elder Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Child_of_the_Elder_Blood "Child of the Elder Blood"). During the entirety of the [Battle Preparations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_Preparations "Battle Preparations") quest, there is also optional dialogue with Avallac'h, basically summarizing the contents of this document. 

## Journal entry

    _[Caranthir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caranthir_Ar-Feiniel "Caranthir Ar-Feiniel") is undoubtedly the most skilled [navigator](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt_Navigator "Wild Hunt Navigator") I have ever had the chance to train. Yet his rash temper and his tendency to take foolish risks has called into question the possibility of ever using him for our purposes. I know [Eredin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eredin_Br%C3%A9acc_Glas "Eredin Bréacc Glas") is ready to put him through the trials now, which Caranthir in his pride would surely take as a sign of trust, and not indifference. The fate of his predecessor clearly was not warning enough for him, or perhaps he truly does have that much faith in his own powers.  
In light of the time I have devoted to him, I would like Caranthir to succeed._
