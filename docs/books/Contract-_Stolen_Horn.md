## Item ID

mq2001_notice_board_holmstein

**Contract: Stolen Horn** is a [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Picking it up from [Holmstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein "Holmstein")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Holmstein\) "Notice board \(Holmstein\)") starts the secondary quest [A Hallowed Horn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Hallowed_Horn "A Hallowed Horn"), but for unknown reasons it can only be read directly from the board itself and isn't added as an item to the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory"), unless obtained through console commands. 

## Journal entry

    _I,[Holger Blackhand](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holger_Blackhand "Holger Blackhand"), do hereby announce the following:_
    _The warriors who brought shame on themselves and their line at the[Battle of the Sodden Fields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Sodden_Hill "Battle of Sodden Hill")[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] have shown that the punishment I meted out to them was far too lenient! Before departing [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"), they stole the [horn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holger_Blackhand%27s_horn "Holger Blackhand's horn") of my ancestors from my hall. I've entrusted the task of retrieving it to my steward, [Mathios](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mathios "Mathios")._
    _Whoever brings him the horn will taste of my generosity. Whoever helps the cowards and thieves who took it will taste my wrath._
