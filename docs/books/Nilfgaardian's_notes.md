## Item ID

lw_gr7_soldiers_note

**Nilfgaardian's notes** is a book and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted from a [Nilfgaardian soldier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army")'s corpse at the hidden treasure marker north of [Ferry Station](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ferry_Station "Ferry Station"). Once looted, the [treasure hunt quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_treasure_hunts "The Witcher 3 treasure hunts"), [Lost Goods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lost_Goods "Lost Goods"), will begin. 

## Journal entry

    _Join the army, they said. You'll kill[Nordlings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"), they said, you'll carry the [Great Sun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Sun "Great Sun") banner to the [Dragon Mountains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon_Mountains "Dragon Mountains"), you'll make the [emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperator "Imperator") proud!_
    _So I enlisted. And they sent me to[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). If this is what the whole [North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms") is like, then I swear on all the [gods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Religion "Religion"), I don't have a clue why we're even bothering. Nothing but filth, clap and graves._
    _I've been sitting here for two months. Haven't seen a single[Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") yet. Instead of fighting, they got us running errands, each stupider than the last. Now the sergeant's sent us to find some chest. Baubles robbed from the Nordlings, seems. Fell off a wagon as the transport was heading south._
    _We've been wandering this swamp for three days now. Only thing we've found was a hole-riddled boot and a[cow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cow "Cow")'s skull. What a waste... but if we come back without the chest, they'll send us to the hole and deduct three weeks' wages, so we're going to keep searching. For the glory of the fatherland._
