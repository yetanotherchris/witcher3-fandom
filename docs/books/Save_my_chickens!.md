## Item ID

**Save my chickens!** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't seem to be found during gameplay. 

## Journal entry

    _I need me a[hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hunter "Hunter") or a tracker - blast it, even a smith or baker, long as he knows a thing or two about [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)"). And if he knows how to kill them - that's even better, for the furry devils have infested the woods near my garden and the [Honeyfill Meadswork](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks")[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] and won't give me a moment's peace._
    _If you're keen on the job and willing to help, come look for[Yanina](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yanina "Yanina")._
