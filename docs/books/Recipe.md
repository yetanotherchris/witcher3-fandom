## Item ID

lw_sb5_giggler_leader_note

**Recipe** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It was supposed to be found in a chest at the cannibals' [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") east of [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle"), along with [diagram: Novigrad longsword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Novigrad_longsword "Diagram: Novigrad longsword") and some [diamond dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diamond_dust "Diamond dust"). However, whether due to oversight or intentionally, the chest in question doesn't appear during gameplay. 

## Journal entry

    _Cut open belly. Pull out offal and wash. If the liver's wrinkled, toss it. Cut everything into small bits. Toss in boiling[water](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water "Water"). Break the bones, extract the marrow. Add. Simmer. Throw in onion, carrot, beans, marjoram. Eat. Eat. Eat._
