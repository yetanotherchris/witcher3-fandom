**Scribbled document** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be looted from the old house near the [windmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard").
