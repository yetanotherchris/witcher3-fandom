## Item ID

Hybrid Monsters vol 1

**Journey to the End of the World** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Reading this tome provides the [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") entries for [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren"), [erynias](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia "Erynia"), [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy"), and [succubi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Succubus "Succubus"), as well as the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Succubus_decoction "Torn-out page: Succubus decoction") for [succubus decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Succubus_decoction "Succubus decoction"), and the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Ekhidna_decoction "Torn-out page: Ekhidna decoction") for [ekhidna decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna_decoction "Ekhidna decoction"). 

It should be available as random loot, but due to a randomization bug can only and always be found in the northern hut at [The Orphans of Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog"), where [Mikula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikula_\(orphan\) "Mikula \(orphan\)") is hiding during [Ladies of the Wood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ladies_of_the_Wood "Ladies of the Wood") if you choose to play hide and seek with the children. It can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kaer_Trolde\) "Herbalist \(Kaer Trolde\)") at [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Marcus T.K. Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson") in his [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Books_and_Scrolls "Books and Scrolls") on [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * Books [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Merchant \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")

## Journal entry

    _Sirens, the harpies' distant relatives, are winged monsters found in the[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") Archipelago, most frequently on unpopulated isles or in the inaccessible coastal areas of populated ones. According to legend they are daughters of the mythcial winged [giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giant "Giant") [Hräsvelg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hr%C3%A4svelg "Hräsvelg"), who lives on the edge of the [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World") and births gales with the waving of his wings. Hräsvelg's daughters seek out the company of giants and often make their nests near their lairs._
    _These creatures attack boats and ships from the air, tearing sails and rigging to shreds and rendering vessels immobile. At times they will snatch men or cattle from the deck and drop them from great heights to their death. They also attack travelers trekking across isolated beaches._
