## Item ID

lore_journals_from_urskar_3

**Memoirs** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It is the 3rd of 7 books collectively known as "journals from [Urskar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Urskar "Urskar")", detailing what led to [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik")'s destruction. Small bits from that still remain in dialogues during [The Lord of Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Lord_of_Undvik "The Lord of Undvik") quest, but the overall story and a lot of other content related to the island have been removed from the game. 

## Journal entry

    _[Crach an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite") invited our [jarl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harald_an_Tordarroch "Harald an Tordarroch") on a hunt. Folk were afraid at first, thinking this meant the [Ard Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") were plotting something. But [Tordarrochs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tordarroch "Clan Tordarroch") don't get scared without damn good reason. Rightly so - this was no ambush. The jarls killed two [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"), half a dosen [moose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deer "Deer") and a smattering of lesser game. During the after-hunt feast Crach announced he wanted to patch things up between Clans [an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") and Tordarroch. To seal this new brotherhood, they've planned a joint raid on [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire")._
    _This is a good sign for Undvik. This friendship will help us through tough times. And they say tough times are on their way._
