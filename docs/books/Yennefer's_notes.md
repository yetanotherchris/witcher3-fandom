## Item ID

q401_yen_journal_2

**Yennefer's notes** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It was likely meant to be found somewhere during the main quest [Ugly Baby](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ugly_Baby "Ugly Baby"), but the container that has it doesn't spawn. 

## Journal entry

    _Speargrass, witchgrass, tear grass. Order doesn't matter?_
    _[Forktail spinal fluid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail_spinal_fluid "Forktail spinal fluid"), 3-4 tablespoons, room temperature._
    _Sad Albert - repair?_
    _[Ida](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ida_Emean_aep_Sivney "Ida Emean aep Sivney") \- curse. Remind [G.](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to behave._

## Notes

  * In game, witchgrass and tear grass are referred to as "Wildrye Juice" and "Mother's Tears", respectively. See also [The Decoctions of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Decoctions_of_the_Grasses "The Decoctions of the Grasses").
