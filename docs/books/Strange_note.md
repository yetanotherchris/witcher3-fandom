## Item ID

lw_sb20_chest_note

**Strange note** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It is supposed to be found in the chest at the [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") between [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp") and [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"), but whether intentionally or due to a bug, it doesn't appear. The camp itself is supposed to have some special "ambush" mechanics, but those can't be observed either.
