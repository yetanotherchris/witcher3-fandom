## Journal entry

    _I am currently taking on pupils for lessons in the playing of[gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). I shall only teach those who display discipline, a logical mindset and the will to learn. Those with special aptitude are particularly welcome. Pupils must acquire and supply their own decks._
    _I am also seeking more experienced players as partners to provide a challenge and a chance for the mutual improvement of our skills._
    _–[Ermion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mousesack "Mousesack") , [druid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid")_
