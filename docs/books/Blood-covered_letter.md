**Blood-covered letter** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

This letter is found on a corpse at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower"), inside the main tower. However, it can be difficult to obtain as it can only be retrieved before the [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") there is cleared of [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul"), which constantly keep [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") in combat mode, which prevents looting. As soon as the last ghoul is killed, the area is liberated and cleared of any corpses, including the one with the letter on it.
