**Angrily scrawled notes** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be looted from the [Amavet Fortress Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amavet_Fortress_Ruins "Amavet Fortress Ruins") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

Though not part of the quest and being located way out of its relevant locations, this actually points out one of the deserter camps visited during [Dirty Funds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dirty_Funds "Dirty Funds"). 

## Journal entry

    _What am I supposed to fucking die for?[Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria")? Da died for that, so died my three brothers, and what'd that get them? Fuck all, that's what. Not even a decent burial. Da always said a wise man learns from others' mistakes, so here I am, learning from his - and instead of dying for Temeria, I'm going to rob [those](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") who killed Temeria's sons. We've pitched a camp, got us a great spot, middle of the woods, near that [old mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)"). Now we're all set to go out on our first raid._
