**Applied Golemancy** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which was meant to be found in the library during the secondary quest [The Tower Outta Nowheres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Tower_Outta_Nowheres "The Tower Outta Nowheres"), where it can still be examined and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has a unique line for it, but it cannot be picked up. 

Its contents are still present in the game files, but were never attached to an [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") item definition. 

## Journal entry

    _[Golems](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem") lack independent will and obey orders unconditionally – this is what makes them practical tools for both domestic and public service. A golem never wavers, hesitates or doubts, nor does it have any of the bodily needs of other creatures fit for servitude. Golems' strength and endurance exceed that of any other humanoid being, and furthermore they do not complain, suffer from bodily aches or demand payment of any kind, requiring only the occasional bit of specialized conversation work to maintain their labor._
