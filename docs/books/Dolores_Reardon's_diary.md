## Item ID

sq102_dolores_diary

**Dolores Reardon's diary** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found during the secondary quest [The Fall of the House of Reardon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Fall_of_the_House_of_Reardon "The Fall of the House of Reardon"), in the eastern house at [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor"), the one with the breakable door. 

Despite being the only item not functionally required for anything during the quest, it is also the only one that can't be found after the quest completes and [Dolores Reardon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dolores_Reardon "Dolores Reardon") returns to the estate. 

## Journal entry

    _The 3rd of[Feainn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feainn "Feainn")_
    _Hot summer days have come earlier than usual this year.[Humbert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Humbert_Reardon "Humbert Reardon") has grown unbearable. He gets angry whenever I accompany the milkmaids to the river. He says I'm not to fraternize with the help. But what makes him truly furious is when I converse with Yan and Fred. He claims a well-born lady should not mix with men of lower rank. That doing so undermines his rightful authority over them. Perhaps once the summer swelter begins to fade he'll become more tolerable._
    _The 20th of Feainn_
    _The boredom weighs heavier and heavier upon me. I love my brother and am glad we did not sell the estate after our parents died, but at times I daydream of what it would be like to live somewhere else, with other people..._
    _The 43rd of Feainn_
    _We've a guest. He's the son of a lord from somewhere near[Gors Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gors_Velen "Gors Velen"). A hunt took him across our land and he asked for lodging. Oh, what joy! Finally, something is happening!_
    _The 15th of[Lammas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lammas "Lammas")_
    _The time has come to admit it – I love[Roderick](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roderick_\(Gors_Velen\) "Roderick \(Gors Velen\)"). And I feel he loves me too. I hope Humbert will be pleased!_
    _The 18th of Lammas_
    _I told Humbert about Roderick and me. He flew into a rage. He said that match was out of the question. Perhaps I can convince him otherwise._
    _The 21st of Lammas_
    _Humbert remains implacable, but I shan't abandon the love of my life for him. If I'm left with no other choice, I will run away with Roderick._
