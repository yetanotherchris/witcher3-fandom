## Item ID

lore_elder_blood

**The Elder Blood** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is scripted to appear in the following places: 

  * A mattress in the [the Mysterious Elf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Mysterious_Elf "The Mysterious Elf")'s hideout during [Wandering in the Dark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_in_the_Dark "Wandering in the Dark")
  * In the underground library during [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never")
  * On the second floor of [Vilmerius Hospital](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vilmerius_Hospital "Vilmerius Hospital"), outside of [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla")'s room during [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins")

It should also be available as random loot in approximately 15 base game locations, but due to a randomization bug, the containers there always select a different book to appear. In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, the book can actually be found as random loot on bookshelves in houses around [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"). 

It can also be purchased from [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") or the [Ducal Camerlengo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rafael_de_Surmann "Rafael de Surmann") in [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair"). 

## Journal entry

    _What exactly is the[Elder Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Blood "Elder Blood")?  
According to some, it is a powerful [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") [curse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Curse "Curse") passed from generation to generation. Those in whose veins the infamous Hen Ichaer flows are said to carry death and destruction within them, to sow hatred and disdain in the hearts of men. It was from this contaminated blood that [Ithlinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ithlinne "Ithlinne") prophesied an avenger would be born, a destroyer of nations and worlds.  
Others claim the Elder Blood is an extremely rare inherited talent, granting control over time and space to a degree unattainable even to elven [Sages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sage "Sage"). Sadly, few bearers of this gift have been able to control it in full. This merely partial mastery inevitably leads to sudden outbursts of the [Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Power "Power") that take the form of unpredictable, uncontrollable and thus extremely dangerous explosions. Behind the dark legends about the Curse of the Elder Blood lies the truth of these tragic cases.  
For obvious reason the bearers of the Elder Blood were always subjects of great interest to the [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World")'s mighty and to [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") alike. The former counted on the truth of Ithlinne's prophecy, on an avenger being born who could destroy worlds, and who they could thus use for their purposes. As for the latter, they hoped to harness the magic of Hen Ichaer to broaden their own knowledge and powers.  
Ultimately, however, all these plans were for naught - the Elder Blood line broke off with the disappearance of the heiress to the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") crown, [Cirilla Fiona Ellen Riannon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")..._
