**Notice! Delay affecting orders placed with Anselm!** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't seem to be found during gameplay. 

## Journal entry

    _To everyone who has put an order with[Anselm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anselm_\(merchant\) "Anselm \(merchant\)") recently and knows how to read:_
    _We kindly inform you that orders placed before the last full moon will be delivered with a slight delay._
    _The delay is due to factors beyond the control of the merchant Anselm (the ongoing hostilities have made hiring an escort for the journey to[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") a nigh unto impossible feat), and therefore no discounts or refunds will be given._
    _-Anselm_
