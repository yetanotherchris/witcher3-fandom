**Radovid V - Defender of the North** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It should appear as random loot in some treasure chests and two bookshelves in [Dijkstra's bathhouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund%27s_Bathhouse "Sigismund's Bathhouse"). However, due to a randomization bug it can always be found on one exact bookshelf there, and nowhere else. 

## Journal entry

    _(...) and so the[emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor")'s [brutes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") crossed the [Yaruga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yaruga "Yaruga")'s waters. Yet this splashing was but a foretaste of the ocean of [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") blood they would yet spill. Black has swarmed over our sweet Temerian soil, like ants swarming a drop of honey fallen in their path. [Emhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis")'s cutthroats adore their master and long to emulate his desecrations. The [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") onslaught will spare no one. Under its sun-adorned banners hide dark hordes, cackling with anticipation, gleefully practicing how they will prance on the graves of our elders, women and children alike..._
    _Good folk of the[North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms")! The time has come to unite! Forget our past feuds and quarrels! There is but one foe, one despicable to us all! Let us spare no blood, for each drop spilled might save the life of a brothers._
    _Yet is there any man who will not yield before their frightful charge, before that mass clad in plate black as pitch and stinking of the blood of the raped and murdered...?_
    _I tell you there is, brothers and sisters. There is one man whom the loathsome foe frightens not one bit. A man who, armed with wisdom, courage and cunning, can give fight to the southern plague. That man's name is[Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"). The Stern, they call him, and in this we are blessed, for only such a man can turn back the deluge of iron and steel, send it rushing back whence it came._
    _Mothers, weep not for your sons! Death for a noble cause is an honor every man should embrace. Let your menfolk gather round the banners of[Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania")'s ruler, for he is no longer just Redania's - he is the proud commander of the united forces of the Northern Realms!_
