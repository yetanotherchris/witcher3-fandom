**The Lonesome World Guide to Ard Skellig** is a readable item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When read, it marks the following [fast travel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") signposts and [harbors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harbor "Harbor") on the map: [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor"), [Eldberg Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eldberg_Lighthouse "Eldberg Lighthouse"), [Redgill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redgill "Redgill"), [Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grotto_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Grotto \(Ard Skellig\)"), [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig"), [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn"), [Holmstein's Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein%27s_Port "Holmstein's Port"), [Ruined Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_Inn "Ruined Inn"). 

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") at the roadside camp between the [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") [crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Crossroads \(Ard Skellig\)") and [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Kaer_Trolde_Harbor\) "Merchant \(Kaer Trolde Harbor\)") at Kaer Trolde Harbor
  * [Wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Drummond_sawmill\) "Merchant \(Drummond sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(Drummond\) "Sawmill \(Drummond\)") south of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund"), after liberating it

## Journal entry

    _What time of year is the best for visiting[Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig")? Any time. To whom is such a sojourn recommended? To anyone who seeks adventure, craves miraculous views and adores charming villages. These last Ard Skellig has in spades: it is the most densely-inhabited isle in the archipelago, though this does not mean it lacks virgin forests or untrammeled landscapes. Particularly worthy of a visit are the villages of Rannvaig, Arinbjorn and, last but not least, [Holmstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein "Holmstein"), one of the most important ports in all the Isles._
    _Anyone lucky enough to enjoy the sea voyage to Arinbjorn will surely notice the majestic lighthouse guiding ships to its harbor. Built at the behest of the famed Jarl[Skjordal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skjordal_Drummond "Skjordal Drummond"), it constitutes one of the most fascinating spots on the isle and offers breathtaking views of the entire region._
    _The most spectacular vista on Ard Skellig, however, must certainly be that of[Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde"), a [fortress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") cut out of the mountain overlooking the bay and serving as home seat to the powerful an Craite clan. According to legend, [Grymmdjarr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grymmdjarr "Grymmdjarr"), mythical Skellige hero and founder of [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite"), carved the fortress with his bare hands (for more on this, see the "History" and "Culture" sections)._
