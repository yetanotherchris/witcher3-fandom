**Heroes of Skellige: Grymmdjarr** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ describing the heroic deeds of semi-legendary [Grymmdjarr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grymmdjarr "Grymmdjarr"). 

It is supposed to be sold by a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Harviken\) "Merchant \(Harviken\)") in [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken"), who however never appears, and it was at some point intended to appear as random loot as well, but no containers in the game are currently able to spawn it. Thus, it can only be obtained through console commands. 

## Journal entry

    _Grymmdjarr, first lord of[Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") and founder of [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite"), was famed for his incredible strength. When he traversed the isle for the first time and found no place worthy of making his seat, he asked [Hemdall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall "Hemdall") to give him the thickest chain he could forge. Once he received the chain, Grymmdjarr dove into the depths of the sea and used it to drag an enormous rock to the surface, placing it on Ard Skellig's north-eastern shore. Then he carved [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") out of this rock with his bare hands, and there it stands to this day, the pride and glory of his clan._
