**Announcement: The Oxenfurt Regatta** is an unseen notice in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can only be obtained via the console. The notice was meant to promote boat racing, which was cut from the final release of the game. 

## Content

    _The[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") Regatta_
    _By writ of our most gracious ruler,[King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"), the [Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy") has been closed indefinitely, but in his limitless generosity our dear king as[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] allowed us to hold our annual regatta nonetheless. Let us not inquire too intently into his reasons - perhaps he mistakenly thought "regatta" meant a particularly creative form of persecuting [nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman"), perhaps he has taken up an interest in water sports - the important part is that we shall race!_
    _We thus invite any interested in taking part to report to the Racemaster clad in appropriate yachting attire, ready to defend the honor of his faculty and bring glory to his Fatherland and Alma Mater, our souls' beloved and loving parents!_
