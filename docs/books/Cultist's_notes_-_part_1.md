## Item ID

gp_prologue_cultist_note01

**Cultist's notes - part 1** is a [cut-content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found on a body at the [Place of Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Place_of_Power "Place of Power") marker between the [Sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(White_Orchard\) "Sawmill \(White Orchard\)") and [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), only when clipping out of the reduced world borders during the empress version of the [Something Ends, Something Begins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)") quest. 

## Journal entry

    _The road to purification leads through pain._
    _Whosoever shall read my notes, know that we have found liberation. The Prophet teaches that the pox was sent by[demons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Demon "Demon"), and the gods have turned from us. The only way to cleanse the disease is to placate the demons.  
To conciliate them, by doing what they ask of us._
    _The gods have turned from us. May our new lords be praised through all eternity._

## Notes

  * It is unknown exactly what these notes were used for, though their contents suggest relation to the cut plague questline. However, in the winter White Orchard map, the Place of Power is replaced by a sacrificial circle, and a dead body.
