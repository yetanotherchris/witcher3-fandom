## Item ID

sq108_griffon_contract

**Griffin in the Highlands** is a document and [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It appears on the [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Notice board \(Crow's Perch\)") at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") after [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") delivers the [High-quality smithing tools](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/High-quality_smithing_tools "High-quality smithing tools") during [Master Armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Armorers "Master Armorers"). 

## Journal entry

    _Read close if you want to earn good coin and help your fellow men!_
    _In the hills north of[Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), there's some sort of beast lurking about, a [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)"), it would seem. If you kill it, bring the trophy to the [quartermaster of Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quartermaster_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Quartermaster \(Crow's Perch\)") and you'll get a fitting reward._
    _And if you don't give a whit about your fellow men and don't have the bollocks to face a griffin, stay far away from that place, or else you'll end up as the beast's dinner._
    _Which I suppose means if you're feeling suicidal, you can go right ahead, waltz up to the monster and end your sorry life._
    _-Quartermaster of Crow's Perch_
