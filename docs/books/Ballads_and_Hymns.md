## Item ID

Theatre Glossary vol 1

**Ballads and Hymns** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which contains the lyrics to the song sang by [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla"), when she is first seen at [The Kingfisher Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn") during [Broken Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broken_Flowers "Broken Flowers"). 

It can be found as random loot on most bookshelves and some wooden chests throughout the entire game, but due to a randomization bug one copy is always in Priscilla's upstairs room at the inn, and at least one more is at [Marabella's School for Tots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marabella%27s_School_for_Tots "Marabella's School for Tots"). It can also be purchased from the books [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Merchant \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). 

In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can be found as random loot inside rich houses around [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair").

## Notes

  * In early development of the game, certain "perk" abilities were meant to be acquired by reading books such as this one. The idea was eventually [scrapped](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") and this particular book was repurposed for lore instead, with no clues as to what perk it would've provided.
