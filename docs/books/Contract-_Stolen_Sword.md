**Contract: Stolen Sword** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Journal entry

    _[Olaf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olaf "Olaf"), lead guard at [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde"), would like to make the following known:_
    _Someone's snuck into the[an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") armory and stolen the sword known as [Kuliu](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kuliu "Kuliu"). The yellow-bellied scoundrel who did the deed was seen leaving the village via the southern gate, heading towards the [old fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fort_Grymmdjarr "Fort Grymmdjarr")._
    _Whoever helps recover the sword will receive a reward worthy of his efforts, and whoever helps the thieves - a death worthy of his treachery._

## Associated quest

  * [The Family Blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Family_Blade "The Family Blade")

## Notes

  * Despite the contents of the note pointing to "the old fort" (i.e. Fort Grymmdjarr), the first quest location marked on the map is that of the obelisk southwest of [Miners' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Miners%27_Camp "Miners' Camp"). Only after finding the [Thief's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thief%27s_journal "Thief's journal") there is Fort Grymmdjarr marked as a quest location.
