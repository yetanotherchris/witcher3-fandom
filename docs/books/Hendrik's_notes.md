## Item ID

q101_hendrik_notes

**Hendrik's notes** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It describes [Hendrik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hendrik "Hendrik")'s account of [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") and the events in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen").
