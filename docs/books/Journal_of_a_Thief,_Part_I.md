## Item ID

mq2001_journal_2a

**Journal of a Thief, Part I** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Related to the secondary quest [A Hallowed Horn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Hallowed_Horn "A Hallowed Horn"), this item can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't seem to be found anywhere during gameplay. 

## Journal entry

    _[Holger Blackhand](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holger_Blackhand "Holger Blackhand")... Good one! They should call him Holger Hole-in-the-Head, Holger Two-Left-Hands, or even better, Holger the Cretin. We turned chicken? What's he got in his head where his brains should be – [whale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale "Whale") blubber? Outnumbered ten to one, we were! And up to our knees in mud, whilst they charged down at us from the hill! I understand it's an honor to die in battle – but that weren't no battle. 'Twas a slaughter! He exiled us, the bloated walrus, the [siren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") and [troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll "Troll")-spawned miscreant... And so off we went, but not empty-handed. Since he weren't using his [ancestral horn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holger_Blackhand%27s_horn "Holger Blackhand's horn") to sound no retreat anyways, not even when his shield bearers were dying like flies, well, we took it off his hands. Kjarre took the horn with him and went north, into the fjords, where pirates and smugglers cast anchor atimes – says maybe they'll buy it off us... and then [Mathios](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mathios "Mathios") will never find it. Good. He didn't say a word in our defense when Holger was driving us off, so let him go out in a blaze of shame and disgrace._
