## Item ID

mq1022_notice

This version can be picked up from [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Oxenfurt\) "Notice board \(Oxenfurt\)") to start the secondary quest, [The Volunteer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Volunteer "The Volunteer"). 

### Journal entry

    _CAUTION!_
    _A[troll's been seen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trollololo "Trollololo") (and heard) to the east of Oxenfurt, on the left bank of the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)").[1] Said troll has been known to sing [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") marching tunes. It is suspected this is part of some [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") diversion tactic._
    _It is recommended that this area be avoided until the appropriate branch of[His Majesty](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s services can take care of the problem._
    _In the event of an encounter with the troll, it is recommended to remain immobile, keeping low to the ground and covering one's head with one's arms. There exists a chance that the beast will not then attack, for it will mistake the potential victim thus situated for a rock._
    _Berto Bertolomiu, Captain of the Garrison_

## Item ID

mq1006_notice

This version relates to the unmarked quest [Harassing a Troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harassing_a_Troll "Harassing a Troll"). However, it doesn't seem to be found on any notice boards, despite its contents suggesting that it should appear at the [Inn at the Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads "Inn at the Crossroads"). 

### Journal entry

    _Fellow countrymen! Travelers and locals!_
    _If any of you get to feeling like wandering around these parts, pay extra care near the northern edge of our isle, for a troll's known to lurk thereabouts. He doesn't seem threatening, just growls from time to time.  
He knows a bit of [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") speech and says his name is [Borys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boris_\(troll\) "Boris \(troll\)")[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")], but we all know how it is with trolls - you never can tell what thoughts are brewing in those thick skulls. So be careful!_
    _-Innkeep._
