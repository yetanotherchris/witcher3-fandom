## Item ID

mq1060_third_letter

**Fifth letter from Reinald** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It's found on the entrance door into the salt cavern in the [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit") mine.
