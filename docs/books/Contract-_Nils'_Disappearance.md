**Contract: Nils' Disappearance** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and found on [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Svorlag\) "Notice board \(Svorlag\)"). 

## Journal entry

    _To anyone who might know anything!_
    _Nils from our village - he's disappeared! It's been many a day now and he's still nowheres to be seen. So if you know what's become of him, don't keep it to yourself, come see me and let me know, even if the truth's grim and dark, for my sister, Britt, who was his betrothed, she's going out of her mind with worry, crying seas of tears and such. I swear to[Hemdall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall "Hemdall"), whoever brings me news will get an honorable reward._
    _-Kevan_
