## Item ID

mh202_contract

**Contract: Monster on the High Road** is a [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board") posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Larvik\) "Notice board \(Larvik\)"), and when read begins [Contract: Strange Beast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Strange_Beast "Contract: Strange Beast").
