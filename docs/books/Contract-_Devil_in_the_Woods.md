**Contract: Devil in the Woods** is a notice board posting and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on either [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Cunny_of_the_Goose\) "Notice board \(Cunny of the Goose\)") or [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners")' [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Farcorners\) "Notice board \(Farcorners\)") and is needed to turn in the quest [Contract: Lord of the Wood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Lord_of_the_Wood "Contract: Lord of the Wood"). 

## Journal entry

    _Dear[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), sir (if any be present hereabouts, that is)_
    _I've a hunch - eh, what am I saying - I'm dead certain that the woods I was lucky enough to gain possession of - or at least, I felt lucky about that till recently - that these woods are home to a[cruel evil, a devil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kernun "Kernun") of some kind. I hired a gang of [dwarven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") loggers not long back to harvest lumber. Strapping lads, every last one having cut down many a mighty tree in his life. So you can imagine my surprise when said loggers disappeared, all save one, and he claimed to have seen a monster. I've marked out a bounty for freeing my stretch of woods from all foul creatures, and dearly hope that soon I will have the pleasure of giving it to someone._
    _Sincerely,_
    _-[Brean Hotsch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brean_Hotsch "Brean Hotsch")_
