**Notice about mysterious tower** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Intended as a starting point for the quest [The Tower Outta Nowheres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Tower_Outta_Nowheres "The Tower Outta Nowheres"), it exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but for unknown reasons doesn't appear during gameplay. 

## Journal entry

    _Fair folk!_
    _If you happen to be heading to[An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"), pay careful heed round the village of [Urialla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Urialla_Harbor "Urialla Harbor") and its nearby coasts. Some time ago a tower appeared near the village - that's right, appeared, out of nowheres, and along with it came a terrible storm which lasts to this day. And if you happen to know where it came from and how to get rid of it, then make use of your knowledge, for us folk in Urialla are damned sick of this blasted rain and this nasty neighbor._
