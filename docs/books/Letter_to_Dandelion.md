## Item ID

q304_priscilla_letter

**Letter to Dandelion** is a note in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found in [The Kingfisher Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn"), on a table in the room [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla") takes you after performing during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests") [Broken Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broken_Flowers "Broken Flowers"). 

## Journal entry

    _My Dearest[Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion")!_
    _Since you told me of your plan to rob the city's[kings of crime](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four"), I can think of nothing else. Several times I have wished to talk to you, to drive this mad notion from your head, but you are never at the [Rosemary and Thyme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme") when I visit. I hope the lesser evil is true and you are busy preparing for the heist - and not avoiding me..._
    _I believe, I want to believe, that this entire matter is about something more than mere gold and mid-life hunger for adventure, but either way I would much prefer you abandoned it. Surely you can find another way to solve your[old friend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")'s problem. We both know quite a few folk in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), many of whom owe us favors. Perhaps it's time to call them in? Think it over - before you get in too deep..._
