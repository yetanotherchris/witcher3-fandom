## Item ID

lore_cirilla_of_cintra

**Royal Lineages of the North** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found as random loot, usually on bookshelves, throughout the entire game. It is also scripted to appear during the main quest [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"), on a bookshelf opposite of the entrance in [Margrave Henckel's house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margrave_Henckel%27s_house "Margrave Henckel's house"). 

## Journal entry

    _[Cirilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") Fiona Elen Riannon, born in [1251](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1251 "Timeline/Games"), heiress to the throne of [Cintra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cintra "Cintra"), [Princess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Princess "Princess") of [Brugge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brugge "Brugge") and [Duchess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Duchess "Duchess") of [Sodden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sodden "Sodden"), heiress to Inis [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") and Inis [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig") and Suzerain of [Attre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Attre "Attre") and [Abb Yarra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abb_Yarra "Abb Yarra"). Daughter of [Pavetta](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pavetta "Pavetta") (see Pavetta Fiona Elen) and the [Urcheon of Erlenwald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Urcheon_of_Erlenwald "Urcheon of Erlenwald") (see [Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis")). Granddaughter of the famous Lioness of Cintra, [Queen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen "Queen") [Calanthe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calanthe "Calanthe") (see Calanthe Fiona Riannon)._
    _A shipwreck occurred during a journey from Cintra to[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") which took the lives of the Urcheon and Pavetta. Cirilla's further upbringing was then entrusted to her grandmother. In [1260](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1260 "Timeline/Games"), afraid of the looming [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") threat, Queen Calanthe sent Cirilla to the court of [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Ervyll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ervyll "Ervyll") (see Ervyll of [Verden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Verden "Verden")), where the heiress of Cintra was to marry the heir to the throne of Verden, Prince [Kistrin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kistrin "Kistrin") (see Kistrin of Verden). Though allying with Verden and gaining the aid of that realm's army was at the time Calanthe's top priority, no marriage ever occurred, and Cirilla returned to her grandmother's court. In [1262](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1262 "Timeline/Games"), during the so-called [Cintra Massacre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slaughter_of_Cintra "Slaughter of Cintra"), Cirilla went missing (...)._
