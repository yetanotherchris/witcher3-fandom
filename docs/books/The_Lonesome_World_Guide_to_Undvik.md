**The Lonesome World Guide to Undvik** is a readable item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. When read, it marks the [Marlin Coast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marlin_Coast "Marlin Coast") and [Undvik Boatyards](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik_Boatyards "Undvik Boatyards") [fast travel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") signpost and [harbors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harbor "Harbor") on the map. 

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") at the roadside camp between the [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") [crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Crossroads \(Ard Skellig\)") and [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Kaer_Trolde_Harbor\) "Merchant \(Kaer Trolde Harbor\)") at Kaer Trolde Harbor
  * [Wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Drummond_sawmill\) "Merchant \(Drummond sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(Drummond\) "Sawmill \(Drummond\)") south of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund"), after liberating it
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Holmstein%27s_Port\) "Merchant \(Holmstein's Port\)") in [Holmstein's Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein%27s_Port "Holmstein's Port")

## Journal entry

    _Once-prospering[Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") was the second (after [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig")) most-frequently visited isle in the archipelago. Among its ample charms are a prime location, picturesque scenery and a sublime local cuisine. Sadly the isle was recently devastated when some monstrous [frosty giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant") awoke from his long slumber and began murdering and destroying whatever lay at hand._
    _After this occurrence many realms issued an official edict of warning cautioning their residents to avoid travel to Undvik. Nevertheless, the isle still attracts many travelers. They are no longer primarily lovers of carefree relaxation, however. Instead they are adventure seekers and mercenaries hoping to earn the bounty put on the giant's head, or, barring that, at least get a glimpse of this fantastic creature with their own eyes._
    _Though this is indeed a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to witness such a sight, we are duty-bound to advise anyone thinking of visiting Undvik to postpone their trip until conditions improve, or, even better, alter their plans and visit one of Skellige's many other attractive isles, where the risk of being disemboweled by a colossal man-beast is significantly lower._
