**Heroes of Skellige: Otkell** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can only be purchased from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Holmstein%27s_Port\) "Merchant \(Holmstein's Port\)") at [Holmstein's Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein%27s_Port "Holmstein's Port"). 

## Journal entry

    _Legend states that when[Otkell](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otkell "Otkell"), son of [Hemdall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall "Hemdall"), was sailing to [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"), a terrible storm broke. Otkell called for his father's help, but his father could not hear, for powerful thunder drowned out Otkell's voice. In despair Otkell began yelling for anyone who heard his cries to come to his aid. In reply, [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") descended and gave Otkell a set of pipes._
    _When the hero blew on them, the waves calmed, the storm passed and Otkell could safely reach the shore. When Hemdall learned of the help the goddess had given Otkell, he ordered him to raise a[temple to her](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_of_Freyja "Temple of Freyja") on the isle. Ever since that day, [Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") have worshipped Freya, and the women of Otkell's line have often chosen to become her [priestesses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priest "Priest")._
