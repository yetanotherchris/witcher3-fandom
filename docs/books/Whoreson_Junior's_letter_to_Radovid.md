**Whoreson Junior's letter to Radovid** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

Obviously related to the [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior") quest, though its purpose and the reasons why it was removed are unknown. 

## Journal entry

    _[Your Royal Highness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")_
    _It's my pleasure to report that I've completed the plan we decided on at our last meeting. I can state with pride and not a speck of exaggeration that the[Big Four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four") no longer exists. I hope Your Majesty is pleased. Now I will move on to the next stage of my plan. I trust our arrangement still holds and [Redanian soldiers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") won't cause any trouble..._
    _Your Majesty's Humble Servant,_
    _[C.W.](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley")_
