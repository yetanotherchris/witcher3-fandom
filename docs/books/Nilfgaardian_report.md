## Item ID

q505_nilf_diary_lost2

**Nilfgaardian report** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Similarly to a [Nilfgaardian's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian%27s_journal#Nilfgaardian's_journal_\(Redania_victory\) "Nilfgaardian's journal"), it can be found on a [Nilfgaardian soldier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army")'s corpse during the "Witcher ending" [epilogue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)") if [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") wins the [war](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III"). It is located on the road southeast from the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") near the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison"), among other corpses and between two Nilfgaardian banners. However, the container itself is bugged and invisible, so one must know exactly where to look - see the image in the gallery below. 

## Journal entry

    _To[Lieutenant General](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Military_ranks "Military ranks") van Obreer. [Redanian troops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") seen three days' march to the east of Rustfield. Their numbers exceed previous estimates fourfold._
    _Two men lost during reconnaissance. Private du Bois was captured by the enemy, while Private Hogenbirk was executed for attempted desertion in accordance with Article 22, Item XII of the Hoertogensbosch Codex._
