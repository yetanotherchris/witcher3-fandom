## Item ID

lw_sk25_bandits_note

**Highwayman's letter** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It was supposed to be found at [Kaer Gelen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Gelen "Kaer Gelen"), and along with [Fridebjorg the Bloody](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fridebjorg_the_Bloody "Fridebjorg the Bloody") is linked to an encounter with a "Blood Countess" that is likely the [katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan") at the top level there. 

## Journal entry

    _Gunnar,_
    _You don't go climbing to the top of that tower, you hear? Don't try to be a hero, no one's gonna sing about a bunch of bandits anyhow._
    _I hear there's a[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") snooping around the [isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). I'll try to get him to take care of it. We'll figure out how to settle his bill once he's done. A dagger between the ribs, that sounds like fair pay. Till then - sit on your arse and wait. And if the howling gets to you at night - stick something in your ears._
    _\--Kvegg._
