## Item ID

Armor maintenance

**Prayers to the Eternal Fire** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

This book should've been available and very common as random loot, as well as sold by the books [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Merchant \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). However, a simple typo in the game files (referencing "Armor maintenance**t** " instead of its correct item ID) prevents it from appearing during normal gameplay, and it can only be obtained through console commands. 

## Journal entry

    _Litany of the[Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire")_
    _O Fire Which lighteth Us, protect us from Evil._  
_O Purest Fire, heal our Hearts._  
_Fire Most Holy, drive all Monstrosities from us._  
_O Fire Which Turneth All to ash, incinerate the hearts of our Foes._  
_O Fire Which Danceth, show us the Path which most pleasest thee._  
_Red Flame which Burneth Brightly, spare us from Suffering._  
_Fire which Burneth Eternally, guide us after Death._  
_Heating Fire, guide us while we yet Live._  
_Eternal Fire, to Thee we raise our Prayers._  
_Eternal Fire, in Thee we trust._  
_Eternal Fire, we have no other gods before Thee._

## Notes

  * In early development of the game, certain "perk" abilities were meant to be acquired by reading books such as this one. The idea was eventually [scrapped](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content"), and this particular book originally would've provided some sort of armor maintenance perk - something later merged into the [Care for Your Sword, Soldier!](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Care_for_Your_Sword,_Soldier! "Care for Your Sword, Soldier!") book. It was eventually fully repurposed (renamed and entirely changed contents) for lore instead.
