**Monster in my Manor!** is a notice board posting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Journal entry

    _Good people!_
    _My ancestral farmstead,[Reardon Estate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor"), is infested with [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"). The manor house and all the surroundings have been occupied, leaving one most dreadfully afraid even to take a peek, let alone live therein. That manor is all that I have left to cherish in this life, so I plead for mercy - whoever can find the charity in their soul to help an old woman in need, come see me, and in return you shall receive my eternal gratitude._
    _-[Dolores Reardon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dolores_Reardon "Dolores Reardon")_
