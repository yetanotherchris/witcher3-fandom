**Third letter from Reinald** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It can be found inside the [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit") mine during [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow"), just before a breakable wall after taking the left branch of the tunnels. 

## Journal entry

    _O._
    _Had to sit for a spell. The[priests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") still won't open the gate and my strength is fading. My body feels like it's on fire. I have no memory of the past... hour? Day?_
    _I'm trapped in this darkness, haunted by foolish thoughts with no way to distract myself._
    _Remember when we learned to hunt[ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul")? The folk in that village believed [necrophages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage "Necrophage") to be "the dead arisen", animated by foul [magics](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic"). Hah!_
    _Wanted to ask you what will happen if I fail here... but I've suddenly forgotten your name... Osmar, was it? Omunds. Amund?_
    _Osmund, yes! Gods, it's so clear now, but for a moment then... nothing. A gaping hole in my memory. Not an inkling of who you are._
    _Starting to think I will never leave this place._
    _[R](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald "Reinald")._
