## Item ID

Fiber

**Fiber** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [ashes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ashes "Ashes"). 

It can be found as regular random loot, random drop from [leshens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshy "Leshy") and [spriggans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spriggan "Spriggan"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")

## Notes

  * According to the _Official Prima Game Guide_ , fiber was meant to be used as a component in making [rope](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rope "Rope"), [twine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Twine "Twine"), [blunt crossbow bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blunt_crossbow_bolt "Blunt crossbow bolt"), [broadhead bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broadhead_bolt "Broadhead bolt"), and the default [infinite bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts#Bolts_\(game_item\) "Bolts"). However, the latter was later changed to not require crafting, while the rest no longer use fiber as a component.
