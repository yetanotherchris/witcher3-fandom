## Item ID

Iron ingot

**Iron ingot** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [iron ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_ore "Iron ore"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

Other items: 

  * [Blunt crossbow bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blunt_crossbow_bolt "Blunt crossbow bolt")
  * [Dorian sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dorian_sword "Dorian sword")
  * [Koviri cutlass](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Koviri_cutlass "Koviri cutlass")
  * [Nilfgaardian longsword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_longsword "Nilfgaardian longsword")
  * [Novigrad longsword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_longsword "Novigrad longsword")
  * [Skellige longsword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_longsword "Skellige longsword")
  * [Steel ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_ingot "Steel ingot")
  * [Temerian poniard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_poniard "Temerian poniard")
  * [Velen longsword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen_longsword "Velen longsword")
