## Item ID

Powdered pearl

**Powdered pearl** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Wind "Northern Wind")
  * [Enhanced Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Northern_Wind "Enhanced Northern Wind")
  * [Superior Northern Wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Northern_Wind "Superior Northern Wind")
  * [Lesser Glyph of Infusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Glyph_of_Infusion "Lesser Glyph of Infusion")
  * [Enhanced Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Dimeritium_bomb "Enhanced Dimeritium bomb")
  * [Superior Dimeritium bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Dimeritium_bomb "Superior Dimeritium bomb")

It can be dismantled from [pearls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pearl "Pearl") or [lesser Glyph of Infusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Glyph_of_Infusion "Lesser Glyph of Infusion"), found as random loot, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
