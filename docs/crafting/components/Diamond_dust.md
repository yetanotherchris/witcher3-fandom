Diamond dust

**Diamond dust** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Lesser Triglav runestone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Triglav_runestone "Lesser Triglav runestone")
