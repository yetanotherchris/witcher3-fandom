## Item ID

Sapphire dust

**Sapphire dust** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Black Unicorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Unicorn "Black Unicorn")

  * [Impera Brigade armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Impera_Brigade_armor "Impera Brigade armor")
  * [Lesser Glyph of Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Glyph_of_Aard "Lesser Glyph of Aard")
