## Item ID

Infused shard

**Infused shard** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [infused dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Infused_dust "Infused dust"). It is needed to craft [infused crystal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Infused_crystal "Infused crystal").
