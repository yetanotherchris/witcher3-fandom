## Item ID

Silver ore

**Silver ore** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that can be dismantled into [silver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver "Silver"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Blunt crossbow bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blunt_crossbow_bolt "Blunt crossbow bolt")
  * [Bolt with bait](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolt_with_bait "Bolt with bait")
  * [Broadhead bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broadhead_bolt "Broadhead bolt")
  * [Lesser Glyph of Warding](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Glyph_of_Warding "Lesser Glyph of Warding") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Silver ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_ingot "Silver ingot")
  * [Tracking bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tracking_bolt "Tracking bolt")
