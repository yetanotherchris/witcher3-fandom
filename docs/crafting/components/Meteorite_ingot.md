## Item ID

Meteorite ingot

**Meteorite ingot** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [meteorite ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meteorite_ore "Meteorite ore"). It is needed to craft: 

  * [Meteorite silver ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meteorite_silver_ingot "Meteorite silver ingot")
  * [Thyssen armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thyssen_armor "Thyssen armor")
