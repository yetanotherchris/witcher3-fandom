**Diamond** is a [valuable](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Precious_stones "Precious stones") in _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_. 

### Uses

  * The Gossip in Chapter II will accept either [red gloves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clothing "Clothing") or a diamond for a sexual encounter.
  * The [clerk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clerks "Clerks") at the [town hall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Town_hall "Town hall") takes diamonds as gifts in return for sexual favours.
  * The [druids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") in the [cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_cave "Druids' cave") in the [swamp cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_cemetery "Swamp cemetery") will [exchange this gem for potions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Green_March "The Green March").

### Source

  * You can win a diamond from [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") in your first drinking contest with him.
  * In a trunk from the [Lionhead Spider Cultists' lair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lionhead_Spider_Cultists%27_lair "Lionhead Spider Cultists' lair").
  * You can buy it from a merchant in front of the [Gamblers' den](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gamblers%27_den "Gamblers' den") in Trade Quarter for 120 orens.
  * There is also a quest diamond ([Gramps' diamond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gramps%27_diamond "Gramps' diamond")) in Chapter IV, but it can not be sold and looks entirely different.

## Item ID

Diamond

**Diamond** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"). It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [diamond dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diamond_dust "Diamond dust"). 

It can be found as random loot, guaranteed during a few [treasure hunt quests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_quests#Treasure_hunts "Blood and Wine quests") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
