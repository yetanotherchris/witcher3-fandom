## Item ID

Coal

**Coal** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [ashes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ashes "Ashes"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Black armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_armor_dye "Black armor dye") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Steel ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_ingot "Steel ingot")
  * [Zerrikanian powder](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_powder "Zerrikanian powder")
