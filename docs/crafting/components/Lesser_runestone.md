## Item ID

Runestone lesser

**Lesser runestone** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft all lesser [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"). In the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion, it is also needed to craft [pyerog runestone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pyerog_runestone "Pyerog runestone") and [tvarog runestone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tvarog_runestone "Tvarog runestone").
