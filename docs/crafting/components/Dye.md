## Item ID

Dye

**Dye** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ but is not needed to craft anything. 

It can be obtained by dismantling [inkwells](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") and [trapper's boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trapper%27s_boots "Trapper's boots"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith")
  * Armors and weapons [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(boatyard\) "Merchant \(boatyard\)") in the [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Calveit_Watchtower\) "Merchant \(Calveit Watchtower\)") at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Coast_of_Wrecks\) "Merchant \(Coast of Wrecks\)") at [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Ursten\) "Merchant \(Ursten\)") in [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(battlefield\) "Merchant \(battlefield\)") by the road west of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Mohrin_Village\) "Merchant \(Mohrin Village\)") in [Mohrin Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mohrin_Village "Mohrin Village") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Stacheier\) "Merchant \(Stacheier\)") in [Stacheier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stacheier "Stacheier") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)

In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion quest [A Portrait of the Witcher as an Old Man](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Portrait_of_the_Witcher_as_an_Old_Man "A Portrait of the Witcher as an Old Man"), dye is a themed joke drop by the painter, in the event that he dies if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") runs away from the [Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)").
