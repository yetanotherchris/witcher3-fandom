## Item ID

Amethyst

**Amethyst** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [amethyst dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amethyst_dust "Amethyst dust"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Arbitrator](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arbitrator "Arbitrator")
  * [Longclaw](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Longclaw "Longclaw")
  * [Toussaint Ducal Guard Officer's steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_Ducal_Guard_Officer%27s_steel_sword "Toussaint Ducal Guard Officer's steel sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Toussaint Ducal Guardsman's steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_Ducal_Guardsman%27s_steel_sword "Toussaint Ducal Guardsman's steel sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can be found as random loot, guaranteed in the treasure chest for [Freya Be Praised!](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya_Be_Praised! "Freya Be Praised!"), given as a reward for [Contract: Devil by the Well](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Devil_by_the_Well "Contract: Devil by the Well") if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") refuses [Odolan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Odolan "Odolan")'s payment, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Coast_of_Wrecks\) "Merchant \(Coast of Wrecks\)") at [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks")
