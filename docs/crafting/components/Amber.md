**Amber** is considered a [valuable](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Precious_stones "Precious stones") in _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_. In essence, this means it's not needed for any alchemical purposes. However, the [druids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") in the [swamp cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_cemetery "Swamp cemetery") require it for their [Green March](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Green_March "The Green March") and will exchange it for 3 [willow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Willow "Willow") potions. 

### Sources

  * Wardrobe at the [Reverend's house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reverend%27s_house "Reverend's house") in the [outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts "Outskirts").
  * Sometimes also upstairs at the house across the street from the [Reverend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reverend%27s_house "Reverend's house")'s.
  * Sometimes in the trunk next to [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold")' bed.
  * Sometimes on [Dagon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dagon "Dagon")'s body after the duel in [Chapter IV](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chapters "Chapters").
  * On [Gramps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gramps "Gramps")' body if you choose to kill him in [Chapter II](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chapters "Chapters").

## Item ID

Amber

**Amber** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that can be dismantled into [amber dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amber_dust "Amber dust"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Toussaint knight's steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_knight%27s_steel_sword "Toussaint knight's steel sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Toussaint steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_steel_sword "Toussaint steel sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can be found as random loot, guaranteed in one of the treasure chests inside [Yustianna's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna%27s_Grotto "Yustianna's Grotto"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
  * Armors and weapons [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(boatyard\) "Merchant \(boatyard\)") in the [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Calveit_Watchtower\) "Merchant \(Calveit Watchtower\)") at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower")
