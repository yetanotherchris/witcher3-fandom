## Item ID

Dark iron ingot

**Dark iron ingot** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [dark iron ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_iron_ore "Dark iron ore"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Axeman's trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axeman%27s_trousers "Axeman's trousers")
  * [Dark iron plate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_iron_plate "Dark iron plate")
  * [Dark steel ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_steel_ingot "Dark steel ingot")
  * [Lyrian hardened leather trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lyrian_hardened_leather_trousers "Lyrian hardened leather trousers")
  * [Sapper's trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sapper%27s_trousers "Sapper's trousers")
  * [Toussaint Ducal Guard Officer's steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_Ducal_Guard_Officer%27s_steel_sword "Toussaint Ducal Guard Officer's steel sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Toussaint Ducal Guardsman's steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_Ducal_Guardsman%27s_steel_sword "Toussaint Ducal Guardsman's steel sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
