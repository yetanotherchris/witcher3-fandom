## Item ID

Endriag chitin plates

**Endrega armor plates** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [chitinous shells](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chitinous_shell "Chitinous shell"). 

It is a random drop from [endrega drones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_drone "Endrega drone") and [warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_warrior "Endrega warrior").
