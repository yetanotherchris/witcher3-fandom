## Item ID

Amber dust

**Amber dust** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Lesser Dazhbog runestone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Dazhbog_runestone "Lesser Dazhbog runestone")
  * [Lesser Glyph of Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Glyph_of_Igni "Lesser Glyph of Igni")
  * [Ofieri boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofieri_boots "Ofieri boots") (regular game only) ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Ofieri gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofieri_gauntlets "Ofieri gauntlets") (regular game only) ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Order of the Flaming Rose sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Order_of_the_Flaming_Rose_sword "Order of the Flaming Rose sword") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Shiadhal's armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shiadhal%27s_armor "Shiadhal's armor")

It can be found as regular random loot, random drop from [archgriffins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin "Archgriffin"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
