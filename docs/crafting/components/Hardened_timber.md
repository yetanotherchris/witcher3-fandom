## Item ID

Hardened timber

**Hardened timber** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [timber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timber "Timber") and [resin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Resin "Resin"). It is needed to craft many of the [Feline](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") armor pieces and several other items. 

**Crafting Requirements** ![Tw2 icon crafting](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/9e/Tw2_icon_crafting.svg/revision/latest?cb=20110531194409)
