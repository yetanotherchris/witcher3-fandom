## Item ID

Ruby

**Ruby** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that can be dismantled into [ruby dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruby_dust "Ruby dust") and is needed to craft several items. 

While usually restricted to random loot from rich treasures, a ruby can always be found on a few charred corpses near burned down buildings: 

  * At the [ghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") [nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") west-southwest of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch")
  * At the ghoul nest northeast of the [Burned Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Burned_Ruins "Burned Ruins") near Crow's Perch
  * At the [alghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul") [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") southeast of [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit")
  * In [Mowshurst](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mowshurst "Mowshurst")
  * On the peninsula northeast of [Olena's Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olena%27s_Grove "Olena's Grove")

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
