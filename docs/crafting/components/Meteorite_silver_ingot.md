## Item ID

Meteorite silver ingot

**Meteorite silver ingot** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be dismantled into [meteorite ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meteorite_ingot "Meteorite ingot") and [silver ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_ingot "Silver ingot"), and is needed to craft several items.
