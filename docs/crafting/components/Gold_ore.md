## Item ID

Gold ore

**Gold ore** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [gold nuggets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gold_nugget "Gold nugget"). It is only needed to craft the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords"), [Sarrim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarrim "Sarrim"). 

It can only be found as random loot on cave walls in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(boatyard\) "Merchant \(boatyard\)") in the [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp")
