## Item ID

Steel plate

**Steel plates** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [steel ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_ingot "Steel ingot"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Axeman's trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axeman%27s_trousers "Axeman's trousers")
  * [Ban Ard breastplate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ban_Ard_breastplate "Ban Ard breastplate")
  * [Cidarian cavalry armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cidarian_cavalry_armor "Cidarian cavalry armor")
  * [Journeyman's armor repair kit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Journeyman%27s_armor_repair_kit "Journeyman's armor repair kit")
  * [Master's armor repair kit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master%27s_armor_repair_kit "Master's armor repair kit")
  * [Redanian halberdier's armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redanian_halberdier%27s_armor "Redanian halberdier's armor")
  * [Salmian brigandine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Salmian_brigandine "Salmian brigandine")
