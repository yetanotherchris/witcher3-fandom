# Nails

**Nails** is an item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in two different forms: 

  * Nails (crafting component)
  * Nails (quest item)

The [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") is requested by Octo for the ship he is buiding during [The Lord of Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Lord_of_Undvik "The Lord of Undvik"). 

Some can be found near the boat hull itself, some on the other side of the barricade, west of the shipyard rail and about halfway to the water, and then more are around [Dorve Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dorve_Ruins "Dorve Ruins"). They come in large stacks and, if picked before the quest completes, later turn into junk that can be sold for a total upwards of 120 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown").
