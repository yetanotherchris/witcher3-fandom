## Item ID

Silver ingot

**Silver ingot** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [silver ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_ore "Silver ore"). It is needed to craft several [silver swords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") and [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") [bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts").

It can be found as random loot, and one can also always be found inside the hut in [Drudge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drudge "Drudge") where the [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") [A Dog's Life](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dog%27s_Life "A Dog's Life") ends. However, unlike most components of its kind, it cannot be purchased. The easiest way to obtain it is by dismantling the [junk items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk"), pure silver and silver candelabrum. 

Silver ingots may also be dismantled from a lot of the more common silver swords in the game, the [Nine-Tailed Vixen silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nine-Tailed_Vixen_silver_sword "Nine-Tailed Vixen silver sword") and [White Widow of the Valley of Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Widow_of_the_Valley_of_Flowers "White Widow of the Valley of Flowers"), as well as all of those that can be crafted with it.
