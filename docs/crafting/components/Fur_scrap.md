## Item ID

Fur square

**Fur scrap** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [leather scraps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leather_scraps "Leather scraps"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_boots "Ursine boots")
  * [Enhanced Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Ursine_boots "Enhanced Ursine boots")
  * [Legendary Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_boots "Legendary Ursine boots")
  * [Enhanced Legendary Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Legendary_Ursine_boots "Enhanced Legendary Ursine boots")

It can be found as regular random loot, random drops from [panthers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Panther "Panther") and some bandits in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith")
