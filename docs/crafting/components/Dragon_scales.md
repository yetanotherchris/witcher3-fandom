**Dragon scales** can be found in a few places, namely: 

  * [Philippa Eilhart's house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart%27s_house "Philippa Eilhart's house") in [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne"). The door to her house can be found to the left of the door to the [Redanian Quarter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redanian_Quarter "Redanian Quarter"), next to the crier, and near the [Eternal Flame](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Flame "Eternal Flame") tavern noticeboard in the market square. 
    * There is a [possibly unintentional] way players on [Roche's path](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roche%27s_path "Roche's path") can access the house early. After speaking to [Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roche "Roche") upon entering Loc Muinne, as part of the [For Temeria!](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/For_Temeria! "For Temeria!") questline Roche will ask the Redanian guards for an audience with [Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"). Once the Redanian guards unlock the door to the [Redanian Quarter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redanian_Quarter "Redanian Quarter"), [Philippa Eilhart's house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart%27s_house "Philippa Eilhart's house") will also be unlocked and accessible.
    * Players on [Iorveth's path](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iorveth%27s_path "Iorveth's path") must complete [The Spellbreaker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Spellbreaker "The Spellbreaker") or kill [The Operator](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Operator "The Operator").
    * After completing [Of His Blood and Bone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_His_Blood_and_Bone "Of His Blood and Bone") you can enter [Philippa Eilhart's house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart%27s_house "Philippa Eilhart's house") freely.
  * In the remains of [The Operator](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Operator "The Operator")
  * In [Dethmold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dethmold "Dethmold")'s chest during [Of His Blood and Bone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_His_Blood_and_Bone "Of His Blood and Bone")
  * **Pre-[EE](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Enhanced_Edition "The Witcher 2: Enhanced Edition") only: **In one of the crates in the [Temerian Quarter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Quarter "Temerian Quarter")

## Item ID

Dragon scales

**Dragon scales** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"). It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [chitinous shells](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chitinous_shell "Chitinous shell"). 

It is dropped by almost all [draconid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Draconid "Draconid") monsters (except [cockatrices](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice "Cockatrice")), and can also be occasionally found as random loot in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint").
