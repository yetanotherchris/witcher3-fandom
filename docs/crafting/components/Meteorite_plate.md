## Item ID

Meteorite plate

**Meteorite plate** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [meteorite ingots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meteorite_ingot "Meteorite ingot"). It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't appear during gameplay.
