## Item ID

Hardened leather

**Hardened leather** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that can be dismantled into [cured leather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cured_leather "Cured leather") and [oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oil_\(crafting_component\) "Oil \(crafting component\)"). It is needed to craft many items, most of which are armors.
