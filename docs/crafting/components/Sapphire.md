A valuable item which can be sold or given to someone. 

### Source

  * On [Savolla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Savolla "Savolla")'s body in the Prologue.
  * In a cave of [Swamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp "Swamp") in Chapter II. (There's only one cave there, where wolfs + one of the [Sephirot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sephirot "Sephirot") can be found)
  * Wardrobe, 2nd floor, non-human barracks in [Temple Quarter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Quarter "Temple Quarter") during Chapter III.
  * In the "goal" trunk in the [Bandits' hideout (Fisstech lab)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Salamandra_hideout_in_the_sewers "Salamandra hideout in the sewers") in the [Sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sewers "Sewers") during Chapter III.

### Notes

  * [Blue Eyes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_Eyes "Blue Eyes") takes sapphires as payment (Chapter III).
  * Can exchange this gem for (3) [Full Moon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Full_Moon "Full Moon") potions in the [Druids' cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_cave "Druids' cave") during Chapter V (See [The Green March](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Green_March "The Green March")).

## Item ID

Sapphire

**Sapphire** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that can be dismantled into [sapphire dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sapphire_dust "Sapphire dust"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Beann'shie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beann%27shie_\(sword\) "Beann'shie \(sword\)")
  * [Belhaven blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Belhaven_blade "Belhaven blade") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Gesheft](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gesheft "Gesheft") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Grandmaster Feline silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Feline_silver_sword "Grandmaster Feline silver sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Grandmaster Feline steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Feline_steel_sword "Grandmaster Feline steel sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Grandmaster legendary Feline steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_Feline_steel_sword "Grandmaster legendary Feline steel sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can be found as random loot, guaranteed in one of the treasure chests inside [Yustianna's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna%27s_Grotto "Yustianna's Grotto"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
  * Armors and weapons [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(boatyard\) "Merchant \(boatyard\)") in the [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Calveit_Watchtower\) "Merchant \(Calveit Watchtower\)") at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Coast_of_Wrecks\) "Merchant \(Coast of Wrecks\)") at [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Ursten\) "Merchant \(Ursten\)") in [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(battlefield\) "Merchant \(battlefield\)") by the road west of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Mohrin_Village\) "Merchant \(Mohrin Village\)") in [Mohrin Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mohrin_Village "Mohrin Village") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Stacheier\) "Merchant \(Stacheier\)") in [Stacheier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stacheier "Stacheier") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
