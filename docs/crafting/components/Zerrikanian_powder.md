The only known loot sources are [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") halberdiers killed during the [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests"), [An Eye for an Eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Eye_for_an_Eye "An Eye for an Eye"). In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it should appear as random loot in a few places, but due to a randomization bug, only one dose can be found in the vintner's camp at [Chuchote Cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chuchote_Cave "Chuchote Cave") and is guaranteed to appear.
