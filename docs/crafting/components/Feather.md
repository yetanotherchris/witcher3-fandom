## Item ID

Feather

**Feather** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [ashes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ashes "Ashes"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Blunt crossbow bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blunt_crossbow_bolt "Blunt crossbow bolt")
  * [Broadhead bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broadhead_bolt "Broadhead bolt")
  * [Exploding bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Exploding_bolt "Exploding bolt")
  * [Lesser Glyph of Infusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Glyph_of_Infusion "Lesser Glyph of Infusion")
  * [Ofieri saber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofieri_saber "Ofieri saber") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Split bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Split_bolt "Split bolt")

The easiest way to obtain feathers is by slaughtering birds, which will randomly drop them. They can also be found as both random and guaranteed loot in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):
