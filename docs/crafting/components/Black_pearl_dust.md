## Item ID

Black pearl dust

**Black pearl dust** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [lesser runestone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_runestone "Lesser runestone") (the crafting component). 

It can be obtained by dismantling [black pearls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Black_pearl "Black pearl"), found as random loot, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):
