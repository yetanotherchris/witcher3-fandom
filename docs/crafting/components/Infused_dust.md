## Item ID

Infused dust

**Infused dust** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft: 

  * All lesser [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph")
  * [Infused shard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Infused_shard "Infused shard")
  * [Lesser runestone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_runestone "Lesser runestone")
