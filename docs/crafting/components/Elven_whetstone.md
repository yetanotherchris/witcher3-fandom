## Item ID

Whetstone elven

**Elven whetstone** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [amateur's weapon repair kits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amateur%27s_weapon_repair_kit "Amateur's weapon repair kit"). It is not needed for anything else nor can it be dismantled. 

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
  * [Bram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bram "Bram") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Calveit_Watchtower\) "Merchant \(Calveit Watchtower\)") at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Lindenvale\) "Merchant \(Lindenvale\)") in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Midcopse\) "Merchant \(Midcopse\)") in [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(battlefield\) "Merchant \(battlefield\)") by the road west of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
  * Weapons [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp
  * [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") during [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen") (dependent)
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Stacheier\) "Merchant \(Stacheier\)") in [Stacheier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stacheier "Stacheier") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
