## Item ID

Rope

**Rope** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [twine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Twine "Twine") and [resin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Resin "Resin"). It is needed to craft [Ard Skellig aketon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig_aketon "Ard Skellig aketon"). 

It can be found as both random and guaranteed loot, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Poor merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(The_Bits\) "Merchant \(The Bits\)") near the [Loan Shark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loan_Shark "Loan Shark") in [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits")
  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Ardaiso_Quarry\) "Blacksmith \(Ardaiso Quarry\)") at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

Rope can also be obtained by dismantling: 

  * [Ard Skellig aketon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig_aketon "Ard Skellig aketon")
  * [Rope ladder](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk")
  * [Skellige breeches](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_breeches#Skellige_breeches_\(Version_2\) "Skellige breeches") (version 2)

## Notes

  * Rope was originally meant to be craftable using [fiber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiber "Fiber"), but this was later [removed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from the game.
