## Item ID

Steel ingot

**Steel ingot** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [iron ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_ore "Iron ore"). It is needed to craft all [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") [bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts"), among several other items.
