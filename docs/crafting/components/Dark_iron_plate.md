## Item ID

Dark iron plate

**Dark iron plate** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [dark iron ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_iron_ingot "Dark iron ingot"). It is needed to craft the following items:

## Notes

  * Prior to [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"), this item could not be crafted, and it was also silently added to merchant stores in an unknown patch (possibly also 1.22, just unannounced). It did exist in the first game release and could be found in Arnvald's chest, but didn't have any purpose until the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 
    * The crafting diagram added by patch 1.22 also technically existed in the game files for the first release, but was commented-out and explicitly marked as something that would be part of future DLC.
