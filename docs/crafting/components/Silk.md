## Item ID

Silk

**Silk** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [linen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Linen "Linen"). It is needed to craft many [witcher gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_witcher_gear "The Witcher 3 witcher gear") trousers and some other items. 

**Crafting Requirements** ![Tw2 icon crafting](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/9e/Tw2_icon_crafting.svg/revision/latest?cb=20110531194409)  

**Min. Required Level:** Journeyman [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer")/[blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith")  

[![Tw3 linen](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0d/Tw3_linen.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/40?cb=20170216062819)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0d/Tw3_linen.png/revision/latest?cb=20170216062819)
