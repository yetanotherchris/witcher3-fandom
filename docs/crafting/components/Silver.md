# Silver

**Silver** holds a special place among metals in the witcher universe. In particular, weapons and other items made of silver repel almost all [magical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") [beasts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") as they have a [sensitivity](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sensitivity_to_silver "Sensitivity to silver") to the metal. It is mined in [Kovir and Poviss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir_and_Poviss "Kovir and Poviss") and is the basis for many [currencies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Currency "Currency").

## Item ID

Silver mineral

**Silver** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that is needed to craft the following items: 

**Witcher gear:**

  * [Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_gauntlets "Ursine gauntlets")
  * [Enhanced Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Ursine_gauntlets "Enhanced Ursine gauntlets")

  * [Enhanced Legendary Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Legendary_Ursine_gauntlets "Enhanced Legendary Ursine gauntlets")
  * [Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_trousers "Ursine trousers")
  * [Enhanced Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Ursine_trousers "Enhanced Ursine trousers")
  * [Legendary Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_trousers "Legendary Ursine trousers")

5 silver is always found in the big chest near the [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), and it can also be found as random loot, or dismantled from [silver ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_ore "Silver ore") and many [junk items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk"), particularly silver pantaloons and jewelry with "silver" in the names, as well as from any of the above-listed [Ursine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") items crafted with it.
