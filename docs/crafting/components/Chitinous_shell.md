## Item ID

Chitin scale

**Chitinous shell** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [cured draconid leather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cured_draconid_leather "Cured draconid leather"). 

It is a random drop from all [insectoid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid "Insectoid") [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") except [sandcrabs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sandcrab "Sandcrab"), and base game [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"). As regular loot, chitinous shells can be found in three [bandit camps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") \- at the [Whale Graveyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale_Graveyard "Whale Graveyard"), at [Giants' Toes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giants%27_Toes "Giants' Toes"), and the fishermen's camp east of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig"). However, in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion they appear in many treasure chests, both as random and guaranteed loot.
