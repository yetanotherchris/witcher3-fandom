## Item ID

Wire

**Wire** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [iron ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_ore "Iron ore"). It is needed to craft the following items:
