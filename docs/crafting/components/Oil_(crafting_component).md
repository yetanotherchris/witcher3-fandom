**Oil** can be looted in many places or purchased from [Fioravanti](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fioravanti "Fioravanti") in [Flotsam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flotsam "Flotsam") and [Felicia Cori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Felicia_Cori "Felicia Cori") in [Vergen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vergen "Vergen"). Oil can also be crafted by craftsman using [Diagram: Oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Oil "Diagram: Oil") or by alchemy using [Formula: Blade oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formula:_Blade_oil "Formula: Blade oil").

## Item ID

Oil

**Oil** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Amateur's weapon repair kit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amateur%27s_weapon_repair_kit "Amateur's weapon repair kit")
  * [Axeman's boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axeman%27s_boots "Axeman's boots")
  * [Executioner's boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Executioner%27s_boots "Executioner's boots")
  * [Griffin decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_decoction "Griffin decoction")
  * [Mountain Folk boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mountain_Folk_boots "Mountain Folk boots")
  * [Reaver's boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reaver%27s_boots "Reaver's boots")

It can be found as random loot, guaranteed at some locations during the "Wine Wars" [quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_quests "Blood and Wine quests") line in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"):
