**Iron ore** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_crafting_components "The Witcher 2 crafting components") which is quite easily found. It is required for most [swords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_steel_swords "The Witcher 2 steel swords"), even [silver swords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_silver_swords "The Witcher 2 silver swords") as a pure silver sword would not be hard enough to be of use. ## Item ID

Iron ore

**Iron ore** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Iron ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_ingot "Iron ingot")
  * [Ofieri scale armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofieri_scale_armor "Ofieri scale armor") ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") only) ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Ofieri sharovary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofieri_sharovary "Ofieri sharovary") ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") only) ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Steel ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_ingot "Steel ingot")
