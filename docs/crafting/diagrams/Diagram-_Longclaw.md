**Diagram: Longclaw** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Longclaw](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Longclaw "Longclaw"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), but one is always found in the right-side locked room at [Freya's Garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindar "Hindar") during the secondary quest [In Wolf's Clothing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_Wolf%27s_Clothing "In Wolf's Clothing").
