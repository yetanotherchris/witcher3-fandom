**Diagram: Scoia'tael sword** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Scoia'tael sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_sword "Scoia'tael sword"). 

It can be found at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), in the reward chest for [The Things Men Do For Coin...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Things_Men_Do_For_Coin... "The Things Men Do For Coin..."), or the following deserter [bandit camps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp"): 

  * North of [Marauders' Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marauders%27_Bridge "Marauders' Bridge")
  * Near [Mowshurst](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mowshurst "Mowshurst") (northeast of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch"))
  * Hill between [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") and Lurtch
  * Far north of [Road to Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Road_to_Bald_Mountain "Road to Bald Mountain"), on the coastline

It can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Scoia'tael merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael\)") in the forest south of Novigrad
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
