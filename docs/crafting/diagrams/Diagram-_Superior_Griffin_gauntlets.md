**Diagram: Superior Griffin gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Superior Griffin gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Griffin_gauntlets "Superior Griffin gauntlets"). 

It can be found north of [Giants' Toes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giants%27_Toes "Giants' Toes"), along with the [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_Griffin_armor "Diagram: Superior Griffin armor"), [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_Griffin_boots "Diagram: Superior Griffin boots"), and [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_Griffin_trousers "Diagram: Superior Griffin trousers") diagrams.
