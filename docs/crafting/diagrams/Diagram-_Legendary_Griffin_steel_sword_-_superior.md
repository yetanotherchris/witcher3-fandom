**Diagram: Legendary Griffin steel sword - superior** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [legendary Griffin steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Griffin_steel_sword_-_superior "Legendary Griffin steel sword - superior") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It's found behind a shrine, north of the ruined village just west of [Yngvar's Fang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yngvar%27s_Fang "Yngvar's Fang") in [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig").
