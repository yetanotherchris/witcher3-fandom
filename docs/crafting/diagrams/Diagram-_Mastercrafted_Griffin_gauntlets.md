**Diagram: Mastercrafted Griffin gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Mastercrafted Griffin gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Griffin_gauntlets "Mastercrafted Griffin gauntlets"). 

It can be found at the hidden treasure marker northeast of [Redgill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redgill "Redgill"), along with the [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Griffin_boots "Diagram: Mastercrafted Griffin boots"), [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Griffin_armor "Diagram: Mastercrafted Griffin armor"), and [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Griffin_trousers "Diagram: Mastercrafted Griffin trousers") diagrams.
