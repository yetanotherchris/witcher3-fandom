**Diagram: Legendary Ursine gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Legendary Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_gauntlets "Legendary Ursine gauntlets") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It's found, along with the [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Ursine_armor "Diagram: Legendary Ursine armor"), [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Ursine_boots "Diagram: Legendary Ursine boots"), and [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Ursine_trousers "Diagram: Legendary Ursine trousers") diagrams, in [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach").
