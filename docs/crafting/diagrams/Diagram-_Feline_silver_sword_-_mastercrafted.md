**Diagram: Feline silver sword - mastercrafted** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Feline silver sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Feline silver sword - mastercrafted"). 

It's found in a cave east of [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken"), just below where [Jutta an Dimun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jutta_an_Dimun "Jutta an Dimun") is fighting.
