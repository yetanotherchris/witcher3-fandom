**Diagram: Legendary Feline armor** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Legendary Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_armor "Legendary Feline armor") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It's found on Mad [Kiyan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kiyan "Kiyan")'s body along with the [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Feline_trousers "Diagram: Legendary Feline trousers"), [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Feline_boots "Diagram: Legendary Feline boots"), and [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Feline_gauntlets "Diagram: Legendary Feline gauntlets") diagrams, as well as [moonblade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moonblade "Moonblade") and [laboratory notebook](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Laboratory_notebook "Laboratory notebook").
