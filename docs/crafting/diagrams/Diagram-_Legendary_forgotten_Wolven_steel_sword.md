**Diagram: Legendary forgotten Wolven steel sword** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). It is needed to craft [Legendary Forgotten Wolven steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Forgotten_Wolven_steel_sword "Legendary Forgotten Wolven steel sword") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It's received automatically from [Reinald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald "Reinald") at the end of [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow"), regardless how you complete it.
