**Diagram: Precision bolt** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [precision bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Precision_bolt "Precision bolt"). 

It can be found in the treasure chest for [Tough Luck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tough_Luck "Tough Luck"), at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Armorer \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Novigrad\) "Blacksmith \(Novigrad\)") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
