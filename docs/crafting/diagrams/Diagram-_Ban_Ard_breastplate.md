**Diagram: Ban Ard breastplate** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Ban Ard breastplate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ban_Ard_breastplate "Ban Ard breastplate"). 

It is received as a reward when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") sets sail to [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") during the main quest, [Destination: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige"). It can also be found at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")

## Associated quest

  * [Destination: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige")

## Notes

  * Oddly, this diagram is also awarded for completing [Flesh for Sale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flesh_for_Sale "Flesh for Sale"), despite there being no way to complete that quest before already having obtained the diagram by sailing to Skellige.
