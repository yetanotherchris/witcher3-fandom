**Diagram: Serpentine steel sword** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Viper steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_steel_sword "Viper steel sword"). 

It can be looted at the [Amavet Fortress Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amavet_Fortress_Ruins "Amavet Fortress Ruins") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") along with an [interrogation report](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Interrogation_report "Interrogation report").
