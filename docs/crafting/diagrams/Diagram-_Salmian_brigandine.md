**Diagram: Salmian brigandine** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Salmian brigandine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Salmian_brigandine "Salmian brigandine"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Mohrin_Village\) "Merchant \(Mohrin Village\)") in [Mohrin Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mohrin_Village "Mohrin Village") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)

## Notes

  * This diagram was supposed to be found at the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") just south of [Frischlow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Frischlow "Frischlow"), in the cave with [Diagram: Enhanced Griffin gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Enhanced_Griffin_gauntlets "Diagram: Enhanced Griffin gauntlets"). However, the treasure chest there is fully randomized instead.
