**Diagram: Legendary Griffin silver sword - enhanced** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Legendary Griffin silver sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Griffin_silver_sword_-_enhanced "Legendary Griffin silver sword - enhanced") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It's found in a cave east of the [Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Velen\) "Crossroads \(Velen\)") signpost in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") (the same one you can explore during [Contract: Patrol Gone Missing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Patrol_Gone_Missing "Contract: Patrol Gone Missing")).
