**Diagram: Griffin trousers** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Griffin trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_trousers "Griffin trousers"). 

It's found next to [George](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/George_of_Kagen "George of Kagen")'s tomb in [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto") along with the rest of the standard [Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") diagrams, including [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Griffin_boots "Diagram: Griffin boots"), [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Griffin_armor "Diagram: Griffin armor"), and [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Griffin_gauntlets "Diagram: Griffin gauntlets").
