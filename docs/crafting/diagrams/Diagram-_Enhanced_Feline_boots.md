**Diagram: Enhanced Feline boots** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Enhanced Feline boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_boots "Enhanced Feline boots"). 

It can be found in the large cave east of [Toderas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toderas "Toderas") (the same cave during [Contract: Phantom of the Trade Route](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Phantom_of_the_Trade_Route "Contract: Phantom of the Trade Route")).
