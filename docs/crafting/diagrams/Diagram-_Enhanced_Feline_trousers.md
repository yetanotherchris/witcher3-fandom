**Diagram: Enhanced Feline trousers** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Enhanced Feline trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_trousers "Enhanced Feline trousers"). 

It can be found in the [Oxenfurt sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_sewers "Oxenfurt sewers"), in the tunnels below the [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Oxenfurt\) "Blacksmith \(Oxenfurt\)") by entering the house just to the east of him. 

A second way down is through a fake wall in the cellar of the house just northwest of the above described house.
