Rune triglav lesser schematic

**Diagram: Lesser Triglav runestone** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [lesser Triglav runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Triglav_runestone "Lesser Triglav runestone"). 

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") outside of [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") except [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig") and the [Freya Priestess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya_Priestess "Freya Priestess") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
  * Weapons [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Calveit_Watchtower\) "Merchant \(Calveit Watchtower\)") at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(battlefield\) "Merchant \(battlefield\)") by the road west of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort")
  * [Scoia'tael merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael\)") in the forest south of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") during [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen") (dependent)
