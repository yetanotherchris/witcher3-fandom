**Diagram: Broadhead bolt** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [broadhead bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broadhead_bolt "Broadhead bolt"). 

It can always be found in a small chest by some stalagmites near the [Place of Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Place_of_Power "Place of Power") inside the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt"), during [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone") quest. It also should appear in a similar small chest in the room with the [nightwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith") during [Peace Disturbed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peace_Disturbed "Peace Disturbed"), but it appears to be chance-based there (just on whether it appears; the chest itself can only contain this diagram and a [runestone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone")). However, it is much more likely to have found it earlier at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"). 

It can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Armorer \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")
  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Novigrad\) "Blacksmith \(Novigrad\)") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
