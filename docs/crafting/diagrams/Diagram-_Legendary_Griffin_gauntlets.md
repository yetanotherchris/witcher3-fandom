**Diagram: Legendary Griffin gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Legendary Griffin gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Griffin_gauntlets "Legendary Griffin gauntlets") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It's found next to [George](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/George_of_Kagen "George of Kagen")'s tomb in [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto") along with the rest of the standard [Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") diagrams, including [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Griffin_boots "Diagram: Legendary Griffin boots"), [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Griffin_trousers "Diagram: Legendary Griffin trousers"), and [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Legendary_Griffin_armor "Diagram: Legendary Griffin armor").
