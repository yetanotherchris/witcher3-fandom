Explosive Bolt schematic

**Diagram: Exploding bolt** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [exploding bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Exploding_bolt "Exploding bolt"). 

It can be found in an iron chest inside the [Isolated Shack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Shack "Isolated Shack"), at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Novigrad\) "Blacksmith \(Novigrad\)") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market")
  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Silverton\) "Blacksmith \(Silverton\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton")
  * [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori") in his [workshop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hattori%27s_home "Hattori's home") in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane")
  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigurd "Sigurd") in [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
