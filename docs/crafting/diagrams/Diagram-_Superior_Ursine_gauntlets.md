**Diagram: Superior Ursine gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Superior Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Ursine_gauntlets "Superior Ursine gauntlets"). 

It can be looted from a [monster den](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_den "Monster den") northwest of [Hanged Man's Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Tree "Hanged Man's Tree"), along with the [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_Ursine_armor "Diagram: Superior Ursine armor"), [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_Ursine_trousers "Diagram: Superior Ursine trousers"), and [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_Ursine_boots "Diagram: Superior Ursine boots") diagrams.
