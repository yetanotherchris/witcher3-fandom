**Diagram: Angrenian cuirass** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Angrenian cuirass](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Angrenian_cuirass "Angrenian cuirass"). 

It is dropped by [Nithral](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nithral "Nithral") during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests") quest [Wandering in the Dark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_in_the_Dark "Wandering in the Dark"). It can also be found at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")

## Notes

  * At some point, it was intended to be found in the treasure for [Hidden in the Depths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hidden_in_the_Depths "Hidden in the Depths"), but the chest is randomized instead.
  * This diagram was also supposed to be a reward for winning a boat race quest near [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde"), which was [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from the game.
