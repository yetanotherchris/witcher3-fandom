**Diagram: Mastercrafted Ursine boots** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Mastercrafted Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Ursine_boots "Mastercrafted Ursine boots"). 

It's found along with the [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Ursine_armor "Diagram: Mastercrafted Ursine armor"), [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Ursine_gauntlets "Diagram: Mastercrafted Ursine gauntlets"), and [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Ursine_trousers "Diagram: Mastercrafted Ursine trousers") diagrams at the [Destroyed Bastion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destroyed_Bastion "Destroyed Bastion").
