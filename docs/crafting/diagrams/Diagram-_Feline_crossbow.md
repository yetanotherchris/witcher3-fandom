**Diagram: Feline crossbow** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Feline crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_crossbow "Feline crossbow"). 

It can be found along with [mage's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage%27s_notes#Mage's_notes_\(Flying_Stag\) "Mage's notes") in the wreck of the [Flying Stag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flying_Stag "Flying Stag"), southwest of the [Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lighthouse "Lighthouse").
