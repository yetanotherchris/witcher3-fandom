## Item ID

Arbitrator schematic

**Diagram: Arbitrator** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Arbitrator](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arbitrator "Arbitrator"). 

Starting at level 10, It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"). Unlike other Relic weapon diagrams, this one additionally appears in some lower-grade treasure chests as regular random loot, making it easier to find. 

## Notes

  * This diagram was at some point also intended to be sold by a "master [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith")" whose store inventory is still present in the game files. It's unclear if that's an earlier, possibly even transitional version of [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori") before he is fully unlocked, or an entirely different person altogether.
