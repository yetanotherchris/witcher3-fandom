**Diagram: Ursine crossbow** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Ursine crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_crossbow "Ursine crossbow"). 

It's found in the same cave during [Contract: Here Comes the Groom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Here_Comes_the_Groom "Contract: Here Comes the Groom") with a [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_to_Ingeborga_Kalebsdotter "Letter to Ingeborga Kalebsdotter") on a skeleton in [Melusine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Melusine "Melusine")'s caverns south-southwest of [Old Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Watchtower "Old Watchtower") on [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog").
