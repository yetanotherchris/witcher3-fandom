**Diagram: Dazhbog runestone** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Dazhbog runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dazhbog_runestone "Dazhbog runestone"). 

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
  * All [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") except [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna") in [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig") and the [Freya Priestess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya_Priestess "Freya Priestess") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on Hindarsfjall
  * [Scoia'tael merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael\)") in the forest south of Novigrad
  * [Runewright](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runewright_\(character\) "Runewright \(character\)") at [Upper Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Upper_Mill "Upper Mill") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Ducal Camerlengo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rafael_de_Surmann "Rafael de Surmann") in [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
