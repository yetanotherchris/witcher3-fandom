**Diagram: Gnomish Gwyhyr** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Gnomish Gwyhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnomish_Gwyhyr "Gnomish Gwyhyr"). 

It's a possible spawn at a [random diagram location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations") or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")

It can also be received as reward for sparing the [imp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imp "Imp") during [Contract: An Elusive Thief](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_An_Elusive_Thief "Contract: An Elusive Thief").
