**Diagram: Ursine boots** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_boots "Ursine boots"). 

It's found, along with the [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Ursine_armor "Diagram: Ursine armor"), [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Ursine_gauntlets "Diagram: Ursine gauntlets"), and [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Ursine_trousers "Diagram: Ursine trousers") diagrams, in [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach").
