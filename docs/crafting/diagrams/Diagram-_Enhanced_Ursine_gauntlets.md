**Diagram: Enhanced Ursine gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [enhanced Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Ursine_gauntlets "Enhanced Ursine gauntlets"). 

It's found at the ruins southeast of [Fort Etnir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fort_Etnir "Fort Etnir") and northeast of [Rogne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rogne "Rogne").
