**Diagram: Ursine silver sword - superior** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Ursine silver sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_silver_sword_-_superior "Ursine silver sword - superior"). 

It's found in a cave southwest of [The Orphans of Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog") (the same cave during [Contract: Swamp Thing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Swamp_Thing "Contract: Swamp Thing")).
