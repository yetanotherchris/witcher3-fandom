**Diagram: Superior legendary Feline gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [superior legendary Feline gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_legendary_Feline_gauntlets "Superior legendary Feline gauntlets") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It can be found along with the [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_legendary_Feline_boots "Diagram: Superior legendary Feline boots"), [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_legendary_Feline_armor "Diagram: Superior legendary Feline armor"), and [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Superior_legendary_Feline_trousers "Diagram: Superior legendary Feline trousers") diagrams at an unmarked ruin southwest of [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto").
