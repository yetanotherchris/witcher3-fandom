**Diagram: Enhanced Griffin gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Enhanced Griffin gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Griffin_gauntlets "Enhanced Griffin gauntlets"). 

It's found next to the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") just south of [Frischlow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Frischlow "Frischlow") and is guarded over by a [noonwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith "Noonwraith").
