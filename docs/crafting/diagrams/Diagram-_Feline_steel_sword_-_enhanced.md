**Diagram: Feline steel sword - enhanced** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Feline steel sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_steel_sword_-_enhanced "Feline steel sword - enhanced"). 

It's found in the cave northeast of [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor") (the same cave where the DLC quest, [Fools' Gold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fools%27_Gold "Fools' Gold"), takes place). The diagram is located in a chest on the left a short ways in after entering.
