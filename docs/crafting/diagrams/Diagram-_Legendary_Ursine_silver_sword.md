It's found at [Fort Etnir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fort_Etnir "Fort Etnir") along with [The knight Chalimir's letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_knight_Chalimir%27s_letter "The knight Chalimir's letter").
