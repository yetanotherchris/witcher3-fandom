**Diagram: Mastercrafted legendary forgotten Wolven gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). It is needed to craft [Mastercrafted legendary forgotten Wolven gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_legendary_forgotten_Wolven_gauntlets "Mastercrafted legendary forgotten Wolven gauntlets") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

This one, along with all the other mastercrafted and grandmaster diagrams for the [set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_Wolf_School_Gear "Forgotten Wolf School Gear"), is given after completing [Scavenger Hunt: Forgotten Wolf School Gear Diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Forgotten_Wolf_School_Gear_Diagrams "Scavenger Hunt: Forgotten Wolf School Gear Diagrams").
