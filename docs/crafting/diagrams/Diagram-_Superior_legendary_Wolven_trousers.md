**Diagram: Superior legendary Wolven trousers** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Superior legendary Wolven trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_legendary_Wolven_trousers "Superior legendary Wolven trousers") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It's found in a cave north of the [Bastion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastion "Bastion") and northwest of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen").
