**Diagram: Enhanced legendary Griffin trousers** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [enhanced legendary Griffin trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_legendary_Griffin_trousers "Enhanced legendary Griffin trousers") in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

It can be found in a chest in the [Burned Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Burned_Ruins "Burned Ruins") east of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch").
