**Diagram: Serpentine silver sword** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Viper silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_silver_sword "Viper silver sword"). 

It can be looted from inside the chapel at [White Orchard cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_cemetery "White Orchard cemetery") alongside a [letter from witcher Kolgrim of the Viper School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_witcher_Kolgrim_of_the_Viper_School "Letter from witcher Kolgrim of the Viper School").
