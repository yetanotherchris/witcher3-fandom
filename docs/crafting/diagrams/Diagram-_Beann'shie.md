**Diagram: Beann'shie** is a diagram in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ that is needed to craft [Beann'shie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beann%27shie_\(sword\) "Beann'shie \(sword\)"). It can be found in a chest within the ruins of the Asylum burnt down Hospital on Chapter 1, but this is _extremely_ rare. It is also possible to find it in the chest in Ruined elven baths. 

In order to attempt farming it, the save game has to be made before jumping down into the (second pair) of wraiths you fight. The chest will be in the middle up against the right wall.
