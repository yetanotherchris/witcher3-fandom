**Diagram: Wolven boots** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Wolven boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_boots "Wolven boots"). 

It can be found, along with the [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_armor "Diagram: Wolven armor"), [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_gauntlets "Diagram: Wolven gauntlets"), and [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_trousers "Diagram: Wolven trousers") diagrams and some [notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notes_written_in_a_shaky_hand "Notes written in a shaky hand"), in a cave beneath the [old signal tower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_signal_tower "Old signal tower") in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen").
