**Diagram: Enhanced Griffin armor** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Enhanced Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Griffin_armor "Enhanced Griffin armor"). 

It's found at [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort"), right next to [Trollololo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trollololo "Trollololo")'s shack.
