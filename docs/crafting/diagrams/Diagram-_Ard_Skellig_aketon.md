**Diagram: Ard Skellig aketon** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Ard Skellig aketon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig_aketon "Ard Skellig aketon"). 

It can be received as a reward for killing [Nathaniel Pastodi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nathaniel_Pastodi "Nathaniel Pastodi") during [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins"), found at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Mohrin_Village\) "Merchant \(Mohrin Village\)") in [Mohrin Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mohrin_Village "Mohrin Village") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
