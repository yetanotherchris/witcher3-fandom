**Diagram: Mastercrafted Feline gauntlets** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Mastercrafted Feline gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Feline_gauntlets "Mastercrafted Feline gauntlets"). 

It can be found along with the [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Feline_boots "Diagram: Mastercrafted Feline boots"), [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Feline_armor "Diagram: Mastercrafted Feline armor"), and [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mastercrafted_Feline_trousers "Diagram: Mastercrafted Feline trousers") diagrams in a cave south-southwest of [Trottheim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trottheim "Trottheim").
