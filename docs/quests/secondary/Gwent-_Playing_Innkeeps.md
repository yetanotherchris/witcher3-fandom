## Additional Information

### Type

[Secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests")

**Gwent: Playing Innkeeps** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. To advance, you must defeat every player in [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). 

    _**[Gwent: Play a Round with Stjepan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent:_Play_a_Round_with_Stjepan "Gwent: Play a Round with Stjepan")**_

    _Beat everyone you've ever played in[gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent")? Consider yourself a prime player ready to face the best of the best? Then try your hand against [Stjepan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stjepan "Stjepan") \- a man so good it seems the cards play themselves!_
    _To learn more, come to[the Alchemy Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Alchemy "The Alchemy") in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")._
    _– Stjepan, barkeep_

_Note:_ For those looking to get the Card Collector [achievement](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_achievements "The Witcher 3 achievements"), this quest must be completed.

## Walkthrough

While the quest may be started from a notice at Oxenfurt's [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Oxenfurt\) "Notice board \(Oxenfurt\)"), it's usually started by playing with the [Inkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(Inn_at_the_Crossroads\) "Innkeep \(Inn at the Crossroads\)") at the [Inn at the Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads "Inn at the Crossroads"), as he is encountered very early in the main story. Other than him, you must also challenge [Stjepan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stjepan "Stjepan") at [The Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Alchemy "The Alchemy") in Oxenfurt and [Olivier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olivier "Olivier") at [The Kingfisher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn") in Novigrad. 

Stjepan uses the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck") deck and uses the [Emhyr var Emreis: The White Flame](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis:_The_White_Flame_\(gwent_card\) "Emhyr var Emreis: The White Flame \(gwent card\)") leader card, rendering your leader's ability useless, so be aware of that. He also makes good use of the available [spy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_spy_cards "Gwent spy cards"), [medic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_medic_cards "Gwent medic cards"), and [decoy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Decoy_\(gwent_card\) "Decoy \(gwent card\)") cards in his deck. Defeating him will earn 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and the [Yennefer card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg_\(gwent_card\) "Yennefer of Vengerberg \(gwent card\)"). 

The Innkeep at the Inn at the Crossroads also plays the Nilfgaardian deck and just like Stjepan, effectively uses spy and medic cards. However, he uses the [Emhyr var Emreis: Emperor of Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis:_Emperor_of_Nilfgaard_\(gwent_card\) "Emhyr var Emreis: Emperor of Nilfgaard \(gwent card\)") leader card. Defeating him will earn 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and the [Menno card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Menno_Coehoorn_\(gwent_card\) "Menno Coehoorn \(gwent card\)"). 

Olivier uses the [Monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck") deck and defeating him will earn the [Tibor card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tibor_Eggebracht_\(gwent_card\) "Tibor Eggebracht \(gwent card\)"). If Olivier died during [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), his unique card will be located in the room next to the bar and the unnamed Innkeep, in a small lockbox over the cabinet. Obtaining his card in either fashion will earn 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

Obtaining all three cards will conclude the quest. 

## Journal entry

    _It is an innkeeper's job to tend to his guests' needs. Usually this takes the form of serving strong drinks and listening to tales of loves lost and wounds received. Yet at times a patron will wish to play a hand of gwent, which is why all good innkeepers keep a deck at the ready at all times. And the excellent ones - they keep excellent decks, full of top-notch cards that can provide an evening of challenge and excitement to even the most refined gwent player. Geralt was determined to win some of these cards for himself._
    _Geralt played and defeated all the excellent innkeepers he could find and added many powerful new cards to his collection._

## Objectives

  * Win Stjepan's unique card.
  * Win a unique card from the innkeep at the Inn at the Crossroads.

**If Olivier is still alive:**

  * Win Olivier's unique card.

**If Olivier is dead:**

  * Find a unique card in the Kingfisher.
