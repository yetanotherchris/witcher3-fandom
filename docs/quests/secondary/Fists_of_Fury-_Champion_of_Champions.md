## Walkthrough

This is the final fist-fighting quest in the base game, issued after becoming the fist-fighting champion in all three main regions that have fist fights: [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). After becoming champion in the third location, wherever that happens to be, a man will approach and offer you the chance to fight the [Champion of Champions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Athak_Akydalv "Athak Akydalv"). He instructs you to sail to Spikeroog and head to the arena, which is located in the village of [Hov](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hov "Hov"). 

Upon arriving at the village, speak to the Bookie who is leaning against a wooden pillar in front of the arena's entrance. If you have not yet completed the quest [Master of the Arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_of_the_Arena "Master of the Arena"), the Bookie will inform you that the arena is closed due to an unresolved incident. Otherwise, he reveals that the reigning champion is, in fact, a [rock troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rock_troll "Rock troll"), and initiates the fight. During this fight, it is possible to use consumables such as food items and potions. 

_Important:_ completion of this quest also unlocks the "[Brawl Master](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brawl_Master "Brawl Master")" achievement/trophy, though if you didn't defeat the troll, then the fight cannot be repeated and this achievement unobtainable. Creating a save prior to the fight however will allow you to repeat the fight. 

After defeating the rock troll, Geralt is proclaimed the Champion of Champions, and the Bookie says that he will send word of this news to the four corners of the world. The quest then ends, and you are awarded 90![Crownitem](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/22/Crownitem.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/15?cb=20180420173351) and a diagram for [The Tamer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Tamer "The Tamer"). You can talk to the troll after the fight and he'll tell you what his name is. 

## Journal entry

    _Geralt had already won a great many victories while fistfighting opponents across the land, but still could not call himself the best of the best. The champion of champions remained undefeated, waiting for Geralt to challenge him for his title._

## Objectives

  * Go to [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog") to find out about this mysterious opponent.
  * Enter the arena.
  * Kill or be killed.

## Notes

  * The final journal objective is somewhat misleading as there is no need to kill the [troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll "Troll"), but merely to defeat him.
  * The troll's mysterious moniker is Athak Akydalv as revealed after the fight when the troll is approached. 
    * In the Polish (original) version it's spelled _"Asiak Akydałw"_ which is _"Kasia Władyka"_ backwards, which is the name of one of CDPR quest designers.
  * This fight is unlike both all other fist fights and all other troll fights. Consumables may be used and the troll is somehow vulnerable to fist attacks from behind whereas sword attacks are generally not effective against trolls.

## Bugs

  * If one starts the fight immediately after fighting in the previous quest [Master of the Arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_of_the_Arena "Master of the Arena") without replenishing their health before fighting the troll, the game will automatically think that Geralt lost to the troll and flag the quest as failed.
