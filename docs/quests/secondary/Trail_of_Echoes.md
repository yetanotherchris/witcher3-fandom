**Trail of Echoes** is an unmarked [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be started in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"). 

The [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") rewarded in this quest is unaffected by [difficulty level](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Difficulty_level#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Difficulty level"). 

## Walkthrough

Follow the road which leads northwest of the Kaer Morhen keep to the end, and enter the cave at the end of the path, the same cave one is sent to search through during the [Scavenger Hunt: Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Wolf_School_Gear/Part_4 "Scavenger Hunt: Wolf School Gear/Part 4") quest, which will begin the quest. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") makes several comments, including that the cave was formerly used for the [Trial of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trial_of_the_Grasses "Trial of the Grasses"), before Kaer Morhen was built, but is now infested by monsters. 

In addition to killing the [nekker warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior "Nekker warrior") and a [devourer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devourer "Devourer"), one must inspect the various pieces of equipment and read the [Trial of the Grasses registry tome](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trial_of_the_Grasses_registry_tome "Trial of the Grasses registry tome"), containing a list of old [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") candidates who underwent the Trial. Exploring the cave sufficiently and examining enough items ends the quest and rewards 25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level").
