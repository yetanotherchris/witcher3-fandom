## Walkthrough

After clearing the [casino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_casino "Whoreson Junior's casino") of [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley")'s [men](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_gang "Whoreson Junior's gang") during the main quest [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"), Geralt finds a beaten [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") spy named [Rico Meiersdorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rico_Meiersdorf "Rico Meiersdorf") sent by [Francis Bedlam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam"), the King of Beggars, in the top floor. If the witcher let Rico go, he will promise that his boss, the King of Beggars, will reward him. Geralt can then head back to [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove") during [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior") to claim a free reward from Francis Bedlam. The King of the Beggars then gives you [Freya's Warriors' armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya%27s_Warriors%27_armor "Freya's Warriors' armor") and [Eirlithrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eirlithrad "Eirlithrad"). 

## Journal entry

    _It turned out even thieves have honor. Rico had not lied - the King of Beggars truly did reward Geralt handsomely for the services rendered to his subordinate._
    _Geralt freed a halfling named Rico from certain and painful death at the hands of thugs in Whoreson Junior's casino. The halfling promised Geralt his employer - the King of Beggars - would repay the witcher for this help._

## Objectives

  * Collect a reward from the King of Beggars for freeing Rico.

## Notes

  * Rico Meiersdorf is actually the youngest son of [Holofernes Meiersdorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holofernes_Meiersdorf "Holofernes Meiersdorf"), the halfling who runs the [Honeyfill Meadworks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks") east of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad").
