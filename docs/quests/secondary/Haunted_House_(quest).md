### Type

[Secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests")

**Haunted House** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

    _**[Beware! Haunted House& Cheating Merchant!](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beware!_Haunted_House_%26_Cheating_Merchant! "Beware! Haunted House & Cheating Merchant!")**_

    _To all upstanding residents of[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")! Hark the words and warning of a loyal fellow townsman - do not do business with that blackguard [de Jonkheer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rudolf_de_Jonkheer "Rudolf de Jonkheer")! Though his line is held in high esteem and considered honorable, he himself is a swine and a cheat. He sold me a residence at a most attractive price - knowing all the while the house was haunted! There can be no thought of inhabiting it, for an evil spirit torments all who set foot therein! For now the [ghost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") contents itself with rattlings and prankings, but who knows what foul deeds it will turn to in time!_
    _Let then anyone with half a bit of wit about them stay far away from said home, and never make a deal of any sorts with this defrauding de Jonkheer!_

_Prerequisite:_ This quest only becomes available if [Sarah](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarah "Sarah") is allowed to stay in the house during [Novigrad Dreaming](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Dreaming "Novigrad Dreaming"). 

## Walkthrough

You'll find the notice on [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Notice board \(Hierarch Square\)") directing you to re-visit the house. 

Head back there and inside to hear [Corinne Tilly](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Corinne_Tilly "Corinne Tilly") and Sarah, with the latter complaining about having braids put in. As you enter the bedroom it'll cut to Corinne telling you how she wanted to meet Sarah after learning she was responsible for the nightmares, but took a liking to the godling and bought the property so they could live together. The quest will then show a small outcome scene and then complete here. 

## Journal entry

    _While strolling the bustling streets of[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") came across a most intriguing notice. It suggested the man named [de Jonkheer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rudolf_de_Jonkheer "Rudolf de Jonkheer") had sold some poor fool the house which Sara, the lovable yet mischievous godling used as her dwelling and playground. The notice made it clear the new owner was not amused by his unwanted tenant's pranks. Geralt decided to check up on how Sara and see how she was doing..._
    _Geralt wasn't sure what he expected to find in the "haunted house" - but it sure wasn't that. The dreamer, having learned about Sara from Geralt, had decided to get to know her. They soon became fast friends and had begun living together in the de Jonkheer's former property. Corinne cared for the godling, and Sara made sure the home did not lose its haunted reputation._
