**Fencing Lessons** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

_Note:_ this quest is only available if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has won both fights against [Rosa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosa_var_Attre "Rosa var Attre") during the [Broken Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broken_Flowers "Broken Flowers") quest; if Rosa gained the upper hand at least once, she has no reason to ask Geralt for fencing lessons and so the quest won't start. 

_Important:_ if the first part of the quest isn't completed by the time [The Isle of Mists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Isle_of_Mists_\(quest\) "The Isle of Mists \(quest\)") is started, it will fail. 

## Walkthrough

After fencing with [Rosa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosa_var_Attre "Rosa var Attre") during the quest [Broken Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broken_Flowers "Broken Flowers"), return to the [var Attre villa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Var_Attre_Villa "Var Attre Villa") and say "Due to meet Rosa" to the [guard captain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Var_Attre_Guard_Captain "Var Attre Guard Captain") at the door. He will inform you that Rosa went to the Temple Bridge and asked you to join her there, earning 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

Upon meeting with Rosa, she'll show disdain at having a guard accompanying her, but manages to send him off and asks you to follow her. Answer however you like, then follow a short distance to the center of the bridge where she'll challenge you to a duel. Regardless of who wins the duel, Rosa will run off after tricking [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") into looking the other way, earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

Use your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses") to discover her footprints and Geralt will note she headed in the direction of the [Seven Cats Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Seven_Cats_Inn "Seven Cats Inn"). Follow them there to find Rosa being harassed by two drunks who realize she's a [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") and not too happy about it. You can then do the following: 

  * use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (requires Delusion level 1, earns 30 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * pay them 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") to leave (earns 5 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * fight and kill them

Afterwards you will talk about the fate of the drunks. Regardless of what you reply you'll part ways here and the quest will end, earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

## Journal entry

    _Geralt had learned that Rosa var Attre was a passionate devotee of the art of swordplay. The fencing lesson Geralt had given her while searching for yours truly had thrilled her to the core - and left her asking the witcher if they could meet again for a repeat. Geralt had plenty of experience teaching young women with fiery personalities how to swing swords and so agreed._
    _Once they were done with their lesson Geralt was supposed to escort the young lady home. Rosa, however, was clearly still in the mood for some recreation and gave him the slip during a moment of inattention. The witcher knew he needed to find her - or land himself in a heap of trouble._
    _Rosa found out for herself that not all residents of[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") respect diplomatic immunity. The witcher saved her from a tight spot - but also discovered a dark side to her character. It was clear to them both that there would be no third fencing lesson._

## Objectives

  * Go to the var Attre residence tomorrow to give Rosa fencing lessons.
  * Tell the guard captain about the fencing lessons.
  * Meet with Rosa.
  * Follow Rosa.
  * Defeat Rosa.
  * Use your Witcher Senses to find Rosa.
  * **If Geralt starts a fight with the men:**
    * Defeat the drunks harassing Rosa.
