## Walkthrough

In the village of [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken") on [Faroe Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle"), a man named [Timmon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timmon "Timmon") will call out by the docks and will explain that his brothers, [Mikkel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikkel_\(Skelliger\) "Mikkel \(Skelliger\)") and [Ulf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulf_\(Timmon%27s_brother\) "Ulf \(Timmon's brother\)"), have not yet returned from fetching his betrothed bride, [Agda](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Agda "Agda"), from [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") and the wedding day is approaching. He'll ask you to go check for him while he finishes the wedding preparations. If you agree to help him, you'll have to travel to the far northeastern end of Faroe Isle. 

_Note:_ depending on whether you've done the quests [Flesh for Sale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flesh_for_Sale "Flesh for Sale") and/or [Following the Thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Following_the_Thread_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Following the Thread \(The Witcher 3\)"), you may have to avoid fast travelling to [Trottheim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trottheim "Trottheim") because of the hostile pirates there. 

Once at the place and having dealt with a couple of [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") guarding the area, search the shores to find the dead body of a woman. You then have to use [Keira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")'s [magic lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_lamp_\(quest_item\) "Magic lamp \(quest item\)") on the dead body to see two ghosts crying over her whilst pleading to [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") for forgiveness for letting their brother's betrothed die under their watch, and her dowry lost after a storm beset them. 

You can then follow the ghosts' footprints further east to a tree to find the dead brothers, having committed suicide to preserve their honor rather than face their brother with the news. On one of the deceased bodies you can find a [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chest_key#Chest_key_\(Faroe\) "Chest key") and a [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_Agda%27s_father_to_Timmon "Letter from Agda's father to Timmon"). The letter suggests that there's a dowry to be found, but doesn't actually give an objective for it. From here, you can return to Timmon or dive in where the ship wrecked just off the coast from Agda's body. If you do the latter, you can unlock the chest there for a [gold diamond necklace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gold_diamond_necklace "Gold diamond necklace"). 

Return to Timmon and give him the bad news. If you retrieved the dowry, you will also have the chance to return it or lie and keep it for yourself with no penalty. The quest will then complete. ## Journal entry

    _In the[Clan Dimun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Dimun "Clan Dimun") village [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") spied a feverish man who was waiting for his brothers to bring his betrothed from one of the neighboring isles. Days had passed, their wedding drew nearer and nearer, and neither the brothers nor the betrothed had arrived. The man asked Geralt for help._
    _Sadly, this story had an unhappy ending. The groom's brothers and betrothed had been struck by a storm during their voyage. The girl had perished, her dowry lost. The brothers, as the harsh law of honor demands, decided to commit suicide. They could not bear to live knowing they had failed their brother by not protecting his beloved from danger._

## Objectives

  * Examine the coasts of Faroe on the side facing Hindarsfjall.
  * Search the shore using your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses").
  * **If Geralt doesn't have Keira's magic lamp:** ​ 
    * Return to this place once you have found another way to examine it.
  * Examine the beach near the dead girl using the magic lamp.
  * Follow the footsteps using your Witcher Senses.
  * Investigate the crime scene using your Witcher Senses.
  * Tell the young man what happened to his betrothed and his brothers. ## Bugs

  * If playing on a game before [Patch 1.10](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.10_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.10 \(The Witcher 3\)"), the chest to the dowry may be bugged and cannot be unlocked.

## Notes

  * Later on, if you revisit the spot where you found the dead bride, Timmon will be there praying, having set up a small altar to his betrothed. He also had carved two faces into the tree where the brothers had died.
  * Despite its name, dismantling the necklace yields only iron ore.
