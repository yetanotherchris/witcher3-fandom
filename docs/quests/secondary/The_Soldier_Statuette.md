**The Soldier Statuette** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. This quest can be chained with [The Nobleman Statuette](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nobleman_Statuette "The Nobleman Statuette") if both quests are available and neither has been finished. 

_Missable:_ The item to start this quest can only be accessed during [A Dangerous Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dangerous_Game "A Dangerous Game"). If it's not retrieved then, this quest cannot be started any other way except via console commands. 

_Note:_ this quest will fail if not completed before talking to [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") about the [Lodge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses") meeting during the main quest [Final Preparations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Final_Preparations "Final Preparations"). 

## Walkthrough

To start this quest, you must find and obtain the [statuette](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jade_figurine "Jade figurine") during the secondary quest [A Dangerous Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dangerous_Game "A Dangerous Game") inside [Caesar Bilzen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen "Caesar Bilzen")'s [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen%27s_house "Caesar Bilzen's house"). To then complete the quest, you just have to bring it to [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") who will revive the soldier. 

_Important:_ if you already completed [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), Triss will no longer be in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and you will have to wait until you finish the [Blindingly Obvious](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blindingly_Obvious "Blindingly Obvious") quest much later in the game. In fact, if Triss has already left Novigrad, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will note that when finding the statuette and the quest will not appear until you can freely talk to her again. 

## Journal entry

    _While visiting an accountant by the name of Caesar Bilzen, Geralt snatched a soldier figurine from his host's collection. While[I](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") usually deplore such acts of petty larceny as bad manners to say the least, this time it led to quite the interesting series of events, so I am prepared to let it slide. The statuette, you see, emanated [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") and our [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") sensed it might be tied to something of prime importance, so he decided to show it to Triss._
    _The witcher's premonition was... well, if not exactly right, still worth having followed. Triss cast a decompression spell on the figurine and it transformed into a living, breathing[human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human"). To be precise, it turned into a [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soldier whom the [sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") [Coral](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lytta_Neyd "Lytta Neyd") had transfigured in the midst of a [battle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Sodden_Hill "Battle of Sodden Hill") many years ago. Nothing of prime importance per se, but quite interesting nonetheless._
