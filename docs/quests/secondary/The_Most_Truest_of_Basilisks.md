## Walkthrough

_Note:_ this quest marker will not appear unless it's between 7am-6pm, otherwise the people and cage are nowhere to be seen. However, once the quest marker does populate, it can be completed no matter what time of the day it is. 

Just outside of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), northeast of [Novigrad Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Gate "Novigrad Gate") and southwest of the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut"), a [Beast Tamer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beast_Tamer "Beast Tamer") advertises a "[basilisk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk")" to a group of [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldiers. In actuality, it's a young, ill-fed [wyvern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern"). 

You can inquire further about this "basilisk" if you wish, or just go straight to pointing out it's not one. Once it's pointed out that it's a wyvern, the soldiers start to realize the man's a cheat and move to take him when the cage suddenly breaks and the wyvern gets out. Kill it to complete the quest.

## Notes

  * Once the fight begins, the Beast Tamer will take off running towards Novigrad Gate where he will cower in fear behind a small building. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can follow him until he stops running, but will not be able to interact with him.
  * This quest is a reference to a scene in _[Time of Contempt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Time_of_Contempt "Time of Contempt")_ , where it was [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") pointing out that it was a wyvern before the cage broke.
