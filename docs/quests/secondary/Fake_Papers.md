## Walkthrough

Due to the [war](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III"), [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") has closed off all the borders into [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") and is only allowing those with a pass through. Naturally, these passes aren't easy to obtain and thus the shady merchant has made some [counterfeits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Transit_pass "Transit pass") for those willing to pay for them. You can do the following: 

  * pay the full 100 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") (earning 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * ask for another way then use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (requires Delusion level 1, earning 30 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")). This will lower it to 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") and earn 5 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") when paid for
  * ask about the other way and he'll ask you to help his brother-in-law, [Albin Hart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albin_Hart "Albin Hart")

If you buy the pass outright, the quest will end here. However, if you inquire about the other option, you'll need to go do [Bitter Harvest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bitter_Harvest "Bitter Harvest") to get the price dropped to 25 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). _Important:_ If you go this route, make sure to keep Albin alive. If he dies and you report this to the merchant, he will refuse to deal with you and will cause the quest to fail. You can however still opt to not mention the matter and pick one of the other options above. 

If you went and helped Albin and he survived, the merchant will sell the pass to you for 25 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), earning 2[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). Regardless, as long as you obtained a pass, the quest will then complete. 

## Journal entry

    _During the war that pitted the[Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms "Northern Realms") against [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"), the border along the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)") river was closed. Whoever wanted to cross at the border posts had to show a pass issued by the Redanian high command. Or, barring that, one of the convincing counterfeits sold by the shady Redanian who hung about near the river crossings._
    **If Geralt buys the false papers:**

    _The false papers proved as good as real ones. Once[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") had acquired them, he could cross the border at will._
    **If Albin Hart dies:**

    _The conterfeiter made Geralt an offer he couldn't refuse: a falsified pass in exchange for helping the man's brother. Geralt eagerly agreed to the exchange, but didn't hold up his end of the bargain. The conterfeiter's brother died, and Geralt had to find another way of securing transit papers._

## Objectives

  * See what the counterfeit pass seller's brother-in-law needs.
  * Return for the discounted pass.

## Notes

  * If Albin dies before you can buy a pass from the merchant, you can still do [Contract: Woodland Beast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Woodland_Beast "Contract: Woodland Beast") or continue the quest [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters") to the point of learning where [Tamara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger") is to obtain one.
  * Even if you already have a pass from another source (like from [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters")) you can still do this quest. However, you'd have to buy the fake pass to actually complete this quest.
