## Walkthrough

Head to the first cave just west of [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") to find two warriors outside the entrance and approach to automatically talk with them to learn they plan to clear the cave of the [necrophages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage "Necrophage") inside. 

If you offer to help they'll accept your assistance. _Important:_ you need to keep **both of them** alive to complete the quest. However, they're both overzealous about glory and will recklessly run in, most likely getting themselves killed in the process. To counter this, try to use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") on at least one of them at the entrance to hopefully keep them at bay a short while. It's then possible to do it a second time inside. 

Head in and go to the right to run into a large group of [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") and [devourers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devourer "Devourer") and kill them off, making sure to watch your allies and their health bars. Throwing a [northern wind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_wind "Northern wind") bomb before engaging is a good idea. Once all these necrophages are dead, head further into the cave to find an [alghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul"). Once it's dead [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will automatically talk with the two warriors again, giving 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 60 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") as a reward, completing the quest. 

## Journal entry

    _Though[Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") are famed warriors able to hold their own in almost any battle, those whom Geralt encountered near the old mine on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") seemed to be in need of help. They had sworn to clear the mine of ghouls and were plucking up the courage to do so, but Geralt did not deem it likely they would manage the task on their own. When he proposed his expert assistance, they hesitated at first, but ultimately decided to take him up on the offer._
    **Successfully keep them alive:**

    _What tremendous good fortune it was that Geralt showed up at just the time and place where he was needed! With a touch of help from the warriors, he made swift work of that pack of nefarious necrophages and cleansed the surrounding area of danger, earning him the gratitude of all who dwelled nearby._

## Objectives

  * Help the warriors kill the necrophages in the old mine.

## Notes

  * Even if one warrior dies making you fail the quest, the other will still carry on, even going as far as trying to take on the alghoul. However, even if you help kill it off, the remaining warrior won't talk to you.
  * In the room with the alghoul, there's a chest that always contains a random relic sword.
