## Additional Information

### Type

[Secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests")

**Gwent: Velen Players** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that involves playing [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") to advance. This quest must be completed for those looking to earn the [Card Collector](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Card_Collector "Card Collector") [achievement](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_achievements "The Witcher 3 achievements"). 

    _**[Gwent: Looking for New Players](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent:_Looking_for_New_Players "Gwent: Looking for New Players")**_

    _The Sharpers, a[gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") playing society based at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), is looking for new members - and opponents._
    _Experience preferred, but we will also school newcomers to the game (but not return any coin lost during training matches)._
    _For more information see the club's chairman,[Phillip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger"), known as the Bloody Baron._

The [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") rewarded in this quest is unaffected by level constraint and [difficulty level](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Difficulty_level#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Difficulty level"). 

## Walkthrough

The quest may be started by either finding the notice on [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") or [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse")'s [notice boards](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board"), or directly by challenging the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") who uses the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"). Once defeated he will hand over the [Sigismund Dijkstra card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra_\(gwent_card\) "Sigismund Dijkstra \(gwent card\)") and give you the names of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")'s best players: [Haddy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Haddy "Haddy"), [Old Sage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Sage "Old Sage"), and a [boatwright](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boatwright "Boatwright"). 

_Note:_ As of [Patch 1.20](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.20_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.20 \(The Witcher 3\)"), if you didn't defeat him before the secondary quest [Return to Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Return_to_Crookback_Bog "Return to Crookback Bog"), you can find the Baron's card attached to the [list of players](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_baron%27s_notes "The baron's notes") in his office. However, it only appears there after the quest has been started, so if it's not active yet look for the notice. 

No matter how you obtain the list, now you can go play the other three in no particular order. 

The Old Sage is in [Benek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Benek "Benek") and plays the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck") and gives the [Crone: Weavess card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crone:_Weavess_\(gwent_card\) "Crone: Weavess \(gwent card\)"). 

Haddy is a young boy in Midcopse, but don't let his age fool you. He plays with the Northern Realms deck and if beaten will give the [Vernon Roche card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche_\(gwent_card\) "Vernon Roche \(gwent card\)"). 

The boatwright lives in [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton") and can be found near the middle of the village with the boats. He uses the [Nilfgaardian deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck") and will give the [Letho of Gulet card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letho_of_Gulet_\(gwent_card\) "Letho of Gulet \(gwent card\)"). 

Once you beat all of them, you'll earn the Monsters leader's card, [Eredin: Destroyer of Worlds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eredin:_Destroyer_of_Worlds_\(gwent_card\) "Eredin: Destroyer of Worlds \(gwent card\)"). 

## Journal entry

    _Though you might think it unlikely, even war-ravaged Velen boasted many prominent gwent players. They possessed certain valuable cards which Geralt wished to add to his collection. To do that, he needed to defeat them. The first on his "To Fleece" list: the Bloody Baron._
    _In the end, Geralt crossed every name off his list and acquired all the valuable cards he sought._

## Objectives

  * Win a unique card from the baron.
  * **If the Bloody Baron isn't defeated before his questline ends:**
    * Look for a unique card and information about other players in the baron's office.
  * Win a unique card from the man in Oreton.
  * Win a unique card from Haddy of Midcopse.
  * Win a unique card from the soothsayer.

## Bugs

  * After playing the Old Sage in Benek and completing the quest, he'll direct you to play Haddy in Midcopse, even if you've already beaten Haddy.
