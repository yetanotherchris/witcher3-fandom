## Walkthrough

This quest is started by speaking to the priest of the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") along the road just west of [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale"). The guards standing nearby will initially warn off [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") but the priest will ask you to approach. He'll be rather condescending but nonetheless asks you to do him a job by setting fire to several of the dead on the battlefield for a proper rite. He then gives you [holy oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_oil "Holy oil") to set afire at each location. 

The bodies can be burned in any order you choose with each spot having a number of enemies. 

  * Just southwest of the priest and his guards. A [rotfiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend "Rotfiend") and possibly a few wild [dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog") may block your path but once dispatched use the holy oil on the bodies in the pit and ignite them with [Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni").
  * Directly north of the first pit, over the hill and down into the thicket. Once the four [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") are defeated, set fire to the bodies in the pit.
  * A fair distance away to the southeast is a site with several ghouls.

No matter what order you did them, the first 2 pyres will give 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") each, however the last site you get to will have a single man fending off a pack of ghouls. Intervene quickly to save the man who will introduce himself as [Titus Gielas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Titus_Gielas "Titus Gielas"). _Important:_ Titus can die here so make sure to jump in quickly to keep him alive if you wish to learn more and get more of a reward. 

If he survived, Titus will immediately mention a priest and if you ask further on this, Titus will reveal that he was attacked and left to die there by the very same priest who contracted the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") to burn the bodies as the priest was a [fisstech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fisstech "Fisstech") customer of his but on the last trade decided to kill off Titus, then hired Geralt to burn the evidence, earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). However, if you chose "nonsense" instead, you won't earn the 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") or learn this information. Regardless what you chose, once the conversation is complete go burn the bodies to complete the objective, earning 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

Meet with the priest near the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post") and, depending on what you learned, you have a few options. 

  * If Titus died or you didn't question him about the priest, you'll only earn 20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") and 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")
  * If Titus was questioned about the priest: 
    * You can accept the priest's bribe, earning 60 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") and 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")
    * You can refuse the bribe, resulting in having to kill the 3 men. While you only earn 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") here, make sure to loot the priest's body for 200 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")

No matter what you choose, the quest will then end. 

## Journal entry

    _In[No Man's Land](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") Geralt was given a contract by an Eternal Fire priest. His task: to burn the bodies of soldiers who had fallen during the recent battle in order to keep [necrophages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage "Necrophage") from eating them._
    **If Geralt accepts the bribe, didn't question Titus, or Titus died:**

    _Though his contract giver had proven to be a hypocrite and a drug dealer, Geralt accepted his coin. As the ancient proverb says, pecunia non olet._
    **If Geralt doesn't accept the bribe:**

    _The witcher followed the priest's directions and went to burn the bodies. Doing so revealed the clergyman's true motivations: lighting the fire he had requested would also burn evidence of the priest's involvement in the fisstech trade..._

## Objectives

  * Find and burn the bodies lying in pits on the battle field. 3/3
  * Return to the priest of the Eternal Fire.

## Notes

  * With the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), if this quest was completed prior to [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow"), Geralt can mention his interaction with this quest's priest depending on the outcome, with a more pessimistic remark if he learned the truth or a more neutral one if he helped and didn't learn what the priest was doing.

## Bugs

  * Some players may experience a bug, where once you've burned all three pits and return to the priest, he'll still be asking if you finished burning them, even after finding Titus. Loading an older save is known to fix this.
