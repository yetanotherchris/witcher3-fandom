## Walkthrough

Locate the caravan heading along the dirt pathways near the ponds, northwest of [Marauders' Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marauders%27_Bridge "Marauders' Bridge"). An easy way to find it is by sticking to the path between this bridge and the [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fireburst_Harbor "Fireburst Harbor") further west. Sounds of commotion from up ahead will prompt you to interject in a fight between two [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") and a group of five bandits. 

Slay the men fighting the merchants then speak to the pair, [Anselm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anselm_\(merchant\) "Anselm \(merchant\)") and [Griswold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griswold "Griswold"), to learn their hiring methods are somewhat lacking. Despite their impoverished state, Anselm gives a reward of 25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") \+ 20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), concluding the quest. A week later, he will arrive at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), making Anselm a merchant in the village that you can sell and buy goods with. 

However, it is also possible that both merchants die, though very unlikely as they're quite resilient. If that happens, there is still a reward for killing the bandits. After they're finished off, a chest on the merchants' wagon will be filled with 20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), 5 [deer hides](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deer_hide "Deer hide"), 5 [bear hides](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_hide "Bear hide"), 5 spools of [thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thread "Thread"), and a stack of 4 [cured leathers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cured_leather "Cured leather"), plus the merchants' bodies can be looted as well - no [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"), but a lot more coin value.

## Notes

  * A [notice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice!_Delay_affecting_orders_placed_with_Anselm! "Notice! Delay affecting orders placed with Anselm!") for this quest exists in the game files, but has been [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") and doesn't appear during gameplay.
  * Some optional dialogue during the cutscene after the rescue has also been removed. With it, Geralt could say that the guards likely decided they weren't being paid enough, but Anselm would retort that he even paid them more than they were worth, because of rumors about [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") and other monsters on the road.
  * It is technically possible for Anselm to die, while Griswold survives, but this is yet untested and with an unknown outcome.

## Bugs

  * In the event that Griswold dies, he still appears alongside Anselm in the following cutscene. Immediately after, even his corpse can be looted, yet later dialogue at Crow's Perch still mentions the pair as being rescued together.
