## Walkthrough

This quest may be started at any of the Heroes' Pursuits horse race starts on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), by speaking to [Svana](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svana "Svana"), or finding the notice on [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Larvik\) "Notice board \(Larvik\)") in [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"). 

However you find it, the rules are simple: you must win at least each race once and it doesn't matter how you do it. Before you can compete in the championship against last year's winner, [Astrid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Astrid_the_Vipress "Astrid the Vipress"), you must win each of the following: 

  * [The Heroes' Pursuits: Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_Kaer_Trolde "The Heroes' Pursuits: Kaer Trolde")
  * [The Heroes' Pursuits: Fayrlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_Fayrlund "The Heroes' Pursuits: Fayrlund")
  * [The Heroes' Pursuits: Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_Fyresdal "The Heroes' Pursuits: Fyresdal")

After you win all of them, head back to Svana to let her know and you can then race against Astrid. This race will navigate towards [Freya's Garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya%27s_Garden "Freya's Garden") from the starting line at Larvik's western gate. Just be wary of a few sharp turns in the race, particularly near the garden. Also note that at the crossroads, while the path indicates you have to go into the sharp turn, you can cut through using the path right before it. The good thing is, even if you lose, you can keep racing against Astrid until you win. 

Once you win the race, Svana will reward you with the [Zerrikanian saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_saddle "Zerrikanian saddle") along with 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 90 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). 

## Journal entry

    _Those on the Continent worship gods by praying, burning incense and making offerings. In[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), all these are practiced, but the islanders consider the best way of honoring the gods to be risking one's life while performing feats of bravery. A favorite way to do this? Taking part in the Heroes' Dash, a race full of strong emotions and frightening dangers sure to make the goddess smile._
    _[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") is not the most pious of fellows, but the prize on offer for the race's winner (as well as the chance to test [Roach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roach "Roach")'s speed against the best Skellige mounts) convinced him to join in._
    _Geralt managed to win all of the qualifying heats and earn himself a spot in the Dash's final race, to be held on the isle of Hindarsfjall._
    _There he faced the winner of last year's Dash: Astrid the Vipress. Though she was as vast and dangerous as her name-snake, even she was unable to beat the[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). Geralt's victory brought glory to him and joy to the goddess - with a handsome prize making it all the sweeter._

## Objectives

  * **If Geralt found the notice:**
    * Talk to Svana the priestess about the Heroes' Dash.
  * Win each of the qualifying races at least once.
  * Go to the race's starting line.
  * Be the first past the finish line.
