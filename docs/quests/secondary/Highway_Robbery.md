## Walkthrough

Along the road between [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") and [Boatmakers' Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boatmakers%27_Hut "Boatmakers' Hut") you'll find what appears to be a trader calling for help as he's surrounded by 3 bandits. However, when you get close enough it's revealed the trader is a bandit himself and all 4 will try to attack you. Kill them off to complete the quest.
