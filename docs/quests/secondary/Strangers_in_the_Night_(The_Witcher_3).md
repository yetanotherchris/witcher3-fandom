## Walkthrough

On the banks of the river east of the [Eternal Fire Chapel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Chapel "Eternal Fire Chapel") and west of the [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence"), are three men around a campfire: [Manfred](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manfred_\(merchant\) "Manfred \(merchant\)"), [Rufus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rufus "Rufus"), and [Theophile Maria Rosco](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Theophile_Maria_Rosco "Theophile Maria Rosco"). You may approach and talk with them, but note that if you don't back out after introductions, someone will be killed. 

If you sit down to talk, introduce yourself as [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") if you don't want to be attacked. If you refuse to state your name, Rufus and Manfred get suspicious and will attack you, leaving you no choice but to kill them both. (25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")) 

After introductions, you can choose to leave now or ask about the world. This is your only chance to butt out and leave with no one dying (earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")). 

If you ask about the world, Manfred shows he's very knowledgeable about what's happening with the war, making Rufus suspicious and then decides to try and arrest Manfred, who refuses to go without a fight. You can tell Rufus "hands off the merchant" which will mean you have to kill Rufus (earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")) or tell them both to calm down. 

If you choose the latter, no matter your next choices Manfred will die, the only difference are the rewards: 

  * If you refuse to help, you get no [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") or reward and can't attack Rufus as he kills Manfred.
  * If you choose to help Rufus, he gives [Diagram: Skellige gambeson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Skellige_gambeson "Diagram: Skellige gambeson") as a reward after killing Manfred, along with 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level").

## Notes

  * If you sided with Rufus, he would note that Manfred had [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") [florens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Floren "Floren"), confirming his accusations. However, as Manfred is a merchant, there's nothing outright suspicious with him having Nilfgaardian currency.
  * In early versions of the game [Diagram: Cidarian cavalry armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Cidarian_cavalry_armor "Diagram: Cidarian cavalry armor") was awarded instead.
  * The characters will talk as if [Henselt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henselt "Henselt") had "perished in the battle for Lormark" even if the player chose Ioverth's Path or let Henselt live in Roche's Path, either through a save file import or by answering the questions in [Imperial Audience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Audience "Imperial Audience"). 
    * However, if you import a save where Roche killed Henselt then Theophile will also add "Blasted [Dragonslayer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Saesenthessis "Saesenthessis")."
