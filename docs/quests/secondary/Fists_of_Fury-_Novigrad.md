**Fists of Fury: Novigrad** is one of the [fistfighting](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_fistfighting "The Witcher 3 fistfighting") quests in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

    _**[Fist Fights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fist_Fights#Novigrad "Fist Fights")**_

    _Anyone looking to bash some heads will find plenty of worthy ones in our fair[city](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Those interested in joining the fun, sign up for our tournament. You'll face these most highly honorable gents: [Georgius Georg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Georgius_Georg "Georgius Georg"), known as the Piledriver, [Archibald O'Neill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archibald_O%27Neill "Archibald O'Neill"), [Captain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Captain "Captain") [Iron Mortimer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_Mortimer "Iron Mortimer") and [Durden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Durden "Durden"), known as the Tailor._

The [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") rewarded in this quest is unaffected by level constraint and [difficulty level](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Difficulty_level#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Difficulty level"). 

## Walkthrough

This quest may be started via its notice on [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Notice board \(Hierarch Square\)") or simply encountering one of the spots where [fist fights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_fistfighting "The Witcher 3 fistfighting") take place. 

The first location is [The Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon") where you'll find [Master Claytop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Claytop "Master Claytop"). Talk to him to setup a match between you and [Georgius 'Piledriver' Georg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Georgius_Georg "Georgius Georg"). Before you start the fight though, Claytop will pull you to the side and asks that you throw the match, and will pay you 80 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), on top of doubling what you bet (meaning you could get a max. of 240 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")). You'll also be able to net an extra 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") by throwing it. 

If you agree and lose you'll be able to fight Georgius again for real. You can also simply decline and fight for the win (note that agreeing doesn't mean you have to lose either, you can choose to go back on your word). If you choose not to throw the fight, later you may be attacked in Novigrad by three Bandits saying "We'll show you not to mess with Master Clayton!" 

Regardless what you choose, once you defeat Georgius you'll earn 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

The other two opponents are simple fistfights: 

  * [Archibald O'Neill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archibald_O%27Neill "Archibald O'Neill") is located just inside [Portside Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portside_Gate "Portside Gate")'s archway, in the small fenced in area
  * [Iron Mortimer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_Mortimer "Iron Mortimer") is located to the west of [Southern Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Southern_Gate "Southern Gate")

Once you defeat all three, you'll be directed to [Durden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Durden "Durden"), out in [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners"). Despite being told to fight after sundown, they'll be out there at any time of day to fight. Once Durden is defeated you'll earn 75 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and double your bet, completing the quest.

    _Geralt found out a series of fistfights was being organized in[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He had participated in such fights in the past and grown to like the feel of his fist pounding into some lunkhead's skull. He thus decided to take part._

    _Fight after fight Geralt laid low the best Novigrad had to offer. In the end he was declared the city's champion._

## Objectives

  * Defeat Georgius Georg.
  * Defeat Archibald O'Neill.
  * Defeat Iron Mortimer.
  * Fight the Tailor for the Novigrad title.

## Bugs

  * Claytop may ask Geralt to make the fight entertaining, but not actually request he throw the fight. This will result in no reward and Claytop will give the same stern talking as if Geralt hadn't thrown the fight.
  * Geralt will be told to fight the Tailor after sundown, but in fact he can start the fight anytime of day.
  * The Iron Mortimer fight is often bugged, resulting in an invisible barrier preventing Geralt from closing in on him. He can still hit Mortimer if the timing is right, but even when Mortimer's health is depleted, he is not knocked out. He might also retreat to an area beyond the fighting zone, both circumstances making the fight impossible to win. A verified workaround indicated on Reddit: Talk to the Bookie, but be sure to ask about the fight organizers. Then leave the conversation, do an manual save, and then ask to start the fight again. After this, the fight proceeds as normal.

## Trivia

  * When Durden first approaches Geralt, his speech is a reference to the film _A Knight's Tale_.
  * Durden the Tailor is a reference to Tyler Durden in _Fight Club_.
  * Georgius Georg is a reference to Gorgeous George, an American professional wrestler. 
    * Georgius Georg and Claytop are also references to the Guy Ritchie film, _Snatch_.
