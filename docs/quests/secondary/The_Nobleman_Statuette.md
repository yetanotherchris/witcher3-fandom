## Additional Information

### Type

[Secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests")

**The Nobleman Statuette** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. This quest can be chained with [The Soldier Statuette](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Soldier_Statuette "The Soldier Statuette") if both quests are available and neither has been finished. 

_Note:_ this quest will fail if not completed before talking to [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") about the [Lodge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses") meeting during [Final Preparations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Final_Preparations "Final Preparations"). 

## Walkthrough

No real challenge, just buy the [jade figurine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jade_figurine "Jade figurine") from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Gildorf\) "Merchant \(Gildorf\)") in [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") (he also sells the [lizard figurine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lizard_figurine "Lizard figurine") required for [Of Dairy and Darkness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Dairy_and_Darkness "Of Dairy and Darkness")), and bring it to [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"), who will revive the poor fellow. 

The only real problem is that if you already completed [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never") Triss will no longer be in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and you will have to wait until [Blindingly Obvious](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blindingly_Obvious "Blindingly Obvious") is completed much later in the game. 

## Journal entry

    _While examining a Novigrad stallkeeper's wares,[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") spied a remarkable jade statuette. It was in the form of a nobleman and emanated a strange energy. The [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") decided to purchase it and show it to Triss._
    _This turned out to be a wise move. The sorceress used her magic and lifted the spell on the object, transforming it back into a Temerian nobleman. From his tale they gathered he had been compressed into an object by the sorceress[Coral](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coral "Coral") some years ago. Grateful for being rescued, the nobleman gave Geralt a reward and went off to piece his life back together._
