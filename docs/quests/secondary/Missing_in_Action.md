I'd bury him 'neath the barrow where our parents lie, not leave his corpse to be ate by the [corpsers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") prowling the battleground._
    _So I seek a man brave, able with a sword, and willing to venture out with me to find Bastien. I won't pay much, for I've not much to give, but I'm not stingy with gratitude and sure to show it aplenty._

_Note:_ this quest will fail if not completed before finishing [Lilac and Gooseberries](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lilac_and_Gooseberries "Lilac and Gooseberries"). It is also not accessible until meeting the Griffin of White Orchard for the first time during this main quest. 

## Walkthrough

If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") discovered this quest via the notice on the notice board in White Orchard he will first have to find Dune. Dune Vildenvert can be found muttering to a dog sitting outside a destroyed hut in the ransacked village. When Geralt speaks to him, Dune explains the search he's undertaken to find his lost brother [Bastien](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastien_Vildenvert "Bastien Vildenvert") and offers a reward if you aid him. 

After agreeing to help Dune find his brother, he'll ask Geralt to meet him at the [battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_\(White_Orchard\) "Battlefield \(White Orchard\)"). Follow him there or meet him whenever is convenient. Once Geralt speaks to Dune at the battlefield he will advise searching the site for [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") shields with flowers drawn on them, which indicate White Orchard recruits. 

Geralt will have to defeat a few [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") that are prowling the battlefield. Use [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses") to locate the Temerian shields if necessary, although they are rather easy to spot and their blue color makes them stand out. After inspecting the burned corpse of a fallen soldier (near the woods) [Hussar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hussar "Hussar") will pick up the scent of Bastien. 

Follow the dog or use Witcher Senses to follow the dog's trail of prints to an abandoned cabin. As Geralt approaches the cabin a cutscene occurs. Geralt finds a badly wounded Bastien sitting and talking with a wounded [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soldier, [Rhosyn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rhosyn "Rhosyn"). Bastien explains how they helped each other reach this cabin after they were both wounded and demands that Dune help both him and Rhosyn return home. Dune is skeptical about taking in a Nilfgaardian deserter which leaves the decision of Rhosyn's fate to Geralt. 

If Geralt says it's too risky for Dune and his family to harbor a deserter, Rhosyn will die in the cabin. If you convince Dune to help the Nilfgaardian because Rhosyn helped save Bastien, then he can be found later living with Dune and his family. Either decision ends the quest and you are rewarded with a bit of coin and XP. 

If, however, you found the men at the cabin before speaking to Dune, they'll instead ask you to find Dune. When you do, he will rescue Bastien, but leave Rhosyn to die (as Geralt isn't there to convince Dune otherwise). Even if you should follow Dune all the way to the cabin, he'll simply say thanks, cutting out any option to try and speak up for Rhosyn.

## Objectives

  * Find Dune Vildenvert.
  * Meet Dune near the battlefield.
  * Using your Witcher Senses, search for Temerian shields with small white flowers painted on them.
  * Follow the dog's trail.

## Notes

  * If you leave for [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") before completing this quest you can find the bodies of Bastien and Rhosyn later.
  * If you convinced Dune to take Rhosyn in, everyone (including the woman Dune mentioned and the dog) can be found later at their farm to the east of White Orchard village. The woman, Liesje, can actually be found at the farm at any time. 
    * If Emhyr wins the war, visiting their farm will show Nilfgaardian soldiers surrounding it and all except a crying Liesje are gone. It's unclear though what happened to them.
  * When first passing Dune's camp during Lilac and Gooseberries with [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir"), cut voice lines involved them asking each other why he would talk to his dog, and Vesemir suggesting Geralt get involved as he did not want to. Talking to Dune at this point would add an extra line of dialogue asking if he had seen [Yennefer of Vengerberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") and describing her, before he switches over to explaining his situation with Bastien, starting the quest.
