**The Bastion** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

_Prerequisite:_ if you haven't completed the secondary quest [Magic Lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Lamp_\(quest\) "Magic Lamp \(quest\)"), the second part of this quest will be unreachable. 

## Walkthrough

You'll find the [Bastion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastion "Bastion") west of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), if you are doing [Scavenger Hunt: Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Wolf_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Wolf School Gear") you'll be directed there in search of the [Diagram: Wolven steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_steel_sword "Diagram: Wolven steel sword"). 

The Bastion will contain [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") so be on your guard. After killing all the wraiths, use the [magic lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_lamp_\(quest_item\) "Magic lamp \(quest item\)") to find 5 locations where the lamp can be used. It can be hard to see the spots with the sun shining, so you may want to meditate until night time. 

There is 1 on the ground level, 3 on the second level and 1 inside a ruined tower where you'll find the [bones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boy%27s_remains "Boy's remains") of a young [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") trainee who was stabbed with a pitchfork. The first 4 will show images of the boy's training days, and seeing them will give the objective to find his remains. 

To reach the tower where the remains are found, you'll need to climb on the wall using a ladder located on the eastern wall. There you can see the last lamp projection as well, showing the boy's [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") instructor tell him and other trainees to hide there. When you pick up the remains, more wraiths will spawn below. The ladder can be particularly tricky to use downwards though, so be careful not to fall. If you must, drop down without using the ladder, but to avoid fall damage, do it exactly at the slightly elevated spot where it is located. 

Once the wraiths are dealt with, go to the marked location to bury the remains and the quest will finish and advance you to the morning time. 

## Journal entry

    _An observation tower located on a hillside near Kaer Morhen caught[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s eye. Though the structure had been there for as long as he could remember, this was the first time in ages he had bothered to inspect it more closely. When he did, he discovered a whole heap of wraiths had taken up residence within. I've said it before and I'll say it again - witchers make horrible housekeepers._
    _Geralt was able to deduce from the evidence at hand that the fortress was haunted by the ghost of a boy who had once trained to be a witcher. His monster slaying career had been tragically nipped in the bud when enraged fanatics attacked Kaer Morhen._
    _Geralt found and buried the poor lad's remains, and along with them two swords, one of[silver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword"), the other of [steel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_sword "Steel sword"). He hoped this would give the boy's tortured soul some peace and allow him to depart from the old bastion._

## Objectives

  * Eliminate the wraiths haunting the old bastion.
  * Explore the old bastion near Kaer Morhen using your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses").
  * Use [Keira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")'s lamp to investigate the old bastion.
  * Look for the boy's remains around the bastion using your Witcher Senses.
  * Use Keira's lamp to find out what happened to the boy.
  * Pick up the boy's remains.
  * Bury the boy's remains.
