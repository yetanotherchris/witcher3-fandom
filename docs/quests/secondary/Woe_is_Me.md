## Walkthrough

If you approach the [Palisade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Palisade "Palisade") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") from the west, you'll see a peasant calling out for help. However, if you try to talk to him he'll suddenly call out to 2 others in hiding behind the Palisade, undoubtedly looking to rob and kill you. Simply kill them off to finish the quest. 

Note that you can also simply bypass the "wounded" peasant altogether: if you do and go through the Palisade, all 3 will automatically become hostile as you've spotted the hiding men.
