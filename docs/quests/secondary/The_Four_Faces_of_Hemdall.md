## Walkthrough

Northwest of [Trottheim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trottheim "Trottheim") on [Faroe Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle") you'll come across a shrine being blocked by several pirates, who refuse to let a pilgrim pass unless they pay. Approach and a few choices can be made: 

  * pay 20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). They'll make some remarks but ultimately let you and the pilgrim through without incident (also gives 2 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * threaten them (will lead to a fight)
  * be attacked (this happens if [Hammond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hammond "Hammond") was killed during the secondary quest [Following the Thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Following_the_Thread_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Following the Thread \(The Witcher 3\)"), in which case they'll recognize [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"))

Regardless of how they're dealt with, the pilgrim will thank you and can tell you about [Hemdall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall "Hemdall") if you wish. You'll then earn 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and the quest will end.

## Notes

  * There are three Hemdall shrines across Faroe that can be prayed at (you have to climb up the shrines to the statues to do so). The three are: 
    * the northern shrine (the one the pirates are blocking)
    * the western shrine, located right next to the [Place of Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Place_of_Power "Place of Power")
    * the central southern shrine, south of the hidden treasure marker
  * There's also an obelisk with the same markings in the central part of the island but it can't be interacted with.
