## Walkthrough

Walk in front of [the Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon"), and you will come across two drunk butchers asking for some coins. You have three options: 

  * give them 25 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") (2[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * cast [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (requires [Delusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Delusion "Delusion") level 1 for a minimum of 25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * refuse to help

If you refuse to help, one of the drunkards will get mad while his companion warns him not to start a fight with you. You will then be given another chance to choose through the former options. If you still refuse and tell them to leave, a fistfight with the aggressive one ensues. After defeating the drunkard, he will concede and the quest will end. (25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")) If the drunkard manages to defeat you, you will wake up near a gutter with a small amount of coin missing.
