**Racists of Novigrad** is an unmarked [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Walkthrough

This quest is encountered in two parts, where generally the female elf has to be done first before the second part with the male elves will appear. 

### Female Elf

Head north from [Portside Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portside_Gate "Portside Gate") into [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") where, at the second intersection, you'll come across a couple townsmen harassing an [elf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf"). The men are convinced she's lying and up to something. You can ask why she doesn't tell the truth but she'll stay quiet. You then have two options: leave or defend her. 

If you leave, the men will insult her once more before letting her go. If you defend her, the men will back off knowing you can easily kill them. Despite this, no matter what you say to the elf, she'll still be bitter and sarcastically responds to you before leaving. 

### Male Elves

Northeast of [Tretogor Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tretogor_Gate "Tretogor Gate"), where the dye pits are, you'll find some townsmen harassing some elves. You can choose to interfere and tell the [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") to leave the elves alone, or go your own way. If you stand up for the elves you'll have to the kill the townsmen. Afterwards, you can warn the elves to leave Novigrad as eventually [The Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Eternal_Fire "The Eternal Fire") will come after them or simply say farewell. Regardless, they're annoyed with their limited choices but the quest will complete here. 

_Cutoff point:_ this encounter is unavoidable during the [Contract: An Elusive Thief](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_An_Elusive_Thief "Contract: An Elusive Thief") quest where you have to chase the Imp exactly near these elves. Stopping then to deal with them will fail the contract as the Imp gets away.
