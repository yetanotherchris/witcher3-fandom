_Important:_ if the first part of the quest isn't completed by the time [Ghosts of the Past](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghosts_of_the_Past "Ghosts of the Past") is started, it will not be available until after Ghosts of the Past is finished. 

_Note:_ The [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") rewards in this quest will decrease after reaching level 11 (7 before [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)")). 

## Walkthrough

Just northwest of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch"), on the main road, you can come across a cart full of dead [plague](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Catriona_plague "Catriona plague") victims and several [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") running around with a man trapped under the cart. _Proximity sensitive:_ once the quest appears in your logs, don't travel too far away, otherwise it'll fail. 

Kill the ghouls and talk to the man, [Gert Borel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gert_Borel "Gert Borel"), to find out he ran into some bad luck where his cart's axle broke and when the ghouls howled, his horse spooked and tipped over the cart, burying him under the dead bodies. He'll then point out this is how he makes his living: carting away the bodies of plague victims as he's adamant he won't catch it as he never gets ill. 

You can then depart now, or convince him (10[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")) he needs to burn the cart and the bodies. 

If you choose to burn the cart and bodies, simply use [Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni") to set fire to the cart (10[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")) and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will then tell Gert to burn his clothes and scrub himself down, though Gert will remark it seems unnecessary as he never catches anything. _Important:_ if you told him to burn it, you need to burn it while still here. Once you travel too far away, the quest will automatically fail if you didn't. 

While the quest will finish here, no matter what you told him, several weeks later you can find Gert between [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor") and [Benek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Benek "Benek"), between several [Nekker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker") [nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"). You then discover that he did, in fact, catch the plague and he asks Geralt to kill him, thus sparing him the misery. You can either choose to kill him or not, as there are no repercussions for either choice. If you choose to kill him (earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")), you can set fire to his body like the other plague victims. 

## Journal entry

    _On one of[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")'s muddy roads Geralt came across an unfortunate soul trapped under his cart. The man had had an accident while hauling corpses of plague victims. The [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") decided to help him._
    **If Geralt chooses to help with the cart:**

    _Geralt helped the man right his cart. He also advised him to burn everything he was wearing and give his body a good scrubbing, yet the man brushed off this bit of sage counsel, claiming no contagion could touch him._
    **If Gert's request is refused:**

    _Some time later Geralt met the cart driver again and discovered he was now in the last stages of a terminal illness. The man asked the witcher to put him out of his misery, but Geralt refused._
    **If Gert is killed:**

    _Some time later Geralt met the cart driver again and discovered he was now in the last stages of a terminal illness. The man asked the witcher to to put him out of his misery, and Geralt glumly agreed to do so._

## Objectives

  * Help the trapped man.
  * **If you tell him to burn the cart:**

## Bugs

  * If you agree to kill Gert, journal entries for refusing to kill Gert and for killing him will both appear.
  * Sometimes after one chooses to kill Gert, an alive version of him will spawn near the dead body, preventing you from setting fire to it. However, as the quest concludes after killing him here, you don't need to burn the body.
  * It has been reported that even after completing the quest, the exclamation quest mark still appears at the spot on the map and there is no available quest.

## Notes

  * Despite him giving you coin in the dialogue if you choose to kill him, no coin is actually added to one's inventory.
  * If you tell Gert that you won't kill him, he becomes immune to damage. "You cannot attack this target," and Dragon's Dream and Igni will knock him down, but not kill him.
