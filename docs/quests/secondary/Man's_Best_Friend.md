## Walkthrough

Along the road northwest of [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale"), you'll find a lone hut with a wild [dog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog") outside, trying to get in. Kill it off and, if you don't interact with the door right away, you'll hear children talking inside wondering about you. 

Interact with the door and a girl will answer, who'll reveal they haven't had much to eat and are hungry. If you wish, you can ask her more like where are their parents, but she'll reveal they're all essentially alone: orphans or kicked out of their own homes as there were too many mouths to feed. You can then choose to leave or help them. 

If you help them (either by giving them food or 25 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")) the girl will thank you and give you a few things, notably 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and the book [In Beast's Clothing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_Beast%27s_Clothing "In Beast's Clothing") (which can give the [werewolf decoction manuscript](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Werewolf_decoction "Torn-out page: Werewolf decoction") and bestiary entries for [werewolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") and [ulfhedinn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulfhedinn "Ulfhedinn") if you don't have them already) completing the quest.
