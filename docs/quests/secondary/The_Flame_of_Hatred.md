## Walkthrough

To the east of [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") you'll find an [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") preacher condemning [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage"), alchemists, and especially [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). Once you're close enough he'll start to personally insult you until you interact with him, where you can choose from 3 different options: 

  * ask how many lives he's saved
  * threaten him to shut up
  * just leave

Any of them will earn 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and the crowd will disperse, concluding this part of the quest. 

However, if you chose any option but to leave, a few days later you'll be approached by two guards next to a well in [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") (right in the middle between [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and [Southern Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Southern_Gate "Southern Gate")) with orders to interrogate you, accusing [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") of offending religious sentiment. You then have two choices: hand over your weapons or refuse. Regardless what you choose, the guards will then remind you of the preacher you humiliated before attacking. If you surrendered your weapons you'll have to use your fists or other methods to fight them. After they're successfully taken care of, the quest will then end, earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). If you handed your weapons over remember to loot them back.
