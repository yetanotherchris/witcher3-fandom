**Take What You Want** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It only becomes available if [Gaetan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gaetan "Gaetan") is spared in the DLC quest [Where the Cat and Wolf Play...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Where_the_Cat_and_Wolf_Play... "Where the Cat and Wolf Play..."). 

## Walkthrough

Head to the location north of [Keira Metz's hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz%27s_hut "Keira Metz's hut"), where you'll find 3 bandits standing around the entrance to Gaetan's stash. Kill them off, then climb down into the hole where the chest is right there, containing 250 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), [Teigr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Teigr "Teigr"), and a [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_to_Gaetan "Letter to Gaetan"), completing the quest. However, you can also examine the trophies in the small alcove as well if you wish. 

## Journal entry

    _Gaetan probably hadn't expected that[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") would spare his life. Out of gratitude, he told Geralt where he'd hidden a stash of goods and said he could take from there whatever he wanted. Then the two [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") parted ways - and never met again.  
It turned out local bandits had found Gaetan's stash first. Yet before they could seize it and liquidate its assets, Geralt liquidated them._

## Objectives

  * Go to the [School of the Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Cat "School of the Cat") witcher's stash to collect your reward.

## Bugs

  * Despite this quest only being in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), the quest log shows the "multiple locations" icon and won't show a quest marker on the map unless you are in Velen or Novigrad.

## Notes

  * If Gaetan was not spared, his hideout can still be explored though there is no valuable item inside and Geralt does not make any comment on the trophies.
  * Unlike other witcher gear, [Teigr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Teigr "Teigr") will scale to your level when found, so it may be a good idea to leave this quest for last if you're preparing for a [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +").

## Trivia

  * In Gaetan's hideout, the letter is an easter egg reference to [Schrödinger's Cat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schr%C3%B6dinger%27s_Cat "wikipedia:Schrödinger's Cat").
