## Walkthrough

This quest may be started via its notice on [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Notice board \(Crow's Perch\)") or simply encountering one of the spots where [fist fights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_fistfighting "The Witcher 3 fistfighting") take place. 

[Stan Fishgulper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stan_Fishgulper "Stan Fishgulper") can be found in front of [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale")'s [inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale_tavern "Lindenvale tavern"). Before you start, he'll ask to speak to you in private. He knows he can't beat you, but he really needs the prize, a sack of flour, so he can have some food. You can choose to throw the match or not. Problem is you already placed your bet, so if you bet the maximum 70[![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") this will be a poor choice for you, so if you intend to do it then it's best to reload the game and place the minimum bet before the match which is a measly 1[![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). If you do throw it, you can then do a re-match, this time beating him. If you don't throw the match, you can speak with Fishgulper after the fight to offer him money so he can buy food for his family (5 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")), which he will happily accept. Once you beat him you'll earn 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

The other two regular fighters, [Jonah](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonah "Jonah") and [Smithy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Smithy "Smithy"), will fistfight as normal, earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") after each win. Once you've defeated all 3 (doesn't matter which order) you can then face the [Sergeant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sergeant_Ardal "Sergeant Ardal") in [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). 

The group will be fistfighting in the courtyard near the barn. Interact with him and place your bet. Once you defeat the Sergeant, the quest will complete, earning 75 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 160 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). 

Note: The Sergeant will not be available to challenge until after the conclusion of the main quest [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters"). Approaching him before this will only result in him dismissing Geralt with "Nothing here for you." 

## Journal entry

    _When[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") found out a fistfighting circuit was operating in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), he decided to test his strength and enter. You must know Velen bouts were remarkable for their dirty and no-holds-barred fighting, but to someone who fights monsters dirtily and leaves no holds untried on a daily basis, this was no worry._
    _As any half-intelligent odd-maker would have predicted, Geralt defeated all the regional champions and was granted to the right to fight for the Velen title._
    _The final bout was no walk in the park, but thanks to his well-honed skills and a bit of luck, Geralt emerged victorious. From then on he was known throughout Velen as "That Bloody Outsider Who Defeated Our Best Lads."_

## Objectives

  * Defeat Smithy.
  * Defeat Fishgulper.
  * Defeat Jonah.
  * Fight the sergeant for the Velen title.

**Bugs**

When at Crows Perch, the Sergeant will not acknowledge Geralt, instead staying in the Bloody Barons barracks room. Not sure how, why this happens even though all other fights are won. (This is due to him not being available to fight until after the Baron leaves Crow's Perch at the conclusion of Family Matters).
