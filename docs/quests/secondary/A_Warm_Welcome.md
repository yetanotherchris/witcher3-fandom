## Walkthrough

Head along the road southwest from [Wheat Fields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wheat_Fields "Wheat Fields"), towards the riverbank to find several [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") [soldiers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Soldier "Soldier") and some refugees arguing, as the refugees are trying to flee [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). If you approach you can ask to let them through or agree with the soldier in that they need to leave. 

If you ask for them to be let through, you have a few options: 

  * pay 100 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") to let them through
  * use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (requires Delusion level 2, gives 35 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * threaten him (will result in fighting the 3 soldiers)
  * simply leave

As long as you help the refugees, you'll earn 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"), with the refugee telling you about a hero's tomb near [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren") that's supposed to have treasure. The quest will then conclude here.
