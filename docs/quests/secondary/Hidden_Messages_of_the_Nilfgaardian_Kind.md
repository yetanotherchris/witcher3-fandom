## Walkthrough

Head north along the road that's to the west of the [Crooked house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crooked_house "Crooked house"). As you near the archway just before the morgue location, you'll see two shady individuals, one standing guard and the other crouching. As you get close enough they'll finish whatever they were doing and leave and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will make a small remark. Note that this interaction is very easy to miss as no marker shows up in the quest. 

Head to the archway they were standing near and use your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses") to see a brick in the wall and interact with it to get a [note left by spy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Note_left_by_spy "Note left by spy"). Read it to learn the next drop location is near [Crippled Kate's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crippled_Kate%27s "Crippled Kate's"), earning 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

Head to the brothel area where, in the archway just to the east, you'll find another hidden brick and message, this time with a [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ornamented_chest_key "Ornamented chest key"). Read it for the final location (earning another 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")). 

Head to the [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district, near the well, and open up the grate to head down into the sewers. As you head toward the search area here, three [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowned_dead "Drowned dead") will appear (one will be behind you). Once they're killed off, in the dead end hall you'll find the bodies of those who were trying to make the trade. Loot the locked chest to complete the quest, earning 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). Inside you will find the final note, as well as 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") among other things. 

## Journal entry

    _During his travels through[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") witnessed an extraordinary event. Right before his eyes a mysterious man put something in a hidden dropbox located in the wall of a building. Geralt looked into the box and found a note which directed him to the next location where secret notes were left._
    _Geralt followed the thread back to its source and wound up at a place where[Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") spies loaded and unloaded contraband. Geralt might not take a keen interest in politics, but he noted the [emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor") certainly seemed to be taking a keen interest in Novigrad._
