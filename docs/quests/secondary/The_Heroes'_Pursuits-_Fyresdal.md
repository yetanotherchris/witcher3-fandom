## Additional Information

### Type

[Secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests")

**The Heroes' Pursuits: Fyresdal** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is one of the four Heroes' Pursuits quests which involve [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") racing. 

The [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") rewarded in this quest is unaffected by the ±5 level constraint and [difficulty level](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Difficulty_level#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Difficulty level"). 

## Walkthrough

Between [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare") and the [Palisade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Palisade "Palisade"), near [Kvalheim Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kvalheim_Isle "Kvalheim Isle"), you'll find [Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") getting ready to race. Talk to the race master here and let them know when you're ready to race. 

Note that while there are no obstacles, a storm will hit shortly after you start no matter what, though this shouldn't really have an effect on the race itself. The race is fairly long: you'll race through the Palisade and all the way to [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal"). Once you've won you'll gain 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"), 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), and the [cavalry saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cavalry_saddle "Cavalry saddle"). 

## Journal entry

    _The glory of Modron[Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") knows no bounds and in Skellige everyone pays their resects_[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")]_to this goddess. In Fyresdal and many other, similar locales, the Great Mother was honored by holding horse races.[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") decided to take part._
    _Skelligers make fine riders, but Geralt is no spring chicken himself when it comes to horse racing. Thanks to his well-honed skills,[Roach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roach "Roach")'s effort and the goddess' favor, the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") triumphed._

## Objectives

  * Go to where the race starts.
  * Ride as fast as you can and win!
