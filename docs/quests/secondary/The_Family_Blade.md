## Walkthrough

Pick up this quest from [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Kaer_Trolde_Harbor\) "Notice board \(Kaer Trolde Harbor\)") about the stolen sword, then make your way to the the obelisk southwest of [Miners' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Miners%27_Camp "Miners' Camp") to find a group of renegades leaving you no choice but to cut them down. Once done, loot one of the bodies for the [Thief's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thief%27s_journal "Thief's journal") and read it to learn your next location is [Fort Grymmdjarr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fort_Grymmdjarr "Fort Grymmdjarr"), which is southwest of [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal"). 

At this next location are more bandits so make quick work of them and loot their bodies for yet another [Thief's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thief%27s_journal "Thief's journal"), this time pointing to the [Whale Graveyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale_Graveyard "Whale Graveyard"). Head there to deal with the last group of thieves and loot them for the third and final [Thief's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thief%27s_journal "Thief's journal") along with the stolen sword, [Kuliu](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kuliu "Kuliu"). 

Now with the stolen sword in hand, head back to Kaer Trolde Harbor and speak with [Olaf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olaf_\(guardsman\) "Olaf \(guardsman\)") on the bridge who'll be very pleased you recovered it. He'll reward you with 20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") and 75 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and the quest will then complete. 

## Journal entry

    _[Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skelliger "Skelliger") value three things above all else: honor, the memory of their ancestors and sturdy weapons. That is why [Crach an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite") flew into a fury when he found out someone had stolen his family blade, Kuliu, from the Kaer Trolde treasure room. The infuriated jarl promised a fat bounty to whoever could find his lost sword and punish the thief._

  * Track down the thieves at the old fort.
  * Kill the thieves.
  * Search the thieves' camp for the sword named Kuliu.
  * Read the journal to learn about the sword named Kuliu.
  * Look for thieves near the whale graveyard.
  * Kill the thieves.
  * Search the thieves' camp for the sword named Kuliu.
  * Return Kuliu to the Kaer Trolde guardsman named Olaf.
