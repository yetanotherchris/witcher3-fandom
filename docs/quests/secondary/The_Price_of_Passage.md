**The Price of Passage** is an unmarked [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can encounter some guards trying to extort some coin out of him. 

_Prerequisite:_ the "drunk" section of [Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Oxenfurt_Drunk "Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk") must be completed before this quest becomes available. 

The [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") rewarded in this quest is unaffected by the ±5 level constraint and [difficulty level](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Difficulty_level "Difficulty level"). 

## Walkthrough

When traveling in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), you will be approached by two [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldiers asking for a "war tax." They can be found under the archway leading from the [Oxenfurt Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Harbor "Oxenfurt Harbor") signpost to the marketplace. You have two options: 

### Option 1: Pay Them

This will cost you 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") but earn a total of 15 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") after the conversation ends. A few days later, you'll be able to find these guards on the western end of [Novigrad Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Gate "Novigrad Gate"), where they'll once more ask for money, this time 100 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). If you pay again you'll earn a total of 20 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") this time and you'll never see them after this, but if you refuse you'll only earn 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and you may run into them on the eastern outskirts of Oxenfurt a few days later. If this is the case, they'll be angry at you for getting them into trouble. Regardless if you fight them with your fists or kill them with your sword, the quest will then end. 

### Option 2: Refuse to Pay

They will call their superior and accuse you of being a spy but the superior will not take the bait and will threaten to send them straight into battle for extorting money. The quest then finishes and you can be on your way. After some time, they can be encountered again east of Novigrad Gate (right next to two houses, close to the quest [The Most Truest of Basilisks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Most_Truest_of_Basilisks "The Most Truest of Basilisks")). You'll have the option to use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (and earn 40 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")), pay them 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") (for 5 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")), or engage them in a fist fight (or other weapons for a more lethal approach).
