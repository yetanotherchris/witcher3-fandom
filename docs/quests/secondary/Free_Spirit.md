## Walkthrough

To start this quest, go to [Snidhall Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Snidhall_Isle "Snidhall Isle"), the island north of the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt") where, along the southern shore, you'll find two [Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") about to enter a fight. If you don't interfere, there's a chance the quest giver will be killed so jump in if you want the quest. 

Despite both showing exclamation marks, only the one in the blue tunic matters as he's the quest giver. Kill the angry warrior and [Ivar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ivar "Ivar") will speak with you, explaining he wants to marry the warrior's sister, Irma, but she's a priestess and their religion forbids it, but there's bound to be loopholes. He'll then ask you to find him books on the subject as you travel (25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")). 

Depending on how much you've traveled and bought, you might have some (or all) of the books already which include: 

  * [The Cult of Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Cult_of_Freya "The Cult of Freya"): bought from the [alchemist preacher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Electors' Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Electors%27_Square "Electors' Square") (doesn't appear at night)
  * [The Curious Cases of Virtuous Vegga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Curious_Cases_of_Virtuous_Vegga "The Curious Cases of Virtuous Vegga"): bought from [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shop_\(Gildorf\) "Shop \(Gildorf\)")'s [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Gildorf\) "Merchant \(Gildorf\)") in Novigrad
  * [Change your life! A Handbook](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Change_your_life!_A_Handbook "Change your life! A Handbook"): bought from the [Loan Shark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loan_Shark "Loan Shark") in [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") in Novigrad
  * [Tristianna and Isador](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tristianna_and_Isador "Tristianna and Isador"): bought from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Lindenvale\) "Merchant \(Lindenvale\)") in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale")
  * [Religion and Life](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Religion_and_Life "Religion and Life"): bought from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Merchant \(Crow's Perch\)") in [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), or (since [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)")) [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Devil%27s_Pit\) "Merchant \(Devil's Pit\)") at [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit")

You can talk and hand over any books at any time to Ivar, though naturally as he's out of the way it's best to do it in one trip if possible. _Bug:_ sometimes if you hand over all 5 books at once, the quest won't complete. If you run into this, try to hand over one at a time. 

Once you hand them all over, he'll decide he can't learn anymore sitting there on the island and thanks you, giving 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and completing the quest. 

While the quest may be complete, you can bump into him later just east of where [Odrin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Odrin "Odrin") can be found in the [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") district of Novigrad. He'll explain he hasn't given up on being with Irma but decided to seek out more culture in Novigrad to learn more, but as he needs to also earn money he's looking to sign on with a ship. 

## Journal entry

    _On one of Skellige's beaches,[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") ran into a man desperately fending off an enraged assailant. The witcher could hardly look upon this spectacle impassively and decided to help. After the fight, Ivar, for this was the man's name, admitted his attacker had been the brother of the woman Ivar loved. He had sought to kill Ivar for seducing his sister, who was a priestess of Freya and thus bound by custom to live a life of chastity. Ivar, however, refused to let religion stand in the way of his love and aimed to scour learned books for a precedent that would help him negotiate his beloved's release from her vows. Geralt agreed to bring him any relevant books he found._
    _Geralt kept his word and brought Ivar all the books about love and religion he could find._

## Objectives

  * Gather books describing religious customs and precedents and take them to Ivar.
  * Take the books you found to Ivar, he might find them interesting.

## Bug

  * There might be a bug where Ivar would not complete the quest if all 5 books were given all at once. This might be solved by transferring the books one by one in each conversation.
