### Type

**The Heroes' Pursuits: Fayrlund** is a [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is one of the four Heroes' Pursuits quests which involve [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") racing. 

The [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") rewarded in this quest is unaffected by the ±5 level constraint and [difficulty level](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Difficulty_level#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Difficulty level"). 

## Walkthrough

On the road east-northeast of [Fayrlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fayrlund "Fayrlund"), and far south of the [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig") signpost, you'll find people getting ready to race. Talk to the race master here and he'll set up the race when you're ready. 

This race is simple but short: you'll race along the road which at one point rides along the edge of the cliff and across a small bridge, before reaching the finish line on the other side of Fayrlund. The main kicker is to watch out for Red Rikka: she's clearly faster than your other opponent. However, she does make a few flaws that might give you enough room to get past her if you're having trouble. 

Once you come in first, you'll gain 25 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"), 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), and the [Zerrikanian saddlebags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_saddlebags "Zerrikanian saddlebags"). 

## Journal entry

    _There are various ways to honor the gods: prayer, bloody offerings butchered on altars marked with pentagrams and donating to temples, to name a few.[Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), however, have their own, unique way of doing so. Since they value physical strength and warrior spirit above all else, they organize horse races in the goddess' name. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") decided to take part._
    _The goddess smiled on the[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") and saw fit for him to win the race. But this was not the end of the tournament - a final challenge awaited him on the isle of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")._
