## Walkthrough

Along the road west of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund") you'll find a shepherd and his [sheep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sheep "Sheep") under attack by [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren"). After saving the man, you can talk to him who'll reveal the sirens are coming from [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"). The quest will then complete, giving you 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

While complete, a few days later you can run into the shepherd reuniting with his brother in [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn") around the middle of the village (where one path splits to go up to the cliffs). On spotting you, they'll once more thank [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") for his help.
