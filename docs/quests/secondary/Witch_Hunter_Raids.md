_Prerequisite:_ the main quest [Pyres of Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pyres_of_Novigrad "Pyres of Novigrad") must be completed before this becomes available. 

_Note:_ this quest will no longer be available after completing [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"). However, the enemies encountered here are considerably higher level than the suggested level for Now or Never, so it is advisable to delay completing it while still possible.

## Walkthrough

The [nonhuman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman") slum of [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") is home to many folk hated by the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") and the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter"), none more so than [Remi Villeroy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Remi_Villeroy "Remi Villeroy"), a [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") sentenced to death. Go to Remi's front door to see two witch hunters attempting to apprehend him. 

An argument erupts inside the house, with the hunters ready to shackle him and his wife. You can slink away, or help Remi defeat the two witch hunters. If you help and Remi survives, you receive a few [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone") for your troubles.
