## Walkthrough

This quest may be started in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") after [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s meeting with [Cleaver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese") in a shop near [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). Taking the [Gate of the Hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gate_of_the_Hierarch "Gate of the Hierarch") as a starting point, head into the city and follow the main path as it curves to the right around the [Rosemary and Thyme/Chameleon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme"). You will eventually sense the stench of decomposing, [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") flesh. Using [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses") will reveal a scent leading a short distance off the path into a grassy area. Follow it, and you'll happen upon a man's corpse. Inspect the cadaver to begin the quest (See the image below if you are unable to locate the scent/body). 

Inspecting the body will reveal that a sword wound was inflicted upon the man. Though shallow, it had severed an artery, and the man presumably bled to death shortly thereafter. Geralt will also automatically pick up a [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Key_\(quest_item\)#Key_\(Corpse\) "Key \(quest item\)") from the corpse. 

There are footprints and a trail of blood leading towards the scene. Inspecting them indicates that the man was running from his assailant. Backtrack along the trail, and you'll find that it leads to a building relatively close to the scene. Inside, you'll be greeted by a group of Cleaver's henchdwarves. 

After Geralt's question concerning the blood trail, a masked [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") recounts that he had a falling out with Derrick, the now-deceased, but later could not retrieve the corpse. However, the dwarf's companion, Grun, doesn't take as kindly to Geralt's intrusion, since Cleaver's orders entailed the killing of anyone who happens upon their den. The unnamed dwarf insists on taking a more diplomatic approach, as Cleaver ordered them to open a drop box with the key that Geralt is now in possession of. Thus, he offers an ultimatum – either Geralt receives money in exchange for the key, or a "chib between the ribs" should he make a fuss. There are two ways here in which to proceed: 

  1. _Fine, take the key._
     * You give the bandits the key they so desperately desire in exchange for 20![Crownitem](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/22/Crownitem.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/15?cb=20180420173351), and are transported out of the building as the quest ends.
  2. _I don't like being threatened._
     * You fight the bandits. Once they are defeated, you may freely explore the hideout and collect loot from it, including going upstairs to take the loot from the drop box for yourself. The treasure chest contains [Guardsman's boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guardsman%27s_boots "Guardsman's boots"), 50+![Crownitem](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/22/Crownitem.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/15?cb=20180420173351) and 200 [florens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Floren "Floren") which highly exceeds the dwarves' reward. One of the dwarves also drops [Diagram: Mahakaman steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Mahakaman_steel_sword "Diagram: Mahakaman steel sword").

## Journal entry

    _Geralt spent some time rutting around in the dank alleyways of Novigrad and at one point came upon the body of a mysterious man. A cursory inspection revealed that this unfortunate soul had been struck several times with a sharp object. Though he seemed to have managed to flee his attacker and hide in this alley, he had bled to death shortly thereafter._
    _Geralt realized he could follow the trail of blood and find out who had done this to him - and why._
    **If Geralt leaves the bandits be:**

    _The bandits were guilty of murder, but Geralt realized even the harshest of punishments would not bring their victim back to life. Thus when they proposed a trade - the key he found on the body in exchange for gold - Geralt readily agreed and went on his way._
    **If Geralt decides to kill the bandits:**

    _It turned out bandits had murdered the man. The[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") could not restore him life, but he could at least punish the guilty party. Which is what he did._

## Objectives

  * Use your Witcher Senses to figure out what happened to the murdered man.

**If Geralt refuses to give the key:**

  * Kill the bandits.
  * Find the treasure chest.

## Notes

  * This quest becomes available only after completing [Suspicious Shakedown](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Suspicious_Shakedown "Suspicious Shakedown") quest.
  * The quest name is possibly in direct reference to [George R.R. Martin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_R._R._Martin "wikipedia:George R. R. Martin")'s novel, _[A Feast for Crows](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Feast_for_Crows "wikipedia:A Feast for Crows")_.
