## Walkthrough

To start the quest, head to the larger of the islands north of [Giants' Toes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giants%27_Toes "Giants' Toes")' signpost (the one with a shipwreck) to find several bandits trying to loot the crates. Kill them and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will comment on the crates, starting the quest. You can then examine each crate to see one has [Clan Brokvar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Brokvar "Clan Brokvar")'s insignia on it and the other contains the [Solid Skellige sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Solid_Skellige_sword "Solid Skellige sword"). 

Head to [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag") village on [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog") and speak with the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Svorlag\) "Merchant \(Svorlag\)") at the docks about the crates and he'll comment that it must have been [one of their ships](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall%27s_Wrath "Hemdall's Wrath") that sailed out a month ago to raid the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent") but never returned. He'll then ask about the decorated sword which, if you retrieved it, Geralt will automatically hand it over for a finder's fee of 55 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") and 75 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"), completing the quest. If you didn't get the sword, you'll instead be paid 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") (but same amount of [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")). 

## Journal entry

    _Strewn across the[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") shore lay the remnants of an entire fortune, testimonies to a tragedy that had struck some poor member of Clan Brokvar. Pirates were currently engaged in looting anything of value that had survived and Geralt decided he should try to inform someone with a right to the goods before they finished the job._

## Objectives

  * Look for signs indicating the cargo's owner using your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses").
  * Ask the shopkeeper in the Clan Brokvar village on Spikeroog about the shipwrecked cargo.

## Notes

  * It's impossible to keep the sword to sell it off elsewhere. Even if you don't retrieve it and turn in the quest, if you go back to get it it will be set in your quest inventory tab, making it impossible to drop or sell.
