## Walkthrough

After clearing out the [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") north of [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle"), a woman will appear crying nearby. Talk to her to learn her fiancé disappeared while checking his snares. 

_Important:_ Moving too far away from the area after accepting the quest will cause it to fail. 

Follow the tracks nearby until you find a pack of wild [dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog") around a trap and a body. Once they're killed, examine the body to confirm it's the woman's betrothed, then head back to let her know the bad news. 

She'll try to give you a reward, which you can accept or refuse. If you refuse you'll get 60 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"), otherwise accepting it will give 20 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 40 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). No matter what you chose, the quest will then end and the woman will head off to where her fiancé's body is. 

## Journal entry

    _While wandering[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") happened across a woman who was weeping violently. She explained that her betrothed had gone into the woods to check his traps but never returned - and she feared the worst. Geralt calmed her and promised to check what had become of her beloved._
    _When Geralt returned from the woods, he did not bring good news with him. The woman saw this in his face at once. Though her hands trembled and her eyes welled with bitter tears, she did not forget to pay the witcher what he was owed and thank him with some kind words._

## Objectives

  * Find the woman's missing fiancé in the woods using your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses").
  * Deliver the bad news to the girl.
