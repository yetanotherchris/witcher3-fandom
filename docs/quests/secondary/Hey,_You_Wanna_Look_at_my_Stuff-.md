## Walkthrough

In [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") district, near the [bottles-selling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Silverton_docks\) "Merchant \(Silverton docks\)") merchant you'll find a [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(halfling\) "Merchant \(halfling\)") calling out to you. If you talk to him, he'll reveal he has a bunch of [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage")' items for sale: apparently pilfered from the homes of those burned at the stake before the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") could gather up the contraband. 

Ask to see what else he has, and as he begins to tell you he suddenly looks scared and runs off, asking for you to not reveal why he was there. A moment later several witch hunters appear, asking why the [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") ran off when he saw them. You can cover for him (10[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")) or turn him in. 

_Important:_ You have to agree to see the halfling's inventory and lie for him for the quest to start. If you don't, you won't be able to use him as a merchant later either. 

If you covered for him, examine the item near where he was standing to pick up a scent trail, then follow it a short distance around to his hiding spot. He'll thank you for not ratting him out and offers a discount on his stuff, earning 25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and completing the quest. 

## Journal entry

    _Novigrad, the most cosmopolitan city of the North, attracts its fair share of oddballs and eccentrics. One of them was a merchant dealing in unusual goods. While talking to Geralt he spied some witch hunters approaching and fled at once. Still interested in the secretive merchant's secret wares, Geralt decided to hunt him down._

## Objectives

  * Find the mysterious merchant using your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses").
  * Search the area using your Witcher Senses.

## Notes

  * Ironically, sometimes his hiding spot will be right behind where a witch hunter can spawn.
