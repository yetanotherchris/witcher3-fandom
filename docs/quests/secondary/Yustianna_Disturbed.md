## Walkthrough

Head to [Yustianna's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna%27s_Grotto "Yustianna's Grotto") in northern [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") and after you enter the cave, take the right path to eventually overhear some bandits talking in a central chamber. _Note:_ if you take the left path and use [The Eye of Nehaleni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Eye_of_Nehaleni "The Eye of Nehaleni"), you won't get the option to converse with the bandits like outlined below. 

While initially marked as friendly, if you get close one of them will all but threaten you and you can either do the following: 

  * pay him 150 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")
  * cast [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (Requires [Delusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Delusion "Delusion") level 2, earning 35 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * attack them

If you choose any but the last one, you'll be transported back to the area at the entrance of the cave. If you chose to attack them (or head back after paying or using Axii) they'll become hostile and you must kill all 4 of them off if you wish to explore the central chamber. 

Whatever you choose, the quest will finish. If you attacked the bandits, you can further explore the main area to find the [highwaymen's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Highwaymen%27s_journal "Highwaymen's journal") on one of the bandits, and _a lot_ of loot. Many of the chests can contain random relic items and diagrams, and one of them has a second copy of the highwaymen's journal. 

If you also have the Eye of Nehaleni, you can further explore the other hidden part of the cave for a few more lootable crates.
