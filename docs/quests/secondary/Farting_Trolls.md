## Walkthrough

Along the road between [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn") and [Kaer Muire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Muire "Kaer Muire"), you'll find an unmarked cave entrance just west of the [Place of Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Place_of_Power "Place of Power") here and, almost as soon as you enter it, you'll hear the unmistakable sounds of farting. 

Head further in to hear 3 [rock trolls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rock_troll "Rock troll") conversing (while continuing to fart). However, they're not friendly and if they sense you they will attack. Unfortunately if you wish to explore the cave you'll have no other choice but to kill them off. 

Once they're dead that'll complete the quest. However if you're also looking for [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") diagrams, a chest near the boat contains the [Enhanced Ursine boots diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Enhanced_Ursine_boots "Diagram: Enhanced Ursine boots").

## Notes

  * While there is no reward for the quest, you will gain 3 [cave troll livers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cave_troll_liver "Cave troll liver") by doing it.
  * This is one of the best spots to farm for a [troll mutagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll_mutagen "Troll mutagen").

    * [Joe Vargas](http://www.youtube.com/user/AngryJoeShow)
