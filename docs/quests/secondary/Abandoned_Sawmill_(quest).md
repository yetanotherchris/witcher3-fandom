## Walkthrough

The first time you visit the [Abandoned Sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Sawmill_\(location\) "Abandoned Sawmill \(location\)") location on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), this quest will start, with [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") making a comment on it being abandoned. 

All you really need to do is examine the [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") bones (around the open shed area, third building from the right) but if you want to find all the clues first, go into the far left building and, using [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard") to blast the door open, loot the chest here for [Letter from a druid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_a_druid "Letter from a druid"). Then go into the far right hut for [Lumberjack's letter to his wife](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lumberjack%27s_letter_to_his_wife "Lumberjack's letter to his wife"), and the hut next it (the building between the lumberjack's letter and the bones) for [Sawmill owner's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_owner%27s_notes "Sawmill owner's notes"). 

Once you view the bones, the [leshen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshen "Leshen") will spawn, either near the far left or far right. Kill it off to finish the quest, gaining 20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") and 50 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

## Journal entry

    _In a certain forest Geralt found himself looking at a clearly abandoned sawmill enveloped in thick undergrowth. He decided to investigate._
    _Curiosity once again dragged the witcher into trouble. Entering the sawmill provoked the leshen who had killed the woodcutters who had once worked here. Geralt had no choice but to fight the monster._

## Objectives

  * Search the abandoned sawmill using your Witcher Senses. 0/4
  * Kill the leshen.
