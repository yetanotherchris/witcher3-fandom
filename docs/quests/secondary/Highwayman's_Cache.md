## Walkthrough

A little farther east of [Boatmakers' Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boatmakers%27_Hut "Boatmakers' Hut") is a trail leading to a bridge over to [Bandits' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandits%27_Camp "Bandits' Camp"), an island of thugs. Guarding the bridge are three bandits, who don't attack but instead engage you in conversation. They want your coin to pass safely over the bridge. You can: 

  * Tell them to try and take your coin, which starts a fight where you must then kill all three.
  * Show them your [medallion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Medallion "Medallion"), which causes one of the bandits to pause. Recognizing it as a witcher medallion, they decide it's best not to challenge you and depart without incident.

Afterwards, use your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses") at their campfire to follow some footprints to a partly-hidden chest in the weeds under the bridge. Pry it open for some coin and a couple of diagrams.
