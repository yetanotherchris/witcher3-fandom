## Walkthrough

Head south of the [Novigrad Docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks") signpost to find a man threatening a strumpet. If you intervene the woman tells [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to leave, as she can handle things. You now have a few options: 

  * use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (requires Delusion level 1, earning 30 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * threaten the hooded man
  * tell the woman you can make the man leave (earns 10 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * leave as requested

Unless you leave as requested, it's soon revealed the two were merely acting to get in the mood and Geralt just killed it. The quest will then end, with slightly different comments depending on what was chosen.
