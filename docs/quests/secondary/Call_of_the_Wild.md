## Walkthrough

Just east-southeast of [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn") is a forest inhabited by a large pack of white [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)") and, only at night, the [ulfhedinn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulfhedinn "Ulfhedinn") will also appear with them. Kill them off to complete the quest.

## Notes

  * While mislabeled, the "[werewolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf")" is in fact an ulfhedinn.
  * The white wolves will respawn around the area, but the werewolf will not after it is killed.
