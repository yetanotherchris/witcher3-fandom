## Walkthrough

This quest can be started by reading the [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(White_Orchard\) "Notice board \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") or talking to the blacksmith [Willis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Willis "Willis") directly. Willis will tell [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") how someone burned down his [forge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Willis%27_forge "Willis' forge"). If Geralt says that he can find the arsonist, the blacksmith will offer gold and suggest checking for clues behind his house and the quest will begin. 

Go behind the house to find the tracks and follow them, eventually leading Geralt down to the [river](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ismena "Ismena"). Follow the river bank north (left) to the other side of the bridge where the trail picks up again. Geralt will learn that the arsonist ran into a [drowner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") and was wounded, at which point four drowners may attack, but only if doing the quest during the night. Once taken care of, continue to follow the trail back to the village and into a house. 

Inside, Geralt finds the culprit, [Napp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Napp "Napp"), hiding in the back room with an injured hand. Napp will offer a bribe (20[![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")) for Geralt to drop the whole thing. If Geralt refuses the bribe, he can either fistfight Napp (losing equals death and save reload) or use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (extra 25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")) before making Napp stagger down to the blacksmith. 

If Geralt turns in the arsonist, Willis will call over some soldiers, who will take Napp away, presumably to be hanged, after which he pays you the promised reward, 20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). 

Regardless of choices, you get 25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") when you talk to Napp (not counting Axii bonus) and then 50[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") when you return to the blacksmith. The quest then completes and Willis will provide his smithing services. 

## Journal entry

    _There were only a handful of[nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman") in White Orchard. One of them was the [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") Willis, an excellent blacksmith whose wares attracted peasants from far and wide. Sadly, his forge had burned to the ground shortly before Geralt arrived. Willis suspected the fire had been started deliberately and asked the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") to investigate this alleged arson._
    **If Geralt turns in the arsonist:**

    _Geralt brought the arsonist to Willis. The dwarf immediately summoned[Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soldiers - with whom it turned out he was on quite good terms. The imperials quickly declared the man guilty, handed down a sentence and carried it on the spot. Harsh justice - or maybe just harsh?_
    **If Geralt takes the bribe:**

    _In the end Geralt decided not to reveal the arsonist's identity to Willis. Perhaps it was the coin offered in exchange for his silence that convinced him, or perhaps he simply concluded the man was clearly the village idiot and shouldn't be punished for his deeds which, while dastardly, could not possibly have been done with much deliberation and forethought - for the man was capable of neither._

## Objectives

  * Use your [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses") to look behind the forge for clues about the arsonist.
  * Follow the trail using your Witcher Senses.
  * Look for someone with wounds dealt by drowners using your Witcher Senses.
  * Talk to the suspect. (25[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * **If used Axii:**
    * Escort the arsonist back to the blacksmith. (50[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * **If accepted bribe:** (50[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level")) 
    * Return to the blacksmith.
  * **If said "Try me":**
    * Defeat the arsonist in a fistfight. (50[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))

## Notes

  * While if you turn in the arsonist, Willis says he'll give you a good price, there's no actual discount on anything from him. His prices are the same regardless of your choices.

## Trivia

  * The name of the quest is likely a reference to the song "Firestarter" by The Prodigy.
