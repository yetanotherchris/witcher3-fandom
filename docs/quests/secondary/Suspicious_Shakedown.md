## Walkthrough

Head to the house just north of the [Rosemary and Thyme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme"), almost directly north of the [Gate of the Hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gate_of_the_Hierarch "Gate of the Hierarch") signpost, to find an alley, where behind the alley's door are some bandits harassing a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Silverton\) "Merchant \(Silverton\)"). Interact with them and the bandits will try to get you to leave. If you choose "at least I tried" [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will leave them to it and the bandits will take the merchant's money and let him go. _Important:_ if at any time you leave without helping, once the merchant returns to his store he'll refuse to do business with you. 

If you choose to protect the merchant, you'll have three options: 

  * threaten the bandits (will result in a fight)
  * use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") (requires [Delusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Delusion "Delusion") level 1 for a minimum of 30[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"))
  * leave (will lock you out of doing business with the merchant)

As long as you help get rid of them, the merchant will thank the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") and heads back home, concluding the quest. You can then find him later at his shop in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") where he'll either refuse to do business (if you left him to the bandits) or will be glad to see Geralt (if you helped him). If he was rescued, he'll also say he's giving a discount, but that's only dialogue and no price change occurs.
