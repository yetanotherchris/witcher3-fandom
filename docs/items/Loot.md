**Looting** is the primary method by which [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") acquires [items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Items "Category:Items") of various kinds. He can loot [containers and corpses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Containers "Containers"). Items which can be acquired in this manner are listed below in general categories:
