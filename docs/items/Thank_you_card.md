## Item ID

mq1058_drawing

**Thank you card** is an item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") takes [Millie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Millie "Millie") to [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton"), a few days later she'll give this to the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") as thanks.
