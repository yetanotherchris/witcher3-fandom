## Item ID

Ashes

**Ashes** are a [junk item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is very common as loot everywhere, although typical to find around burned down buildings and on non-enemy corpses. It can be dismantled from a lot of other junk or discardable quest items, and it is sold by the [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Midcopse\) "Merchant \(Midcopse\)") in [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). 

In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it is also used as an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") for crafting [gray armor dye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gray_armor_dye "Gray armor dye").
