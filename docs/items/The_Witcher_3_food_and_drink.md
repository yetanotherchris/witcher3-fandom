**Food and drinks** are used to regenerate health in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Each item is linked to an ability that defines the magnitude and duration of the effect. There are 3 quality tiers for food and another 3 for drinks. Originally, food items had [weight](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Weight "Weight"), but this was later removed. 

Prior to [Patch 1.10](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.10_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.10 \(The Witcher 3\)"), food and drinks effects were stackable (could consume one food and one beverage to receive two different health restoration effects). However, this no longer works and now only the last consumed food or beverage will determine the effect (i.e: if one consumes chicken and then water right after, the water will replace the effect even though it gives less restoration than chicken). 

It is recommended to sell food and drink items to [innkeepers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeeper "Innkeeper"), as they offer a +5-12% increase in profit.
