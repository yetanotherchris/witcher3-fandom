## Item ID

q403_ciri_meteor

**Magic Amulet** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , given to [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") by [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") after the council during the main quest [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen"). 

When activated, it summons a [fireball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fireball "Fireball") to explode where Ciri is standing after a short delay. The explosion harms all enemies caught within the relatively small blast radius, but take heed as the explosion can harm Ciri as well if she does not get out of the way. 

## Associated quest

  * [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen")

## Notes

  * If the item is obtained on [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") through console commands, it will be visible in the "Quest Items" [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") tab and can be put in a pocket like a [torch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torch "Torch"). However, upon attempting to use it, Geralt will simply be shown holding an invisible object, with his hand in the same position as if holding the [magic lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_lamp_\(quest_item\) "Magic lamp \(quest item\)").
