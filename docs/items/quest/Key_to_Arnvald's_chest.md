## Item ID

**Key to Arnvald's chest** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , needed to unlock [Arnvald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arnvald "Arnvald")'s chest in the servants' room at [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel"). 

It can only be obtained if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") decides to help [Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite") during the quest [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"), and subsequently lets Arnvald die (including if he escapes). Then, while looking for evidence in the room, this key can be found in Arnvald's shoe, tossed under a table by the door. 

The chest itself is somewhat notorious for being annoyingly locked with _seemingly_ no key for it, as the conditions required to be able to get this key are very specific. However, while being quest-related, there's nothing important in there - 20 relic-tier [exploding bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Exploding_bolt "Exploding bolt"), [axeman's trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axeman%27s_trousers "Axeman's trousers"), a [dark iron plate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_iron_plate "Dark iron plate") and a couple of random [alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and/or [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components").
