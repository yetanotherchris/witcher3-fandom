## Item ID

lw_tm6_treasure_key

**Common key** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found, along with [notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marauder%27s_notes "Marauder's notes"), on one of the deserter's bodies at the hidden treasure marker south of [Duén Hen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Du%C3%A9n_Hen "Duén Hen").
