## Item ID

**Water-damaged letter** is a book and a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found on a decaying corpse on the shore northwest of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley"), and starts the [Sunken Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sunken_Treasure "Sunken Treasure") quest. 

## Journal entry

    _Molke,_

We gotta move one more load - and it's the last, I promise. It's a simple job, couple of chests, risk's minimal and the reward's substantial. Please, do it - if not for the gold, then at least for old time's sake._
