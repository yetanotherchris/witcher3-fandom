## Item ID

**Key to Dijkstra's treasury** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. During the main quest, [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"), after [Caleb Menge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Menge "Caleb Menge") is dealt with, [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") will find this key on his body and give to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to give to [Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"). However, if Menge wasn't asked about the treasure, the key will ultimately be useless. If he was asked about it though, Geralt learns it's the key to a vault in a [Koviri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir") bank and passes on this information to Dijkstra.
