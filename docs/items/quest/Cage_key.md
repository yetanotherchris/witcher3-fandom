# Cage key

There are several **cage keys** in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and are usually meant to help free [person(s) in distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_distress "Person\(s\) in distress") and so they show in the quest items tab. Once used, they typically disappear from inventory.

## Item ID

lw_gr29_cage_key

This **Cage key** releases the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post") [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Border_Post\) "Merchant \(Border Post\)"), at the [Person(s) in distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_distress "Person\(s\) in distress") marker north of [Hanged Man's Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Tree "Hanged Man's Tree"). It is dropped by the Deserter Leader near the cage. 

### Notes

  * Because the surrounding road is used during [A Deadly Plot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Deadly_Plot "A Deadly Plot"), the bandits are despawned for the duration of that quest. If the Deserter Leader hasn't been killed yet and you can't find him, mediating nearby will respawn all the enemies.

This **cage key** is from the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, found together with [Ernest Scrawler's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ernest_Scrawler%27s_journal "Ernest Scrawler's journal"), on a scaffolding around a tree near the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(The_Cockatrice_Inn\) "Merchant \(The Cockatrice Inn\)") in distress at [Delenfer Pass](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Delenfer_Pass "Delenfer Pass"). 

### Associated quest

  * [Knight for Hire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Knight_for_Hire "Knight for Hire") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

### Bugs

  * It is supposed to be dropped by one of the bandits at the site, and his remains glow in red like a quest item, but upon looting there are only regular drops. The actual loot location near the tree is likely a workaround for this.

## Item ID

lw_sk29_cage_key

This **Cage key** exists in the game files, but doesn't appear during gameplay and can only be obtained through console commands. 

It is part of [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") or otherwise redesigned content, where the [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kvalheim_Isle\) "Herbalist \(Kvalheim Isle\)") at [Kvalheim Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kvalheim_Isle "Kvalheim Isle") (south from [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare")) was originally caged like other persons in distress throughout the game.
