## Item ID

**Worn-out and faded notes by Hieronymus on the witcher Elgar** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

When read it'll give areas to look for: 

  * all the standard [Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") armor diagrams
  * the mastercrafted steel sword, silver sword, and armor diagrams

## Journal entry

    _[Elgar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elgar "Elgar") hid improvements for [School of the Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") gear in various corners of the world, so that witchers could make use of them on the Path. Some he hid in [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") ruins and a sunken ship in the south of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen").. Others he put in a hideout far to the south of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")._
