## Item ID

mq4004_boy_remains

**Boy's remains** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the skeleton of a [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") trainee in the [Bastion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastion "Bastion") west of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), in the northern tower ruin, during the secondary quest [The Bastion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bastion "The Bastion"). It needs to be buried in order to complete the quest.
