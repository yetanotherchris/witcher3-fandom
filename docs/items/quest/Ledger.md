## Item ID

sq304_ledger_book

**Ledger** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It was intended to be part of the quest [Of Swords and Dumplings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Swords_and_Dumplings "Of Swords and Dumplings"), most likely as a clue in a plan to sneak into [Ernst van Hoorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ernst_van_Hoorn "Ernst van Hoorn")'s warehouse instead of conveniently making the guard go away. The game files also contain an unused "warehouse indoor key" tied to the quest, and on the adjoined building behind the (now single) warehouse there are still climbable ledges and scaffolding leading to blocked-off entrances - either the way to sneak in, or warehouse B mentioned in the contents.
