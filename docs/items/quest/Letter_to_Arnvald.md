## Item ID

q206_arnvalds_letter

**Letter to Arnvald** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can only be obtained if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") decides to help [Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite") during the quest [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"), and subsequently lets Arnvald die (including if he escapes). The letter is only then found in the secret compartment. 

It is the same letter that incriminates [Birna Bran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Birna "Birna") regardless of what happens with [Arnvald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arnvald "Arnvald"), but though the ending cutscene of the quest shows Geralt handing it over, it is only added to the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") under the above-described conditions, and stays in after the quest completes. 

## Journal entry

    _Arnvald,  
The great moment is nigh. Do not let me down, and I will shower gold and gratitude upon you.  
You are to do the following. Before the feast, you will put the [maerdrome](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Psilocybe "Psilocybe") mushrooms included in this letter into one of the barrels of mead, and then pour a measure of [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") blood in after them. You will give the drink thus seasoned to the [Vildkaarls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildkaarl "Vildkaarl") \- it will cause them to transform. Note that they are not aware of our plan - that should remain the case to the very end. Think of the Vildkaarls not as allies, but as tools.  
Once the deed is done, leave [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") as quickly as possible. My men will await you on the beach as promised, and will hide you until matters calm.  
Some will call you a traitor. A disgrace. Do not listen to those fools. You have shown great courage by standing on the right side of history.  
Glory to [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"),  
The Future King_

## Notes

  * Despite dialogue in cutscenes saying that the letter is from Birna, and even explicitly described as _unsigned_ , its journal entry contents are signed by "The Future King". While that could be dismissed as a secret codename, there is a lot of [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") around the major quests in Skellige and it is more likely that [Svanrige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svanrige_Tuirseach "Svanrige Tuirseach") was originally intended as the plotter, and the letter's appearance under such contrived conditions is simply the one case where the developers forgot to remove it from.
