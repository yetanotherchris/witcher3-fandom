**Silver seaweed ring** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It exists in the game files, but doesn't appear during gameplay and can only be obtained through console commands. 

Its item ID suggests that it, along with a [massive ring](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Massive_ring "Massive ring"), should've been part of the [Monster Slayer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_Slayer "Monster Slayer") quest, but its purpose is unknown.
