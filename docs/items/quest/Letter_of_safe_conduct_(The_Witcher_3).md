# Letter of safe conduct (The Witcher 3)

**Letter of safe conduct** is a document and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

As King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") has blocked off access to [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") across the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)"), [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can use one of these to cross through the military checkpoints, as part of the secondary quest [Thou Shalt Not Pass](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thou_Shalt_Not_Pass "Thou Shalt Not Pass"). Alternatively, a fake [Transit pass](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Transit_pass "Transit pass") may also be used.

## Item ID

q103_safe_conduct

This version is received from the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger"), upon reporting to him that his daugther [Tamara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger") is in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). He gives it to help in reaching her on the other side of the border, as he demands that Geralt checks in on her in person. 

In a _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion standalone playthrough, this version is also included in the starting equipment. 

### Journal entry

    _In the name of His Majesty Radovid V, King of Redania, the bearer of the present document is entitled to the right of unfettered passage across all blockades on the Pontar River._

This version can be obtained by completing [Contract: Woodland Beast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Woodland_Beast "Contract: Woodland Beast"), for which Captain [Felix Grubb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Felix_Grubb "Felix Grubb") promises is as part of the reward. However, he will not award it if Geralt decides to cover for [Vernossiel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernossiel "Vernossiel")'s [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") unit and not mention them. 

Unlike the Baron's version, this item isn't readable and thus doesn't have a journal entry.
