## Item ID

th1007_journal_bear_part4

**Letter to Ingeborga Kalebsdotter** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is found together with [Diagram: Ursine crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Ursine_crossbow "Diagram: Ursine crossbow"), on a skeleton at [Melusine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Melusine "Melusine")'s caverns on [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog"). Reading it directs [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach") during [Scavenger Hunt: Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Bear_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Bear School Gear"). 

## Journal entry

    _My dearest Ingeborga,_
    _[illegible fragment] ... arrived at[Fort Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach") on summer's first day. He tarried there a while, draining more than one barrel of mead with the jarl. Soon both were fast friends. Then one day Jarl [Torgeir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torgeir_the_Red "Torgeir the Red") summoned me and ordered me to sail to [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog") with him, to show him to where the [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") were thickest._
    _It's no exaggeration, my little mountain[goat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Goat "Goat"), to say I've never met a warrior like him in my life. He's lithe as an eel and strong as a [bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear")! When the sirens came at us, I hadn't even time to seize my axe before he'd gutted them all. And his weapon, why, I've never seen its like – a [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_crossbow "Ursine crossbow"), but so small he wields it with one hand. I know he has a sketch of it in his saddlebags, for I spied it when he was packing up. Once he's asleep, I'll try to sketch a copy to take to our smith on [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"), so he can make others. So we can protect ourselves from the sirens, case they return._
    _[illegible fragment]... soon as dawn breaks, we're off, sirens are swarming thicker and thicker, my hands are numb from so much sword-swinging and my breath's ragged, but thinking of you gives me strength... [rest of letter illegible]_
