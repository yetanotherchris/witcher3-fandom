## Item ID

sq202_half_seal

**Broken seal** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in 2 halves. During the secondary quest, [The Last Wish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Last_Wish_\(quest\) "The Last Wish \(quest\)"), the first half is recovered from a sunken ship which is then used to teleport near the second half, which is on the ship on the mountainside. Both halves are then used to summon the [djinn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Djinn "Djinn").
