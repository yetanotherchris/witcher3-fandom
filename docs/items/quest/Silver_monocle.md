## Item ID

mq0004_thalers_monocle

**Silver monocle** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that belonged to [Thaler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thaler_\(spy\) "Thaler \(spy\)"). It can be found during the short quest [A Frying Pan, Spick and Span](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Frying_Pan,_Spick_and_Span "A Frying Pan, Spick and Span") while in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") during the prologue. 

As of [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), it can later be given to Thaler. 

### Associated quest

  * [A Frying Pan, Spick and Span](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Frying_Pan,_Spick_and_Span "A Frying Pan, Spick and Span")

### Notes

  * "Silver monocle" is a mistranslation, in the Polish (original) version this item is called "Broken monocle."

### Bugs

  * Prior to [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)") there was no way to mention this item to Thaler. As a result, once picked up, it stayed indefinitely in the "Quest items" tab, and couldn't be removed without the use of console commands or mods.
