## Item ID

mq2010_lumbermill_journal_3

**Letter from a druid** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in the far left hut at the [Abandoned Sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Sawmill_\(location\) "Abandoned Sawmill \(location\)") during the [secondary quest of the same name](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Sawmill_\(quest\) "Abandoned Sawmill \(quest\)"). 

## Journal entry

    _You have gone mad if you think that these attacks and accidents are our doing. Not a single[druid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") has taken action against you and none of us wish you harm. Our calling is to care for nature, but not at the price of [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") lives. In fact, man is also a part of nature and we are not allowed to do him any deliberate harm. Note, however, that nature is ruled by her own laws and if she has let you know you are not welcome here, it would be wise to consider a halt to your woodcutting. For your good, and for the good of the forest._
