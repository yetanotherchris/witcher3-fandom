## Item ID

th1009_map_wolf_upgrade1b

**Slightly torn notes by Hieronymus on the witcher Elgar** is a document and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

When read it'll give areas to look for: 

  * all the standard [Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") armor diagrams
  * the enhanced steel sword, trousers, and gauntlets diagrams

## Journal entry

    _It seems[Elgar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elgar "Elgar") hid some upgrades to his gear in the area around [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"). Some he hid in the deepest cave he could find, the one in the northern portion of the [Kaer Morhen valley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen_valley "Kaer Morhen valley"). Others he hid in the abandoned, crumbling watchtower in the same area. Still others he placed amidst the rocks on the shore of the valley's lake._
