## Item ID

q106_anabelle_remains

**Anabelle's remains** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

They are given by the [ghost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Plague_maiden "Plague maiden") of [Anabelle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anabelle "Anabelle") on [Fyke Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyke_Isle "Fyke Isle") if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") chooses to believe her story during the secondary quest [A Towerful of Mice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Towerful_of_Mice "A Towerful of Mice"), and must be taken to [Graham](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Graham "Graham") in [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton").
