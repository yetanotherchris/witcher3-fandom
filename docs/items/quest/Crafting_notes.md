## Item ID

th1005_journal_gryphon_part6

**Crafting notes** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted alongside a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Griffin_silver_sword "Diagram: Griffin silver sword") for the [Griffin silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_silver_sword "Griffin silver sword") inside a chest in [Lornruk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lornruk "Lornruk"), and provides directions to [Hindhold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindhold "Hindhold") during [Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear"). 

## Journal entry

    _(...) now there's a commission I haven't seen the likes of in ages. Got a visit from that[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") the [baronet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baronet "Baronet") summoned about them evil powers took over the lighthouse and were crashing ships against the rocks. He stood there in the doorway of my forge, looking noble as a [prince](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prince "Prince") or [duke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Duke "Duke") or something, and said his [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") got damaged during his last job. Blade was chipped bad, almost broken in two spots. I asked how he expected me to know how to fix a weapon like that, and he gave me a diagram and said I was to forge him a new one, and promised quite the sum in return._
    _...damn near tuckered myself out making that thing! Aye, I did my apprenticeship with Master Barnaby of[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and I've made a witcher blade or two in my life, but it's been over three years since I last touched anything of the sort. In the end didn't matter, though - afore I could finish, the witcher figured out what was going on with the lighthouse and had to make haste to the baronet's castle, for... [rest illegible]_
