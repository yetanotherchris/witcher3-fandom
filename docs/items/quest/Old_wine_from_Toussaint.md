## Item ID

q310_wine

**Old wine from Toussaint** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be bought from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Gildorf\) "Merchant \(Gildorf\)") in [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district's [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shop_\(Gildorf\) "Shop \(Gildorf\)") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

It was once needed for the quest [It Takes Three to Tango](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/It_Takes_Three_to_Tango "It Takes Three to Tango"), but instead some regular [wines](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink") serve its purpose and this item no longer works. 

This item appears to re-stock on occasion in the merchant's shop, even past the point of when the associated quest happens. As it's still considered a quest item, there's no way to drop it without using console commands, so it's best to avoid buying it.
