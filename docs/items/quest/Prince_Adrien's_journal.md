## Item ID

**Prince Adrien's journal** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found in a chest in [Drahim Castle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drahim_Castle "Drahim Castle")'s basement, along with [Diagram: Feline steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Feline_steel_sword "Diagram: Feline steel sword"). 

Reading it provides directions to the [Flying Stag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flying_Stag "Flying Stag") shipwreck during [Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear"). 

## Journal entry

    _[Kiyan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kiyan "Kiyan") performed wonderfully! It's a shame, of course, that Professor Gloger disappeared somewhere in the ruins of [Est Tayiar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Est_Tayiar "Est Tayiar") along with the rest of the [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") diagrams, but those sketches for a steel sword alone are worth as much as a village in southern [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania")!_
    _We split the takings equally, as per our agreement: I kept the[diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Feline_silver_sword "Diagram: Feline silver sword") for the [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_silver_sword "Feline silver sword"), while Kiyan took the [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_crossbow "Feline crossbow") [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Feline_crossbow "Diagram: Feline crossbow"). I must admit, I didn't exactly need every member of the expedition's head preserved in vinegar, but they do constitute impressive proof of Kiyan's conscientiousness and professionalism. The [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") swore none of the archaeologists would leave Est Tayiar alive - and none did._
    _All the more shame, then, that Kiyan didn't accept my offer. As my personal bodyguard, he would never have wanted for a thing. Steady pay, caviar and strawberries for breakfast, lobster for lunch, daily baths, a court surgeon and courtesans of both sexes for all his needs... and if he still lacked for anything, he had but to knock on my chamber door._
    _He must very much love all that trudging around the world, because I do not believe Jacob of Dembich could have dangled a better offer than I did! Really now - Jacob of Dembich?! A second-rate captain transporting whores to[dockside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harborside "Harborside") [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") pimps for a fistful of [crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")? With any luck, sooner or later a storm will sink that leaky tub of his and Kiyan will return to me, tail between his legs!_
    _[illegible scribbling] scrubbed [illegible] waxing [illegible] [illegible][blade oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_oils "The Witcher 3 oils") [illegible] [Devil's Puffball](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Puffball "Devil's Puffball") [illegible] contortions (...)_
    _[ink visibly fresher than previous entries, many years must have passed] I have heard the Flying Deer, the vessel on which Kiyan was sailing, wrecked off the coast of[Grassy Knoll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grassy_Knoll "Grassy Knoll"). Was it my curse? Hm. Not bad, for a beginner. Perhaps I should think about enrolling at [Ban Ard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ban_Ard_Academy "Ban Ard Academy")?_
