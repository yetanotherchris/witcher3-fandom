**Roche's notes** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , added by [Patch 1.20](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.20_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.20 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

If [Vernon Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche "Vernon Roche") dies at the end of [Reason of State](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reason_of_State "Reason of State") and he hasn't yet been beaten at [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), then as long as the [Gwent: Old Pals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent:_Old_Pals "Gwent: Old Pals") quest is active, this can be found in his hideout, providing directions to the other players to look for. Attached to it is Roche's unique card, [Saesenthesis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Saesenthessis_\(gwent_card\) "Saesenthessis \(gwent card\)").
