## Item ID

q304_dandelion_diary

**Dandelion's Planner** is a book and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

When starting [Broken Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broken_Flowers "Broken Flowers"), this planner is used to track down and talk to several women the missing [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") had been seeing just before he disappeared.
