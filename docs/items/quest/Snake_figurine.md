**Snake figurine** is an item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , found in a chest near [The Emmentaler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Emmentaler "The Emmentaler") and needed to open the exit [portal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portal "Portal") during the secondary quest [Of Dairy and Darkness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Dairy_and_Darkness "Of Dairy and Darkness").
