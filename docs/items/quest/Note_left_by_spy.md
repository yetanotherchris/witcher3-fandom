## Item ID

mq3002_hidden_messages_note_01

This is the first note, required to start quest. It can easily be missed if one's not paying attention to their surroundings or [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s comments. 

After [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Never Trust Children](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Never_Trust_Children "Never Trust Children") has been completed, a couple of men can be seen under the archway just east of [The Kingfisher Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn"). They'll soon walk away when they notice Geralt, to which he comments with "Interesting". Examine the brick wall of the archway to find this. 

### Journal entry

    _Negotiations with the[Trade Corporation](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guild_of_Merchants "Guild of Merchants") continue. The merchants have agreed to almost all of the [emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis")'s conditions. Outlook positive._
    _Rumor is[Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s found a way to get his hands on Novigrad. The key to Novigrad's the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") \- control the [hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyrus_Engelkind_Hemmelfart "Cyrus Engelkind Hemmelfart"), you control the city. A plan's in the works. Next message - the stash at [Crippled Kate's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crippled_Kate%27s "Crippled Kate's")._

This will be pointed to by the first note found near the Kingfisher.

You get our girl into the[Vegelbuds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family")' [bash](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)").
