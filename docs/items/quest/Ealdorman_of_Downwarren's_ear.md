## Item ID

**Ealdorman of Downwarren's ear** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. This item normally can't be seen in the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") as, soon as it's acquired, the next cutscene takes place and the item gets removed during it. 

It is a left [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") ear that belongs to the [ealdorman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ealdorman_\(Downwarren\) "Ealdorman \(Downwarren\)") of [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren"), before he cuts it off in tribute to the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"). [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") then delivers the ear to [The Orphans of Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog") where he places it on an offering stone. Once the Crones appear in their true form, [Whispess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whispess "Whispess") takes the offering and threads it onto her necklace of ears.
