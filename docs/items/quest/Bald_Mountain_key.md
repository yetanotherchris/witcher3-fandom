## Item ID

q111_fugas_top_key

**Bald Mountain key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

The key is dropped by [Fugas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fugas "Fugas") upon his death in the caves below [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") during the [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(quest\) "Bald Mountain \(quest\)") quest. 

The key is only supposed to be lootable after completing the quest as Fugas's death immediately triggers a cutscene, followed by a sequence of playing as [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"). However, once the player regains control of Geralt during the quest, they can go back and, with precise positioning and a little luck, jump on the spikes of the blockade and then to the top of the platform to gain access to the room during the quest.
