## Item ID

q103_botch_blood

**Botchling blood** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

If you decide to kill the [botchling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Botchling "Botchling") during [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters"), you must harvest its blood to take to [the Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar"). The Pellar then uses the blood in a ritual to try to locate [Anna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anna_Strenger "Anna Strenger") and [Tamara Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger").
