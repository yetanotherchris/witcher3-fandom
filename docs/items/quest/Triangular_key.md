## Item ID

sq310_triangle_key

**Triangular key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the second floor of [Caesar Bilzen's house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen%27s_house "Caesar Bilzen's house") during the secondary quest [A Dangerous Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dangerous_Game "A Dangerous Game"), and unlocks the hidden room where the [jade figurine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jade_figurine "Jade figurine") and [Fringilla Vigo gwent card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fringilla_Vigo_\(gwent_card\) "Fringilla Vigo \(gwent card\)") are found.
