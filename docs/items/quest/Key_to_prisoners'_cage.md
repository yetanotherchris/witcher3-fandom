## Item ID

lw_gr13_poppy_cage_key

**Key to prisoners' cage** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

This appears to be an older version of the [slavers' key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slavers%27_key "Slavers' key") currently used to free [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale")'s [swordsmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swordsmith_\(Mulbrydale\) "Swordsmith \(Mulbrydale\)") at [Swamp Bint Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_Bint_Harbor "Swamp Bint Harbor"). It was likely replaced in a change where he was moved from a regular [person(s) in distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_distress "Person\(s\) in distress") cage to the current in-building structure. A different version of the [unsent letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(Swamp_Bint_Harbor\) "Unsent letter") found there also exists in the game files, labeled "Slavers' notes" (ID: lw_gr13_poppy_slaver_note), but has empty contents.
