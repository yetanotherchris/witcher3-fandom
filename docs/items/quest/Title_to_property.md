## Item ID

mq1028_muggs_papers

**Title to property** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the secondary quest [The Dwarven Document Dilemma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Dwarven_Document_Dilemma "The Dwarven Document Dilemma"), these must be retrieved from [Gerd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gerd_\(peasant\) "Gerd \(peasant\)") to complete the quest. Eventually [Rostan Muggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rostan_Muggs "Rostan Muggs") will reveal the title belongs to his employer and is a deed to a townhouse in [Vengerberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vengerberg "Vengerberg").
