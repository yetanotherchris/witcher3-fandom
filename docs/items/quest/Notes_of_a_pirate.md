## Item ID

mq2015_kurisus_note

**Notes of a pirate** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be found at the site of a shipwreck southwest of [Lofoten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lofoten "Lofoten") proper, just off the [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") coast on a small island. 

## Journal entry

    _Hauled in a mighty fine catch this time. Seven women and ten men, each healthy and strong. Top-quality goods. Captain doesn't know yet where we'll sell them, but for merchandise like this, we're sure to get a good price anywhere. Perhaps we'll sail to [illegible]_
    _There's this one we've got[who's damned pretty](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kurisu "Kurisu") \- but a damned pain the arse, too. If not for that, I'd keep her for myself. But when she's not bawling her eyes out, she's yelling threats at us. Demands we let her go or drop her off at [Freya's temple](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya%27s_temple "Freya's temple"). And that if we don't, her brother will sail after her and slaughter us all. Now that'd be the day when [illegible]_
    _First three days at sea were quiet, but now a storm's blowing in. Blasted[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") weather. Why couldn't it just wait till we left these waters [text ends abruptly]_
