**Silver lid** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. During the main quest, [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"), after figuring out [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") and his group broke into [Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra")'s vault using a type of bomb, this is found near the bath they used to get the bomb in the correct place to break the walls down.
