**Key to katakan's hideout.**[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Oxenfurt_Drunk "Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk"), it's found on [Gael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gael "Gael")'s body after the [katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan") is killed and is needed to unlock the hut's door to get out.
