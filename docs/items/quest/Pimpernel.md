## Item ID

sq205_fernflower_petal

**Pimpernel** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

While it's needed for a particular quest, it can be found beforehand, removing a lot of the steps to get it later on. 

It can be found in a [succubus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Succubus "Succubus")' cave far south of the [Druids' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_Camp "Druids' Camp"), either by her offering it to you or by taking it after killing her.
