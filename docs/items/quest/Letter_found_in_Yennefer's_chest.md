# Letter found in Yennefer's chest

**Letter found in Yennefer's chest** is a document and quest item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). ## Item ID

ngu_gear_letter_db

This version can be seen when a new game has been started after the game has already been updated to [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It's found in [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") in a chest under a window, along with the [Dol Blathanna Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna_Armor_Set "Dol Blathanna Armor Set"), the [White Tiger of the West Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Tiger_of_the_West_Armor_Set "White Tiger of the West Armor Set"), and the [Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swords_of_the_Nine-Tailed_Vixen "Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen"). 

## Journal entry

    _My[Dear Friend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"),_
    _If you're reading this, it means you've found the chest I mentioned in my previous letter. You'll be pleased to know its contents belong to you._
    _After I spoke with the[emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor") regarding additional resources to aid in our quest, [he](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis") was willing to part with these items from his personal collection. The armor from [Dol Blathanna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna "Dol Blathanna") is a rare piece that marries the strengths of [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") and [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") craftsmanship. Apparently, [Francesca](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francesca_Findabair "Francesca Findabair") accepted Emhyr's offer of Nilfgaardian plate for her forces. Though, despite his attempt to significantly bolster an ally on the fringes of the [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"), only a portion of his aid ever reached its destination. To no one's great surprise, the elves were unimpressed with what [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") steel they did receive and thus refashioned the armor to their liking. You'll finally have the opportunity to masquerade as the [Queen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen "Queen") of Dol Blathanna's personal guard. It's a dream I know you speak of often._
    _As for the second set of armor, the emperor proved far more cryptic. He claims they were diplomatic gifts from distant kingdoms. The tiger armor is specifically designed to suit a western ruler – obviously Emhyr in this case. It was forged in the ancient style of a blacksmith who stole the secrets of its construction from their gods... Or so the legend goes._
    _And finally, the swords (to my eye they resemble a[Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") saber, though I'm no expert). These blades supposedly once belonged to a hero of myth and mystery. The man forged a pact with a vixen spirit and, through her blessing, was granted the ability to slay the monsters that had long plagued his village. As you can see, he carried both a steel and [silver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver "Silver") sword – a fact I thought might pique your interest._
    _Unfortunately, legends are all I can offer. Emhyr was stingy with the details – even more so than usual. It's a shame because I must admit I haven't the faintest idea where they might originate. That being said, I do believe[Zerrikania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikania "Zerrikania") and [Ofier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofier "Ofier") can be ruled out._
    _Dear friend, I trust you appreciate my efforts to help. Use these gifts as you see fit. I hope they will hasten your search for that which we have lost._
    _Your friend, Yennefer_

## Item ID

ngu_gear_letter_ck

This shorter version can be seen when a pre-[Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)") save is loaded. 

It is found in the same chest as the other version, but in this case only the [White Tiger of the West Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Tiger_of_the_West_Armor_Set "White Tiger of the West Armor Set") will be alongside it in the chest, while the [Dol Blathanna Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna_Armor_Set "Dol Blathanna Armor Set") will have been automatically added to the inventory.
