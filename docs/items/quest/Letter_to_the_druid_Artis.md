## Item ID

q206_arits_letter

**Letter to the druid Artis** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is the letter found by [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") on the remains of [Artis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Artis "Artis") at [Fornhala](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fornhala "Fornhala"), if he decides to help [Hjalmar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar_an_Craite "Hjalmar an Craite") during the quest [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"). However, it never appears in the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") and can only be obtained through console commands. 

## Journal entry

    _Artis,  
I know I'm asking a great deal of you. I know using the [Vildkaarls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildkaarl "Vildkaarl") sounds like treason and perfidy. But I also know in your heart you understand this must be done. This is the only way to heal our isles.  
If [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") is to survive, it has to change. And for that we need a strong leader and an end to the eternal quarreling between the jarls. The only way that will happen is if someone shakes our countrymen, and hard, so that they wake from their slumber of foolish and destructive traditions. The feast at [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") is our chance to deliver this shake.  
Convince the Vildkaarls to ride to [an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite")'s [castle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel"), but don't tell them what fate has in store for them. No doubt many of their number would see the justice of our cause and willingly give their lives for Skellige — but the fewer who know about our plan, the better. We can't risk someone warning [Crach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite"). Let them be unwitting tools with which we will forge a better future for out[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] isle.  
Do as I ask, and my gratitude will know no limits. You will sit by my throne as my advisor. I will expel [Ermion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mousesack "Mousesack") from the [Druids' Circle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_circle "Druids' circle") and make you [hierophant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierophant "Hierophant"). I will drive off [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya")'s [priestesses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priest "Priest") and make you lord of their [temple](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_of_Freyja "Temple of Freyja") and land.  
I'm counting on you. Glory to Skellige,  
The Future King_
