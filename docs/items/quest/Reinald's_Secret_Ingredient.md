## Item ID

mq1060_reinalds_ingredient

**Reinald's Secret Ingredient** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It is automatically looted when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") finds and reads [Reinald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald "Reinald")'s [second letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Second_letter_from_Reinald "Second letter from Reinald") that is needed to craft the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")'s own [philter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald%27s_Philter "Reinald's Philter").
