## Item ID

Triss mask

**Triss' fox mask** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. During the secondary quest, [A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)"), this needs to be bought from [Elihal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elihal "Elihal") to give to [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") so [Ingrid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ingrid_Vegelbud "Ingrid Vegelbud") will recognize her at the party.
