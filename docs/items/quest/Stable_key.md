## Item ID

q310_backdoor_key

**Stable key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Found near the guard inside, it is needed to unlock the stables during the main quest [Payback](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Payback "Payback"), if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") agrees to steal the [horses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") with [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") and [Aegar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aegar "Aegar").
