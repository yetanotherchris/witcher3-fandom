## Item ID

lw_gr_poi_042_chest_key

This **Small key** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , needed to open the chest for the treasure hunt quest [Tough Luck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tough_Luck "Tough Luck"). 

It can be found on the woman's body at the hidden treasure maker southeast of [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit") and northwest of [Marauders' Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marauders%27_Bridge "Marauders' Bridge") (closer to and directly north of where [Caravan Attack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caravan_Attack "Caravan Attack") takes place).

## Item ID

poi_san_b_02_key

This **Small key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_quest_items "Blood and Wine quest items") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, and can found on a corpse far west of [Flovive](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flovive "Flovive"), along with [Letter to Pascal Pélissier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_to_Pascal_P%C3%A9lissier "Letter to Pascal Pélissier") at the start of [The Black Widow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Black_Widow "The Black Widow"). It is needed to unlock a submerged chest in the [Sansretour River](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sansretour_River "Sansretour River").
