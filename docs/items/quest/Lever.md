## Item ID

q310_lever

**Lever** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is found during [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape") and used to activate a mechanism which opens the door into the [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") [sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_sewers "Oxenfurt sewers").
