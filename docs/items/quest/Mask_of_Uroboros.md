## Item ID

q203_eyeofloki

**Mask of Uroboros** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") find it during the main quest [The King is Dead – Long Live the King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_King_is_Dead_%E2%80%93_Long_Live_the_King "The King is Dead – Long Live the King"), at which point Yennefer "borrows" is to find out what happened to [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). During the next main quest, [Echoes of the Past](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Echoes_of_the_Past "Echoes of the Past"), she gives it to Geralt to use at the site of a cataclysm where she suspects Ciri was. Once the mask has been used, it becomes the [broken mask of Uroboros](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broken_Mask_of_Uroboros "Broken Mask of Uroboros").
