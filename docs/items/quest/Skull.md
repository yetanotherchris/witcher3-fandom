## Item ID

q201_skull

**Skull** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in [Ermion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ermion "Ermion")'s lab during the main quest [The King is Dead – Long Live the King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_King_is_Dead_%E2%80%93_Long_Live_the_King "The King is Dead – Long Live the King"), where at a certain point an item must be placed in a statue's hand to open a hidden area. The correct item is [mead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mead_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Mead \(The Witcher 3\)"), while this skull and a pine [cone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cone "Cone") are the two incorrect options. Afterwards, it can be discarded as [junk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk").
