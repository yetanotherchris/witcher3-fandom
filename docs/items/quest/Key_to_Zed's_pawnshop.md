## Item ID

sq310_zed_door_key

**Key to Zed's pawnshop** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found, along with the [Isengrim Faoiltiarna card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isengrim_Faoiltiarna_\(gwent_card\) "Isengrim Faoiltiarna \(gwent card\)"), on the remains on one of the bandits who have to be killed at [Zed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zed "Zed")'s pawnshop during the secondary quest [A Dangerous Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dangerous_Game "A Dangerous Game"), and is required to unlock the front door as an exit.
