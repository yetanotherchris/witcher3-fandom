**The Doppler's Salvation** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It has the same contents as [A Changeling Rescued](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Changeling_Rescued "A Changeling Rescued"), but only a different title, depending on a choice made at the start of the main quest [The Play's the Thing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Play%27s_the_Thing "The Play's the Thing").
