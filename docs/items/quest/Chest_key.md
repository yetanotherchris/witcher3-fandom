## Item ID

lw_sk20_chest_key

This key can be found on a corpse at the hidden treasure marker on the shore directly north from [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund"), and opens a treasure chest on the nearby rocks. 

This is directly behind where [Birna Bran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Birna "Birna") is chained if she is exposed during [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit").

This key is found along with a [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_Agda%27s_father_to_Timmon "Letter from Agda's father to Timmon") on one of the bodies of [Ulf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulf_\(Timmon%27s_brother\) "Ulf \(Timmon's brother\)") and [Mikkel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikkel_\(Skelliger\) "Mikkel \(Skelliger\)"). It is necessary to retrieve [Agda](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Agda "Agda")'s [dowry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gold_diamond_necklace "Gold diamond necklace").
