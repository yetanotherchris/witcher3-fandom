## Item ID

sq106_manuscript

**Letter written on elegant stationery** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be found on [Hammond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hammond "Hammond")'s body on [Faroe Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle") after killing him during the [Following the Thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Following_the_Thread_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Following the Thread \(The Witcher 3\)") quest. 

## Journal entry

    _Hammond,_
    _Thought I made it clear during our last face-to-face talk. I don't want to keep in touch with you, and I especially don't want to do any business with you. I've no doubt the enterprise you proposed would be profitable. Nevertheless, to speak colloquially: count me out._
    _There are plenty of other potential buyers in[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"): goldsmiths, jewelers and merchants dealing in luxury goods, for starters. I don't want to get involved._
    _Whatever you decide, I wish you well in it. Treat this letter as our final farewell._
    _[Karadin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jad_Karadin "Jad Karadin")_
