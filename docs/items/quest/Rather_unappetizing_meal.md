## Item ID

q401_disgusting_meal

**Rather unappetizing meal** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

If [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") is romantically involved with [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") during the main quest [Va Fail, Elaine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Va_Fail,_Elaine "Va Fail, Elaine"), she would ask him to prepare a meal for her and this is one of the possible options for it. She would specifically ask for scrambled eggs or an omelete, and if no [eggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Egg "Egg") were used to cook it, this is the meal that Geralt would prepare. Upon receiving it Yennefer would be baffled, asking _"What... What is this?"_ , to which the witcher replies with _"My own original recipe... It ain't pretty, but it's edible. I think."_

The other three meal options are [scrambled eggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scrambled_eggs "Scrambled eggs"), [omelet for Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Omelet_for_Yennefer "Omelet for Yennefer") and [omelet à la Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Omelet_%C3%A0_la_Geralt_of_Rivia "Omelet à la Geralt of Rivia"). 

All of this is still present in the game and can be enabled via this console command, used at the start of the quest: addfact(q401_cooking_enabled)
