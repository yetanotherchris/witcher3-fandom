## Item ID

Potestaquisitor

**Potestaquisitor** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Disturbance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Disturbance "Disturbance"), [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") gives this to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to locate the source of the disturbance affecting her [megascope](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Megascope "Megascope"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Disturbance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Disturbance "Disturbance")

## Notes

  * It appears to be affected by presence of [cats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(creature\) "Cat \(creature\)"), and while near them it squeals just like it would when it locates [magical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") disturbances. This is likely because cats are the only creatures apart from [dragons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon "Dragon") who can absorb magical energy, although it's unknown what they do with it.
