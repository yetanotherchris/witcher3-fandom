## Item ID

q103_incense

**Incense** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found during the main quest [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters"), after agreeing to help the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger"). It smells strongly of saffron and leaves a trail behind that [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can follow from [Tamara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger")'s room to a locked room in the basement that holds an altar to the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire"). After the quest completes, it can be sold or dismantled.
