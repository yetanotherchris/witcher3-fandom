## Item ID

noon_shadow_loot_note

**Poem** is a book and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is found on a skeleton near the rock formation at the end of the path running north from [Kaer Gelen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Gelen "Kaer Gelen"), and contains a hint to the location of a treasure chest. 

At midday, the treasure's location can be approximated by following the shadow cast by the rock formation - near [Yustianna's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna%27s_Grotto "Yustianna's Grotto"), northeast from the entrance and about halfway into the forest. The treasure contains 2 random diagrams and other random loot that has a high chance to include Relic armors and [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"). 

## Journal entry

    _When the sun peaks in the skies,  
Seek where shadow's end lies.  
Where light into darkness folds,  
You'll find treasures untold._

  * If having trouble finding the chest, you can teleport to it via the following console command, but be prepared to fight nearby [endrega drones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_drone "Endrega drone"): tptopos(725,729,87)
