## Item ID

q308_last_invite

**Letter found on a murder victim's body** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

If [Hubert Rejk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk") wasn't identified as the serial killer during the secondary quest [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins"), after it completes, a woman's body will appear on the eastern side of [Triss' hideout](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss%27_hideout "Triss' hideout") in [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits"). If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") walks by it, a cutscene will trigger with him finding this letter and realizing that [Nathaniel Pastodi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nathaniel_Pastodi "Nathaniel Pastodi") was the wrong guy.
