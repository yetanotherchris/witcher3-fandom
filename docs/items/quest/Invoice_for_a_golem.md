## Item ID

th1003_journal_lynx_part6

**Invoice for a golem** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found in the cave beneath [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") during [Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear"). It is at the end of the side tunnel behind the breakable wall to the right from the entrance. 

## Journal entry

    _Issued by: Jehuda Löw. Purchaser: Irenues var Steingard_
    _Good purchased: Guardian[golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem"), clay (porcelain)_
    _Warranty: In the even of damage to the ceramic, please report immediately to the nearest branch of Jehuda Löw and Apprentices. The golem can only be serviced by qualified company personnel._

## Trivia

  * This is likely a reference to [Judah Loew ben Bezalel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judah_Loew_ben_Bezalel), who was said to have created The Golem of Prague to protect Jews in the area.
