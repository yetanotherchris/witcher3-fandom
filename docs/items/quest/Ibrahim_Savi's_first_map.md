## Item ID

th1007_map_bear_upgrade1a

**Ibrahim Savi's first map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be purchased from the [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Kaer_Trolde_keep\) "Armorer \(Kaer Trolde keep\)") at [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel"). 

When read it'll give the areas to look for: 

  * All standard [Ursine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") armor diagrams at [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach")
  * Enhanced Ursine [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_silver_sword_-_enhanced "Ursine silver sword - enhanced"), [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Ursine_boots "Enhanced Ursine boots"), and [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Ursine_armor "Enhanced Ursine armor") diagrams

## Journal entry

    _After the discovery of marks relating to the[Bear School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Bear "School of the Bear") of [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") in the ruins of castle [Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tuirseach "Clan Tuirseach"), Professor [Atimstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slavko_Atimstein "Slavko Atimstein") sent me to prepare a map similar to the one prepared by A. Kermith indicating the various locations in the archipelago where these symbols occur. Professor Atimstein was of course correct in his suspicions - I quickly found such locations and have marked them on this map._
    _[Ibrahim Savi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ibrahim_Savi "Ibrahim Savi"). Adjunct in Natural History at [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/University_of_Oxenfurt "University of Oxenfurt")._
