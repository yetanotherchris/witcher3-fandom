## Item ID

mq2006_key_1

This **old key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found on a skeleton in the unmarked, abandoned hut southeast of [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare"), along with a [map](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mysterious_map "Mysterious map").

This **old key** is a quest item in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 

It is found along with [clerk's diary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clerk%27s_diary "Clerk's diary") on a corpse at the [Bastoy Prison Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastoy_Prison_Ruins "Bastoy Prison Ruins") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), after defeating the [alp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alp "Alp") there.
