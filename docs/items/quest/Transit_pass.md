## Item ID

mq1023_fake_papers

**Transit pass** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Despite being an obvious forgery of the official [letter of safe conduct](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_of_safe_conduct_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Letter of safe conduct \(The Witcher 3\)"), it still allows [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to pass through the [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") checkpoints. 

There are two ways to obtain this one: 

  * Buy one from the [shady merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shady_merchant "Shady merchant"): 
    * 100 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") (initial price)
    * 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") if [axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") [sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") is used (with at least level 1 of [Delusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Delusion "Delusion"))
    * 25 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") if [Fake Papers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fake_Papers "Fake Papers") is completed **and** you kept [Albin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albin_Hart "Albin Hart") alive
  * During the secondary quest, [A Deadly Plot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Deadly_Plot "A Deadly Plot"), [Gregor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gregor "Gregor") will give one if no other pass has been obtained yet

## Journal entry

    _Know ye that these present writings do give and grant the right of passage across the[Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)") and its subsidiaries, unobstructed by any blockade or other such obstacle, to the bearer thereof. Whoever doth hinder this right must desist in his unlawful skullduggery or sorely regret it forthwith. Thus speaketh I, [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"), called the Stern by some, a man too busy to go about applying my royal seal to trivialities such as this._

## Notes

  * This pass is as effective as the official one, though the guard at the [Western Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Western_Gate "Western Gate") will remark it's missing a seal. However, this appears to happen common enough that he waves it off as the city's fault and lets Geralt through.
  * If Albin dies, the shady merchant will refuse to sell a pass to Geralt if you didn't buy one beforehand.
