## Item ID

Tutorial Bomb

**Training bomb** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and a [bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is only obtainable and usable during the tutorial quest, [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen_\(quest\) "Kaer Morhen \(quest\)"), to show how to throw bombs in the game.
