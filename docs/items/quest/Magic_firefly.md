**Magic firefly** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") receives it from [Avallac'h](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Avallac%27h "Avallac'h") at the end of the [Va Fail, Elaine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Va_Fail,_Elaine "Va Fail, Elaine") quest, to help him find [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") during [The Isle of Mists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Isle_of_Mists_\(quest\) "The Isle of Mists \(quest\)"). During the latter, the dwarves of _"Rabenick and Company"_ mention having one like it themselves.
