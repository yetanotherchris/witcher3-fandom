**Skellige shawl** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

After starting the secondary quest [The Nithing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nithing "The Nithing"), this is found nearby and can be asked around [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig") about to learn its owner is [Jonna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonna "Jonna").
