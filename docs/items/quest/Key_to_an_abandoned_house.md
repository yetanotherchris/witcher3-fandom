## Item ID

mh306_dao_manor_door_key

**Key to an abandoned house** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Once you agree to help [Kurt Dysart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kurt_Dysart "Kurt Dysart") with his house problem, he'll give this key to unlock [Moldavie Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moldavie_Residence "Moldavie Residence")'s door.
