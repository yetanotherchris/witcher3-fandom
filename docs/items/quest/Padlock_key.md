## Item ID

sq201_padlock_key

**Padlock key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found alongside old skeleton remains in the flooded cave under [Morkvarg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg "Morkvarg")'s lair at [Freya's Garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya%27s_Garden "Freya's Garden"). It unlocks the two doors to the inside garden area, as well as the two rooms inside the house near the sacred tree there.
