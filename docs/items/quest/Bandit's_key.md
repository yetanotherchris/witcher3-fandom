## Item ID

lw_cb17_key

**Bandit's key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found along with [bandit's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit%27s_notes#Bandit's_notes_\(Kimbolt_Way\) "Bandit's notes") south-southwest of [Kimbolt Way](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kimbolt_Way "Kimbolt Way") and is needed to unlock the nearby wooden door.
