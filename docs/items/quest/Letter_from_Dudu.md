## Item ID

q303_dudus_briefing

**Letter from Dudu** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on a chest inside the hidden room in the late [Margrave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margrave "Margrave") [Henckel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henckel "Henckel")'s [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margrave_Henckel%27s_house "Margrave Henckel's house") during the main quest [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"). 

## Journal entry

    _[Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"),_
    _If you're reading these words, that means you have solved my[riddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mysterious_note_\(The_Witcher_3\)#Novigrad "Mysterious note \(The Witcher 3\)"), for which you have my congratulations. It also means you have not yet been caught and are still in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") \- which worries me greatly. I do not know exactly what you did on [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle"), but [Menge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Menge "Caleb Menge") is furious and will not rest until he captures you, skins you alive and burns you at the stake. And now that he's got his sanctimonious paws on [Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra")'s treasure, he can hire enough men to search every home in the city thrice over. I am a [doppler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler "Doppler"), so I will be able to hide... But you, you must flee. While you still can._
    _Your[Dudu](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dudu "Dudu")_
    _P.S. Don't even think about trying to break[Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") free from Menge's clutches - it's impossible. But knowing our friend, he'll wriggle his way out somehow. Or at least I hope he will._
    _P.P.S. Forgive me for not writing how to contact me, but a) you are to flee, remember? b)[Melitele](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Melitele "Melitele") guards those who guard themselves._
