**Key to Vigi's cage** is a key and a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted near the [ice giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant") during the main quest [The Lord of Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Lord_of_Undvik "The Lord of Undvik"), and is used to free [Vigi the Loon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vigi_the_Loon "Vigi the Loon") before attacking.
