## Item ID

q303_bomb_fragment

**Fragment of a silver cylinder** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

While exploring the sewers during the main quest [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"), this is found in the big room with the [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"). It is one part of a bomb that was used to break into [Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra")'s vault, and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") notes that runes are etched into it and it once contained [wyvern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern") oil and sugar.
