## Item ID

mh305_doppler_letter

This **Short letter** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") found on a table inside the [doppler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler "Doppler")'s house during [Contract: An Elusive Thief](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_An_Elusive_Thief "Contract: An Elusive Thief"). 

### Journal entry

    _[Janne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imp "Imp"), please - cut out this nonsense at once. This might come to you as a surprise, but a giant [cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(creature\) "Cat \(creature\)") will tend to stick in people's memory. Why don't you pick some other, safer form, like I did? I've been walking around as a beggar for three years now and no one's batted an eye. And such earnings! You wouldn't need to steal anymore. So, how'll it be? If you decide on it, I'll put in a word for you with the [King of Beggars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam")._
    _Till our next meeting,_
    _Louis_
    _P.S. Burn this letter once your read it, all right?_
    _P.P.S. Yes, yes, I know - I'm paranoid. But humor me and burn it._
    _P.P.P.S. If find this letter next time I come see you, you owe me a beer._

## Item ID

lw_sk32_note

This **Short letter** is found among the remains of certain mercenary known as "Master Sigvard", along with 100 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), 2 [meteorite silver plates](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meteorite_silver_plate "Meteorite silver plate") and a few random items. It is located at the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") marker on a small lake southeast of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund") and northeast of [Fornhala](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fornhala "Fornhala"), guarded by an [ekhidna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna "Ekhidna"). 

### Journal entry

    _My Dearest Master Sigvard,  
I have received your letter. I thus now know that you have a great many doubts regarding the contract I have offered you - and that you clearly suffer from gross misconceptions regarding the nature of a mercenary's trade, something which, given that this is a trade you yourself profess to practice, constitutes a highly puzzling paradox._
    _So let me inform you that a mercenary is not paid for asking questions or listing reservations, but for performing a concrete task. This task was described in simple, unambiguous words: you are to capture a[siren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") \- alive - and bring her to me. If you will not perform this task, you are of no interest to me. As to why I need this siren, or what I intend to do with her, that should not be of any interest to you. If these conditions do not suit you, I am sure I will find another man who will not refuse my coin._
    _-Count de Botton_

### Notes

  * In the same location, there's also the start of a blood trail, right next to the body, which can't be interacted with. Data in the game files suggests that it was supposed to lead to a "jewel chest" containing [Assassin's boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Assassin%27s_boots "Assassin's boots"). No such chest exists and, as this is not a hidden treasure marker, this is obviously not a bug, but rather some [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content").
