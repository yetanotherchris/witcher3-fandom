**Wooden amulet** is an unused [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , obtainable only via the console. 

It was meant to be used in the quest [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters"), evident in part by its item ID, but additionally it is moved out of the "Quest items" [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") tab when that quest completes.
