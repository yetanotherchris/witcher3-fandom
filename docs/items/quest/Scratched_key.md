## Item ID

gp_prologue_key01

This key is found on a body at the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison"), behind the locked gate. To get to it, from inside the garrison, one has to climb through the small opening on the far southeast corner of the fortress near the locked gate. A small walkway is just on the outside of the building with a weak wall on the far end of the walkway that can be knocked down with [aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard"). Jump through to reach the other side of the gate and the body with the key is on the furthest eastern point of this walkway.
