## Item ID

sq302_crystal

This is the megascope crystal found in [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart")'s hideout at [Est Tayiar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Est_Tayiar "Est Tayiar") during [Redania's Most Wanted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania%27s_Most_Wanted "Redania's Most Wanted"). It can later be given to the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") awating outside, [Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"), [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") or [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"), and/or shown to either of the two sorceresses in order to view its contents. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") may also choose to keep it for himself and later sell it.
