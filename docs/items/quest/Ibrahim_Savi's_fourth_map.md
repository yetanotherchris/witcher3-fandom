## Item ID

th1007_map_bear_upgrade3

**Ibrahim Savi's fourth map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be purchased from the [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Kaer_Trolde_keep\) "Armorer \(Kaer Trolde keep\)") at [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel"). 

When read it'll give the areas to look for: 

  * All standard [Ursine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") armor diagrams at [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach")
  * All mastercrafted Ursine diagrams
