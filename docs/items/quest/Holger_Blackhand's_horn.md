## Item ID

mq2001_horn

**Holger Blackhand's horn** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found during the secondary quest [A Hallowed Horn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Hallowed_Horn "A Hallowed Horn"), or by accident while exploring and looting the chest at the unmarked [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") north of [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn").
