## Item ID

lw_gr13_slavers_key

**Slavers' key** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , needed to free [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale")'s [swordsmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swordsmith_\(Mulbrydale\) "Swordsmith \(Mulbrydale\)") from his prison at [Swamp Bint Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_Bint_Harbor "Swamp Bint Harbor") (Peet's Landing), southwest from the village. It is dropped by a Nilfgaardian Deserter nearby, who is apparently the leader of a slaver group.
