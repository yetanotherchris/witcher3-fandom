**Doll** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ for the DLC quest, [Where the Cat and Wolf Play...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Where_the_Cat_and_Wolf_Play... "Where the Cat and Wolf Play..."). 

It belongs to [Millie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Millie "Millie") and can be used to get her to reveal what happened to [Honorton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honorton "Honorton").
