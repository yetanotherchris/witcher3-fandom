## Item ID

lw_sb13_note

**Fisherman's notes** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which however only exists in the game files and cannot be obtained without the use of console commands or mods. 

It is associated with the treasure hunt quest [A Costly Mistake](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Costly_Mistake "A Costly Mistake"), and likely was intended to appear instead of the [smuggler's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Smuggler%27s_notes "Smuggler's notes") at its start, but only if the nearby [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") has already been liberated from the pirates. Two different letters found at the hut there alternate based on the same event - [pirate's version](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_a_pirate_leader "Letter from a pirate leader") correlating to the smuggler's notes, and [fisherman's version](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_\(The_Witcher_3\)#Letter_\(Hangman's_Alley\) "Letter \(The Witcher 3\)") correlating to this one. 

## Journal entry

    _[Fish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish "Fish") soup for breakfast. Salted herring for dinner. Sprats and onions for supper. Day in, day out. For twenty years. I know other have got hunger to deal with, that it's a sin to complain... but what can I do? I'm sick to death of fish, had too damn much of pulling their bones out from between my teeth, of shitting out their scales._
    _But instead of sitting and complaining about life, it's better to change it. And it so happened Sobemir saw a pirate ship sink to the bottom not far off the coast. We're both good swimmers, so if the sea stays calm, we'll dive down and see what sort of treasure lies in its hold. And then, I swear to[Melitele](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Melitele "Melitele") \- I'll never eat another fish as long as I live._
