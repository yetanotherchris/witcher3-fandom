## Item ID

mq2001_kuilu

**Kuliu** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is considered [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite")'s ancestral steel sword. It's only found during [The Family Blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Family_Blade "The Family Blade") on one of the bodies at the [Whale Graveyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale_Graveyard "Whale Graveyard").
