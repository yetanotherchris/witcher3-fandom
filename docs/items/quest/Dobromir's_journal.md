**Dobromir's journal** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is found beside a chest inside [Lornruk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lornruk "Lornruk")'s tower. 

## Journal entry

    _(...) Da's chewing my head off about going to the lighthouse, about the fire needing tending. Thing is, don't give a ploughin' rat's arse, frankly! What am I, some[golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem"), fit for naught but climbing up there four times a day to drop off wood and pour oil?_
    _I'd much rather learn how to work a forge in town or at some[gnomish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnome "Gnome") foundry. Grandpa Amzvary was a smith, weren't he? Even forged [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") blades, so who's to say none o' his talent trickled down to me? (...)_
