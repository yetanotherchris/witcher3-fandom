## Item ID

**Phylactery** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , automatically obtained at the end of the main quest [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"). 

## History

This item was first used by [Eredin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eredin_Br%C3%A9acc_Glas "Eredin Bréacc Glas"), who released a powerful [curse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Curse "Curse") from it to turn [Avallac'h](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Avallac%27h "Avallac'h") into [Uma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Uma "Uma"). However, the nature of the [spell](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spell "Spell") took some time to actually manifest and so [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") took the now broken item with her to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), where she found [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") and asked for his help in repairing it, in hopes of lifting the curse. 

While the [bard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bard "Bard") didn't know how to fix it, he figured [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior "Whoreson Junior") could and so they took it to him. However, the criminal leader demanded a steep price to fix it: for them to steal [Sigi Reuven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra")'s closely guarded treasure. They left the broken phylactery with Whoreson Junior while they made plans to steal the treasure. 

The plan went wrong though and they never returned. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), knowing Junior knew something about the two, assaulted the criminal in his [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_mansion "Whoreson Junior's mansion"), who eventually mentioned the deal he'd made with them and Geralt took the phylactery with him. When he ran into [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") later in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), he handed it off to her for safe keeping. 

At [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), Yennefer informed Geralt that it needed to be charged, so he and [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert") set out to the [Circle of Elements](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Circle_of_Elements "Circle of Elements") and re-charged it. When they then began the [Trial of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trial_of_the_Grasses "Trial of the Grasses") on Uma, Yennefer was able to reverse the spell and had it re-captured back into the phylactery.
