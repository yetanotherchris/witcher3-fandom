**Sturdy barn key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found in the northwestern house at [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor") and is used to open the small door to the barn, where the reward promised from [Dolores Reardon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dolores_Reardon "Dolores Reardon") is hidden.
