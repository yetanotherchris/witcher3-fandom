## Item ID

sq205_preserved_mash

**Mash** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in the [distillery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Distillery "Distillery") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), during the [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests"), [Practicum in Advanced Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Practicum_in_Advanced_Alchemy "Practicum in Advanced Alchemy"). 

While it can be retrieved before being given the task by [Gremist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gremist "Gremist"), one can't make the [spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_spirit "Skellige spirit") until they actually get to that part in the quest.
