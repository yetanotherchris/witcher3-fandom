**Letter to Caleb Menge** is a document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , found during the main quest, [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"), but only if [Caleb Menge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Menge "Caleb Menge") is asked about the treasure. Otherwise, it won't appear as Caleb will burn it prior to checking in on [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"). 

## Journal entry

    _Caleb, I have new instructions regarding the organization of the next execution. When you are ready to meet, place[your Holy Tome](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Menge%27s_Holy_Tome "Menge's Holy Tome") in the drop box located in the column of the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") altar in the southwest corner of [the Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits"). We will meet at the usual spot. Do not tell anyone where you are going and bring no one with you. Burn this letter after reading._
    _[Yamurlak](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yamurlak_\(spy\) "Yamurlak \(spy\)")_
