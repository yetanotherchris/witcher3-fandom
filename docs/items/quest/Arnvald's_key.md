## Item ID

q206_arnvalds_key

**Arnvald's key** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on [Arnvald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arnvald "Arnvald")'s body if he is killed during [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit") and used to unlock a hidden compartment in his room.
