**Sewer key** is a key and a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Side_Effects_quest_items "Side Effects quest items") in the _[Side Effects](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Side_Effects "Side Effects")_ premium module. 

It is obtained by outdrinking [Orel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orel "Orel") during the [Down the Hatch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Down_the_Hatch "Down the Hatch") quest.
