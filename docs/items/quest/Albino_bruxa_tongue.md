**Albino Bruxa Tongue** is, or rather was, an ingredient used to make [the Decoctions of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Decoctions_of_the_Grasses "The Decoctions of the Grasses"), the potion used in the first part of the [Trial of the Grasses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trial_of_the_Grasses "Trial of the Grasses") by the [School of the Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") to create [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). 

A table in the [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") keep provides this, along with two other rare ingredients ([Forktail spinal fluid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail_spinal_fluid "Forktail spinal fluid") and [Manticore poison gland](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manticore_poison_gland "Manticore poison gland")) so [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") can perform the first part of the Trial of the Grasses, making [Uma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Uma "Uma") receptive to magic and hopefully lift the curse.
