## Item ID

q210_letter_for_emhyr

**Letter from Yennefer to Emhyr var Emreis** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

At the start of the [Battle Preparations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_Preparations "Battle Preparations") quest, it is given by [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), to give to [Emhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis") in order to demand the release of [Fringilla Vigo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fringilla_Vigo "Fringilla Vigo") during the subquest [Veni Vidi Vigo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Veni_Vidi_Vigo "Veni Vidi Vigo"). 

## Journal entry

    _Your Imperial Majesty,_
    _Allow me to remind you that our arrangement encompassed all the[sorceresses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorceress "Sorceress") wishing to take part in the risky endeavor whose success is so vital to us. I find it hard to believe that in the current situation, when I have already succeeded in gathering so many members of the [Lodge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses"), you would refuse to allow Fringilla Vigo to join our company.  
I understand there are personal factors that make you reluctant to part with your subject, but I assure you our interests are aligned in those matters as well.  
I promise that I will uphold the conditions of our arrangement, despite the fact that they will demand sacrifices of me and will leave me in an unfortunate situation._
    _I therefore request, politely yet with great insistence, that you release Fringilla._
    _With all due respect,  
Yennefer_
