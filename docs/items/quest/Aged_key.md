## Item ID

lw_de31_chest_key

**Aged key** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found underwater at the [Spoils of War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spoils_of_War "Spoils of War") located south of [Swamp Bint Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_Bint_Harbor "Swamp Bint Harbor"), in a wrecked boat. It unlocks the chest right next to the boat, containing a [Moonblade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moonblade "Moonblade"). 

## Notes

  * Despite being a quest item, this key is not part of any marked quest.
