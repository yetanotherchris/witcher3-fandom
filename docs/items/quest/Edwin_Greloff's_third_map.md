## Item ID

th1005_map_gryphon_upgrade2

**Edwin Greloff's third map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be purchased from the [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Armorer \(Hierarch Square\)") in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). 

Reading it provides the areas to look for: 

  * All standard [Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") armor diagrams at [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto")
  * Superior Griffin [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Griffin_armor "Superior Griffin armor"), [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_silver_sword_-_superior "Griffin silver sword - superior"), and [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_steel_sword_-_superior "Griffin steel sword - superior") diagrams

## Journal entry

    _Beautiful. Professor[Slavko Atimstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slavko_Atimstein "Slavko Atimstein") will be furious. I lost the first map in a game of [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), and then the second was stolen from me while I was slightly indisposed after a hard night in the casino. I cannot arrive empty-handed at the station near the [tomb under Crookback Hills](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto") or the professor will vivisect me on the spot. The four new locations I have marked on this map might be enough to protect me from his wrath._
    _[Edwin Greloff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Edwin_Greloff "Edwin Greloff"). Adjunct in Natural History at [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")_
