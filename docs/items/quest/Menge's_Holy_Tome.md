## Item ID

q303_marked_bible

**Menge's Holy Tome** is a readable [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can only be looted from [Caleb Menge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Menge "Caleb Menge")'s corpse during the main quest [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"). The tome is then used to signal to the [spy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yamurlak_\(spy\) "Yamurlak \(spy\)") that "Caleb Menge" wishes to meet with him.
