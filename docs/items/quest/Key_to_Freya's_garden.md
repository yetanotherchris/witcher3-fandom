## Item ID

q205_key_to_burrow

**Padlock key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is found on the remains of a priestess in a cave under [Freya's Garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya%27s_Garden "Freya's Garden"), just past [Skjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skjall "Skjall")'s body. It's only purpose is to unlock the door right next to it, as returning the way Geralt reached it isn't possible. Thus, a player may get stuck in the cave without this key, if dropped down the well without Yennefer being there to trigger the scripted event during [Nameless](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nameless "Nameless").
