## Item ID

th1005_map_gryphon_upgrade1b

**Edwin Greloff's second map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be purchased from the [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Midcopse\) "Armorer \(Midcopse\)") in [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). 

When read it'll give the areas to look for: 

  * All standard [Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") armor diagrams at [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto")
  * Enhanced Griffin [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Griffin_armor "Enhanced Griffin armor"), [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Griffin_gauntlets "Enhanced Griffin gauntlets"), and [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_silver_sword_-_enhanced "Griffin silver sword - enhanced") diagrams

## Journal entry

    _Ploughing syphilitic son of a poxy whore. I lost the first map I prepared for Professor[Atimstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slavko_Atimstein "Slavko Atimstein") in a game of [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). I must now return to my base camp near the [tomb under Crookback Hills](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto") and start anew. Meanwhile, I will mark further points of interest on this map._
