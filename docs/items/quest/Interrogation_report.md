**Interrogation report** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be looted from the [Amavet Fortress Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amavet_Fortress_Ruins "Amavet Fortress Ruins") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), alongside the [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Serpentine_steel_sword "Diagram: Serpentine steel sword") for the [Viper steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_steel_sword "Viper steel sword"). 

If not already found, it will direct [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to the [White Orchard cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_cemetery "White Orchard cemetery") for the [Viper silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_silver_sword "Viper silver sword") [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Serpentine_silver_sword "Diagram: Serpentine silver sword"). 

## Journal entry

    _Location: Amavet fortress  
Persons taking part: Sir [Ignatius Verrieres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignatius_Verrieres "Ignatius Verrieres"), Bart., the bailiff Andreas Blume, Sir Ignatius' underlings: Carmy, Gardy and Bellsy.  
Suspect: [Kolgrim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kolgrim "Kolgrim"), [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") of the [Viper School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Viper "School of the Viper"). _
    _The witcher Kolgrim stands accused of kidnapping[Vitty](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitty "Vitty"), son of [Paul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Paul "Paul"), a beekeeper residing in [White orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). Suspect was detained pending trial. Upon detention he was searched and a diagram for the forging of a steel sword was found on his person. It is suspected this sword was used to intimidate or possibly kill the kidnapped child. It is likewise suspected that the accused possesses other such diagrams for the forging of death-dealing implements, though none have been found to date. Sir Ignatius has requested the witcher undergo preliminary interrogation with the application of torture. This will take place once a torturer with the proper qualifications has been found._
    _Addendum:  
It was later discovered [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") were responsible for the boy's disappearance. Unfortunately Kolgrim died before this information reached the investigators, for rather than face the baronet's judgment, he chose [trial by ordeal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trial_by_ordeal "Trial by ordeal"). Sir Ignatius agreed and ordered he cleanse the Verrieres family crypt of [specters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Specter "Specter"). Kolgrim never emerged from said crypt. It is highly likely the motley [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"), specters and evil powers residing therein proved too much for him._
