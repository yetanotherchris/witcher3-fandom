## Item ID

th1005_map_gryphon_upgrade3

**Edwin Greloff's fourth map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be purchased from [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori") (after completing [Of Swords and Dumplings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Swords_and_Dumplings "Of Swords and Dumplings")). 

Reading it reveals the locations for: 

  * All standard [Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") armor diagrams at [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto")
  * Mastercrafted Griffin [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Griffin_armor "Mastercrafted Griffin armor"), [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Griffin steel sword - mastercrafted"), and [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Griffin silver sword - mastercrafted") diagrams

## Journal entry

    _[the writing on the map is smeared with dried blood, cannot be read]_
    _[addendum] Map ain't worth shit. Only thing I found were some[griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") symbols scratched on the walls. Can see we're not going to get anything worth near enough to pay back the debts that dandy doctor wracked[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] up with us. Good thing he's worm food now. But maybe it would be worthwhile to loot that [tomb under Crookback Hills](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto")? I doubt our doctor friend would've wasted his time just running around looking for primitive drawings - there's gotta be some treasure about._
