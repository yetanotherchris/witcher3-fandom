## Item ID

q103_spinning_top

**Top** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is a wooden spinning top that [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") carved for [Gretka](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gretka "Gretka"). 

In the quest [Ciri's Room](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri%27s_Room "Ciri's Room"), [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can pick the top off the floor of Ciri's room in [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), and take it to Gretka, for which she will be grateful.
