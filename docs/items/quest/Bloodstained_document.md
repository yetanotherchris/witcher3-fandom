## Item ID

lw_gr39_note

**Bloodstained document** is [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found on a body, along with a [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bloodstained_key "Bloodstained key"), at the hidden treasure marker northwest of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort"). 

## Journal entry

    _I've stopped the bleeding. I need to collect my thoughts._
    _I could go for help. There's a village not far, I can hear[dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog") barking. But people would see what uniform I wear. They'd ask what I was doing in the swamps. They'd figure it out. They'd take everything, maybe kill me. This is [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), after all._
    _I've lost a quart of blood. Maybe more. If the wound is still clean come dawn, I should pull through. I'll leave at daylight, maybe then the[drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") won't attack. I'll bury the treasure and head to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Should be able to find a decent man there to patch me up. Someone who won't ask questions. Who doesn't care if you're a deserter._
    _All that's left to me now is to parry that no one sees the tracks I left. That they don't follow the blood trail and find treasure. But no one will come through here. I hope. Everything will be fine._
