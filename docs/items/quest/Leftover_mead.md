## Item ID

q206_wine_sample

**Leftover mead** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is the horn with spiked mead, which [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can find while investigating after the [berserkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werebear "Werebear")' attack during [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"), if he chooses to help [Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite"). It is then given to [Hjort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjort "Hjort"), who extracts [psilocybe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Psilocybe "Psilocybe") [herbs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjort%27s_herbs "Hjort's herbs") from it.
