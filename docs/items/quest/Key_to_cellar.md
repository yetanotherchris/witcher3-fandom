**Key to cellar** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , used to open the cellar in the [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Udalryk%27s_old_family_home "Udalryk's old family home") that holds the sword [Brokvar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brokvar "Brokvar"). It is found in the room adjacent to the fire oven in the same building.
