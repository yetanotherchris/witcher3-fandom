**Power cell** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

There are two obtainable power cells, both in [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart")'s hideout at [Est Tayiar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Est_Tayiar "Est Tayiar") during [Redania's Most Wanted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania%27s_Most_Wanted "Redania's Most Wanted"). They are to be placed in slots and activated with [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard") in order to activate portals. The first one is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by the [witch hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") who he meets inside, while the second needs to be recovered from [nekkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker") who have stolen it.
