## Item ID

th1005_map_gryphon_upgrade1a

**Edwin Greloff's first map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be purchased from the [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Midcopse\) "Armorer \(Midcopse\)") in [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). 

When read it'll give the areas to look for: 

  * All standard [Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") armor diagrams at [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto")
  * Enhanced Griffin [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Griffin_boots "Enhanced Griffin boots"), [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Griffin_trousers "Enhanced Griffin trousers"), and [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_steel_sword_-_enhanced "Griffin steel sword - enhanced") diagrams

## Journal entry

    _After being recruited by Professor[Slavko Atimstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slavko_Atimstein "Slavko Atimstein") to join his research group, I began a study of the [Griffin School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Griffin "School of the Griffin") of [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). My base of operations was the [tomb under the Crookback Hills](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto"). The professor claimed this was the final resting place for one of the "griffins" and wished to find a way inside it._
    _I quickly discovered a range of places of interest, each marked with a pictogram in the shape of a Griffin School[witcher medallion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_medallion "Witcher medallion"). I've marked these points on this map._
    _[Edwin Greloff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Edwin_Greloff "Edwin Greloff"). Adjunct in [Natural History](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy_faculties "Oxenfurt Academy faculties") at [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")._
