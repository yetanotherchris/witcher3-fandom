**Locked strongbox** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be obtained from the swamp south of the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") during the secondary quest, [Precious Cargo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Precious_Cargo "Precious Cargo").
