## Item ID

q105_book_about_witches

**The Ladies of the Wood** is a book and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") shortly before the end of [Wandering in the Dark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_in_the_Dark "Wandering in the Dark"). 

It will stay in the "Quest items" [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") tab until Geralt reaches [The Orphans of Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog"), after which it can be sold. 

## Journal entry

    _The gods have abandoned us. The mighty of this earth care not for our fate. Only the[Ladies of the Wood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones") watch over [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). In foul times, when plague or famine steals our harvest, we must beg the Ladies for help. If they deem fit, they will hear our pleas and knock back fortune's foul blows._
    _This is how one begs help from the Ladies:_
    _Find a child, young and innocent, and take it to[Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crookback_Bog "Crookback Bog"). Search out the Ladies' shrine - that is where the Trail of Treats begins. Set the child off on the trail and it shall follow its sweet track and find the Good Ladies. The child will never want for anything ever again, for the Ladies are kind and generous._
    _Standing before their shrine, pronounce your request and the Good Ladies will hear, for they see and hear all that takes place in their demesne. If you made the offering as it must be done, your supplication will be heard._
