## Item ID

mh103_killers_knife

**Assassin's knife** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the road west of [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"), and helps [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") obtain more information about [Jenny o' the Woods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jenny_o%27_the_Woods "Jenny o' the Woods").
