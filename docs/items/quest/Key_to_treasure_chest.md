## Item ID

lw_de11_treasure_key

**Key to treasure chest** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found along with some [crumpled notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crumpled_notes#Crumpled_notes_\(Mowshurst\) "Crumpled notes") in the abandoned village of [Mowshurst](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mowshurst "Mowshurst"), situated north of [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor") and northeast of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch"). The key is looted from the dead body of the last survivor of that village, who had barricaded alone in a storm cellar.
