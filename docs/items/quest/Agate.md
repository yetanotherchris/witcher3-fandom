## Item ID

sq302_agates

**Agate** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart")'s quarters under [Est Tayiar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Est_Tayiar "Est Tayiar") during [Redania's Most Wanted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania%27s_Most_Wanted "Redania's Most Wanted"). However, despite being a quest item, it doesn't serve a functional purpose other than [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") commenting that Philippa wore several similiar agates around her neck, and it stays in the quest items inventory forever. It was part of [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") where Philippa was more heavily involved, but now only remains as a token item.
