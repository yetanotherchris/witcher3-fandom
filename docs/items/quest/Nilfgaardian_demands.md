## Item ID

q103_nilfgaardian_demand

**Nilfgaardian demands** is a document and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found in Baron [Phillip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s study in [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), usually during the quest [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters").
