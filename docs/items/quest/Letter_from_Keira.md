## Item ID

q106_note_from_keira

**Letter from Keira** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") readable [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

In an under development iteration of the quest [A Towerful of Mice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Towerful_of_Mice "A Towerful of Mice"),[1] the story of the tower on [Fyke Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyke_Isle "Fyke Isle") is told to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [the Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar"), who suggests that Geralt should seek [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") for help in lifting the curse off the island [N 1] , but also says that Keira has gone in hiding after [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") arrived in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")[N 2] and he doesn't know where she is. Geralt then decides to look for clues in her last known [home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz%27s_hut "Keira Metz's hut"), where this letter is found.
