**Aen N'og Mab Taedh'morc** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted from a caged skeleton at the lowest level inside [Hindhold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindhold "Hindhold"). While part of the [Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear") quest, it doesn't serve a functional purpose.
