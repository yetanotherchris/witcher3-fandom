## Item ID

sq106_hammond_whereabouts

**Orders from Hammond** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the dead bodies of the Dagr guarding the gates of [Trottheim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trottheim "Trottheim") on [Faroe Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle") if they are killed. One can be looted on each pirate labeled as "Dagr" guarding the two gates of the village.

## Bugs

  * The item can only be obtained if [Flesh for Sale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flesh_for_Sale "Flesh for Sale") hasn't been completed. After that quest is done, the dagr guarding the gates no longer spawn, despite the cutscene with them magically triggering then [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") appraches - after the dialogue ends, they are nowhere to be seen.
