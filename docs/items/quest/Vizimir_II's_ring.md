## Item ID

**Vizimir II's ring** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart") during [Reason of State](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reason_of_State "Reason of State"), to be then given to [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") as proof that the witcher has found the sorceress.
