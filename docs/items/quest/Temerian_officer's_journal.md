## Item ID

lw_temerian_soldiers_journal

**Temerian officer's journal** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It is related to the [treasure hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_treasure_hunts "The Witcher 3 treasure hunts") quest [Temerian Valuables](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Valuables "Temerian Valuables"), and it seems logical that it should've been found along with the [Temerian lily key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_lily_key "Temerian lily key") at the hidden treasure marker west of the [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)"), or close to it. However, in the game files it is defined inside a different loot container, which can't be found during gameplay. 

Interestingly, when added via console commands, the journal is automatically marked as already read - possibly as a dirty fix to have a location for the next objective where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has to look after obtaining the key, which isn't coupled with other clues. 

## Journal entry

    _The 15th day of[Yule](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yule "Yule")_
    _It's confirmed - we will be escorting a portion of the royal treasures. Quite a large portion, in fact. Orders from the very top, they say... On the one hand it's an honor, on the other... it's frightening._
    _The 17th day of Yule_
    _Everything's ready. Tylar, head of special forces, has prepared the travel documents and other required papers. Let's hope that's enough. Tomorrow they'll hand it over and we're off._
    _The 20th day of Yule_
    _Everything went well - at first. But after two days we spotted them -[Black Ones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army"), hot on our trail._
    _We had to hide the treasure. We stopped one night by the old mill, and at dawn everyone but me and Yorga went east. The Nilfs bought it - they followed the others, and we were able to bury the treasure undisturbed._
    _Let's hope it stays there until the end of the war - or at least until the Nilfs stop hounding our patrol._
