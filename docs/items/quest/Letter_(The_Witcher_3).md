## Item ID

lw_cp39_captains_log

This letter is found in a chest at the hidden treasure marker on a small island southeast of [Widows' Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Widows%27_Grotto "Widows' Grotto"). Picking it up starts the [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks_\(quest\) "Coast of Wrecks \(quest\)") treasure hunt quest. 

### Journal entry

    _Collect the goods from the same crate as usual. If you get it to the drop-off on time, you'll get the full sum we agreed to. If you get it there no later than a week after we agreed, you'll get half. If you're later than a week, you're dead._
    _-Hugo._
    _P.S. Don't want any witnesses. If someone notices you - kill them first, ask questions later._

## Item ID

lw_de32_treasure_note

This letter is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") book, dropped by one of the deserters at the hidden treasure marker southeast of [Marauders' Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marauders%27_Bridge "Marauders' Bridge"). Picking it up starts the [Hidden from the World](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hidden_from_the_World "Hidden from the World") treasure hunt quest. 

### Journal entry

    _Drabik,_
    _Listen up, you stupid git. Go on keeping the treasure out for all to see like that, we won't keep it very long at all. What, you think we're the only bandits around? Well, knowing what a fool y'are, perhaps you do, so here's me telling you - no. We ain't. So be a good little dunce and hide it somewhere where only you and me will know how to find it again. Otherwise I'll tan your arse till it's red as a watermelon._
    _-Mettky_

## Item ID

lw_gr_poi_042_letter

This letter is found on a man's body at the hidden treasure marker southeast of [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit") and northwest of [Marauders' Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marauders%27_Bridge "Marauders' Bridge") (closer to and directly north of where [Caravan Attack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caravan_Attack "Caravan Attack") takes place). Picking it up starts the [Tough Luck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tough_Luck "Tough Luck") treasure hunt quest. 

### Journal entry

    _Juenner,_
    _Of course you can stay with us! What kind of question's that?! Come, bring the whole family. War hasn't struck here yet, we've got plenty of food - my belly's even grown a bit in recent weeks. So if you take a bit of beer and bacon out of my mouth, you'll actually be doing me a favor._
    _We're already preparing a bed for you. You'll stay in the attic - it's comfortable there, long as you don't forget to duck when you go up the stairs._
    _Happy trails, Juenner. See you soon._
    _\--Matsk_

## Item ID

lw_gr34_note

This letter is found along with a [treasure key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure_key "Treasure key"), on a [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf")'s corpse at the hidden treasure marker west of the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post") and south from [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten"). 

### Journal entry

    _Villard, you old bastard!_
    _I ken where you can drub up the coin to pay off your many debts -[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). Now, blatherheads say those bogs ain't worth a muffled fart from a saggy sow, but don't ye believe them, mate, they dinnae ken shite from sugar! A man can make a killing there - and a dwarf can do twice as good, I'm sure._
    _So here's the craik. While crossing Velen a week or so past, I spied a few well-dressed stiffs laying about the forest, travel bags untouched. Refugees, see, fleeing the[war](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III") \- and whoever flies up to them first gets all their worldly belongings. You ken what I'm saying, mate? Grab a chest, head off cross the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)") posthaste and strip those stiffs of rings, necklaces and everything else afore some nimbler prick does! Oh, but keep a sharp axe round your belt all the while, for Velen's pure heevin with [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"). But hey, it's all in the name of a good cause - filling your pockets!_
    _Your ever-loving Gruggen_

Found inside the fishing hut at the [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Abandoned site \(Spitfire Bluff\)") west of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley"), after the site is liberated. ## Item ID

lw_sk_poi_005_treasure_note

This letter is found in a chest inside the western hut at the [Ruined Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_Inn "Ruined Inn") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), and is needed to start the [Family Fortune](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Fortune "Family Fortune") treasure hunt quest. 

### Journal entry

    _Fjale,_
    _I've got bad news for you, Your father's ship sank off the southern coast of[Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). Far as I know, there were no survivors. Only a few bodies were recovered. They were already blue and bloated. I could only recognize Jovik by his tattoo. I'll do what I can to prepare the body for the funeral, but brace yourself. It won't be a pretty sight._
    _I know what was on that ship. Everything you had. So if you need to borrow some coin, don't hesitate to write. Your father and I were bound by blood - I'll never refuse you help._
    _\--Varlind_
