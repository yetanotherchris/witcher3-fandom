## Item ID

mq2020_pirate_lord_house_door

**Key to pirate leader's house** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and unlocks the pirate leader's house in [Trottheim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trottheim "Trottheim"). 

It can only be looted off the slave driver's body during the [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests"), [Flesh for Sale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flesh_for_Sale "Flesh for Sale"), which means you'd have to attack the pirates. Technically, the quest can first be completed peacefully and the key will still drop afterwards, before the slave driver has been despawned - at least while [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") is still in the vicinity of Trottheim (and possibly until [Following the Thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Following_the_Thread_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Following the Thread \(The Witcher 3\)") requires him to go there, though this hasn't been verified), but ultimately there's only one way to get it.
