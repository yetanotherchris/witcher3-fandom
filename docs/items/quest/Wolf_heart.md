## Item ID

sq204_wolf_heart

**Wolf heart** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the [contract](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_contracts "The Witcher 3 contracts") [In the Heart of the Woods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Heart_of_the_Woods "In the Heart of the Woods"), if you side with [Harald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harald_\(Fayrlund\) "Harald \(Fayrlund\)") and go to the altar, you must kill the 5 white [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)") that spawn here and loot each one for a wolf heart to place on the altar. 

## Bugs

  * Despite being burned in the ritual, the hearts are not removed from the inventory, even after the quest ends. The only way to remove them is on PC via the console command removeitem('sq204_wolf_heart') (executed as many times as the amount of hearts you've looted).
