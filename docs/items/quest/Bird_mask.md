## Item ID

Geralt mask 03 (Elihal)  
EP2 mask 03 (Pierre)

**Bird mask** is a [mask](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_masks "The Witcher 3 masks") and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is one of the three masks [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can choose to don for the ball at the [Vegelbud Estate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Estate "Vegelbud Estate"). 

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Elihal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elihal "Elihal") at his [workshop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elihal%27s_workshop "Elihal's workshop") outside [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * [Pierre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pierre "Pierre") at his [workshop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tailor%27s_Workshop "Tailor's Workshop") in [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair")

## Associated quest

  * [A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)")

## Notes

  * The mask bought during [A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)") goes in the Quest Items section of the inventory and stays there forever - it can't be removed without console commands. However, the one you can buy from [Pierre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pierre "Pierre") goes into the Weapons section and you can do whatever you wish with it.
