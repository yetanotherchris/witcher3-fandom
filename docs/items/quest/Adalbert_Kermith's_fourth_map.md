## Item ID

th1003_map_lynx_upgrade3

**Adalbert Kermith's fourth map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be purchased from the [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Lindenvale\) "Blacksmith \(Lindenvale\)") in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale"). 

When read it'll give the areas to look for: 

  * All the standard [Feline](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") armor diagrams
  * All the mastercrafted Feline diagrams

## Journal entry

    _The trail of symbols relating to the[Cat School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School "Cat School") of [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") led me to four additional locations marked with the same sign. I was not surprised to see that the pictograms found in each do not differ from the one [Professor Atimstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slavko_Atimstein "Slavko Atimstein") discovered in the caverns beneath [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle")._
    _As instructed, I have marked these locations on this map._
    _[Adalbert Kermith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Adalbert_Kermith "Adalbert Kermith"). Adjunct in [Natural History](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy_faculties "Oxenfurt Academy faculties") at the [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")._
