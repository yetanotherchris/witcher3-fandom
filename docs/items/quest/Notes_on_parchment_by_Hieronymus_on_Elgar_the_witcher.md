## Item ID

th1009_map_wolf_upgrade3b

**Notes on parchment by Hieronymus on Elgar the witcher** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be purchased from [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde")'s [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Kaer_Trolde_keep\) "Armorer \(Kaer Trolde keep\)") along with several other maps. 

When read it'll give areas to look for: 

  * all the standard [Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") armor diagrams
  * the mastercrafted boots, gauntlets, and trousers diagrams

## Journal entry

    _Some of the improvements to[School of the Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") gear are hidden in the [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") Isles. [Elgar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elgar "Elgar") left them in the central part of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") and in the ruins of two watchtowers: one on [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"), one on [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog"). He wanted them to be available for [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") who need them and are clever enough to find them._
