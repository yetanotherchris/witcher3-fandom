## Item ID

Snow Ball

**Snowball** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and a [bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is only obtainable and usable during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests") [Blood on the Battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_on_the_Battlefield "Blood on the Battlefield"), if one chooses to say that they might know how to lift up [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")'s spirits. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will then start a snowball fight with her. To make more snowballs, simply interact with the snow piles nearby.
