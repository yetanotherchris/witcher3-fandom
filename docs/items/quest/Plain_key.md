## Item ID

**Plain key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found at the hidden treasure marker southwest of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") at the end of the narrow strait, on a body. It unlocks the nearby chest that's guarded by a [bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear").
