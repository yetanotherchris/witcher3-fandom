## Item ID

sq201_cursed_jewel

**Cursed fang** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be acquired or looted from [Einar of Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Einar_\(Larvik\) "Einar \(Larvik\)"), and later used to lift [Morkvarg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg "Morkvarg")'s curse.
