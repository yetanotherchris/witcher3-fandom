## Item ID

q202_shackles

**Shackles** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is found near the big boat hull on [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") and is supposed to be the "twine" Octo asks for during [The Lord of Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Lord_of_Undvik "The Lord of Undvik"). However, whether due to a bug or intentionally, they don't really serve a purpose as the game will always act as if they are given to [Octo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harald_an_Tordarroch "Harald an Tordarroch") once he receives [nails](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nails#Nails_\(quest_item\) "Nails"). If found before the quest completes, the item will turn into sellable [junk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk").
