## Item ID

sq303_pollys_key

**Key to Polly's home** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is found during the secondary quest [Cabaret](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cabaret "Cabaret"), under a bucket in front of [Polly](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Polly "Polly")'s home and then used to unlock the door there.
