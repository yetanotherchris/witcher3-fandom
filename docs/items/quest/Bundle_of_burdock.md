## Item ID

q301_burdock

**Bundle of burdock** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Novigrad Dreaming](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Dreaming "Novigrad Dreaming"), if one wishes to chase out [Sarah](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarah "Sarah") rather than let her stay, this is required to burn in the stove, as [godlings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Godling "Godling") don't like the smell of it. It can be purchased from the [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Glory_Lane\) "Herbalist \(Glory Lane\)") in [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Novigrad Dreaming](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Dreaming "Novigrad Dreaming")

## Notes

  * Despite what you did with it, this item will remain in your [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory"), even after the quest is completed.
