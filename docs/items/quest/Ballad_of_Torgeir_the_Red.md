## Item ID

**Ballad of Torgeir the Red** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found lying on the floor by the old dungeons of the ruins of [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach") and details how the castle came to ruin, and the fate of [Jarl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jarl_of_Skellige "Jarl of Skellige") [Torgeir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torgeir_the_Red "Torgeir the Red") and the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Gerd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gerd_\(witcher\) "Gerd \(witcher\)") of the [Bear School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School "Bear School"). 

## Journal entry

    _Hark! ... I shall sing you a tale  
Of a jarl, brave and true, a warrior bold  
Friend to his people, and to his foes wrath unrolled.  
Like a sturdy ship, his courage never leaked.  
Might was Jarl Torgeir of [Clan Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tuirseach "Clan Tuirseach")._
    _For a man so grand,[the Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") held plunder too few  
So up [Alba](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alba_\(river\) "Alba \(river\)")'s shining waters he sailed with his crew.  
Arriving at the [City of Golden Towers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaard_\(city\) "Nilfgaard \(city\)")' walls  
He razed, ravaged and roared and shook the [emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor")'s halls._
    _But[Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") knows to suture strife with coin  
And so out cutthroats crept, the jarl in battle to join  
Lured by the fattened Nilfgaardian purse  
To Skellige sailed bandits, villains and worse._
    _Girded for battle, the jarl stood in his fort  
Yet Nilfs know no more honor than [demon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Demon "Demon") or [chort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort "Chort").  
A catapult they brought 'gainst the jarl's mighty keep.  
The walls crumbled, buying his warriors deep._
    _An ocean of rubble swallowed men a great many,  
Here we name but few of that noble company:  
The pilgrim Tore, Stig of [Dovre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dorve_Ruins "Dorve Ruins")[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] born, Slumbering Sigvard  
And the witcher Gerd, to the last the jarl's faithful guard._
    _[rest of ballad illegible]_
