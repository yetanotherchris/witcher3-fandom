## Item ID

mq1060_armor_vesemir_note

**Vesemir's note** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

After completing the secondary quest [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow"), it can be found in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") keep on a bookshelf on the upper platform to the left of the main doors, along with [Osmund's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Osmund%27s_notes "Osmund's notes"). 

## Journal entry

    _These ideas for gear improvement are useless. What a damned shame. According to chronicles from the end of the tenth century, Osmund suffered a severe head wound and never reached[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). When he regained consciousness, the [school](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf")'s leader confirmed permanent damage had set in. His ability to speak, even communicate simply, was all but completely lost. With [Reinald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald "Reinald") and the armor diagrams still missing, I will leave these notes here for their historical value. It's the only value they have left._
