**Wedding ring** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

This ring once belonged to [Lessy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lessy "Lessy")'s unnamed grandmother. Shortly before the grandmother died, she found out her dead husband, [Jackamar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jackamar "Jackamar"), had had several lovers on the side so she didn't want to be buried by him when she died. In the afterlife she later changed her mind and asked [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), not knowing she was a ghost, to place her wedding ring on her husband's grave so they could be together again.
