## Item ID

q103_tamara_shrine_key

This key is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It unlocks an altar to the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") and is found in [Tamara Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger")'s room.

## Item ID

mq6002_house_key

This key is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone_quest_items "Hearts of Stone quest items") in the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion and can be found on [Judd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Judd "Judd")'s body during the quest [Without a Trace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Without_a_Trace "Without a Trace") and unlocks the couple's hut (though it doesn't work on the interior door). 

It can be found either by killing the couple yourself or waiting until [Otto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber") sends people to kill them (but only happens if he's told the truth).
