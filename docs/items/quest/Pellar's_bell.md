## Item ID

q103_bell

**Pellar's bell** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests"), [A Princess in Distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Princess_in_Distress "A Princess in Distress"), it is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [the Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar"), for the sole purpose of calling [Princess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Princess_\(goat\) "Princess \(goat\)"), his [goat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Goat "Goat"), back home. 

Once she's back safely, the bell is removed from Geralt's [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory").
