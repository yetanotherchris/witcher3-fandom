## Item ID

yennefers_omelette

**Omelet for Yennefer** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

If [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") is romantically involved with [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") during the main quest [Va Fail, Elaine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Va_Fail,_Elaine "Va Fail, Elaine"), she would ask him to prepare a meal for her and this is one of the possible options for it. She would specifically ask for scrambled eggs, or if you wish to spoil her, an omelet made with [onions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Onion "Onion"), [mushrooms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mushroom "Mushroom") and "peppers",[N 1] and naturally [eggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Egg "Egg"). As long as those 4 ingredients are used, this is the meal that Geralt would prepare and she would be pleased. 

The other three meal options are [scrambled eggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scrambled_eggs "Scrambled eggs"), a [rather unappetizing meal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rather_unappetizing_meal "Rather unappetizing meal"), and [omelet à la Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Omelet_%C3%A0_la_Geralt_of_Rivia "Omelet à la Geralt of Rivia"). 

All of this is still present in the game and can be enabled via this console command, used at the start of the quest: addfact(q401_cooking_enabled)

## Notes

  * In addition to the cut quest phase, there was also an entirely separate cooking system, presumably meant to create regular [food](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink") for Geralt. In it, there's a recipe for _"Yennefer's Omelette"_ requiring 3 eggs, 6 [olives](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olive "Olive"), onion and black pepper as ingredients.
