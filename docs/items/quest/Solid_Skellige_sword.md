## Item ID

mq2002_sword

**Solid Skellige sword** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be found in a crate during the secondary quest [Finders Keepers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Finders_Keepers_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Finders Keepers \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

Unlike other quest items, this one can't be kept and sold off to anyone else but to the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Svorlag\) "Merchant \(Svorlag\)") during the quest, who'll pay 55 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") as [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") hands it over automatically if you picked it up. 

If you forgot to get the sword, there's no way for you to retrieve it and return it, causing it to sit indefinitely in your quest [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") tab if you do so.
