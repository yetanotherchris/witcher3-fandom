## Item ID

sq302_philippa_key

**Key to Philippa Eilhart's hideout** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is a wall tile which, when inserted into the correct wall slot, removes the protective magical barrier blocking the way into the ruins of [Est Tayiar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Est_Tayiar "Est Tayiar"). It is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by one of the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") outside the ruins during [Redania's Most Wanted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania%27s_Most_Wanted "Redania's Most Wanted").
