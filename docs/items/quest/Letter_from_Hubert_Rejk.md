## Item ID

q308_psycho_farewell

**Letter from Hubert Rejk** is a letter and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in the [Novigrad Morgue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Morgue "Novigrad Morgue") after the [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins") secondary quest, only if [Hubert Rejk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk") wasn't successfully identified as the killer. 

## Journal entry

    _[Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"),_
    _Since you are reading this letter, you have finally guessed who the murderer was. Only after finding my subtle hints, undoubtedly, but still. I congratulate you - and at the same time inform you that you will never have the pleasure of meeting me again._
    _I long deluded myself into thinking I could wake the citizens of Novigrad from their slumber, make them aware of their corruption and point them on the right path - that which leads to the Eternal Fire and the cleansing it provides._
    _Yet even though - as you well know - I stopped at nothing in my efforts, it appears they will not bring about the desired results.[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s moral pox has spread too far and too deep - it is now incurable and fatal. Though many consider me a fanatic, even I realize further shock therapy of the kind I have been administering will do no good._
    _I will thus depart for another, smaller settlement, where the process of moral decay has just begun and where my intervention may yet stand a chance of stopping it. Forgive me for not giving you the details of my itinerary, but I fear you would try to hinder my mission._
    _My regards,  
Hubert Rejk, alias Concerned Citizen_
