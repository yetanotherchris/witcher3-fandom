**Key attached to a crumpled note** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found, along with [crumpled notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crumpled_notes#Crumpled_notes_\(Battlefield_Loot\) "Crumpled notes"), on a body at the hidden treasure marker south of the [Eternal Fire Chapel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Chapel "Eternal Fire Chapel") and is needed to unlock the reward chest for the quest [Battlefield Loot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_Loot "Battlefield Loot").
