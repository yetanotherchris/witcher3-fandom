## Item ID

lw_001_monstrous_remains

**Ancient monster bone** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [junk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the mini-peninsula southwest of the [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)") signpost on the winter [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") map, if one manages to clip out of the reduced map borders during the Empress version of the epilogue quest, [Something Ends, Something Begins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)"). The purpose of the item is unknown and there are no concrete clues to a quest that it may be even remotely related to. 

## Notes

  * The item appears with the standard junk item caption, but it seems to have an unused, custom description: _"[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") scholars are usually interested in such objects."_
