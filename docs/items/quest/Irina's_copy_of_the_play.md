## Item ID

q305_script_for_irina

**Irina's copy of the play** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is a placeholder copy of [The Doppler's Salvation](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Doppler%27s_Salvation "The Doppler's Salvation") / [A Changeling Rescued](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Changeling_Rescued "A Changeling Rescued") that is given to [Irina Renarde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Irina_Renarde "Irina Renarde") during [The Play's the Thing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Play%27s_the_Thing "The Play's the Thing"). However, unlike the script that Geralt himself has to read, this item isn't a readable book.
