I gave away everything I had, I did penance for all my sins, I compensated those I had wronged - and what? Nothing. I feel empty, bereft. Let devils take Lebioda and all his followers!_

## Item ID

q101_candle_instruction

These **Notes** are a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

They were originally intended to be found in [Hendrik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hendrik "Hendrik")'s hidden basement, in the chest that now only contains [crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), and provided instructions on a series of turns to be performed on the candlestick that opens the secret compartment. It was likely cut because the puzzle simply felt like a needless complication and annoyance. 

### Journal entry

    _Every wanderer must know the points of the compass. Every man who longs to reach a destination must first find the route. The fire can only be lit by facing north, then flicking your eyes first to the west, then to the east, and finally glancing westward twice more. May the gods watch over your journey's path..._

## Notes (Undvik)

There are two books named **Notes** cut from around [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

They are the 5th and 7th books respectively out of 7, collectively known as "journals from [Urskar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Urskar "Urskar")", detailing what led to Undvik's destruction. Small bits from that still remain in dialogues during [The Lord of Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Lord_of_Undvik "The Lord of Undvik") quest, but the overall story and a lot of other content related to the island have been removed from the game.
