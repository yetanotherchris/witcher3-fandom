**Cloth doll** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that [Phillip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") gives to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to give to his daughter, [Tamara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger"), during [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters") when Geralt figures out she's in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). Tamara later reveals the doll's name is Clara.
