**Silver-plated key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found just north of the [Boxholm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boxholm "Boxholm") signpost, on a body along with an [old, yellowed letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old,_yellowed_letter "Old, yellowed letter").
