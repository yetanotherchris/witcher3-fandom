## Item ID

**Hieronymus' notes** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in a chest at the [old signal tower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_signal_tower "Old signal tower") near [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), and directs [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to examine the [portal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portal "Portal") mechanism there, as well as points him to the [bastion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastion "Bastion") and [ruined watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_Watchtower "Ruined Watchtower") if the respective [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") diagrams in those places haven't been found yet. 

## Journal entry

    _My study of[Elgar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elgar "Elgar")'s armor has allowed me to devise new alloys and increase the [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") conductivity of the [second crystal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Polished_crystal#Old_signal_tower "Polished crystal"). This might be the breakthrough I needed in my work on the [teleport](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Teleportation "Teleportation"). For my trial run, I have calibrated the portal to exit in the cave near the tower's base. I need now only charge the crystal with solar energy, for which purpose I have placed it in the outer wall of the tower, near the scaffolding. Only hours remain before it is entirely charged!_
    _I have finished my work on a diagram which will allow us to replicate this armor and equip the entire[School of the Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") with it. [Varin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Varin "Varin"), the witchers' fencing instructor, has taken credit for this find, but it was I who, using logical deduction and only a slight dollop of magic assistance, uncovered the chamber where Elgar must have once resided. The space was hidden behind a primitive illusion, one needed merely look carefully to see through it. I found notes which indicate Elgar had worked out diagrams for improvements to his equipment which he then hid for safekeeping in various locations around the world. Careful study of his notes allowed me to write up instructions on how to find these upgrades. I went to the old watchtower and gave this research to Chird. As soon as he finishes preparing the [silver sword diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_silver_sword "Diagram: Wolven silver sword"), he will set out to gather all the upgrade diagrams. Varin has the finished [steel sword diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_steel_sword "Diagram: Wolven steel sword") and will begin forging blades based on it. I hope he will distribute them to the poor lads he trains - they at least deserve proper equipment after going through the horror he calls training._
    _By the gods - I think I see[fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire "Fire") on the horizon, coming from where the old watchtower stands! I can also hear some kind of commotion outside my tower._
    _The way down is blocked - what is going on?!_
