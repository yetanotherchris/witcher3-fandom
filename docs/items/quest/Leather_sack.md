## Item ID

q301_triss_parcel

**Leather sack** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the main quest [Pyres of Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pyres_of_Novigrad "Pyres of Novigrad"), after first encountering [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"), she'll eventually need this sack recovered from the canal nearby after it was dumped by [Kluhg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kluhg "Kluhg") to avoid being caught with it. The sack contains [magic incense](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_incense "Magic incense") used in the next part of the quest.
