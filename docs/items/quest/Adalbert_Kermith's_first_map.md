## Item ID

th1003_map_lynx_upgrade1a

**Adalbert Kermith's first map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be bought from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Blackbough\) "Merchant \(Blackbough\)") in [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough"). 

When read it'll give the areas to look for: 

  * All the standard [Feline](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") armor diagrams
  * Enhanced Feline [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_armor "Enhanced Feline armor"), [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_boots "Enhanced Feline boots"), and [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_silver_sword_-_enhanced "Feline silver sword - enhanced") diagrams

## Journal entry

    _While conducting studies under the guidance of Professor[Slavko Atimstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slavko_Atimstein "Slavko Atimstein"), I happened across four places where carved engravings portraying the sign of the [Cat School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Cat "School of the Cat") of [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") could be found. All these pictograms are identical to that which we discovered in the caverns beneath the [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). I have marked them on this map._
    _[Adalbert Kermith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Adalbert_Kermith "Adalbert Kermith"). Adjunct in [Natural History](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy_faculties "Oxenfurt Academy faculties") at the [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")._
