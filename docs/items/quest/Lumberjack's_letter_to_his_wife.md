## Item ID

mq2010_lumbermill_journal_2

**Lumberjack's letter to his wife** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in the far right hut at the [Abandoned Sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Sawmill_\(location\) "Abandoned Sawmill \(location\)") in [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") during the [secondary quest of the same name](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Sawmill_\(quest\) "Abandoned Sawmill \(quest\)"), though it is not necessary to progress the quest. 

## Journal entry

    _Don't know how long I'll stay here. Till now the work was tough, sure, but we earned a decent living. Lately, though, I fear for my life.[Something](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshen "Leshen") haunts the place at night. It roars and wails, we can't stand it no more. And the [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)"), they're always howling. Yesterday Seamund went to the privy during the night and never returned. Didn't find no body nor tracks. Who knows, maybe he ran off? I'd rather that than something worse to have happened. Truth be told, I think about leaving more and more lately, about going back to you and the young one. I'm no fool, maybe I could learn another trade and we'd get by somehow._
