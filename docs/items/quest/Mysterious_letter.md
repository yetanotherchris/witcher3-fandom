## Item ID

q103_letter_from_graden_2

This letter is found during [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters") if one learns where [Tamara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger") is and goes to [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") to talk with her. This is found on the table right below the windows. 

### Associated quest

  * [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters")

### Journal entry

    _All is prepared. We await only your arrival. When I told the commander of your commitment and zeal, he agreed to welcome you into our ranks at once. At first, you will be put through preliminary training, but you already know how to ride and wield a blade and thus have already mastered the essentials. The rest will not take much time. As soon as you don our colors, we will set out in search of your mother. And we will find her – I promise. Even if we have to comb every inch of[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")._

## Item ID

q302_roche_letter

This letter is found in the secret room in [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior "Whoreson Junior")'s [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_arena "Whoreson Junior's arena") during the main quest, [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"), firmly incriminating him as working against the [Big Four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four") criminals of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

### Associated quest

  * [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior")

### Journal entry

    _To Whoreson Junior_
    _King[Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") is unhappy with your performance. You were to rid of the other members of the so-called "Big Four," yet not only do they still live, but they also now know of your role in their attempted assassination. That was not our arrangement._
    _For the time being, hide in the place which the messenger delivering this missive will indicate to you and stay out of sight. His Majesty lives in the hope that mutual animosity between the other members of the Big Four will finish the job which you attempted, yet flubbed so badly._
    _M._
