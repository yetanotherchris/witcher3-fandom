**Crones' doll** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is used in a ritual to break the [curse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Curse "Curse") on [Anna Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anna_Strenger "Anna Strenger") placed by the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"). It only comes into play during [Return to Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Return_to_Crookback_Bog "Return to Crookback Bog") if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") spared [The Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree") during [The Whispering Hillock](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Whispering_Hillock "The Whispering Hillock").
