## Item ID

mh303_succubus_house_key

**Key to house in Novigrad** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be obtained one of two ways during [Contract: Deadly Delights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Deadly_Delights "Contract: Deadly Delights"): from the [madame](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Madame_\(Crippled_Kate%27s\) "Madame \(Crippled Kate's\)") if [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") is used, or by killing [Salma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Salma "Salma").
