## Item ID

mq1058_cat_medallion

**School of the Cat Medallion** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ for the DLC quest, [Where the Cat and Wolf Play...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Where_the_Cat_and_Wolf_Play... "Where the Cat and Wolf Play..."). 

After finding and talking to [Millie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Millie "Millie"), she'll give [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") this, revealing the [other witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gaetan "Gaetan") is from the [School of the Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Cat "School of the Cat").
