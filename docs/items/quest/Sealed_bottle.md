## Item ID

q105_johnnys_doll

**Sealed bottle** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that contains Johnny's "voice" so that he can't talk. He apparently lost it because he spoke badly of the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones") who then decided to punish him by taking his voice and bottling it up. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") must retrieve this to get [Johnny](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Johnny "Johnny") to talk again so he can tell Geralt what he knows about [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"). It can be obtained prior to actually finding Johnny, whereupon it'll sit in the quest items inventory until needed.
