## Item ID

**Key to Yennefer's room** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

After landing in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and talking to [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") during the main quest [Destination: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige"), she'll give this key to gain access to her room at the [The New Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port") to change clothes; somewhat unnecessarily as it only unlocks the direct front door, while the one from inside the inn is never locked and thus the room is always accessible. 

## Associated quest

  * [Destination: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige")

## Notes

  * Prior to [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), this was a Common tier item. It was likely changed due to the online "memes" denoting Yennefer's promiscuousness because of the rarity.
