## Item ID

q301_crematory_key

**Key to grate in sewers** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's only found during the main quest [Pyres of Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pyres_of_Novigrad "Pyres of Novigrad") after visiting [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold")' [former home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss%27_house_\(Novigrad\) "Triss' house \(Novigrad\)") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). To find it, enter the sewer entrance just below the [Glory Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Gate "Glory Gate") archway into the city (the farthest eastern archway under the bridge) and it'll be on the body near some [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"). It's used to unlock the nearby grate to take the back entrance into the [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove").
