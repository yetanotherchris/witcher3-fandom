## Item ID

mq0004_frying_pan

**Frying pan** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") helps the [old gran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Woman_\(White_Orchard\) "Old Woman \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") with retrieving her old soot-covered pan, he will find it, but now completely spotless. It turns out the [mysterious man](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thaler_\(spy\) "Thaler \(spy\)") that borrowed it wanted her pan for the soot to make ink. Geralt can then give the pan back to the old woman.
