**Blood-soaked military orders** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

These orders are found in a chest under a bridge west of the [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)"), though the [Temerian lily key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_lily_key "Temerian lily key") is needed to unlock the chest. 

## Journal entry

    _To Whom It May Concern, By Order of the Legion Commander:_
    _The men bearing this missive are to be escorted north, to the border post near[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), where they will find a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") division waiting for them. As noble-born [Temerians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria"), they are under special protection and not a hair on their heads is to be harmed. Everything they carry is their own private property and is to remain in their possession._
    _Any failure to heed this order will be punished in an accelerated military tribunal._
    _Randal Vittgenberg_
