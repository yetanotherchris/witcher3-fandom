## Item ID

sq201_werewolf_meat

**Werewolf meat** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted from [Morkvarg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg "Morkvarg")'s [werewolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") form remains, and serves as one of the ways to permanently kill him.
