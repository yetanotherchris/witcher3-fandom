## Item ID

mh301_merc_contract

**Mercenary contract** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on a body at [Opinicus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Opinicus "Opinicus")' nest and when read, can be used to get a better reward during [Contract: The Creature from Oxenfurt Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Creature_from_Oxenfurt_Forest "Contract: The Creature from Oxenfurt Forest"). However, this may have future implications during the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion quest [Open Sesame: The Safecracker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Open_Sesame:_The_Safecracker "Open Sesame: The Safecracker").

    _Dear Master[Hans of Cidaris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hans_of_Cidaris "Hans of Cidaris"),_
    _I have decided to accept your condition that the fee for the beast's demise be paid in advance. The reward you demand, however - six full purses of[crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") \- continues to seem quite excessive to me. The three I have included with this missive should be more than suffice[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] for you and your crew. Note that I expect a quick and final resolution to this [monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") problem. Should you determine that you are not up to the task, I expect you to return every last copper of the advance I enclose - under penalty of flogging and imprisonment - and to immediately leave the area._

    _Klaus van Trontke_
