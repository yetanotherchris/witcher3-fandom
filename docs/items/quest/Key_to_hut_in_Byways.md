**Key to hut in Byways** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Mystery_of_the_Byways_Murders "Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders"), if you choose to investigate further, [Bytomir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bytomir "Bytomir") will eventually give you this key to unlock the hut.
