# Jade figurine

In _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , there are two [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") cursed by [Coral](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coral "Coral"), turned into **jade figurines** via [artefact compression](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Artefact_compression "Artefact compression"). One of them is a noble-born [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") and the other is a [Nilfgaardian soldier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army").

This figurine has only a single opportunity to be obtained. During the quest [A Dangerous Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dangerous_Game "A Dangerous Game"), when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") is looking for the [Fringilla Vigo card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fringilla_Vigo_\(gwent_card\) "Fringilla Vigo \(gwent card\)") in a secret room at [Caesar Bilzen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen "Caesar Bilzen")'s [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen%27s_house "Caesar Bilzen's house"), the figurine can be found in a miniature chest atop the cabinet closest to the stairs.
