## Item ID

cg100_barons_notes

**The baron's notes** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , added by [Patch 1.20](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.20_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.20 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

If the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") isn't beaten at [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") before [Return to Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Return_to_Crookback_Bog "Return to Crookback Bog") is completed, then as long as the [Gwent: Velen Players](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent:_Velen_Players "Gwent: Velen Players") quest is active, this can be found in his office, providing directions to other players to look for. Attached to it is the Baron's unique card, [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra_\(gwent_card\) "Sigismund Dijkstra \(gwent card\)").
