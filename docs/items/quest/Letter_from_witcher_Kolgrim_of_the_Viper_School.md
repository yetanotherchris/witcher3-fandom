**Letter from witcher Kolgrim of the Viper School** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in the [White Orchard cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_cemetery "White Orchard cemetery"), underground in the crypts, alongside the [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Serpentine_silver_sword "Diagram: Serpentine silver sword") for the [Viper silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_silver_sword "Viper silver sword"). If not already found, it will direct [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to [Amavet Fortress Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amavet_Fortress_Ruins "Amavet Fortress Ruins") for the [Viper steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_steel_sword "Viper steel sword") [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Serpentine_steel_sword "Diagram: Serpentine steel sword"). 

## Journal entry

    _To:[Ivar Evil-Eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ivar_Evil-Eye "Ivar Evil-Eye"), master [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") of the [Viper School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Viper "School of the Viper")._
    _I have now found all the legendary diagrams once belonging to our order. Certain complications have arisen, however. On the way back to our keep, I stopped to rest the night in a village in the[Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") borderlands. The blasted peasants decided to make me into their scapegoat and accused me of kidnapping some [beekeeper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Paul "Paul")'s [brat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitty "Vitty"). They took me into the local [lordling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignatius_Verrieres "Ignatius Verrieres")'s [castle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amavet_Fortress_Ruins "Amavet Fortress Ruins") for interrogation, during which they found and confiscated one of the diagrams. But do not fear, I will get it back. They did not find the others._
    _I have invoked Temerian common law and demanded a[trial by ordeal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trial_by_ordeal "Trial by ordeal") instead of submitting to the whims of this baronet. My request was granted and tomorrow I am to cleanse the baronet's family crypt of [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"). I expect this will prove little trouble. Perhaps I will already have returned to our keep by the time you receive this letter. Light the furnances - we'll have some forging to do._
    _[Kolgrim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kolgrim "Kolgrim")_
