## Item ID

th1003_journal_lynx_part3

**Mage's notes** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found along with the [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Feline_crossbow "Diagram: Feline crossbow") for the [Feline crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_crossbow "Feline crossbow") in the wreck of the [Flying Stag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flying_Stag "Flying Stag"). 

Reading it provides directions to [Est Tayiar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Est_Tayiar "Est Tayiar") and the passages underneath [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") during [Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear"). 

### Journal entry

    _[Kiyan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kiyan "Kiyan") has enlisted with the Flying Stag as a guard of sorts. A hefty sack of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") [crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") convinced the captain to lodge me in his cabin. All is going according to plan._
    _I'm actually finding this expedition amusing. I'm glad I didn't contract the kidnapping out to some bulgy armed halfwit. I'll do it more gracefully myself._
    _He's cautious. I won't be able to put him down with a casually placed poison apple. Guess I'll have to zonk him out with a spell and whisk him away via teleport. I just hope it won't plop us down somewhere in the middle of[Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") instead of in my quarters below it. The power pulsating from my laboratory tends to throw off a good portion of my attempts to teleport home._
    _Very high level of resistance to[psionic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Psionic "Psionic") spells. Time needed for full penetration of his nervous system – a half an hour at least._
    _A few more moments and the[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") will be ready for transport. Now I just need to wipe our tracks. Perhaps a little change in the weather?_
    _While waiting for more favorable atmospheric conditions, I looked through his things. Some very intriguing documents. A map of[Est Tayiar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Est_Tayiar "Est Tayiar"), crafting diagrams of [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") provenance (margin note: highly interesting sketch for a one-handed crossbow, most likely used to hunt [draconids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Draconid "Draconid") or [ornithosaurs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ornithosaur "Ornithosaur"), must ask about this during interrogation. Confirms my theory that the witchers of the [Cat School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School "Cat School") use elven weapons.) and, wait for it.... [Professor Sigismund Gloger's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Professor_Sigismund_Gloger%27s_notes "Professor Sigismund Gloger's notes"). It's clear the witcher had something to do with the famous professor's disappearance._
    _Think I might have overdone it a bit with those cumulonimbi... No point in tarrying, time to evacuate._

This version of **Mage's notes** in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_quest_items "Blood and Wine quest items") found near the first campfire during [What Lies Unseen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/What_Lies_Unseen "What Lies Unseen") after ending up in the unusual caves of [Hen Gàidh](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hen_G%C3%A0idh "Hen Gàidh"). 

### Journal entry

    _Ten minutes after arrival -_
    _The teleportation worked: I managed to get into the caves with most of my equipment. Most importantly - the[megascope](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Megascope "Megascope") is in perfect condition, so I should be able to communicate with [Ban Ard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ban_Ard_Academy "Ban Ard Academy"), I just have to set it up in the right place._
    _\- Thirty minutes after arrival -_
    _I have already observed numerous gravitational anomalies, which suggests that[Sorel Degerlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorel_Degerlund "Sorel Degerlund") was right -- the boundary between spheres really is very thin here. More tests should allow me to determine whether a passage to our [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World") has really opened up._
    _\- One hour after arrival -_
    _I haven't met any[vampire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire")'s[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] yet, but I can hear their voices. I'm going to explore. I'll have to be careful._
