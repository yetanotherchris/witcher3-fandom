## Item ID

mq1060_key_to_pit

**Key to Devil's Pit** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It's needed to unlock the abandoned mine system at [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit") and given by the young deacon at the start of the quest. Once the quest completes, this is removed from the inventory and the mine system is re-locked, making the tunnels inaccessible once more.
