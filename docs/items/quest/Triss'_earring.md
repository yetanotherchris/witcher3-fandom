## Item ID

q401_triss_earring

**Triss' earring** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found at [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") and later given to [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"). 

To find it, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") must examine what remains of the bed [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") threw out the window - it is located north of the keep entrance. He then has the option to leave or take the earring. If given back to [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"), she will have the other half of the set and will start wearing them.
