## Item ID

lw_sk57_treasure_note

**Message** is a book and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and can be found on a body just outside a cave entrance southwest of [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken"). It can be found along with a [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scratched_key "Scratched key").
