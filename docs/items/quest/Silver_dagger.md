## Item ID

**Silver dagger** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Contract: The White Lady](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_White_Lady "Contract: The White Lady"), it's revealed this dagger was used by Luzi to commit suicide on her wedding day rather than be married off. [Helma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Helma "Helma") will then give the dagger to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to draw out [The White Lady](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_White_Lady "The White Lady").
