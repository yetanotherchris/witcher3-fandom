## Item ID

q301_drawing_crib

**Drawing of a crib** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found in the attic during the main quest, [Novigrad Dreaming](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Dreaming "Novigrad Dreaming"), with the strong suggestion of placing the [rag doll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rag_doll "Rag doll") in the crib downstairs.
