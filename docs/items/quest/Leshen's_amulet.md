## Item ID

sq204_leshy_talisman

**Leshen's amulet** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") quest item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

The item exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't appear during gameplay. While its specific purpose is unknown, it is obviously related to the [Woodland Spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Woodland_Spirit "Woodland Spirit").
