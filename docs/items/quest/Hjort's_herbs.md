## Item ID

q206_herb_mixture

**Hjort's herbs** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is the [psilocybe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Psilocybe "Psilocybe") (mardroeme), which [Hjort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjort "Hjort") extracts from the [horn with spiked mead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leftover_mead "Leftover mead") during the quest [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"), if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") chooses to help [Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite"). However, it doesn't do anything and will stay indefinitely in the "Quest items" [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") tab.
