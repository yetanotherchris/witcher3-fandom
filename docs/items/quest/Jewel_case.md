**Jewel case** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , added automatically to the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") for the "Witcher ending" version of the epilogue quest, [Something Ends, Something Begins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)"). 

It contains the gems that Geralt pays Master Ort with for [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")'s [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zireael_\(sword\)#Silver_sword "Zireael \(sword\)").
