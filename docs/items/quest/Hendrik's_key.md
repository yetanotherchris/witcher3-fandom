## Item ID

q101_hendrik_trapdoor_key

**Hendrik's key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the main quest [The Nilfgaardian Connection](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nilfgaardian_Connection "The Nilfgaardian Connection"), it is found in [Hendrik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hendrik "Hendrik")'s hut in [Heatherton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heatherton "Heatherton"), on his corpse (inside his boots), and is needed to unlock the cellar.
