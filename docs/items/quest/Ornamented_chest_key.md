## Item ID

mq3002_chest_key

**Ornamented chest key** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Hidden Messages of the Nilfgaardian Kind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hidden_Messages_of_the_Nilfgaardian_Kind "Hidden Messages of the Nilfgaardian Kind"), this key is found at the second drop location and is needed to unlock the chest in [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf")'s sewers.
