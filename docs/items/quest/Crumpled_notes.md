## Item ID

lw_de11_note

These notes are found along with a [key to treasure chest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Key_to_treasure_chest "Key to treasure chest"), in a cellar at the hidden treasure marker in [Mowshurst](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mowshurst "Mowshurst"), an abandoned village east of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch") and north of [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor"). 

### Journal entry

    _Soldiers have come. Whether ours or there's[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] \- don't know. Don't really matter anymore. I heard them riding down the road. Hid. Nailed the door shut. Maybe they won't get in._
    _They spent the whole night killing and raping. I saw it all through the cracks in the wall. Bit my finger to the bone trying to keep from screaming. Don't know if anyone else managed to hide. Maybe not. I saw them drag Antosh out. He had hid in the fields, next to the haystack where he'd stashed his things. They flailed him._
    _They've heard me. Know I'm in here. I didn't open up when they pounded. I thought they'd burn me out, just like they did those who hid in the granary. Then they got on their[horses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse"), rode off. Quiet, now. All I can hear's the flies circling the bodies._
    _I tried to knock open the door. Open the window. Dig myself out. Make a hole in the wall or roof. Nothing doing._
    _I heard people who came to rob corpses. Don't know of what, all's left is soiled shirts and foot wrappings. I called for help. They didn't answer. Only ones who'll come now are the[ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul")._

### Journal entry

    _I always said Musky was a clever pup! He understands more words than quite a few men. He can do all sorts of tricks - sit, roll over, and if you give him a scrap of bacon, he'll even shake your hand. But today, why, he's outdone himself today._
    _I look, and there's Musky, running in from the field carrying something in his mouth. I think, "Maybe it's a hare, and I'll be able to whip up a stew, sell the fur?" But no, it was a sack. All covered in blood, so no wonder he sniffed it out. I look inside... and inside there's a key. A little one, all engraved and ornamented, like it fits some fancy chest or box. So I think, "Maybe there's treasure hidden hereabouts, and that's what this key's for?" So I think I'll go for a walk, see what I can see. If I find it, I swear to[Melitele](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Melitele "Melitele"), Musky's going to eat nothing but veal for the rest of his days!_
    _That blood on the sack's got me a bit worried, though... But I'll take a thick club with me, so if anything pops out of the bushes, it'll get a thumping. You don't live but once, so you might as well take some risks, I always say!_
