## Item ID

**Arrest warrent for the witcher Gerd** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be found in two places: [Fort Etnir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fort_Etnir "Fort Etnir") beside a dead body, and in the [Ruined Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_Inn "Ruined Inn"). 

## Journal entry

    _Let it hereby be known that the[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") known as [Gerd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gerd_\(witcher\) "Gerd \(witcher\)") has committed foul crimes against the Ducal Tiara, namely: insulting Her Grace's majesty, resisting Her Grace's guards, collaborating with the [usurper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Usurper_\(Arcsea\) "Usurper \(Arcsea\)") and other such deeds bringing harm to Ducal Tiara._
    _Any persons providing him shelter will be considered complicit in his crimes. It is the duty of each and every one of the Ducal Tiara's subjects to provide any and all possible help to the agencies of the law who seek to capture the bandit. For the apprehension of Witcher Gerd, dead or alive, we have set a reward in the amount of 300[arcsea](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arcsea "Arcsea") [denars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Denar "Denar")..._
    _in the name of Her Grace[Helena Lange-Haare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Helena_Lange-Haare "Helena Lange-Haare"), heiress to the duchy of Arcsea, souzeraine of [Gelibol](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gelibol "Gelibol")  
[Hieronymus Ussar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hieronymus_Ussar "Hieronymus Ussar"), castellan_
