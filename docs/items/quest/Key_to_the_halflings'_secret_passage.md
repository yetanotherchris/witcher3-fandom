**Key to the halflings' secret passage** is a [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_keys "Category:The Witcher 3 keys") and a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. During the main quest [A Poet Under Pressure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Poet_Under_Pressure "A Poet Under Pressure"), after tracking [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") and the [witch hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") to the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut "Isolated Hut"), [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will receive this key from a [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") standing outside the hut with two [halflings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling"). It is used to unlock the trap door behind the hut.
