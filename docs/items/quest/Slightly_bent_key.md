## Item ID

**Slightly bent key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found along with an [unsent letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(Dowry\) "Unsent letter") on a woman's body at the hidden treasure marker north-northeast of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort"), beginning the treasure hunt quest [Dowry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dowry "Dowry"), and is needed to unlock the nearby related chest.
