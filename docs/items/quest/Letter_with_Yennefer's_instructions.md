## Item ID

ngu_gear_intro_letter

**Letter with Yennefer's instructions** is a document and quest item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It's given automatically to one's inventory to direct them on how to find the new armor sets added in with the update (which can all be found in [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") in a chest under a window). 

## Journal entry

    _My[Dear Friend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"),_
    _I had a feeling we wouldn't have a chance to talk before Vizima, so I leave you this message. I know there's much we both wish to say, but there will come a time for that later. In any case, a letter is no place to broach topics of such importance to us both._
    _So, why I write you now: you have something waiting for you at the[royal palace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Palace_\(Vizima\) "Royal Palace \(Vizima\)") in Vizima. Consider it a gift from [Emhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis") and myself. You can pick it up at your leisure after your audience with him. When you exit the throne room, simply follow the covered walkway in the courtyard, turning right at the corner, then take the first door on your left. You'll be inside a chamber with a fireplace. Take the door in the back right to the adjoining room. There, under the window, you'll find the chest with your gift. But in case these directions prove too complicated for you, I'm certain the [chamberlain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mererid "Mererid") would not hesitate to lead you there himself. Expert tracker that you are, however, I expect you'll manage._
    _Your friend, Yennefer_
