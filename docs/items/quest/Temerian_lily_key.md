**Temerian lily key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found on a body at the bridge west of the [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)") and is needed to unlock the nearby submerged chest.
