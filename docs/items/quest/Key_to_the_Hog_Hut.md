## Item ID

sq107_vault_key

**Key to the Hog Hut** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is given during the DLC secondary quest, [Fools' Gold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fools%27_Gold "Fools' Gold"), if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") sides with [Yontek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yontek "Yontek"). It is needed to open the "Hog Hut" near [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch"), a long forgotten temple with [pig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pig "Pig") statues inside.
