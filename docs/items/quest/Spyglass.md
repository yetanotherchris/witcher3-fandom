## Item ID

mq1051_spyglass

**Spyglass** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't appear during gameplay and there are no visible clues about its purpose. However, it was obviously intended to be found during [Contract: Patrol Gone Missing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Patrol_Gone_Missing "Contract: Patrol Gone Missing").
