## Item ID

q310_cell_key

**Cell key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is obtained by killing the [Prison Warden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prison_Warden "Prison Warden") during the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests"), [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape"), along with a [witch hunter's sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter%27s_sword "Witch hunter's sword") and [dimeritium shackles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dimeritium_shackles "Dimeritium shackles"). 

It is needed to open the door to [Margarita Laux-Antille](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margarita_Laux-Antille "Margarita Laux-Antille")'s cell, but may open all the other prison cells too. 

## Associated quest

  * [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape")

## Notes

  * This key was at some point supposed to be found in/on an "officer's desk", possibly as part of some [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") replicating the stealthy navigation from [The Dungeons of the La Valettes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Dungeons_of_the_La_Valettes "The Dungeons of the La Valettes") in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_.
