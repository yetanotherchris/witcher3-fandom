## Item ID

th1003_journal_cat_lady

**Julia Kenyon's diary** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found inside one of the houses west of [Portside Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portside_Gate "Portside Gate") in [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners"). Reading it can start the quest [Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear") and will provide directions to the passages underneath [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle"). 

## Journal entry

    _We sold the artifacts we acquired during our last expedition at the Hraki Bazaar in[Cidaris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cidaris "Cidaris"). Made only a modest profit, but a profit all the same._
    _Dicker has came across information about a purported[elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") treasure hoard said to be hidden in the [sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_sewers "Novigrad sewers") beneath Temple Isle. To my thinking these rumors might very well be true. After all [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") cities, [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") included, were built on elven foundations. So why should we not search for elven treasure beneath its streets? If but a tenth of what the tales say is true, this hoard contains enough riches to last us to the end of our days._
    _Today is the first of[Feainn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feainn "Feainn"). We are setting out. Our party consists of Rian, Dicker, Maxi, Miko and me._
    _The entrance to the underground passages was protected by an illusion. Luckily Miko was able to dispel it. There truly must be something of value down here. We shall descend deeper._
    _This does not look like a treasury. It is rather some sort of hospital. Or a laboratory._
    _[after this point the entries are chaotic, with many blots and incomplete words] A massacre, I... Only I... Could do nothing - the others... Dead, all! The elves, that was their laboratory, and... What we saw... It... He..._
    _I cannot sleep for the umpteenth night in a row. I see them all whenever I close my eyes - Rian, Dicker, Maxi, Miko... This insomnia is killing me. And that symbol... That cat... It stares at me..._
    _Here kitty kitty kitty!_
    _Meow._
