During the Empress ending variant of [Something Ends, Something Begins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)"), four of these have to be looted after throwing a bomb into the ice hole.
