## Item ID

lw_gr34_treasure_key

**Treasure key** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , needed to unlock the treasure chest for [The Things Men Do For Coin...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Things_Men_Do_For_Coin... "The Things Men Do For Coin...")

It's found along with a [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_\(The_Witcher_3\)#Letter_\(Pontar_battlefield\) "Letter \(The Witcher 3\)") on a [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf")'s body at the hidden treasure marker west of the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post") and south from [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten").
