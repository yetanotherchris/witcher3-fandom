**Damp, moldy notes by Hieronymus on the witcher Elgar** is a document and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be purchased from [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde")'s [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Kaer_Trolde_keep\) "Armorer \(Kaer Trolde keep\)") along with several other maps. 

When read it'll give areas to look for: 

  * all the standard [Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") armor diagrams
  * the [superior steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_steel_sword_-_superior "Wolven steel sword - superior"), [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_silver_sword_-_superior "Wolven silver sword - superior"), and armor diagrams

## Journal entry

    _[Elgar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elgar "Elgar") hid further improvements to the equipment used by the [School of the Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") in various regions of the [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") Isles. He put some in a [wooden fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fort_Grymmdjarr "Fort Grymmdjarr") in southern [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). Another set he hid in the ruins in the western portion of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"). A third stash was hidden in the burial mounds found in western Ard Skellig._
