**Elven-made key** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found in the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), on a body impaled by spike traps, at the hidden treasure marker next to the room with the [venomous arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Venomous_arachas "Venomous arachas"), after the crypt has been opened during [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone") quest. It opens the locked chest on the other side of the traps. 

It is technically tied to the quest, but [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can come back to loot after it has already been completed.
