## Item ID

mq2043_conviction_token

**Path of Warriors completion token** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is needed to complete the cave path trial during [The Path of Warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Path_of_Warriors "The Path of Warriors") and is found in the second underwater tunnel, in a chest.
