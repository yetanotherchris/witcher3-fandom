## Item ID

mq4005_note_1

**Berengar's notes** is a document and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in a chest near the western wall of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), to the west of the [grindstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grindstone "Grindstone") in the second courtyard, and upon obtaining, will begin the [Berengar's Blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berengar%27s_Blade "Berengar's Blade") quest. 

## Journal entry

    _Damn hunk of metal. A week sweating with bellows and forge and, what do you know, there's[Tor'haerne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tor%27haerne "Tor'haerne"), shining in my hands... and then there it goes, cracking into shards with the first practice strike. Must've botched something in the alloy composition or cooled it too fast after forging. Hard to say. Just gotta try again, I guess, but not here, because [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir")'s already badgering me with his blasted words of wisdom. Should've known asking his advice was opening a bottomless can of worms... Don't know how [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") puts up with that without going the rest of the way crazy._
