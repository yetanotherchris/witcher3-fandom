**Sunstone** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , retrieved in [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart")'s expedition through the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt"), during the aptly named main quest [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone"). 

It is an artifact that can be used to summon the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt"). According to [Avallac'h](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Avallac%27h "Avallac'h"): 

    _"Gealbhan was a[Sage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Saevherne "Aen Saevherne") of the [Alder Elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Elle "Aen Elle"). He fell in love with an [Aen Seidhe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Seidhe "Aen Seidhe") [elf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") \- Dillian. On the day the White Fleet was to set sail, Gealbhan gave Dillian a gift - the Sunstone. Yet she spurned him. He believed Dillian's heart would soften in time. Once her longing for him burgeoned, she was to use the Sunstone. Though worlds away, he would return for her. [..] She never used the stone, and Gealbhan died of sorrow."_

After it is obtained, the Sunstone is given to Avallac'h, who is later seen using it together with [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") in the opening cutscene for the main quest [On Thin Ice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/On_Thin_Ice "On Thin Ice").
