## Item ID

q309_glejt_from_dijkstra

**Eternal Fire letter of safe conduct** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. After completing [A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)"), the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") crack down on finding [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") in the city and thus those passing through require this to pass through into [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") undisturbed. 

This is given automatically to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") when he goes near a city entrance, thanks to [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"). 

## Journal entry

    _To whom it may concern,_
    _Let it be known that the bearer of the present document may enter and leave the Free City of Novigrad at will, both during the day and at night, and shall for no reason be perturbed, harmed, harassed or molested in any way._
    _His Holiness,_
    _[Hierarch Hemmelfart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyrus_Engelkind_Hemmelfart "Cyrus Engelkind Hemmelfart")_
