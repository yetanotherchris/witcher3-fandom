## Item ID

q308_coroner_msg

**Message from Hubert Rejk** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but whether due to a bug or intentionally, doesn't appear during gameplay. 

During the [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins") quest, this message is effectively delivered in spoken form to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by a [guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard"), which suggests that this item was simply [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content"). However, with multiple other documents during this quest missing for ambiguous reasons, it's possible that it is only not appearing because of a bug.
