## Item ID

th1009_journal_wolf_part4

**Notes written in a shaky hand** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found in the cave below the [old signal tower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_signal_tower "Old signal tower"), along with all the standard [wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") armor diagrams. 

## Journal entry

    _Cannot move. Spine broken, liver (perhaps spleen?) pierced. Knew jumping into[portal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portal "Portal") was extremely risky with only 1 [crystal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Polished_crystal "Polished crystal") powering it - but still better than certain death at hands_[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")]_of enraged peasants. Mob broke into tower, cut off path to second crystal. Had to flee. Grabbed diagrams for[Wolf School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") gear before going - hoping [Varin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Varin "Varin") would come save them, if not me. Perhaps he will come still, will jump through the portal, retrieve his diagrams, rescue me... A foolish hope. My time is very short._
