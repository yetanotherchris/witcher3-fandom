## Item ID

lw_sk3_ship_note

**Cargo list** is a book and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's found on a corpse at the hidden treasure marker on the southeastern shore of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig").
