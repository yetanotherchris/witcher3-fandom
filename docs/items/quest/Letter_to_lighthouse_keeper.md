## Item ID

mh207_lighthouse_keeper_letter

**Letter to lighthouse keeper** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Phantom_of_Eldberg "Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg"), this can be found inside [Eldberg Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eldberg_Lighthouse "Eldberg Lighthouse") after getting the [lighthouse key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lighthouse_key "Lighthouse key") from [Mikkjal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikkjal "Mikkjal"). However, note that Geralt must unlock the door before returning to Mikkjal, or he'll never be able to enter and find the letter. Reading it will lead to some additional dialogue with the lighthouse keeper.
