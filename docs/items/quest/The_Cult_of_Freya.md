## Item ID

mq2049_book_4

**The Cult of Freya** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be bought from the [alchemist preacher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_Priest_\(Electors%27_Square\) "Eternal Fire Priest \(Electors' Square\)") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Electors' Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Electors%27_Square "Electors' Square") and is one of five books needed for the secondary quest [Free Spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Free_Spirit "Free Spirit"). 

## Journal entry

    _[Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") Modron, the Great Mother, teaches us to put our faith in her no matter what life brings. Thus warriors pray to her before setting off for battle, and the women they leave behind pray for their menfolk's safe return. Freya, as a mother and a goddess, understands all the trials of mankind, she sooths our pains and provides comfort. Yet woe to him who acts against her and violates her eternal laws, handed down for the good of men and the world. Condemnation awaits such men – they will be cut off from the life-giving source of motherly love, and if they repair not their ways and, renouncing evil, return to the mother-goddess' bosom, they shall be cursed for all eternity – they and all their line (...)_
