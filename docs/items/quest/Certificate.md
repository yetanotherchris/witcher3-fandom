## Item ID

q302_zdenek_contract

**Certificate** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the main quest [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"), as one nears [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior "Whoreson Junior")'s [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_arena "Whoreson Junior's arena"), they can come across some bandits that kill a man, [Zdenek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zdenek "Zdenek"), before running off. This can then be looted off of his body and used to impersonate Zdenek and enter the arena. 

## Journal entry

    _Let it hereby be known that the Bearer of this document is entitled to fight in the ring under the name of Zdenek._
