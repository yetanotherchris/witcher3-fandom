## Item ID

th1009_journal_wolf_part2a

**Varin's journal** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on a skeleton at the [Bastion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastion "Bastion") west of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), along with the [Wolven steel sword diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_steel_sword "Diagram: Wolven steel sword"). 

## Journal entry

    _[Elgar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elgar "Elgar")'s gear is fantastic. I knew it at once, soon as I found it. Right away I said every new [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") we train should be given such equipment - that is, if we are serious about making monster slayers out of this band of prissy schoolboys._
    _How many times must I ask our honorable resident[mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") to devote some of his precious time to actual work! Resident like that's as useful as a boil on the bottom. At least his assistant, Chird, knows a thing or two about metallurgy. The two of them have finally gotten down to work._
    _The steel sword diagram is now ready, and, assuming they didn't foul anything up, tomorrow I'll forge the first blade. I'll got to Chird's workshop in the[old watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_Watchtower "Ruined Watchtower") after today's training and see if he's finished the [silver sword diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_silver_sword "Diagram: Wolven silver sword") as he promised. Then I'll go to [Hieronymus' tower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_signal_tower "Old signal tower") \- he should have finished that armor diagram by now._
    _Thought I heard a signal horn in the distance a second ago. Hooch must be getting to my head. No one's fool enough to attack a keep full of witchers._
