## Item ID

q303_wine_bottle

**Bottle of wine** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. During the main quest, [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure"), one finds this while exploring [Henckel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henckel "Henckel")'s [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margrave_Henckel%27s_house "Margrave Henckel's house") beside a [note](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mysterious_note_\(The_Witcher_3\)#Novigrad "Mysterious note \(The Witcher 3\)") telling [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") to put it back after tasting it. This is an obvious hint to place it on the correct wine rack, which will then show a hidden room to continue the quest. 

## Associated quest

  * [Count Reuven's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Reuven%27s_Treasure "Count Reuven's Treasure")
