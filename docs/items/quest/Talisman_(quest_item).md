**Talisman** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was created by the [Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") for [Anna Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anna_Strenger "Anna Strenger"), wife of [Philip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") (the Bloody Baron), to protect her from "[evil magics](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones")". 

It's found during [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters") by following the smell of wine from a broken bottle to one of the landings on the stairs where the talisman is hidden beneath a floor board. After the quest completes, it can be sold or dismantled.
