## Journal entry

    _As per the instructions of our supervisor, Ireneus var Steingard, we have conducted a series of studies on the genotype of the creatures commonly known as[drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"). Sadly, administering a poison made of fugu fish induced a coma in all the specimens in the study. After 24 hours the vital functions of all above-mentioned specimens ceased, prohibiting us from continuing the study._
    _Matteo Sykula_
