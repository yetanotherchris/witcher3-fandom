**Hornwall horn** is an item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It may be placed in one of the pocket slots in the inventory panel, and activated at will to cause nearby [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") to drop to the ground. 

The horn was stolen from [Hjalmar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar "Hjalmar") by one of his crew. It can be found near a warrior's remains, north of the [Abandoned Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Village_\(Undvik\) "Abandoned Village \(Undvik\)") on [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"), near the entrance to the trolls' cave. 

Ironically the item cannot be used while steering a boat, while a [Crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") can. Thus it's not very effective at warding off sirens attacks while at sea.
