# Squirrel tail

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Squirrel puff](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/ac/Items_Squirrel_tail.png/revision/latest?cb=20081201214920)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/ac/Items_Squirrel_tail.png/revision/latest?cb=20081201214920 "Squirrel puff")

_Token of the Scoia'tael_

**Squirrel puffs** , or tails, are skinnable items, of a sort except these come from _people_. The tails are used as a form of symbol and decorate the helmets of members of the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael"). They, like all other [additional goods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Additional_goods "Category:Additional goods"), have no alchemical value whatsoever. They can not be sold or traded, as it seems. 

### Associated quest

  * [The Flame that Cleanses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Flame_that_Cleanses "The Flame that Cleanses")

### Location

Wherever one finds dead [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael"): 

  * [Outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts "Outskirts")
  * [Swamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp "Swamp")
  * [Vizima cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima_cemetery "Vizima cemetery")
  * [Old Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Vizima "Old Vizima"), the best and "richest" source.

This version is a [junk item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") and was likely intended to be more common, but after large portions of [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content"), only one known source of these remains - the [Temerian Guerillas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Army "Temerian Army") who help in [Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s assassination during [Reason of State](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reason_of_State "Reason of State"). Some of them will inevitably die and squirrel tails can appear as random loot on their remains. Theoretically, other corpses classified as [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") should be able to have the item as random loot, but it is unknown if such are even placed anywhere. 

Squirrel tails should also be sold by a [quartermaster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quartermaster_\(Temerian_Partisan_Hideout\) "Quartermaster \(Temerian Partisan Hideout\)") at the [Temerian Partisan Hideout](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Partisan_Hideout "Temerian Partisan Hideout"), who however doesn't appear during gameplay due to a bug.
