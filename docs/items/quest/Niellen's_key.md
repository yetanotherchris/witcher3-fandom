## Item ID

sq104_key

**Niellen's key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can only be acquired if [Niellen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Niellen "Niellen") is killed during the secondary quest [Wild at Heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_at_Heart "Wild at Heart"), from his body, and can then be used to open a chest in the [Isolated Shack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Shack "Isolated Shack").
