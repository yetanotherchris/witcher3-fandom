## Item ID

th1003_ireneus_lab_key

**Laboratory key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's obtained from the cave beneath [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") and is used to open a nearby door to the laboratory where [Kiyan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kiyan "Kiyan") is found. To obtain the key, a puzzle involving statues and levers must be completed to reveal a pool with [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") in which it is found.
