## Item ID

th1007_journal_bear_part3

**The Ballad of Witcher Gerd** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found in the chest by the old throne in [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach") along with [Chronicles of Clan Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chronicles_of_Clan_Tuirseach "Chronicles of Clan Tuirseach") and the 4 standard [Ursine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") armor diagrams. 

## Journal entry

    _Righteous and brave, of death never scared,  
Such a man had we in the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Gerd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gerd_\(witcher\) "Gerd \(witcher\)").  
Pursued by foul foes with intentions most vile,  
He made a last stand on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig")'s fair isle.  
Fighting, all the while._
    _He arrived on her shores 'midst sobs and bawls,  
"Help! A [dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon "Dragon") yon [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") ruins crawls!"  
Hefting his sword in an expert grasp,  
He set off at once and slew the winged asp.  
Smiling, as it drew its last gasp._
    _But fierce as[fiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend") his foes followed his path,  
Hounding his footsteps, hearts brim full of wrath.  
At night they attacked, using darkness as shield,  
To no avail – soon their own lives they did yield.  
By witcher's hand was much blood spilled._
    '_Tis a witcher's right nature to guard men from beasts,  
And so Gerd's [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") did not rust in its sheath.  
Instead, to [Clan Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tuirseach "Clan Tuirseach") from [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") defend  
He forthwith to the Caverns of [Melusine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Melusine "Melusine") did mend  
And took the first step towards his end._
    _[fragment missing]  
Jarl [Torgeir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torgeir_the_Red "Torgeir the Red") returned and exclaimed with pride:  
Fortunate he who has friends at his side.  
Proud is the jarl who can quit his home keep  
And find in his absence no dangers did creep  
And witcher saved all from death's deadly sleep.
