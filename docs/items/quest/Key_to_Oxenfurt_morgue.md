## Item ID

mh304_morge_door_key

**Key to Oxenfurt morgue** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Oxenfurt_Drunk "Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk"), it's given by the [medic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Medic_\(Oxenfurt_Morgue\) "Medic \(Oxenfurt Morgue\)") so one can enter the impromptu morgue to examine the victims' bodies.
