## Item ID

**Brokvar** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , found in the cellar of [Udalryk's old family home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Udalryk%27s_old_family_home "Udalryk's old family home") during [Possession](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Possession_\(quest\) "Possession \(quest\)"). 

It is the ancestral family sword of [Clan Brokvar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Brokvar "Clan Brokvar"), and originally the source of conflict between [Udalryk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Udalryk "Udalryk") and his younger brother [Aki](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aki "Aki").
