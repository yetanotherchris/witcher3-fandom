## Item ID

mh307_minion_lair_key

**Cellar key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's given by one of [Holofernes Meiersdorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holofernes_Meiersdorf "Holofernes Meiersdorf")'s sons and is needed to open the cellar of the half-finished house near [Honeyfill Meadworks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks").
