## Item ID

**Small chest key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found, along with [itinerant merchant's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Itinerant_merchant%27s_notes "Itinerant merchant's notes"), on the dead merchant's body on the road southeast of [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare").
