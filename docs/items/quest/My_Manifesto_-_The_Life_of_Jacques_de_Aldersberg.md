**My Manifesto - The Life of Jacques de Aldersberg** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 

During the secondary quest [Message from an Old Friend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Message_from_an_Old_Friend "Message from an Old Friend"), this is found just before the third floor stairs in [Marcus T.K. Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson")'s [bookstore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Books_and_Scrolls "Books and Scrolls"). When picked up, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will automatically recover a [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_%22A%22 "Letter from "A"") inside addressed to him. 

Another copy can be found in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, at the [Tourney Grounds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tourney_Grounds "Tourney Grounds") in [Tailles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tailles "Tailles")' tent. However, this copy is only placed for theming purposes as Tailles represents the [Order of the Flaming Rose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Order_of_the_Flaming_Rose "Order of the Flaming Rose") at the knights tournament,[1] and it doesn't update a quest despite glowing in red like a quest item. 

## Journal entry

    _The reasons for choosing[Jacques de Aldersberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jacques_de_Aldersberg "Jacques de Aldersberg") as [Grandmaster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster "Grandmaster") remain a mystery. The [Order of the White Rose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Order_of_the_White_Rose "Order of the White Rose") had gone through a crisis in those times and was on the verge of collapse, so one might guess that the brethren wished to have someone decisive as their leaders,_[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")]_someone with a clear vision. De[Aldersberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aldersberg "Aldersberg") was precisely such a man. One of his first decisions was to change the brotherhood's name to the [Order of the Flaming Rose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Order_of_the_Flaming_Rose "Order of the Flaming Rose")._
    _The most puzzling aspect, however, is that the order and the[king](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest") himself decided to trust a man who, for all intents and purposes, had appeared out of nowhere. They say he was a wanderer, an itinerant priest who moved crowds with his speeches declaiming [nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman"). They say he worked miracles and showed his flock visions of a world destroyed by the [White Frost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Frost "White Frost"). He was undoubtedly a man of great charisma, one instilled with unshakeable principles which he in turn tried to instill in others._
    _Was he truly a[Source](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Source "Source")? Was he indeed gifted with raw [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") talent? That we will never know for certain._
