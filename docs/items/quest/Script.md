**Script** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , automatically given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion"), when they are in front of Sophronia's house during the secondary quest [Cabaret](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cabaret "Cabaret").
