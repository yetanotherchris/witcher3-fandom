## Item ID

**Magic trinket from Yennefer** a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") during [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape"), to be used when he finds [Margarita Laux-Antille](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margarita_Laux-Antille "Margarita Laux-Antille").
