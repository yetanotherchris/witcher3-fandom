**Key to Whoreson Junior's estate** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

If one decides to fight their way into [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior "Whoreson Junior")'s [mansion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_mansion "Whoreson Junior's mansion") during the main quest [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"), this must be looted off a henchman's body to open the door into the courtyard.
