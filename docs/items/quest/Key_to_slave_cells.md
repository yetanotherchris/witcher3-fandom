## Item ID

mq2020_slave_cells_key

**Key to slave cells** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and unlocks the slave cell in [Trottheim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trottheim "Trottheim") during the [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests"), [Flesh for Sale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flesh_for_Sale "Flesh for Sale"). 

It can either be given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by the slave driver (if dealt with peacefully) or looted off his body.
