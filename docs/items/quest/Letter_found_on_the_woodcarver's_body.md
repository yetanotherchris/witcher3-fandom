## Item ID

q308_priscilla_invite

**Letter found on the woodcarver's body** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

This is the letter made of [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") skin, which Eustace the corpse collector found on [Fabian Meyer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fabian_Meyer "Fabian Meyer")'s corpse. It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but whether due to a bug or intentionally, doesn't appear during gameplay.
