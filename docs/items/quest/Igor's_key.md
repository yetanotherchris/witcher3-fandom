## Item ID

q302_ring_door_key

**Igor's key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the main quest [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"), one can loot this off of [Igor the Hook](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igor_the_Hook "Igor the Hook")'s body, either after fighting in the [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_arena "Whoreson Junior's arena") or attacking the place with [Cleaver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese")'s men. This is then used to open the small box next to Igor's chair to find a [message from Igor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Message_from_Igor "Message from Igor"), which notes of a secret area in the arena.
