## Item ID

Voorhis mask

**General Voorhis mask** is a [mask](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_masks "The Witcher 3 masks") and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is the lion mask worn by [Morvran Voorhis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvran_Voorhis "Morvran Voorhis") at the ball at the [Vegelbud Residence.](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence")
