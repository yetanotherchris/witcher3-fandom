## Item ID

**Lizard figurine** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is needed to activate the first portal in the secondary quest [Of Dairy and Darkness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Dairy_and_Darkness "Of Dairy and Darkness"). 

It can only be bought from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Gildorf\) "Merchant \(Gildorf\)") in the [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad").
