## Item ID

mh103_girls_journal

**Letter found in a grave** is a document and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found during [Contract: Jenny o' the Woods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Jenny_o%27_the_Woods "Contract: Jenny o' the Woods"), in a shallow grave along the road west of [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"), not far from the [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp"). [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") burns the note to progress through the quest.
