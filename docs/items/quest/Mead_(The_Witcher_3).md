## Item ID

q201_mead

**Mead** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is found in [Ermion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ermion "Ermion")'s lab during the main quest, [The King is Dead – Long Live the King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_King_is_Dead_%E2%80%93_Long_Live_the_King "The King is Dead – Long Live the King"), where at a certain point an item must be placed in a statue's hand to open a hidden area. This is the correct item, while a [skull](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skull "Skull") and a pine [cone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cone "Cone") nearby are incorrect.
