## Item ID

q105_witch_bones

**Woman's remains** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

The bones of [The Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree")'s body before they were killed, these remains can be obtained during the main quest [The Whispering Hillock](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Whispering_Hillock "The Whispering Hillock"), if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") chooses to help the spirit. For the ritual, she requires this, along with a [raven's feather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Raven%27s_feather "Raven's feather") and a wild, black [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") to release her and, when retrieved, the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") will comment how the remains don't appear [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human").
