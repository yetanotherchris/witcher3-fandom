## Item ID

mq3026_varese_invitation

**Message from Carlo Varese** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It will be delivered to Geralt after he wins the horse races agains all three opponents at the Vegelbud Estate.
