## Item ID

mq1019_oil

**Holy oil** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is a flammable oil given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by an [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") priest at the start of [Funeral Pyres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Funeral_Pyres "Funeral Pyres") to use on piles of corpses before incinerating them. Once Geralt uses the oil on the third and final body pile, the item is removed from his inventory.
