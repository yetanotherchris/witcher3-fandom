## Item ID

q305_dandelion_signet

**Pegasus signet** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the main quest [A Poet Under Pressure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Poet_Under_Pressure "A Poet Under Pressure"), when you try to ambush the convoy and [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") is taken away on horseback, he'll drop his ring near the small lake with [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"). You can find it near the shore on a small rock. It can later be returned to Dandelion or sold.
