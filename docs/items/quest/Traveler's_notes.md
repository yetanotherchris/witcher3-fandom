## Item ID

sk48_splintered_ships_note

These **Traveler's notes** can be found at the hidden treasure marker southeast of [Tor Gvalch'ca](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tor_Gvalch%27ca "Tor Gvalch'ca"), on a corpse in a shipwreck, shortly before the entry point for the [Isle of Mists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isle_of_Mists "Isle of Mists"). They are necessary to start the [Precious Haul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Precious_Haul "Precious Haul") treasure hunt quest. 

### Journal entry

    _I swear, not in my whole life have I ever seen such waves. Ten, twelve, fourteen feet! And the wind - why, it shook the mast and battered the sails so they were near about to fly off. Rocks everywhere, currents treacherous... Helmsman said we'd pull through, that he'd seen worse and come out all right. Me, I'm not so sure. If the storm don't calm soon, this tub's going to break in two._

## Item ID

q311_lost_diary4

These **Traveler's notes** can be found while navigating the [Ice Plains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_Plains "Ice Plains") during the main quest [Through Time and Space](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Through_Time_and_Space "Through Time and Space"). 

### Journal entry

    _The worst part? The all-encompassing quiet. It seems there is not a living soul around - only snow, snow, everywhere snow, as far as the eye can see. Though sometimes we find people still alive in their homes. Everyone is hungry, everyone is frozen to the bone, but no one refuses help. They let us warm ourselves by their fire and regain some strength before we move on, towards the lighthouse. They say food supplies there have not yet run out._
