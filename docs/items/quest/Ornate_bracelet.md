## Item ID

mq0003_ornate_bracelet

**Ornate bracelet** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found at the bottom of the well in the [Abandoned Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Village_\(White_Orchard\) "Abandoned Village \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and has an inscription on the inner side that reads: _"To[Claer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claer "Claer") \- from [Volker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Volker "Volker")."_.
