## Item ID

mq1060_armor_osmund_note

**Osmund's notes** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

After completing the secondary quest [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow"), it can be found in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") keep on a bookshelf on the upper platform to the left of the main doors, along with [Vesemir's note](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir%27s_note "Vesemir's note"). 

## Journal entry

    _A breakthrough! Oh how I've long awaited this day._
    _The experimental armor[Reinald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reinald "Reinald") is working on has proved undeniably more durable than the [wolf school](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") standard. We initially thought it impossible to improve the design without adding unreasonable weight, but recent trials have demonstrated we only lacked the right materials. I'm traveling to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") soon to meet with him. We have much to discuss and new modifications to test. All the details are scribbled below. When I return, I'll add Reinald's diagrams to my own ideas here for the final draft._
