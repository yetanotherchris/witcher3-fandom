## Item ID

th1003_map_lynx_upgrade1b

**Adalbert Kermith's second map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be bought from [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough")'s [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Blackbough\) "Blacksmith \(Blackbough\)"). 

When read it'll give the areas to look for: 

  * All the standard [Feline](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") armor diagrams
  * Enhanced Feline [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_gauntlets "Enhanced Feline gauntlets"), [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_trousers "Enhanced Feline trousers"), and [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_steel_sword_-_enhanced "Feline steel sword - enhanced") diagrams
