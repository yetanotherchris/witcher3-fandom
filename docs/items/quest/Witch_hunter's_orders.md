## Item ID

q309_witch_hunters_orders

**Witch hunter's orders** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

Similarly to a [crumpled letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crumpled_letter#Crumpled_letter_\(Now_or_Never\) "Crumpled letter"), it is supposed to appear in the [sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_sewers "Novigrad sewers") during the quest [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), on one of the two [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter")' corpses seen shortly before encountering the [katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan"). However, whether due to a bug or intentionally, only one of the corpses can be interacted with, to be examined by [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"). 

Geralt notes that the corpses are fresh and those two witch hunters were actually just killed by the katakan, after they unlocked the grate door that prevents Geralt from exploring the sewers before the quest. That is exquisite timing for gameplay purposes, but may also suggest that someone has betrayed the [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") and told the witch hunters about their escape route.
