## Item ID

lw_sb13_smugglers_note

**Smuggler's notes** is a book and a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be looted from a corpse next to a boat along the coast west of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley"). 

## Journal entry

    _Those were the days... You'd smuggle a few bottles of hooch into[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), then loiter about the dormitories selling it to students. You'd come away with a hefty sack of coin, and they'd have their spirits fortified for study. Then along came [Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"), who shut down the [university](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"), chased the students away and positioned [ballistae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ballista "Ballista") on the riverbank to sink everything floating up or down the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)"), swans included._
    _But there's not been a dark cloud yet that didn't have a silver lining. Yesterday I saw our brave soldiers sink a merchant vessel. Went straight to the bottom, it did, along with its whole crew... and its cargo. A shame about the men, really, but it would just as big a shame to let all those goods go to waste. So me and Martin will fish them out, dry them off and sell them – and maybe at last I'll have something to put in my pot._
