# Concerned Citizen's sermon

There are five **concerned Citizen's sermons** found all around the city of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). All are part of the [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins") secondary quest, but despite being [quest items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") they aren't required for anything.

## Item ID

q308_sermon_1

Multiple copies of this can be found lying around [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") as soon as the quest has started. Known locations are: 

  * Near a [sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_sewers "Novigrad sewers") entrance in [the Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits"), the one just around the foot of the stairs to [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf").
  * On the western side of the [Loan Shark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loan_Shark "Loan Shark")'s shop.
  * On Gildorf's market square, near the well.
  * Two on [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") \- by the [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Hierarch_Square\) "Herbalist \(Hierarch Square\)")'s stand, and between the pyres.
  * Near the [Rosemary and Thyme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme"), in front of a preacher by the city walls.
  * Near the [Eternal Fire shrine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire_shrine_\(Novigrad\) "Eternal Fire shrine \(Novigrad\)") in front of [Hattori's home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hattori%27s_home "Hattori's home").
  * Just inside [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove").
  * In an alcove at the stables near the eastern wheel at the [docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks"), where [A Walk on the Waterfront](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Walk_on_the_Waterfront "A Walk on the Waterfront") starts.
  * Inside the warehouse where [Hubert Rejk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk") can be fought at the end of the quest.
  * In [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton"), on the other side of the triangular street garden in front of the [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Silverton\) "Blacksmith \(Silverton\)").

### Journal entry

    _Remember, dear people, that a[fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire "Fire") once lit cannot be stopped, that it consumes everything in its path and only ceases to rage once all has been turned to ash. Men cannot flee this element, and if they bow before it, if they let it into their hearts, they too turn to ash. Search then your consciences and live in accordance with the [Church](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire")'s dictates - before the time for penance has passed._
    _\- Concerned Citizen_

### Associated quest

  * [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins")

## Item ID

q308_sermon_2

Found on an anvil in front of the workshop where [Fabian Meyer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fabian_Meyer "Fabian Meyer") was murdered. 

### Journal entry

    _Remember, good people, that fire can be both a blessing and a curse. Fire can warm with its flames, fire can prepare our nourishment, fire can drive off the horrors of the dark. Yet fire can also scorch, burn and incinerate. That is why we must live in harmony with[Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") and its commandments. Whoever fails to heed its lessons, whoever lives in ignorance of it, whoever mocks it, the same shall lose his life in its flames. Take my counsel to heart, good people, or you will reap the consequences of your deeds._
    _\- Concerned Citizen_

### Associated quest

  * [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins")

## Item ID

q308_sermon_3

Found on the wall at the crime scene where [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla") was attacked, near [Tretogor Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tretogor_Gate "Tretogor Gate"). 

### Journal entry

    _Remember, good people, the light cast by the Fire dispels every darkness, reveals each lie, exposes every unworthy and debauched soul. Not even shape-shifting dopplers can hide from its light, not even witches and sorceresses, so adept at fooling the common folk, not the loose women who walk the street and fog men's minds with their charms. Abandon then the treacherous and immoral magic arcane, cover your bodies in modesty and provoke no unclean thoughts, and the Eternal Fire will expose for you all monsters and sinners – or else burn you too in its flames._
    _\- Concerned Citizen_

## Item ID

q308_sermon_4

Found on the bed on which [Patricia Vegelbud](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patricia_Vegelbud "Patricia Vegelbud") was murdered. 

### Journal entry

    _Remember, good people, that the Fire devours everything in its path, sparing no one and stopping for nothing. The Fire burns all sinners alike, be they paupers or princes, men or nonhumans, weak or powerful. Whoever breaks its commandments, whoever does not heed the Church's counsel, whoever worships false gods, the same shall be turned to ash in its flame. Live then in the truth of the Eternal Fire, listen to its holy men - for they are your shepherd._
    _\- Concerned Citizen_

## Item ID

q308_sermon_5

The most easy one to miss, it can be found north from the well in the Bits lying between 2 staircases, but only after the autopsy has been completed. 

### Journal entry

    _What Fire has turned to ash, no man can restore. Thus we too, having surrendered our souls to its Church, can never abandon our holy faith. He who once knows the warmth of its holy flame and then renounces it commits a sin a thousand times worse than the ignorant man who spits on our faith without ever knowing it. Such an apostate will surely suffer a long and painful death, and then eternal torture in the afterlife._
    _\- Concerned Citizen_

### Associated quest

  * [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins")

## Notes

  * While the sermons' labels don't have numerals in the English version of the game, they do in the Polish (Original) version.
  * There is some evidence of a 6th sermon in the game files, but it doesn't seem to exist as an item. Only a container called "sermon 6" is defined with the 5th sermon inside it and it doesn't appear during gameplay.
