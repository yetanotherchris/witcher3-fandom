## Item ID

**Perfumed letter** is a letter and a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

If the quest [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never") can be, but has not started yet, it is delivered to Geralt when he leaves the [HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/HMS_Oxenfurt-Tretogor "HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor") after [A Favor for Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Favor_for_Radovid "A Favor for Radovid").
