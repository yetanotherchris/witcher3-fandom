## Item ID

gp_prologue_bandit_note03

**Scrawled notes** is a book and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted from the big chest at the hidden treasure marker east-northeast of the [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and starts the [Dirty Funds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dirty_Funds "Dirty Funds") treasure hunt quest. 

## Journal entry

    _Things are going better and better for us. we've pitched camp in the heart of the forest, near the old mill, and have already done quite nice for ourselves. Folk are poor, so they say, but squeeze them and something always comes out - a sack of grain here, a few[crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") there. Not bad for easy work, certainly better than the [army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Army "Temerian Army"). At least we've got something to fill our bellies, and the risk that we'll pay for this all with our lives, well, it's still less than when we were charging the [Nilfs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") at [Natalis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jan_Natalis "Jan Natalis")' orders._
