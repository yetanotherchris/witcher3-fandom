## Item ID

**Adalbert Kermith's third map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that be purchased from the [quartermaster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quartermaster_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Quartermaster \(Crow's Perch\)") at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). 

When read it'll give the areas to look for: 

  * All the standard [Feline](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") armor diagrams
  * All the superior Feline diagrams

## Journal entry

    _As per[Professor Atimstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slavko_Atimstein "Slavko Atimstein")'s instructions, I will not be conducting further explorations of the sites where I discovered pictograms relating to the [Cat School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School "Cat School") of [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). These pictograms are identical to the one which the Professor found in the caverns beneath [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle")._
    _[Adalbert Kermith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Adalbert_Kermith "Adalbert Kermith"). Adjunct in [Natural History](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy_faculties "Oxenfurt Academy faculties") at the [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")._
