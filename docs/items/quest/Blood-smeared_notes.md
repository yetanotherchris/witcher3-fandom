## Item ID

lw_tm_underwater_dungeon_note

**Blood-smeared notes** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and document in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on a corpse at the hidden treasure marker on a small island south of [Fyke Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyke_Isle "Fyke Isle"). It directs [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to the chest for the treasure hunt quest [Sunken Chest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sunken_Chest "Sunken Chest"). 

## Journal entry
