**Letter found on Joris Aquinus' body** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , found on the body of Joris Aquinus, whom [Hubert Rejk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk") autopsies during [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins"). The letter is automatically given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") during the cutscene.
