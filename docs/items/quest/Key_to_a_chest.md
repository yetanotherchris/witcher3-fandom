## Item ID

lw_tm15_chest_key

**Key to a chest** is a key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found, along with an [unsent letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(Don't_Play_with_the_Gods\) "Unsent letter"), on a body at the hidden treasure marker in [elven ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elven_ruins_near_Lake_Wyndamer "Elven ruins near Lake Wyndamer") southeast of [Duén Hen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Du%C3%A9n_Hen "Duén Hen").
