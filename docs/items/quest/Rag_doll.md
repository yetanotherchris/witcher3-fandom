## Item ID

q301_haunted_doll

**Rag doll** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the main quest [Novigrad Dreaming](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Dreaming "Novigrad Dreaming"), this is picked up in the attic room and, after finding the [crib drawing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drawing_of_a_crib "Drawing of a crib"), needs to be placed in the crib on the lower floor.
