## Item ID

**An invitation to the Memorial Derby** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , and can be looted from a table in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). 

## Journal entry

    _Take Heed, One and All!_
    _Citizens of the Free City of[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and foreigners alike!_
    _The venerable[Vegelbud family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family") has the honor of inviting you to the annual Great Erasmus Vegelbud Memorial Derby._
    _We welcome both spectators and contestants wishing to take part in the[races](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse_Races "Horse Races")._
    _As it is every year, the Derby will be held on the grounds of the[Vegelbuds' residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence"). Seek out the Master of the Races with any and all questions._
