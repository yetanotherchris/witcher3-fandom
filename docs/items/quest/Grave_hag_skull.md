## Item ID

mh106_hags_skulls

**Grave hag skull** is a quest item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , needed during [Contract: The Merry Widow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Merry_Widow "Contract: The Merry Widow") to lure out [Mourntart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mourntart "Mourntart"). 

Perhaps incorrectly named, as it's not actually the skull of a [grave hag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grave_hag "Grave hag"), but rather a bunch of human victim skulls.
