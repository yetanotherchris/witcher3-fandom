## Item ID

q105_ravens_feather

**Raven's feather** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is needed to help release [The Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree"), though it can accidentally be picked up before the quest is even started, like the [bottle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sealed_bottle "Sealed bottle") that contains [Johnny](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Johnny "Johnny")'s voice.
