## Item ID

mq4003_letter

This **Old letter** in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ is a document in reference to the story of _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_. 

It can be found in the cave near the [Lakeside Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lakeside_Hut "Lakeside Hut") during the secondary quest [Monster Slayer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_Slayer "Monster Slayer"). 

### Journal entry

    _All those chosen by the[Professor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Professor_\(assassin\) "Professor \(assassin\)") should be present outside the keep at the designated time. Tardiness, failure to bring the proper equipment or insubordination will be punished with death._
    _We shall mount our principle attack on[Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") with our [frightener](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Frightener "Frightener"). Gaining mastery of the keep should not present much of a problem, for we have heard only a handful of [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") remain in residence there. Therefore you must make haste: a 50 [orens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren") reward awaits you for every mutant head you sever._
    _Once we have taken the witchers' secrets, we must separate, in order not to attract attention. Every one of you will then be left to find your own way back to[Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), where we will reunite._
    _-[Azar Javed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Azar_Javed "Azar Javed")_

## Item ID

lw_de_wett_note

This **Old letter** is a book in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , and also a reference to _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ events and characters. 

It can be found in the secret room accessible after interacting with a pressure plate in the [Royal Palace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Palace_\(Vizima\) "Royal Palace \(Vizima\)") gardens in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"). 

### Journal entry

    _Dear Sir[Roderick de Wett](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roderick_de_Wett "Roderick de Wett"),_
    _Please forgive me for my tardy response – I was waiting for[Golan Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golan_Vivaldi "Golan Vivaldi") to confirm sufficient funds were available to redeem the bill of exchange you provided. Not that I don't trust you, but the gods help those who help themselves, as the [Kaedweni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedwen "Kaedwen") saying goes._
    _Getting straight to your question: Princess[Adda](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Adda_the_White "Adda the White")'s curse could indeed be caused to return. The witcher from [Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rivia "Rivia") had only a rudimentary knowledge of magic arcana, and so was not able to fully remove the spell, and instead only temporarily disrupted its workings._
    _For Adda to become a[striga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Striga "Striga") once more, one would have to sprinkle her food with three drops of blood from her father, King [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest"), three drops of blood from a [wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)") and three drops of bile from a freshly-buried woman, one who died having finished one hundred years but not yet having started her hundred and first. Then one must cast the [Incepted Triangle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triangle_Within_a_Triangle "Triangle Within a Triangle") over the meal thus prepared. That should do the trick – yet please consider carefully whether you truly wish to carry out such an ignoble deed._
    _Hoping to do business again with you in the future,_
    _-Alyssandra Deviel_

This **Old letter** is a document in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 

It can be found on a table at the guarded treasure east of [Termes Palace Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Termes_Palace_Ruins "Termes Palace Ruins") and is part of the mini sub plot tied to [Scavenger Hunt: Grandmaster Wolven Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Grandmaster_Wolven_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Grandmaster Wolven Gear"), but is not part of the quest.
