## Item ID

q107_guslar_cell_key

**Key to pellar's basement** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") key and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

It is unknown what it was supposed to unlock and there is no basement even remotely close to any of [the Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar")'s possible locations, locked or not. Even more oddly, its item ID suggests a link with the quest [Return to Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Return_to_Crookback_Bog "Return to Crookback Bog"), which he has no involvement in. The most logical conjecture would be that it had been planned at some point for the Pellar to at least attempt to cure [Anna Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anna_Strenger "Anna Strenger").
