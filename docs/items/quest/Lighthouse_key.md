## Item ID

mh207_lighthouse_door_key

**Lighthouse key** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") and key in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

After starting [Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Phantom_of_Eldberg "Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg") and talking to [Mikkjal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikkjal "Mikkjal"), he'll give this to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to open the [lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eldberg_Lighthouse "Eldberg Lighthouse"). While not required to enter it, there is an interesting [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_to_lighthouse_keeper "Letter to lighthouse keeper") inside that can be read to learn more about what happened. The key is returned to Mikkjal during their next conversation, so this is a missable opportunity.
