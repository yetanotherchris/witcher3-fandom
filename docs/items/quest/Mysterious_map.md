## Item ID

mq2006_map_1

**Mysterious map** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. While it's a document, it has no actual journal entry other than an image. 

It's found on a skeleton in the unmarked, abandoned hut southeast of [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare"), along with a [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_key "Old key").
