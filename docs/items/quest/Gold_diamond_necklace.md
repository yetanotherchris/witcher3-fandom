**Gold diamond necklace** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") [The Price of Honor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Price_of_Honor "The Price of Honor"), after finding the [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_Agda%27s_father_to_Timmon "Letter from Agda's father to Timmon") and [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chest_key#Chest_key_\(Faroe\) "Chest key") on one of [Timmon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timmon "Timmon")'s brothers' bodies, you can go back to where [Agda](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Agda "Agda")'s body was found and see a ship's mast out in the water. There's a sunken chest there with this inside. 

You can choose to return this or not if it was retrieved before turning in the quest. If kept, it can be sold for a nice profit or dismantled into [iron ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_ore "Iron ore").
