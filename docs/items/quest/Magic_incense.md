**Magic incense** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the [main quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests"), [Pyres of Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pyres_of_Novigrad "Pyres of Novigrad"), 3 of these need to be placed around the warehouse to help get rid of the [rats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rat "Rat").
