## Item ID

q106_anabelle_vial

**Potion vial** is a unique item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is a glass vial that once held the paralysing sleeping potion which [Anabelle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anabelle "Anabelle") drank before being devoured by rats. It is found near [her remains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anabelle%27s_remains "Anabelle's remains") inside the tower on [Fyke Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyke_Isle "Fyke Isle") during [A Towerful of Mice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Towerful_of_Mice "A Towerful of Mice"). 

It serves no purpose and is effectively Junk, despite being categorized as "Other".
