## Item ID

Geralt mask Nilf

**Nilfgaardian Helmet** is a mask in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

It's found at the [House of Respite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/House_of_Respite "House of Respite") by the far right window as you enter the house. 

## Notes

  * Sometimes instead of being found at the window, it can instead be found under the cart to the left of the food merchant behind the mother from [Blood Ties](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_Ties "Blood Ties").
