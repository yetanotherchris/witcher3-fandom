# Floren

[![Gwent cardart nilfgaard treason old](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d0/Gwent_cardart_nilfgaard_treason_old.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/174?cb=20190601054658)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d0/Gwent_cardart_nilfgaard_treason_old.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190601054658)

**Floren** , or **florin** ,[1] (alternatively: _Nilfgaardian Floren_) is the [monetary unit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Currency "Currency") used throughout the [Nilfgaardian Empire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") and its provinces. In [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1267 "Timeline"), one [mark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mark "Mark") was worth 60 florens,[2] and 100 [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") [orens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren") were the equivalent of 9 florens.[3]

* * *

The following is considered game canon only and may contradict [Andrzej Sapkowski](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Andrzej_Sapkowski "Andrzej Sapkowski")'s works.

* * *

By [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), the value of the floren had plummeted to one floren to three orens or [Novigradian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") [crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown").[4][N 1]

## Item ID

Florens

**Florens** can be looted throughout the game, particularly off bodies, although the currency of choice is the crown. Florens cannot be used to purchase items from [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"), but instead can be brought to [Vimme Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vimme_Vivaldi "Vimme Vivaldi") at the [Vivaldi Bank](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vivaldi_Bank "Vivaldi Bank") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") (or the [Cianfanelli Bank](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cianfanelli_Bank "Cianfanelli Bank") of [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion) and exchanged for crowns. One floren is worth three crowns. 

## Trivia

  * The [Florin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Florin_\(Italian_coin\) "wikipedia:Florin \(Italian coin\)") is a gold coin created in Florence, Italy in 1252, which was later used by many other European countries.
