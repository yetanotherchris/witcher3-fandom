## Item ID

Assire var Anahid's Necklace

**Assire var Anahid's necklace** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [junk item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

This is the necklace belonging to [Assire var Anahid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Assire_var_Anahid "Assire var Anahid"), which [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can examine in the secret room at [Caesar Bilzen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen "Caesar Bilzen")'s [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen%27s_house "Caesar Bilzen's house"), during the secondary quest [A Dangerous Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dangerous_Game "A Dangerous Game"). It was at some point intended to be lootable and still exists in the game files but, because of the single quote character in its item ID, it cannot be obtained even through console commands. In its place, a regular gold sapphire necklace appears instead.
