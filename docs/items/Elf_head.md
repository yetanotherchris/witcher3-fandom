**Elf head** is a [junk item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is a reward from [Boris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boris_\(troll\) "Boris \(troll\)"), if he is helped in the unmarked [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") [Harassing a Troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harassing_a_Troll "Harassing a Troll").
