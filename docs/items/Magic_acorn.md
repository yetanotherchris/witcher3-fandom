## Item ID

q111_imlerith_acorn

**Magic acorn** from the oak tree grown on the peak of [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). It can be looted from [Imlerith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imlerith "Imlerith")'s body. 

If you choose to speak with Stregomir (by the fire) he will inform you that the Great Oak may yield many Magic Acorns some years, while other years it may not yield as many. 

He then describes what the acorns are used for, explaining that sometimes they are ground up and used for fertilizer to yield a large harvest. While other times they are mixed in with medicines to cure illnesses. 

[Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") asks if the acorns provide enough to help all of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), and Stregomir replies by saying "it's never enough".

## Trivia

  * The magic acorn grants 2 [Ability Points](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities") when eaten. Geralt can also give the acorn to the villagers, though this changes nothing story-wise, and you lose 2 potential Ability Points.
  * The acorn can still be looted even if the base game was skipped as Imlerith's body will still be there when starting from either expansion.
