# The Witcher 3 mutagens

[![Tw3 mutagen green unique new](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/00/Tw3_mutagen_green_unique_new.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/60?cb=20180502160330)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/00/Tw3_mutagen_green_unique_new.png/revision/latest?cb=20180502160330) [![Tw3 mutagen red unique new](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f9/Tw3_mutagen_red_unique_new.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/60?cb=20180502160333)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f9/Tw3_mutagen_red_unique_new.png/revision/latest?cb=20180502160333) [![Tw3 mutagen blue unique new](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3a/Tw3_mutagen_blue_unique_new.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/60?cb=20180502154149)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3a/Tw3_mutagen_blue_unique_new.png/revision/latest?cb=20180502154149)

Activate **mutagens** by placing them in the diamond-shaped slots on the Character panel. Next to each slot is a set of square [Ability](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities") slots. Activated mutagens grant special bonuses to your character statistics. Different types (colors) of mutagens grant different kinds of bonuses. 

Each Ability placed in a slot near a mutagen of the same color increases that mutagen's bonus by 100%. So if all three Abilities in a set of slots match their nearby mutagen's color, the mutagen's bonus will be increased by 300%. The magnitude of this bonus can be further increased by the "Synergy" [ability](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities") in the Alchemy tree. Abilities whose color does not match their nearby mutagen have no effect on the mutagen's bonus – they neither increase nor decrease it. 

## Usage

[![Witcher3characterpage](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/37/Witcher3characterpage.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20180716081338)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/37/Witcher3characterpage.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180716081338) [![Witcher3characterpage](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/37/Witcher3characterpage.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20180716081338)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/37/Witcher3characterpage.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20180716081338) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Witcher3characterpage.jpg)

Up to four mutagens can be active at once (unlocked at levels 4, 9, 16, 28). Each mutagen slot has three Ability slots: each ability placed in a slot connected to a mutagen of the same color increases that mutagen's bonus by 100%. 

## Regular mutagens

Regular mutagens drop from some of the slightly stronger, organic monsters (i.e. no [specters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost"), [elementa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa "Elementa") or [beasts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_beasts "The Witcher 3 beasts")). That notably excludes the weakest enemies such as [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") and [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul"), while their stronger conterparts ([drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowned_dead "Drowned dead") and [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul") respectively) may drop them at random. The truly powerful foes that can be looted for special mutagens are also usually unable to drop these, though not all. 

In the base game, only the lesser versions can be looted and the higher tiers must be crafted from them. However, monsters in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion can also drop "medium" and greater mutagens.

## Notes

  * With the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, you can dismantle monster mutagens at the Alchemy Lab in the cellar of [Corvo Bianco](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Corvo_Bianco "Corvo Bianco"), yielding a lesser mutagen of the corresponding color. If a mutagen is an ingredient for a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") you have not yet obtained, the game will ask you to confirm before dismantling it (i.e. if you are not prompted you can safely dismantle it).
  * With the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, you can transform mutagens to different color of same quality (e.g. greater green to greater blue or greater red)
  * Originally, all alchemical ingredients, including mutagens, weighed 0.01 , but as of [Patch 1.07](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.07_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.07 \(The Witcher 3\)") they weigh nothing at all.
