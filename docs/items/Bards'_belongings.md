## Item ID

mq3016_bards_belongings

**Bards' belongings** is a [junk item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Novigrad Hospitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Hospitality "Novigrad Hospitality"), if you demand both your stuff and the [bards](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bard "Bard")', you'll gain this from them, leaving them in their underwear. 

## Associated quests

  * [Novigrad Hospitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Hospitality "Novigrad Hospitality")
