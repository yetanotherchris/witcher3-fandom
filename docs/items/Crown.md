# Crown

[![Currency](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7c/Items_Orens.png/revision/latest?cb=20081113120917)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7c/Items_Orens.png/revision/latest?cb=20081113120917 "Currency")

The **crown** (also known as the **Novigradian crown**) is a monetary unit which is used in some [Northern Kingdoms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"), including: 

  * [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"), where [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") is located
  * [Brugge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brugge "Brugge")
  * [Cidaris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cidaris "Cidaris")
  * [Kerack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kerack "Kerack")
  * [Ellander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ellander "Ellander")

One crown is the equivalent of one hundred [coppers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Copper "Copper"). In _[The Voice of Reason](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Voice_of_Reason "The Voice of Reason")_ , [Nenneke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nenneke "Nenneke") tells [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), that the crown currently has a value of six and a half [orens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren") and is still rising. 

In _[Baptism of Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baptism_of_Fire "Baptism of Fire")_ , [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") comments that a good arrow is worth half a crown, to which [Milva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Milva "Milva") replies 'He knows his prices'. 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")

**Crown** is the main currency of the third game, where most of the game takes place around Novigrad. It replaces the currency used in the previous two games, which was the oren ([Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") currency). Orens and [florens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Floren "Floren") can only be brought either to [Vimme Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vimme_Vivaldi "Vimme Vivaldi") at Novigrad's [Vivaldi Bank](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vivaldi_Bank "Vivaldi Bank") or to [Giacomo Cianfanelli](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giacomo_Cianfanelli "Giacomo Cianfanelli") at [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair")'s [Cianfanelli Bank](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cianfanelli_Bank "Cianfanelli Bank") and exchanged for crowns. 

### Exchange rates

  * 1 oren = 1 crown
  * 1 floren = 3 crowns
