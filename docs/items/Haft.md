## Item ID

Haft

**Haft** is a craftable [junk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [hardened timber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hardened_timber "Hardened timber"). It is not needed for anything.

## Notes

  * There is no monetary advantage to crafting this or dismantling: it's just above the break even point to purchase one and dismantle for the hardened timber to sell for [crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"), and crafting a haft costs more than its worth to sell. Likewise, crafting to dismantle later is a money sink.
  * As hardened timber is an easily accessible crafting item, it's unlikely one will need to resort to dismantling hafts to get it.
  * Due to the oddity of it being craftable but not actually needed for anything, it's likely it was originally meant for crafting other items but was later [changed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") (similar to how [fiber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiber "Fiber") was meant to be needed for crafting items but was similarly changed).
