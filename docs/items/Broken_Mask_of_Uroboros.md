**Broken Mask of Uroboros** is an item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is what remains of the [Mask of Uroboros](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mask_of_Uroboros "Mask of Uroboros") after it has been used at the site of the cataclysm on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig").
