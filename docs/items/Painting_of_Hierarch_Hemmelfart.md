## Item ID

q305_painting_of_hemmelfart

**Painting of Hierarch Hemmelfart** is a painting in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is a portrait of [Cyrus Engelkind Hemmelfart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyrus_Engelkind_Hemmelfart "Cyrus Engelkind Hemmelfart"), [Hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch "Hierarch") of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire"), and was painted by the [halflings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") living in [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut "Isolated Hut"). 

After receiving it, it can be given to [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") to hang in [Rosemary and Thyme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme") or be used to decorate [Corvo Bianco](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Corvo_Bianco "Corvo Bianco"). 

A copy of the painting also hangs in the [Moldavie Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moldavie_Residence "Moldavie Residence"). 

## Associated quest

  * [A Poet Under Pressure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Poet_Under_Pressure "A Poet Under Pressure")

## Notes

  * If the painting is in one's [stash](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stash "Stash") when you talk to Dandelion and choose the option to give him the painting, it won't be removed from the stash. In this case, one can then hang it at Corvo Bianco as well.
  * Despite what its caption says, this item cannot be dismantled.

## Trivia

  * It may be a reference to the painting _[Portrait of Pope Innocent X](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portrait_of_Innocent_X)_ by the Spanish painter [Diego Velázquez](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diego_Vel%C3%A1zquez).
