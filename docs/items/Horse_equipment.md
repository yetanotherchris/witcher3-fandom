# Horse equipment

**Horse equipment** can be acquired throughout _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and its expansions, _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion and _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. The equipment can be broken down into four categories: 

  * **Blinders** : increase [Roach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roach "Roach")'s fear level limit so they won't panic as easily
  * **Saddle** : increase Roach's stamina so Geralt can gallop further before having to rest
  * **Saddlebags** : increase [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s inventory weight limit so he can carry more
  * **Trophies** : provide certain effects when strapped to Roach's saddle

The lists below include all items from [DLCs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") (which are free) and both expansions. Expansion items have been marked.

## Trophies

    _Main article:[The Witcher 3 trophies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies")_

Trophies are special monster parts that can be placed in the trophy slot, and thus strapped to Roach's saddle. They provide bonuses such as additional [XP](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP "XP") for killing certain enemies, or a chance of finding extra gold or herbs. 

## Tips

Using this method it is possible to quickly obtain high tier horse equipment for Roach. Which in fact will be the best in the base game excluding a few that are obtained in _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 

To start you need access to [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), see [Destination: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige") for tips on how to reach it early in the game. 

Go to [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") and use the local [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Larvik\) "Notice board \(Larvik\)") to obtain the quest [The Heroes' Pursuits: For the Goddess' Glory!](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_For_the_Goddess%27_Glory! "The Heroes' Pursuits: For the Goddess' Glory!"), then talk to [Svana](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svana "Svana") to join the competition which will give 3 other quests each being a race. Now do them in the following order: 

Start with [The Heroes' Pursuits: Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_Fyresdal "The Heroes' Pursuits: Fyresdal"), after you win you will obtain the [Cavalry saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cavalry_saddle "Cavalry saddle"), which will make the other races more easy. 

Now do [The Heroes' Pursuits: Fayrlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_Fayrlund "The Heroes' Pursuits: Fayrlund") which will award you the [Zerrikanian saddlebags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_saddlebags "Zerrikanian saddlebags") and [The Heroes' Pursuits: Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_Kaer_Trolde "The Heroes' Pursuits: Kaer Trolde") which will award you [Zerrikanian blinders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_blinders "Zerrikanian blinders"). 

After that do the final race from The Heroes' Pursuits: For the Goddess' Glory! which will award you the [Zerrikanian saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_saddle "Zerrikanian saddle"). 

### With _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion

If you have Hearts of Stone then instead do [Races: Swift as the Western Winds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Races:_Swift_as_the_Western_Winds "Races: Swift as the Western Winds") (although suggested level is 32 the quest can be done at any level as it's only a race) if you win you will obtain the [Ofieri stock saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofieri_stock_saddle "Ofieri stock saddle") which is even better than the Zerrikanian saddle. Then do The Heroes' Pursuits: Fayrlund for the saddlebags and The Heroes' Pursuits: Kaer Trolde for the blinders.
