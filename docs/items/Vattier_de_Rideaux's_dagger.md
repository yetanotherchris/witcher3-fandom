## Item ID

Vattier de Rideaux's Dagger

**Vattier de Rideaux's dagger** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [junk item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

This is the dagger belonging to [Vattier de Rideaux](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vattier_de_Rideaux "Vattier de Rideaux"), which [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can examine in the secret room at [Caesar Bilzen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen "Caesar Bilzen")'s [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen%27s_house "Caesar Bilzen's house"), during the secondary quest [A Dangerous Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dangerous_Game "A Dangerous Game"). It was at some point intended to be lootable and still exists in the game files but, because of the single quote character in its item ID, it cannot be obtained even through console commands. In its place, two [ornate swords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") appear instead.
