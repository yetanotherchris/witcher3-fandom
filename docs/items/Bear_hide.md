## Item ID

Bear pelt

**Bear hide** is a [junk item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , that is also required as an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") to craft [enhanced beast oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_beast_oil "Enhanced beast oil"). 

While it can be found as random loot, it is naturally dropped by [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") and sold by most [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist"), though some other [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") will offer it as well.
