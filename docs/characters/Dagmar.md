## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Dagmar** was a peasant from [Claywich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claywich "Claywich"), [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). Like many others from his village, he worshiped the [Allgod](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Allgod "Allgod"), as did his forefathers, not realizing it was no [deity](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Religion "Religion") at all but a [sylvan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sylvan "Sylvan"). 

When the [Third Nilfgaard War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Nilfgaard_War "Third Nilfgaard War") hit, the area fell on lean times and with nothing left to offer the Allgod but scraps not even fit for feeding to their [dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog"). The Allgod, incensed by these meager offerings, threatened to curse them if they didn't offer better. 

_If Geralt bargains with the Allgod:_ Thankful to the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), Dagmar and the rest continued to worship the Allgod, albeit with smaller offerings while the war continued. 

_If Geralt doesn't bargain:_ Sadly accepting they had no other recourse, Dagmar proceeded to offer what little food he had left in storage. 

_If Geralt kills the Allgod:_ Distraught, Dagmar and [Preben](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Preben "Preben") proclaimed the Allgod said worse things would happen to them if he ever died and the two ran home to do a group prayer in hopes it'd save them. 

_If Geralt reveals the cellar:_ Dagmar and Preben eyed the now exposed cellar with curiosity and some time later, the cellar was covered up with no worshipers showing up at the shrine.
