## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Simun Brambling** is a prisoner of [Madman Lugos](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Madman_Lugos "Madman Lugos") at [Kaer Muire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Muire "Kaer Muire") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). He seems strangely comfortable with being in such a place and he can help [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") get out of there, in exchange for a favor to be returned at a later date. 

If the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") takes Simun's help to escape, he'll return outside the [New Port Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port") at [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") asking for the debt to be repaid and to meet him at a hut on the lake near [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare") without specifying why. If Geralt agrees and heads to the hut, Simun will antagonize [Volund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Volund "Volund") before asking Geralt to kill him. Volund will then tell Geralt that Simun killed his brother by tricking and betraying him, leaving it up to the witcher to honor his debt or not. 

_If Geralt fulfills his debt to Simun:_ The witcher and Simun kill Volund along with one of his warriors. Simun reveals that Volund had hunted and sent assassins after him wherever he went on the [Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). Debt repaid they part ways. 

_If Geralt refuses to fulfill his debt:_ The witcher and Volund with one of his warriors kill Simun.
