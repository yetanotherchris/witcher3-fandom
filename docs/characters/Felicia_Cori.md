**Felicia Cori** was a [sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorceress "Sorceress") who graduated from [Aretuza](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aretuza "Aretuza").[1]

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Felicia was in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), when she was captured by the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") and taken to [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"), where she was burned at the stake for being a mage, just as [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") arrived in the square.[2]

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

She was a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_merchants "The Witcher 2 merchants"), [dice poker player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_dice_poker "The Witcher 2 dice poker"), and [coiffeuse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barbers_and_Coiffeuses "Barbers and Coiffeuses"). 

### [Journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_journal "The Witcher 2 journal") entry

[![Tw2 journal Feliciacori](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/6/6d/Tw2_journal_Feliciacori.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/153?cb=20110612005652)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/6/6d/Tw2_journal_Feliciacori.png/revision/latest?cb=20110612005652) [![Tw2 journal Feliciacori](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/6/6d/Tw2_journal_Feliciacori.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/153?cb=20110612005652)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/6/6d/Tw2_journal_Feliciacori.png/revision/latest?cb=20110612005652) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw2_journal_Feliciacori.png)

    _Young sorceresses from the school at Aretuza must, apart from passing exams, complete an internship under a mistress' watchful eye. As part of such an internship, the young Felicia Cori peddled[magical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") curios of her own production in [Vergen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vergen "Vergen")._
    _Hoping to earn approval and a passing grade, Felicia had come to[Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne") with [Philippa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart"). She waited there for her instructor to find some free time for her._
    _As it turned out, the young adept of magic, Felicia Cori, was also a talented coiffeuse. Geralt could avail himself of her services and change his hairstyle for a modest fee._

    _In Loc Muinne's central square, in addition to selling a variety of useful items, Felicia Cori also offered hairstyling services._

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

### Journal entry

    _Geralt had been in situations where he could not prevent someone's death on more than one occasion. In fact, you might say he had grown accustomed to that particular form of agony. It is another thing altogether, however, to stand powerless and watch the unjust, cruel execution of an acquaintance for whom one has developed a fondness._
    _Such was his lot as regards Felicia Cori, a young sorceress whom Geralt had met while chasing the kingslayer, Letho. Though Geralt had not gotten to know her deeply, he had nonetheless taken a liking to this former pupil of Philippa Eilhart, who had seemed to him a pleasant and enterprising young woman. Yet she was a wielder of magic and, as such, met her end on a pyre._
