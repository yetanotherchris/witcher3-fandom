## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Beast Tamer** was a traveling showman in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), selling tickets for people to see rare and exciting creatures like a [basilisk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk"). In reality, the guy was either ignorant or deliberately cheating people out of their money, as it wasn't a basilisk he had but a young, ill-fed [wyvern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern"). 

_If Geralt intervenes:_ as he showed off the "basilisk" to a group of [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldiers, the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") came forth, eventually pointing out it was a wyvern. Realizing they'd been had, the soldiers began to move in to grab the man when the beast's cage suddenly broke and the wyvern got out while the "beast tamer" hid.
