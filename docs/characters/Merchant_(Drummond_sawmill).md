## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** appears at [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond")'s [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(Drummond\) "Sawmill \(Drummond\)") south of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund"), if the site is liberated. He sits inside the single stone hut there and is an exact clone of the roadside camp [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") south of [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor"), selling [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") location maps and a small selection of other items.
