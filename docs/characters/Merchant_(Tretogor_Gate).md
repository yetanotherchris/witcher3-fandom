\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant** is located at the marketplace in the northwest of the [Tretogor Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tretogor_Gate "Tretogor Gate") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He sells a few [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting components") and some [food and drink](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"). He is also a [Gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players") using the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck") deck.
