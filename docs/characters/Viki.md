**Viki** was a widowed royal family member[1] from a far-off land with the coat of arms of a zebra. 

## Biography

Around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), a coup was instigated in her kingdom and the royal family was overthrown. She and her [son](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Plaetyn "Plaetyn")[2] boarded one of two ships set for [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair"), where they were likely seeking asylum. 

However, the [usurper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dirgelech "Dirgelech") back home sent warriors after them and one of the ships wrecked on an island in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), though luckily Viki and the young boy were on the other ship. The two made it as far as [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") before their ship also crashed, stranding them in a foreign land and, to make matters worse, Viki couldn't speak any of the [local language](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Speech "Common Speech"). They then took refuge in [Widows' Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Widows%27_Grotto "Widows' Grotto") as Viki tried to figure out what to do next. 

_If Geralt finds them and protects them:_ while she couldn't understand the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), or he understand her, she was grateful nonetheless and, now that the last warrior that'd been following them was dead, she and the boy set out once more. 

_If Geralt finds them but stands aside:_ Viki met her end far from home as the warrior cut her down without a second thought.
