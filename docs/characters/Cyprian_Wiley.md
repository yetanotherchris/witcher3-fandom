**Cyprian Wiley** , more well-known as "**Whoreson Junior** ", was one of the [four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four") crime bosses of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") alongside [Sigi Reuven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"), [Carlo Varese](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese"), and [Francis Bedlam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam"). His [gang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cutups "Cutups") used to control, together with the [Blindeyes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blindeyes "Blindeyes"), [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") district of the city, where they were in possession of an [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_arena "Whoreson Junior's arena"), a [casino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_casino "Whoreson Junior's casino"), a [hideout](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_hideout "Whoreson Junior's hideout") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and a [residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_mansion "Whoreson Junior's mansion") in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt").

## Biography

Little is known about Cyprian Wiley's life before becoming part of the criminal underworld of Novigrad. He was the son of [Alonso "Whoreson" Wiley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alonso_Wiley "Alonso Wiley") who, just like him, was a feared and ruthless crime lord. As a young boy however, Cyprian was often beaten by his father,[1] which might have been the cause of his future cruel and merciless attitude. 

### Syndicate

In the end, Wiley decided to put a stop to his father's reign by ordering his assassination. Soon after, now under the nickname of Whoreson Junior, he inherited all of his father's possessions like casinos, fighting rings, and brothels and became one of the most powerful crime lords in the city.[2]

Sometime between [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1267 "Timeline/Games") and [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), the most noted crime lords of Novigrad decided to set aside rivalries and forge an alliance of coin: thus Cyprian Wiley, [Carlo Varese](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese"), [Francis Bedlam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam"), [Gudrun Bjornsdottir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gudrun_Bjornsdottir "Gudrun Bjornsdottir"), [Sigi Reuven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigi_Reuven "Sigi Reuven"), and [Hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch "Hierarch") [Hemmelfart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemmelfart "Hemmelfart") founded the [Syndicate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Syndicate "Syndicate"). 

Wiley and Hierarch Hemmelfart started a collaboration: the latter started selling zealots of the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") to Wiley, who were later, without their knowledge, sent to his [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_arena "Whoreson Junior's arena") to be slaughtered in order to please the audience, as well as fattening the purses of both Wiley and the Hierarch.[1]

### Big Four

Eventually, the four most influential gang bosses of the Syndicate became known as the [Big Four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four"), where Wiley still held a well-to-do position. 

In 1272, Whoreson Junior was contacted by [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") and [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"), who needed his connections to find someone who could repair a [phylactery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phylactery "Phylactery"). Junior accepted to help them, provided that they would bring him Count [Reuven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra")'s treasure. However, Wiley began to suspect that Ciri and Dandelion were trying to cheat on him, so he kidnapped their friend [Dudu](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dudu "Dudu") in order to capture them when they would come to save him. In the end, Ciri managed to free Dudu and escape from Junior.[2]

Not long after, Whoreson Junior was hired by King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") of [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"), who tasked him with starting a gang war in the city, so that he could take control of Novigrad with ease. 

Following Radovid's orders, Whoreson sent his henchmen to the city [bathhouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund%27s_Bathhouse "Sigismund's Bathhouse"), where the other three members of the Big Four were having a meeting, having already noted Whoreson was acting outside the group's interests, in order to kill them. The attempt failed, however, and [Carlo Varese](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese") started hunting Whoreson for vengeance, sending his [men](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crownsplitters "Crownsplitters") to raid Junior's hideouts. 

At the same time, [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") started looking for him too, hoping to find information about Ciri. In the end, King Radovid himself betrayed Junior as he no longer needed him, and revealed that Whoreson was hiding in a [mansion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_mansion "Whoreson Junior's mansion") in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). 

The [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") headed towards Oxenfurt and finally confronted Whoreson who, after a painful interrogation, revealed everything he knew about Ciri. 

    _If Geralt kills Whoreson Junior:_ Whoreson Junior was killed by the witcher. Taking advantage of the situation, the [doppler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler "Doppler") Dudu decided to hide in Novigrad by hiding under the guise of Whoreson Junior himself. Despite that, the doppler didn't want to act as a criminal, thus he revealed to his men that he finally converted to the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire"), selling all his illegal enterprises and instead acting as a respectable businessman. Cyprian Wiley, the man who never helped anyone during his entire life, became one of the most highly regarded citizens of Novigrad.

    _If Geralt spares Whoreson Junior:_ Despite what he had done to Ciri, the witcher decided to spare Wiley. Due to the details allegiance with Radovid having been leaked, Junior lost all of his power and was forced to live on the streets; he was endlessly belittled by the city's residents for his predicament, insofar that Ciri considered this to be a better punishment than death.

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

### Journal entry

    _Cyprian Wiley the younger was one of Novigrad's most dangerous criminals. He had inherited his[father](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alonso_Wiley "Alonso Wiley")'s casinos. fighting ring and brothels - as well as the old man's sadistic tendencies, ruthlessness and lack of scruples._
    _These traits had made the moniker "Whoreson" something of a family heirloom for his powerful criminal clan._
    _Cyprian also took after his father in terms of ambition. He broke the unwritten armistice between Novigrad's various criminal groups and tried to murder the other three leading underworld bosses._
    _Whoreson's audacity had a very specific cause - King[Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"). who had commissioned the gangster's actions._
    _As it turned out. Radovid's plan was for Whoreson to start a gang war in Novigrad. In the resulting chaos. Radovid would take control of the city with ease._
    _While searching Cyprian's home den of iniquity.[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") discovered the nickname "Whoreson" was in fact an understatement - something much stronger was needed to describe a man who delighted in murdering captive women._
    **If Geralt spares him:**

    _Though few would have mourned his passing. Geralt decided not to kill Whoreson. As soon as Radovid withdrew his protection and his old enemies were free to exact revenge. Whoreson's career was doomed to an inglorious end._
    **If Geralt kills him:**

    _In addition to blackmail, extortion, murder and torture, the list of Whoreson's crimes also included trying to harm Ciri. Geralt took this very personally and made the bandit pay for this last sin with his life._
