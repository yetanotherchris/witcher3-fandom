The **merchant** in [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton") is a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player using the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"). He mostly sells [junk items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk"), but does also sell [rugged saddlebags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rugged_saddlebags "Rugged saddlebags") and [mandrake cordial](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mandrake_cordial "Mandrake cordial").
