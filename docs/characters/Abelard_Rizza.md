## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Abelard Rizza** was an actor residing in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), who belonged to [Irina Renarde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Irina_Renarde "Irina Renarde")'s troupe. 

## Biography

An alcoholic who performed in theater plays at [the Butcher's Yard theater](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Butcher%27s_Yard_theater "The Butcher's Yard theater"), Abelard was a reliable and steady actor, highly regarded by Irina Renarde, but he did not always fully captivate the crowd with his performances. 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Irina and [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") decided to write a play, to lure out [Dudu](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dudu "Dudu"), and they had the choice to have either Abelard or [Maxim Boliere](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maxim_Boliere "Maxim Boliere") to play the role of the [doppler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler "Doppler"). 

_If Abelard is hired:_ the play went well, Abelard was sober performed well, and after receiving his applause, he left the stage.
