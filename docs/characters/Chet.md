## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Chet** (Polish: _Ciesław_) was one of the folks living at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). He took in [Symko](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Symko "Symko") after the [orphan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orphans "Orphans")'s [father](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Borzoy "Borzoy") died fighting the [Shrieker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shrieker "Shrieker").
