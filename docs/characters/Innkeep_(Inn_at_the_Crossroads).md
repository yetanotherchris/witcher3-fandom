## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **innkeep** runs the [Inn at the Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads "Inn at the Crossroads") and plays [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). He uses the [Nilfgaardian Empire deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck") and will give the [Menno Coehoorn card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Menno_Coehoorn_\(gwent_card\) "Menno Coehoorn \(gwent card\)") once beaten. 

_If Geralt talks to him to find Hendrik:_ In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") he pointed [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") towards [Heatherton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heatherton "Heatherton"), where [Hendrik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hendrik "Hendrik") was living. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What Gwent deck does the Innkeep at the Crossroads use?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Innkeep at the Inn at the Crossroads in The Witcher 3 uses the Nilfgaardian Empire deck in Gwent. Defeating him rewards players with the Menno Coehoorn card. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How can you obtain the Menno Coehoorn card from the Innkeep?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

To obtain the Menno Coehoorn card, defeat the innkeep at the Inn at the Crossroads in a game of Gwent in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. This card, part of the Nilfgaardian Empire deck, has a strength of 10 and is immune to weather effects. It also has a medic ability to revive a card from your discard pile. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Where does the Innkeep direct Geralt to find Hendrik?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In 1272, the Innkeep at the Inn at the Crossroads directed Geralt to Heatherton to locate Hendrik, as part of the quest 'The Nilfgaardian Connection.' 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What issue might occur with the Innkeep during The Nilfgaardian Connection?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In 'The Nilfgaardian Connection' quest of 'Witcher 3', issues with the Innkeep at the Inn at the Crossroads may occur if Geralt previously killed the Baron's men. This action causes fear among the townsfolk and hostility from the guards, blocking Geralt's access. If Geralt avoided the Baron's men, the guards will distrust but not attack him, necessitating a payment to continue. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How can you fix the Innkeep's disappearance at the Inn at the Crossroads?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

To fix the disappearance of the Innkeep at the Inn at the Crossroads in The Witcher 3, update the game to the latest patch, such as patch 1.22, which addresses character disappearance issues. Also, review any installed mods for conflicts. 

Provided by: Fandom

  * He may disappear after talking to him during [The Nilfgaardian Connection](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nilfgaardian_Connection "The Nilfgaardian Connection"). His icon shows him located behind the counter as usual, but he can't be seen. Meditating or entering the inn from the side door fixes the issue.
