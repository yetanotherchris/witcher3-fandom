## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), during the [witch hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunts "Witch hunts") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), Anisse and her husband [Berthold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berthold "Berthold") went into hiding in their house. Approached by [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"), they were invited on a ship to [Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir") to safety. With a plan to meet at [The Kingfisher Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn"), they became trapped as [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") surrounded their house. 

_If Geralt saves them:_ Making the decision to leave none behind, Triss and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") went to Anisse and Berthold's hideout and killed the witch hunters there. Anisse and her husband then went with them to The Kingfisher Inn, and escaped to the [docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks") through the [Novigrad sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_sewers "Novigrad sewers") with the other mages. There, they boarded the ship and departed for a new and safer life in Kovir. 

_If Geralt saves her, but Berthold dies during the fight:_ Triss tried to convinve Anisse to leave Berthold and flee on her own, but failed to do so. Geralt was forced to use the [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") [Sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") to convince Anisse to come to the Kingfisher Inn.
