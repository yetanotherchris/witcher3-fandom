### Games

**Oswin** was the stablemaster at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). 

## Biography

In May [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), the fortresses' barn was struck by lightning and set ablaze, and Oswin became trapped in the stables. 

_If Geralt chose to save him:_ The [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") climbed through the roof down into the stables and opened the barred door to rescue him from the fire. He later showed his gratitude by offering a monetary reward to Geralt the next time the witcher visited Crow's Perch.
