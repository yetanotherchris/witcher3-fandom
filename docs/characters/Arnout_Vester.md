### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Arnout Vester** was a famous [bounty hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") from the [Nilfgaardian Empire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") and one of many that aimed to catch the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Letho of Gulet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letho_of_Gulet "Letho of Gulet"). 

## Biography

_If[Ghosts of the Past](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghosts_of_the_Past "Ghosts of the Past") is completed:_ In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he ended up in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") with his gang, having gotten word that Letho was hiding out at the abandoned [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor"). However, Arnout didn't realize the witcher had tracked him down already and bribed his arbalist [Ben of Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ben_of_Kovir "Ben of Kovir") to help fake his death. Letho then confronted Arnout directly in Lindenvale. Letho didn't go down without fight, taking out most of Arnout's men. 

    _If Geralt kills the rest:_ incensed by this, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") then ran in and decided he could at least finish them off, killing Arnout.
    _If Geralt lets the rest go:_ deciding this wasn't his fight, Geralt let Arnout and the rest of his men go, who then spread the word that Letho was dead.
