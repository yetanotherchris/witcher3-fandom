## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **innkeep** in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale")'s [tavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale_tavern "Lindenvale tavern") has the same inventory as [Inn at the Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads "Inn at the Crossroads")' [innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(Inn_at_the_Crossroads\) "Innkeep \(Inn at the Crossroads\)"), and for unknown reasons his appearance is allowed to change. 

## Notes

  * In earlier versions of the game, this innkeep would sell the same things as [wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Pontar\) "Wandering merchant \(Pontar\)"), and he still has the regular trader's icon.
