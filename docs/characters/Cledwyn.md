**Cledwyn** was the imperial [barber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barber "Barber") of [Emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor") [Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis") and subordinate of [Mererid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mererid "Mererid"), the emperor's chamberlain. He was the who shaved off [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s beard, when the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") visited the emperor in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), while [General Voorhis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvran_Voorhis "Morvran Voorhis") asked some questions about recent events.

## Trivia

  * Morvran Voorhis interviews Geralt while being shaved, explaining that "men turn honest when they feel a blade at their throat."
