## Biography

At some point Elsa gave birth to a son, but the umbilical cord wrapped around his neck, choking him, though his life was quickly saved by a woman named [Annie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Annie "Annie"), who unwrapped the cord.[1]

### Meeting Geralt

In May [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), tensions were high after a [recent defeat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_White_Orchard "Battle of White Orchard") at the [battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_\(White_Orchard\) "Battlefield \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). Elsa suffered from rumors saying she was "[Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire")'s whore", due to her taking down the [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") lilies, as soldiers would burn the inn if they saw it. After saving her cousin [Bram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bram "Bram"), the [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") met Elsa at the tavern. 

    _If Geralt refused payment from Bram:_ Elsa, overjoyed, thanked the witchers for saving her cousin, and gave Geralt four [roasted chicken legs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roasted_chicken_leg "Roasted chicken leg").

Geralt asked whether Elsa had seen [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"), though she hadn't, so she recommended asking the other patrons. 

### Incident in the Tavern

Later, when Geralt and Vesemir were preparing to leave White Orchard, Annie's sister began accosting Elsa for removing the [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") lilies from the wall. The woman became violent and grabbed Elsa by the head and proceeded to bash it against the counter repeatedly. Geralt and Vesemir intervened and attracted the attention of some local men who believed that the witchers were conspiring with [Emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor") [Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis"). The men refused to back down, and thus the situation ended in a massacre. Both women became terrified of the witchers, and Elsa, cowering behind the counter, told them to leave and never return. 

### Novigrad

Unable to continue living in White Orchard after the incident, Elsa traveled for a time and eventually ended up in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Incidentally, while she visited [The Kingfisher Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn") one night and finished watching [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla")'s performance, she spotted Geralt in the crowd and accosted him in front of the crowd, noting he was the one who "murdered those Temerian boys" in White Orchard. However, the rest of the patrons paid no mind to this and became annoyed that she was ruining the atmosphere. 

After Novigrad, she settled down in a place called Rustone.[2]

## Notes

  * Before [Patch 1.04](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.04_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.04 \(The Witcher 3\)"), it was required to buy Elsa's [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") cards before leaving White Orchard to avoid failing the secondary quest [Collect 'Em All](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Collect_%27Em_All "Collect 'Em All"), as she leaves at the same time as Geralt and ceases to be a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"). Since Patch 1.04, Elsa's irrecoverable cards are also sold by Bram as a workaround.
  * Elsa buys [junk items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") at higher prices than Bram, some even higher than [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") prices. Thus, selling junk to her before she leaves is a good way to earn extra [crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") early.
