## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Thecla** was a very old, blind woman that was considered to be wise by the locals around the [village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Road_to_Bald_Mountain "Road to Bald Mountain") below [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") and who worshiped the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"). A cunning senior, she was accompanied by her young helper or more precisely servant, [Marica](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marica "Marica"). 

At one point, she claimed that those in [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren") were being punished with [The Whispering Hillock](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Whispering_Hillock "The Whispering Hillock") for disrespecting the Crones.
