## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

Corporal **Lyffned** was a [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") military officer in the [Imperial Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army"). 

## Biography

In May [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), while stationed at the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") under Captain [Peter Saar Gwynleve](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peter_Saar_Gwynleve "Peter Saar Gwynleve"), Lyffned was ordered to collect medical supplies from another barracks. While on his return trip through the swamp near the garrison, he was ambushed and killed by [John Geermer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/John_Geermer "John Geermer"), a [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") guerrilla, with an arrow to the neck.
