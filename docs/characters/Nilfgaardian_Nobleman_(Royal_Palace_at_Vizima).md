## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed**Nilfgaardian nobleman** is a character in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ temporarily residing in the [Royal Palace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Palace_\(Vizima\) "Royal Palace \(Vizima\)") at [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), his wealth, power, and social influence put him in an influential position in the court of [Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis"). 

He plays the [Nilfgaardian Empire deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck") \- a true patriot. If beaten, he awards the player with [Foltest: The Siegemaster (gwent card)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest:_The_Siegemaster_\(gwent_card\) "Foltest: The Siegemaster \(gwent card\)").
