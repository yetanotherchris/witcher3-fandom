## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Freya Priestess** is old and wise serving the goddess Modron [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") residing near [Freya's Garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya%27s_Garden "Freya's Garden") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"). She is an [alchemist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") and sells [alchemical supplies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients").
