## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Wolverstone** was the captain of the _[Atropos](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Atropos "Atropos")_ , serving routes between [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he was the only person willing to take [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to Skellige, as all the other captains at the [Novigrad Docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks") deemed the journey too risky. Wolverstone demanded a fee of 1000[![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") for passage, owing to the lack of competition and the risks associated with the trip. In his usual style, he began the trip intoxicated. 

On its approach into [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor"), the _Atropos_ was boarded by pirates of [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite"). This, combined with terrible weather, led to the ship veering off course south towards [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig"), near which it wrecked, leading to Wolverstone's death.
