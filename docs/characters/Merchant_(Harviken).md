\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant** should be present in the village of [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken") on [Faroe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle"), where he has a nice trader's spot that can still be observed, but himself doesn't appear during gameplay due to a bug. He could play [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), and would sell 3 unique books, one of which cannot be found anywhere else.
