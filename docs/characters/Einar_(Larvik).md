## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Einar** , son of Toradar, is a pirate living in [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik"). He was a member of the [Terror Crew](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Terror_Crew "Terror Crew"), a pirate crew lead by the infamous pirate [Morkvarg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg "Morkvarg"). 

Einar was present during Morkvarg's ransacking of [Freya's Garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya%27s_Garden "Freya's Garden"). It was commonly assumed that Priestess [Ulve](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulve "Ulve") had cursed Morkvarg before he had killed her, but it was in fact Einar who had placed the curse on him. Disgusted with the atrocities Morkvarg committed against the gods by defiling the garden, he transformed Morkvarg into a [Werewolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") using a cursed pendant given to him by his father.
