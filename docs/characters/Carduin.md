**Carduin** of [Lan Exeter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lan_Exeter "Lan Exeter") was a [Kovirian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir") [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") and member of the [Council of Wizards](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Council_of_Wizards "Council of Wizards"), the lower chamber within the [Brotherhood of Sorcerers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brotherhood_of_Sorcerers "Brotherhood of Sorcerers").[1]

He also served as a court mage for [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Esterad Thyssen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Esterad_Thyssen "Esterad Thyssen") of [Kovir and Poviss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir_and_Poviss "Kovir and Poviss"),[2] and possibly later King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") of [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania").[3]

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What role did Carduin play in the Grand Mage's Assembly?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Carduin was the main spokesman for the new Council and Conclave during the Grand Mage's Assembly in Loc Muinne. He was instrumental in the summit where mages aimed to revive the Council under King Radovid's influence. Despite his dependence on the king, Carduin's pride led him to play a key role in these reformation efforts.

**How did Carduin assist Geralt at the Aretuza banquet?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Carduin aided Geralt during the Aretuza banquet by assisting in transporting the injured Dorregaray to Marti Södergren amidst the coup. He remained neutral and later escaped to Kovir. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is Carduin's connection to King Esterad Thyssen?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Carduin's connection to King Esterad Thyssen is not detailed in the available context. Esterad Thyssen, king of Kovir and Poviss, was the son of Baldwin and Blanka. His family includes his son Tankred, a potential suitor for Cirilla of Cintra. No specific link between Carduin and King Esterad Thyssen is mentioned. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Which council was Carduin a member of in the Witcher universe?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Carduin was part of the Council of Sorcerers in the Witcher universe. Known for his influence, he became the main spokesman for the new Council and Conclave after the original Council's collapse during the Thanedd Isle coup. He was active in the Loc Muinne summit, where mages aimed to revive the Council under King Radovid's rule.

## Biography

### Thanedd coup

On June 30th [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1267 "Timeline"), he travelled to [Thanedd Island](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thanedd_Island "Thanedd Island") to participate in the [Grand Mage's Assembly](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thanedd_coup "Thanedd coup") between the Council and [Chapter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chapter_of_the_Gift_and_the_Art "Chapter of the Gift and the Art"). He participated in the evening banquet in [Aretuza](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aretuza "Aretuza") and later helped [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") bring the wounded [Dorregaray](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dorregaray "Dorregaray") to [Marti Södergren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marti_S%C3%B6dergren "Marti Södergren") during the unfolding coup. He was considered a neutral participant and survived the incident, fleeing from [Garstang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Garstang "Garstang") down to [Loxia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loxia "Loxia"), and then finally back to Kovir.[1]

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

**Carduin** was the main spokesman for the new Council and Conclave during the deliberations in [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne"). Also using the [Dearhenna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dearhenna "Dearhenna")'s [Megascope](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Megascope "Megascope") he can be seen plotting conspiracy together with Radovid V during a game of chess. 

### Journal entry

    _Carduin of Lan Exeter had once been a member of the Council of Sorcerers. During the Thanedd coup he had attempted to maintain both reason and neutrality. He certainly offered proof of the former when it turned out that those conspiring with[Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") thought nothing of his impartiality - teleporting back to Kovir was an extremely reasonable, and life-saving, course of action._
    **If Geralt chooses to rescue[Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") during Chapter III:**

    _After hearing the accusations against[Síle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/S%C3%ADle_de_Tansarville "Síle de Tansarville"), Carduin demonstrated keen intuition and political savvy. He immediately demanded that she be removed from the Conclave, thus proclaiming the newly formed institution's stance on the matter._
    **If Geralt chooses not to rescue Triss during Chapter III:**

    _Their protests were for naught – Carduin and the other mages were arrested in connection with the accusations against the[Lodge of Sorceresses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses")._

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Carduin** can appear once more depending on dialogue choices during [Imperial Audience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Audience "Imperial Audience"). If Geralt tells General [Morvran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvran_Voorhis "Morvran Voorhis") that he had to save Triss, then you can meet him in Oxenfurt near Radovid's ship [HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/HMS_Oxenfurt-Tretogor "HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor"). 

### Journal entry

    _Carduin of Kovir was once a wealthy and influential member of the Council of Sorcerers, a man known and respected by all the rulers of the[North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"). After the Council imploded during the coup on Thanedd Isle, Carduin spared no expense in his quest to rebuild his brotherhood's hard-won position._
    _This motivated him to take part in the eventful summit in Loc Muinne, during which a handful of mages seeking to reactivate the Council were forced to accept[King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") Radovid's patronage. After that Carduin found himself on the king's leash, completely dependent on the whims and moods of a mad ruler whom he served not out of respect but fear. The mage's pride, however, forced him to don a brave face and play out the hand fate had dealt him._
