## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Egill** aka the "Taciturn Druid" was a [druid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") who lived in a grotto under the sacred tree [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). By [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") he hadn't spoken in three years as he'd taken a vow of silence. 

_If Geralt "cures" him:_ [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), tricked into thinking it wasn't selective mutism, did all he could to scare or irritate the druid until, out of anger, Egill yelled at the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") before stopping mid-sentence, realizing he'd broken his vow. He then told Geralt and the trickster off.
