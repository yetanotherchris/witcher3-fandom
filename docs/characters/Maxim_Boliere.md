## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Maxim Boliere** is an actor residing in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He belongs to the troupe of [the Butcher's Yard theater](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Butcher%27s_Yard_theater "The Butcher's Yard theater"). Maxim has a drinking problem and is usually seen with a bottle in his hand outside the theater. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can choose either him or [Abelard Rizza](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abelard_Rizza "Abelard Rizza") to play the role of the [doppler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler "Doppler") during the quest [The Play's the Thing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Play%27s_the_Thing "The Play's the Thing").
