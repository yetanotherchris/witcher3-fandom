## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Dilvyn** was a regular soldier in [Imperial Army of Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Army_of_Nilfgaard "Imperial Army of Nilfgaard") about to be lynched by some farmers. [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") had the option to let the peasants proceed or to intervene and save the man. If Geralt left the man to his fate, he could loot his corpse and retrieve a [letter from his wife](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_Nilfgaardian%27s_wife "Letter from Nilfgaardian's wife").
