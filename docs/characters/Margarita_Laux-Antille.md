**Margarita Laux-Antille** was a [sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorceress "Sorceress") and a rector of [Aretuza](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aretuza "Aretuza"). She was also the granddaughter of the famous [Ilona Laux-Antille](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ilona_Laux-Antille "Ilona Laux-Antille") and a founding member of the [Lodge of Sorceresses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses"). She had no interest in politics, but she cared deeply for her school and students.[1][2]

She had a figure outshining statues, fair-haired, tall, and often wore a green dress with a plunging neckline.[1]

Margarita Laux-Antille, a sorceress and rector of Aretuza, was a founding member of the Lodge of Sorceresses. Her involvement in the Lodge was driven by her dedication to her school rather than political interest. She co-authored 'Masters of Magic on Curses – Selected Writings' with Tissaia de Vries. Following a conspiracy that resulted in the deaths of King Demavend and Foltest, she was sentenced to death with other Lodge members.

## Biography

### Relationship with Lars

In [1263](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1263 "Timeline"), Margarita met [Lars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lars "Lars"), and the two quickly developed a passionate relationship. For four years, their connection slowly dissolved and Margarita felt isolated in her thoughts. This, and his ongoing marriage to another woman, led Margarita to finally end things. Later, Tissaia assumed that this humiliating break up may have been the reason why Margarita would chose not to attend the banquet at Aretuza.[1]

### Meeting Ciri

On June 30, [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1267 "Timeline"), after moving the adepts to different sleeping areas due to needing the dormitories for the upcoming Grand Mage's Conclave, several students escaped to the [main city](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gors_Velen "Gors Velen") to indulge in its delights. Margarita and [Tissaia de Vries](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tissaia_de_Vries "Tissaia de Vries") searched the city, detecting a [magical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") interference nearby. They found [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") and [Fabio Sachs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fabio_Sachs "Fabio Sachs") hiding from an angry crowd; and believing she was one of the missing students, Tissaia put her under a paralysis spell while Margarita held off Fabio. They took the young clerk's advice and carried the girl to [Giancardi Bank](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giancardi_Bank "Giancardi Bank"), where [Yennefer of Vengerberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") proved Ciri's identity, followed by constant apologies from the rectors. They all, save Fabio, went back to [The Silver Heron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Silver_Heron "The Silver Heron") inn, and bathed for several hours, until Ciri decided to flee to [Hirundum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hirundum "Hirundum"), followed by Yennefer.[1]

### Fall of the Brotherhood

The following day, the whole [Brotherhood of Sorcerers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brotherhood_of_Sorcerers "Brotherhood of Sorcerers") met on [Thanedd Island](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thanedd_Island "Thanedd Island") but that evening, a large-scale [coup](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thanedd_coup "Thanedd coup") took place between the mages allied with the [North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms") and those with [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"), resulting in numerous mages being killed or captured, and spelling the end of the Brotherhood.[1] Despite remaining neutral, with mages now distrusted by many of the rulers, Margarita escaped to [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart")'s castle, [Montecalvo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Montecalvo "Montecalvo"), where she, alongside [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") and [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"), attempted to assist [Queen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen "Queen") [Hedwig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hedwig_of_Malleore "Hedwig of Malleore") to regain control of [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania").[2]

### Formation of the Lodge

On August 5th, the four used a [projection](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Telecommunicator "Telecommunicator") spell to begin their first true meeting, mainly around recruitment and their purpose, summoning [Assire var Anahid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Assire_var_Anahid "Assire var Anahid"), [Sabrina Glevissig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sabrina_Glevissig "Sabrina Glevissig"), [Sheala de Tancarville](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sheala_de_Tancarville "Sheala de Tancarville"), and [Francesca Findabair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francesca_Findabair "Francesca Findabair"). Despite initial disagreements over the events of Thanedd, they agreed to work together to protect the safe practice of [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic"). Backed by Margarita's evidence of women performing better with magic (based off comparing pupils from Aretuza and [Ban Ard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ban_Ard "Ban Ard")), they agreed women would only be allowed to join.[2]

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

Margarita Laux-Antille is mentioned by ambassador [Shilard Fitz-Oesterlen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shilard_Fitz-Oesterlen "Shilard Fitz-Oesterlen"), as one of the sorceresses accused to be a member of the conspiracy, which murdered both [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Demavend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Demavend_III "Demavend III") and [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest"). She is also sentenced to death, as were the other members of the Lodge of Sorceresses. 

She is also mentioned by Geralt as co-author of the compendium _Masters of Magic on Curses – Selected Writings_ , with Tissaia de Vries. 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

After the massacre at [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne"), Margarita, along with the other Northern Lodge sorceresses, went into hiding. However, she still kept in contact with Philippa and the two discussed a plan in hopes to reform the Lodge. However, those plans were put on hold. Fearing [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") would inevitably attack Aretuza, Margarita and her students went to Novigrad, hearing Triss was organizing an escape for mages to [Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir"). Sadly, she and her students were captured and imprisoned, and her pupils were burned one by one, with Radovid saving Margarita for last. Luckily, she was rescued by Geralt and Yennefer before her execution. 

### Journal entry

    _The full list of Margarita Laux-Antille's outstanding mental, spiritual and physical attributes would consume a mountain of parchment. Rita, as her friends called her, once held the position of rector at the Aretuza Academy for Sorceresses on the Isle of Thanedd - the same school attended by her famous ancestor, Ilona Laux-Antille._
    _Quite unusually for one of her profession, Margarita showed no interest in politics - it was only care for the good of her school that led her to join the Lodge of Sorceresses._
    _Finally, her beauty deserves mention as well. It was said that not even the marble likenesses of goddesses and nymphs chiseled out by the greatest sculptors could rival this sorceress's figure._
    _Yet with all her astuteness, Laux-Antille still could not escape the same fate that so many of her fellows met. She had been arrested some time before our tale begins and was staying in the Oxenfurt prison, awaiting a summary and pre-determined trial._
    _Only Geralt and Yennefer's daring jailbreak saved Margarita from certain execution._
