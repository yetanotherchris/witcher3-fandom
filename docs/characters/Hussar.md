## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hussar** was a [dog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog") that belonged to [Bastien Vildenvert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastien_Vildenvert "Bastien Vildenvert"). When Bastien went missing during the war, [Dune Vildenvert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dune_Vildenvert "Dune Vildenvert"), Bastien's brother, took care of him. 

_If Geralt helps Dune find Bastien:_ Hussar helped the men search the battlefield near [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), eventually catching the scent of his owner and led them to an abandoned hut in the nearby woods. There, Bastien was badly wounded but alive, along with a [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soldier named [Rhosyn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rhosyn "Rhosyn"). Hussar, now reunited with his owner, return to the family's [farm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildenvert%27s_farm "Vildenvert's farm") just east of [White Orchard inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_inn "White Orchard inn").
