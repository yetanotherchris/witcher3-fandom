The **merchant** from [Claywich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claywich "Claywich") does not appear there until he is freed from the [Bandits' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandits%27_Camp "Bandits' Camp") located on a large island to the east of [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton"). Initially, he is marked on the map as a [person in distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_distress "Person\(s\) in distress"). Once freed, the merchant will return to his home in Claywich, where he rewards [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") with 6 bottles of [dwarven spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarven_spirit "Dwarven spirit") when first visited, and plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"). He sells five gwent cards, [rugged saddlebags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rugged_saddlebags "Rugged saddlebags") and a small variety of alcohol, [food](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink") and [junk items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk").
