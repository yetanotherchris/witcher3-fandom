## Appearance(s)

### Games

**Eryka** was a blonde [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") woman who sometimed watched fistfights organized in front of [The New Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port"). Among folks of [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") she is considered beauty and [Valgard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Valgard "Valgard") is trying to win her heart.
