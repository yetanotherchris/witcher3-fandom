## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Joachim von Gratz** was chief surgeon of [Vilmerius Hospital](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vilmerius_Hospital "Vilmerius Hospital") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

## Biography

In his past he studied at [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"), where he even became member of staff but later, after failed [uprising](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Revolution "Oxenfurt Revolution"), left for the free city. He later spent a year in the dungeons of [Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tretogor "Tretogor") and even took part in the [Battle of Brenna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Brenna "Battle of Brenna"). 

## Journal entry

    _We met Joachim von Gratz, chief surgeon at Vilmerius Hospital, in very unfortunate circumstances. My[Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla") had been attacked and he was tending to her wounds._
    _I must admit I was so shaken I hardly remember our first meeting, save that he was a cool-headed doctor who seemed to know a great deal about the previous murders and offered his help investigating the matter._
    _It seemed the surgeon was no stranger to violence, for he bore a formidable scar worthy of a veteran of the[Battle of Brenna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Brenna "Battle of Brenna")._
    _Geralt also noted that Von Gratz seemed quite experienced at navigating the[sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_sewers "Novigrad sewers") and fighting the monsters dwelling within it._
    _It was hard not to notice that Joachim von Gratz did not care much for the city coroner,[Hubert Rejk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk"), who dropped in unexpectedly on Geralt and the doctor at the morgue._
    **If Geralt didn't catch the real murderer:**

    _In the end von Gratz was eliminated as a suspect, but like Geralt he had to admit defeat in his attempts to outwit the psychopathic murderer._
