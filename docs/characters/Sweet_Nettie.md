## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Sweet Nettie** was a [prostitute](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prostitute "Prostitute") working at [Crippled Kate's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crippled_Kate%27s "Crippled Kate's") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

_If Geralt investigates into[Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla")'s attacker:_ Sweet Nettie's services were bought by [Nathaniel Pastodi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nathaniel_Pastodi "Nathaniel Pastodi"), who asked her if she'd do anything and she responded yes, for the right price. However, this turned out not to be a question about sex. Nathaniel tied and gagged her before he began to torture her with a hot [poker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Poker_\(weapon\) "Poker \(weapon\)") when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") burst in, intent on finding and putting a stop to the serial killer in the area.
