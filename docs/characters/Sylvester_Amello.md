## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Sylvester Amello** was a merchant in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). At that time, he was troubled by a so-called "[Imp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imp "Imp")" whom the merchant posted a contract on after it stole from his stall. Eventually, [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") responded to the notice, and Sylvester bade him to deal with the imp problem. 

If Geralt brought him the Imp's head, Sylvester paid the witcher the agreed upon amount; otherwise, he only paid half.
