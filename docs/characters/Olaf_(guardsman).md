## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Olaf** was a [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") member that was in charge of his clan's armory in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). However, during this time the clan's ancestral sword, [Kuliu](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kuliu "Kuliu"), went missing which, if it wasn't recovered, he'd have to pay back with his own blood.
