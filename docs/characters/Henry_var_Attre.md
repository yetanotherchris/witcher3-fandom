**Henry**[1]**var Attre** was a noble who served as a diplomat to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") for thirteen years, first as consul and later as [ambassador](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ambassador "Ambassador"). He lived there in his [Var Attre Villa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Var_Attre_Villa "Var Attre Villa") in the rich [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district. He informed [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") of the political situation in the [Northern Kingdoms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms") during the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")'s visit to [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Palace_\(Vizima\) "Royal Palace \(Vizima\)"). He was the father of both [Edna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Edna_var_Attre "Edna var Attre") and [Rosa var Attre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosa_var_Attre "Rosa var Attre"). 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**Who are the children of Henry var Attre?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Henry var Attre, the Nilfgaardian ambassador, has two children: Edna and Rosa var Attre. Both daughters are part of the var Attre family and appear in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is the significance of the Var Attre Villa in Gildorf?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Var Attre Villa in Gildorf, Novigrad, is the home of Nilfgaardian ambassador Henry var Attre. It plays a key role in diplomatic activities for Nilfgaard in Redania. The villa is featured in quests like 'Broken Flowers' and 'Fencing Lessons' in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How long did Henry var Attre serve as a diplomat in Novigrad?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Henry var Attre was a diplomat in Novigrad for thirteen years, starting as consul and later becoming ambassador for the Nilfgaardian Empire. He lived in the Var Attre Villa in the Gildorf district. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What role did Henry var Attre play in Novigrad's politics?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Henry var Attre was the Nilfgaardian ambassador in Novigrad, significantly influencing its politics. He reported on the political climate, noting King Radovid's plans and the power held by local crime lords. Var Attre resided in the Var Attre Villa in Gildorf, providing insights to the Nilfgaardian Empire about the Northern Kingdoms. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How is Duke var Attre related to Henry var Attre?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Duke var Attre is related to Henry var Attre. Duke var Attre commanded the 7th Ymlats Infantry Regiment and was dispatched to Skellige in 1272 by Emperor Emhyr var Emreis. He is also the father of Edna and Rosa var Attre. 

Provided by: Fandom
