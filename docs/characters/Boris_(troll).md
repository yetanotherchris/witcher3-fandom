## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Boris** was a [rock troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rock_troll "Rock troll") living in [Mudplough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mudplough "Mudplough"), [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). The area was also teeming with [nekkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker") and so the troll put to good use one his favored recipes, nekker soup, which the [ogroids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ogroid "Ogroid") didn't take to very well and started to attack the rock troll in retaliation. 

_If Geralt intervenes:_ deciding to help the outnumbered rock troll, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") killed off the attacking nekkers, for which Boris was grateful and gave the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") a reward. After explaining he was cooking nekker soup, the witcher then told the troll to refrain from cooking any more of them to prevent future attacks.
