## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Griswold** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") merchant who worked in conjunction with his business partner [Anselm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anselm_\(merchant\) "Anselm \(merchant\)"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1272 "Timeline"), the two traveled to [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") from an unspecified place to trade with [Phillip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger"), the Bloody Baron, having deemed the venture worthwhile due to the lack of competition in the area. The merchants hired a number of former [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") soldiers as guards to keep them safe in the war-torn lands of Velen, however, the guards themselves turned on the merchants after the group were out of sight of any settlements. [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") encountered the bandit guards attacking Anselm and Griswold on the road south of [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale"). 

_If Geralt saved the merchants:_ Anselm and Griswold completed their plan by traveling to [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), and began trading with the locals there, out of a stall near the main gate.
