## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Sverre** was a [Clan Heymaey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Heymaey "Clan Heymaey") warrior who cobbled a crew together to try to deal with the [beast on the High Road](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Monster_on_the_High_Road "Contract: Monster on the High Road"). However, they were unprepared for such a beast that could lay ambushes and only 4 of the men survived the attack, including Sverre. Despite this, the survivors were set on trying to go back for their fallen comrades to at least give them a proper burial.
