## Appearance(s)

### Games

**Travik** was one of the [orphans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orphans "Orphans") that [Gran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gran "Gran") looked after in a small village in [Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

## Biography

Travik used to live with his parents somewhere in the [Northern Kingdoms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms") when [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") invaded. On that day, he'd been hunting for berries and on the way home he heard the soldiers kill his parents. 

He later ended up in the care of Gran in [Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crookback_Bog "Crookback Bog"), as did many other children orphaned by the war. There, he made friends with a [godling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Godling "Godling") named [Johnny](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Johnny "Johnny"), whom he directed [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to when the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") was searching for [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

_If Geralt freed[the Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree"):_ Travik, along with all the other orphans from Crookback Bog, was saved by the Ghost from the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"). The orphans later ended up in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and were placed under the care of [Marabella](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marabella "Marabella") at [her school](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marabella%27s_School_for_Tots "Marabella's School for Tots"). 

_If Geralt killed the Ghost in the Tree:_ Travik, along with all the other orphans from Crookback Bog, was taken to [Aard Cerbin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") by the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"), where he was killed and consumed by them.

## Notes

  * Out of all the orphans, he's considered the closest to Johnny and likes to eat snails with the godling. He's also unafraid of the woods, like the rest of the children are, though [Genny](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Genny "Genny") will comment he's not brave, he's just stupid, as one time he sat his bare butt down on an anthill.
