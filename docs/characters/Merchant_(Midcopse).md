\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant** in [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse") plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Monster deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"), and sells a variety of miscellaneous items, as well as four gwent cards. 

## Notes

  * He boasts a particularly pathetic monster deck where nearly all of his muster cards are impaired. Similar if not the same as the [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren") [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Downwarren\) "Merchant \(Downwarren\)"), Making him an easy source of income at the beginning of the game. However, unlike the latter he is not killed off later in the game.
