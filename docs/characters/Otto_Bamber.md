**Otto Bamber** is a [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") residing at his [hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut") outside [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). He plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck") and gives a random, non-unique card if beaten. 

He sells many [alchemical formulae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") and [ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"), including [rotfiend blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend_blood "Rotfiend blood") and [nekker warrior's liver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior%27s_liver "Nekker warrior's liver"), both of which are otherwise only available as [monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") loot. 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Bamber accommodated [Ewald Borsodi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ewald_Borsodi "Ewald Borsodi") and his associates in a hidden cellar under his hut, while the group planned a heist on the [Borsodi Brothers' Auction House](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Borsodi_Brothers%27_Auction_House "Borsodi Brothers' Auction House"). During this time, the alchemist was also accosted by [Fallen Knights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fallen_Knight "Fallen Knight"), though he was protected from them by [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"). 

In the same year, Bamber's apprentice [Folkert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Folkert "Folkert"), whom Bamber sent to [Deadwight Wood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deadwight_Wood "Deadwight Wood") to collect [han fiber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Han_fiber "Han fiber"), went missing. Bamber then posted a [notice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Apprentice_Gone_Missing! "Contract: Apprentice Gone Missing!") on [Brunwich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brunwich "Brunwich")'s [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Brunwich\) "Notice board \(Brunwich\)") to appeal for someone to search for Folkert. Geralt of Rivia responded to the notice, and searched for the missing apprentice near the village of [Erde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erde "Erde").[1]

_If Geralt spared the couple but told the truth to Otto:_ Bamber hired a group of mercenaries to kill the elderly couple who cannibalized Folkert, regardless of whether or not Folkert was already killed by wolves beforehand.
