This **merchant** is located in an alleyway at the northern end of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") district. He sells lesser [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph"), [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"), and a few books and alchemy ingredients. However, he won't be available unless [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") covers for him when approached by [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter").
