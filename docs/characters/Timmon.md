## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Timmon** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") living in [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken") on [Faroe Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). He was engaged to [Agda](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Agda "Agda") and they were to be married once she arrived in Harviken. 

## Biography

While he prepared for his wedding, his brothers, [Ulf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulf_\(Timmon%27s_brother\) "Ulf \(Timmon's brother\)") and [Mikkel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikkel_\(Skelliger\) "Mikkel \(Skelliger\)"), were sent to pick up Agda and bring her to Faroe. Unfortunately, a storm hit during their trip back and Agda drowned. Rather than face Timmon over their disgrace at failing to protect Agda, his brothers then committed suicide to maintain their honor.
