## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**La Valette Guard Captain** was an old and experienced soldier who served [Baroness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baroness "Baroness") [Maria Louisa La Valette](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maria_Louisa_La_Valette "Maria Louisa La Valette") as commander of her guard around her [villa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baroness_la_Valette%27s_Villa "Baroness la Valette's Villa") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he met [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") when the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") came looking for Maria Louisa. While he originally objected to their meet, Maria and [Morvran Voorhis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvran_Voorhis "Morvran Voorhis") came out of their own accord to meet Geralt.
