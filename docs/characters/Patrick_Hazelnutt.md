### Games

**Patrick Hazelnutt** was a resident of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), and a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player. 

## Biography

In 1272, Hazelnutt participated in the gwent tournament at the [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora") organized by Count [Tybalt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Tybalt_\(Novigrad\) "Count Tybalt \(Novigrad\)"). He was eliminated in the first round after playing against [Harald Featherbuns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harald_Featherbuns "Harald Featherbuns"). Hazelnutt was accompanied at all times by three female paramours, whom, it was said, he is never seen in public without.
