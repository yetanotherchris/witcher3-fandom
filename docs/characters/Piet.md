## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Piet** is a minor character and [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") Soldier who appears in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ in the end of mission, The Great Escape. He is a member of the Town Patrol of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and assigned to [Deireadh prison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deireadh_prison "Deireadh prison"). He is one of numerous guards tasked with safeguarding outer ring of prison from any possible intruders. Even though he is not fond of [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage"), he feels pity for sorceresses and sorcerers tortured by a [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") inside of the prison because, as he says, at the end they are also humans.
