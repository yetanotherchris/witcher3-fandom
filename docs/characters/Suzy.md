## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Suzy** was a [prostitute](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prostitute "Prostitute") working at [Crippled Kate's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crippled_Kate%27s "Crippled Kate's") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). If approached, she initially tried to be somewhat seductive, stating she was under a spell and asking if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") came there to kiss her to break it, but when asked to reveal anything about herself she refuses. 

## Notes

  * Note that employing the services of strumpets at Crippled Kate's will never result in any backlash from either [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") or [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") and will not affect any of the romance endings Geralt can receive.
