## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Leif** was the father of [Kori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kori "Kori") and [Kraki](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kraki "Kraki") and part of [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). He was considered to be a troublemaker who detested visitors from the continent, a view he made sure to pass on to his sons. 

_If Kori and Kraki are killed:_ despite his sons being troublemakers themselves and having instigated the deadly fight that cost them their lives, Leif demanded that the lone survivor, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), be tried by their jarl, [Madman Lugos](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Madman_Lugos "Madman Lugos"), for his sons' deaths.
