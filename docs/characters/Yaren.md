## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Yaren** was a [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") man who was killed by the [King of the Wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_the_Wolves_\(creature\) "King of the Wolves \(creature\)") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") and [Gretka](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gretka "Gretka") encountered his corpse while searching for the beast. He had been torn about and thrown against a tree. Ciri was only able to tell that the beast was a [werewolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") after inspecting Yaren's corpse.
