\- Herbalist  
  
  
The **herbalist** in [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") village is a [gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players"), using the [Nilfgaardian Empire deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck"). He also sells two rare books, one of which can only be bought from him, and some [alchemy formulae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae") including the [recipe for drowner pheromones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manuscript_page:_Drowner_pheromones "Manuscript page: Drowner pheromones").
