**Iron-Arse Hans** was a [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") racer outside [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") and, by his appearance, a mercenary. His horse was called Beecher. 

No matter what order he's raced against, he will give [saddlebags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Saddlebags "Saddlebags") and 30 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") when beaten. Sometimes when racing him an obstacle (loose barrels or a log) may appear.
