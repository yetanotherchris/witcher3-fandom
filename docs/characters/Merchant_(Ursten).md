## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** has situated himself by the [grindstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grindstone "Grindstone") in the northern end of [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten"). Somewhat unusually for a non-moving merchant, his appearance is allowed to change between visits. 

Presumably taking advantage of the [Redanian troops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") nearby to do business with them, he sells [armors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_armor "The Witcher 3 armor"), lesser [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph"), [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") and [components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") \- anything that a generic [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") would sell, and at the same pricing, despite technically being in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") territory.
