\- Merchant  
  
  
The **merchant** plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck") and awards a random, non-unique card if beaten. Despite his greeting line, he doesn't have any milk or eggs in stock, but he does sell a few [food and drink](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink") items, and strong alcohols used in alchemy. 

He eventually disappears from the village after the quest [The Whispering Hillock](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Whispering_Hillock "The Whispering Hillock"), regardless of how [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") deals with [the Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree"). 

## Notes

  * He boasts a particularly pathetic Monsters gwent deck where nearly all of his muster cards are impaired, except for the [nekker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_\(gwent_card\) "Nekker \(gwent card\)") of which he has 2 instead of 3. That makes him an easy source of income at the beginning of the game.
