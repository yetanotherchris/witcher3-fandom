The **Grossbart Brothers** were a trio of [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") brothers who murdered, raped, and burned down homes so severely a bounty was put out on them in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), drawing the attention of [Djenge Frett](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Djenge_Frett "Djenge Frett"), a [bounty hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") in the area. At the time, the three were camping out in the [Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grotto_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Grotto \(Ard Skellig\)") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig").

## Trivia

  * They are most likely a reference to the book _[The Sad Tale of the Brothers Grossbart](https://books.google.com.ph/books/about/The_Sad_Tale_of_the_Brothers_Grossbart.html?id=MBL0lwEACAAJ)_ by [Jesse Bullington](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jesse_Bullington "wikipedia:Jesse Bullington"), in which a pair of ne'er-do-well brothers traverse medieval Europe and encounter witches, demons, and other supernatural monsters in their search for treasure.
