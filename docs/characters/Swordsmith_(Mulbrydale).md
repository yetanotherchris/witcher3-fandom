The **swordsmith** in [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale") is a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player, using the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"), and despite his misleading name, is an amateur [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer"). If freed from his captors at [Swamp Bint Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_Bint_Harbor "Swamp Bint Harbor"), he'll head back home and will then offer his services to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"). 

## Biography

At some point, he employed the [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") [Fergus Graem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fergus_Graem "Fergus Graem") and his apprentice [Yoana](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yoana "Yoana"), though they later moved to set up shop at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he was kidnapped and held hostage at [Peet's Landing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peet%27s_Landing "Peet's Landing") by [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") deserters and bandits.

## Notes

  * Like some other [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") who can be rescued, he says that he'll have a reward for Geralt, but that's only dialogue and he won't give anything.
  * After rescuing him, a couple notices can be found in Mulbrydale thanking Geralt for rescuing their people (like on the workshop near his home and on a house on the western side of the village).

## Bugs

  * There's a known bug where he'll appear next to his workbench but be unavailable to talk with. A save and reload of the area, or simply meditating, fixes this.
  * When first beaten in gwent, he may pay out with crafting materials even if there are still non-unique cards remaining to be awarded.
