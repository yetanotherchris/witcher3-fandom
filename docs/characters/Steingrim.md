## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Steingrim** was the first [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skelliger "Skelliger") that [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") met after washing up on the shore of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), the largest of the [Skellige Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). He had a companion, a wolf named Trofast. 

He made a living by scavenging off of dead bodies that washed up on the shore, though he made the mistake of trying this on Geralt and was taken by surprise as a result. 

He is one of the many victims who died at the hand of the [berserkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berserker_\(creature\) "Berserker \(creature\)") transformation in [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") during the feast.
