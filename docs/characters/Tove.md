## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Tove** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") who lived around [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). She'd once been married and had a son, but her husband was attacked one night out on the cliffs while trying to find their son, who'd sadly met the same fate, dying at the hands of a strong [water hag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag"), [Abaya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abaya "Abaya").
