## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Holofernes Meiersdorf** was a [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") that ran the successful [Honeyfill Meadworks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks") east of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") and was the father of 4 sons: Franklin, Bernie, Hugo, and [Rico](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rico_Meiersdorf "Rico Meiersdorf"). However, the last one decided to seek out his own path and left for Novigrad and began to work for the [King of Beggars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam") but Holofernes held out hope that Rico would one day return. 

However, his bee business ran into a problem when something they called [The Apiarian Phantom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Apiarian_Phantom "The Apiarian Phantom") was destroying bee hives and had killed a farmhand. If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") helps him out, he'll learn that it was a lone [Wild Hunt hound](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt "Hound of the Wild Hunt").
