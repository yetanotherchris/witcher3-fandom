## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Kobe** was a [nordling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nordling "Nordling") living around [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") that, like many, didn't take kindly to [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") waging war with them. 

_If[Fencing Lessons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fencing_Lessons "Fencing Lessons") is done:_ He and [Lucas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lucas "Lucas") were caught threatening [Rosa var Attre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosa_var_Attre "Rosa var Attre") but [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") interfered before they could act on their plans. 

    _If Geralt attacks fights them:_ Unfortunately, Kobe and Lucas tried to attack the witcher, leading to their deaths.
    _If Geralt gets them to leave:_ Kobe and Lucas backed down and walked off. However, Rosa swore she'd remember their names and one day track them down if Nilfgaard won.
