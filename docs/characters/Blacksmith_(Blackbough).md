\- Blacksmith  
  
  
This **Blacksmith** is a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player, using the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck"). He sells [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting components"), [weapons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_weapons "Category:The Witcher 3 weapons") and lesser [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), as well as the [diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") for them. He also sells [Adalbert Kermith's second map](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Adalbert_Kermith%27s_second_map "Adalbert Kermith's second map").
