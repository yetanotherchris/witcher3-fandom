**Ronvid of the Small Marsh** was a very inept yet persistent young [knight](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Knight "Knight") intent on defeating 100 knights to prove his honor for Maid Bilberry in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**Who is Maid Bilberry that Ronvid of the Small Marsh is trying to prove his honor for?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Maid Bilberry is the individual whom Ronvid of the Small Marsh, a self-styled knight, seeks to honor. Her existence and location remain uncertain. Ronvid is committed to besting 100 knights in her name, traversing Velen and extolling her virtues of beauty, wisdom, empathy, and charm. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Why does Ronvid of the Small Marsh want to defeat 100 knights?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In 1272, the young knight Ronvid of the Small Marsh aimed to defeat 100 knights. His goal was to demonstrate his honor for Maid Bilberry. He initiated his mission near Crow's Perch, daring anyone he met to a duel. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What happens when Geralt encounters Ronvid of the Small Marsh?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Ronvid of the Small Marsh, a persistent knight, repeatedly challenges Geralt to duels to honor Maid Bilberry. Despite his repeated defeats, he persists, even hiring thugs to aid him. After his third defeat, he finally accepts Geralt's advice to stop his challenges. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How does Ronvid of the Small Marsh react after being defeated by Geralt?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Upon his defeat by Geralt, Ronvid of the Small Marsh recruits thugs to balance the fight. Despite this, he is defeated once more. Recognizing Geralt as a witcher, Ronvid's subsequent actions depend on Geralt's. He either ceases his attempts to fight Geralt or persists in his efforts to overcome the witcher. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Where does Ronvid of the Small Marsh first appear on his mission?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The mission of Ronvid of the Small Marsh, an aspiring knight, commenced outside Crow's Perch. His objective was to triumph over 100 knights in duels to demonstrate his honor for his beloved, Maid Bilberry. 

Provided by: Fandom 

## Biography

To begin his mission, Ronvid appeared just outside [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), trying to dare anyone that crossed his path to a duel. 

_If Geralt runs into him all 3 times:_ Not paying attention to what [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") was, Ronvid forced the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") to fight him but was easily overpowered. He then vowed he'd defeat Geralt later before heading off and eventually ended up just outside [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Geralt happened to cross his path again and this time Ronvid had a shield and a little bit more armor. However, this didn't help in the least and Ronvid was defeated yet again. After this, Ronvid finally realized Geralt was a witcher and proceeded to hire a couple thugs to try and even the playing field, eventually running into the witcher a third time within the [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") district. 

    _If Geralt uses[Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") on them:_ Now calmed down, Ronvid decided to finally take the witcher's advice and stopped trying to fight him before heading off to sit along the piers.
    _If Geralt doesn't use Axii:_ Unswayed by trying to face off against a witcher with 2 thugs with him, Ronvid sadly met his end in Novigrad, having not even defeated one knight.

## Notes

  * The difficulty of the fight is elevated with each encounter. He starts off with nothing but a blade. He later appears bearing a shield, while boasting about his training with it. Finally, the knight recruits two allies to assist him.
  * If Ronvid is killed, a document titled [To the Most Beautiful Woman in the World](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/To_the_Most_Beautiful_Woman_in_the_World "To the Most Beautiful Woman in the World") may be looted off his body.
  * A girl named Bilberry is mentioned to have died on a post made on a [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Notice board \(Crow's Perch\)"). The notice says that Bilberry was only 4 years old, however it's unclear if this has any relation to Ronvid, although this seems very unlikely.
  * Ronvid could be based on another character from the franchise, the [Chevron Knight](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chevron_Knight "Chevron Knight").
