## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Bernard Tulle** was a [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") and according to him, a mushrooms farmer working for the [Zerrikanian Spice Company](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanian_Spice_Company "Zerrikanian Spice Company") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

He participated in the [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") tournament of the [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora") using the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"). His skills in this particular card game were however quite ridiculous, but it sort of made sense: Bernard was not at all a simple spices merchant; indeed, he was a professional thief who entered the tournament just to steal the 9000 [Crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") prize.
