# Merchant (Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp)

There are three identically labeled **merchants** at the [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp"), in addition to Quartermaster [Tavar Eggebracht](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tavar_Eggebracht "Tavar Eggebracht"). They're all quite close to each other, near the many tirelessly working smiths, directly north of the camp's [fast travel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") signpost.
