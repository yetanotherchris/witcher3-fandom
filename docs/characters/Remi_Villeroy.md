**Remi Villeroy** was a [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") from the [Northern Kingdoms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"), who went into hiding in the [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") with his wife, Louisa, and his adoptive son. 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), after the [witch hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunts "Witch hunts") broke out, he went on the run from the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter"). Officially, they wanted him for contacting the [Lodge of Sorceresses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses") via a [megascope](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Megascope "Megascope"), and poisoning the minds of [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldiers. 

If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") intervened and helped Remi and his family, the mage thanked him and expressed a desire to give up magic before rewarding Geralt with [Perun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Perun_runestone "Perun runestone"), [Veles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Veles_runestone "Veles runestone"), and [Chernobog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chernobog_runestone "Chernobog runestone") [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"). 

If Remi was saved, he will later appear in [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never").
