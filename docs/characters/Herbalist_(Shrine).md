## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed **herbalist** set up shop by the roadside shrine east of the [abandoned village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Village_\(White_Orchard\) "Abandoned Village \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), only if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") dispatched the [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") there prior. 

## Notes

  * Due to a bug, when you first clear the ghouls, the cutscene for completion, and the herbalist's arrival, will not trigger until you [meditate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meditation "Meditation"), spawning the final hidden ghoul.
