## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Master Claytop** was a [fistfight](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_fistfighting "The Witcher 3 fistfighting") organizer at [the Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

## Biography

_If Geralt participates in Novigrad's fistfighting:_ When [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") sought to become the Golden Sturgeon's fistfighting champion, Claytop pit him against [Georgius Georg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Georgius_Georg "Georgius Georg"), but not before making a proposition that were Geralt to throw the fight, he would receive a reward greater than that if he won. 

    _If Geralt agrees and throws the match:_ True to his word, Claytop rewarded Geralt with a nice amount of [coin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Currency "Currency").
    _If Geralt declines or accepts but then wins the match anyways:_ Claytop told Geralt that he would "regret it", before letting the fight proceed under normal rules.

## Associated quest

  * [Fists of Fury: Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fists_of_Fury:_Novigrad "Fists of Fury: Novigrad")

## Notes

  * Choosing to not throw the match will cause a few bandits to appear in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") later on that will attack you.

## Bugs

  * Claytops bandits may appear even if you threw the first match.

## Trivia

  * Master Claytop's name and criminal rigging of his boxing is in reference to Bricktop from the film Snatch, who similarly has boxing matches fixed. His boxer, [Georgius Georg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Georgius_Georg "Georgius Georg"), is also a reference to the film.
