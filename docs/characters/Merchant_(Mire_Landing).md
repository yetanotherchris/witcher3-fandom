## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** arrives in [Mire Landing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mire_Landing "Mire Landing") (also known as Cestersover) after the village is cleared of bandits. Like other [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") in liberated [abandoned sites](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), his appearance may change and sells the same things as [wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Pontar\) "Wandering merchant \(Pontar\)").
