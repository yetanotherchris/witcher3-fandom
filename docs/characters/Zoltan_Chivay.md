**Zoltan Chivay** was a veteran of the [Second Nilfgaard War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_II "Northern War II") and a friend of the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"). They first met when Geralt and [Zoltan's company](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay%27s_company "Zoltan Chivay's company") were going towards the [Yaruga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yaruga "Yaruga") [river](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rivers "Rivers") from [Brokilon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brokilon "Brokilon"), but the [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") advised them to join his company and go eastwards. It is from Zoltan that Geralt got his sword - a [sihil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sihil "Sihil").[1]

After the [Battle of Brenna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Brenna "Battle of Brenna"), where him and his mates killed Field-Marshal [Menno Coehoorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Menno_Coehoorn "Menno Coehoorn"), Zoltan got engaged to [Eudora Breckenriggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eudora_Breckenriggs "Eudora Breckenriggs").[2]

He also had a talking parrot, [Field Marshal Windbag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Field_Marshal_Windbag "Field Marshal Windbag"), which he sold to the [gnome](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnome "Gnome") [Percival Schuttenbach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Percival_Schuttenbach "Percival Schuttenbach") when he started a jeweler's workshop in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") following the war.[2]

## Biography

### Forming a company

When the Second Northern War erupted in July [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1267 "Timeline"), the dwarves, Chivay, [Munro Bruys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Munro_Bruys "Munro Bruys"), [Caleb Stratton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Stratton "Caleb Stratton"), [Yazon Varda](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yazon_Varda "Yazon Varda") and [Figgis Merluzzo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Figgis_Merluzzo "Figgis Merluzzo"), along with a gnome named Percival Schuttenbach and a green parrot named Field Marshal Windbag, formed a company to escape from the enemy front and reach [Sodden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sodden "Sodden"), passing through the [Turlough Hills](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Turlough_Hills "Turlough Hills"), the river [Chotla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chotla "Chotla") and the [Ina](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ina "Ina"). 

Stopping in [Dillingen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dillingen "Dillingen"), the group waylaid and robbed a rich [hawker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hawker "Hawker") who tried to flee the town after the townsfolk exposed his trade with the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael"). The [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant "Merchant") defended his property like a lion, shouting for aid several times before the dwarves attacked him with batons. Intending to fund future enterprises of each individual with the treasure, the group helped load the trunks on a wagon and set off into the forest. During their trip forward, they met some refugees from [Kernow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kernow "Kernow") (only women and children), and they decided to help them and travel with them.[1]

### Meeting Geralt

In early August, three days after joining the refugees, they found the witcher Geralt of Rivia with his friends [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") and [Milva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Milva "Milva"), and told them of the dwarves' plan to travel to the [Turlough Hills](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Turlough_Hills "Turlough Hills"), then the [Old Road](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Road "Old Road") to the river [Chotla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chotla "Chotla") in [Sodden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sodden "Sodden"), before continuing onto the [Ina](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ina "Ina"). Zoltan invited the three wanderers to join them on their march east, which was accepted.[1]

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Zoltan Chivay in The Witcher](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/49/People_Zoltan_full.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/165?cb=20080924091935)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/49/People_Zoltan_full.png/revision/latest?cb=20080924091935 "Zoltan Chivay in The Witcher")Zoltan Chivay in The Witcher

Geralt first meets Zoltan in the [Outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts "Outskirts") of [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), surrounded by thugs. After giving them a sound beating, the dwarf is surprised and very happy to see Geralt, whom he claims to have witnessed dying five years earlier with his very own eyes. 

During the ensuing chat, the dwarf also mentions that his in-laws-to-be, the Breckenriggs (this name is spelled in a few different and interesting ways throughout the game: Breckering, Breckenridge, ...), who value material possessions more than Zoltan does. Lucky thing, since he has far fewer after the [Common Cause Mine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Cause_Mine "Common Cause Mine") failed. 

Zoltan is the one to acquaint Geralt with [dice poker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_dice_poker "The Witcher dice poker"), and to give him a couple of pointers about whom to challenge. After the initial meeting, he can be found in the [Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts_Inn "Outskirts Inn") for the remainder of Chapter I. 

Later in the game, Zoltan can be found wandering around the [Temple Quarter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Quarter "Temple Quarter") (roughly from 8:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m.) and, if choosing to side with the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") or take the neutral path, in the [Old Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Vizima "Old Vizima"). He also often spends time with [Golan Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golan_Vivaldi "Golan Vivaldi"). Arrives at Golan Vivaldi's home at 10:30 during chapter 2. 

If Geralt outdrinks him at the Inn, Zoltan will give him a [diamond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diamond "Diamond").

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

[![Zoltan Chivay in The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/95/Tw2_full_Zoltan_1.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/205?cb=20141117173517)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/95/Tw2_full_Zoltan_1.png/revision/latest?cb=20141117173517 "Zoltan Chivay in The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")Zoltan Chivay in The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings

Zoltan once again appears in the second witcher game. His appearance has only slightly changed. Although originally expected to have the [same voice actor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peter_Marinker "Peter Marinker") as the original witcher game, he was ultimately voiced by [Alexander Morton](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0607916/). His first appearance in the game is standing on a gallows platform in [Flotsam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flotsam "Flotsam") during Chapter I. He is saved from being hanged (His crime? Colluding with the Scoia'tael) alongside another friend of Geralt's - [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") (himself accused of debauchery, or maybe setting fire to a tower, or possibly both!). 

Since they saw each other last, Zoltan's engagement to [Eudora Breckenriggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eudora_Breckenriggs "Eudora Breckenriggs") has fallen through, as has his career in the mining industry, so he's settled for life as a "simple merchant", and occasional jewelsmith for a [certain sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/S%C3%ADle_de_Tansarville "Síle de Tansarville"). 

After Flotsam, he heads to [Vergen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vergen "Vergen") where he is reunited with his old pals, [Yarpen Zigrin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yarpen_Zigrin "Yarpen Zigrin") and [Sheldon Skaggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sheldon_Skaggs "Sheldon Skaggs").

### Hero Presentation

    _Dwarven adventurer and close friend of Geralt of Rivia, Zoltan Chivay is an excellent warrior, who excels at close quarters combat. Zoltan is the perfect initiator, his skills allow him to quickly jump into the fray, as well as vacate battlefield hotspots when the situation becomes too intense. His passive skill allows him to deal 25% more damage to stunned enemies._
    _Bull Rush is a skill that can be used in various situations. You can employ it both to initiate attacks and to run away from enemies. Zoltan rushes towards the enemies, lifting them up in the air and temporarily disabling them. Bull Rush also does damage, but its main purpose should always be immobilizing enemies, so your team can finish the job. If you want to deal the finishing blow, you can activate Whirling Dwarvish, Zoltan's main offensive skill. Upon activation, the dwarf begins to spin franticly dealing damage to opponents around him. As it's an AoE (or Area of Effect) attack, it's best used when there are multiple enemies around. The skill can be used while Zoltan is moving, so it's also a good idea to utilize it when entering or escaping combat._
    _Worth it! is Zoltan's special skill. It allows him to leap forward and sends a brutal shockwave that deals damage and knocks back enemies caught within it. Use it wisely as it can be a powerful tool capable of inflicting damage upon entire groups of foes._[3]

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

Geralt meets Zoltan in Novigrad. Where he is throwing vagrants out of Dandelion's newly inherited tavern. Zoltan also wondering where Dandelion had disappeared to assisted Geralt and this lead them to [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla") a Bard Dandelion was very close to. Upon discovering where Dandelion was and when he would be taken to Oxenfurt in a prison cart Zoltan organized an attack on it with several fellow Dwarves and Geralt. 

### Journal entry

    _A dwarf, a soldier of fortune, a veteran of the Battle of Brenna, an unassailable optimist, a committed altruist, a lover of rough drinks and rougher songs, a master gwent player and, above all, a dear and loyal friend to me and Geralt – ladies and gentlemen, allow me to present Zoltan Chivay. We first became fast friends years ago, when Geralt and I ran into Zoltan and the band he was leading at the time across war-stricken Riverdell._
    _It proved our fate to cross paths many times after that, and we eventually became inseparable companions, sticking together through good and ill. At our tale's beginning Zoltan and I had gone into business together running a Novigrad tavern called the Rosemary and Thyme._
    _By saving me from the transport taking me to my doom Zoltan proved yet again he would never abandon a friend in need. In return, Zoltan soon found himself on the receiving end of an act of friendship. Suffering some financial difficulties, he had taken out a loan from one of the Novigrad mob bosses, and as a result had made many unfortunate enemies._
    _He planned to repay his debt by gathering a prize collection of gwent cards – a risky endeavor, given the cutthroat nature of Novigrad gwent circles at the time, and one that would have never worked without Geralt's help._

## Trivia

  * Zoltan (Zoltán) is an old Hungarian name, which originates from an Arabic sovereign type called 'sultan'.
  * Chivay is a name of a town in the Colca valley in southern Peru, known for nearby hot springs.
  * The original game is inconsistent in its treatment of Zoltan's relationship with Eudora. She is variously referred to as his wife or his fiancée. Depending on language version.
