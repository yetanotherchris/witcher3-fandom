\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant** works out of a store in the [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") and is a [gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players"), using the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck") deck. He sells a decent amount of basic [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting components"), [food and drink](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink") and a few [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"), including including alcohol potion bases. 

He will always be at his shop, but can also be encountered during the unmarked quest [Suspicious Shakedown](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Suspicious_Shakedown "Suspicious Shakedown"), where he is cornered by a couple of bandits in a back alley north of the [Rosemary and Thyme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme"). If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") tries to intervene, but then backs out and doesn't defend him, the merchant will refuse to do business with the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Suspicious Shakedown](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Suspicious_Shakedown "Suspicious Shakedown")

## Notes

  * Like other [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") who can be helped during quests, he may say that he's giving Geralt a discount for his deeds, but that's only dialogue and there's no price change.
