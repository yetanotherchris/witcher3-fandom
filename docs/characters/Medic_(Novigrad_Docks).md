## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed **medic** used to work near the [docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks") of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") he was tasked with doing autopsies of some dead bodies who were recently discovered near [Crippled Kate's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crippled_Kate%27s "Crippled Kate's") brothel. Apparently all the corpses looked dehydrated and without any visible signs of wound but light scratches.

## Trivia

  * The pendent on his neck incorrectly depicts the [Staff of Hermes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Staff_of_Hermes "wikipedia:Staff of Hermes") from [Greek mythology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greek_mythology "wikipedia:Greek mythology"), instead of the correct recognised medical symbol of the [Rod of Asclepius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rod_of_Asclepius "wikipedia:Rod of Asclepius"), which the singular entwined serpent.
