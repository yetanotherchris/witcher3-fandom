**Dune Vildenvert** was a villager from [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") who lived on a [farm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildenvert%27s_farm "Vildenvert's farm") with his family, just east of White Orchard village. 

## Biography

In early [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Dune managed to avoid being drafted into the [Temerian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Army "Temerian Army") by purposely injuring himself, unlike his brother [Bastien](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastien_Vildenvert "Bastien Vildenvert"). When [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") and [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") forces clashed on a [battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_\(White_Orchard\) "Battlefield \(White Orchard\)") during the [Battle of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_White_Orchard "Battle of White Orchard"), Bastien went missing and Dune posted [a notice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brother_Missing "Brother Missing") on the White Orchard [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board "Notice board"), requesting help in finding his brother, as he wanted to find his brother alive or, at the very least, give his brother a proper burial. 

_If Geralt helps him find his brother:_ they found Bastien along with a Nilfgaardian soldier, [Rhosyn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rhosyn "Rhosyn"), and Bastien explained how both were wounded in the battle and helped each other to find shelter in a small hut in the woods. Dune, however, didn't want to take in the Nilfgaardian deserter. 

    _If Geralt convinced him to take in Rhosyn:_ Dune was able to disguise the deserter and all of them tended to their family farm.

    _If Geralt doesn't convince him to take in Rhosyn:_ Dune returned to his family farm with his brother, leaving the deserter to die in the abandoned hut.

_If Geralt found the brother before talking to Dune:_ Dune went and gathered his brother but, with nobody there to speak up for Rhosyn, he left the deserter to die.
