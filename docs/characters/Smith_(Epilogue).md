## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **smith** may appear during the "Witcher" version of the [epilogue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)"), only if [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") wins the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III") (regardless of who the head of state is). If that is the case, he will be in the [Royal Redanian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") camp setup near [Mislav](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mislav "Mislav")'s hut. 

Acting as a very short-term replacement [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), he sells the same items as the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison")'s [quartermaster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quartermaster_\(Nilfgaardian_Garrison\) "Quartermaster \(Nilfgaardian Garrison\)") plus a variety of [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") [bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts").
