**Svanrige Tuirseach**[1] was the proud firstborn son of [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Bran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bran_Tuirseach "Bran Tuirseach") and his wife [Birna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Birna "Birna"). Following his father's death, he became the formal leader of [Clan Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tuirseach "Clan Tuirseach") and jarl of the vast territory of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Svanrige and his mother were present at the funeral of his father Bran. At the feast in Kaer Trolde celebrating his passing, Svanrige hid in the room of [Hjalmar an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar_an_Craite "Hjalmar an Craite"), by instruction of Birna, while the [vildkaarls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildkaarl "Vildkaarl") attacked the feast hall. 

In the investigation following, suspicious of his mothers actions, admitted what she had done to the jarls, condemning Birna to a sentence of death. 

_If[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") refused to help [Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite") or Hjalmar an Craite during the quest [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"):_ Svanrige was crowned as the [King of the Skellige Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_the_Skellige_Isles "King of the Skellige Isles") and established the first hereditary dynasty in the [Skellige Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). 

## Journal entry

    _A Hard life awaits the child who cannot escape his parents' shadow. Following the death of his father, the beloved King Bran, Svanrige became the formal ruler of Clan Tuirseach, which controlled vast territory on the isle of An Skellig._
    _Yet this quiet and mild-mannered young man seemed firmly tied to the apron strings of his mother, Birna, who sought to control his every waking deed. With such an overbearing caretaker it was hard to imagine how Svanrige could ever grow into a man able to rule a mighty Skellige clan in his own right._
    **If Geralt does not intervene during King's Gambit:**

    _Svanrige's election as ruler of Skellige was a rather unexpected turn of events. It was widely thought to have been the result of backroom maneuvering by Birna, who used the chaos in the aftermath of the other claimants' deaths and the shame that fell On the an Craites to achieve her ambition of placing a puppet in her control on the Skellige throne._
    _It thus came as a great shock for her when young Svanrige showed his claws and tore up the humiliating treaty with Nilfgaard she wanted him to sign - and then washed the shame of this near-submission from the isles with the blood of their eternal enemy. This blood simultaneously wiped away any doubts whether the new king was worthy._
