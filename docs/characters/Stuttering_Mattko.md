**Stuttering Mattko** was a [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") racer outside [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") and, by his appearance, a mercenary. 

No matter what order you race him, he'll always give a [racing saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Racing_saddle "Racing saddle") and 20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") when beaten. He also appears to be the easiest to beat at Crow's Perch as he never has obstacles appear when racing him.
