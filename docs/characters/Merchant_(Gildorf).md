\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant** has a [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shop_\(Gildorf\) "Shop \(Gildorf\)") in the [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and is a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player using the [Nilfgaardian deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck"). Among other things, he sells a variety of unique items, including the [elven crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elven_crossbow "Elven crossbow") and multiple [quest items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items").
