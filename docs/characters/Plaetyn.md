**Plaetyn** (foreign word for "child",[3] name is unknown) was a disputed royal heir[4] from a far away land, who was fleeing his homeland around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), after a coup had overthrown the royal family in his kingdom. 

## Biography

The boy, accompanied by his mother [Viki](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viki "Viki"),[1] boarded one of two ships set for [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair"), where they were likely seeking asylum. 

However, the [usurper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dirgelech "Dirgelech") back home sent warriors after them and one of the ships wrecked on an [island](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall%27s_Finger "Hemdall's Finger") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), though luckily Viki and the young prince were on the other ship. The two made it as far as [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") before their ship also crashed, stranding them in a foreign land and, to make matters worse, Viki couldn't speak any of the [local language](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Speech "Common Speech"). They then took refuge in [Widows' Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Widows%27_Grotto "Widows' Grotto") as Viki tried to figure out what to do next. 

_If Geralt finds them and protects them:_ now that the last warrior that'd been following them was dead, Viki and the boy set out once more. 

_If Geralt finds them but stands aside:_ the boy's mother was literally cut down in front of him by the warrior, who conversed with the boy before taking him back home.
