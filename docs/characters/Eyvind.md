**Eyvind** was a [skald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skald "Skald") from [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn") and considered an expert on legends about [Aen Elle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Elle "Aen Elle") and [Aen Seidhe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Seidhe "Aen Seidhe") [elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf"), having translated a legend about Gealbhan. Even though he had this extensive knowledge his only true life goal was to see the legendary white [whale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale "Whale"), [Gevorg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gevorg "Gevorg"), and write the first trustworthy ballad about it. 

_If Geralt sought out his help:_ the creature finally swam by but he was distracted in talking to Geralt and thus didn't see Gevorg.
