**Brewess** was one of the three [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones") in [Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crookback_Bog "Crookback Bog"). The other two were [Weavess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Weavess "Weavess") and [Whispess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whispess "Whispess") and they were supposed to be the daughters of [She-Who-Knows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whispering_Hillock "Whispering Hillock"). 

Brewess had a limited control over plant life, as well as the ability to magically manipulate blood coating the floor, enabling it to drain life from those standing in it, as well as the ability to take on [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") female form, Like her sisters, and the shape of those they feed on. The sisters also have power over the monsters of their lands. 

## Journal entry

    _The Crone known as Brewess was the middle of the three demonic sisters in terms of age – but the first in terms of size. Brewess was said to be a master concocter of magical mixtures, and, in the more ghastly legends, was said to know over a dozen different recipes for[human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") soup._
    _During the Crones' sabbath it turned out the sisters truly did feed on human flesh, cooking them up in infernal pots spiced with root from the cursed tree growing on[Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)")._
    _In the end Brewess shared Whispess' fate: she was slain by[Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")'s blade._
