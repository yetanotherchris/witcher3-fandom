**Vairmont Jonne** or **Gustavo**[1] was a professor from the [Faculty of Applied Archeology](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy_faculties "Oxenfurt Academy faculties") at [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he happened across a map that was said to lead to the Dar'Ah Manuscript, a supposedly priceless [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") tome that could give valuable insight into the [Aen Seidhe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Seidhe "Aen Seidhe")'s history. The map led him to the elven ruins beneath [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") but he was unable to search for it as a sarcophagus in the ruins was sealed up tight. 

_If Geralt helps him:_ Spotting the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), Vairmont got [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to help him unseal the tomb, only for them to find a rather annoyed [vampire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire") inside who promptly shut the lid once more. However, Vairmont didn't have a chance to look for the manuscript and asked for the witcher to open it again. 

    _If Geralt checks it again:_ now angry at being woken up again, the vampire attacked the men, leaving Geralt no choice but to kill it. For his part, Vairmont merely noted how he didn't get a chance to ask the vampire about the manuscript before shrugging it off and to continue searching.
