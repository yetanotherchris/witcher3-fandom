\- Uve, _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_  
  
**Uve** was a [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") guard who liked to tell jokes, usually about [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). 

He can be found patrolling access to [Holmstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein "Holmstein") through the gate. 

## Notes

  * Uve does not have his own model and appears in the form of a random Clan Drummond warrior.
