## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Smith** living in [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal") is the local [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"). He is also [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player ([Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck")) and sells [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"), and a number of [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams").
