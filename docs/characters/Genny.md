## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Genny** was one of the [orphans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orphans "Orphans") that [Gran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gran "Gran") looked after in [Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). At one point Genny got a fever but the [godling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Godling "Godling") [Johnny](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Johnny "Johnny") helped cure it. 

_If Geralt freed[the Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree"):_ Genny, along with all the other orphans from Crookback Bog, was saved by the Ghost from the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"). The orphans later ended up in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and were placed under the care of [Marabella](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marabella "Marabella") at [her school](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marabella%27s_School_for_Tots "Marabella's School for Tots"). 

_If Geralt killed the Ghost in the Tree:_ Genny, along with all the other orphans from Crookback Bog, was taken to [Aard Cerbin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") by the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"), where he was killed and consumed by them.
