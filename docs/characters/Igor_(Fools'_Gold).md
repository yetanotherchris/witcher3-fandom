## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Igor** was the local [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch"), until [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), when he was transformed into a [pig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pig "Pig"). 

## Biography

He and the other villagers, save [Yontek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yontek "Yontek"), robbed from a nearby altar, not realizing it was cursed. Shortly thereafter all the villagers turned into pigs and somehow Igor got left behind at the altar location, now in pig form. 

_If Geralt visited the "Hog Hut":_ the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") found Igor and proceeded to have a "conversation" with him in the form of oinking a number of times for yes or no. After this, Geralt could then help lift the curse, transforming Igor back to [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") form.
