**Donar an Hindar** of [Clan Heymaey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Heymaey "Clan Heymaey") was a jarl of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") during the [13th century](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#13th_century "Timeline/Games"). 

## Journal entry

    _Few jarls of Skellige were as honored and obeyed as Donar an Hindar, the eldest of their number. Despite his advanced age he remained the active leader of his clan and its representative at all official gatherings. The only person able to break his composure was[Madman Lugos](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Madman_Lugos "Madman Lugos"), [whose clan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") had long feuded with the an Hindars._
    _Donar's home was, like[Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya")'s [temple](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_of_Freyja "Temple of Freyja"), on Hindarsfjall. As befits a neighbor to the [gods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nordling_Pantheon "Nordling Pantheon"), Donar was famed for his wise, considered opinions and his piety. This last did not, however, extend to the gods of the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent"), whose rich sanctuaries he had often raided in his youth._
