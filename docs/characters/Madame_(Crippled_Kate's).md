## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed woman was **madame** of the [Crippled Kate's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crippled_Kate%27s "Crippled Kate's") brothel in the [Harborside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harborside "Harborside") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), working in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"); although she, her girls, and the brothel lived in poverty, she was willing to play [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck").
