## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**John Verdun** was a former soldier in the [Temerian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Army "Temerian Army"), having served in the 10th [Maribor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maribor "Maribor") division before [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), but deserted before it was destroyed by the [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") during their [invasion of the North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War"). He fell in with a group of fleeing refugees but, when they learned he had deserted, the refugees tied him up and left him for [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") on the river bank west of [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale"). 

_If Geralt leaves him there:_ John insinuated there was more to the story than just being a deserter but refused to elaborate. Not trustful of this, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") left him there and John was summarily killed by drowners a short time later. 

_If Geralt frees him:_ John thanked the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") and headed off. Later on, he gathered some men and hunted down and killed the refugees that left him on the river bank. When he spied the witcher crossing his path again, he welcomed Geralt and offered to pay him for saving his life earlier, despite it being obvious he'd killed the refugees at the camp. 

    _If Geralt takes the reward:_ Not seeing the point in questioning it further, Geralt accepted John's payment and they went their separate ways.
    _If Geralt fights him:_ Not wanting to let it go that John had killed all the refugees, Geralt and John fought, unfortunately ending in John's death.

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What was John Verdun's role in the Temerian Army?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

John Verdun, a former soldier in the Temerian Army, was stationed in the 10th Maribor division. He deserted his post before 1272, which was before the division's annihilation by the Nilfgaardians. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What happened to John Verdun after he deserted the army?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

John Verdun served in the 10th Maribor division of the Temerian Army. He deserted prior to 1272, evading the division's destruction by the Nilfgaardians. Post-desertion details are dependent on the individual player's actions. 

Provided by: Community 

**What is the mystery behind John Verdun's desertion that he refuses to elaborate on?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

After he deserted, John ended up in a group of fleeing refugees. However, when they learned he was a deserter, they tied him up and left him on a river bank for drowners to finish off. However, he insinuates there was more to the story in why he got tied up than just being a deserter, but he refuses to elaborate on this to plead his case.
