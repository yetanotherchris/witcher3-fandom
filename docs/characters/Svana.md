## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Svana** was a [priestess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priest "Priest") of [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") and, around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), the race organizer for [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")'s races, known collectively as [The Heroes' Pursuits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Heroes%27_Pursuits:_For_the_Goddess%27_Glory! "The Heroes' Pursuits: For the Goddess' Glory!"). She stationed herself in [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") where everyone who wished to compete placed a horseshoe on the table beside her.
