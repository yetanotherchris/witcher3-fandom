**Draig Bon-Dhu** was a young [skald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skald "Skald") from [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") who accompanied [Eist Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eist_Tuirseach "Eist Tuirseach"), [Crach an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite"), and [Mousesack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mousesack "Mousesack") to the feast held by [Queen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen "Queen") [Calanthe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calanthe "Calanthe") in honour of her daughter, the Princess [Pavetta](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pavetta "Pavetta")'s [coming of age](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Question_of_Price "A Question of Price").[1]

He was described as "a strapping fellow with bagpipes". 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

He appeared only briefly at [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde"), where he performed a ballad about [Hjalmar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar "Hjalmar") slaying the [giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_Giant "Ice Giant") on [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"). Later on he was among the victims of the [Berserker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berserker_\(creature\) "Berserker \(creature\)") attack. 

### Associated quest

  * [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit")

## Notes

  * In [A Question of Price](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Question_of_Price "A Question of Price") he played the bagpipes while in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ he plays a lute.
  * He does not have a unique model, therefore it is possible to see "him" playing at the [Coronation](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coronation "Coronation") among other skalds.
