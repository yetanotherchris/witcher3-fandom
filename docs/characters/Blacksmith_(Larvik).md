## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **blacksmith** in [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"). He sells a couple of [crossbows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow"), [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") and swords [diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams").
