## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Durden** "**The Tailor** " was an [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") fistfighter and champion of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

## Biography

_If[Fists of Fury: Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fists_of_Fury:_Novigrad "Fists of Fury: Novigrad") is completed:_ the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") defeated him for the title. 

## Associated quest

  * [Fists of Fury: Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fists_of_Fury:_Novigrad "Fists of Fury: Novigrad")

## Notes

  * He is one of the hardest fist fights in the game due to the limited area provided during the fight, and the difficulty of dodging short, quick jabs, instead of keeping your range for wide overhead swing parries.

## Trivia

  * His name is a reference to the character Tyler Durden from _[Fight Club](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fight_Club_\(novel\) "wikipedia:Fight Club \(novel\)")_.
  * When Durden first approaches Geralt, he'll make a comment that's a variation of the speech that Count Adhemar gives William in the 2001 film _[A Knight's Tale](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Knight%27s_Tale "wikipedia:A Knight's Tale")_.
