## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** appears some time after [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks") is cleared of pirates. He sells the same things as a generic [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") \- lesser [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph"), [diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") and some armors.
