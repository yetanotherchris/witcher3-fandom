## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ole-Kristian** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skelliger "Skelliger") man who served at [Kaer Muire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Muire "Kaer Muire") with loyalty to [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") and Jarl [Madman Lugos](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Madman_Lugos "Madman Lugos"). He could have been seen discussing a situation on the isles with another islander by the fire near castle's prison. He feared that, after the death of [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_the_Skellige_Isles "King of the Skellige Isles") [Bran Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bran_Tuirseach "Bran Tuirseach"), chaos would come and that [an Craites](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") would try to attack Kaer Muire.
