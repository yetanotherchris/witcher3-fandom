## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hortensio** was a [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") crossbowmen fighting as guerilla soldier under command of [Vernon Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche "Vernon Roche") during the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War"). He guarded the entrance to the [Temerian Partisan hideout](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Partisan_hideout "Temerian Partisan hideout").
