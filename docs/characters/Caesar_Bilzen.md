**Caesar Bilzen** was the accountant of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") crime lord [Cleaver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese"). He resided in a [spacious manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen%27s_house "Caesar Bilzen's house") on [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). A friend of [Zoltan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay")'s and an avid [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") card collector, he was in possession of the unique "[Fringilla Vigo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fringilla_Vigo_\(gwent_card\) "Fringilla Vigo \(gwent card\)")" card. 

In the [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf")'s house [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") discovers that he is actually a secret admirer of [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"), with a room filled with various Nilfgaardian memorabilia. 

_If Geralt helps Caesar:_ When Cleaver sent some of his [men](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crownsplitters "Crownsplitters") to capture Caesar to punish him for some discrepancies in the ledger, Geralt and Zoltan helped Caesar to beat them, after which the dwarf packed and left the city.
