**Anselm** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") who worked in conjunction with his business partner [Griswold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griswold "Griswold"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), the two traveled to [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") from an unspecified place to trade with [Phillip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger"), the Bloody Baron, having deemed the venture worthwhile due to the lack of competition in the area. The merchants hired a number of former [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") soldiers as guards to keep them safe in the [war](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III")-torn lands of Velen, however, the guards themselves turned on the merchants after the group were out of sight of any settlements. [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") encountered the bandit guards attacking Anselm and Griswold on the road south of [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale"). 

_If Geralt saved the merchants:_ Anselm and Griswold completed their plan by traveling to [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), and began trading with the locals there, out of a stall near the main gate. 

## Associated quest

  * [Caravan Attack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caravan_Attack "Caravan Attack")

## Notes

  * If rescued, he will appear at Crow's Perch in roughly eight days, but the trigger for this isn't as straightforward. The quickest way to spawn him as a merchant is, after completing the quest, ride to [Marauders' Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marauders%27_Bridge "Marauders' Bridge"), [meditate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meditation "Meditation") for eight days by the signpost there, and only then look for him - it's important that the trigger around the eighth day happens in Velen, but sufficiently far away from Crow's Perch.
  * Like other merchants who can be helped during quests, he may say that he's giving Geralt a discount for his deeds, but that's only dialogue and there's no price change.
