## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[Margrave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margrave "Margrave") **Vladimir de Cret** was a powerful [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") magnate and aristocrat, with close ties to the [Vegelbud family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family"). He was also a recognized [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player who was not particularly averse to losing, given his wealth. 

## Biography

_If[A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)") is completed:_ In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1272 "Timeline"), during one of Vegelbud's masquerade balls at their [family estate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence"), de Cret participated in a gwent contest and gave [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") the [Milva card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Milva_\(gwent_card\) "Milva \(gwent card\)") if beaten, himself using the [Nilfgaardian Empire deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck").
