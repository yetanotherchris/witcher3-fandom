## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Bjorg** was a master shipwright in [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor") during [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") and where he was training his apprentice, [Rurik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rurik "Rurik"). Finally fed up with what he perceived were just [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") killing people near the cliffs, he decided to find someone to take care of the problem. 

_If Geralt accepts the contract:_ He informed [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") the two known witnesses he remembered seeing the monster's attack: his apprentice, Rurik, and a widow named [Tove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tove "Tove").
