**Zdenek** was an aging [champion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fistfighters_in_The_Witcher "Fistfighters in The Witcher") of [fistfighting](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_fistfighting "The Witcher fistfighting"), considered the world's best boxer throughout [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") due to never being defeated twice in a row. However, when he went to enter [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior "Whoreson Junior")'s [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_arena "Whoreson Junior's arena") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he was murdered by thugs just outside the arena.

## Biography

Little is known about his past. "Zdenek" was his pseudonym but he kept his real name a secret. During [1270](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1270 "Timeline/Games"), Zdenek could be found in the [southernmost refugee cave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_caves "Refugees' caves") in the [swamp cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_cemetery "Swamp cemetery"), keeping his fistfights to the privacy of his own chamber in the cave. One of his biggest fans was [Zahin Schmartz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zahin_Schmartz "Zahin Schmartz"), who would pay 250[![Items Oren](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/75/Items_Oren.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/12?cb=20170504012521)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren") for Zdenek's tooth. 

After leaving Vizima he went to [Flotsam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flotsam "Flotsam") where he became champion soon enough. He also slightly changed his appearance by shaving his head. 

In 1272 he ended up in Novigrad where he was murdered by thugs near [The Nowhere Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nowhere_Inn "The Nowhere Inn") on his way to join fistfights in Whoreson Junior's arena.
