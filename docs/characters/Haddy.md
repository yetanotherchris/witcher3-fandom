## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Haddy** was a young boy from [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"), [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") who was considered one of the best [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") players in the area despite being only a child. His preferred choice of deck was [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"). 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What makes Haddy a top Gwent player in Midcopse?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Haddy, a young boy from Midcopse, Velen in 1272, was known as a top Gwent player. He excelled with the Northern Realms deck, and defeating him would earn the Vernon Roche card.

**What card does Haddy give when defeated in Gwent?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Haddy provides the Vernon Roche card when defeated in Gwent. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Why does Haddy prefer the Northern Realms deck?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Haddy prefers the Northern Realms deck due to its focus on damage output and staying power. It offers strategic benefits like drawing a card after winning a round and can enhance unit strength with the Tight Bond ability across all rows. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What role does Haddy play in Velen's Gwent scene?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Haddy, a young Gwent prodigy from Midcopse, Velen, is known for his skill in the game during 1272. He favors the Northern Realms deck and, when defeated in the quest 'Gwent: Velen Players,' he provides the Vernon Roche Gwent card as a reward. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How did Haddy become skilled at Gwent at a young age?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Haddy, from Midcopse, Velen, gained recognition as a top Gwent player by 1272, despite his young age. His skill with the Northern Realms deck set him apart in the game. 

Provided by: Fandom
