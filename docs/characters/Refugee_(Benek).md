## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The man only known as **Refugee** can be found sitting outside one of the cottages in [Benek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Benek "Benek"). He asks the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") to find some "local lads" who have gone missing after being hired to investigate some missing shipments. One of the missing men is Thomil, his brother.
