## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **armorer** at [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") is based in the courtyard adjacent to the entrance hall of the keep. He plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"), in addition to selling the equipment included in the free [Skellige Armor set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_Armor_set "Skellige Armor set") DLC.
