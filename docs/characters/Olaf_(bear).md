## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_  
 _[Gwent: The Witcher Card Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent:_The_Witcher_Card_Game "Gwent: The Witcher Card Game")_

**Olaf** was a [bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") who held the position of reigning champion "[fistfighter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_fistfighting "The Witcher 3 fistfighting")" in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Fists of Fury: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fists_of_Fury:_Skellige "Fists of Fury: Skellige")

## Notes

  * There is also another bear-fistfighter in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") named [Boris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boris_\(bear\) "Boris \(bear\)").
  * His likeness appears on a [gwent card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olaf_\(gwent_card\) "Olaf \(gwent card\)") in the [Skellige deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_Gwent_deck "Skellige Gwent deck").
