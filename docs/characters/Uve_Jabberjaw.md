## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Uve Jabberjaw** was the younger of [Blueboy Lugos](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blueboy_Lugos "Blueboy Lugos")'s friends. He did not have a tongue, and therefore couldn't speak. If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") asked about his condition, Lugos says that Jabberjaw tore out his own tongue because he cursed the monarch [Bran Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bran_Tuirseach "Bran Tuirseach") while drunk. He lost his power to speak... but kept his honour.
