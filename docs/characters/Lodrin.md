## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lodrin** was a guard that [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") encountered in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), when he was looking for the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger"). He lets Geralt through the gate to go see the Bloody Baron.
