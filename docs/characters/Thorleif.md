## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Thorleif** was the village elder of [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), when an [unknown beast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hagubman "Hagubman") started to ambush people on the main road, just outside the village. Immediately realizing this was no normal creature, he posted a notice, hoping someone skilled with a blade could handle it.
