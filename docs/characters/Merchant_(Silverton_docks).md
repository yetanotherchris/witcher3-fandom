## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **merchant** by the docks in the north of [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") sells bottles for "Odrin & Friends". He stands next to the place where [Odrin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Odrin "Odrin") lies. The merchant has 215–230 [empty bottles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Empty_bottle "Empty bottle") for sale, and is willing to buy almost anything.
