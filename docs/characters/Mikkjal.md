## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Mikkjal** was the caretaker of [Eldberg Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eldberg_Lighthouse "Eldberg Lighthouse") in western [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") and belonged to [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

## Biography

He has a rather dark secret though: using his position as the lighthouse keeper, he made a deal with others to occasionally have some ships go "astray" by not lighting the [fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire "Fire") and thus having them crash into the island and they'd then split the spoils. However, someone eventually found out and placed a strong curse on the lighthouse and against Mikkjal in particular, which caused not just a dense fog on the island but a strong [wraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"), known as a [penitent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Penitent "Penitent"), to appear.
