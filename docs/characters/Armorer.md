# Armorer

[![Armorer's Guild of ](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d3/Armorer%27s_Guild_Murivel.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20231025092319)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d3/Armorer%27s_Guild_Murivel.png/revision/latest?cb=20231025092319) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Armorer%27s_Guild_Murivel.png)

_Armorer's Guild of[Murivel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Murivel "Murivel")_

**Armorers** are makers, suppliers, or repairers of armor throughout the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent"). 

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

There are a few **armorers** in _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ : 

  * The [Order armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_of_the_Order "Armorer of the Order") in the [Temple Quarter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Quarter "Temple Quarter")
  * [Malcolm Stein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Malcolm_Stein "Malcolm Stein") in the [non-human district](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman_district "Nonhuman district") of the [Temple Quarter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Quarter "Temple Quarter") and later in [Old Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Vizima "Old Vizima") (Scoia'tael [path](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_storyline "The Witcher storyline") only)
  * The [Armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marketplace "Marketplace") near [The New Narakort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Narakort "The New Narakort") in the [Trade Quarter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trade_Quarter "Trade Quarter")
  * The [Order armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Makeshift_forge "Makeshift forge") in [Old Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Vizima "Old Vizima") (Order path)
  * [Kalkstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kalkstein "Kalkstein"), in Chapter V (Witcher / neutral path only)

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 icon armorer](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/6/6e/Tw3_icon_armorer.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/22?cb=20170901041520)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Armorer")

Only an armorer can craft [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_armor "The Witcher 3 armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will have to bring the required materials to an armorer to have new professionally crafted armor. Similarly, weapons can only be forged by [Blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"). It is also better to sell armor and upgrades to armorers because they offer an extra +20% profit compared to other types of [shopkeepers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants").
