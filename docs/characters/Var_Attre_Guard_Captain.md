## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Var Attre Guard Captain** keeps watch outside the [Var Attre Villa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Var_Attre_Villa "Var Attre Villa") in [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") at [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He takes his duty very seriously and can be consulted only regarding the whereabouts of ambassador [Henry var Attre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henry_var_Attre "Henry var Attre")'s daughter, [Rosa var Attre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosa_var_Attre "Rosa var Attre").
