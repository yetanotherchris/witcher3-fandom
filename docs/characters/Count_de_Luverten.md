## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Count de Luverten** was the youngest of the [Duke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Duke "Duke") de [Salm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Salm "Salm"), from its ruling family well-known and rich from trading with gems. He was a friend of [Vegelbuds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family") and portraitist [Henri Rautlec](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henri_Rautlec "Henri Rautlec"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Rautlec promised [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") to make new posters for [Chameleon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme") but since he got in debt again he couldn't bring them. The Count met with Henri on races in front of [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence") to discuss this and he offered a deal to the halfling that he would pay his debt if he wagered one more time on a racer and won.
