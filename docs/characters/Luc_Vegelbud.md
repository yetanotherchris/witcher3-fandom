**Luc Vegelbud** , [Albert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albert_Vegelbud "Albert Vegelbud")'s cousin, is the black sheep of the [Vegelbud family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family") and a horse racer, whose steed is named "Succubus". His aunt, [Ingrid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ingrid_Vegelbud "Ingrid Vegelbud"), particularly dislikes him and wants to see him fail.

## Notes

  * It's not exactly clear on how he's related to the Vegelbud family. He's described as Albert's cousin, therefore one of his parents must be a sibling of Ingrid, but there's no mention of [Patricia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patricia_Vegelbud "Patricia Vegelbud") having other children.
