## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** appears at [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite")'s [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig"), if the site is liberated. His inventory and pricing is that of a generic [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer"), although even more oddly he is the only such merchant, while all armorers on the isles have a slightly customized inventories.
