\- Herbalist  
  
  
This halfling **herbalist** has a shop off [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane"), which is located north of [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove"), and across the canal to the south of [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He sells the quest item "[bundle of burdock](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bundle_of_burdock "Bundle of burdock")", in addition to many [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and [formulae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae"). 

He is also a steady [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player, using the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck").
