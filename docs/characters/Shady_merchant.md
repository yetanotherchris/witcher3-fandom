## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Shady merchant** was an unnamed man located on the [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") side of the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), where he made and sold counterfeit [transit passes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Transit_pass "Transit pass") for refugees looking to cross over to [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"). Even though this was technically illegal, the nearby guards knew about this and even made hints to others that there were other ways to obtain a pass. 

_If Geralt talks to him:_ he'll offer [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") a pass for 100 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). If the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") balked at it, he then offered a discount if the witcher would help his brother-in-law, [Albin Hart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albin_Hart "Albin Hart"). 

_If Geralt helps Albin and he survives:_ pleased, the merchant then offered to sell the pass to Geralt for 25 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). 

_If Geralt helps Albin but he dies:_ upset over this, the merchant refused to do anymore business with Geralt.
