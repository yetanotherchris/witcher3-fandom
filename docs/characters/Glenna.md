**Glenna** was a peasant who once settled in the village of [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough"). 

## Biography

Glenna once lived in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") with her husband who was a butcher there. Later, for unknown reasons, the pair relocated to the village of Blackbough in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). Though her husband was still known as a butcher, meat was incredibly scarce in Velen following the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") invasion of the region in the [Third Nilfgaard War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Nilfgaard_War "Third Nilfgaard War"), and so the couple was forced to subsist on mostly selling other animal parts.
