**Georgius "The Piledriver" Georg** was the tavern fistfighting champion at the [Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games").

## Trivia

  * Georgius Georg is a reference to Gorgeous George, the ring name of wrestler George Wagner who retired in 1962 and whose credo was "Win if you can, lose if you must, but always cheat!"
  * Georgius Georg (and Claytop) are also references to the Guy Ritchie film, _[Snatch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snatch_\(film\) "wikipedia:Snatch \(film\)")_.
