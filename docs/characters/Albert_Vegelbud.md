**Albert Vegelbud** was an amateur [alchemist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") from the [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") region and part of the wealthy [Vegelbud family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family"). 

## Biography

After [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne")'s events and [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s witch hunts, Albert became a target for his dabbling in alchemy. His mother, [Ingrid Vegelbud](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ingrid_Vegelbud "Ingrid Vegelbud"), used the family's wealth and influence to pay off the city guards to not tell the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") about her son, but somehow they still found out and planned to attack him on his [family's own estate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence"). 

_If Geralt helps Triss get Albert out of Novigrad:_ with [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s help, Albert was safely evacuated from his family's estate during a masquerade ball. When other hiding mages fled Novigrad, he went with them to [Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir_and_Poviss "Kovir and Poviss"), a known safe haven for [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") users.
