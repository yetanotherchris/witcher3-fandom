\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant'** s stall is located northwest of [The Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") district. He only sells three varieties of [fish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"), and is a [gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players") using the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck").
