## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

"**Yamurlak** " (real name unknown) was a [spy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spy "Spy") in the [Redanian Secret Service](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redanian_Secret_Service "Redanian Secret Service"). During the [witch hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunts "Witch hunts") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), he served as a liaison agent between [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") and [Caleb Menge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Menge "Caleb Menge").
