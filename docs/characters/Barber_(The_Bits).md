## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **barber** lived in a shop in the eastern part of [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits"), the poorest district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He only charged 5 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") for cutting [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s beard or hair, but is permanently drunk, so you better look for another barber. 

## Notes

  * He has a small chance of giving a different haircut from the one you asked. The first time this happens, Geralt will openly protest against his behavior, with the barber responding in lack of care for his opinion.
