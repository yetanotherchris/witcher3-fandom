## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** appears after the small [fishing village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(fishing_village\) "Abandoned site \(fishing village\)") on the coast east of [Stonecutters' Settlement](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stonecutters%27_Settlement "Stonecutters' Settlement") is cleared of [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"). Like many other [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") at liberated [abandoned sites](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site"), he sells the same things as [wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Pontar\) "Wandering merchant \(Pontar\)") and his appearance is allowed to change between visits.
