\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant** should be present near the [notice board](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Notice_board_\(Farcorners\) "Notice board \(Farcorners\)") in [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners"), doesn't appear during gameplay, either intentionally disabled or due to a bug. He could play [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck"), and would sell mostly [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"), but also [Triss' fox mask](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss%27_fox_mask "Triss' fox mask") for [A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)"). 

## Notes

  * He is actually defined as a "[Toderas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toderas "Toderas") village trader" in the game files, but during development a lot of NPCs originally meant for Toderas have been moved into Farcorners instead.
