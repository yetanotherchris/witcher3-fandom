## Biography

### Meeting Geralt

_If[Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") didn't return to [Tor Gvalch'ca](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tor_Gvalch%27ca "Tor Gvalch'ca"):_ in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") returned to the bog to hunt down the last of the three crone sisters, [Weavess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Weavess "Weavess"), and take back Ciri's stolen [witcher medallion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_medallion "Witcher medallion"), which was originally [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir")'s. Berem found Geralt in the swamp and introduced himself. 

    _If Geralt kills Berem:_ Geralt decided Berem was too much a threat and killed him to protect the area, and Geralt found Weavess on his own.

    _If Geralt allows Berem to live:_ Geralt saw that Berem could help him, getting the werewolf to take him through the swamp and lead him to Weavess.
