## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Old Sage** was a soothsayer living in [Benek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Benek "Benek") who got his seer abilities in his youth when he got hit in the back of the head with a rock. However, his gift doesn't let him see what would help him win at [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") but, despite this, he's considered one of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")'s best [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") players. 

He uses the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck") and if beaten he gives the [Crone: Weavess card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crone:_Weavess_\(gwent_card\) "Crone: Weavess \(gwent card\)") as a reward.

## Trivia

  * The joke the old sage told about falling into a witch's magical brew as a child before getting his visions could be a reference to Obelix from _Asterix and Obelix_ comic series, who fell into his village druid's brew of potion as a baby which made him inhumanly strong.
