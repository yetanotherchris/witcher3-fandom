## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Vilmar** was one of many [druids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") living in the grotto under the sacred tree [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

He appeared to be snarky and when another druid approached him in hurry with news about budworms and weevils that had infested every pine on [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog") he said that he would help pesticide.
