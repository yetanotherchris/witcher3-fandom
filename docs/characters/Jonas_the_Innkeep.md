## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Jonas** is an innkeeper that can be found at [New Port Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port") in [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor"). He plays [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"). 

He was born in [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"), where he ran "[The Old Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Old_Port "The Old Port")". This was before the [Ice Giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_Giant "Ice Giant") arrived, which led to the exodus of the local [Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") of [Clan Tordarroch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tordarroch "Clan Tordarroch"). He will sell four gwent cards: 

  * [Botchling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Botchling_\(gwent_card\) "Botchling \(gwent card\)")
  * [Earth Elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_Elemental_\(gwent_card\) "Earth Elemental \(gwent card\)")
  * [Eredin: Commander of the Red Riders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eredin:_Commander_of_the_Red_Riders_\(gwent_card\) "Eredin: Commander of the Red Riders \(gwent card\)")
  * [Scorch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scorch_\(gwent_card\) "Scorch \(gwent card\)") (1 of 3)
