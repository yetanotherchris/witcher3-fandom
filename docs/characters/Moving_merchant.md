This **Merchant** likes to travel and will move his stand between 3 locations, seemingly at random or at least he's not always present when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") passes through them: 

  * In [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough"), by the cart in the middle of the village
  * At [Carsten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carsten "Carsten"), by the signpost
  * At a crossroads right in the middle between the [Gate of the Hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gate_of_the_Hierarch "Gate of the Hierarch") and [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") (though perhaps it's easiest to follow the straight road east of [Glory Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Gate "Glory Gate"))

Also, at each location he has a unique conversation option. He sells a few [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"), most animal hides, and after reaching level 13, lesser [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone") as well. He doesn't play [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). 

## Notes

  * His pricing isn't affected by him changing areas and he is considered a [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") merchant.
  * His first appearance seems to be scripted to be in Blackbough, instead of that being coincidental with Geralt usually going through there first, and he doesn't seem to appear before [The Nilfgaardian Connection](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Nilfgaardian_Connection "The Nilfgaardian Connection") is completed.
  * His move seems to be triggered by travelling between different regions, e.g. between [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and Novigrad.

## Bugs

  * He should always be at one of the three locations, but he seems to temporarily disappear when his move has triggered. When this happens, his merchant icon will still be on the map at the last location where he was seen, even though he isn't there anymore.
