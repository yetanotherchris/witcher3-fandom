## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This wandering **merchant** is an exact clone of the ones walking the roads around [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), selling the same items and at the same prices. However, likely because of a bug, he only seems to appear in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") during the "Witcher" version of the [epilogue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)"). 

He walks between the two unmarked [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") camps - southern one by the road leading out of the the map, south from the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(White_Orchard\) "Sawmill \(White Orchard\)"), and the northern one on a hill northwest of the [cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_cemetery "White Orchard cemetery") and directly east of the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison"). 

Starting from the southern camp, walks east towards the village, then turns north around [Odolan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Odolan "Odolan")'s hut, and upon reaching the river starts following the road northwest, until the shallow river crossing above which the northern camp is located. At night, he may rest in either of the two camps.
