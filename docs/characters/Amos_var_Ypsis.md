**Amos var Ypsis** was a [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") specializing in [djinns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Djinn "Djinn"). According to a book written by him, he had managed to tame a djinn and harness its power. 

## Biography

A powerful mage, var Ypsis was able to tame a djinn and use it as a source of power. He published his findings in a tome that ended up in the Imperial Library, where it was eventually discovered by [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"). 

Var Ypsis's last expedition was to [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). He traveled to the village of [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"), where he had contact with the locals. With his djinn in tow, he departed Larvik by ship and sailed to [Ingdalen Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ingdalen_Isle "Ingdalen Isle") before an unusually powerful storm broke out. In order to get out of the storm, var Ypsis made a wish to his djinn, which teleported its master, along with part of the ship and a section of the seabed, to a mountaintop in northern [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). Due to a very rough landing at the destination, var Ypsis was knocked out of his boots, before being crushed to death by a falling bookcase inside his cabin.
