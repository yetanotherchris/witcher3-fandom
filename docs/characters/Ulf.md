## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ulf** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") who tried to help his friend [Petter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Petter "Petter"), who broke his leg falling off the [Grymmdjarr Monument](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grymmdjarr_Monument "Grymmdjarr Monument") while trying to light the fires in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

_If Geralt comes across them:_ Geralt gave Petter some [Celandine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Celandine "Celandine"), saying it wouldn't fix his leg just make it hurt less. Ulf thanked him and Geralt went on his way.
