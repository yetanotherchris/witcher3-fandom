\- Quartermaster to Geralt  
  
  
This **quartermaster** should be present in the [Temerian Partisan Hideout](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Partisan_Hideout "Temerian Partisan Hideout"), where he would offer [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") services, but doesn't appear during gameplay due to a bug. 

He hates [nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman") and evidently counts [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") as such, because of which he wouldn't trade with [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") if [Vernon Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche "Vernon Roche") didn't order him to. While unlikely that the order also covered [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), he would also play cards with the witcher.
