## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") **quartermaster** acts as a [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") on [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik")'s [Marlin Coast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marlin_Coast "Marlin Coast"), only during the main quest [On Thin Ice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/On_Thin_Ice "On Thin Ice"). He sells the same things as any blacksmith in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") would - melee [weapons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_weapons "Category:The Witcher 3 weapons"), [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") and [components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting components"). 

## Associated quest

  * [On Thin Ice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/On_Thin_Ice "On Thin Ice")

## Notes

  * This quartermaster has an unused custom store definition still present in the game files. With it, he would likely still sell everything that he does as it is, as 99% of the items are standard for all Skelligan blacksmiths, but he would also have all base game [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph"), some [foods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"), and higher chance to offer runestones.

## Bugs

  * Due to a [common bug](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants#Bugs "The Witcher 3 merchants") that affects all merchants, his blacksmith icon stays permanently on the map, even though he is only briefly available.
