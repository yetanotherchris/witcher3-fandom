## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gaspard Sutter** was a [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") from [Mahakam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mahakam "Mahakam") and a member of Rabenick and Company, a real estate appraisal business. 

He suffered from severe narcolepsy, and was constantly troubled by it. 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), the company got stuck on the [Isle of Mists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isle_of_Mists "Isle of Mists"), while travelling for business. 

Gaspard along with two others, Ivo and Ferenc, had set out in different directions in search for help or a way off the island when he ended up at the top of the [lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lighthouse_\(Isle_of_Mists\) "Lighthouse \(Isle of Mists\)") before falling asleep. Luckily [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") arrived in his search for [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") and found the sleeping dwarf. He then revealed to Geralt that he suffered from narcolepsy and that the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") must wake him up repeatedly on the way back to the [hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Strange_Cottage "Strange Cottage") where his remaining comrades were. 

The two troubled up the cliffs towards the hut, along the way Gaspard kept falling asleep as they were being attacked by [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy"). After reaching his friends, the dwarf left the two witchers and the company returned to the shoreline to prepare their boat.
