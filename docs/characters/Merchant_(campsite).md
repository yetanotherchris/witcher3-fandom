## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** appears at the [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(campsite\) "Abandoned site \(campsite\)") west of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch") once it's clear of hostiles. He sells the same things as [wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Pontar\) "Wandering merchant \(Pontar\)"). 

Unlike most other merchants, this one will always be lying down until [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") talks to him, but after exiting the conversation, he'll immediately be positioned lying down again. Despite having this unique posture though, his appearance is otherwise dynamic and will change between visits.
