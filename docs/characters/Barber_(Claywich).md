This **barber** lives in [Claywich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claywich "Claywich") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), but was captured by bandits and held prisoner in a camp west-southwest of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley"). He will return to the village if [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") rescues him. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What happens if the Claywich barber becomes unresponsive?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

If the Claywich barber becomes unresponsive, the interaction prompt might appear but not work. This issue usually resolves itself over time. Once responsive, he offers Geralt a free haircut if selected during their first meeting in Claywich. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Where can you find the Claywich barber in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, the barber in Claywich is initially a prisoner of bandits located west-southwest of Hangman's Alley. He needs to be rescues so he can return to Claywich. 

Provided by: Community 

**What reward does Geralt receive from the Claywich barber?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

After rescuing the Claywich barber from bandits, Geralt is rewarded with a free haircut when he first speaks to the barber in Claywich. Occasionally, the barber may become unresponsive, delaying this reward. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How does Geralt rescue the barber in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In Witcher 3, Geralt rescues the barber during the quest 'Of Sheers and a Witcher I Sing.' He arrives in time to confront Duvall, who had kidnapped the barber over a haircut issue. Geralt uses the Aard Sign to open the salon doors, follows clues with his Witcher Senses, and defeats Duvall and his band, saving the barber. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Why was the Claywich barber captured by bandits?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Claywich barber was captured by bandits and imprisoned in a camp west-southwest of Hangman's Alley. Geralt of Rivia can rescue him by retrieving a cage key from the nearby Deserter Leader. After his release, the barber returns to Claywich. 

Provided by: Fandom 

## Bugs

  * It's possible that he becomes completely unresponsive upon returning to Claywich - the interaction prompt would appear above him, but is effectively dead. The issue eventually resolves itself, though it may take a long time.
  * When saved, he promises Geralt a free haircut and he reportedly delivers it if the option is chosen the first time he is spoken to in Claywich. However, at least on some for some players (possibly only on PC) this doesn't work at all and he will always charge coin.
