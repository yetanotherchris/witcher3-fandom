\- Eternal Fire Priest  
  
  
This **Eternal Fire Priest** is also an [alchemist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist"), though he has to hide this fact to protect himself, making him a rather reluctant servant of the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire"). 

He can be found in [Electors' Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Electors%27_Square "Electors' Square") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") during the day and sells the [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") book [The Cult of Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Cult_of_Freya "The Cult of Freya"), all base game [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone") and [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph"), as well as the diagrams for them, and a few [alchemical supplies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and [formulae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formulae "Formulae"). He also plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with a fairly strong [Nilfgaardian deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck"), though he only awards a non-unique card if beaten.

## Notes

  * In early and/or possibly non-PC versions of the game he may be labeled as "Priest of Eternal Fire" or "Preacher" instead.
  * Like many other [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"), his unique dialogue lines mention a discount, but there's no price change compared to regular alchemists' prices in Novigrad.
