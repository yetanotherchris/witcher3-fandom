## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Aldert Geert** was an [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") scholar and an assistant professor of [Contemporary History](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy_faculties "Oxenfurt Academy faculties") at [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"). 

He was a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player, using the [Nilfgaardian Empire deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck") and, if beaten, gives the [Zoltan Chivay gwent card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay_\(gwent_card\) "Zoltan Chivay \(gwent card\)"). 

## Biography

In May [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Aldert was passing through [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), eager to reach the front lines of the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War"), in order to describe and catalogue it in what he hoped would be his _[magnum opus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unfinished_book "Unfinished book")_. 

While in the small, war-torn village, he dropped into the [local inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_inn "White Orchard inn") at the same time as the [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") and attempted to teach some locals how to play gwent, though both parties got more frustrated than anything else. 

_If Geralt approaches him:_ trying to obtain information about [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"), Geralt asked Aldert about her, but he believed that she didn't exist. Before the witcher left, he asked whether he wanted a game of gwent. 

_If Geralt agrees to a game:_ Geralt played his first ever game of gwent, and if he won, he was rewarded a gwent card and a [book](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Miraculous_Guide_to_Gwent "A Miraculous Guide to Gwent"). 

### Death

Shortly after, in his misguided attempt to be in the middle of the action, Aldert was caught and hanged from [Hanged Man's Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Tree "Hanged Man's Tree") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") by an unknown faction.

## Notes

  * Beneath his corpse you can find his unfinished book and the Zoltan card if you didn't beat or talk to him in White Orchard.
  * Geralt can try to discourage Aldert from visiting the front lines, stating that the scholar would be killed for boots. Indeed, his body is one of the two lacking boots when found hanged.
  * If you play him at gwent, after the match has finished, he will recommend that if you are in Oxenfurt, play the innkeep [Stjepan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stjepan "Stjepan"), which will activate the quest [Gwent: Playing Innkeeps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent:_Playing_Innkeeps "Gwent: Playing Innkeeps").
  * Speaking to Aldert is completely optional, as speaking to [Gaunter O'Dimm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gaunter_O%27Dimm "Gaunter O'Dimm") is the only patron required to speak to Geralt to advance the quest.
