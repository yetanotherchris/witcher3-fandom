**Sofus the Bull** was a [fistfighter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_fistfighting "The Witcher 3 fistfighting") from [Metinna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Metinna "Metinna"), along with his brother [Hal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hal "Hal"), in the [13th century](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#13th_century "Timeline/Games"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), while hosting some fights in the [Novigrad Docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks") with his brother, Sofus was approached by [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), who was looking for ushers for an upcoming play at [The Butcher's Yard theater](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Butcher%27s_Yard_theater "The Butcher's Yard theater"). The [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") could either pay 70![Crownitem](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/22/Crownitem.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/15?cb=20180420173351) or fight the two brothers for their compliance. If Geralt lost the fight, they still ushered, but he paid 140![Crownitem](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/22/Crownitem.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/15?cb=20180420173351).
