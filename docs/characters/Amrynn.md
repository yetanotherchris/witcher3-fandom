### Games

**Amrynn** was an [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") [prostitute](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prostitute "Prostitute") working at the [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). She was proud to be an elf and never tried to cover it up.
