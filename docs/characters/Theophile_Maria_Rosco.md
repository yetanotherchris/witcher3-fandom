## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Theophile Maria Rosco** was a [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") passing through between [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") when he set up camp with two others: a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldier named [Rufus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rufus "Rufus") and a merchant named [Manfred](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manfred_\(merchant\) "Manfred \(merchant\)"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Strangers in the Night](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Strangers_in_the_Night_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Strangers in the Night \(The Witcher 3\)")

## Notes

  * Regardless how [Strangers in the Night](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Strangers_in_the_Night_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Strangers in the Night \(The Witcher 3\)") is done, he remains neutral, staying out of the argument.
  * Oddly, _The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt: Official Prima Guide_ lists his middle name as "Mario" instead of "Maria". It is unclear what caused this discrepancy.
  * Considering [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s stance on killing every mage, it's unclear why Rufus doesn't bother Theophile. It's suggested that maybe Theophile is supporting Radovid's plan somehow or just a developer oversight.
