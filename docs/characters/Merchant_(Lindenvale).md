## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **merchant** is a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player using the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"). He sells the [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") book [Tristianna and Isador](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tristianna_and_Isador "Tristianna and Isador"), five gwent cards, [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manuscript_page:_Chort_lure "Manuscript page: Chort lure") for [chort lure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort_lure "Chort lure"), and a variety of miscellaneous items.
