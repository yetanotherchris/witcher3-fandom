The **alchemist** at the [Crematory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crematory "Crematory") in the northeast of the [Oxenfurt Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Gate "Oxenfurt Gate") was a [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") in hiding, under the guise of an apothecary, selling a variety of [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting components"), as well as all base game [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone") and [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph"), and the diagrams for them. 

While he doesn't give a unique card, he will play [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [Nilfgaardian deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck"). 

## Biography

In [1271](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1271 "Timeline/Games"), the alchemist attended [The Summit at Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Summit_at_Loc_Muinne "The Summit at Loc Muinne"), condemning [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") for his persecution of mages. [Carduin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carduin "Carduin") ordered the alchemist beat and so, after the [dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon "Dragon") attack, he chose to flee the city. He fled to the woods near [Ban Gleán](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ban_Gle%C3%A1n "Ban Gleán"), surviving off roots. He then moved to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), where he started to beg, until he was noticed by the [King of Beggars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam"), who provided him work at the Crematory. 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), if met by [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), he asked the alchemist whether he was selling potion ingredients, but the man lied, saying he was just an apothecary and not a mage, but Geralt saw through his lies, as he had seen the man at the summit at [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne").
