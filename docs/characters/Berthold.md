## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), during the [witch hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunts "Witch hunts") in Novigrad, Berthold and wife [Anisse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anisse "Anisse") went into hiding in their house. Approached by [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"), they were invited on a ship to [Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir") to safety. With a plan to meet at [The Kingfisher Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn"), they became trapped as [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") surrounded their house. 

_If Geralt saves them:_ making the decision to leave none behind, Triss and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") went to Anisse and Berthold's hideout and killed the witch hunters there. Berthold and his wife went with the two to The Kingfisher Inn, and escaped to the [docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks") through the [Novigrad sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_sewers "Novigrad sewers") with the other mages. They boarded the ship and departed for a new and safer life in Kovir.
