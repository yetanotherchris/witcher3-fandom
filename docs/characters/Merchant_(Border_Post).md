## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** is on the [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") side of the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post") and right across the road from the [shady merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shady_merchant "Shady merchant"). Unlike the shady merchant though, this merchant sells some basic [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). He will not show up until rescued from the [Person(s) in distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_distress "Person\(s\) in distress") marker southeast of the Border Post and north of the [Hanged Man's Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Tree "Hanged Man's Tree"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Person(s) in Distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_Distress "Person\(s\) in Distress")

## Notes

  * According to an [unsent letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(Border_Post\) "Unsent letter") found by his cage, he is actually [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_\(people\) "Nilfgaardian \(people\)") and speaks little [Common](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Speech "Common Speech").
  * The [cage key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cage_key#Cage_key_\(Border_Post\) "Cage key") needed to free him can be a bit hard to get if the quest [A Deadly Plot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Deadly_Plot "A Deadly Plot") is started.

## Bugs

  * This merchant isn't always marked with an icon on the map.
  * Upon being interacted with, he might be "cloned" with a second, slightly different character model (beside the first, who just keeps doing his ambient animation), and is prone to the impossible to trade [bug](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants#Bugs "The Witcher 3 merchants").
