## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Rurik** (Polish: _Ruryk_) was the boatwright [Bjorg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bjorg "Bjorg")'s apprentice and had witnessed a particularly strong [water hag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag"), [Abaya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abaya "Abaya"), pull his cousin Ulf off the cliffs as they were trying to pull in their fishing lines.
