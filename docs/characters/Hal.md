## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hal** was a fistfighter from [Metinna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Metinna "Metinna") that [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") met in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), when he was looking for ushers in the [Novigrad Docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks"). Geralt can either pay 70 ![Crownitem](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/22/Crownitem.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/15?cb=20180420173351) or fight the two brothers and they will agree to usher for the play. If Geralt loses the fight they will still usher, but he will need to pay 140![Crownitem](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/22/Crownitem.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/15?cb=20180420173351).
