## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Rufus** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldier stationed between [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). Being a soldier, he knew the general situation of what was going on during the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War"). At one point, he ended up camping out with two others: a merchant named [Manfred](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manfred_\(merchant\) "Manfred \(merchant\)") and a sorcerer named [Theophile Maria Rosco](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Theophile_Maria_Rosco "Theophile Maria Rosco"). 

_If Geralt stops by to talk and refuses to get involved or helps Rufus:_ Rufus killed Manfred, believing he was a spy, though he had no proof. On searching Manfred's body, he felt the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") [florens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Floren "Floren") the merchant had been carrying were enough to justify his suspicions. 

_If Geralt stops by to talk but then defends Manfred:_ Sadly, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") had no other choice but to kill Rufus, who was dead set on killing Manfred, believing he was a spy. 

_If Geralt stops by but leaves after introduction:_ Rufus remained passive, and the original three men continued to camp out.
