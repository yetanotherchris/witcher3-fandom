## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** appears by the nearest road directly west of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort"), approximately 4 days after the area to his north-northwest is cleared of [rotfiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend "Rotfiend") and their [monster nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") (directly east of [Hindhold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindhold "Hindhold")) is destroyed. He arrives together with the [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") [army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army"), which populates the entire area. 

Appropriately for his company, his store is that of a generic [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), selling some weapons, lesser [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams"), and [components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"). Similarly to other [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") in liberated areas, his appearance is allowed to change between visits.
