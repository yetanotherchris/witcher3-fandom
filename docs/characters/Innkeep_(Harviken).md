## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **innkeep** in [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken") plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). He also sells a few cards from the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"), including: 

  * ["Nekker"](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_\(gwent_card\) "Nekker \(gwent card\)")
  * ["Vampire: Fleder"](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire:_Fleder_\(gwent_card\) "Vampire: Fleder \(gwent card\)")
  * ["Harpy"](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy_\(gwent_card\) "Harpy \(gwent card\)")
  * ["Ghoul"](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul_\(gwent_card\) "Ghoul \(gwent card\)")

He also sells [cherry cordial](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cherry_cordial "Cherry cordial"), [mandrake cordial](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mandrake_cordial "Mandrake cordial") and [dwarven spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarven_spirit "Dwarven spirit"). 

## Notes

  * If [Fists of Fury: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fists_of_Fury:_Skellige "Fists of Fury: Skellige") is active, instead of being in Harviken's tavern he will be part of the crowd spectating the fight with Einar. Because of this, if Einar has not been beaten in a fight yet he will be unavailable to trade with.
