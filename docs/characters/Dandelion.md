**Julian Alfred Pankratz** , **Viscount de Lettenhove** , better known as **Dandelion** (Polish: _Jaskier_), was a poet, minstrel, [bard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bard "Bard"), and close friend of the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia").

In The Witcher series, 'Jaskier' is the name of a prominent character who is also known as 'Dandelion'. He is a bard who often accompanies Geralt of Rivia on his adventures. The name 'Jaskier' is actually a Polish word that translates to 'Buttercup' in English. However, in the English translations of the books and the video games, his name is changed to 'Dandelion'. This character is known for his flamboyant personality, his love for music, and his knack for getting into trouble. He is a fan favorite and adds a touch of humor and lightness to the otherwise dark and serious tone of the series.

## Biography

### Early life

Not much is known about Dandelion's family except that he was of noble birth and was a cousin of [Ferrant de Lettenhove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ferrant_de_Lettenhove "Ferrant de Lettenhove"), the royal instigator of [Kerack](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kerack "Kerack").[4]

He received his early education in a temple school, where, according to Geralt, literacy was "beaten into him with a cane". However, he didn't truly get into poetry until he was 19, after being inspired by his love for [Countess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Countess "Countess") de Stael.[5] Dandelion studied the seven liberal arts for four years at [Oxenfurt University](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy") before later becoming a professor, but only taught students for a year before leaving the academy to travel the [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World"). He would, however, visit [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") from time to time to give guest lectures.[6]

Within a very few years he gained worldwide fame and became known as one of the best minstrels in the [Northern Kingdoms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"), with his best known song was the Ballad of the [Lion Cub of Cintra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"). 

### Meeting Geralt

In the mid-to-late [1240s](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1240 "Timeline"), Dandelion first met Geralt at a fête in the town of [Gulet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gulet "Gulet") in [Aedirn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aedirn "Aedirn"). There, the poet had knocked up a girl under the musician's podium, and her four sturdy brothers were looking for him all over town, threatening to geld him and cover him in pitch and sawdust. 

The so called [Valley of Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Valley_of_Flowers "Valley of Flowers") seemed just right the right location for the duo's swift escape, as it was supposed to be the edge of the inhabited world, the last outpost of civilization. It was in the small town of [Upper Posada](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Upper_Posada "Upper Posada"), located in a part of the [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms "Northern Realms") that later became [Dol Blathanna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna "Dol Blathanna"), that the pair had their first adventure together, involving a nasty [sylvan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sylvan "Sylvan") and an encounter with Dol Blathanna's native inhabitants, the [elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf"). During this adventure, Dandelion received his priceless lute from [Toruviel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toruviel "Toruviel").[7]

### Temple of Ellander

Later, in [1252](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1252 "Timeline"), Dandelion was in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") and heard word of Geralt being injured and recovering at the [Temple of Melitele](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_of_Melitele_\(Ellander\) "Temple of Melitele \(Ellander\)") in [Ellander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ellander "Ellander"). He visited the temple and spoke to [Nenneke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nenneke "Nenneke"), trying to win her favor, despite her annoyance of him flirting with one of the priestesses. 

After, he went and saw Geralt and the two spent time discussing Dandelion's education, as well as the lack of work for the witcher. Later that day, the two left for the [city of Ellander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ellander_\(city\) "Ellander \(city\)"). Once they arrived in the city, they were confronted by [Dennis Cranmer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dennis_Cranmer "Dennis Cranmer"), [Arthur Tailles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arthur_Tailles "Arthur Tailles") and Count [Falwick](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Falwick "Falwick"), and Geralt was forced to duel Tailles. After the conflict the witcher and the bard returned to the temple and said their goodbyes before leaving.[7]

### After the First Northern War

In [1263](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1263 "Timeline"), during the [First Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/First_Northern_War "First Northern War"), Dandelion was around the [Yaruga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yaruga "Yaruga"), but fled far north, unknowing of Geralt's fate. He went to [Pont Vanis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pont_Vanis "Pont Vanis"), in [Kovir and Poviss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir_and_Poviss "Kovir and Poviss"), to join King [Esterad Thyssen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Esterad_Thyssen "Esterad Thyssen")'s court. After a long time, he left the king's company to go to the [Hengfors League](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hengfors_League "Hengfors League"), into service with King [Niedamir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Niedamir "Niedamir"). He also became an associate of the [Redanian Secret Service](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redanian_Secret_Service "Redanian Secret Service"), answering to [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"), who requested information on King [Ervyll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ervyll "Ervyll") after Dandelion visited [Verden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Verden "Verden").[6]

### Bleobheris

In [1266](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1266 "Timeline"), he performed a concert reciting stories of Geralt and [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") to over a hundred at the giant oak [Bleobheris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bleobheris "Bleobheris") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). [Donimir of Troy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Donimir_of_Troy "Donimir of Troy"), [Sheldon Skaggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sheldon_Skaggs "Sheldon Skaggs") and [Radcliffe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radcliffe "Radcliffe") were among those who attended. 

After sneaking away from the concert, he travelled to a nearby town and visited the brothel [Mama Lantieri's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mama_Lantieri%27s "Mama Lantieri's"). At the brothel, he was approached by the mage [Rience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rience "Rience"), who was looking for Ciri. After refusing to give up information and attempting to run, Dandelion was tortured by Rience and two other men in the pigstys behind the brothel. Yennefer then appeared, scarring Rience's face, and creating a [portal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portal "Portal") for the two of them to escape.[6]

### Oxenfurt

After Yennefer's help, in early [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1267 "Timeline"), Dandelion went to Oxenfurt, to report details of Verden to Dijkstra. In the marketplace he marveled at the sights and services, noticing he was being tailed by two men, and so rode on to the [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"), where he signed in at the [main gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philosophers%27_Gate "Philosophers' Gate") and watched the students and tutors hard at work. 

As he entered the academy, he saw the young student [Shani](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shani "Shani"), and proceeded to pretend to flirt with her, as to detach attention from the two men following him. After she promised to deliver a message to Geralt, Dandelion went up to the men, who were spies working for Dijkstra. They led him to the spymaster, joined by [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart") and [Ori Reuven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ori_Reuven "Ori Reuven") for the meeting. Sigismund revealed their true interest was Geralt, and asked the bard for information on his location. Dandelion played dumb, having already seen Geralt in Oxenfurt, and so Philippa pushed for information about Ciri instead. After the bard promised to try and arrange a meeting, he left the building and spent several hours lingering around the academy, visiting different [faculties](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy_faculties "Oxenfurt Academy faculties"), until it got dark. He used the night to sneak across the city to [Wolfgang Amadeus Goatbeard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolfgang_Amadeus_Goatbeard "Wolfgang Amadeus Goatbeard")'s house, a brewer friend of his where Geralt was staying. After entering through the attic window, and discovering Geralt and Shani naked, Dandelion revealed Dijsktra's main objective was to find Ciri, and so if Geralt didn't flee, he would be forced to give up information. As the three went to leave through the window, they were interrupted by Philippa who [polymorphed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Polymorphism "Polymorphism") from an owl, and told them Dijkstra was under strict orders from King [Vizimir II](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizimir_II "Vizimir II") and Shani revealed she knew Rience, as he had visited a local store owned by [Myhrman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myhrman "Myhrman") two days previous. 

That night, the four went to the [docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Harbor "Oxenfurt Harbor") where they broke into Myhrman's house, and Geralt and Philippa tortured him for information on Rience. They were able to obtain an magic amulet that Geralt used to message Rience, and before dawn they prepared to meet him. While Geralt dealt with the [Michelet brothers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Michelet_brothers "Michelet brothers") and Rience, Dandelion and Shani stood on the palisades watching. After the last brother fell, Shani cried out and attempted to run and heal them as she was a medic, though Dandelion held her and told her it was too unsafe.[6]

### Meeting Ciri

After Oxenfurt, Dandelion stopped in the city of [Dorian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dorian "Dorian") in [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria"), where he told [Codringher and Fenn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Codringher_and_Fenn "Codringher and Fenn") of the events with Rience, somewhat limited by his promise of secrecy with Geralt. He next went with Geralt to [Hirundum](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hirundum "Hirundum"), the [Hofmeier family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hofmeier_family "Hofmeier family") farm, where he would spend nights discussing political matters with [Bernie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bernie_Hofmeier "Bernie Hofmeier") while Geralt hunted a monster. They were deep in a conversation during the stormy night of June 29th, when the young girl Ciri rode up to the farm, followed by Geralt and then Yennefer, all from different ways. The next morning's talk between the sorceress and witcher included an invitation for Dandelion to accompany them to [Thanedd Island](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thanedd_Island "Thanedd Island"). The following day they all went to Thanedd, and during the reception at [Loxia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loxia "Loxia"), Dandelion spent his time collecting gossip from the arriving mages.[8]

After the reception, Dandelion found some minstrels and performed ballads to them throughout the night, including _[Elusive](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elusive "Elusive")_. In the early hours of the morning however, a large-scale [coup](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thanedd_coup "Thanedd coup") took place between the Brotherhood, with many siding with the [North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms") or [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). Dandelion fled the conflict, and being told of Geralt's injuries and location by [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold"), after the beginning of the [Second Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Second_Northern_War "Second Northern War"), he travelled to the city of [Brugge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brugge_\(city\) "Brugge \(city\)"). There, he met with King [Venzlav](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Venzlav "Venzlav") and told him of his mission to find Geralt so as to receive safe conduct through [Brugge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brugge "Brugge"), which was met with a royal guard envoy. By early August, they escorted him to the [Ribbon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ribbon "Ribbon") river near Brokilon, though left Dandelion somewhat short of the riverbank, fearful of the [dryad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dryad "Dryad")'s arrows. He rode his horse [Pegasus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pegasus "Pegasus") deep into the forests where he found and began singing the ballad _[Elaine Ettariel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elaine_Ettariel "Elaine Ettariel")_ to the dryad [Fauve](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fauve "Fauve") and her friend, who led him to the healing Geralt. He told the witcher of the events of the Nilfgaardian invasion, including details of the fall of [Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rivia "Rivia") and [Aedirn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aedirn "Aedirn"). Fauve returned and the three of them noticed a group of [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") breaking through the tree line; Geralt left to aid them and so Fauve spoke quickly to Dandelion and then returned to the underbrush. After an hour, the witcher came back with Pegasus and another mare given to him by the Scoia'tael, before they set off from Brokilon to Nilfgaard, as [Milva](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Milva "Milva") had told the witcher of a [false Cirilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cirilla_Fiona_\(imposter\) "Cirilla Fiona \(imposter\)") (unknowingly) who had been transported there.[8]

### On the road

Geralt and Dandelion began their journey south following the Ribbon to the [Ceann Treise](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ceann_Treise "Ceann Treise") falls, where they planned their march to [Verden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Verden "Verden"), and then further onto Cintra along the [Yaruga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yaruga "Yaruga"). As they set off and a heavy storm hit, they were surprised by two [havekars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Havekar "Havekar"), [Kolda](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kolda "Kolda") and his boss, though were quickly let go due to the traders mistaking the pair for elves because of their Brokilonian clothes. As they went to leave, however, [Imperial agents](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Military_Intelligence "Nilfgaardian Military Intelligence") arrived to grab the havekar's tied-up prisoner: [Cahir Mawr Dyffryn aep Ceallach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cahir_Mawr_Dyffryn_aep_Ceallach "Cahir Mawr Dyffryn aep Ceallach"). While first furious at the belief the traders had killed Cahir, due to him being tied up in a coffin, the meeting then turned into a mutiny when the Imperials attacked their so-called "allies". Milva then arrived, having been following Geralt's trail, and helped the witcher kill the remaining Imperials. Spotting the coffin again, the group opened it and untied Cahir, and upon Geralt's first unveiling and near-distain of the [Vicovarian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vicovaro "Vicovaro"), they ambivalently decided to spare only his life, untie him, and leave him there to fend for himself, this being the second time the knight received mercy from the witcher, though he was warned of his doom should they meet again. 

Despite Geralt's warning, Cahir took off after the group, though he never directly approached them. It was only several days later when the group, fed up with knowing someone was following them but not sure who, confronted them only to find Cahir once again in their presence. As Geralt demanded Cahir fight him, the knight stated he didn't wish to fight but to join them in rescuing Ciri. Geralt strongly objected to this, threatening to kill him once more but Cahir, unwillingly to pull out his weapon like Geralt demanded, reiterated that he didn't want to fight, leaving the two in a stalemate. Only when Milva stated she could put an arrow in his horse to prevent Cahir from catching up with them as they continued on did Cahir mount and run off. 

Eventually, while travelling through the forest, they came across the dwarf [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") and [his company](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay%27s_company "Zoltan Chivay's company"). The dwarves told them of their plan to travel to the [Turlough Hills](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Turlough_Hills "Turlough Hills"), then the [Old Road](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Road "Old Road") to the river [Chotla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chotla "Chotla") in [Sodden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sodden "Sodden"), before continuing onto the [Ina](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ina "Ina"). Zoltan, after hearing of the three wanderers journey south toward enemy clutches, invited them to join the dwarves on their march east, which was accepted.[9]

### Ciri returns

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Dandelion was contacted by Ciri for aid in fixing a phylactery, which could either be used to cast or lift a curse; Ciri kept her objective to herself and Dandelion was unwilling to pry any further. Unfortunately, with mage hunts going on in Novigrad, Dandelion decided to turn to [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley"), a member of the [Big Four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four") crime lords of Novigrad. Junior agreed to find someone to repair the phylactery but for a steep price: he wanted Dandelion to steal [Sigi Reuven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra")'s treasure and who was another Big Four. 

Dandelion eventually conjured up a plan with the help of [Dudu](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dudu "Dudu") and [Kalkstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kalkstein "Kalkstein") and while he succeeded in getting the treasure, the commander of the [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard"), [Caleb Menge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Menge "Caleb Menge"), found out and took the treasure for himself. Believing the bard had betrayed him, Whoreson Junior's men chased Dandelion and Ciri all the way to [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle"). Ciri manged to barely escape from being killed by teleporting away, but Dandelion was unfortunately captured by Menge's men and was jailed while Menge waited to publically execute the bard on Oxenfurt.

## Notes

  * Ironically, while Dandelion is known for his promiscuousness, his mount, [Pegasus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pegasus "Pegasus"), is a white gelding.
  * Dandelion's original Polish name, "Jaskier", literally means "Buttercup", but was changed in the English translation because "Buttercup" sounded too feminine in English. 
    * To add to the confusion, in the Czech translation of the books and the game, Dandelion's name is "Marigold" and [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") is called "Triss Ranuncul" instead.
  * All the journal entries and character descriptions in the second and third game are written by Dandelion.

### Nationality

  * In _[Blood of Elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_of_Elves "Blood of Elves")_ , Dandelion claims to support [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"), head of [Redanian Secret Service](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redanian_Secret_Service "Redanian Secret Service"), out of "patriotic duty". It's uncertain whether he means patriotism towards [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") in particular or towards [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms "Northern Realms") as a whole; it's also possible that the bard is simply being sarcastic.
  * With Dandelion's cousin Ferrant being the royal instigator in Kerack and the bard himself being a frequent visitor of King [Ethain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ethain "Ethain")'s court, it could be speculated that De Lettenhove family is based in the broad [Cidaris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cidaris "Cidaris")-Kerack region, northwest of Brokilon. 
    * While Bremervoord, stated to be the poet's birthplace in _[The Witcher: Sirens of the Deep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher:_Sirens_of_the_Deep "The Witcher: Sirens of the Deep")_ , is located in the region mentioned above, it likely isn't his homeland in the book canon as he never raises this topic in the original _[A Little Sacrifice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Little_Sacrifice "A Little Sacrifice")_ short-story despite talking with other characters about this duchy a number of times.
