## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **herbalist** appears in the [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp") once the area is liberated. His appearance is allowed to change between visits, and he sells the same things like any other [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") appearing at liberated [abandoned sites](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") \- mostly [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and a handful of [formulae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formulae "Formulae").
