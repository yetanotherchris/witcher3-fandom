# Innkeeper

Throughout the games, there are several **innkeepers**. 

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

There are several **innkeepers** in _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_. They can all arrange for [storage and accommodations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_character_interaction "The Witcher character interaction"), and some will even impart some useful information for the right price: 

  * Innkeeper Olaf in the [Outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts_Inn "Outskirts Inn")
  * Innkeeper at [The Hairy Bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Hairy_Bear "The Hairy Bear")
  * Innkeeper at [The New Narakort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Narakort "The New Narakort")
  * Innkeeper in [Murky Waters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Country_Inn "Country Inn") (male)
  * Innkeeper in [Murky Waters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Country_Inn "Country Inn") (female)

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 icon innkeep](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/6/69/Tw3_icon_innkeep.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/28?cb=20170901053623)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Innkeepers "Category:Innkeepers")

Innkeepers provide conversation, refreshment, and often a few hands of [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). Innkeepers also function as [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"). Often innkeepers will pay higher prices for miscellaneous items but they tend to have lower amounts of crowns available. It is recommended to sell [food and drink](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink") items to innkeepers, as they offer a +5-12% increase in profit.
