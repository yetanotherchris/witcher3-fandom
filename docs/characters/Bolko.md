## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Bolko** was the village elder of [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). He is one of the few individuals who treats [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") warmly upon first meeting, comparing him to the Witchers from storybooks he read when younger, and offering sincere gratitude once his [Contract](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_contracts "The Witcher 3 contracts") is completed. 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he sent a [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_to_pellar "Letter to pellar") to [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") instructing him to host a [Forefathers' Eve](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forefathers%27_Eve "Forefathers' Eve"), a ceremony used for giving peace to the dead, due to the increasing number of dead souls who would "never know comfort".
