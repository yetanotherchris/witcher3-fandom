The **old brickmaker** was one of the workers living in the [village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brickmakers%27_village "Brickmakers' village") in the [swamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp "Swamp") near [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"). 

## Biography

When he was just six years old, a brick fell on his head which made his speech somewhat defect. However, he could still provide a lot of information about the swamp.[1]

At some point between [1270](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1270 "Timeline/Games") and [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he moved to [Mire Landing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mire_Landing "Mire Landing") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and at times reminisced about brickworks near Vizima.[2]
