## Biography

Prior to [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest")'s death, the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Letho](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letho "Letho") decided to hunt down Louis to turn him in for a nice reward, as regular witcher contracts were hard to come by at the time. Louis turned out to be very easy to capture, as he was enjoying himself in the Golden Hanged Man in [Hagge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hagge "Hagge") just before Letho nabbed him. However, as the two talked to pass the time until the guard post opened up, Louis proved to be a very nice conversationalist and instead Letho let him go. 

_If Letho was spared:_ Sometime later in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Louis and Letho met once more in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), but this time Letho himself was a wanted man. Louis then suggested that the witcher hide out in the abandoned [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor") to try and escape any bounty hunters looking for him. However, Louis then decided the bounty on Letho was too good to pass up and informed a famous [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") bounty hunter, [Arnout Vester](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arnout_Vester "Arnout Vester"), on where Letho was hiding for a cut of the reward. 

    _If[Ghosts of the Past](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghosts_of_the_Past "Ghosts of the Past") is completed:_ Louis, hiding out at a bandit camp in central Velen, was soon approached by Letho with another witcher, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), in tow. He tried to poorly lie about Arnout finding out where Letho was, but soon fessed up before being cut down by the witchers and left to bleed out.
