**Claer** was a [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") woman living in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") in the [1250s](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1250 "Timeline/Games")[1] and was a subject of the violent local lord, [Ignatius Verrieres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignatius_Verrieres "Ignatius Verrieres"). 

## Biography

Her husband [Volker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Volker "Volker") sent a petition to the court in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), listing out all their grievances against Ignatius, like how he refused to make amends to his subjects when he trampled their crops and killed whoever when it suited him. The court recognized these as valid reasons and waived Ignatius' subjects bonds and duties to him. 

With this, Claer and Volker set up a village named [Hovel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Village_\(White_Orchard\) "Abandoned Village \(White Orchard\)") to the south of White Orchard village and appeared to be doing well. However, Ignatius came to the village, under the pretext of asking them to come back to White Orchard, but instead he killed every last one of them. 

Because Claer had such a sudden and cruel death, she was still tied to the world of the living in the form of the [Devil by the Well](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil_by_the_Well "Devil by the Well"). 

## Notes

  * Claer was a friend of the local herbalist, [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira"). In the [leaked materials](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content"), originally the two were supposed to be twin sisters but this was later re-written.
