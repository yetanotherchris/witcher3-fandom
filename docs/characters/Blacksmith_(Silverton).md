\- Blacksmith's comment on [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori")  
  
  
This **blacksmith** has an outdoors shop right in the middle of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") district. Aside from the usual stuff sold by other Novigraadan [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), he also sells the [blunt sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blunt_sword "Blunt sword") and 4 [bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts") diagrams. He is also a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player using the [Nilfgaardian deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck").
