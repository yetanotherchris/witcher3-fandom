## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Nikolas Friedman** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") officer serving as [Commander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Commander "Commander") of the [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") City Regiment in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

At one point he signed off on a contract as a [mysterious monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gael "Gael") was attacking and killing people in Oxenfurt as he didn't have the manpower to deal with it with the war going on.
