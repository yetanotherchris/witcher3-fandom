**Yoana** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") master [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") from [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") who moved to [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") to try and branch out. She was also a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player with a fairly strong [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck").

In the Witcher universe, Yoana is a master armorer hailing from Undvik. She relocated to Crow's Perch in Velen to broaden her craft. Master armorer Yoana opted to be Fergus Graem's assistant due to societal biases. Anticipating skepticism about her skills as a human female armorer, especially against a dwarf like Fergus, they agreed on a setup where Yoana, despite doing all the work, would be seen as Fergus's assistant.

## Biography

Despite being a master armorer, Yoana became [Fergus Graem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fergus_Graem "Fergus Graem")'s assistant because many would never believe she, a [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") female, would be capable of making master armor. Fergus, being a [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf"), was far more likely to be perceived as a master armorer and thus the two struck an agreement for her to be his assistant, but she was the one crafting all the complex pieces. They began working for the [swordsmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swordsmith_\(Mulbrydale\) "Swordsmith \(Mulbrydale\)") in [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale"), but soon moved to Crow's Perch due to the demand from the [Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s men and to become independent. 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), when the local [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") [commander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harmon_aep_Groenekan "Harmon aep Groenekan") came by and ordered a suit of armor that could withstand a shot from a [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow"), Fergus stated it couldn't be done and Yoana, having enough, stated it could actually, with the [right tools](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/High-quality_smithing_tools "High-quality smithing tools"). 

_If Geralt helps Yoana out and wears the armor:_ With the special tools from Skellige and help from [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), Yoana created a piece of armor that, true to her word, effectively repelled crossbow bolts while Fergus' didn't, showing everyone who was the real master armorer. Impressed, the commander promoted her to be the head armorer and Fergus, saddened, got ready to leave. However, she offered for him to stay, but this time in the appropriate roles: she as the master armorer and Fergus as the apprentice, and the dwarf agreed. 

_If Geralt refuses to wear the test armor:_ Having nobody to test her armor for her, the commander pronounced her as a fraud. Upset, she packed up her things and left Velen for parts unknown.
