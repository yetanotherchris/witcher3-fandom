## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed **pirate leader** is the boss to all pirates in the [Spitfire Bluff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spitfire_Bluff "Spitfire Bluff") area in western [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

When the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III") broke out, he took advantage of [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") and [Redanian forces](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") being too busy with each other, allowing him to ramp up raids. The old pirate was wary of landing near populated areas, but sometime in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), having suffered damage to his ship in a storm, he was forced to disembark on Velen's shores for repairs. Much to his surprise, there was no force to oppose his [gang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gang "Gang") on the land either, only occasionally having to deal with the [local warlord](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s men who proved to be no challenge.[1]

Emboldened by this development, the pirate setup a [land base](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate_base_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Pirate base \(Spitfire Bluff\)") on a peninsula northwest of [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle") and, having heard of a [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") nearby, decided to build a brand new ship instead of merely repairing the old damaged boat.[1] His men took over the boatyard, and while waiting for the new ship to be built,[2] the pirate leader sent out others [north](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks") to search for [Queen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Political_titles "Political titles") [Zuleyka](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zuleyka "Zuleyka")'s [treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen_Zuleyka%27s_Treasure "Queen Zuleyka's Treasure").[1][3]

_If Geralt killed the pirate leader:_ The veteran pirate's luck eventually ran out when he crossed paths with the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), who was no match for a band of bandits skilled only in robbing peasants.
