## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Nissa** was an [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") performer who was a part of the performing trio, [The Puffins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Puffins "The Puffins"). [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") met her in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), when he was looking for people to spread the word about [Madame Irina](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Irina_Renarde "Irina Renarde")'s new play.
