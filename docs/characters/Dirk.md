## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Dirk** was a cruel and sadistic torturer who worked for the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter"). One of his favorite ways to torture mages was taking a fingernail, or as he said, "better three". He died when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss "Triss") destroyed the [witch hunters' outpost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_Hunter_Barracks_Outpost "Witch Hunter Barracks Outpost") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games").
