**Artis** was a fallen [druid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") of [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") circle who at some point become priest to cruel deity of [Svalblod](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svalblod "Svalblod") and as such had also became the leader of the [Vildkaarls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildkaarl "Vildkaarl").
