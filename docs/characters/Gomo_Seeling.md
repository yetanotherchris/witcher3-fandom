## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[Count](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Maravel%27s_Correspondence "Count Maravel's Correspondence") **Gomo Seeling** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") aristocrat and a close friend of the [Vegelbud family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family") as well as a passionate of [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck"). This love for gamb however didn't bring much fortune to his family since both him and his father lost their houses in the [Nimnar valley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nimnar_valley "Nimnar valley") for their debts. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can play with him during the masquerade ball at the [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence") and will receive the [Bruxa card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire:_Bruxa_\(gwent_card\) "Vampire: Bruxa \(gwent card\)") if he beats him.
