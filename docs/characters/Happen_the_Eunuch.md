**Happen the Eunuch** was an assistant of [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"), and the bookkeeper of the [bathhouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund%27s_Bathhouse "Sigismund's Bathhouse") of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), where he had the assignment of receiving guests and writing their names in a ledger. 

## Biography

Dijkstra once helped Happen escape from a [Kovirian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir_and_Poviss "Kovir and Poviss") prison the night before his execution, thus placing the eunuch in his eternal debt. In turn, this caused Dijkstra to trust his assistant completely as he believed saving one's life meant they wouldn't turn against him. 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Happen answered the bathhouse doors to the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), who was looking for Dijkstra. While at first surprised the [spymaster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spy "Spy") wanted to see the witcher, Happen led Geralt to get changed and then onto the [Big Four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four")'s meeting. Following this, after Dijkstra and Geralt had done some further digging into the recent robbery of the bathhouse, Happen was instructed to drain pool four and bring the ledger for the suspect name: [Margrave](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margrave "Margrave") [Henckel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henckel "Henckel").
