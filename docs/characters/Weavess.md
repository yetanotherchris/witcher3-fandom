**Weavess** was one of the three [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones") in [Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crookback_Bog "Crookback Bog"). The other two were [Whispess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whispess "Whispess") and [Brewess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brewess "Brewess") and they were the supposed to be the daughters of [She-Who-Knows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whispering_Hillock "Whispering Hillock"). 

Like her sisters, she can shapeshift into a young woman and take the shape of those she feeds on and, like Whispess, she can also transform into a flock of crows. The sisters as a whole also have some power over monsters in their region, though it's unclear if this is done by one particular sister or if all three have to assist in this endeavor. 

## Biography

_If Ciri doesn't come back from fighting the White Frost:_ Geralt afterwards ventured into the Bog and killed Weavess, reclaiming Cirilla's medallion. 

## Journal entry

    _Though the youngest of the three Crones, the Weavess was not one jot less ugly or evil than her older sisters. It was she who wove their[magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") tapestries of [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") hair gathered as tribute from the young children of nearby villages during their "cutting" ceremonies. These tapestries were how the worshipers of the Ladies of the Wood made contact with and paid homage to their deities._
    _The fight on[Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") was meant to put an end to all three Crones, but Weavess evaded her elder sisters' fate. She was able to escape by transforming into a flock of ravens and took [Cirilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")'s medallion with her._
