## Appearance(s)

### Games

The unnamed **gravedigger** in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") is a character in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can learn more about the monster from the village's cemetery, and haggle for the price of killing it from him.
