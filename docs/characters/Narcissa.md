## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Narcissa** was a [prostitute](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prostitute "Prostitute") working in the [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). Despite the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") condemning prostitution, many [Temple Guards](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") use the Passiflora's services and treat the women well. 

## Notes

  * Due to her using the same hair model as [Tamara Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger"), some players confuse her with the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s daughter.
