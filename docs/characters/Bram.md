## Biography

In May [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Bram was attacked by a [royal griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_griffin "Royal griffin") at the [ford](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ford "Ford"), while transporting a wagon of goods to his store. 

As the [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") neared the ford, they heard Bram yelling for help and found him hiding under his wagon while nearby the griffin feasted on his dead [mare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse"). After Vesemir was clawed by the beast and it had flown off with the horse, the witchers helped the Bram up, who was eager to reward them. 

_If Geralt accepts a reward:_ Bram was very thankful to the witchers, so he gave them a reward and went on his way. 

_If Geralt refuses the reward:_ Geralt told the merchant that they had seen someone in trouble and decided to help, and that they did not need a reward, which surprised Bram, who thought that witchers couldn't feel empathy. Later for helping him, Bram's cousin [Elsa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elsa "Elsa") gave Geralt four [roasted chicken legs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roasted_chicken_leg "Roasted chicken leg"). 

Bram then headed back to White Orchard and set up shop next to the [White Orchard inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_inn "White Orchard inn").
