## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** appears at the [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp") and north of [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle"), after the site is liberated from pirates. His appearance is allowed to change and he sells the same things as a generic [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer").
