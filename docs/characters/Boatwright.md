## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Boatwright** in [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton") plays [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can play him in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") and, if beaten, can win the [Letho of Gulet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letho_of_Gulet_\(gwent_card\) "Letho of Gulet \(gwent card\)") card. 

He complains that it's a bad time to be a boatwright considering there are no fish.
