Hanne Jooker, known as the **Loan Shark** , was a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant "Merchant") in [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). While he normally just gave out loans, he did resell pawned off items like the book [Change your life! A Handbook](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Change_your_life!_A_Handbook "Change your life! A Handbook"). He also played [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck").

## Biography

At some point, the merchant came into business with the pirate [Morkvarg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg "Morkvarg") from [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). He gave the man a loan, and the two went into business often. 

_If Geralt lets Morkvarg live:_ In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), on Morkvarg's instructions, [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") came to the loan shark's pawnshop and retrieved an [alchemy formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Werewolf_decoction "Torn-out page: Werewolf decoction") and the sword [Deithwen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deithwen_\(sword\) "Deithwen \(sword\)"), which Morkvarg had left there.

However, this has since been patched out as it allowed for convenient money-making exploits.
