## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **herbalist** outside the [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") sells mostly [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). Oddly for a non-moving merchant, his appearance is allowed to change. 

## Bugs

  * Because he has dynamic appearance, he is prone to the impossible to trade [bug](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants#Bugs "The Witcher 3 merchants"), and triggers it very often.
  * Despite being located in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s outskirts, his store and pricing is that of a [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist").
