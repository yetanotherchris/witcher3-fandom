**Igor** was one of [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley")'s employees and the administrator of the [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_arena "Whoreson Junior's arena") of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

_If Geralt goes to the arena alone :_ Igor and all his men were killed by [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") inn his search for Whoreson Junior. 

_If Geralt helps Cleaver's men:_ Fearing an upcoming war between him and Whoreson Junior, [Cleaver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese") decided to strike first, and thus sent his [gang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crownsplitters "Crownsplitters") and Geralt to raid Whoreson Junior's main hideouts, the [casino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_casino "Whoreson Junior's casino") and the arena, where Igor and all his men were killed.
