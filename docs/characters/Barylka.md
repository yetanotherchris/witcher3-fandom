## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Barylka** was a stray [dog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") whose owner had been killed when bandits attacked their small village, [Drudge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drudge "Drudge") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

## Biography

_If Geralt meets and follows the dog:_ [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), having rescued the dog from a pack of [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)"), noted the dog was rather friendly and had a [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Key_\(quest_item\) "Key \(quest item\)") sewn into its collar. The dog then led Geralt to his former owner's hut and the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") deduced what'd happened. However, when he went back outside to see Barylka, the dog was nowhere to be found. 

_If Geralt doesn't save the dog:_ Unfortunately Barylka met their end when a pack of wolves attacked the dog, killing it. 

_If Geralt helps but doesn't follow:_ Barylka tried to get the witcher to follow, but for whatever reason Geralt didn't. The dog was never seen again.
