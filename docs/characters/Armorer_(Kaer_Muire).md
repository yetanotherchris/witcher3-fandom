## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Armorer** in [Kaer Muire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Muire "Kaer Muire") plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") and uses the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"). He sells [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"), [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyphs "Glyphs"), and some crafting diagrams. 

Depending on choices made during [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"), he may later become unavailable when the story progresses to the early parts of [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone") quest.
