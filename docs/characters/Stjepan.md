## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Stjepan** was [The Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Alchemy "The Alchemy")'s innkeep and a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player, using the [Nilfgaardian Empire deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck"). If beaten, he'll give the [Yennefer gwent card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg_\(gwent_card\) "Yennefer of Vengerberg \(gwent card\)"). 

On top of the usual food and drink, he also sells three gwent cards: 

  * [Dwarven Skirmisher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarven_Skirmisher_\(gwent_card\) "Dwarven Skirmisher \(gwent card\)") (10 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"))
  * [Mahakaman Defender](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mahakaman_Defender_\(gwent_card\) "Mahakaman Defender \(gwent card\)") (20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"))
  * [Vrihedd Brigade Veteran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vrihedd_Brigade_Veteran_\(gwent_card\) "Vrihedd Brigade Veteran \(gwent card\)") (20 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"))

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What gwent deck does Stjepan use?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Stjepan uses a Gwent deck with neutral cards. Players can win the Yennefer of Vengerberg hero card from him in the quest 'Gwent: Playing Innkeeps' in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What does Stjepan sell besides food and drink?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Besides food and drink, Stjepan sells Gwent cards. These are available in his store, with prices potentially discounted after completing the main quest 'The Play's the Thing' correctly. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is Stjepan's role in The Alchemy?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Stjepan serves as the innkeeper of The Alchemy in Oxenfurt, featured in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. He plays Gwent with the Nilfgaardian Empire deck and offers the Yennefer card as a reward if beaten. Additionally, he sells Gwent cards like Dwarven Skirmisher and Mahakaman Defender. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Which gwent card does Stjepan give if beaten?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Stjepan gives the Yennefer of Vengerberg hero card when defeated in Gwent during the "Gwent: Playing Innkeeps" quest in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Who recommends playing gwent against Stjepan?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Players are advised to challenge Stjepan at the Alchemy Inn in Oxenfurt if they believe they are skilled at gwent. Stjepan is recognized for his exceptional ability in the game. 

Provided by: Fandom
