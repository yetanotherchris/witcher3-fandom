## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Cerbin**[1] is an [elf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") captured by some humans east of the [Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Novigrad\) "Crossroads \(Novigrad\)"). 

After freeing him, he walks to an unmarked Scoia'tael camp. Starting where you freed him, walking south, you'll hit the road, don't follow it. Cross it and the camp should be on the other side. The merchant marker should show when you get close. 

It is advisable to take the time and escort the merchant through the woods until he reaches his camp, as he can get stuck between some wolves on the way. Unlike with other persons in distress, meditating immediately after his release doesn't seem to fix the issue, but he also makes good on his promise for a reward and yields 150[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") once talked to at the camp.
