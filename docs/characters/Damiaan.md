## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Damiaan** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") archer who served on [HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/HMS_Oxenfurt-Tretogor "HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor"), protecting [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"). 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he and his comrades, [Caspar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caspar "Caspar"), [Jouke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jouke "Jouke"), [Freek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freek "Freek") and witch hunter [Egbert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Egbert "Egbert") were commanded by King Radovid to stay near the gate to [St. Gregory's Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/St._Gregory%27s_Bridge "St. Gregory's Bridge") and secure the area. He died by the hand of either [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), [Ves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ves "Ves"), [Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roche "Roche") or some [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") partisan.
