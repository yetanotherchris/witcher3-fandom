## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ruar** was a warrior of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") who can be seen repairing his hut in the [town](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") under [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") while his two friends, [Folmer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Folmer "Folmer") and other warrior, are advising him to not eat so much before the raid.
