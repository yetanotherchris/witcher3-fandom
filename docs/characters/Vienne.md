## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Vienne** is a former member of [Jad Karadin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jad_Karadin "Jad Karadin")'s gang and an [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") sharpshooter, being able to hit a sparrow from a hundred paces. Other members of the gang were [Hammond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hammond "Hammond"), [Lund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lund "Lund") and [Selyse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Selyse "Selyse"). 

She is one of the people involved in the death of the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Aiden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aiden "Aiden") of the [School of the Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Cat "School of the Cat"). 

Before joining Karadin, she was a guerilla-fighter of the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael"). After her unit got decimated she joined Karadin's assassins. 

Vienne can be encountered in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in the [Seven Cats Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Seven_Cats_Inn "Seven Cats Inn"), where she is getting drunk when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert") enter the tavern. She can provide useful information for the quest [Following the Thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Following_the_Thread_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Following the Thread \(The Witcher 3\)"), such as the names of her former colleagues and where to find them. 

Furthermore, she claims that Jad Karadin has cut all ties with his former employees and he is rumoured to have changed his ways. She recalls that she perhaps had a hand in Aiden's death, but not the details. 

When Vienne has finished her story, Geralt can either advise Lambert to "do what he thinks is right" or suggest Lambert spares Vienne. If the second option is chosen, Lambert agrees to Geralt's suggestion and the two leave the place peacefully. If the first option is chosen, however, Vienne gets enraged over what she feels is Geralt's hypocrisy and calls other elven patrons of the Seven Cats Inn to her aid. A short fight ensues in which Vienne is killed.

**What role did Vienne play in Aiden's death?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Vienne was directly involved in Aiden's death. During a heated exchange over a failed contract, she lost her nerve as a sniper and shot Aiden in the eye, resulting in his death. This event contributed to the breakup of Karadin's group. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What led Vienne to join Jad Karadin's gang?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Vienne joined Jad Karadin's gang after her Scoia'tael unit was destroyed. As an elven sharpshooter, she became part of the group with members like Hammond, Lund, and Selyse. Vienne was involved in the death of the witcher Aiden from the School of the Cat. She can be found in Novigrad at the Seven Cats Inn, where she provides information about her former gang members. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What was Vienne's involvement with the Scoia'tael?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Vienne was once part of the Scoia'tael, a group engaged in conflicts with human forces. She later joined Jad Karadin's gang, known for her exceptional archery skills. This shift reflects a change in her alliances and roles. 

Provided by: Fandom
