## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Fugas** was one of many servants of the [Ladies of the Woods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones"), who lived in [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)"). 

## Journal entry

    _The[devil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sylvan "Sylvan") Fugas held a very prestigious and responsible position during the Crones' sabbath: that of goon restricting access to the peak. He in no way resembled [Torque](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torque "Torque"), the irritating but harmless "deovel" [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [I](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") had encountered many, many years prior._
    _Fugas' size resembled that of a somewhat overgrown[troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll "Troll"), and he likewise displayed a troll's lack of subtlety. There was not a jot of mischievous puck or cunning verbal trickster about this "devil." Instead, he carried out his task with the commitment and professionalism of a [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") bouncer._
    _Trying to stop Geralt, though, turned out to be Fugas' dumbest and final idea, ending both his life and his flourishing career in the Crones' employ._
