The **armorer** in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") operated out of a small store in an alcove located on the square's west side. In addition to a standard [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer")'s inventory he also sells a few [bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts") diagrams, two maps for witcher gear [treasure hunt quests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_treasure_hunts "The Witcher 3 treasure hunts") and a few other miscellaneous items. He is one of very few prominent [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") who doesn't play [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). 

As of [Patch 4.01](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.01_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.01 \(The Witcher 3\)"), he also sells relic [witcher's steel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher%27s_steel_sword#Witcher's_steel_sword_\(relic\) "Witcher's steel sword") and [silver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher%27s_silver_sword#Witcher's_silver_sword_\(relic\) "Witcher's silver sword") swords. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What unique items does the armorer in Novigrad sell?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The armorer in Hierarch Square, Novigrad, sells a typical Novigrad armorer's inventory, including various armors, diagrams, crafting components, and all glyphs. No unique items are specified. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What changes occurred to the Novigrad armorer in Patch 4.01?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Patch 4.01 updated the Novigrad armorer's inventory to include an upgraded New Game + version of armor, influenced by Undvik mastercraft. This version is available at level 41, replacing the level 16 version, and can be purchased in a regular game. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What maps for witcher gear can be found with the Novigrad armorer?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Novigrad armorer offers maps for witcher gear diagrams. These can be obtained from all armorers in Novigrad, Oxenfurt, and Skellige. They are crucial for crafting gear like Bear, Cat, Griffin, Manticore, Wolf, and Viper School Gear. Players can find these diagrams through Scavenger Hunt quests, categorized as Treasure Hunts. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Where is the armorer's shop located in Hierarch Square?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The armorer's shop in Hierarch Square is situated in an alcove on the west side of the square. Known for the greeting 'Top notch swords!', this Novigrad armorer provides a standard inventory and some bolt diagrams. 

Provided by: Fandom 

## Notes

  * In [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), his standard greeting "Top notch swords!" was replaced by a more generic "Welcome, welcome!", as the former didn't make sense for an armorer. However, as the change proved to be quite unpopular with players, it was reverted back in the next patch, 4.01, and the two new relic swords were added to his store instead.

## Bugs

  * It is possible that the armorer may be absent from his shop during working hours. Meditating should fix the issue. Note that this is a persistent bug that affects several merchants of all kinds.
