## Appearance(s)

### Games

**Valgridovt**[2] was a [prince](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prince "Prince") from a distant land relative to the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent"), who was fleeing his homeland around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), after a coup had overthrown the royal family in his kingdom. 

## Biography

Shortly after he fled his home island[3] and headed for [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair"), Valgridovt lost his life when his ship crashed on a [small island](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall%27s_Finger "Hemdall's Finger") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), while being pursued by enemies sent by the [usurper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dirgelech "Dirgelech") after him and his family. His last comfort was that his other family members traveled on a separate ship[2] and continued ahead towards the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent").
