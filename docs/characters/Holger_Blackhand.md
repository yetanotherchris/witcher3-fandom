**Holger Blackhand** was the jarl of [Faroe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe "Faroe"), and the leader of [Clan Dimun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Dimun "Clan Dimun"). 

## Journal entry

    _The dour-faced jarl of Faroe owed his nickname to the dark coloring of his hand and forearm, left there by frostbite he suffered when his longship hit an underwater boulder during a freak autumn blizzard._
    _Even judging by[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") standards, where pillaging and plundering are practically rites of passage, Holger was considered a brutal pirate and raider. This opinion surely resulted from the fact that he attacked the villages and ships of enemy Skellige clans with as much nerve as he did the coastal settlements of [Cidaris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cidaris "Cidaris"), [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire")._
