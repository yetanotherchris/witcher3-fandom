## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Napp** was a peasant who lived in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). One night in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he got extremely drunk and proceeded to set fire to the local [blacksmith's workshop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Willis%27_forge "Willis' forge"), believing the [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf"), [Willis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Willis "Willis"), had been profiting off of helping the [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") not to mention Napp's general dislike for non-humans. However, on the way home, he ran into [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") and while he managed to escape them, he suffered a bad injury to his right hand. 

_If Geralt turns him in:_ Napp was sentenced to death by hanging as the Nilfgaardians viewed his act as a crime against their war efforts. 

_If Geralt accepts his bribe:_ Napp wasn't turned in and the witcher simply told Willis he couldn't find the arsonist.
