## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Vernossiel** was the commander of a small [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") faction in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") that was attacking [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") army supply transports during the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War"), reasoning that it wasn't civilians they were killing but soldiers who killed, tortured, and raped their fellow brethren. 

_If Geralt kills her:_ she met her end in Velen, when Geralt killed her and her small band. 

_If Geralt lets her be but then tells[Felix Grubb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Felix_Grubb "Felix Grubb") about them:_ presumably her group was attacked by Redanian soldiers though she managed to escape. Knowing Geralt was the only one to know about them, she later tried to ambush the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") near [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), resulting in her death. 

_If Geralt lets her group be:_ she continued to attack supply caravans with her group.

  * If Vernossiel and her unit are killed at their camp, leaving and then returning to the camp some time later causes all of them to respawn.

## Notes

  * If you decide to betray her she'll try to kill you near [Glory Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Gate "Glory Gate").
  * Vernossiel was intended to be [Iorveth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iorweth "Iorweth")'s daughter. However, after Iorveth's cut this detail was scrapped as well and her quest was redesigned.[_[citation needed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Citations_needed "Category:Citations needed")_]
