## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Yorg** was a man that, in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), was chained up to a rock in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), having been accused of killing his uncle, [Gretter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gretter "Gretter"), after [his sister](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yorg%27s_sister "Yorg's sister") told him Gretter had molested her. However, this was actually a lie and his sister simply didn't like the uncle and thought he stank, not realizing Yorg would kill him. 

_If Geralt frees him:_ he will thank the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), promising to repay him should they ever cross paths again. 

_If Geralt leaves him to his fate:_ Yorg soon died from the punishment he was sentenced to.
