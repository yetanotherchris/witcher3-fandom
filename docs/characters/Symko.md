## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Symko** (Polish: _Szymek_) was an [orphan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orphans "Orphans") in [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") whose father, [Borzoy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Borzoy "Borzoy"), was killed by a large and nasty [cockatrice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice "Cockatrice") known as the [Shrieker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shrieker "Shrieker"). As his mother had died a long time ago, leaving him without a family, [Chet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chet "Chet") took him in. Still processing his father's death and the war going on, he tends to not interact with others.
