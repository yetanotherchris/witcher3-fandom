## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed **hunter** living in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), observed large tracks in the woods near his village, and was fearful to follow them. He later found a dozen dead [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)") while hunting in the woods, with many of them uneaten. He put up a [notice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract_on..._Gods_Know_What_On! "Contract on... Gods Know What On!") and in time, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") read it and could choose to help.
