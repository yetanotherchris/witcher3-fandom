## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Dromle**[1] was a peasant from [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

## Biography

In May [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he and his friend, [Mikah](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikah "Mikah"), were having a drink at the [White Orchard inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_inn "White Orchard inn") when the [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") passed through in their search for [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"). 

_If Geralt approaches the two:_ Dromle showed obvious contempt towards the witcher, telling Geralt to leave and how he was ruining their drink. 

    _If Geralt uses Axii on Mikah:_ Dromle tried to attract attention form the others over this [witchcraft](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") but quieted down when Geralt threatened him.
    _If Geralt simply presses on them to answer:_ Dromle finally answered the question, stating they haven't seen or heard of such a woman.
