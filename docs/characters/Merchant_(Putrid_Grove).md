## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Merchant** resides in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove"), an enclave within the district of [Lacehalls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lacehalls "Lacehalls") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He is a [Gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players") ([Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck")). He sells [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components").
