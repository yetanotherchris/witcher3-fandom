**Djenge Frett** was a [bounty hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bounty_hunter "Bounty hunter") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") who considered himself the most famous bounty hunter north of the [Yaruga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yaruga "Yaruga"). 

## Biography

In 1272, he found himself scouring Skellige for the [Grossbart Brothers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grossbart_Brothers "Grossbart Brothers"), a trio who had done so many misdeeds a bounty had been placed on their heads. 

_If Geralt helps him:_ with the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")'s help, Djenge was able to bring an end to the brothers' reign of terror. 

_If Geralt doesn't help him:_ unfortunately Djenge overestimated himself and tried to take on the brothers by himself and met his end.

## Trivia

  * His name and profession are both a reference to [Jango Fett](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jango_Fett "wikipedia:Jango Fett"), a bounty hunter in the _[Star Wars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Wars "wikipedia:Star Wars")_ series or to Django, another bounty hunter in the movie [Django Unchained](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Django_Unchained "wikipedia:Django Unchained").
  * When fighting the Grossbart Brothers, Djenge Frett remarks "I like the way you die." This is another reference to Django Unchained, quoting a line from the movie.
