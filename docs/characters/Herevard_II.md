**Herevard II**(Polish: _Hereward II_) is the successor of [Hereward](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hereward "Hereward") as [Prince](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prince "Prince") of [Ellander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ellander "Ellander"). He was always considered the rightful heir and as such also obtained a proper education.[1] When Nilfgaardians conquered [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), he decided to not object to his new ruler and "fell in line with the new order."[2]

## Trivia

  * The name "Hereward" is derived from the Old English elements _here_ "army" and _weard_ "guard". This was the name of an 11th-century Anglo-Saxon leader who rebelled against Norman rule.[3] The _v_ instead of _w_ in English translation is for better pronunciation.
