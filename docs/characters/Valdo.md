## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Valdo** is an [elf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf"). He and his friend [Aegar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aegar "Aegar") are part of a non-human travelling circus. Valdo became friends with [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") and helped her to escape the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt").
