## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Carlo Rycher** was a merchant who operated near the docks of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He was a medium class trader who lived in one of the villages near the city, but traded in the metropolis. When he was questioned by the [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") if he had any [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") or [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") in the family, he responded that his father was a mere stove-fitter and his mother stayed at home. 

When they asked about [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") and/or other [nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman") blood in him he stated that they only crossed [horses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") and asses in the village and that they took care about inhabits blood staying pure.
