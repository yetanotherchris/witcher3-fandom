## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **blacksmith** is based in an alcove on the northern side of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market"). He is a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player (using the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck")), and sells weapons, [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") and a number of [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams"), with 4 of them for [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") [bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts").
