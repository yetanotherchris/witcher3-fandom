\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant** has setup shop in the lower part of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), near the village entrance. He plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"), and among other things also sells the [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") book [Religion and Life](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Religion_and_Life "Religion and Life").
