## Biography

In 1272, [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") contacted Polly to recruit her for his planned cabaret at the premises of the former [Rosemary and Thyme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme"). However, Polly's betrothed Hubio was against the idea of her working there, believing the Rosemary and Thyme to still be a brothel per the old publicity placards which he came across. If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") chose to help Dandelion in setting up the cabaret, they went to Polly's house and settled a domestic dispute between Polly and Hubio, after which she started choreographing shows at the Chameleon.
