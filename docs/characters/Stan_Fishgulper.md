## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Stan Fishgulper** , known to the locals simply as **Fisherman** , was one of the fistfighters in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") who lived in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale"). 

If one chooses to participate in the fighting tournaments, he can be fought. However, he makes a small request from Geralt prior to doing so: he needs the reward (a sack of flour) to feed his family and asks that you throw the match. If agreed, you can throw the match then do a rematch to continue the quest. If you choose not to throw the match, you can still give him a small sum later to feed his family if you want to.
