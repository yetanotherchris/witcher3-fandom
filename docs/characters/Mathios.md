## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Mathios** was a steward in service to [Holger Blackhand](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holger_Blackhand "Holger Blackhand"). In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he resided in the village of [Holmstein](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein "Holmstein"), where he awaited a man whosoever would return to him [the carved horn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holger_Blackhand%27s_horn "Holger Blackhand's horn") of the jarl's ancestors.
