## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Petter** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skelliger "Skelliger") who in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), fell off the [Grymmdjarr Monument](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grymmdjarr_Monument "Grymmdjarr Monument") when he tried to light the braziers on top. He broke his leg and his friend [Ulf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulf "Ulf") didn't know how to help, though was chastising Petter for complaining about breaking his leg. 

If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") passes by, he can choose to help, by giving Petter a little bit of [Celandine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Celandine "Celandine"), a herb which wouldn't fix his leg but it wouldn't hurt as much.
