**Rhosyn** was a [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soldier who fought in the [Battle of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_White_Orchard "Battle of White Orchard") during the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War"). 

## Biography

In the Spring of [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Rhosyn fought in a battle near [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), where he twisted his leg, and attempted to crawl away from the [battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_\(White_Orchard\) "Battlefield \(White Orchard\)") to safety. In the process, he encountered a wounded [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") soldier named [Bastien](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastien_Vildenvert "Bastien Vildenvert"), and the two helped each other find refuge in a nearby hut in the woods. They sat there for several days waiting for help, as they were unable to move anywhere due to their injuries. To pass the time, they'd tell stories to each other, like the time Rhosyn's commander once demanded his soldiers obey his every order, including "shitting on command." 

If Geralt decides to help [Dune Vildenvert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dune_Vildenvert "Dune Vildenvert") find Bastien, they encounter Bastien and Rhosyn in the hut after being led there by Bastien's dog, [Hussar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hussar "Hussar"). Bastien refuses to abandon Rhosyn, while Dune initially refuses to take him in due to his deserter status, as the whole family could be punished if Rhosyn is ever found to be in their fold. Ultimately, Geralt must make the choice of whether to agree with Dune, or persuade him to take Rhosyn in. 

_If Geralt tells Dune to take him in:_ everyone, including the woman Dune mentioned and the dog, can be found at their [farm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildenvert%27s_farm "Vildenvert's farm") just east of White Orchard village. 

_If Geralt tells Dune it's too dangerous to take in Rhosyn or finds the cabin before talking to Dune:_ Rhosyn dies. 

_If Geralt doesn't do the quest:_ Rhosyn and Bastien both die.
