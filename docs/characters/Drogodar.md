### Books

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Drogodar** was a grey-haired [bard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bard "Bard") at the royal court of [Cintra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cintra "Cintra") in service of [Queen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Queen "Queen") [Calanthe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calanthe "Calanthe") where he was popular among nobility. His instrument of choice was the lute. 

## _[The Hexer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Hexer "The Hexer")_

In first movie and TV series, Drogodar was played by Polish actor [Dariusz Siastacz](https://pl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dariusz_Siastacz) and sang ballad about Calanthe's deed during [Battle of Hochebuz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Hochebuz "Battle of Hochebuz"). 

### Episodes

  * 6: "[Calanthe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calanthe_\(episode\) "Calanthe \(episode\)")"

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

He appeared only briefly in third game where he performed a ballad about [Cerys an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite")'s encounter with the [hym](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hym "Hym"). Later on, he was among the victims of the [Berserker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berserker_\(creature\) "Berserker \(creature\)") attack. 

### Associated quest

  * [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit")
