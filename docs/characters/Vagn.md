## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Vagn** was the elder of [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). However, despite being an elder he lacked wisdom in regards to monsters: in particular being able to tell a [dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon "Dragon") from a [forktail](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail "Forktail") and instead relied on folklore. 

_If Geralt helps him out and lies:_ Vagn was short and to the point, claiming they had a dragon problem and so [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") set abut helping him, eventually luring out the forktail with [Mia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mia "Mia"). Not wanting to dampen the man's spirits, Geralt lied and told him it was a (now dead) dragon, much to Vagn's delight. 

_If Geralt helps him out but tells the truth:_ Geralt helped out Vagn but soon learned it was just a common forktail, a much smaller looking creature compared to a dragon. However, when Geralt told Vagn this after killing it, Vagn claimed he could have dealt with the "forkytail" himself if he'd known and thus cut Geralt's agreed upon reward in half.
