**Ernst van Hoorn** was a swordsmith living in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") and worked out of a modest dock workshop. However, he wasn't as skilled as others in the large city, so he turned to other methods to push out his competition. 

## Biography

What van Hoorn lacked in his swordsmithing abilities he made up for in his undermining skills. With a plan in mind, he approached [Cleaver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese") and managed to make a deal with his [gang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cleaver%27s_gang "Cleaver's gang") to back him up and help monopolize the swordsmithing trade in the city. With the gang at his back, Van Hoorn then began to either buy out his competition by buying their debts and running them out of business, making accusations or, if they were [nonhuman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman"), accused them of being [havekars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Havekar "Havekar"), such as the master swordsmith [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori"). 

To further protect his monopoly, van Hoorn also worked with Cleaver to control all the supplies needed to make swords, hoarding most of them in warehouses and where they sat unused. 

_If Geralt helps Hattori get back into business:_ Van Hoorn soon learned that Hattori planned to get around his monopoly and set out to stop him, eventually confronting [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and the [elf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf")'s bodyguard, [Sukrus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sukrus "Sukrus"), as they were stealing supplies from one of his warehouses. 

    _If Geralt runs off:_ With Geralt suddenly running off, Van Hoorn managed to defend his monopoly with Cleaver's bandits.
    _If Geralt fights him:_ Van Hoorn was summarily defeated and, before he could come to, Sukrus and his brother-in-law bound him up to be taken to [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and where he wouldn't be anymore trouble for Novigrad. They sailed to [Urialla Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Urialla_Harbor "Urialla Harbor") in [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig") and set up a shop trading in imported wines and liquors, despite van Hoorn requesting they start a forge there. However, due to the Skelligers being content drinking cheap mead, their business quickly struggled.
