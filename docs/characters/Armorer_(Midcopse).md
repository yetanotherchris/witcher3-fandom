The **armorer** based in [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse") is a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player and uses the [Nilfgaardian Empire deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck"). 

Despite advertising the [Gabriel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gabriel_\(weapon\) "Gabriel \(weapon\)") [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow"), he does not sell it nor any other weapons. He sells [Edwin Greloff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Edwin_Greloff "Edwin Greloff")'s [first](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Edwin_Greloff%27s_first_map "Edwin Greloff's first map") and [second](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Edwin_Greloff%27s_second_map "Edwin Greloff's second map") maps, and is one of very few to offer [lesser glyphs of Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Glyph_of_Quen "Lesser Glyph of Quen") and [chitinous shells](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chitinous_shell "Chitinous shell") at any level. He also sells [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") and a standard [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") selection of [armors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_armor "The Witcher 3 armor"), lesser [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph") and [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams").
