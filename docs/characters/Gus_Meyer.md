## Biography

Gus owned the woodcarving workshop "Meyer and Meyer" in partnership with his brother [Fabian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fabian_Meyer "Fabian Meyer"). In 1272, they began carving wooden statues that represented the old gods but had large busts, which offended a serial killer, the so-called [Concerned Citizen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk"), who tortured and murdered Fabian while Gus was away on a business trip to [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). Gus was already returning to Novigrad when he spotted a large plume of smoke rising above the city and was stunned to discover that it originated from his workshop which was in total disarray. Following this incident, Gus bought a pair of vicious guard [dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog"), named Mauler and Striga. 

_If Geralt investigates:_ [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") dropped in as the same serial killer had nearly killed [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla") and thus was trying to learn more about the killer's methods to track him down. Gus informed Geralt about his brother's tendency to put larger busts on the statues before informing the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") he planned to sell the workshop and move back home to [Mahakam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mahakam "Mahakam") to be closer to the rest of his family.
