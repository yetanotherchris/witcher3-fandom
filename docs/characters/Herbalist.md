## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 icon herbalist](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/9b/Tw3_icon_herbalist.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/28?cb=20170901053622)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Herbalists "Category:Herbalists")

Herbalists buy and sell [alchemical ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"), and therefore make it easier for [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to find the components or ingredients he needs for [alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt_-_Guide_to_Alchemy "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Guide to Alchemy"). It is also better to sell alchemical ingredients to herbalists as they buy them at the highest prices compared to other types of [shopkeepers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"), higher even than [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist").
