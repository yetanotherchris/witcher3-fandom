## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Count Tybalt** was the wealthy host of the high stakes [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") tournament at the [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora"). He was a calm and balanced nobleman without any indication of racism that was so common in his time. 

He uses the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck").
