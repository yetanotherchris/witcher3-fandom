## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** is located near [Marcus T.K. Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson")'s [store](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Books_and_Scrolls "Books and Scrolls"). He sells primarily [junk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") items, and the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torn-out_page:_Chort_decoction "Torn-out page: Chort decoction") for [chort decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chort_decoction "Chort decoction"). However, he also buys the same items as an [innkeeper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeeper "Innkeeper") would, and with the same pricing - highest for valuables and other junk. As he is the closest such merchant near a [signpost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel"), that makes his store an excellent place to offload pretty much anything that isn't equipable gear.
