**Mislav** was a [hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hunter "Hunter") who lived in a small hut near [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

## Biography

When he was younger, he served as the [local lord](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignatius_Verrieres "Ignatius Verrieres")'s hunter in his [employer's manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amavet_Fortress_Ruins "Amavet Fortress Ruins") until [Dieter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dieter "Dieter"), another servant, discovered the homosexual relationship between Mislav and [Florian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Florian_Verrieres "Florian Verrieres"), the lord's son. Dieter outed them and Mislav was exiled from the manor and became an outcast. 

When [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") came to White Orchard in the May of [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he was contracted by [Captain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Captain "Captain") [Peter Saar Gwynleve](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peter_Saar_Gwynleve "Peter Saar Gwynleve") to kill the [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") that was prowling the area, and he would provide Geralt with information about the location of [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"). After Geralt agreed, he was given two names, [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira"), the local herbalist and Mislav, the local hunter. 

Geralt found Mislav in the woods, hunting a pack of [wild dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog"), in which he got assistance from Geralt. Mislav then showed him the camp where the griffin attacked a group of [Nilfgaardian soldiers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). Geralt thanked Mislav, and continued on his hunt. 

## Notes

  * If Geralt finds the griffin's nest on his own, Mislav can still be found outside his hut, but it would not be possible to start a conversation with him.
