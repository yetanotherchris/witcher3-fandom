## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Karl** , also known as "**Whistling Whacker** ", is a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") who should be present at [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde"), on the northern side of [The New Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port"), but doesn't appear during gameplay due to a bug. He could play [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") and would award a random, non-unique card if defeated.
