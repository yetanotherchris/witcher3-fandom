**Ida Emean aep Sivney** was an [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") [sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") from the [Blue Mountains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_Mountains "Blue Mountains") and a member of the [Lodge of Sorceresses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses"). She was also an _[Aen Saevherne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Saevherne "Aen Saevherne")_ , who were considered to be powerful, but enigmatic, elven sages.[1]

Little is known of her origins or motives, but she appeared in [Dol Blathanna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna "Dol Blathanna") as an associate and advisor of [Francesca Findabair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francesca_Findabair "Francesca Findabair"). It was on Francesca's insistence that Ida joined the Lodge of Sorceresses, presumably due to her valuable and extensive knowledge of _Hen Ichaer_ , the [Elder Blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Blood "Elder Blood"), and the power associated with it.[1]

She had cinnabar red hair, and wore elven makeup. She was dressed in an airy chiffon dress the color of daffodils. She didn't wear precious metals or stones, preferring instead pearls, coral, and amber.[1]

## Biography

* * *

The following is considered game canon only and may contradict [Andrzej Sapkowski](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Andrzej_Sapkowski "Andrzej Sapkowski")'s works.

* * *

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") discovered that the being, [Uma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Uma "Uma"), had some connection to finding [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"), Yennefer contacted Ida using a [megascope](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Megascope "Megascope"). After pronouncing the incantation of the curse, Ida starts to back away, but when pressed she reveals that it was a powerful curse in the forgotten language, [One Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/One_Speech "One Speech"). She gave the two advice on possible solutions to reversing the curse, then warning them that the [Elder blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_blood "Elder blood") could initiate or stop the [White Frost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Frost "White Frost") and that Geralt will be the one to influence the events. Later, when [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") tries to contact help from the Lodge to fight the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt"), Ida outright refused. 

* * *

End of game canon content.
