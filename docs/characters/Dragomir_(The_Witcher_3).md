## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ (mentioned)

**Dragomir** was a man that was hanged by the [Nilfgaardian Empire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") for spying on them. He was accused while he was serving in the [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") and was caught eavesdropping on the soldiers and reporting their plans to the [Redanians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"). His corpse can be found right next to the location of the quest [Man's Best Friend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Man%27s_Best_Friend "Man's Best Friend").
