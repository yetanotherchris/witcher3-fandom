**Black Bogdan** was a [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") racer outside [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). 

No matter what order he's raced against, he'll always give [horse blinders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse_blinders "Horse blinders") and 40 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") when beaten. He may also have either obstacle appear (loose barrels or log) when racing him.
