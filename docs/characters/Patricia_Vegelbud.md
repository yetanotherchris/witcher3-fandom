## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Patricia Vegelbud** was the mother of [Ingrid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ingrid_Vegelbud "Ingrid Vegelbud") and grandmother of [Albert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Albert_Vegelbud "Albert Vegelbud"). Being the oldest living family member, she was also technically the matriarch, though her daughter Ingrid led the family instead. 

She'd been a follower of the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") until sometime around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), when she began to question it and was subsequently killed by the [Concerned Citizen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk").
