## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Innkeep** of [The Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") is a [gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players") ([Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck") deck). He is quite often absent from his workplace. He also sells a few cards, including: 

  * [Barclay Els](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Barclay_Els_\(gwent_card\) "Barclay Els \(gwent card\)")
  * [Dol Blathanna Scout](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna_Scout_\(gwent_card\) "Dol Blathanna Scout \(gwent card\)")
  * [Mahakaman Defender](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mahakaman_Defender_\(gwent_card\) "Mahakaman Defender \(gwent card\)")
  * [Siege Technician](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siege_Technician_\(gwent_card\) "Siege Technician \(gwent card\)")
