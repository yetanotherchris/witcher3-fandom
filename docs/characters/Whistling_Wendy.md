**Whistling Wendy** was a [Kaedweni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedwen "Kaedwen") [prostitute](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prostitute "Prostitute"). She first worked in [Kaedweni Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedweni_Army "Kaedweni Army") but later moved to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

## Biography

In June of [1271](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1271 "Timeline/Games"), Whistling Wendy was one of the girls working for [Madame Carole](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Madame_Carole "Madame Carole") at the [Kaedweni brothel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedweni_brothel "Kaedweni brothel") and was considered the most beautiful of her kind by [Henselt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henselt "Henselt")'s soldiers. 

A year later she had moved on to working on the street corners of Novigrad, but this time on her own. When [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla") was viciously attacked, she tried to help avenge the trobairitz as Priscilla had always treated her like an equal rather than look down on her like so many others. 

Going by the belief that attackers tended to return to the scene of the crime, she waited in the alley where Priscilla was attacked with some thugs when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), who was also investigating into the attack, came along. After a quick fight, Wendy realized the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") was also trying to help and apologized before letting Geralt continue his investigation.
