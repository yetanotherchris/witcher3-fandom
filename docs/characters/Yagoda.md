## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Yagoda** was a woman that lived in [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch") when, in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), the village inhabitants robbed from a nearby altar, not realizing it was cursed. Shortly after, she and all the rest were transformed into pigs. Unfortunately, due to a war going on, famine was rampant and when several bandits passed through, they captured Yagoda in her pig form and killed and roasted her, not realizing her true origins.
