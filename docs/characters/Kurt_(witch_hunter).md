**Kurt** was a [witch hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") who, similarly to [Moreelse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moreelse "Moreelse"), blindingly followed [Caleb Menge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caleb_Menge "Caleb Menge") and could even be considered as one of his personal guardians. He died when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss "Triss") destroyed a [basement of hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_Hunter_Barracks_Outpost "Witch Hunter Barracks Outpost") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") with fire and steel.
