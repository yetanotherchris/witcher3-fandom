## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

Throughout the [Pontar region](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(region\) "Pontar \(region\)") ([Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") areas), there are several merchants constantly roaming the roads in an attempt to cover sparsely populated areas. Commonly referred to as wandering, but also known as traveling merchants, their appearances may vary each time they are seen, and although encountered at different spots, all offer the same goods for sale as well - mostly [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"). 

## The Mire

This merchant starts his walk at [Claywich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claywich "Claywich"), taking the western (wider on the map) road to [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton"), until he reaches the thinner shortcut road north of the village and takes that, turning around when he reaches the huts. At night, he likes to rest at the campfire by a small boulder just south of Claywich, or at another campfire by Oreton. 

## Battlefield

This merchant walks along the war-ravaged roads east of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), from the eastern gate of [Stonecutters' Settlement](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stonecutters%27_Settlement "Stonecutters' Settlement"), through the [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") military camp and then north a short distance on the road (to a point exactly east of the [Codgers' Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Codgers%27_Quarry "Codgers' Quarry") signpost), where going further would put him in danger from nearby [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"). 

## Grassy Knoll

There are two wandering merchants in the [Grassy Knoll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grassy_Knoll "Grassy Knoll"): 

  * On the western side, starting from a campfire next to [Lucian's Windmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lucian%27s_Windmill "Lucian's Windmill") (where he also spends the night) and follows the road northeast to [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") until [Portside Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portside_Gate "Portside Gate"), and then east about halfway to [Glory Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Gate "Glory Gate") before turning back around.
  * On the eastern side, starting from a campfire at the hut southeast of [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") (where he also spends the night) and going south almost until reaching the [wyvern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern") [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure"), where he turns to follow the road west turning around at the first crossroads past the small hut with a [rotfiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend "Rotfiend") [monster nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), at a point south from [Drahim Castle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drahim_Castle "Drahim Castle"). If the nest isn't cleared yet though, he'll stop before reaching it and after awhile turns around there.

## Gustfields

Three merchants walk the roads in the [Gustfields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gustfields "Gustfields"): 

  * Between [Sarrasin Grange](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarrasin_Grange "Sarrasin Grange") and the unmarked trading camp southeast of [Yantra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yantra "Yantra"), which he passes through.
  * Between [Dancing Windmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dancing_Windmill "Dancing Windmill") and the small bridge just past [Yanina](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yanina "Yanina")'s hut, almost reaching [Honeyfill Meadworks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks").
  * Between [Winespring Grange](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Winespring_Grange "Winespring Grange") and [Moldavie Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moldavie_Residence "Moldavie Residence"), walking the circle between the two buildings.

With the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion, a fourth one appears at the crossroads north of [Martin Feuille's Farmstead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Martin_Feuille%27s_Farmstead "Martin Feuille's Farmstead"), where he likes to rest by a fire at night, and during the day walks until the arch that marks the border of [Brunwich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brunwich "Brunwich"), before turning around. 

## Notes

  * Prior to [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), these merchants had the **Trader** label instead.
  * Despite most appearing in Novigrad areas, all of them are considered Velen merchants for pricing purposes.
