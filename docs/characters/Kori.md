## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Kori** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and part of [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). While other clans were ok with visitors, he and his brother, [Kraki](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kraki "Kraki"), despised anyone from the continent and made sure to voice this opinion to any that dared to visit [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn"). 

_If Geralt visits Arinbjorn's inn:_ Kori and Kraki immediately "introduced" themselves to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), making it clear they thought very little of him as a continental and that he wasn't welcome. However, [Jorund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jorund "Jorund") interrupted before they could escalate it into a fight and the two brothers walked off. 

    _If[King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit") hasn't been started before [Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Phantom_of_Eldberg "Contract: The Phantom of Eldberg") is completed:_ After visiting the inn again to talk to Jorund, Kori and Kraki, fed up with visitors, decided to fight Geralt and Jorund. In the end, the two brothers were killed, but not before they also killed Jorund.
