## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Liesje** was a farmer from [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), and [Dune Vildenvert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dune_Vildenvert "Dune Vildenvert")'s wife. She lived on a [farm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildenvert%27s_farm "Vildenvert's farm") just east of [White Orchard inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_inn "White Orchard inn") with Dune and his brother [Bastien](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastien_Vildenvert "Bastien Vildenvert").
