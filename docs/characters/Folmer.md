## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Folmer** was a warrior from [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), who can be encountered in the [town](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") under the [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel"), with another warrior advising his friend [Ruar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruar "Ruar") to not eat so much, as he would likely be in pain and thus unable to go raiding otherwise.
