## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **smith** in [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag") plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") using the [Nilfgaardian deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck"). He also sells [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), [weapons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_weapons "Category:The Witcher 3 weapons"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"), and several [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams").
