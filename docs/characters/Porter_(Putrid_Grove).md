## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **unnamed porter** was a man in the service of [Francis Bedlam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam"), better known as the _King of Beggars_ , working in the [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), where he was tasked with guarding the main gate to greet the guests and send away the intruders.
