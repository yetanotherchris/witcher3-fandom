## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gottard** is peasant living in village [Yantra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yantra "Yantra") on the east side of the lake, northeast of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). When leader of bandits known as [Little Red](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Little_Red_\(bandit\) "Little Red \(bandit\)") wants to settle score with [Bertram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bertram_\(peasant\) "Bertram \(peasant\)"), his neighbor, he is among villagers wanting to somehow protect him. When Geralt appears he is unwelcome at him but then changes his mind and tells everything he know.
