## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Marius Florin** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") aristocrat and a close friend of the [Vegelbud family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family"), as well as a passionate of [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") using the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"). It is said that he never loses thanks to his [zeugl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zeugl "Zeugl")-bone amulet. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can play with him during the masquerade ball at the [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence") and will receive the [Dandelion card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion_\(gwent_card\) "Dandelion \(gwent card\)") if he beats him.
