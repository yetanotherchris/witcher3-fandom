## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **Merchant** has setup a roadside camp at the crossroads east of [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale"), between the village and the [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit"). He isn't marked on the map and his appearances may change between visits. 

## Bugs

  * Due to being allowed to change appearances, he is prone to the impossible to trade [bug](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants#Bugs "The Witcher 3 merchants").
  * All of his camp surroundings except the small campfire may sometimes disappear, particularly if the containers around him are looted.
  * After yet unknown conditions are satisfied, another Merchant may appear in [Mulbrydale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mulbrydale "Mulbrydale") proper, who is marked with a trader's icon on the map, but can't be interacted with at all (doesn't even have a passive reply, though he can be seen having a conversation with another person). It is not unlikely that the two are erroneously linked and the icon is thus placed on the wrong person.
