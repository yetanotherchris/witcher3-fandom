## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Evald** was one of six guardsmen watching over the outermost gate leading to [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

He, his friend, and a shieldmaiden stood around discussing various topics, such as who would become the next [king](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_the_Skellige_Isles "King of the Skellige Isles") after the death of [Bran Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bran_Tuirseach "Bran Tuirseach"), and when the next raid would occur and so on.
