## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Liglad** was the captain of one of the ships docked in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s port. 

_If approached to[sail](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige") to [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"):_ he refused to sail [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to the islands, being wary of being attacked by Skelligers and [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") alike, and thus advised the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") to go find [Captain Wolverstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Captain_Wolverstone "Captain Wolverstone") at [the Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon") instead.
