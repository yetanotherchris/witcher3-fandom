## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gert Borel** was a peasant that hauled [plague](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Catriona_plague "Catriona plague") victims' bodies as he was confident he was immune to it considering all his life he never got sick. However, later on he was found wandering the battlefields of southern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") (near a burned-out settlement east of [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor")), begging for death, having come down with the plague himself. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can choose to end his suffering and burn his body.
