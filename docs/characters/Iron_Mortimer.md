## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Iron Mortimer** was the [captain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Captain "Captain") of the [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") and one of the fistfighters in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

## Fighting tactics

He's a tough, sinewy chap with a constitution to match his name, so more counters than usual are necessary. Timing and patience are required. 

Once you land a successful counter, make sure to follow with one or two quick jabs with a heavy punch before withdrawing before he can regain his composure.[1]
