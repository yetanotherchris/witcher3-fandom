## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion

**Igor de Sade** was a [Kaedweni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedwen "Kaedwen") noble-born [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage"), or in fact student at [Ban Ard Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ban_Ard_Academy "Ban Ard Academy") who wrote _On the Workings and Construction of Golems, Based on the Work of Master[Alzur](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alzur "Alzur") _just some minutes before he died by hands of an earth elemental ([golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem")).[1]

## Trivia

  * Igor de Sade's story is loosely based on Dr. [Victor Frankenstein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victor_Frankenstein). 
    * His name is also a reference to [Igor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Igor_\(character\)), Frankenstein's assistant in various movie adaptations of the story.
    * Igor has the same last name as Marquis De Sade, a French philosopher whose last name created the word "sadist" because of his gruesome writings.
