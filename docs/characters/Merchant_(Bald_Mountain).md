# Merchant (Bald Mountain)

There are two merchants who appear near [Bald Mountain (location)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") once the main quest [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(quest\) "Bald Mountain \(quest\)") begins.

## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **Merchant** is in the little [village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Road_to_Bald_Mountain "Road to Bald Mountain") where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") are invited to sit and talk with Stregomir. He remains there even after the quest is completed, and sells a few [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components").

## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **Merchant** appears near the bonfire above the little village, standing on the opposite side of [Thecla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thecla "Thecla")'s tent. He sells the same items as [wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Pontar\) "Wandering merchant \(Pontar\)"), and will leave after the quest completes.
