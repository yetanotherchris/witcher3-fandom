\- Armorer  
  
  
This **armorer** is in a building right off of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt")'s market and sells various armors, diagrams, [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") and all [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph"). He also players [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck"). 

He talked a bit to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") initially, excited to see a [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") visit his shop as he'd have him craft far more interesting things than what the locals typically need.
