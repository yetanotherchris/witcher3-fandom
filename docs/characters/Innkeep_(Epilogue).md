This **innkeep** takes over the [White Orchard inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_inn "White Orchard inn") during the the Empress version of the epilogue. He makes his own triple-distilled plum brandy, popular with the inn patrons and thanks to which his business is booming. 

He sells a couple of strong alcohol [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and some food. 

## Associated quest

  * [Something Ends, Something Begins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)") (dependent)

## Notes

  * He has optional dialogue, during which Geralt asks about the _"stuff we drank yesterday"_ , referring to the innkeep's homemade brandy. A "charred oak spirit" is also mentioned, but unfortunately neither is available for sale.
  * He only appears on the winter [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") map, so he won't be present after the epilogue quest ends and that's the only opportunity to interact with him.

## Bugs

  * After accessing and exiting his shop inventory, the dialogue options may get hidden, appearing "stuck" and unable to exit the interaction. However, the menu choices are still accessible using the arrow keys. This is the same as [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")'s possible bug during [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen").
  * The mug that he's constantly polishing during his ambient animation can remain hanging in the air while talking to him.
