\- Medic to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_  
  
This **medic** ran the impromptu [morgue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Morgue "Oxenfurt Morgue") in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

Despite his profession, he appeared to show no regard for those he viewed as low status. In particular, with a [mysterious monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gael "Gael") running around and killing drunk beggars and vagrants, he hadn't even bothered to do an autopsy on the victims, and forgot to even burn the bodies.
