## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

Lieutenant General **Harmon aep Groenekan** , commander of Second Mounted Army of [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"), was a connoisseur and collector of armors. During [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), he demanded that the armorers at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") make him a suit, but insisted that the finished product be tested before paying. This evolved into small contest of skills between [Fergus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fergus_Graem "Fergus Graem") and [Yoana](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yoana "Yoana").
