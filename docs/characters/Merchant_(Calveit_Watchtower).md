## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower") after it is cleared of monsters. Somewhat unusually for a non-moving merchant, his appearance is allowed to change between visits. 

Well-fitting with his placement by an anvil in a military camp, he has the inventory and pricing of a generic [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), selling weapons, lesser [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") and some [diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams").
