## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Torgun** was one of the inhabitants of [Lofoten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lofoten "Lofoten") who survived the attack of the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt"), but his wife and daughter, Duva and Edla, unfortunately did not. He then started to repair his hut and when his brother from [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") came to ask him if he would move to Larvik to live with him. To that Torgun responded that he would have to see his brother's wife and daughter forever and it would cause him painful memories.
