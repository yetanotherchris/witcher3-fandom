**Maria**[1]**Louisa La Valette** was [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest") of [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria")'s secret mistress from [1263](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1263 "Timeline")[2] until his death in [1271](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1271 "Timeline/Games").[3] It was widely rumoured that her younger two children: a son, [Boussy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boussy_La_Valette "Boussy La Valette") and a daughter, [Anaïs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ana%C3%AFs_La_Valette "Anaïs La Valette"), were not the old [baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baron "Baron") [La Valette](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Baron_La_Valette "Old Baron La Valette")'s children, but Foltest's. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What was the relationship between Maria Louisa La Valette and King Foltest of Temeria?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Maria Louisa La Valette was the secret mistress of King Foltest of Temeria from 1263 to 1271. Their alleged illegitimate children, Boussy and Anaïs, caused a scandal and a revolt among the nobility. Maria, in response, declared La Valette lands independent from Temeria, triggering a month-long siege of La Valette Castle by the king's forces. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Who was the father of Maria Louisa La Valette's oldest son, Aryan?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Aryan, the eldest son of Maria Louisa La Valette, was fathered by Old Baron La Valette. He was brought up to become a knight and shared a half-sibling relationship with Anaïs and Boussy La Valette. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What was the cause of the conflict between King Foltest and Baroness La Valette?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Baroness Maria Louisa La Valette incited a rebellion among the barons by declaring La Valette lands independent from Temeria. She appointed her son, Aryan, as the leader of her subjects. King Foltest retaliated by leading a month-long siege of La Valette Castle with the royal army, overpowering the La Valette troops. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What was the fate of Maria Louisa La Valette after King Foltest's death?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Post King Foltest's demise in 1271, Maria Louisa La Valette remained involved in significant events. Her admiration for Geralt of Rivia, the savior of her son Aryan, was evident. However, the siege resulted in the tragic death of her eldest son, who valiantly defended her honor. Additionally, rumors suggested that her younger offspring, Boussy and Anaïs, were King Foltest's children, not Baron La Valette's. 

Provided by: Fandom 

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

King [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest") moves to capture the stronghold of the rebellious [Baroness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baroness "Baroness") La Valette. Additionally, it is revealed that she had one son with the "Old Baron", called [Aryan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aryan_La_Valette "Aryan La Valette"). 

### Associated quest

  * [The Dungeons of the La Valettes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Dungeons_of_the_La_Valettes "The Dungeons of the La Valettes")

### Journal entry

    _I have chanced to visit the court of the La Valettes on several occasions in the past. Invariably, I was greeted and received in a manner befitting my fame and talent. Baroness Mary Louisa was a very comely lady, her beauty having fully blossomed, her hair dark, her lips sensuous. As with many women married to markedly older men, rumors about her were plentiful. Among them, one claiming that she had had a love affair with King[Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest") himself, and that the monarch had fathered her younger children._
    **If[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") manages to save her from the dungeons:**

    _The witcher got a chance to behold the baroness in nearly all her beauty when he ripped her from the hands of a cruel torturer in the dungeon of her own abode. Mary Louisa La Valette eluded a terrible fate as they fled the underground prison, though they did so with the aid of an[emissary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shilard_Fitz-Oesterlen "Shilard Fitz-Oesterlen") of the Empire of [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). The Nilfgaardian, however, was not helpful out of the kindness of his heart..._[![Tw2 journal Maryluisa](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/b/b7/Tw2_journal_Maryluisa.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20110529035927)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/b/b7/Tw2_journal_Maryluisa.png/revision/latest?cb=20110529035927)

    **If[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") does not manage to save her from the dungeons:**

    _Mary Louisa La Valette managed to flee the cell in the dungeon of her own castle with the help of an emissary of the Empire of[Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). She thus eluded a terrible fate, but it can be said with some certainty that the Nilfgaardian was not helpful out of the kindness of his heart..._

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

Maria Louisa appears in The Witcher 3, having taken up residence in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). The emblem of the [La Valette](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/La_Valette "La Valette") family still flies high in her [Villa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baroness_la_Valette%27s_Villa "Baroness la Valette's Villa"). She is obviously supporting Nilfgaard in the [Third War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War"). She is also a friend of [Morvran Voorhis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvran_Voorhis "Morvran Voorhis") and [Ingrid Vegelbud](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ingrid_Vegelbud "Ingrid Vegelbud"). 

### Associated quests

  * [Broken Flowers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broken_Flowers "Broken Flowers")

### Journal entry

    

    _Baroness Maria Louisa la Valette was the talk of Temeria in her time. This noblewoman had been King Foltest's mistress and even bore him two illegitimate children._
    _This caused quite the scandal and prompted several notable families to take up arms in revolt, only to be bloodily crushed by the king's forces, who took La Valette Castle during a hard-fought siege._
    **If Geralt has previously saved her son:**

    _To the[Baroness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baroness "Baroness")' good fortune Geralt of Rivia was with King Foltest at the time and saved her son Aryan from the fate that otherwise would have been his for having participated in the revolt._
    _Louisa la Valette thus had every reason to think quite highly of the witcher and her esteem became quite apparent the next time they crossed paths._
    **If Geralt has previously killed her son:**

    _Sadly, the siege led to the death of the baroness' eldest son. Sword in hand he had fought to defend his mother's honour but fell to Geralt of Rivia, who was with King Foltest at the time._
    _No one could blame Louisa la Valette for harboring a deep-seated hatred for the witcher, a hatred which became quite apparent the next time they crossed paths._
