## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **herbalist** appears at the [fisherman's hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Abandoned site \(Spitfire Bluff\)") on the northern coast of [Spitfire Bluff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spitfire_Bluff "Spitfire Bluff"), west-northwest of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley"), after the site is liberated from pirates. He sells [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and a few [formulae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formulae "Formulae").
