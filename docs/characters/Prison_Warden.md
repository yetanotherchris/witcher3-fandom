## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed **prison warden** was a high-ranking official of the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") who presided over [Deireadh prison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Deireadh_prison "Deireadh prison") and commanded its garrison until [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). During his infiltration of the prison, [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") killed the warden in order to obtain the cell key, which would be used to free [Margarita Laux-Antille](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margarita_Laux-Antille "Margarita Laux-Antille"). 

## Combat tactics

The warden emerges from his quarters after most of the witch hunters and [Redanian soldiers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") guarding the courtyard of the prison have been killed. He wields a two-handed sword in combat. After being hit once, he parries the second strike and stuns Geralt. 

## Notes

  * Upon death, the warden drops one pair of [dimeritium shackles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dimeritium_shackles "Dimeritium shackles"), a [witch hunter's sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter%27s_sword "Witch hunter's sword"), and the [cell key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cell_key "Cell key").
  * If the [Isengrim Faoiltiarna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isengrim_Faoiltiarna_\(gwent_card\) "Isengrim Faoiltiarna \(gwent card\)"), [John Natalis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/John_Natalis_\(gwent_card\) "John Natalis \(gwent card\)") and [Fringilla Vigo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fringilla_Vigo_\(gwent_card\) "Fringilla Vigo \(gwent card\)") gwent cards weren't received during [A Dangerous Game](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dangerous_Game "A Dangerous Game"), they will also be dropped by him.
