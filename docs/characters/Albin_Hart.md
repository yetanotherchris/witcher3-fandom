## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Albin Hart** was a scavenger working in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") in the year [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), where he looted the bodies of dead soldiers on battlefields to try and make ends meet. 

_If Geralt helps him and he survives:_ Albin was grateful and rewarded the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") for his help. His [brother-in-law](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shady_merchant "Shady merchant") also rewarded the witcher with a discount on a [counterfeit pass](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Transit_pass "Transit pass"). 

_If Geralt helps him and he dies:_ Unfortunately the [necrophages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage "Necrophage") proved too much and Albin was killed by them while trying to loot the corpses on the battlefield.
