## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Helma** lived in the [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), in a hut just on the edges of the fields around the area. She had been previously married to a man named Ignatius, but after he died she lived by herself, not wishing to remarry despite others pestering her about it. 

At one point Luzi, a young woman in the area, was arranged to be married off by her parents to a far older man. Not wishing to go through with it, Luzi committed suicide on her wedding day with a [silver dagger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_dagger "Silver dagger") and her parents, refusing to sell it off or bury it, entrusted it to Helma instead. 

_If Geralt helps her:_ After learning [The White Lady](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_White_Lady "The White Lady") was a [noonwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith "Noonwraith"), Helma deduced it was Luzi, and revealed what happened to the young woman to the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). She then gave [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") the dagger to use to draw out the noonwraith and finally lay Luzi's spirit to rest.
