## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ravik** , also known as **Ravvy** , was a friend of [Zoltan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay")'s and a fellow [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") card collector. He had the unique "[John Natalis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/John_Natalis_\(gwent_card\) "John Natalis \(gwent card\)")" gwent card. You can find him at [The Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon"), where he is being held hostage by [Earl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earl_\(gangster\) "Earl \(gangster\)").
