### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Innkeep** in [Urialla Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Urialla_Harbor "Urialla Harbor") plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck"). He also sells a few cards, including: 

  * [Arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas_\(gwent_card\) "Arachas \(gwent card\)")
  * [Werewolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf_\(gwent_card\) "Werewolf \(gwent card\)")
  * [Scorch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scorch_\(gwent_card\) "Scorch \(gwent card\)")
