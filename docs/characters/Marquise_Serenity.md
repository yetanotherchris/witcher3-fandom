## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Marquise Serenity** was the madame at the [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). She was also a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player, with [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck") being her choice of deck, and will award the [Morvran Voorhis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvran_Voorhis_\(gwent_card\) "Morvran Voorhis \(gwent card\)") card if beaten.

## Biography

Marquise Serenity worked at the Passiflora for many years, persevering with her job even after most of her friends had left to pursue other ventures. She recounted once meeting [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") when they arrived at the brothel with two [halflings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling"). 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), she had provided room and board at the Passiflora to [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra") and his associates as they were plotting to assassinate King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"). The madame settled Dijkstra on the top floor in one of the brothel's best suites, which was up a staircase hidden behind a secret door. 

## Notes

  * Certain dialogue options reveal that she is one of the few women in the Witcher's World to have served in an official capacity in a nation's military, with another example being [Ves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ves "Ves").
