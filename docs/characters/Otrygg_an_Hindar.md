## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Otrygg an Hindar** of [Clan Heymaey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Heymaey "Clan Heymaey") was the grandson of the ruling jarl of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"), [Donar an Hindar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Donar_an_Hindar "Donar an Hindar"). He was the youngest claimant to the crown of the [Skellige Islands](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_Islands "Skellige Islands") and the heir to [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"). Him and his grandfather were planning (after the coronation) to plunder the [Great Temple of the Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Temple_of_the_Eternal_Fire "Great Temple of the Eternal Fire") because, although both of them were from amongst the most pious kin on the Isles, they did not understand how someone could believe in simple [fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire "Fire"). 

## Biography

_If[King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit") is completed:_ In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), his plans for coronation were for naught as he perished during the attack by [berserkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werebear "Werebear") in [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde"). 

## Journal entry

    _Otrygg an Hindar, Jarl Donar's grandson and heir, was the youngest of the claimants to Skellige's throne. He dreamed of the heroic deeds and daring raids he would one day lead his subjects in accomplishing._
    _Otrygg never got to live out those dreams, however. During the tragic feast that came to be known as the "Bloody Banquet" he lost is[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] life when a furious bear tore his flesh to small pieces._
