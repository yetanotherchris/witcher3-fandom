## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** is an [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") member of the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") commando hiding out in the forest clearing south of the [Loggers' Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loggers%27_Hut "Loggers' Hut"), along with some other Scoia'tael soldiers. 

He typically sells a few swords, [food](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"), [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyphs "Glyphs"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting components"), and some [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams"). He also plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") and, if beaten, gives the [Cirilla gwent card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cirilla_Fiona_Elen_Riannon_\(gwent_card\) "Cirilla Fiona Elen Riannon \(gwent card\)").
