## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gascoigne** was a [bard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bard "Bard") traveling with a fellow bard, [Victor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Victor "Victor"), along the road to [Yantra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yantra "Yantra") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") when the two had stopped to set up a small campfire and drink. 

_If Geralt partakes in drinking with them:_ after [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") fell asleep, the two bards took all the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")'s possessions and ran off to Yantra, intending to sell the items. 

    _...and demands their stuff:_ already pissed off, Geralt caught up to them and not only demanded his stuff back but their stuff as well, leaving the bards clad only in their underwear.
    _...and forgives them:_ on catching up to them, Geralt decided to just demand back his stuff, which the bards readily handed over.
