## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lil' Bleater** was a [goat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Goat "Goat") that belonged to [Eskel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eskel "Eskel"). 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has to track her trails during [To Bait a Forktail...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/To_Bait_a_Forktail... "To Bait a Forktail...") and she is later seen during a [No Place Like Home](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/No_Place_Like_Home "No Place Like Home") cinematic as well. Outside of these two quests, she can be seen around [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), even after Eskel leaves the place, despite commentary that she never leaves his side.
