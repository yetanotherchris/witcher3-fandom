**Henri Rautlec** , called familiarly **Hank** , was a famed [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") portrait artist and a friend of such names as [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") or [Count de Luverten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_de_Luverten "Count de Luverten"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Rautlec was hired by Dandelion to paint new placards for the [Chameleon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme") but he got in debt and thus required help to get out of it. After a group of angry creditors seized his home in order to either take him or anything valuable, he ran to the races near [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence") where his friend the Count made him an offer: he was to wager on one more race and if he won, the Count would pay all his debts.

## Trivia

  * This character draws many comparisons with and references the French 19th-century painter and illustrator [Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henri_de_Toulouse-Lautrec). He produced posters for the famous Moulin Rouge cabaret upon its inaugural opening and had a rather short stature at 1.42 metres (4 ft 8 in) due to stunted leg growth.
