## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Finneas** was a racist [half-elf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Half-elf "Half-elf") and one of the contestants in the [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") tournament at the [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora"). [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") played him if he first beat [Bernard Tulle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bernard_Tulle "Bernard Tulle") and [Sasha](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carthia_van_Canten "Carthia van Canten"). If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") defeated Finneas, the half-elf accused the witcher of cheating and fisticuffs broke out. 

Finneas unknowingly had something to thank Geralt for, as he was the largest debtor to [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley")'s [casino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_casino "Whoreson Junior's casino"), which the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") effectively shut down on his quest to find [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"), and by extension relieved the half-elf of the debt.[1]

He used the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck").
