## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Boris** was a [bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") that fought in [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley")'s [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_arena "Whoreson Junior's arena"). 

_If Geralt didn't involve Cleaver's thugs:_ [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s last fight in the arena involved Boris, a bear. As the bear stood in his way, Geralt had no choice but to kill Boris.
