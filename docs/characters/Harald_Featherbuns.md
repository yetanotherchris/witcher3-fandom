**Harald Featherbuns** was a resident of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), and an avid [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player. 

## Biography

In 1272, Featherbuns participated in a [gwent tournament](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/High_Stakes "High Stakes") at the [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora") organized by Count [Tybalt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count_Tybalt_\(Novigrad\) "Count Tybalt \(Novigrad\)"). In the first round, he was pitted against [Patrick Hazelnutt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patrick_Hazelnutt "Patrick Hazelnutt") and won, however he lost in the second round when he played against [Finneas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Finneas "Finneas").
