## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unidentified [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") woman was a long-time concubine of [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Bran Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bran_Tuirseach "Bran Tuirseach"). During Bran's funeral, she, and not his primary wife, [Birna Bran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Birna_Bran "Birna Bran"), threw herself on his corpse before the drakkar was launched into the sea. She laid on top of him, crying as [Crach an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite") loosened a flaming arrow onto the pile, burning both.
