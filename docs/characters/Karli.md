## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Karli** was the mother of two young men, Askel and Nilas, who were sadly killed in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") by a [forktail](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail "Forktail") living near [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal"). 

## Biography

With the villagers incorrectly believing it'd been a [dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon "Dragon"), they paid no heed to Karli and practically barged into her home to gape at her sons' bodies. 

_If Geralt helps out:_ Initially believing [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") was just like the rest, Karli showed her clear disapproval with the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") until he explained he was there to avenge her sons. On learning this, and being asked permission for once to view the bodies, she let the witcher do so.
