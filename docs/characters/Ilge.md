## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ilge** was the [innkeeper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeeper "Innkeeper") of the [tavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tavern_\(Arinbjorn\) "Tavern \(Arinbjorn\)") in [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn") during [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

Apart from serving food and beverages, he was also a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player and sold the following four cards: [Thaler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thaler_\(gwent_card\) "Thaler \(gwent card\)"), [Crone: Whispess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crone:_Whispess_\(gwent_card\) "Crone: Whispess \(gwent card\)"), [Fiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend_\(gwent_card\) "Fiend \(gwent card\)"), and [Arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas_\(gwent_card\) "Arachas \(gwent card\)").
