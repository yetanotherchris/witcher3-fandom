## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Carlo Varese** , better known as **Cleaver** , was one of the [four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four") main crime bosses of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), alongside [Francis Bedlam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam"), [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley"), and [Sigi Reuven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigi_Reuven "Sigi Reuven"). He ran a [gang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crownsplitters "Crownsplitters") primarily made up of other [dwarves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf").

Carlo Varese, known as Cleaver in Witcher 3, was a key criminal leader in Novigrad. He supported racial coexistence but selectively. Cleaver believed he had the right to operate his business freely and viewed any opposition, regardless of race, as racist.

**How did Cleaver earn his nickname in Novigrad?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Carlo Varese, called Cleaver, was a key crime boss in Novigrad in 1272. His nickname came from his fierce reputation in the criminal world, leading a gang of dwarves. The name 'Cleaver' was part of a joke in Novigrad's back alleys, reflecting his professional and formidable presence. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Who are Cleaver's main rivals in Novigrad?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Carlo Varese, known as Cleaver, is a key crime boss in Novigrad in 1272. His main rivals are Whoreson Junior, Francis Bedlam (the King of Beggars), and Sigi Reuven. These individuals are central to the city's criminal underworld, with Cleaver leading a gang of dwarves.

## Journal entry

    _A joke circulating through the back alleys and seedy taverns of Novigrad claimed the dwarf Carlo Varese, one of the four leading lights of the local criminal strata, was, in his own way, a fierce proponent of racial coexistence. He applied this principle in a rather selective fashion, however._
    _Cleaver firmly felt that he personally had every right, as an assimilated inhabitant of the city of Novigrad, to run his own business. Anyone who had a problem with the way he ran it or the nature of its dealings, be he dwarf,[human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") or [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling"), revealed himself as a vile racist. The only fitting thing to do with such scum? Feed them to a herd of ferociously hungry hogs – right after slicing off the interloper's fingers with his namesake cleaver. Such tales, of course, could very well have been mere rumor and slander – but very few had the courage test their veracity._
    _Cleaver was famed for his hot-temper. What boiled his blood hottest of all, however, was when somebody thought they could pull one over on him._
    _No wonder, then, that the attack by Whoreson's thugs got him somewhat riled up. The violent tirade he directed at Wiley clearly contained not idle threats but promises he meant to keep, even if doing so meant demolishing half the neighborhood._

## Notes

  * If Geralt helps [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") evacuate the mages during the quest [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") turn their attention to the non-humans. Later, when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") return to Novigrad during the quest [Final Preparations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Final_Preparations "Final Preparations"), an epilogue slide shows a dwarf looking very much like Cleaver, being burned at the stake. However, even if that was originally indeed intended to be him, he is very much alive and can be talked to.
