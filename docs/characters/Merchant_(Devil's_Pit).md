## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** appears in the house outside the gates of the [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit"), if the place becomes a hospital again following the events during [In the Eternal Fire's Shadow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Eternal_Fire%27s_Shadow "In the Eternal Fire's Shadow"). 

Except for his visual appearance, he is effectively a clone of the village [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Merchant \(Crow's Perch\)") at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") in all other regards - store inventory, dialogue lines and playing [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") using the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck").
