## Appearance(s)

### Games

**Bea** was a barmaid at [The Golden Sturgeon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Golden_Sturgeon "The Golden Sturgeon"). 

## Biography

In early [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), Bea sheltered [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"), who was on the run from the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt"), in her house in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). They became friends and when Ciri reappeared in the free city a few months later, she visited Bea alongside [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and gave her a trinket as thanks.
