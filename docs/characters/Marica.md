## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Marica** served as the eyes of the blind [Thecla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thecla "Thecla") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") at [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)"). 

## Biography

During the 1272 Crones' Sabbath, Marica allowed [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") to see Thecla, who selects candidates for meeting the Crones. After Geralt and Ciri were accepted for entry, Marica escorted them to the large doors which lead into the interior of Bald Mountain.
