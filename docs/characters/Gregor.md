**Gregor** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") [soldier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Soldier "Soldier") who enlisted when [Vizimir II](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizimir_II "Vizimir II") was still on the throne but grew dissatisfied with [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s reign so he joined [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"), [Vernon Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche "Vernon Roche"), and [Thaler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thaler_\(spy\) "Thaler \(spy\)") in a secret plot to assassinate the monarch. He acted as [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s contact in the [Redanian army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") during [A Deadly Plot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Deadly_Plot "A Deadly Plot").
