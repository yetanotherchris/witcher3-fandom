The **blacksmith** in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") is an amateur [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") and avid [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player, using the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck"). In addition to the usual items sold by all [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") blacksmiths, he also sells two maps for witcher gear [scavenger hunt quests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_treasure_hunts "The Witcher 3 treasure hunts") and the [mastercrafted saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_saddle "Mastercrafted saddle"). 

If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") asks him if he could craft master-quality weapons, he tells the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") about [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori") of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), which will trigger the respective quest.
