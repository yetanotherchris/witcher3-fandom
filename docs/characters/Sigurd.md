**Sigurd** was a [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmiths "Blacksmiths") from [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn"). When he has a free time he is either playing [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") or writing poems. He sells [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"), [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Runestone"), and a number of [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams"). 

## Trivia

  * Sigurd could be a reference to the [legendary hero of the same name](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sigurd "wikipedia:Sigurd") in Norse mythology.
