## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Soren** was a fisherman and one of [Lofoten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lofoten "Lofoten")'s few survivors after the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") attacked the village in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). He liked to call himself "the Elder", and was a little too proud of himself despite losing three sons in the massacre. 

He is the old man who [Ermion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mousesack "Mousesack") mentions during the closing dialogue of [Echoes of the Past](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Echoes_of_the_Past "Echoes of the Past"), and [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") can question him while following [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")'s trails in Lofoten, but he isn't helpful.
