\- The survivor talking about [Hendrik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hendrik "Hendrik"), _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_  
  
This unnamed **survivor** was the only one who survived when [Heatherton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heatherton "Heatherton") was attacked by the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). However, he was so distraught by what happened to everyone else that when [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") approached to find out what happened to the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") spy, Hendrik, he had to use [Axii](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Axii "Axii") to calm the survivor down. Afterwards, the survivor told Geralt what happened that fateful night.
