**Dea Strenger** was the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s unborn daughter. After his wife [Anna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anna_Strenger "Anna Strenger") miscarried, she turned into a [botchling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Botchling "Botchling"). 

When [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") went to visit the Baron he came to the botchling's grave, unfilled and empty. He saw the botchling and had to make a choice. 

_If Geralt spared the botchling:_ Geralt guided Phillip through an ancient naming ritual and Phillip named the stillborn Dea before burying her under the home's doorway. Later she turned into a [lubberkin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lubberkin "Lubberkin") and helped him find the trails of Anna and their daughter [Tamara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger"). 

_If Geralt killed the botchling:_ Phillip became upset at the witcher for killing his child once more, though nothing else could be done then.
