## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gjarr** was the village elder of [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare"), a miners settlement in the middle of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1272 "Timeline"), despite knowing of a possible [troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wham-a-Wham "Wham-a-Wham") in the northern caves, he decided to send men anyways to see if they could mine some silver. However, when they lost contact with the miners, he put out a notice hoping someone would help learn what happened to them.
