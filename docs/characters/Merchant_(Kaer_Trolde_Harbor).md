## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed **merchant** appears [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")'s docks after [Destination: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destination:_Skellige "Destination: Skellige") completes, as the place is busy with [Bran Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bran_Tuirseach "Bran Tuirseach")'s funeral before that point. He sells the same things as the [roadside camp merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") to the south - maps for [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") locations and some miscellaneous components - but his appearance can change between visits.
