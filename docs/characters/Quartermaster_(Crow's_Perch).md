\- Quartermaster  
  
  
This **quartermaster** has setup shop in the upper part of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"), next to the armorers [Fergus Graem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fergus_Graem "Fergus Graem") and [Yoana](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yoana "Yoana"). He plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Northern Realms deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck") and sells a few cards too. He also sells the [Nilfgaardian Armor set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Armor_set "Nilfgaardian Armor set") and the [Nilfgaardian crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_crossbow "Nilfgaardian crossbow") once the free DLCs are installed. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What type of gwent deck does the Quartermaster at Crow's Perch use?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Quartermaster at Crow's Perch, located next to armorers Fergus Graem and Yoana, uses the Northern Realms deck in gwent games. He also sells gwent cards, the Nilfgaardian Armor set, and the Nilfgaardian crossbow. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What items does the Quartermaster at Crow's Perch sell?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Quartermaster at Crow's Perch offers items such as the Nilfgaardian Armor set and Nilfgaardian crossbow, available post the installation of free DLCs. He also trades Gwent cards and uses a Northern Realms deck. Prices for these items can be reduced by 10% upon completing the secondary quest, Forefathers' Eve, and siding with the Pellar against witch hunters. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is the significance of the Nilfgaardian Armor set sold by the Quartermaster?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Nilfgaardian Armor set, released as a free DLC for The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt on June 3, 2015, is available from the Quartermaster at Crow's Perch. The set includes four armor items and three horse equipment pieces. Players can equip the basic version at level 10, while the upgraded version is available at level 35. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Who are the armorers located next to the Quartermaster at Crow's Perch?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Adjacent to the Quartermaster at Crow's Perch, you'll find the armorers Fergus Graem and Yoana. Their workshops are located in the upper section of Crow's Perch. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is the Quartermaster's role in the Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Quartermaster at Crow's Perch provides Adalbert Kermith's third map in the Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 3. This map is essential for locating all the superior diagrams for the Cat School Gear. 

Provided by: Fandom
