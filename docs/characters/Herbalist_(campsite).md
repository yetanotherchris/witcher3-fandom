## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **herbalist** appears at the [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(campsite\) "Abandoned site \(campsite\)") west of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch") once it's clear of hostiles. Like other [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") at liberated [abandoned sites](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") around [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), his appearances may change between visits and he sells mostly low-grade [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients").
