## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Olivier** was the owner and [innkeeper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeeper "Innkeeper") of [The Kingfisher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), having named it after one of [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion")'s ballads. 

He plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), using the [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"), and will reward the [Tibor Eggebracht card](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tibor_Eggebracht_\(gwent_card\) "Tibor Eggebracht \(gwent card\)") if beaten. He also sells the following cards: 

  * [Havekar Healer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Havekar_Healer_\(gwent_card\) "Havekar Healer \(gwent card\)")
  * [Havekar Smuggler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Havekar_Smuggler_\(gwent_card\) "Havekar Smuggler \(gwent card\)")
  * [Mahakaman Defender](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mahakaman_Defender_\(gwent_card\) "Mahakaman Defender \(gwent card\)")
  * [Vrihedd Brigade Veteran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vrihedd_Brigade_Veteran_\(gwent_card\) "Vrihedd Brigade Veteran \(gwent card\)")

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**Who kills Olivier during the secondary quest 'Now or Never'?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

During the 'Now or Never' quest, Olivier meets his end at the hands of the witch hunters. His position is subsequently filled by an unnamed innkeeper. In case the player hasn't secured Olivier's card, it can be located in the room near the counter, across the entrance to the cellar. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is the significance of the name 'The Kingfisher' for Olivier's inn?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Olivier's inn, The Kingfisher, is a prominent tavern in Novigrad's Hierarch Square district. The name 'The Kingfisher' signifies its status as a major attraction in the city, offering entertainment such as performances by Priscilla, also known as Callonetta, and the opportunity to play Gwent, a popular card game. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What cards does Olivier sell at The Kingfisher?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Olivier sells the Havekar Healer, Havekar Smuggler, Mahakaman Defender, and Vrihedd Brigade Veteran cards. Additionally, he plays gwent using the Monsters deck and gives the Tibor Eggebracht card as a reward if defeated. 

Provided by: Community

## Notes

  * If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") decides to help [Anisse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anisse "Anisse") and [Berthold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berthold "Berthold") during the secondary quest [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), Olivier will be killed by the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter"). He is then replaced by an [unnamed innkeeper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(The_Kingfisher\) "Innkeep \(The Kingfisher\)").
  * If Olivier dies during the Now or Never quest and the player didn't win his card yet, it can be found in the room near the counter, opposite the entrance to the cellar.
