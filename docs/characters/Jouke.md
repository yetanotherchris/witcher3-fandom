**Jouke** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") archer serving on [HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/HMS_Oxenfurt-Tretogor "HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor") to protect King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") during _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Throughout the [Reason of State](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reason_of_State "Reason of State") side quest, he and his comrades, [Caspar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caspar "Caspar"), [Damiaan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Damiaan "Damiaan"), [Freek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freek "Freek"), and the witch hunter [Egbert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Egbert "Egbert") are commanded by King Radovid to stay near the gate to [St. Gregory's Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/St._Gregory%27s_Bridge "St. Gregory's Bridge") and secure the area. He died from a shot by a [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") partisan.
