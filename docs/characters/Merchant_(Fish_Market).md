## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** is located in the northwest corner of the [Fish Market](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fish_Market "Fish Market") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). He plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Nilfgaardian deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck"), and sells primarily [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and some [food and drink](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"). His pricing is that of a generic merchant, which is lower than that of [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") and [innkeepers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeeper "Innkeeper") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

Merchant will appear from 5 am - 7 am, taking an hour break, before appearing again from 8am - 7pm
