This **Quartermaster** was stationed at the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), and was an amateur [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"). 

He's located by the entrance to the main building of the garrison, where [Peter Saar Gwynleve](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peter_Saar_Gwynleve "Peter Saar Gwynleve"), the captain of the garrison, has his working space and desk. When met for the first time, he'll have around 680 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). 

## Biography

When [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") first talked to him, he accused him of spying, showing that he did not trust [Nordlings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nordlings "Nordlings"). He did so again if Geralt asked about camp morale, and added that the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") victory was assured and nothing more. However, this may only have been a warning since he was keen to trade and do blacksmith work.
