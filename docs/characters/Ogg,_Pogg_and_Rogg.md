**Ogg, Pogg and Rogg** are three rock trolls living in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") with whom [Thaler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thaler_\(spy\) "Thaler \(spy\)") is hiding out. The trio appears to be very fond of "shoeses" (shoes) especially those made by Thaler. While hiding out in their cave, Thaler has also instructed them in the fine art of verbal expression. Sadly, the trolls never receive their shoes.
