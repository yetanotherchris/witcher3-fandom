"**Hendrik** " (real name unknown) was a [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") agent living in [Heatherton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heatherton "Heatherton") to spy and report on the local population. 

## Biography

Taking on the guise as a merchant, Hendrik secretly took [notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hendrik%27s_notes "Hendrik's notes") on those in the area. In particular, he was tasked with finding and reporting as much information as he could on [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") back to the [emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor"). 

Sometime in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt"), in their attempt to find Ciri for themselves, learned of Hendrik's true motives and appeared in Heatherton for the information. They attacked the village, killing many of the populace, but Hendrik hid in his hut. They found him, however, despite brutally torturing the spy, they never found his notes on Ciri before they killed him and left empty-handed. Shortly thereafter [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), who'd been sent by [Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis") to see the spy, found Hendrik's corpse and thus had to use his skills to find the spy's notes. 

## Journal entry

    _Considering the way spy corps of all stripes tend to function, "Hendrik" was undoubtedly not this man's real name. Nevertheless, that was the only appellation the[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") knew for His Imperial Majesty's nose to the ground in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")._
    _After asking around, Geralt learned a "Hendrik" lived in the village of Heatherton._
    _Sadly, Geralt arrived too late. The village had been ravaged, its inhabitants slaughtered and Hendrik's service to Nilfgaard terminated in a most violent and disturbing manner._
    _Yet the agent showed such determination before he died that[Vattier de Rideaux](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vattier_de_Rideaux "Vattier de Rideaux"), chief of the [imperial spy corps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Secret_Service "Nilfgaardian Secret Service"), surely beamed with pride if he ever heard of it. Despite undergoing horrific tortures, Hendrik preferred to die in agony rather than reveal where he hid his reports. They remained in his hut for Geralt to find, providing him with several pieces of important information._
