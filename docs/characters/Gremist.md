**Gremist** was a cantankerous [druid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") and master [alchemist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") in [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") by the pond, north of [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith") who jealously guarded alchemy secrets he had gathered over his life. 

He sells a wide variety of [ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"), [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Runestone"), [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Glyph"), and [alchemical formulae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_alchemy_formulae "Category:The Witcher 3 alchemy formulae"), but will only be a vendor after you complete the secondary quest [Practicum in Advanced Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Practicum_in_Advanced_Alchemy "Practicum in Advanced Alchemy"). 

## Biography

Unlike many other druids, Gremist was rather standoffish, wishing to be left alone: more precisely he became a druid specifically so he wouldn't be bothered by people and their petty matters and have to feign compassion when he felt none. 

Despite this gruff manner though, he did have a few close friends, like [Fritjof](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fritjof "Fritjof"), and, when drunk, actually showed a happier side. 

However, having spent countless years toiling away at learning all he could about alchemy, he didn't wish to take on an apprentice for the mere sake that he went through a lot of trouble to learn what he knew. 

_If Geralt helps him out:_ Reluctantly, Gremist accepted [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s help and gave him three tasks to complete before he'd accept the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") as his apprentice. With the [alcohol](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_spirit "Skellige spirit") Geralt recovered, Gremist and Fritjof got drunk and recalled fun times they had in the past before Gremist, true to this promise, taught Geralt what he knew, albeit in a drunk state.
