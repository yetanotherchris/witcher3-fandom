**Madman Lugos** , son of [Lugos Harelip](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lugos_Harelip "Lugos Harelip") and jarl of [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond"), had an especially fiery personality, even more so than the typical residents of [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). He reigned from [Kaer Muire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Muire "Kaer Muire") on the southern half of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig").

**What is the significance of the title 'Madman' given to Lugos Drummond?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Lugos Drummond, known as 'Madman', earned his title due to his fiery personality and violent temper. His reputation for furious outbursts and ongoing feuds made him a figure to be avoided in the isles. His most notable rivalry was with Crach an Craite, whom he accused of encroaching on his domain.

Lugos Drummond, known as Madman Lugos, was the jarl of Clan Drummond in Skellige. Despite his average size and strength, his fiery personality and violent temper made him a formidable figure. His constant feuds, especially with Crach an Craite over territorial disputes, were well-known. Even Donar an Hindar, known for his calm demeanor, was not immune to Lugos' confrontational nature.

Despite lacking exceptional size or strength, Madman Lugos, the jarl of Clan Drummond, commanded respect due to his notorious temper. His propensity for feuds and shouting matches, even with the typically calm Donar an Hindar, made men step aside when he approached. His most significant contention was with Crach an Craite, whom he accused of domain theft.

Madman Lugos, the jarl of Clan Drummond, held his reign from Kaer Muire, situated in the southern region of Ard Skellig. His fiery temperament was well-known, and he was perpetually in disputes with his neighbors, notably Crach an Craite, who he claimed had encroached on his territory.

## Journal entry

    _Though Madman Lugos, jarl of Clan Drummond, was nothing special in terms of size or strength, most men in the isles still stepped aside when they saw him coming. Eternally feuding with his neighbors, Lugos had a reputation for a furious and violent temper and managed to get into shouting matches with even the usually-placid[Donar an Hindar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Donar_an_Hindar "Donar an Hindar")._
    _Yet Lugos' biggest rival was[Crach an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite"), whom he accused of stealing part of his domain (which in his reckoning encompassed all of Ard Skellig). The clans' quarrel went back hundreds of years and there was nothing to indicate it would end while these two fierce enemies lived. Lugos hated Crach with a passion dampened only by the laws of Skellige and the disproportionate balance of power between the two clans._
    **If[Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite") or [Hjalmar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar_an_Craite "Hjalmar an Craite") become the next ruler:**

    _Seeing his own son die and Crach an Craite's child take the throne of Skellige proved too much for Lugos to bear. Enraged and embittered, he refused to listen to reason and attacked the[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), thus picking a fight that could only end one way._
