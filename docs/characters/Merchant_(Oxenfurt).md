## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed **merchant** offers his goods at the marketplace in the north of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). He sells animal hides, [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components"), [mandrake cordial](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mandrake_cordial "Mandrake cordial"), and [paint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Paint "Paint"). He is also a [Gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players") and uses the [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck") deck.
