## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Agda** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") woman from [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"), and the fiancée of [Timmon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timmon "Timmon"). 

## Biography

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1272 "Timeline"), [Ulf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ulf_\(Timmon%27s_brother\) "Ulf \(Timmon's brother\)") and [Mikkel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikkel_\(Skelliger\) "Mikkel \(Skelliger\)"), Timmon's brothers, picked her up and were on their way back to [Faroe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle") when a storm hit, causing their ship to crash and Agda unfortunately drowned.
