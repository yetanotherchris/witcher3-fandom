## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Yontek** was said to be biggest fool in the village of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch") located in central [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). Despite this, he wasn't greedy like the other villagers, which actually resulted him being the only one not turned into a pig when the rest stole from an underground altar. 

## Associated quest

  * [Fools' Gold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fools%27_Gold "Fools' Gold")

## Notes

  * He can be found sometimes with his hand glowing and waving it around and saying "hocus-pocus-ala-docus", suggesting he is capable of magic. After which he may make a remark saying "Mhh, still piggies, didn't help", which suggests he is trying to remove the curse from the villagers. 
    * This would suggest he is able to harness [chaos](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos"), but due to his low intelligence, only to a very limited degree where it pretty much has no real effect.
