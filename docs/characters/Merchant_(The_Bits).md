\- Merchant  
  
  
This **merchant** in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s poor district, [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits"), is a neighbor of the [Loan Shark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loan_Shark "Loan Shark"). He sells mostly [junk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") items and is a [gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players") using the [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms_Gwent_deck "Northern Realms Gwent deck") deck.
