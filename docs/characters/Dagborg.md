## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

Lord **Dagborg** was a [duke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Political_titles#Duke_/_Duchess "Political titles") who was given the fiefdom of [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") in the event that [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") won the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III") and [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") was restored as a province of the Empire. 

## Associated quest

  * [Something Ends, Something Begins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)") (witcher ending)

## Notes

  * During the "witcher" version of the [epilogue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)"), he can be seen just outside the [White Orchard inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_inn "White Orchard inn"), with a messenger proclaiming him as the new ruler to the local peasants.
