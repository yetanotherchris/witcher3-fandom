## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Smith** located in [Kaer Muire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Muire "Kaer Muire") plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") with the [Scoia'tael deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael_Gwent_deck "Scoia'tael Gwent deck"). He sells almost only generic [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith#Skellige "Blacksmith") items, but is guaranteed to offer most lesser [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone") at level 13. 

Depending on choices made during [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"), he may later become unavailable when the story progresses to the early parts of [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone") quest.
