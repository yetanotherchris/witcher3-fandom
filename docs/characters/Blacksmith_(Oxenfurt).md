## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **blacksmith** is located on the eastern side of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and his customized offerings include a couple of [crossbows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") and many types of [bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts") for them. His pricing and inventory is otherwise that of a [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") and as such sells many other weapons, all base game [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone") and diagrams for the non-greater ones, as well as [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting components"). 

He's also a [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") player and has a fairly strong [Monsters deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monsters_Gwent_deck "Monsters Gwent deck"). 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What unique crossbows does the Oxenfurt blacksmith offer?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Oxenfurt blacksmith provides unique crossbows and various bolts. His inventory aligns with Novigrad blacksmiths, offering customized crossbow options. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How does the Oxenfurt blacksmith's inventory compare to Novigrad's?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Oxenfurt blacksmith, found on Oxenfurt's east side, stocks crossbows and bolts. His inventory and prices match those of a Novigrad blacksmith, offering a similar selection. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What runestones are available at the Oxenfurt blacksmith?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Oxenfurt blacksmith provides runestones such as Lesser Devana and Lesser Morana runestones. These can be crafted, looted, or bought at the blacksmith in Oxenfurt. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What crafting components can be found at the Oxenfurt blacksmith?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Oxenfurt blacksmith provides crossbows, bolts, and crafting components similar to a Novigrad blacksmith. Available items include manuscript pages for Dancing Star, Moon Dust, and Devil's Puffball.
