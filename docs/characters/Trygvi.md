## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Trygvi** was one of inhabitants of [Lofoten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lofoten "Lofoten") who survived the attack of the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). He and his wife both came out of the attack without sustaining any injuries, except mental trauma. He wanted to rake off the snow that [Eredin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eredin "Eredin") and his men brought to the village from the roof of his house, but his wife told him to rather not do it, as it could bring some misfortune.
