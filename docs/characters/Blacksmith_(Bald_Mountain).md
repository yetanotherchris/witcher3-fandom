## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **blacksmith** in the little [village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Road_to_Bald_Mountain "Road to Bald Mountain") near [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)") appears at the start of the [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(quest\) "Bald Mountain \(quest\)") quest. Before that, only [foglets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fogler "Fogler") can be seen at the location, and they return after the quest completes as well, but he will remain there regardless. 

He doesn't sell anything interesting, having the inventory and pricing of generic [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), but can be useful for repairs and getting rid of excess gear, as Bald Mountain is part of a long sequence of [main quests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests") during which [fast traveling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") is disabled and gameplay is restricted to story-relevant areas.
