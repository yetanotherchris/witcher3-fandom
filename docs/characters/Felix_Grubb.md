**Felix Grubb** was a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") captain that commanded a regiment stationed at the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post") to prevent refugees from swarming through into [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and Redania. 

When talking to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), he makes it clear in his comments that he's racist, wanting to punishing the [elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") in the camp in retaliation for the small [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") unit that was killing off supply transports despite not having any evidence those in the camp were helping them.
