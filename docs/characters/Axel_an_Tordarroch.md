## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Axel an Tordarroch** was a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") warrior from [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"), somehow related to the erstwhile Jarl [Harald Houndsnout](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harald_Houndsnout "Harald Houndsnout"), whom he served as one of his personal guard. When the [ice giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant") attacked, Axel tried to reach the jarl, but he had to face some [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") and passed out during the fight. Shortly later, a longship with a few warriors guarding children and women brought the unconscious Axel to [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), where he then frequented [The New Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port") tavern.
