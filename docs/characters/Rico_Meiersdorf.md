## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Rico Meiersdorf** was the youngest son of [Holofernes Meiersdorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holofernes_Meiersdorf "Holofernes Meiersdorf"). In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") the [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") was working for [Francis Bedlam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam"), better known as the _King of Beggars_ , as a [spy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spy "Spy"). However [Cyprian Wiley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley"), the person Rico was tasked to spy on, exposed him and brought him to his [casino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_casino "Whoreson Junior's casino") in order to torture him and learn all the information he knew. 

_If Geralt helps Rico:_ Luckily for the halfling, the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") freed him and helped him escape from Wiley's casino. The King of Beggars later recommended he leave the city for his safety.
