## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Yolar** is a [druid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") living in a grotto under [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith"). As a merchant, he sells some herbs and a lot of alchemical [formulae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formulae "Formulae"). He is one of the druids who helps [Ermion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mousesack "Mousesack") get the elements under control after [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") uses the [Mask of Uroboros](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mask_of_Uroboros "Mask of Uroboros").
