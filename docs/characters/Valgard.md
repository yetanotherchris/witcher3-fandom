## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Valgard** was a fistfighter in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). 

He can be found just outside the door to [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room at [New Port Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Port_Inn "New Port Inn") in [Kaer Trolde Harbour](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor"). 

_If Geralt fights him:_ Knowing he had no chance against a [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), he talked to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") in private, asking him to throw the match so he could impress [Eryka](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eryka "Eryka"), a woman he was interested in. 

    _If Geralt loses the first match:_ Valgard thanked the witcher as Eryka approached, clearly impressed with his fighting ability.
    _If Geralt doesn't throw the first match:_ Miffed, Eryka showed her disappointment in Valgard and walked off, despite Valgard calling after her.
