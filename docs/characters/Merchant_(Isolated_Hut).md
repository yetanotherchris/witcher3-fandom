## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **merchant** moves back to the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"), if the site is liberated. He has a [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith")'s inventory, selling [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"), [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") and [crafting diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams"), and is also the only merchant to sell all [bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts") diagrams. 

## Notes

  * He isn't always available for trade even when present, but meditating next to him will fix that issue.
