## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Havart var Moehoen** served in the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") army as [Field marshal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Field_marshal "Field marshal") after the death of [Menno Coehoorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Menno_Coehoorn "Menno Coehoorn"). He was in a position of confidence with [Emperor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor "Emperor") [Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis"), and so was granted governorship over [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria"). 

We can see him for the first time and only time when we come to [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), where he is making deal with the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") but leaves when [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") arrives. After this he is not shown anymore, only mentioned in a [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_demands "Nilfgaardian demands").
