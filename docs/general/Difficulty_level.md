# Difficulty level

**Difficulty level** is a mechanism in [_The Witcher_ games](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/CD_PROJEKT_RED%27s_The_Witcher_franchise "CD PROJEKT RED's The Witcher franchise") that affects the degree of challenge each game features. 

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Tw1 difficulty levels](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/b/b9/Tw1_difficulty_levels.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20190601220756)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/b/b9/Tw1_difficulty_levels.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20190601220756) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw1_difficulty_levels.jpg)

  * [Easy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_gameplay "The Witcher gameplay") \- _"For novice gamers. Combat is easier. Alchemy is not required to survive"._
  * [Medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_gameplay "The Witcher gameplay") \- _"Intermediate level recommended for most gamers. Combat is challenging. Alchemy is useful"._
  * [Hard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_gameplay "The Witcher gameplay") \- _"For advanced gamers. Combat is difficult. Alchemy is required to survive"._

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 difficulties](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/2c/Tw3_difficulties.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20160518145913)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/2/2c/Tw3_difficulties.png/revision/latest?cb=20160518145913) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw3_difficulties.png)

There are four levels of difficulty: 

### Just the Story! (Easy)

_Enjoy a Smooth Ride Through the World_

  * If you are a casual gamer or just someone who wants to play for the story, choose the first difficulty option.
  * Meditation regenerates vitality.
  * Guards take 25% of your coin upon knocking you out.

### Story and Sword! (Normal)

_You're Happy to be Challenged_

  * Players earn the default number of [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") for tasks.
  * Meditation regenerates vitality.
  * Guards take 50% of your coin upon knocking you out.

### Blood and Broken Bones! (Hard)

_You're a Seasoned, Demanding Gamer_

  * players earn 90% of the default number of [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") for tasks.
  * Meditation does NOT regenerate vitality.
  * Guards take 75% of your coin upon knocking you out.

### Death March! (Very Hard)

_You're Truly Insane and Loving It_

  * Choose it if you are a hardcore gamer and worry that the other modes would be too easy for you!
  * Meditation does NOT regenerate vitality.
  * Guards take 100% of your coin upon knocking you out.

### Difficulty settings

These values were pulled from extracted game files and looking through the scripts. 

Stats  | Easy  | Normal  | Hard  | Very Hard   
---|---|---|---|---  
Enemy Health  | -50%  | +10%  | +20%  | +80%   
Enemy Damage Bonus  | 0%  | +40%  | +130%  | +230%   
Experience Gain  | 100%  | 100%  | 90%  | 80%   
  
The difficulty setting can be changed at any point in the game through the Options menu. 

## _[Thronebreaker: The Witcher Tales](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thronebreaker:_The_Witcher_Tales "Thronebreaker: The Witcher Tales")_

[![Thronebreaker difficulties](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0e/Thronebreaker_difficulties.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20190103120212)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0e/Thronebreaker_difficulties.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20190103120212) [![Thronebreaker difficulties](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0e/Thronebreaker_difficulties.jpeg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20190103120212)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0e/Thronebreaker_difficulties.jpeg/revision/latest?cb=20190103120212) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Thronebreaker_difficulties.jpeg)

There are three levels of difficulty: 

### Adventurer (Easy)

_For players who want to sit back, relax, and enjoy the story with the option to skip battles._

### Battle-Hardened (Normal)

_For players seeking a moderate challenge and an impactful story._

### Bonebreaker (Hard)

_For players familiar with[Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent") and ready to put their skills to the test._
