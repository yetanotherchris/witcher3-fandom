_**A Fractured Land: Tales of the Northern Realms**_ is a compendium of in-universe information that's credited as being written by the fictional game character, [Marcus T.K. Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson"), though in reality was written by the character's namesake, David S. J. Hodgson, along with [CD PROJEKT RED](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/CD_PROJEKT_RED "CD PROJEKT RED"). 

It was only released as part of the Prima Guide's Collector Edition for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

The portions credited to [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") are drawn verbatim from the game's journal entries.
