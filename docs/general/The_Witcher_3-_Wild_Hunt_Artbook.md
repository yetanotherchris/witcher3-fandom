## Publication Information

### Author(s)

[CD PROJEKT RED](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/CD_PROJEKT_RED "CD PROJEKT RED")

### Publication date

May 19, 2015

### Type

Artbook

### No. of pages

196

_**The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Artbook**_ is book containing 196 pages of [characters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_characters "Category:The Witcher 3 characters"), [weapons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_weapons "Category:The Witcher 3 weapons"), [armors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_armor "Category:The Witcher 3 armor"), [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary"), and other such concepts from development in between 2011-2015. A physical copy came with the collector's edition of _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ while a digital copy came standard for PC versions (either through Steam or GOG.com).
