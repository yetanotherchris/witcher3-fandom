# The Witcher 3 soundtrack

**The Witcher 3 soundtracks** include 3 soundtracks for the game and its expansions, _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion and _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 

## Original soundtrack

[![Digital Edition Cover](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7a/The_Witcher_3_Wild_Hunt-Soundtrack_cover.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20150613222158)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7a/The_Witcher_3_Wild_Hunt-Soundtrack_cover.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20150613222158) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:The_Witcher_3_Wild_Hunt-Soundtrack_cover.jpg)

**The Witcher 3 soundtrack** for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ was composed by [Marcin Przybyłowicz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcin_Przyby%C5%82owicz "Marcin Przybyłowicz"), Mikołaj Stroiński, and Percival, and consists of 35 tracks with a total running time of approximately 81 minutes and 7 seconds. It is included with the game as well as through download on GOG.com.
