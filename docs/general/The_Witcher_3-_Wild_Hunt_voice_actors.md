# The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt voice actors

For _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , the English **voice actors** were directed by Kate Saxon, with additional directing by Mark Healy and Damien Goodwin. The Polish **voice actors** were directed by Konrad Tomaszkiewicz and Michal Konarski. For voice actors in the first expansion, see [Hearts of Stone voice actors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone_voice_actors "Hearts of Stone voice actors"), and for their colleagues in the second expansion, see [Blood and Wine voice actors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_voice_actors "Blood and Wine voice actors").
