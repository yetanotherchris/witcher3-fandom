# Crafting

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

[![Tw2 icon craft](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/db/Tw2_icon_craft.png/revision/latest?cb=20110710144040)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/db/Tw2_icon_craft.png/revision/latest?cb=20110710144040)

To craft an item in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ , you must have a [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_2_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 2 crafting diagrams") describing how it is produced and the [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_crafting_components "The Witcher 2 crafting components") listed in that diagram. Items can only be crafted with the help of [craftsmen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Craftsmen "Craftsmen"), who collect a fee for their work. Craftsmen can be found in towns and villages, especially in their central sections, among other traders. 

  * Diagrams list the components required to produce a given item. They can be purchased from [traders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_merchants "The Witcher 2 merchants"), though you may also find them lying about for the taking.
  * Crafting components can be purchased from traders or found in various locations. Once you have a diagram and the components it lists, find a craftsman.

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 icon armorer](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/6/6e/Tw3_icon_armorer.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/22?cb=20170901041520)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Armorer")[![Blacksmith](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/1/1f/Tw3_icon_blacksmith.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/30?cb=20160810120328)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmiths#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Blacksmith")

_For crafting components, see[The Witcher 3 crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components")._

In _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , only [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") are able to craft armor and only [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") can forge true weapons. These craftsmen can be located by their icons on the map and its smaller version, the minimap. Since [Patch 1.20](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.20_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.20 \(The Witcher 3\)"), missing components can be purchased directly from the craftsman during the crafting process by pressing the right mouse button.
