    _A virtual**Easter egg** is an intentional [hidden message](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/hidden_message "wikipedia:hidden message") or feature in an object such as a movie, book, CD, DVD, computer program, or video game. The term draws a parallel with the custom of the [Easter egg hunt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easter_eggs_\(decorative\) "wikipedia:Easter eggs \(decorative\)")._

This article catalogues Easter eggs and pop culture references in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. For easier viewing, this page has been broken up into sub-pages based on the topic.
