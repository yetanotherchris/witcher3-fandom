# REDkit

**REDkit** is a modding tool developed by [CD PROJEKT RED](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/CD_PROJEKT_RED "CD PROJEKT RED"). Originally made for _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ , they later released another version under the same name for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

[![Redkitbeta](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/77/Redkitbeta.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20130516024638)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/77/Redkitbeta.png/revision/latest?cb=20130516024638) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Redkitbeta.png)

REDkit was released to the public on 13 May 2013. It allows users to create new lands, quests, and NPCs for the game.[1] For one month prior to the release date, REDkit was in closed beta. 

REDkit aimed to provide a very professional modding experience by what has been seen of it so far and an example of the sort of experience it can create can be seen in the almost completed mod '[Lykaon](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C80zLTe1UGc&feature=player_embedded)'. 

Sometime after March 2016, CD PROJEKT RED removed the download links and re-directed the REDkit page to their main page. However, the link was later re-added. 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 redkit logo white](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/38/Tw3_redkit_logo_white.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20240606171924)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/38/Tw3_redkit_logo_white.png/revision/latest?cb=20240606171924) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw3_redkit_logo_white.png)

A new version of REDkit was released on May 21, 2024 for the third game. It's available for free through Steam, GOG.com, and Epic Games Store.[2]

On top of releasing the new editor, it has its own dedicated website which includes useful guides, from how to set it up to tutorials to explaining the different tools available through REDkit.
