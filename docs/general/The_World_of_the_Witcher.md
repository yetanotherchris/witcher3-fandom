_**The World of the Witcher**_ (Polish: _Wiedźmin. Kompendium o świecie gier_) is a compendium of in-universe information authored by [Marcin Batylda](https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/14070484.Marcin_Batylda), the writer of _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Written from [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion")'s perspective and other friends of [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), it is divided into five different chapters on various subjects. 

## Official statement

    _Dive deep into the world of[monster hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), as the prominent characters from the universe take you on a guided tour of the fascinating dark fantasy adventure that is The Witcher. This gorgeous, illustrated hardbound volume contains in-depth knowledge about the locales, the deadly beasts that inhabit them, and the lethal weapons used to put them down._

## Chapters

  1. [The World](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World") and Its Inhabitants (By [Borch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Villentretenmerth "Villentretenmerth"))
  2. The [Witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") (By [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir"))
  3. The [Magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") and [Religions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Religion "Religion") of the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent") (By [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"))
  4. The Beasts of the [Northern Kingdoms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms") (By Geralt)
  5. The Story of Geralt of Rivia (By Dandelion)
