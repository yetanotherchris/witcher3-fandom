# Mini-games

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

There are four **mini-games** to be found in _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ : 

  * [Dice poker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_dice_poker "The Witcher dice poker") — a good way to make, or lose [orens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren") quickly.
  * [Fistfighting](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_fistfighting "The Witcher fistfighting") — also a good way to make money, more certain than poker and also a way of getting into some areas.
  * [Drinking contests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drinking_contest "Drinking contest") — a definite path to information and some goodies.
  * [Romance cards](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Romance_card "Romance card") — a unique mini-game in which the player collects "naughty" playing cards for Geralt's sexual conquests.
