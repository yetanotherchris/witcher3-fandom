### Occurrence

Beaches

**Crabs** are crustaceans found along shorelines in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are not hostile.
