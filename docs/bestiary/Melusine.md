**Melusine** is an old and powerful [ekhidna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna "Ekhidna") living in a large complex of caves near the village of [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag"), located in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). It is presumed that a cult once worshipped Melusine as evidenced by the large winged statue located in the caves beneath the mountains which she inhabits. 

## Bestiary entry

    _I... I saw a shadow... Great wings, like a cloud passin' o'er the sky. Then I heard him scream.  
– Britt of Svorlag_
    _In the mountains near the village of Svorlag in Skellige lies an enormous complex of caves. For many centuries they were given wide berth on account of the bloodthirsty ekhidna known as Melusine who had made them into her lair._
    _Melusine was a beast so powerful some islanders worshiped her as a semi-divine being. The[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), however, harbored no such delusions. He knew she was an extremely powerful monster - but a monster all the same. This meant she could be killed. To do so, he would need the highest quality bolts, a solid silver blade - and a steady hand._

## Trivia

  * In European folklore, [Melusine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Melusine) is a feminine spirit of fresh water. She is often depicted as half-woman and half-serpent or -fish, similar to a mermaid.

## Notes

  * Melusine is vulnerable to the effects of [Hornwall horn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hornwall_horn "Hornwall horn"), but often flies high enough to recover mid-air or avoid the horn's range of effect.
