# Ghost

The terms **ghost** or **specter** are both hypernyms used to refer to spectral [creatures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Creature "Creature") that generally haunt cemeteries and crypts. Sometimes, however, the specters are tethered to a certain person or a place; some unfinished business keeping them trapped in the mortal plane.
