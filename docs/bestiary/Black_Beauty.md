## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Black Beauty** is the name of the [Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree")'s new body after transferring their soul over to a black [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") chose to free them. 

After the cutscene, she will run to a bridge over a [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") just southwest of [the Crossroads signpost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Velen\) "Crossroads \(Velen\)"). While she seems to disappear, she actually clips through the bridge and can be found pushing against one of the support walls beneath it. Later, she can be found in the swamps east of [The Orphans of Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog") signpost. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What happens to Black Beauty after the soul transfer?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Following the soul transfer, Black Beauty, the Ghost in the Tree's new form, heads towards a bridge over a bandit camp near the Crossroads signpost. If this was part of the main quest where Geralt requested the spirit to save the orphans, the spirit commits to fulfilling this promise before departing. Otherwise, the spirit expresses its freedom and departs. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Where can Black Beauty be found after the cutscene?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Post cutscene, Black Beauty, the spirit now in a horse form, heads to a bridge over a bandit camp southwest of the Crossroads signpost. She seemingly vanishes but is actually beneath the bridge, pushing against a support wall. Subsequently, she can be located in the swamps east of The Orphans of Crookback Bog signpost. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is the significance of the bridge in Black Beauty's story?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The bridge in Black Beauty's story is a crucial location where the horse can meet a bandit camp or a rabid rock troll. This encounter typically happens at night, emphasizing the horse's glowing red eyes. Occasionally, Black Beauty may become trapped under the bridge. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Where does Black Beauty appear in the swamps?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Black Beauty, the horse from Anna Sewell's novel, is located in the swamps east of The Orphans of Crookback Bog signpost. Initially, she is near a bandit camp southwest of the Crossroads signpost, appearing to vanish but is found near a bridge's support. Using Axii enables an extended ride on Black Beauty. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What role does Black Beauty play in The Whispering Hillock Return?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Whispering Hillock Return, Black Beauty, the key horse, appears in the swamps east of The Orphans of Crookback Bog signpost. The horse seems to vanish but is actually located against a bridge support wall. Using Axii before mounting allows for an extended ride on Black Beauty. 

Provided by: Fandom
