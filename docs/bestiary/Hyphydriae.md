# Hyphydriae

**Hyphydriae** are young cubs of [Ilyocoris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ilyocoris "Ilyocoris") protecting their parents against other [marine beasts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sea_creature "Sea creature") and other enemies, like [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hyphydriae** were supposed to appear in the third game during its development in 2013 - 2014 but was removed around this time.[1]

### Information from leaked materials

    _Shellfish like creature resembling[żyrytwa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ilyocoris "Ilyocoris"), but less powerful and a lot smaller. It lives in the water but can go out to the surface._

  * _Role in the group: Invidual_
  * _Preferred behavior: aggressive - chases the opponent and attacks._
  * _Special attacks and actions:_
    * _Fast attacks - when in close range, it attacks with fast paced strikes, dealing medium damage._
  * _Defence:_
    * _Thick chitin armor - gives Hyphydriae a good armor protection._
  * _Vital points:_
    * _Instant kill:_
      * _Piercing book lungs (PL: płucotchawki) - causes the monster to suffocate and die - it still tries to attack for a while, as it doesn't feel pain (cost: 4 points)._
    * _Protection: none_
    * _Critical effect/disable ability: none_
    * _Massive damage_
      * _Piercing abdominal artery - piercing this artery causes a long term Bleeding effect on Hyphydriae (cost: 1 point)._
  * _Immune to: Silver and[Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard")-like forces, [Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni") causes minimal damage, because the creature lives in shallow water, poisoning and bleeding_
  * _Vulnerable to: Steel,[Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden")._
