**Mucknixer** is the name given by [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") to a dangerous species of [drowner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") and [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowned_dead "Drowned dead"). They are different from their brothers in look. It is commonly thought that these creatures arise from the bodies of those who drown in shallow water: lost travelers falling into bogs, children who swim too far from the shore. This opinion is as widespread as it is false, for the beasts are in fact another [post-Conjunction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Conjunction_of_the_Spheres "Conjunction of the Spheres") relic. 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

You can run into **mucknixer** in swamps and beaches of former [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") province of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), combat tactics are basically same as for drowners. Also found at an abandoned site in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige").
