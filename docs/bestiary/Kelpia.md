**Kelpia** (also known as **Spikesprout**) were supposed to appear in the third game during its development in 2013 - 2014 but was removed around this time.[1]

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

### Information from leaked materials

    _Creature similar to[endregas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega "Endrega"), but smaller. Lives in forests and pretends to be a plant / hides in grass. It's small but very fast, resembles a flea with green spikes on its back. Attacks in large groups._

  * _Role in the group: Invidual_
  * _Preferred behavior: suicidal - it acts like a bee, while protecting the nest it tries to perform a suicidal attack._
  * _Special attacks and actions:_
    * _Runs towards target and shoots it's poisoned spikes when in the attack range (2 meters). The attack tell animation lasts for 1 second. Kepia dies after performing the attack._
  * _Defence: none._
  * _Vital points:_
    * _Instant kill:_
      * _Tack down with the sword and crush with foot (this is the only vital spot - the creature is too small to have more)._
    * _Protection: none_
    * _Critical effect/disable ability: none_
    * _Massive damage: none_
  * _Immune to: Silver_
  * _Vulnerable to: Steel,[Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden"), [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard")_
