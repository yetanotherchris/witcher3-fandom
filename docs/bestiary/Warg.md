# Warg

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Warg](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/37/Warg.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20090414105151)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/37/Warg.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20090414105151) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Warg.jpg)

Warg

**Wargs** are [creatures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_bestiary "The Witcher bestiary") which were part of the early design for _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ , but later cut, and likely used as the basis for the [armored hounds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armored_hound "Armored hound") created by the [Grand Master](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master").

## Trivia

  * First appearing in [J. R. R. Tolkien](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._R._R._Tolkien "wikipedia:J. R. R. Tolkien")'s 1937 novel _[The Hobbit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hobbit "wikipedia:The Hobbit")_ , wargs are a particularly large and evil kind of wolf that could be ridden by orcs.
