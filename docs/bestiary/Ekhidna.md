# Ekhidna

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What is the Ekhidna's primary habitat in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Ekhidna, a creature in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, primarily dwells in bodies of water, shorelines, and wetlands. Their activity increases during rainfall, making these aquatic habitats perilous. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How does the appearance of an Ekhidna differ from a regular siren in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3, an Ekhidna is a legendary monster, a stronger and larger variation of the siren. It bears a more human-like appearance, similar to the sea witch in the books. Like the siren, the Ekhidna uses sharp claws in attack. It is more active in the rain and inhabits wetlands. The Ekhidna is susceptible to silver, fire, and kinetic damage. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What weapons are most effective against an Ekhidna in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3, an Ekhidna is best combated using a silver sword, as these are highly effective against monsters and magical creatures. Steel swords and unarmed attacks are not recommended. Crossbows can also serve as a useful tool for grounding flying adversaries. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What are the unique attack methods of an Ekhidna in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, the Ekhidna, a stronger siren variant, employs distinct attack strategies. It uses sharp claws and is particularly active in wetlands during rain. The Ekhidna's tactics include flying and underwater attacks, making aquatic environments perilous. It's susceptible to silver, fire, and kinetic damage, and a crossbow is an effective weapon against it. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What weather conditions make an Ekhidna more active in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Ekhidna, a formidable monster in The Witcher 3, exhibits increased activity during rainy conditions. This creature, a stronger variant of the siren, resides in wetlands and its activity peaks when it rains. 

Provided by: Fandom

## _[The Witcher: Monster Slayer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher:_Monster_Slayer "The Witcher: Monster Slayer")_

The ekhidna is a legendary [monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_Monster_Slayer_bestiary "The Witcher Monster Slayer bestiary") and a stronger variation of the [siren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren"). It looks more human-like, similarly to how the sea witch is depicted in the [books](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sword_of_Destiny "Sword of Destiny"). It attacks just like the [siren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren"), using its sharp claws. Ekhidna dwells in wetlands and is more active during rain. It is vurnerable to silver, fire and kinetic damage.
