**Rabbits** , or **hares** , can be found in wild places throughout the [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World"), whether it be [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), or [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"). They are not hostile and will flee from [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and any [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") near them.
