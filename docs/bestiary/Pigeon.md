## Information

### Class

### Occurrence

Towns. Stick to [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") as their main food source.

**Pigeons** can be found in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , especially on the streets, roofs and bridges of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad").
