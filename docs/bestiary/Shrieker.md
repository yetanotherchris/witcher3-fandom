**What kind of creature is the Shrieker in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In Witcher 3, the Shrieker is a formidable Draconid, a variant of a Cockatrice. It dwells in a cave close to Crow's Perch, exhibiting predatory tendencies towards livestock and humans. Its vulnerabilities include Grapeshot, Draconid oil, and Aard. Defeating the Shrieker yields loot such as a Cockatrice egg, mutagen, stomach, trophy, and diverse monster components.

The Shrieker, a formidable cockatrice in the Witcher 3, is known for its predation on livestock, specifically cows and goats. It also poses a threat to humans, attacking them on sight.

The Shrieker, a creature in the Witcher 3, resides in a cave located near Crow's Perch. This is revealed during the Contract: Shrieker quest, where the player's objective is to eliminate the Shrieker.

**What is the relationship between the Shrieker and the residents of Crow's Perch?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Shrieker, a formidable cockatrice, resides near Crow's Perch, instilling fear among the inhabitants. Known for its lethal attacks on humans and animals, the locals have offered a significant reward for its elimination. The Shrieker's terror, along with the baron's unpredictable behavior and his band's greed, has resulted in a constant state of fear among the residents. The creature primarily targets livestock but doesn't hesitate to attack humans when encountered.

## Combat tactics

The Shrieker is noticeably strong for the recommended level of 8, and will give most players a bit of a headache. The recommended level may be wrong, as an axe with a level requirement of 12 drops from the Shrieker when killed. As such, most players may want to wait until around level 10 or even 12 before taking it on. 

  * Attacks: A frontal attack (peck), side swipes with its wings if you are not directly in front of it. Divebombs when in the air.
  * Weaknesses: [draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Draconid_oil "Draconid oil") and [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard"); Its back. The Shrieker has no rear attacks, making it easily its weakest point. If you can stay at its back, you will have a much easier time with this enemy.

  * Strengths: Frontal attacks may be hard to dodge, as they have little delay to start and have quite a lengthy damage window.
