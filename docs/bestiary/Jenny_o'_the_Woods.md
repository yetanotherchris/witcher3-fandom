**Jenny o' the Woods** was the name given to a powerful [nightwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith") that haunted the fields near the village of [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**Who was Jenny o' the Woods before she became a nightwraith?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Before becoming a nightwraith, Jenny o' the Woods was Zula, a woman from Midcopse, Velen. Her transformation occurred post her murder by a villager, Bokhai, whom she had rejected. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What led to Zula's transformation into Jenny o' the Woods?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Zula, who lived in Midcopse, planned to escape with her partner, Ontan, due to the frightening advances of another villager, Bokhai. Upon discovering their plan, Bokhai pursued and killed Zula, causing her transformation into the nightwraith, Jenny o' the Woods. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Who was the person responsible for Zula's death?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In 1272, Zula, a resident of Midcopse, was murdered by Bokhai, a fellow villager. Bokhai's jealousy and resentment stemmed from Zula's rejection of him due to her relationship with Ontan. Upon learning of Zula and Ontan's plan to escape, Bokhai pursued them and ended up killing Zula. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is the relationship between Zula and Ontan?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Zula and Ontan, residents of Midcopse in 1272, were involved in a romantic relationship. Their bond was disrupted by Bokhai, a villager who harbored unrequited feelings for Zula and persistently pursued her. The tension culminated in Zula's tragic demise, caused by Bokhai. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How can Jenny o' the Woods be defeated in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In Witcher 3, Geralt can defeat Jenny o' the Woods by using the dagger that killed Zula. This action summons the wraith, which Geralt must then swiftly eliminate to prevent her from regaining health. After the fight, Geralt can acquire the nightwraith trophy and Forgotten Vran Sword from her remains and claim his reward from the ealdorman, thereby completing the quest. 

Provided by: Fandom 

## Background

Jenny o' the Woods used to be a woman named [Zula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zula "Zula") who lived in Midcopse. Zula's transformation into a nightwraith came about after she was murdered by another villager named [Bokhai](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bokhai "Bokhai"), whom she rejected as she was in a relationship with [Ontan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ontan "Ontan"). As Bokhai wouldn't leave them alone to the point Zula feared for her life, she and Ontan made plans to run off in the middle of the night. However, Bokhai learned of this attempt to run off and ran after them and ultimately killed Zula. 

## Bestiary entry

    _So... That's to mean... our Zula's the wraith?  
– [Bolko](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolko "Bolko"), ealdorman of Midcopse_
    _It is said true love's flame is never extinguished. This sad truth is the reason why Zula of Midcopse, whom an early death had separated from her beloved, was unable to find peace in the next life, and instead returned to haunt her former environs as a nightwraith._
    _Fighting such an apparition is extremely difficult. A nightwraith will form mirror images of herself to confuse her opponent and aid her in battle. She herself can take on immaterial form, rendering her invulnerable to blows. The best way to force her out of this state is to set a trap with the[Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") Sign, then quickly followup with [Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni") while she is caught. Most important of all, however, is this: never attempt to fight one in the middle of the night, when the moon hangs high in the sky._
    _This vengeful[wraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") might very well have killed every last inhabitant of the village, had not a famous witcher, [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), appeared in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") \- and been in need of a bit of coin._
