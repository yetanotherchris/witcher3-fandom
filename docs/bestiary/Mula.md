## Physical Characteristics

### Distinctions

Invisible to [magical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") scanning

A **mula** or **moola**[1] is a type of intelligent [vampire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire"). Same as [bruxae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bruxa "Bruxa"), [alps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alp "Alp") and [higher vampires](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Higher_vampire "Higher vampire"), they are immune to sun, and can be sometimes mistaken with the previous two. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What type of vampire is Mula?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Mula, alternatively Moola or Muleh, is a type of intelligent vampire that can withstand sunlight and often gets confused with bruxae, alps, and higher vampires. They can elude magical scanning and have the power to become invisible, deceiving their adversaries. Mulas are formidable foes who can attack with aggression, replenish their health by drinking blood, and enter a heightened state known as 'Blood rage' that increases their attack speed and damage. They also have the ability to metamorphose into mist, enabling them to traverse through other beings and heal themselves. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What makes Mula different from other vampires?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Mula, an intelligent vampire, is invisible to magical scanning, a trait that sets it apart. It shares sun immunity with bruxae, alps, and higher vampires. Mula can become invisible, sneak up on victims, and suck their blood. It can also transform into mist, pass through creatures, and regenerate health. Its special attacks include strong attacks, blood drinking to restore health and trigger Blood rage, and a jump attack. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Why was Mula removed from the third game during development?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In the development stages of The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt (2013-2014), a character named Mula, also referred to as Muleh, was planned to be included. Mula was a higher vampire, capable of withstanding daylight and feeding during the night. Despite being a formidable adversary with the ability to turn invisible, Mula was omitted from the final game release for reasons not publicly specified.

## Trivia

  * In [Roma folklore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Romani_society_and_culture "wikipedia:Romani society and culture"), a [Mullo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mullo_\(vampire\) "wikipedia:Mullo \(vampire\)") (_Muli_ : female, _Mulo_ : male) is an undead, revenant, or vampire, which translates to "one who is dead". According to some tales, the body of a Mulo lacks bones and the middle finger of each hand is missing.
