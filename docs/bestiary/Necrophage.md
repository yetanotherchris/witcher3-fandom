# Necrophage

**Necrophage** (from Greek _nekros_ , 'corpse' and _phagein_ , 'to eat') is a hypernym used to refer to [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") that generally haunt cemeteries and battlefields, places where dead bodies lay, eating the remains. Some, however, have decided they have no qualms about being the ones to fill the area with corpses instead of just being carrion [crows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow "Crow"). 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What is the origin of the term 'Necrophage'?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Derived from Greek, 'Necrophage' combines 'nekros' (corpse) and 'phagein' (to eat). This term is a general reference to monsters known for haunting locations with dead bodies, such as battlefields and cemeteries, and consuming the remains. Certain necrophages are also known to cause deaths, not just scavenge. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What types of locations are typically haunted by Necrophages?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Necrophages generally haunt areas with dead bodies, specifically cemeteries and battlefields. Some necrophages, including Cemetaurs and Grave Hags, are particularly associated with these locations, feeding on the remains and sometimes causing more deaths. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What is a common misconception about the classification of Necrophages?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Contrary to popular belief, Necrophages are not merely scavengers. While they are known for haunting cemeteries and battlefields to consume corpses, some Necrophages also act as predators, killing to create their own source of corpses for sustenance. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What are some specific types of Necrophages?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Necrophages, monsters known to inhabit cemeteries and battlefields, feed on the remains of the deceased. Specific types include Cemetaurs, immune to common poisons and found on battlefields and cemeteries, and Grave hags, located in regions like Velen and Skellige. Other types encompass Ghouls, Alghouls, Graveirs, and Devourers. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How do some Necrophages differ in their behavior towards corpses?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Necrophages, typically found in cemeteries and battlefields, consume the remains of the dead. Some, however, actively cause death, creating their own source of corpses. Variants like Cemetaurs knock down and consume their victims alive. Ghouls and graveirs, preferring fresh carcasses, hunt in small groups and will pursue living prey when possible. 

Provided by: Fandom
