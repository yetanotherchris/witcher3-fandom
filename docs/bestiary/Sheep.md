### Loot

[Old sheepskin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk")  
[Raw meat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Raw_meat "Raw meat")

**Sheep** are animals found on farms and pastures in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are often accompanied by shepherds and guard [dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog"). 

They behave quite different from [cows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cow "Cow") and [pigs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pig "Pig"), escaping from the player even if not attacked. Loot can be an [old sheepskin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") or [raw meat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"). Some [innkeepers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeeper "Innkeeper") sell [mutton stew](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink") and [mutton legs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"), but these cannot be obtained from killing sheep, just purchased. 

## Notes

  * A small group of immortal sheep can be found next to a [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") and [cyclops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops") northeast of [Heatherton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heatherton "Heatherton"). Kept inside a fenced area, they're impossible to kill or interact with.

## Trivia

  * The immortal sheep near the cyclops in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") may be a reference to [Polyphemus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polyphemus "wikipedia:Polyphemus") from the Greek epic poem, _[Odyssey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Odyssey "wikipedia:Odyssey")_.
