The **goose** is a domesticated fowl. One of the most common and widespread domestic animals used by [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Humans "Humans") primarily as a source of food - both their meat and their eggs are consumable. Male geese are called **ganders** ; females are simply **geese**. The young are called **goslings**. 

Goose meat, either stuffed or with a sauce, is often prepared in meals, particularly on the holiday of [Saovine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Saovine "Saovine").[1] Roast goose in pear sauce prepared by [halflings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") is seen as a delicacy.[2]

In 1250s, one of the items sold to adventurers by the [gnomish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnome "Gnome") inventor called "[Q](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Q "Q")" in their [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") workshop had been poisoned goose [feather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feather "Feather")s.[3]
