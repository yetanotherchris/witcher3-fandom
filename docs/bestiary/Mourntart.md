**Mourntart** is a powerful [grave hag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grave_hag "Grave hag") prowling the cemetery of [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale"), where she digs up graves and kidnaps wandering children. 

## Journal entry

    _Somebody's doing some cooking... This... it's a[human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") femur... a child's.  
– [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")_
    _Most grave hags rarely attack humans, preferring instead to feed on the rotten remains they dig out of graves. Yet some individuals grow bold over the years and begin sneaking into huts to steal children and kill the elderly. Just such a monster was tormenting the inhabitants of Lindenvale._
    _The[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") knew this grave hag would put up a fierce fight. He would have to watch out for her powerful claws, capable of smashing through any block or parry, and her long, venomous tongue. He also realized her attacks would be so quick not even his mutated reflexes would be able to keep pace - meaning only judicious use of the [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") Sign would all[[_sic_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "wikipedia:Sic")] him to survive and conquer._
    _The outcome of the fight was not hard to predict. The grave hag perished in the very graveyard which had heretofore served as her feeding grounds, and her body was dumped in a grave she had dug with her own claws._
