### Occurrence

Open sea

**Whales** are enormous waterbound creatures living in seas. They are feared but also hunted by [Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). Bones from them are used to build huts in case of the unavailability of wood, as well as [exquisite bows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Milva%27s_bow "Milva's bow").[1] Whales are also featured in legends of islanders: for example, [Broddr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Broddr "Broddr"), heroic founder of [Clan Dimun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Dimun "Clan Dimun"), was said to be eaten by a whale.
