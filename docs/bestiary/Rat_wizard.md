# Rat wizard

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Rat wizard** was supposed to appear in the base game during its development in 2013 - 2014 but beast was removed prior to release.[1]

### Information from leaked materials

    _Ghost of the wizard surrounded by the[rats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rat "Rat"). Similar to "[Morowa dziewica](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Plague_maiden "Plague maiden")"._

  * _Role in the group: Individual_
  * _Preferred behavior: careful - performs melee attacks only when the opponent is near, uses ranged attacks from a greater distance._
  * _Special attacks and actions:_
    * _Fast attacks - when in close range, he attacks with fast paced strikes, dealing medium damage._
    * _Rats summon - he growls and spawns rats swarm that chases his opponents, when it's near the target it deals damage - the rats are climbing on the opponent and bite him (rats VFX is also played on the target). Player can use[Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") to protect himself from the rats. Player can use [Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni") or a bomb to destroy rats. Rat wizard can spawn up to 3 rat swarms._
    * _The flies breath - Rat wizard can also breath flies swarm from his lungs. The flies is a projectile attack._
  * _Defence:_
    * _Shield of rats - Rat wizard can spawn rats that cloak him (he growls to do it). He gains armor this way. Player can disable the shield casting several Igni spells or using bombs (each hit with a spell decreases the number of rats protecting the monster)._
  * _Vital points:_
    * _Instant kill:_
      * _Soul piercing - the soul of this wraith is in its head.[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has to hit the occiput to be able to strike the soul. Player has to get on the back of the monster to be able to use this vital spot (cost 5 points)._
    * _Protection: none._
    * _Critical effect/disable ability:_
      * _Cutting Vocal cords - disables Rats summon attack and the Shield of rats ability (cost 2 points). Not enabled when the monster uses the Shield of rats ability._
      * _Piercing lungs - disables Flies breath ability (cost 2 points). Not enabled when the monster uses the Shield of rats ability._
    * _Massive damage_
      * _Piercing Thymus - will Poison the monster._
  * _Immune to: Steel, all witcher's signs except for the[Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard"). Shield of rats can be destroyed with Igni._
  * _Vulnerable to: Silver, Aard_
