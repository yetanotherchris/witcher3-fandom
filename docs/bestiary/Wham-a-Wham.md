### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What type of creature is Wham-a-Wham in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In Witcher 3, Wham-a-Wham is a Rock Troll of the Ogroid class. This formidable creature inhabits a silver-laden cave on Ard Skellig. It has immunity to the majority of physical attacks, but Ogroid oil and Quen can harm it. Defeating Wham-a-Wham yields various loot, including a cave troll trophy and troll mutagen.

**What is the temperament of Wham-a-Wham?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Wham-a-Wham, a volcanic-like rock troll from Witcher 3, resides in a silver-rich cave on Ard Skellig. Despite being perceived as slow and dim-witted, Wham-a-Wham can become dangerously enraged, making it deadly to those who provoke it.

**Where does Wham-a-Wham reside in the Witcher 3 game?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, the character Wham-a-Wham, a formidable Rock Troll, inhabits a silver-laden cave on Ard Skellig, in proximity to Blandare. The creature is impervious to the majority of physical attacks, but is vulnerable to Ogroid oil and Quen.

**What is the best strategy when encountering Wham-a-Wham?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The optimal strategy when facing Wham-a-Wham, a formidable rock troll, involves either swift evasion or the use of the Quen Sign for protection. His strikes are potent, and his skin, especially robust at the back, resists most sword attacks. Evasion or strategic defense is the key to overcoming this adversary.

Wham-a-Wham, a rock troll in Witcher 3, is deemed deadly due to his volatile temperament and formidable strength. He dwells in a silver-rich cave on Ard Skellig and is easily provoked, especially when his territory is trespassed.

## Bestiary entry

    _Thems in-walk troll house. Troll say, "Out mans!" But they wham-a-wham troll rocks. So troll wham, too!  
– Rock troll known as Wham-a-Wham_
    _Rock trolls come across as dim-witted, ponderous, slow - stupid, to be frank. It's easy to dismiss them - and even easier to enrage them. When this happens, one's best bet is to run as fast as possible, for an angered rock troll is as deadly as a thundering landslide. The truth held in the case of the massive rock troll known as Wham-a-Wham. His every blow struck like a battering ram - meaning one needed either avoid them entirely or protect oneself with the[Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") [Sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign"). Its hard skin meant it was near invulnerable to sword strikes - especially those dealt from behind, where its rock armor is the thickest. The only hope for defeating it lay in taking advantage of its typical troll slowness - especially if further exploited using the [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") Sign._
    _A certain set of[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") miners learned first-hand just how dangerous rock trolls can be. Ignoring all warnings, they started to mine a rich silver vein in the cave which Wham-a-Wham called home. They died soon thereafter - crushed or torn to rough bits. This time, a troll's killings were a justified act of self-defense - after all, the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") reasoned, every troll's cave is his castle._
