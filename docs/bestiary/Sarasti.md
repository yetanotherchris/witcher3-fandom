**Sarasti** is a very old and very powerful [ekimmara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekimma "Ekimma") resting inside an [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") ruin beneath [Byways](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Byways "Byways"), [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

## Bestiary entry

    _We dug out a chamber. There was a beast sleeping inside. We fell over the props to collapse the ceiling, but it must not have buried the monster...  
– [Bytomir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bytomir "Bytomir") of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")_
    _Raiders of elven tombs either get rich quickly, or die trying. The[Aen Seidhe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Seidhe "Aen Seidhe")'s disintegrating temples and palaces hold priceless treasures, true, but within them many a foul monster waits in deep slumber. Such was the case in Byways, where a few peasants, unaware of the dangers they faced, awoke an unusually dangerous ekimmara._
    _The[witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") knew this monster would be no easy kill. That the [vampire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vampire "Vampire") would quickly regenerate, that its blows would cause heavy bleeding, that it was able to disappear into thin air. Yet he also knew he could overcome these advantages. He needed merely to use [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") and [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") Signs and strike heavy blows. Oh, and avoid getting killed._
    _The vampire had already managed to kill several peasants and a patrol of heavily-armed[Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). The list of victims would surely have grown even longer, had not [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), the White Wolf, happened to waltz into town._

## Notes

  * Killing Sarasti awards the [Fearless Vampire Slayer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fearless_Vampire_Slayer "Fearless Vampire Slayer") achievement.

## Trivia

  * He is very likely named after Jukka Sarasti, the vampire character in [Peter Watts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Watts_\(author\))' sci-fi novel _[Blindsight](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blindsight_\(Watts_novel\))_.
