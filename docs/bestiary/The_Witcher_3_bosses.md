**Bosses** are creatures or people you face during the [main quests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_main_quests "The Witcher 3 main quests") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and its expansions. For a complete list of all monsters you can face see [The Witcher 3 bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary"). 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**Who is the final boss in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, the concluding adversary is Eredin, the general of the Wild Hunt. This climactic encounter marks the end of Geralt's narrative. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What strategies can be used to defeat the last boss in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Defeating the last boss in The Witcher 3 involves evasion and strategic use of the Aard sign. Avoid direct combat with the Queen, as she can eliminate Geralt with a single strike. Use the Aard sign to collapse the passage and slow her down, then use it again to destroy the Salamandra Cave supports, causing a collapse on the queen. In the final stage, employ counterattacks and block incoming attacks. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How does the difficulty of bosses in The Witcher 3 compare to other games?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Bosses in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, including the final boss, present varying degrees of challenge based on the selected difficulty level. In 'Hard' mode, bosses exploit every opportunity to attack, increasing the combat difficulty. This feature is less prominent in 'Easy' or 'Normal' modes. This dynamic difficulty adjustment can make Witcher 3 bosses more challenging compared to other games. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What makes the hardest boss in The Witcher 3 particularly challenging?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Witcher 3's hardest boss is challenging due to its unique abilities and the game mode's difficulty. Boss fights, including the final boss, necessitate strategic planning and comprehension of attack patterns. The DEATH MARCH game mode's difficulty level adds to the challenge. Bosses include Eredin, Caranthir, and Imlerith. 

Provided by: Fandom
