# Old Speartip

## Old Speartip

[ ![Tw3 Speartip](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/5b/Tw3_Speartip.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/268?cb=20160529141919) ](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/5b/Tw3_Speartip.png/revision/latest?cb=20160529141919 "Tw3 Speartip")

## Information

### Class

[Ogroid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ogroid "Ogroid")

### Variations

[Cyclops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops")

### Occurrence

Cave in [Kaer Morhen valley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen_valley "Kaer Morhen valley")

**Old Speartip** is the name for a [cyclops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops") that dwells in the caves north of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"). He is used as part of a [Wolf School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") witcher trial: witchers-to-be are sent into his cave and whoever emerges passes. 

If the children were lucky, he was asleep when they ventured through his cave, as the rhyme goes _"Old Speartip is one deep sleeper, wake him up and you'll sleep deeper"_. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**What role does Old Speartip play in the Wolf School witcher trial?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Old Speartip, a cyclops, dwells in a cave north of Kaer Morhen and is integral to the Wolf School witcher trial. Prospective witchers must enter his cave, with successful emergence marking the completion of the trial. Speartip's deep sleep often aids the children in their venture. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What happens when Old Speartip is awakened?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Upon awakening, Old Speartip, a cyclops residing in the caves north of Kaer Morhen, initiates a fight. This occurs if someone attempts to approach him stealthily. Lambert, another character, reacts positively to this confrontation. Old Speartip's existence is tied to the Wolf School witcher trial. Lambert displays disrespect towards Old Speartip by spitting on his remains post-defeat. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**In The Witcher 3, why do Geralt and Lambert have to pass through Speartip's cave?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Geralt and Lambert traverse Speartip's cave in The Witcher 3 to access the Circle of Elements, aiming to infuse a phylactery with elemental power. The player can opt to stealthily navigate the cave or use aard to clear debris, with the latter potentially awakening Speartip. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Where does Old Speartip reside in the world of The Witcher?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Old Speartip, an ogroid cyclops, inhabits a cave in the north of Kaer Morhen valley. This cave plays a crucial role in a Wolf School witcher trial. Prospective witchers must navigate through the cave, their success hinging on whether Speartip is awake or asleep. 

Provided by: Fandom 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

In _The Witcher 3_ , Geralt and Lambert have to pass through Old Speartip's cave in order to reach the [Circle of Elements](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Circle_of_Elements "Circle of Elements") to enrich a [phylactery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phylactery "Phylactery") with elemental power. The player can either sneak through or use [aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard") and blast through debris, and risk waking Speartip. Should the player decide to confront the cyclops, [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert") will spit on its corpse afterwards saying "That's for [Voltehre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Voltehre "Voltehre")." 

Combat with Speartip is no different than combat with any other cyclops, make use of [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") to absorb unavoidable hits, and the use of [igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni")'s alternate form helps to damage him through a constant burning effect.
