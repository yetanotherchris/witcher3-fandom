# Cursed One

**Cursed ones** are a class of monster in the [bestiary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") of _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and its expansions. Beings attributed to this class are usually created as the result of a [curse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Curse "Curse"), often from [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") or humanoid specimens, but not necessarily. All cursed ones are vulnerable to [cursed oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cursed_oil "Cursed oil"), and must be fought with a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") for maximum effectiveness. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**How can players effectively combat cursed monsters in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3, effectively combating cursed monsters involves using the Yrden sign to slow them and make them vulnerable. Players should carry Devil's Puffballs to weaken these fast and lethal creatures. Sturdy armor is essential, as cursed monsters like werewolves can jump great distances at high speeds, posing a significant challenge. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Why is cursed oil effective against cursed monsters in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Cursed oil in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt boosts attack power by 10% against cursed monsters when applied to a sword. This oil is tailored to combat the cursed ones, improving damage dealt to these foes. Crafted with ingredients such as dog tallow and wolfsbane, it is also a component for enhanced cursed oil. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What are the origins of the cursed ones in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, cursed ones are monsters formed due to curses, typically from human or humanoid origins. These cursed monsters are susceptible to cursed oil and are most effectively fought with a silver sword. Their origins are linked to various causes, including divine straying, adultery, or druid curses. Geralt discovers that many were born after their mothers were bitten by a specific beast during pregnancy. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What role do curses play in creating cursed ones in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, curses are key to creating cursed ones. These spells cause transformations or misfortunes, leading to the emergence of cursed monsters like werewolves and botchlings. Such creatures are best fought with a silver sword and cursed oil. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Which cursed ones appear in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt features cursed ones such as Archespores, Berserkers, Botchlings, Lubberkin, The Toad Prince, Ulfhedinn, and Werewolves. These cursed monsters result from curses and are best fought with cursed oil and a silver sword. 

Provided by: Fandom
