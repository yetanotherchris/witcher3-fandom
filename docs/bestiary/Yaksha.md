**Yakshas** are a robust [subspecies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Race "Race") of [sylvans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sylvan "Sylvan") found in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). They are extremely rare and categorized as [relicts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Relict "Relict") by the [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"). 

## Characteristics

### Physical description

Their appearance combines traits of [goats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Goat "Goat") and rotund [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") with size of an overgrown [troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll "Troll"). They have mostly red skin with large black segments on the head, chest, and arms. On their head, yakshas sport large horns while their legs are hairy and end with cloven hooves.[1]

### Mental traits

Unlike typical sylvans, yakshas are fiercely agressive and keen on fighting. They are otherwise lazy but still surprisingly agile.[2]

At least one yaksha has been known to use [fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fire "Fire")-based magic, being able to breathe flames and throw [fireballs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fireball "Fireball") in combat.[1]
