# Nekker warrior

**Nekker warriors** are a larger version of the [nekker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker") and typically distinguished by their red-painted faces. 

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

[![Tw2 Nekker warrior](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/c/c1/Tw2_Nekker_warrior.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20110606121051)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/c/c1/Tw2_Nekker_warrior.png/revision/latest?cb=20110606121051) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw2_Nekker_warrior.png)

They are tougher and along with the regular items, they also drop [warrior nekker blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Warrior_nekker_blood "Warrior nekker blood"), which can be used in a quest in Chapter III. Nekker warriors support their smaller companions when they prepare an attack or ambush an enemy. Nekkers Warriors cease to give XP once killed when Geralt reaches Level 14. 

### Associated quest

  * [An Encrypted Manuscript](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Encrypted_Manuscript "An Encrypted Manuscript")

### Note

  * You can meet these creatures both in Chapter I, in the [Flotsam forests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flotsam_forests "Flotsam forests") and in Chapter II, in the wilderness surrounding [Vergen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vergen "Vergen"). But they are fewer and less aggressive than in the former Chapter.
  * There is a bug in the game and it is possible that the [warrior nekker blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Warrior_nekker_blood "Warrior nekker blood") disappears from Geralt's inventory. If this happens and you cannot find any more nekker around, it means that you are not able to complete the quest in Chapter III at [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne").

## _[The Witcher: Monster Slayer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher:_Monster_Slayer "The Witcher: Monster Slayer")_

The nekker warrior has 3 other variations: **carnage nekker** , **nekker shaman** , and **nekker bonecrusher**. They differ in appearance, mainly in color. The carnage nekker and the nekker shaman are slightly stronger than the nekker warrior.
