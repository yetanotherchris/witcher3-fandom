**Chicken** is a domesticated fowl and prevalent throughout most of the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent"). One of the most common and widespread domestic animals, used by [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") of [Northern Kingdoms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms") and [Nilfgaardian Empire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") primarily as a source of food - both their [meat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Raw_Meat "Raw Meat") and their [eggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Egg "Egg") are consumable.
