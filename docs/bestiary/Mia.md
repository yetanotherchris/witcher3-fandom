**Mia** was a [sheep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sheep "Sheep") from [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") that lived in [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal") that could be used as bait to lure out a [forktail](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail "Forktail"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Contract: Dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Dragon "Contract: Dragon")

## Notes

  * Depending on how quickly one dispatches the forktail, Mia can survive the encounter, in which case [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") will make an amusing remark.
