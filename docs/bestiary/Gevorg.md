## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gevorg** is the legendary white [whale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale "Whale") of [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")'s myths. According to Skelligers, he is to appear when specters descend from the heavens into the waves, the serpent wakes the sea's wroth, and an icy monster devours an isle. 

[Eyvind](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eyvind "Eyvind"), a [skald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skald "Skald") from [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn"), calculated and waited his whole life to become first bard to compose a ballad about him. Another man, Ismael, claims it's his and that he will kill anyone who would try to hunt it.[1]
