## Information

### Loot

[Pork](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pork "Pork")  
[Pig hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pig_hide "Pig hide")

Domestic **pigs** can be found in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , in farms and villages in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). 

They don't run away when approached.
