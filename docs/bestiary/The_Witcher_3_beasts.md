# The Witcher 3 beasts

In _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , **beasts** is the collective term for non-[magical](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") creatures including: 

  * [Bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear")
  * [Dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog")
  * [Panthers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Panther "Panther") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [The Beast of Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beast_of_Beauclair "The Beast of Beauclair")[1] ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Three Little Pigs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Three_Little_Pigs "Three Little Pigs") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Wild boars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_boar "Wild boar") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
