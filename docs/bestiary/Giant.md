A **giant** , sometimes also referred to as a **titan** , is a large, strong creature that usually inhabits mountains, but can also be found in other areas.[2]

By [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") only a handful of them are still alive, making many people believe they're just a myth and even most [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") believe they've gone extinct.[3] Even though they are quite sentient, all attempts to communicate with them have ended in vain.[4]

## Behavior

In [Beauclair Palace](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair_Palace "Beauclair Palace"), there is a tapestry depicting a fight between the giants and [cyclopses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops") while another shows a titan trying to kill a [dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon "Dragon").[5] This may suggest that giants are highly aggressive against others, even if they too are [ogroids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ogroid "Ogroid").[6]

## Trivia

[![Pawel-mielniczuk-giant](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/42/Pawel-mielniczuk-giant.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160305140424)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/42/Pawel-mielniczuk-giant.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160305140424) [![Pawel-mielniczuk-giant](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/42/Pawel-mielniczuk-giant.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20160305140424)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/42/Pawel-mielniczuk-giant.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160305140424) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Pawel-mielniczuk-giant.jpg)

  * [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") town [Yspaden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yspaden "Yspaden") is be a reference to a chief of giants from Welsh tale _[Culhwch and Olwen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Culhwch_and_Olwen "wikipedia:Culhwch and Olwen")_ named "[Ysbaddaden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ysbaddaden "wikipedia:Ysbaddaden")", spelled as "Yspaden" in _[The World of King Arthur. The Malady](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_World_of_King_Arthur._The_Malady "The World of King Arthur. The Malady")_.
  * A giant was supposed to appear in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ as an opponent with his own bestiary entry, unique combat tactics, and other features. In [CD PROJEKT RED](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/CD_PROJEKT_RED "CD PROJEKT RED")'s 2014 leaked materials, he's described as a "Giant human approx. 4 meters high, quite intelligent. Lives in the forests."[7] Character Art Director, Paweł Mielniczuk, even created a "one hour speed sculpts during production practice sessions."[8] However, the giant was removed later in development. It's also possible that he was simply postponed for expansions and dubbed Golyat.
