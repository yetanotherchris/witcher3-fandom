**Hounds of the Wild Hunt** are monsters in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They usually follow riders of the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") or are summoned by them. 

The bestiary entry can be obtained by reading [Of Fire and Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Fire_and_Stone "Of Fire and Stone"), which however (while existing in the base game files) cannot be obtained before the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**How are Hounds of the Wild Hunt summoned?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Hounds of the Wild Hunt are summoned by the spectral riders of the Wild Hunt. These creatures, made from magic ice crystals, follow the Hunt and are known for their fierce nature. They can descend from the night sky, bringing cold and death. Their main weapons are claws and teeth, and they are vulnerable to Dimeritium bombs, Elementa oil, and the Igni and Axii signs.

Hounds are integral to the Wild Hunt, acting as fierce beasts that follow the hunters. In European folklore, these hunt hounds pursue the souls of sinners. They are summoned by the Wild Hunt, symbolizing danger or catastrophe.

**How can players encounter Hounds in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, players encounter Hounds of the Wild Hunt during certain quests. These spectral creatures appear alongside the Wild Hunt's riders. They attack in groups, making strategic combat essential. Players can exploit their susceptibility to fear and knockdown with Group Style tactics. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What legends surround the Hounds of the Wild Hunt?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Hounds of the Wild Hunt are mythical beings from Northern and Central European folklore. They follow the spectral hunters of the Wild Hunt, symbolizing danger or catastrophe. These hunt hounds pursue the souls of sinners, descending from the sky with cold and death. Created from magic ice crystal, they attack with claws and teeth, trailing their masters like dust clouds after a comet.

## Bestiary entry

    _Baying at the heels of the Wild hunt are its Hounds, fierce beasts which follow it like dust clouds trailing after a comet. Hushed legends speak of them losing their way at times and descending from the night sky to earth, cold and death following in their wake.  
– [Essi "Blackjack" Daven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Essi_Daven "Essi Daven"), trobairitz_
    _Born, or so some experts believe, of magic ice crystal, the Hounds of the Wild Hunt race alongside their spectral masters. Like ravenous, feral dogs they are capable only of mindlessly attacking whatever crosses their path._
    _The Hounds' chief weapons are their claws and teeth. Their battle tactics rely on knocking their opponents to the ground before tearing them to shreds. Badly wounded Hounds fall into a kind of frenzy that adds to their deadly might. These beasts also use their powers over the cold to freeze the ground around them and create sharp spikes of ice. Creatures born of frost,they feel pain from the Igni Sign, and there is also reason to believe Axii can momentarily weaken their drive to kill and somewhat weaken these fierce foes._
    _The Hounds' icy nature also means they can draw strength from extreme cold. Thus one should be particularly on guard when fighting them during blizzards or in glacial regions. Elven legends likewise claim they gain strength when a red moon - a known herald of the Hunt's arrival - hangs in the sky._

## Combat tactics

The hounds of the Wild Hunt fight as viciously as wolves or wild dogs. They can be momentarily weakened by axii, but only for an instant. These hounds hunt in packs, and can quickly overwhelm the unprepared. Do not let them surround you if at all possible, use yrden to slow any hounds too close for comfort, and break away if needed. 

[![Tw3 fighting Hound of the Wild Hunt](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/8/8f/Tw3_fighting_Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/294?cb=20160409135211)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/8/8f/Tw3_fighting_Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160409135211) [![Tw3 fighting Hound of the Wild Hunt](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/8/8f/Tw3_fighting_Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/294?cb=20160409135211)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/8/8f/Tw3_fighting_Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160409135211) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw3_fighting_Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt.jpg)

The icy nature of the hounds makes them perfect targets for igni. It also makes it so that any cold environment empowers them. Back off from the hounds if they start forming ice spikes. These barriers will pierce through you if you attempt to strike. 

If Yrden can catch multiple hounds in one circle, throwing in a Dragon's Dream bomb and igniting the gas cloud with igni can seriously wound a large group of hounds.
