The **Royal Griffin** is a sub-species of the common [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)"). It is the first serious monster that you will fight in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. One, and likely the only difference from regular griffins, is their size, with longer wings and a larger body. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), along with [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir"), first encounter this sub-species in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") for a [contract](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beast_of_White_Orchard "The Beast of White Orchard") in exchange for a pouch of gold and info about [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") of [Vengerberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vengerberg "Vengerberg"). 

For strategies, see _[Griffin (creature)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)")_.
