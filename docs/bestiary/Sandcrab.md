**Sandcrabs** are [insectoids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid "Insectoid") encountered for the first time in the [Ddiddiwedht Desert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ddiddiwedht_Desert "Ddiddiwedht Desert"), which [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Avallac'h](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Avallac%27h "Avallac'h") traverse in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Sandcrabs are not particularly strong opponents. As it turns out, they are only dangerous in large quantities. An important fact is that sandcrabs can suddenly jump out of the ground in a similar fashion to [giant centipedes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giant_centipede "Giant centipede"). 

Sandcrabs have well-developed carapaces, and two pairs of legs and a pair of wings, even though it is unknown whether the creatures are able to fly. A most eye-catching feature is the very long horn on their heads. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**Where are sandcrabs first encountered in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, sandcrabs are first encountered during the main quest 'Through Time and Space' in the Ddiddiwedht Desert. Geralt of Rivia and Avallac'h face these insectoids, which can jump from the ground like giant centipedes. Sandcrabs feature sturdy carapaces, multiple legs, wings, and a prominent horn. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What makes sandcrabs dangerous in large numbers?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Sandcrabs are dangerous in large numbers because they can suddenly emerge from the ground, similar to giant centipedes. They have strong carapaces and long horns. Their hunting style involves creating sinkholes to trap prey, then draining them of moisture. In groups, they can coordinate attacks and set multiple traps, increasing their threat. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What unique feature do sandcrabs possess despite their insectoid nature?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Sandcrabs, though insectoid, have the unique ability to fly. They are distinguished by a long horn on their heads, similar to a rhino beetle or scarab. 

Provided by: Fandom
