**The Apiarian Phantom** is a powerful [Hound of the Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hound_of_the_Wild_Hunt "Hound of the Wild Hunt") who strayed from its pack during one of their numerous raids. 

One day, while aimlessly wandering, it came across the [Honeyfill Meadworks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks"), a bee farm owned by a rich [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") family, where it destroyed numerous beehives and killed a few farmhands before running away into a nearby construction site. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**How did the Apiarian Phantom become separated from the Wild Hunt?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Apiarian Phantom, a Hound of the Wild Hunt, strayed from its pack during a raid. It wandered to Honeyfill Meadworks, where it became notorious for attacking the Meiersdorf family's hives. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Why did the Apiarian Phantom target beehives at Honeyfill Meadworks?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Apiarian Phantom attacked Honeyfill Meadworks, a bee farm owned by the Meiersdorf family, due to its tendency to destroy apiaries. In 1272, it damaged many beehives and killed farmhands, leading the family to request assistance from bounty hunters and witchers. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What impact did the Apiarian Phantom have on Honeyfill Meadworks?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Apiarian Phantom devastated Honeyfill Meadworks in 1272 by destroying beehives and killing farmhands. This creature's actions disrupted the operations of the Meiersdorf family's apiary, which had been a livelihood source for generations. The chaos prompted a call for help from bounty hunters and witchers to eliminate the threat. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What are the characteristics of a Hound of the Wild Hunt like the Apiarian Phantom?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Apiarian Phantom is a Hound of the Wild Hunt, an Elementa class monster linked to the Wild Hunt riders. These hounds have thick ice armor, vulnerable to strong blows and fire, especially the Igni Sign. The Apiarian Phantom, separated from its pack, was found near Honeyfill Meadworks. It is susceptible to Dimeritium bombs, Elementa oil, and the Axii Sign. Defeating it yields monster bones, brains, saliva, rotting flesh, sulfur, and a Wild Hunt hound trophy. 

Provided by: Fandom 

## Bestiary entry

    _Something's destroyed our fields and hives. We think it's... the apiarian phantom.  
[Holofernes Meiersdorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holofernes_Meiersdorf "Holofernes Meiersdorf")_
    _The "Apiarian Phantom" destroying the Meiersdorf family's hives turned out to be a lost Hound of the Wild Hunt. When fighting such a beast, one must bear in mind three things. First of all, that the thick armor of ice covering it can only be pierced by strong blows. Second, that, like any other frost-born creature, it is vulnerable to fire, the Igni Sign included. Third and last: that it shall show no mercy._
    _Though the Hound fought fiercely, it nevertheless fell to the witcher's blade. By killing this dangerous monster, Geralt saved the humans, halflings and, last but not least, bees of the area from further torment._
