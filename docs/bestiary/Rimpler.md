**Rimpler**(Polish: _Gnieciuch_), are [rat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rat "Rat")-like monsters that were supposed to appear in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ during its development in 2013 - 2014, but they were removed at some point prior to release.[1] In Slavic mythology, a _Gnieciuch_ is a creature that strangles people in their sleep.

### Information from leaked materials

    _Creature resembling hybrid of a[cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(creature\) "Cat \(creature\)") with a [rat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rat "Rat"). During the night, Gnieciuch sits on a drunk's chest and suffocate him, sometimes to death. The creature is very fast and agile, that's why nobody's, even with a great reflex, can catch it._

  * _Dies after single hit._
  * _Doesn't fight - tries to run away._
