### Occurrence

Basements, cellars, sewers and cargo holds.

**Rats** are medium-sized, long-tailed rodents living in inhabited areas such as [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"). Before the domestication of [cats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_\(creature\) "Cat \(creature\)"), rats had been a great problem to [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") societies, most often solved by hiring a rat-catcher playing on a musical instrument.[1]

Large, savage rats living in the wilderness are called skrekks. Mutated forms of the species are [pseudorats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pseudorat "Pseudorat"),[2] and the cursed people who are able to take the form of half-human, half-rat are [wererats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wererat "Wererat").[3][4]

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

When Geralt run into [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") in Novigrad she has a contract to get rid of rats in warehouse of some merchant. Then with [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") he has to destroy the rat holes with either bombs or Igni spell to receive 100[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") for each destroyed nest. 

Depending on whether you set option "enemy upscaling" off or on, they can be the weakest or the strongest enemies in the game.

## Bugs

  * Rats will sometimes grunt with a female voice when bumped into by the player. This can be noticed during the quest [A Towerful of Mice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Towerful_of_Mice "A Towerful of Mice").

## Notes

  * Keira Metz is terrified of rats, despite being able to easily do away with them.
  * Like all other enemies in the game, rats can be affected by enemy upscaling. This normally isn't a problem until you have to face several rats all at once, creating an almost impossible fight. To fix this, simply turn off enemy upscaling.
