The **goat** is an animal found in the [Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") universe. In _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , they can be found in the [Kaer Morhen valley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen_valley "Kaer Morhen valley"), [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), the [Skellige Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

In _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ they appear in both wild and domesticated version, fenced or wandering around, e.g. in [Yantra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yantra "Yantra"). Their behaviour is different from the [cows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cow "Cow")' and [pigs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pig "Pig")' one as they escape from Geralt even if not attacked. 

Loot is a [Goat Milk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"), [Raw Meat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Raw_Meat "Raw Meat") and [Goat Hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk"), not depending on being wild or domesticated.
