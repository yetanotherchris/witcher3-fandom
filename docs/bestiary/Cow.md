### Eye color

**Cows** can be found in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and other locations. On fields in [Martin Feuille's Farmstead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Martin_Feuille%27s_Farmstead "Martin Feuille's Farmstead") a group of 16 cows can be found, which is probably the greatest number among all _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ pastures. 

When killed it drops up to 4 items together: [Raw Meat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_food_and_drink "The Witcher 3 food and drink"), Cow Milk and Cow Hide. Some corpses cannot be looted. Cow hide can be sold for up to 42 crowns. Best price is paid by innkeepers, also in [brothels](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brothel "Brothel"), e.g. [Passiflora](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Passiflora "Passiflora"). 

Sometimes immortal cows can be encountered, always in a shelter and with buckets, as they were ready to be milked. They serve as decoration, eg. in Martin Feuille's Farmstead or White Orchard. 

### Quick Answers

![fandom logo](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/8941553e-df6a-4310-a080-b9da56fdcbdd)

**Where is the largest cow farm in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

Martin Feuille's Farmstead in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is the largest cow farm, with 16 cows, the most in any pasture. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**How can you farm money using cows in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, you can farm money by killing cows, which drop Raw Meat, Cow Milk, and Cow Hide. Cow Hide is particularly valuable, selling for up to 42 crowns, especially to innkeepers and brothels like Passiflora. Cows are found in locations such as White Orchard and Martin Feuille's Farmstead, where groups of up to 16 can be encountered. This method was widely used for quick money farming before being patched. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Where can you sell cow hide for the best price in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In Witcher 3, cow hide can be sold for up to 42 crowns. Innkeepers, such as those in Passiflora, offer the best prices. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**What items do cows drop in Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, cows drop Raw Meat, Cow Milk, and Cow Hide when killed. Cow Hide can be sold for up to 42 crowns, with the best prices at innkeepers and brothels like Passiflora. Cows are found in fields, including a large group of 16 at Martin Feuille's Farmstead. 

Provided by: Fandom 

**Why are some cows in Witcher 3 immortal?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

In The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, certain cows are immortal and act as decorative elements in areas like Martin Feuille's Farmstead and White Orchard. These cows are typically found in shelters with buckets, indicating they are prepared for milking. Unlike regular cows, which drop items like raw meat, cow milk, and cow hide, these immortal cows do not provide loot when killed. 

Provided by: Fandom
