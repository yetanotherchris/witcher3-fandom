## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lurtch** is a village located in central [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") that is potentially overrun with [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") and [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul") but once [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") liberates this location from necrophages, folks will come back and he will receive 50[![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") and 120[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). 

Note that, with the free DLC quest, [Fools' Gold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fools%27_Gold "Fools' Gold"), this place is no longer overrun with ghouls but instead pigs and Geralt can learn why. 

## Map description

    _Once the Evves family estate was located here and the area bore the name of Lord Evves' wife, Mortilanca. When the couple died, their will stipulated their land be turned over to their serfs. The grateful peasants then founded a village of freeholders and named it after their first ealdorman, Lurtch, who had previously served as the Evves family's butler._
