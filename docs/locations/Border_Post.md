## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Border Post** is a small fortified camp over the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)") manned by [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldiers, who are located on the frontier separating [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). As it is a checkpoint before entering the free city, [papers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_of_safe_conduct_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Letter of safe conduct \(The Witcher 3\)") are required to pass through.
