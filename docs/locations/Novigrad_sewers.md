The **Novigrad sewers** are located underneath the streets of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") between [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and the [Oxenfurt Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Gate "Oxenfurt Gate"). [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") had to venture down there a couple of times, such as the time when he was wandering the sewers, he came across Professor [Vairmont Jonne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vairmont_Jonne "Vairmont Jonne") trying to open a stone sarcophagus. Geralt mistook him as a grave robber until he introduced himself. Geralt helped him open the sarcophagus and they ran into a rather irritable [katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan"). Apart from this Geralt found nothing else of mention in the Novigrad sewers.
