### Games

**The Nowhere Inn** is a lower class inn located in [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") district in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

## Map description

    _An inn much like any other, Nowhere is a favorite among local... "entrepreneurs," as the staff asks no questions and you are free to go about your business unmolested._
