## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Fireburst Harbor** , also known as **Lovers' Landing** , is a [harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harbor "Harbor") located on the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)")'s coast far south from [Devil's Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devil%27s_Pit "Devil's Pit") and north of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). Initially overrun by [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowned_dead "Drowned dead") and [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"), if the monsters are exterminated, 90[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") \+ 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") will be awarded and the location will be taken over by the [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") and some peasants as their workers. 

## Map description

    _Once a favorite midnight haunt for young lovers, this site changed when drowners infested the area. Now the amorous must couple about stables and barns, while only the name of this station remains to remind them of those happy times when they did so beneath an open sky._
