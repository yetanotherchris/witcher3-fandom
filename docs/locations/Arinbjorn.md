## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Arinbjorn** is a village on the western side of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). The village of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund") can be found to the east. 

Most notably, here one can acquire the cards: [Crone: Whispess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crone:_Whispess_\(gwent_card\) "Crone: Whispess \(gwent card\)"), [Arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas_\(gwent_card\) "Arachas \(gwent card\)"), [Fiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend_\(gwent_card\) "Fiend \(gwent card\)"), and [Thaler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thaler_\(gwent_card\) "Thaler \(gwent card\)"). These cards are part of the [Collect 'Em All](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Collect_%27Em_All "Collect 'Em All") quest and can only be found here. You can acquire these cards by buying them from the [innkeeper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(Arinbjorn\) "Innkeep \(Arinbjorn\)") of Arinbjorn's [tavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tavern_\(Arinbjorn\) "Tavern \(Arinbjorn\)").
