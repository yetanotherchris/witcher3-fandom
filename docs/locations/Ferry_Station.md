## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ferry Station** is a [harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harbor "Harbor") located in eastern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). Once the monsters who have overran it are driven away, [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") will take control of it to gain access to supplies coming from [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp"). 

## Map description

    _The ferry's former owners were famed for treating travelers who were forced to wait for better conditions to raucous and unforgettable evenings._

## Trivia

  * Once the area is liberated, two named characters will appear here but with no interactions: Edith and Bayor. They're most likely a reference to two minor characters in the Russian novels, Volkodava (English: _Wolfhound_) series by [Maria Semyonova](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maria_Semyonova).

## Notes

  * While you can kill off the [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") here, it won't officially liberate the area until you clear out the nearby [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site"), [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower"), as only the watchtower is officially recognized as an abandoned site.
  * If you want to loot the containers outside of the hut (2 of them have [random diagrams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), but most of the corpses laying around are also lootable, though not glowing with [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses")), it's best to do it before clearing the abandoned site, as the soldiers will attack you for stealing after they return.
  * [Orders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orders#Orders_\(Ferry_Station\) "Orders") that were supposed to be found on a soldier's corpse in the area provide some additional backstory, but don't appear during gameplay.
