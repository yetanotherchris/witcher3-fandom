## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Margrave Henckel's house** is a house situated west of [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and belonged to a rich nobleman in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"): Margrave [Henckel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Henckel "Henckel"). After the margrave's death, this house was used by [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") to plan the robbery of [Count Reuven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra")'s treasure.
