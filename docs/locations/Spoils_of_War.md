# Spoils of War

[![Tw3 icon spoils of war](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7b/Tw3_icon_spoils_of_war.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/50?cb=20170901053626)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7b/Tw3_icon_spoils_of_war.png/revision/latest?cb=20170901053626)

There are a number of **spoils of war** locations to be found in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard")

**1 location**

  * East of the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison")

## [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(region\) "Pontar \(region\)") ([Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"))

**3 locations**

  * North of [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale") (contains [Moonblade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moonblade "Moonblade"))
  * Southwest of [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten")
  * West of [Byways](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Byways "Byways"), south of [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle")

## [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")

**8 locations**

  * West of [The Pali Gap Coast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pali_Gap_Coast "The Pali Gap Coast") (x2)
  * North of the [Bridge to Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bridge_to_Kaer_Trolde "Bridge to Kaer Trolde")
  * Far east of [Hov](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hov "Hov") (first small island)
  * Far west of [Yngvar's Fang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yngvar%27s_Fang "Yngvar's Fang")
  * East of [Elverum Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elverum_Lighthouse "Elverum Lighthouse"), south of [Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grotto "Grotto")
  * Very far east of [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken"), far south of [Holmstein's Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein%27s_Port "Holmstein's Port")
  * Northeast of [Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grotto "Grotto")
