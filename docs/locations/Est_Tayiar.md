## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Est Tayiar** is a location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and located east-northeast of [Temerian Partisan Hideout](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Partisan_Hideout "Temerian Partisan Hideout"). 

The powerful [Sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorceress "Sorceress") [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart") hid herself here for a time from [King Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"). 

## Map description

    _Long before men first peopled these lands, a beautiful, prospering[elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") city stood here, centered around the palace of King Maeglor. One day, however, the city's inhabitants began mysteriously dying off in large numbers. According to legend, King [Maeglor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Maeglor "Maeglor") sensed he, too, would soon perish and cast a powerful spell that caused the earth to swallow the city whole so that no outsider could ever desecrate it. Centuries later, scholars from the [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy") began painstaking excavations of King Maeglor's palace in a search for the causes of the catastrophe. Yet work came to a sudden halt when when three subsequent expeditions ventured into the ruins' depth - and were never heard from again..._
