## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Udalryk's old family home** was the old seat of [Clan Brokvar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Brokvar "Clan Brokvar") and once the home of [Udalryk's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Udalryk "Udalryk") family, including his belated brother, [Aki](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aki "Aki"). Since the house was said to be haunted, Jarl Udalryk moved his seat closer to [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag") village.
