## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Yantra** is a village located to the northeast of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Its inhabitants are currently having trouble with a local group of bandit led by a [werewolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf"). 

Yantra is a great village for animal exploit. The village is inhabited by 8 [cows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cow "Cow"), at least 6 [goats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Goat "Goat"), and two bird flocks, [chicken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chicken "Chicken") and [goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Goose "Goose") respectively.

## Trivia

  * On the northeastern outskirts of the village is an easel which is facing a field of haystacks. This may be a reference to [Claude Monet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Claude_Monet), the famous impressionist painter who devoted an entire series to [Haystacks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Haystacks_\(Monet_series\)).
  * The name of the village may be a reference to [Yantar](https://stalker.fandom.com/wiki/Yantar "w:c:stalker:Yantar"), a territory in [S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl](https://stalker.fandom.com/wiki/S.T.A.L.K.E.R.:_Shadow_of_Chernobyl "w:c:stalker:S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl").
