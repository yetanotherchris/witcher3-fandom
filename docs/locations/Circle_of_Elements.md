# Circle of Elements

[![Tutorial elemental circles](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3d/Tutorial_elemental_circles.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/270?cb=20170504224344)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3d/Tutorial_elemental_circles.png/revision/latest?cb=20170504224344) [![Tutorial elemental circles](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3d/Tutorial_elemental_circles.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/270?cb=20170504224344)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3d/Tutorial_elemental_circles.png/revision/latest?cb=20170504224344) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tutorial_elemental_circles.png)

A **Circle of Elements** or **Elemental Circle** (not to be confused with a [Place of Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Place_of_Power "Place of Power")) is a place where [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") is strong, enough for people to have erected monuments. 

[Mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") can draw power from Circles of Elements, and witchers can learn new [Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signs "Signs"), as well as getting a momentary boost for the spells they already know. These are likely manifestations of [intersections](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Intersection "Intersection").

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 circle of elements](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/44/Tw3_circle_of_elements.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/280?cb=20160331163823)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/44/Tw3_circle_of_elements.png/revision/latest?cb=20160331163823) [![Tw3 circle of elements](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/44/Tw3_circle_of_elements.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/280?cb=20160331163823)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/44/Tw3_circle_of_elements.png/revision/latest?cb=20160331163823) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw3_circle_of_elements.png)

During quest [The Final Trial](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Final_Trial "The Final Trial") happening near [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") after more witty banter, and heading south up the rough trail to the ancient **Circle of Elements** , you reach your destination. 

Soak in the view, then listen and follow [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert")'s instructions: Move to each of the unlit torches, and use [Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni") to quickly light them. Then place the phylactery on the smooth stone altar. You wait, and Lambert asks how you dealt with trolls during your own trial. Answer how you wish.
