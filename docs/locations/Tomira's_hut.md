## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Tomira's hut** is where the local herbalist, [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira"), resides in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

The hut is equipped with a little garden where Tomira grows herbs for various medicines and ointments. 

_If Geralt seeks out Tomira:_ In May [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") met Tomira here, who helped him find [Buckthorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Buckthorn "Buckthorn"), a particular herb that the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") needed to help get rid of a [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") terrifying the village. 

_If Radovid wins the war:_ [Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") hunted down all the [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage"), herbalists, and alchemists and killed them. This resulted in Tomira's hut being burned down.
