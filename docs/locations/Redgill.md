## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Redgill** is an abandoned village placed on the eastern shores of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), located due east of the [Druids' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_Camp "Druids' Camp"). 

## Map description

    _This village's inhabitants fled in a panic when a mysterious[magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") cataclysm struck the surrounding area._
