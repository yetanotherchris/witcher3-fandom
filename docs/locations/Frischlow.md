## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Frischlow** is a small town in southwestern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). The village consists of 5 buildings. 

Slighly south of the settlement there is a place of haunting. Reading the [note](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Soaked_letter "Soaked letter") from the dead body reveals that the miner, seen with the aid of [Keira's lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_lamp_\(quest_item\) "Magic lamp \(quest item\)"), is digging for the valuables contained within a burial champer. Using [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard") on the rocks he's digging at will reveal two chests, and looting them both will both lead to the place of haunting icon disappearing and yield a [griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_armor "Griffin armor") diagram.
