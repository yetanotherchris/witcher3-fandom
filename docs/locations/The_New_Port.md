## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**The New Port** is a tavern at [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is run by [Jonas the Innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jonas_the_Innkeep "Jonas the Innkeep"), who used to own "[The Old Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Old_Port "The Old Port")" on [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"), before the [Ice Giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant") made the inhabitants flee the island. It is also where [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") stays while in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige").
