## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hemdall's Finger** is what the locals[1] call the unmarked small isle east of [Redgill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redgill "Redgill"), [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

Sometime shortly before or during [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), it became the site of a shipwreck for the Amuanda - one of two ships carrying a royal family from an unknown, faraway island nation, who were fleeing towards [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair") after being dethroned in a coup.[2]
