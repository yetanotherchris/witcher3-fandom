## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**The Mire** is the southernmost region of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

The northern area is heavily forested, with some inhabited villages. Most of the region centers around [Lake Wyndamer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lake_Wyndamer "Lake Wyndamer"), in which lies [Fyke Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyke_Isle "Fyke Isle") in the middle of the lake. The southern part of the region is mostly uninhabited, consisting of bogs, ruined villages, and monsters.
