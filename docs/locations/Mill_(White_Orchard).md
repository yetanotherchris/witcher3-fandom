## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **Mill** , located in northern [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), is where the [royal griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Griffin "Royal Griffin") that was terrorizing the region was killed by [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"). 

After its demise, a large group of bandits made it their home, where they stashed their newly acquired loot.
