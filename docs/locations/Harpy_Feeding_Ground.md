## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Harpy Feeding Ground** is a set of cliffs inhabited by a large flock of [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy"). It is located in the north of [Heatherton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heatherton "Heatherton") and in the southeast of [Lornruk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lornruk "Lornruk").
