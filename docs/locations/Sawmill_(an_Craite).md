## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **sawmill** is an [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), in [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite")'s territory northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig"). 

It is initially overrun by [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") and [mucknixers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mucknixer "Mucknixer"), with a 120[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") reward for clearing it. Upon its liberation, local an Craite [Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") will repopulate the settlement along with a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)"), and a [wandering merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") will also start walking the road through the sawmill, between Rannvaig and [Rogne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rogne "Rogne"). 

## Bugs

  * Before the site is liberated, there are blood splatters on the west end that, when examined, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") says _"Drowners are coming out of there, best blow it up"_ , as if they are [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest").
