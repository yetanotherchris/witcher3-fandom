## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Larvik** is one of the three villages located on the island of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall").[1] One of the older settlements in the [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") Isles,[2] it is the home base of [Clan Heymaey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Heymaey "Clan Heymaey").

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

Larvik is led by its current jarl, Donar an Hindar. A [tavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tavern_\(Larvik\) "Tavern \(Larvik\)"), an [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Larvik\) "Armorer \(Larvik\)"), a [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Larvik\) "Blacksmith \(Larvik\)"), and a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Larvik\) "Merchant \(Larvik\)") can be found there. 

### Map description

    _The largest village on Hindarsfjall and home to Donar an Hindar, head of clan an Hindar. Its inhabitants are just and god-fearing folk traditionalists strident in their devotion to Modron[Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya")._
