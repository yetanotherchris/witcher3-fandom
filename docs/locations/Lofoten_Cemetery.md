## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lofoten Cemetery** is a graveyard found nearby the village of [Lofoten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lofoten "Lofoten"). 

It is here that [Skjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skjall "Skjall") was buried, much to the displeasure of the locals. 

## Map description

    _The inhabitants of Lofoten often visit this small cemetery to care for the graves of their loved ones and ask[Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") for blessings in the afterlife._
