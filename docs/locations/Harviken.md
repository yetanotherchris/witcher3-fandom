## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Harviken** is the only friendly village located on the small island of [Faroe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe "Faroe"). A small [tavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tavern_\(Harviken\) "Tavern \(Harviken\)") is situated here near the hut of Jarl [Holger Blackhand](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holger_Blackhand "Holger Blackhand"). This home seat of [Clan Dimun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Dimun "Clan Dimun") is notorious for a day long feast that is thrown in the warriors' honor after they return from raiding and divide their plunder.[1]

## Map description

    _Home seat to Clan Dimun, and the location where Holger Blackhand divvies out the loot after every successful raid._
