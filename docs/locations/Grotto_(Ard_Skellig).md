## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Grotto** is located on the southeastern facade of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), down the base of the mountain from the [Distillery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Distillery "Distillery"), and directly east of the [Ruined Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_Inn "Ruined Inn"). It has a cave entrance, which is best accessed via sea. 

The place is also the home of the [Grossbart Brothers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grossbart_Brothers "Grossbart Brothers") and the [Enhanced Ursine armor diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Enhanced_Ursine_armor "Diagram: Enhanced Ursine armor").
