## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Vulpine Woods** is a large forest in southwest [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soldiers made an ill-attempt to kill a [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") and destroy its nest. However, this resulted in the griffin's mate going crazy and start attacking everyone in the nearby vicinity, making the problem worse.
