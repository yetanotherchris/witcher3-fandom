## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

_**Flying Stag**_ was once a ship from [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") used by Jacob of Dembich. However, when [Kiyan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kiyan "Kiyan") took a job on it, a [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") that was seeking to capture the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") caused the ship to crash to try and cover his tracks as he teleported himself and Kiyan to his laboratory.
