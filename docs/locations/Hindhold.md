## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hindhold** is a ruined watchtower located on the frontier separating [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). It is now inhabited by [endrega workers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_worker "Endrega worker") and [warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_warrior "Endrega warrior") inside while a large flock of [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") have made their [nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") atop the tower. 

## Map description

    _This watchtower used to protect barges travelling between[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). It once even boasted a bridge connecting the two sides of the [river](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)"), but now it stands abandoned and neglected, its bridge a collapsed ruin._
