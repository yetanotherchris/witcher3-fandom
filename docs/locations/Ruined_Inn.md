## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **ruined inn** , situated on the southern shore of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), is initially overrun by [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren"), an [ekhidna](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna "Ekhidna"), and [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"). 

It'll give 170 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") for liberating this area from the monsters. Note that the [monster nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") has to be destroyed before the site is considered liberated.

## Bugs

  * Sometimes a siren will fly too far away, making it impossible to kill it and thus won't allow inhabitants to return here. This can be solved by using an earlier save prior to finding the area and try to kill them all before one runs away. If on PC, one can use debug mode to enter addfact(sk75_sirens_dead) to kill them all that way.
  * After the liberation and some time, villagers will be gone and the burned inn will be haunted by wraiths again.
