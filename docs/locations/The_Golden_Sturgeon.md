## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**The Golden Sturgeon** is one of many taverns within [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Its [Harborside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harborside "Harborside") location makes the roadhouse a popular location for locals and travelers alike. Frequented by sailors and dockhands, this rowdy and expansive tavern has a reputation for violent behavior, where fistfights and the battle-hardened go to quench a thirst for mead and sometimes bloodletting.
