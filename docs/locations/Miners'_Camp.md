## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Miners' Camp** , located in the northern mountains of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), was used by miners from the village of [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare") who went mining in the area. 

## Map description

    _Small camp which local miners use as a base during their gold prospecting expeditions into the mountains to the north._
