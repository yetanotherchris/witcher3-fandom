The **Oxenfurt Morgue** is the impromptu mortuary in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") on the same riverbank as [harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Harbor "Oxenfurt Harbor") during _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It was established by Commander [Nikolas Friedman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nikolas_Friedman "Nikolas Friedman") when [Gael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gael "Gael"), the [katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan"), started his killing of drunken low class members. The sole operator is a unnamed [medic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Medic_\(Oxenfurt_Morgue\) "Medic \(Oxenfurt Morgue\)") in blue tunic.
