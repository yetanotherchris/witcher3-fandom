## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Arette** is a village located just outside the city of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Most of its inhabitants are [nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman"). 

While after [Following the Thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Following_the_Thread_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Following the Thread \(The Witcher 3\)") quest events, the village is taken by the Redanian army and its nonhuman inhabitants taken away. Later, when and if Redanian forces take control of areas around [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") (e.g. a graveyard around the [Crematory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crematory "Crematory") and [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners")), the willage is abandoned and only one lazy Redanian guard stays there, but a few steps to the Nord from the willage, you can find a burnout pyre and a few corpses... 

## Map description

_Novigrad has always attracted those in search of a better life. Some of them found no welcome within the city walls, and so built huts outside the city._
