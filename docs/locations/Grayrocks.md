## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Grayrocks** is a region in northeastern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), bordered by [Mudplough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mudplough "Mudplough") to the west, [Grassy Knoll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grassy_Knoll "Grassy Knoll") to the north, and [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") to the east.
