## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lindenvale** is a village in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), located west of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch"), that sports a [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Blacksmith") and two [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") in town. 

## Map description

    _One of[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")'s many impoverished villages, its poverty deepened by [war](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III") levies and the epidemic that spread after the [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire")' arrival._
