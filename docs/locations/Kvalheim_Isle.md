## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Kvalheim Isle** is a herbalist hut in central [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Initially, it's overrun by bandits who've taken the [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Kvalheim_Isle\) "Herbalist \(Kvalheim Isle\)") hostage but after they're killed off, he'll return to his work. The place will also become populated once more with druids and locals.
