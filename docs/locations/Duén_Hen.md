## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Duén Hen** is a [location](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_locations "The Witcher 3 locations") in western [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

The location features a fast-travel signpost, as well as a toppled, wooden statue used for prayer. If the shrine is restored as part of [Defender of the Faith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Defender_of_the_Faith "Defender of the Faith"), a group of worshippers will gather here afterwards, despite the dangers posed by [mucknixers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mucknixer "Mucknixer") and [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") that continually prowl nearby.
