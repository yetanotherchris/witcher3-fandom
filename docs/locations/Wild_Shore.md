## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Wild Shore** is a coastal area located south of [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn"). It has a nearby [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") guarded by a [cyclops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops"), which always contains a random relic item. 

## Map description

    _A wild and untamed part of the isle's coastline. A favorite spot for bandits and lovers._
