## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Giants' Toes** are a series of tall rock formations found on the shores of northeastern [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

A local group of pirates have set up camp here. 

## Map description

    _In truth simply a normal rock formation shaped by centuries of wind and water, islanders believe Uroboros punished giants who opposed his will by turning them into stones._
