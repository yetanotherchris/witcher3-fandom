## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Kaer Muire** ([Elder Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Speech "Elder Speech"): _Sea Fortress_) is a fortress nestled in the southern cliffs of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). It is the home base of the [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") and its leader, [Madman Lugos](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Madman_Lugos "Madman Lugos"). 

## Map description

    _This fortress has served Clan Drummond's home base for centuries. The days when it was new and in full repair are a distant memory now._

## Trivia

  * In the real world, Muire is a Gaelic variation of the name Mary.
  * Kaer or Kêr means "city" in [Breton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Breton_language), while Ker means "city" or "fortress" in [Cornish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cornish_language). Caer means the same in [Welsh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Welsh_language).
