## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Undvik Boatyards** is a [harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harbor "Harbor") located on the central north shores of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It doesn't have its own signpost, but it can be [fast travelled](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") to while sailing on a boat. 

The boatyards were thriving right up until disaster struck the island, forcing its inhabitants to flee, after which [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") infested Undvik's shores. Shortly after, an expedition led by [Hjalmar an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar_an_Craite "Hjalmar an Craite") aiming to slay the [ice giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant") [Myrhyff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myrhyff "Myrhyff") landed here. 

## Map description

    _The Undvik boatyards were once famed for their longships, which, while neither particularly swift nor yare, were extremely durable. Cautious[merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") from every corner of the [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/World "World") would order their wares be hauled in vessels made on Undvik._
