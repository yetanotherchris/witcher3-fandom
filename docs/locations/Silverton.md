## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Silverton** is a [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") district encompassing the northwestern part of the main city. It is located south of [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle"), with [Harborside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harborside "Harborside") to the southwest and [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") to the northeast. 

Most of this district is made up of small market stalls as well as residential areas. While the district is poor, it's not nearly as crowded as [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits").
