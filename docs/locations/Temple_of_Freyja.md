**Temple of Freyja** is located on the island of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"), in the grove known as [Hindar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindar "Hindar"), in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). The high priestess there is called Modron [Sigrdrifa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigrdrifa "Sigrdrifa"). 

Inside, a towering statue of Modron [Freyja](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freyja "Freyja"), the Great Mother, dominated the view, and in her arms, the diamond, [Brisingamen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brisingamen "Brisingamen"). 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 Temple of Freya](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7a/Tw3_Temple_of_Freya.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/280?cb=20160824081253)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7a/Tw3_Temple_of_Freya.png/revision/latest?cb=20160824081253) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw3_Temple_of_Freya.png)

In third game **Freya's Temple** is located closely to [Freya's Garden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya%27s_Garden "Freya's Garden"), a few priestesses and worshipers are located here but the interior is not available due to concerns about the possible theft of the famous diamond, [Brisingamen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brisingamen "Brisingamen"), ornamenting the goddess' statue.[1] Its also said that it was [Otkell](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otkell "Otkell"), legendary forefather of both Skellige and [Clan Heymaey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Heymaey "Clan Heymaey"), who erected a beautiful temple for her help.[2]
