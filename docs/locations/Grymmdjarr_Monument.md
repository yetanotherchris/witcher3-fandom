## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Grymmdjarr Monument** is a large inukshuk on the isle of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") consecrated to half-godlike [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") hero known as [Grymmdjarr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grymmdjarr "Grymmdjarr"). 

This is also the location of the small side quest [Brave Fools Die Young](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brave_Fools_Die_Young "Brave Fools Die Young"), where you can choose to help out [Petter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Petter "Petter") or not. Afterwards the monument will have a group of warriors around it.
