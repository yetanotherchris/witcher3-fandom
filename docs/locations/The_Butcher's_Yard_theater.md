**Butcher's Yard theater** is a theater located in the Butcher's Yard of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), just off [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). Having been a diversion for the city's residents for decades, it now hosts a mummers' troupe led by [Irina Renarde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Irina_Renarde "Irina Renarde"). 

## Description

It is an open-air theater featuring rows of wooden benches in front of a stage, behind which are the troupe's accommodations. The northern exit leads to a street that joins with Hierarch Square, while the southern leads to the canal. 

At the northern entrance, a ticket seller controls entry to the theater by way of ticketing visitors, each of whom must pay 50[![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") to be granted entry while a rehearsal or play is in action. 

## Notes

  * Prior to the main quest [The Play's the Thing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Play%27s_the_Thing "The Play's the Thing"), both entrances are unlocked.
  * During the quest, both entrances are locked until [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") takes the script to Irina.
  * While the doors are locked, it is possible to gain entry without buying a ticket by climbing the rooftops east of the southern entrance.
  * Although depicted as a thriving venue during "The Play's the Thing", by the time of [Reason of State](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reason_of_State "Reason of State"), it is described as abandoned.
