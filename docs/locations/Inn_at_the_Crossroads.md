## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Inn at the Crossroads** is a popular meeting place in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). The [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s men often come here to shake down the villagers and get their part of the profit from the [Innkeep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(Inn_at_the_Crossroads\) "Innkeep \(Inn at the Crossroads\)"). 

## Map description

    _A sizeable establishment able to accommodate a crowd of travelers and revelers._

## Trivia

  * This location is likely a reference to George R. R. Martin's _A Song of Ice and Fire_ series, where a lot of important events take place in an inn at the crossroads.
