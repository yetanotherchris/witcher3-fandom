## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **tavern** in [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale"), a village located in the region of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), is not specifically given a name. It is the most prominent building in the village and there are two [innkeeps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Innkeep_\(Lindenvale\) "Innkeep \(Lindenvale\)"), one appearing during night hours and the second during the day. 

There is also a bunch of [Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s "soldiers" most likely not to guard but to find troublemakers (or make them).
