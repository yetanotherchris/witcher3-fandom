## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Isolated Hut** is located on the eastern shores of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") and is a popular spot for fishermen all across the island. It was recently abandoned after a pack of [white wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)") moved in. 

Once the zone has been cleaned, 170[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") / 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") is awarded and a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") will appear. 

## Map description

    _They say this was once home to a herbalist who came to the isles from the continent. Unable to find a place in any of the nearby villages, she settled in this seaside hut, where she received the occasional visitor in need of magic creams or bandages._
