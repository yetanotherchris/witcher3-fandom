## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **monster den** is located in a cave south of [Trottheim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trottheim "Trottheim") on [Faroe Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle"). It extends from its entrance on the southern shore of the island all the way to the village itself. 

Stashed inside are all mastercrafted [Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") diagrams, and it also contains a rotating statues puzzle that opens the doors to an isolated room, inside of which is the designated [monster den](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_den "Monster den") treasure chest, behind a breakable wall. 

Strangely for a monster den, there are no enemies inside it, but this the result of [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") where a [nightwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith") would prowl near the two front statues, while the puzzle also involved a mirror placed opposite of the breakable wall (the spot is quite prominent, just empty now). [Aether](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aether "Aether") and the [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formula:_Aether "Formula: Aether") for it were supposed to be part of the reward for solving the puzzle.

## Notes

  * To solve the puzzle, the two front statues must face each other, while the ones by the doors must be facing away from them. Each statue will glow in yellow (instead of red) in its correct orientation when using [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses").
