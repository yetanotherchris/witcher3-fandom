## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Percival Schuttenbach's Shop** in the [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") is closed by [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). A note outside it reads: 

    _As a result of an incident of a racial nature,  
[Percival Schuttenbach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Percival_Schuttenbach "Percival Schuttenbach") has moved his shop to [Mahakam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mahakam "Mahakam").  
We invite all our loyal customers to visit us on [Mount Carbon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mount_Carbon "Mount Carbon")._
