## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hattori's home** is a location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's a two floor building belonging to the [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") swordsmith [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori"), located in the [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), west of [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove"). 

Originally, it's come across as a home/restaurant, where outside the home are tables with food and customers. However, if [Of Swords and Dumplings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Swords_and_Dumplings "Of Swords and Dumplings") is completed, it'll transform into that of a forge and Hattori will regularly work out here.
