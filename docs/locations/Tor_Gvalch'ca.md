## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Tor Gvalch'ca** ([Elder Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Speech "Elder Speech"): _Tower of the (she-)Falcon_[2] or _[Falka](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Falka "Falka")'s tower_[1]) is a tall [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") tower located on the northern peak of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"). 

After the defeat of the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt"), [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") went into the tower to stop the incoming threat of the [White Frost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Frost "White Frost"). 

## Map description

    _An ancient tower which was erected in the days when[Elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") were the unchallenged masters of these lands._
