## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Boxholm** is a destroyed village situated next to a [river](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rivers "Rivers") that sits on the western base of a mountain located north of [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal") and southeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig") and [Fayrlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fayrlund "Fayrlund") in central [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") of the [Skellige Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_Islands "Skellige Islands"). 

It is the site of a hidden treasure in the middle of the ruined village from the treasure hunt quest, [Inheritance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Inheritance "Inheritance"), and the [mastercrafted Griffin steel sword diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Griffin_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Diagram: Griffin steel sword - mastercrafted") in the ruined castle ruins to the east of the village from the scavenger hunt quest, [Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear"). 

The powerful [Fiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend"), [Morvudd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvudd "Morvudd"), has established itself in the nearby ruins of [Kaer Nyssen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Nyssen "Kaer Nyssen"), attacking everybody who wanders too close. 

## Map description

    _Boxholm was once a thriving village serving the nearby fortress, Kaer Nyssen. Today nothing remains of this past glory but a pile of stones, some debris, and fading memories._
