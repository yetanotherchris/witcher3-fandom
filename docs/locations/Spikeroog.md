## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Spikeroog** is one of the larger islands of the [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") archipelago. It lies to the west across the [Allenker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Allenker "Allenker") sound from the main islands of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") and [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"). There are two villages on the island: [Svorlag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svorlag "Svorlag") and [Hov](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hov "Hov").
