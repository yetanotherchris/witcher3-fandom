## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Alness** is a village located northeast of [Wheat Fields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wheat_Fields "Wheat Fields") and south-southeast of [Dancing Windmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dancing_Windmill "Dancing Windmill"). 

## Map description

    _Until recently, this was a thoroughly unremarkable village, then the[Vegelbuds](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family") began organizing their famous [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") races here, granting Alness the enviable honor of hosting the [region](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gustfields "Gustfields")'s most prestigious equestrian contests._
