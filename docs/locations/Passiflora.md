**Passiflora** is considered the finest brothel in all of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), which is significant as there are a total of twelve within the city. The owner is the so-called [Marquise Serenity](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marquise_Serenity "Marquise Serenity"). The Passiflora houses around 15–25 [prostitutes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prostitute "Prostitute"), but the most known and noteworthy are [Narcissa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Narcissa "Narcissa"), [Amrynn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amrynn "Amrynn") and [Viola](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viola "Viola"). 

It is a favorite venue for conspirators, as [Falka's rebellion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Falka%27s_rebellion "Falka's rebellion"), the murder of King [Vridank](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vridank "Vridank") and the murder of King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_the_Stern "Radovid the Stern") were planned here. It is also favored by traders. The brothel is located near [St. Gregory's Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/St._Gregory%27s_Bridge "St. Gregory's Bridge") to [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") in the northernmost quarter of Novigrad, known as [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf").
