## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Keira Metz's hut** is [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz")'s home and is located northeast of [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). 

The building itself has only 2 rooms: a bedroom and a kitchen/living area, and a small basement. However, Keira created a portal in her bedroom to go to a more secluded area to bathe. 

A [Recipe for black eyeliner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Recipe_for_black_eyeliner "Recipe for black eyeliner") can be looted from the table directly opposite to the door. [Letter to Alexander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_to_Alexander "Letter to Alexander") can be looted off her bed.
