## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Scoia'tael Trading Camp** is a small settlement in the [Hotsch Woods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hotsch_Woods "Hotsch Woods") south-west of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), used by the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") for regroupment and trade. 

_If Geralt beat Dijkstra at gwent:_ the [gwent merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael\)") at the camp was visited by [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), who challenged him for the [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cirilla_Fiona_Elen_Riannon_\(gwent_card\) "Cirilla Fiona Elen Riannon \(gwent card\)") card.
