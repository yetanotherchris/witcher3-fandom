## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Fornhala** is an abandoned village located in the middle of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). Its inhabitants fled the area for unknown reasons, although it is widely known that the locals were worshiping the banished god [Svalblod](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Svalblod "Svalblod"). The [Vildkaarls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Berserker_\(creature\) "Berserker \(creature\)") are said to roam here. 

There is a large cave in this village, the entrance to which is in the southeast house. Presumably, the cave was once used for rituals.
