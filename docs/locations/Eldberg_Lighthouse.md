## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Eldberg Lighthouse** is the northernmost lighthouse on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

It is manned by a [Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") named [Mikkjal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mikkjal "Mikkjal"), who due to his horrible deeds, attracted a [Penitent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Penitent "Penitent"), a rare and powerful [wraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"). 

## Map description

    _Lighthouse built on orders of Jarl[Skjordal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skjordal_Drummond "Skjordal Drummond") in order to light the sea and route to [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn")._
