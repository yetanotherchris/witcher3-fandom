## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Yustianna's Grotto** is a cave found in northern [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

There are [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") ruins inside and many valuable things to loot from a good number of treasure chests. A hidden passage can be revealed by using [the Eye of Nehaleni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Eye_of_Nehaleni "The Eye of Nehaleni"). 

## Map description

    _Yustianna was a pirate born of a[Skelliger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") from [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") and a captive woman taken during a raid. In her time she was feared on the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent") from [Ofir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ofir "Ofir") to [Zanguebear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zangvebar "Zangvebar"). Known for her skill as a navigator and unmatched master of various weapons, she quickly became the terror of the [Great Sea](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Sea "Great Sea"), and when she returned to Skellige, they say this cave is where she hid her loot._
