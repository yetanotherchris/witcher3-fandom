## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Clan Tordarroch Forge** was the best forge in all the [Isles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), located on the island of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"), and was used by [Clan Tordarroch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tordarroch "Clan Tordarroch") to make the finest weapons. After the [Ice Giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myrhyff "Myrhyff") awoke from his slumber, he completely wrecked the place and stored all of his food (mostly comprising of [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") corpses) inside the forge. 

## Map description

    _A famous forge where the best tools and weapons in all of Skellige were once made. When the Ice Giant took over the isle, he turned it into his larder._
