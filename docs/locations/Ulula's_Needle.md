### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ulula's Needle** is little more than a rock outcropping near the mouth of [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog")'s bay.
