## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Eternal Fire Chapel** is a small shrine to the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") located east of the [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") and west of [Carsten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carsten "Carsten"). 

## Map description

    _This shrine greets travelers on their way to[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). [Merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") sometimes stop here to sell goods to pilgrims and visiting scholars._
