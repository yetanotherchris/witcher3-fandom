## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**The Pali Gap Coast** is an isolated cavern found north of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") and west of [Kaer Almhult](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Almhult "Kaer Almhult"). It was used as a secret lab by [Avallac'h](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Avallac%27h "Avallac'h") to study in secret [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")'s powers. 

## Map description

    _Elders say an isolated cave on this coast was once used as a retreat by the world's most famous bard (before the rise of[Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion"), that is): the great Xirdneh of [Zanguebar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zangvebar "Zangvebar"), renowned from [Nazair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nazair "Nazair") to the [Dragon Mountains](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon_Mountains "Dragon Mountains") for his ferocious lute-strumming._

## Trivia

This location is a reference to Jimi Hendrix in many ways: 

  * _[Pali Gap](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wJ7AttIb-kc), _a guitar instrumental, is one of his songs.
  * Xirdneh is a reference to Jimi Hendrix. "Xirdneh" is backwards for "Hendrix" and his style is described as "ferocious lute-strumming", where Jimi Hendrix was known for his fast and intricate solos.
