## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Fish Market** is a square in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") located in the city center just southwest of [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"). It's a place where various goods, mainly all kinds of fish, are brought to and traded. In northeast corner a [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Novigrad\) "Blacksmith \(Novigrad\)") has his shop and there is also a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Fish_Market\) "Merchant \(Fish Market\)") selling mainly crafting components, alchemical ingredients and some food and drink items. 

Peace is ensured by the [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") under commander [Gilbert Witschke](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gilbert_Witschke "Gilbert Witschke"). 

Merchant and Blacksmith will appear from 5 am - 7 am, taking an hour break, before appearing again from 8am - 7pm
