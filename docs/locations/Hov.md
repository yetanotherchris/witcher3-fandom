## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hov** is one of the two villages located on the island of [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog"). The arena found in this town is the one used to host fights to between the current [Champion of champions](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Champion_of_champions "Champion of champions") and the contender. 

## Map description

    _Village known throughout all of[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") for the infamous arena which once hosted fierce fights between the mightiest warriors in the isles._
