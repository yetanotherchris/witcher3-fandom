**Abandoned Village** is a burned down village once known as **Hovel** and located in southern [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

## History

Hovel had been founded by [Claer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claer "Claer") and [Volker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Volker "Volker"), who had had enough of living under White Orchard's [former lord](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignatius_Verrieres "Ignatius Verrieres")'s reign and, with approval from [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima")'s court, they were allowed to create a new village that wasn't under Ignatius' rule. Things appeared to go well for the most part until one day Ignatius requested to meet with them in the village. The villagers thought he was a changed man after certain events, but this proved to be untrue and he ended up slaughtering everyone in Hovel. 

Claer, who'd been hung in the village's well, became a [noonwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith "Noonwraith"), preventing anyone from trying to re-settle the area or even draw water from the well she guarded over.
