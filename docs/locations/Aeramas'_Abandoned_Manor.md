## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Aeramas' Abandoned Manor** is an abandoned estate found east of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") that once belonged to the [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") [Aeramas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aeramas "Aeramas") who was a [tyromancer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tyromancy "Tyromancy"). 

Note that it's very easy to accidentally happen upon an [archgriffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin "Archgriffin")'s nest just north of here due to [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses") picking up on the crow sounds for the related quest, [Contract: The Creature from Oxenfurt Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Creature_from_Oxenfurt_Forest "Contract: The Creature from Oxenfurt Forest").
