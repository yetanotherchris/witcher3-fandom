## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Calveit Watchtower** is a small fortress just down the river from [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy") that is overrun with [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") and an [alghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul"). 

Once it is cleared of monsters, 120[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") \+ 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") is awarded, a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Calveit_Watchtower\) "Merchant \(Calveit Watchtower\)") will setup shop here, and the [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") will take over the place along with the nearby [Ferry Station](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ferry_Station "Ferry Station"). 

## Notes

  * Name is in reference to a Nilfgaardian family, the most famous of which would be Emperor [Jan Calveit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jan_Calveit "Jan Calveit").
  * There is a [Blood-covered letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood-covered_letter "Blood-covered letter") on a corpse on the second floor of the main tower but can only be obtained before the area is cleared of monsters, after which it disappears. 
    * The corpses around the place similarly disappear, but can be looted before that (just minor, non-magic weapons) even though they don't glow.
  * Two containers with minor loot - on the eastern side of the tower, behind the stairs - are "refilled" after the site is liberated, even if looted before.
