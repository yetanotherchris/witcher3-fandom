## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unnamed **merchant building** was established in the [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and is run by an [unnamed merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Gildorf\) "Merchant \(Gildorf\)"). The merchant sells different artifacts once belonging to the [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") [Aeramas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aeramas "Aeramas"), as well as a few [quest items](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items"), and also plays [gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent").
