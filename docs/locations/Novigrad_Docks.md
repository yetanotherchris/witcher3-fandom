## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Novigrad Docks** is a signpost in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") lying in the [Harborside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harborside "Harborside") district. It's the pride of the city with most of the prominent corporations on the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent") having their warehouses here. 

## Map description

    _A den of dirt and depravity and the shadiest part of Novigrad. After dark all one finds here are women of loose morals, hoodlums and drunk sailors._
