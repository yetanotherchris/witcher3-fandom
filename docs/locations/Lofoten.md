## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lofoten** is one of the three villages located on the island of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"). It was destroyed by the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") when they came looking for [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"), who was resting in the village at the time. The east side of Lofoten contains a bath house with a sauna and an outside swimming pool as part of a natural stream. The village also has its own [cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lofoten_Cemetery "Lofoten Cemetery"), located a short distance south.

## Bugs

  * An interactable cup of [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya")'s Firewater will still remain in the pool area behind the sauna building as a leftover from [The Calm Before the Storm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Calm_Before_the_Storm "The Calm Before the Storm") quest. If the player uses the roof of the building to enter the area as [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and interacts with the cup, they will become frozen in place, unable to move.

## Trivia

  * Lofoten is also the name of an archipelago and traditional district in the county of Nordland far up north by the coast of Norway.
