## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Wheat Fields** is located west of the [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence") and southwest of [Alness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alness "Alness"), are one of the main sources of food for the citizens of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

## Map description

    _The fertile soils of the[Pontar Delta](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_Delta "Pontar Delta") guarantee the inhabitants of Novigrad full granaries and full stomachs all year long._
