## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Henge** is a circle of stones to the east of [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

It has a [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") in the center that always contains a random relic item, guarded by an [Earth Elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_Elemental "Earth Elemental"). Also leads to some [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") ruins further away in the nearby cliff.
