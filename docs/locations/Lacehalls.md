## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lacehalls** is a small district within [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane") district and borders alongside [Harborside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harborside "Harborside"). It got its name due to all the [harlots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prostitute "Prostitute") that stroll along its streets.[1] The district is under the control of the [King of Beggars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_Beggars "King of Beggars") and his gang, the [Blindeyes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blindeyes "Blindeyes").
