## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lake Wyndamer** is a large [lake](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lake "Lake") in the [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") region of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

At the lake's center is [Fyke Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyke_Isle "Fyke Isle") while [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton") village sits on the northern shore of the lake, being the only village directly on the shores. The rest of the shoreline tends to be populated by monsters such as [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"), [drowned dead](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowned_dead "Drowned dead"), and [rotfiends](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend "Rotfiend").
