The **Vivaldi Bank** was founded by the [Vivaldi family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vivaldi_family "Vivaldi family"). Through their hard work, it became one of the [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent")'s most powerful banks. 

## History

### Beginnings

The Vivaldi bank was already operating well before the human kingdoms had their first kings.[1] Through the Vivaldi family's hard work, their bank became one of the two most powerful banks on the entire [Continent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Continent "Continent"). The bank's headquarters were located at their branch in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), but they established many other branches. They set up branches in all the larger cities in the [North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"), and to oversee this vast network, they had key branches that oversaw specific regions. The [Pont Vanis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pont_Vanis "Pont Vanis") branch oversaw [Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir"), [Poviss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Poviss "Poviss"), [Caingorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caingorn "Caingorn"), and [Creyden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Creyden "Creyden"). The [Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tretogor "Tretogor") branch oversaw [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") and [Kaedwen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaedwen "Kaedwen"). The [Vengerberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vengerberg "Vengerberg") branch oversaw [Aedirn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aedirn "Aedirn"), [Lyria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lyria "Lyria"), and [Sodden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sodden "Sodden"), and the [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") branch oversaw [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") and neighboring countries.[2] [Vimme Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vimme_Vivaldi "Vimme Vivaldi") carried the torch for his generation, making the Novigrad branch of the bank the largest bank in the city.[1]

### Security Troubles

In early [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1267 "Timeline"), the Vivaldi bank informed [Molnar Giancardi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Molnar_Giancardi "Molnar Giancardi") that several attempts had been made to track the correspondence of the [sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorceress "Sorceress") [Yennefer of Vengerberg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg"). The branch in Vengerberg also discovered an attempt by a disloyal clerk to track all transactions of her private accounts. Vivaldi either didn't or couldn't investigate the case, and the disloyal clerk ended up drowned in a ditch. They also informed Molnar of this.[3]

### Vizima Trouble

[![Vizima branch ad](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/59/Ad_Bank.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20081202010916)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/59/Ad_Bank.png/revision/latest?cb=20081202010916) [![Vizima branch ad](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/59/Ad_Bank.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20081202010916)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/59/Ad_Bank.png/revision/latest?cb=20081202010916) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Ad_Bank.png)

Vizima branch ad

The branch in Vizima encountered some financial difficulties, which led to a hostile takeover of the branch by humans, leaving the bank director [Golan Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golan_Vivaldi "Golan Vivaldi") poor and bitter. The bank was used to pay the bail money for the criminal known as the [Professor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Professor_\(assassin\) "Professor \(assassin\)"). The bank would eventually return to the family's control when the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") under the command of [Yaevinn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yaevinn "Yaevinn") stole documents that allowed Golan to regain control.[4] After regaining control, Golan sent someone to check on the profitability of a mine in [Vergen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vergen "Vergen").[5]

### [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III")

Despite growing pressure on the [nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman") in Novigrad, Vimme Vivaldi managed to keep the bank profitable.[1]

_If both Borsody brothers died:_ He even founded a new branch of the bank in the city of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") after buying the [Borsody Auction House](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Borsody_Auction_House "Borsody Auction House").[6]

### [Hogge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hogge_family "Hogge family") association

At one point, Golan hired the Hogge sisters. They attempted to get the poet [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") to write a ballad about one of the bank's founding anniversaries but eventually left the Vivaldi Bank.[7]

Meanwhile, at the Novigrad branch, there were some hard times, which threatened to become even worse as an employee demanded a raise under the threat of starting his own bank and taking some customers with him. Vimme avoided this by giving the employee a fifteen percent raise.

_Like any large city,[Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") also has a branch of the [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf")-owned Vivaldi Bank. It is located in a sturdy building with thick stone walls. Guards armed to the teeth watch the armoured safes, while wealthy clients can count on discreet, professional service. The bank is renowned and so its reputation did not suffer when rumours began circulating of a hostile takeover. Apparently, the bank's owner was having financial problems, and an anonymous investor took advantage._

**Vivaldi Bank** branch in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") was run by [Golan Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golan_Vivaldi "Golan Vivaldi") in the 13th century until [1270](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1270 "Timeline"), when it was taken over by [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human"). 

There also happens to be a second entrance to the bank, located in the [storehouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Town_hall_warehouse "Town hall warehouse") next door.

### Region

[Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square")

The branch of the Vivaldi Bank encountered in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") allows Geralt to exchange [florens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Floren "Floren") and [orens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren") for [crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). The bank also allows Geralt to take out loans, although the previous loan must be paid back before a new loan can be taken out. 

The Novigrad branch is run by [Vimme Vivaldi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vimme_Vivaldi "Vimme Vivaldi"). 

The bank can be found in Novigrad in the main square with the market, just west of the [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") signpost. The building has a pair of large coin shaped signs hanging in front. 

### Exchange rates

  * The rate of exchange for [orens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren") to [crowns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") is 1:1.
  * The rate of exchange for [florens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Floren "Floren") to crowns is 1:3.
