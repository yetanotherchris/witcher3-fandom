### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Marauders' Bridge** is a bridge located north-northeast of [Toderas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toderas "Toderas") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and currently under [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") control. 

## Map description

    _After the Battle of Velen, marauders swarmed over this bridge in their rush to scavenge the battlefield._
