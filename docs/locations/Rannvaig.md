## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Rannvaig** is a small fishing village located on the shore of the western eastern bay of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), south and across the bay from the [Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Crossroads \(Ard Skellig\)"). 

## Map description

    _Fifteen years ago, one of the fishermen of Rannvaig bagged an enormous halibut, and from that moment on all the other villagers have devoted their lives to beating his record._
