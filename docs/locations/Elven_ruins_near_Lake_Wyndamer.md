## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Elven ruins near Lake Wyndamer**[1] is a location far north of [Byways](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Byways "Byways") and directly west of [Fyke Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyke_Isle "Fyke Isle"), just slightly off the coast of [Lake Wyndamer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lake_Wyndamer "Lake Wyndamer"). It is haunted by [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") and rumored to be cursed by the [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") gods.[2]

## Associated quest

  * [Don't Play with the Gods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Don%27t_Play_with_the_Gods "Don't Play with the Gods")

## Notes

  * The hidden treasure and [Place of Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Place_of_Power "Place of Power") markers in the immediate vicinity are in fact underneath it, in the [golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem")'s area near the chamber where the [Magic Lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Lamp_\(quest\) "Magic Lamp \(quest\)") quest takes place.
  * It's unclear why this location is shown a name in the game overlay, though this could be due to it being the "elven burial ground" mentioned by NPCs in [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"), despite the location being quite far south from the village.
