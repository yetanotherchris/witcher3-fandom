## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Harborside** is one of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s districts and accessible through the [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton") district to the north and the [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane") district to the east. 

Unlike other districts, Harborside has no residential area as its primary purpose is to connect the docks to the city. As such, the whole district is made up of warehouses, both big and small, for easy importing and exporting of goods. 

It is also the place where [HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/HMS_Oxenfurt-Tretogor "HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor") is during [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s stay in the city.
