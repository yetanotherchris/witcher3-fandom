## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Kaer Trolde mountain** is the highest peak in the [archipelago](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") above the [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") where famous [citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") is standing. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") must race [Cerys an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite") to the top where the winner may remove [Hjalmar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar "Hjalmar")'s axe from the stump. 

According to legend, [Grymmdjarr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grymmdjarr "Grymmdjarr"), first lord of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), dove into the depths of the sea and used the thickest chain his father, [Hemdall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall "Hemdall"), could forge to drag an enormous rock to the surface, placing it on Ard Skellig's northeastern shore. This enormous rock was the mountain that Grymmdjarr carved Kaer Trolde out of with his bare hands, and where it stands to this day, the pride and glory of his clan, [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite").
