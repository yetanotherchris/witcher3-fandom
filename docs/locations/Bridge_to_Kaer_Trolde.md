## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Bridge to Kaer Trolde** is a short but thick stone bridge set on the path leading to heavy vertically-closing gate of [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") home base, the [citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") of [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde"). Folks of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") are believing in legend that the heroic founder of [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") and god of warrior, [Grymmdjarr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grymmdjarr "Grymmdjarr"), carved the bridge with only one hand. 

## Map description

    _This stone bridge was, according to legend, carved single-handedly by Grymmdjarr, the heroic founder of Clan an Craite._
