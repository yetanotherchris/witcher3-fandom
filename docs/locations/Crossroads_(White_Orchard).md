## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Crossroads** leads travelers towards two main cities: [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"). This is also the very first signpost the player comes across in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Just to the east is where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") set up camp and where [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s [crystal skull](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crystal_skull "Crystal skull") can be found in the very beginning of the game.
