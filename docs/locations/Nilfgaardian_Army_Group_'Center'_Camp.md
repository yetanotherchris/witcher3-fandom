## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp** is the headquarters of the [Nilfgaardian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") in the [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") region. 

## Map description

    _Pitched in a mere two weeks, the[Army Group 'Center'](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Center_Army_Group "Center Army Group") camp constitutes the quintessence of Nilfgaardian martial architecture._
