## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Troll Bridge** is a drawbridge southwest of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). At one point bandits took up a post on the side of the crank to demand tolls from those wanting to use the bridge. 

## Map description

    _Local legend has it this bridge was erected by[trolls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll "Troll") who were later killed by an anonymous [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher")._
