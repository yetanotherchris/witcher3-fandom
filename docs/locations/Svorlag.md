## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Svorlag** is one of the two villages located on the island of [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog"). It is the home base of jarl [Udalryk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Udalryk "Udalryk") and his [Clan Brokvar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Brokvar "Clan Brokvar"). A [tavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tavern_\(Svorlag\) "Tavern \(Svorlag\)") can be found there. 

It hosts a few [pigs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pig "Pig"), [geese](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Goose "Goose") and [chicken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chicken "Chicken"), so it's great spot for food farming. 

## Map description

    _The village was founded by the mythical[Sove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sove "Sove"), who killed a terrifying and bloodthirsty chimera on this spot._
