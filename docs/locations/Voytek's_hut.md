## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Voytek's hut** is an unmarked location east of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") and the home of [Voytek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Voytek "Voytek"), his wife, and their three sons. In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), [Tamara Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tamara_Strenger "Tamara Strenger") took shelter here before she was sent to [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt").
