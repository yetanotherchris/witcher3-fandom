## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Grassy Knoll** is an island in southern [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"), bordered by the [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") province of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") in the south, and the city of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in the north.
