## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Cleaver's Headquarters** is a building located just west of [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") and is used by [Carlo Varese](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese"), also known as Cleaver, as a headquarters for him and his [gang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crownsplitters "Crownsplitters").
