## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This crooked house situated in [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") was used by [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") as her personal hideout during the [inquisition](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunts "Witch hunts") in [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"). 

According to townsmen working nearby, the house was at first as straight as the others, but sometime after its construction, two neighbors living in the house next door built a tunnel from their cellar, under this house, intending to dig into the [Vivaldi Bank](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vivaldi_Bank "Vivaldi Bank"). The thieves however did not reinforce the tunnel walls and it collapsed over them, leaving both houses crooked due to sinking down.
