## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **abandoned site** is located between [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp") and [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle"). Originally inhabited by pirates, if the area is cleared, the inhabitants will return, including a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(boatyard\) "Merchant \(boatyard\)"). 

120[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") are awarded for liberating this site. 

## Associated quest

  * [Love's Cruel Snares](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Love%27s_Cruel_Snares "Love's Cruel Snares")

## Notes

  * [Pirate's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate%27s_notes#Pirate's_notes_\(boatyard\) "Pirate's notes") and an [unsent letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(boatyard_pirates\) "Unsent letter") can be found here only before the site is liberated, but are difficult to retrieve due to the pirates keeping [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") in combat mode. After the site is liberated, both disappear and a different [unsent letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(boatyard_workers\) "Unsent letter") can be found instead.
