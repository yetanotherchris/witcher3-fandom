## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **sawmill** is an [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), in [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond")'s territory south-southwest of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund"). 

It is initially overrun by bandits, with a 170[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") reward for clearing it. Upon its liberation, local Drummond lumberjacks will repopulate the settlement along with a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Drummond_sawmill\) "Merchant \(Drummond sawmill\)"), and [wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") will also start walking the roads from Sund and [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn") leading to the sawmill (they both sell the same things as the static one though).
