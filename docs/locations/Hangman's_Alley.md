## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Hangman's Alley** is a coastal crossroads filled with hanged bodies, located west of [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough"). A local group of bandits often prowl near this area, looking for incoming caravans to raid. 

## Map description

    _The road is lined with the hanged bodies of peasants who opposed their new rulers or had the bad luck of happening across bandits who had nothing against adding another dangling installation to the boulevard's scenery._
