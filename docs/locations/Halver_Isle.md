## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Halver Isle** is a small island in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Due to its location being between [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post") was set up as a checkpoint between the two regions and is manned by [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldiers.
