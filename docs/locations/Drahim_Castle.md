**Drahim Castle** is a ruined estate located south of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). As its description says, it was a stronghold of powerful [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") House of Moskovitz. After the tragic death of its last member, Prince Adrien, only ruins remain of castle. 

[Prince Adrien's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prince_Adrien%27s_journal "Prince Adrien's journal") and a [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Feline_steel_sword "Diagram: Feline steel sword") for the [Feline steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_steel_sword "Feline steel sword") can be found here. 

## Map description

    _In its glory years, this castle was home to the Redanian Moskovitz of the Sea Cats dynasty, patrons of the arts and admirers of elven culture. After the death by suicide of the dynasty's last member, Prince Adrien, the castle fell into the hands of the Redanian crown – and then into ruin._
