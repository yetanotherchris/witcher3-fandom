## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Dorve Ruins** are the ruins of a village found on the island of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"). 

Being the largest town on the isle, it was presumably the home base of [Clan Tordarroch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tordarroch "Clan Tordarroch") before the [ice giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant"), [Myrhyff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myrhyff "Myrhyff"), ransacked Undvik in [1271](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1271 "Timeline/Games").
