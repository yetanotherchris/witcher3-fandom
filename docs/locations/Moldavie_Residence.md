## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Moldavie Residence** is a large manor located northeast of [Honeyfill Meadworks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeyfill_Meadworks "Honeyfill Meadworks"). 

All the rumbling in this place was caused by an imprisoned [Earth elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_elemental "Earth elemental") named [Therazane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Therazane "Therazane"), who cast it when its previous owners locked him inside a magical cage found underneath the manor. 

Furthermore, depending on one's actions in [Contract: Doors Slamming Shut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Doors_Slamming_Shut "Contract: Doors Slamming Shut"), if you should knock out the support column in the basement, [Kurt Dysart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kurt_Dysart "Kurt Dysart") will be found later crushed to death as the basement collapsed on him. However, if during the fight you were able to leave the column intact, Kurt will be fine.
