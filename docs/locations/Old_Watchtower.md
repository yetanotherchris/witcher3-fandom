## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Old Watchtower** is a location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's located on [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog") and is home to a local group of bandits. 

## Map description

    _The ruins of an old watchtower in which, according to legend, a crazed[Koviri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir") [princess](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Princess "Princess") once locked herself up, convinced only a man able to free her from this tower would be fit to be her husband. No one even tried, however, the princess died of old age and the tower fell into disrepair with the passage of time._
