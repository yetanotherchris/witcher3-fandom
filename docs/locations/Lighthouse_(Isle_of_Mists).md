## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lighthouse** is an abandoned lighthouse on the [Isle of Mists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isle_of_Mists "Isle of Mists"). You can meet one of the [dwarves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") here on the top of the lighthouse, who you need to find in case if you want to enter the [Strange Cottage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Strange_Cottage "Strange Cottage").
