**Mulbrydale** is a small village located in the [Grayrocks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grayrocks "Grayrocks") region of northern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen").[1] [Coën](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Co%C3%ABn "Coën") and Narsi Sattelbach were both hired here by elder Stakhomir to hunt [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner").[2]

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

At one point after [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria")'s fall during the [third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War"), the villagers here helped out [Vernon Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche "Vernon Roche")'s guerilla members. 

_If[An Eye for an Eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Eye_for_an_Eye "An Eye for an Eye") is done:_ [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soldiers, having learned that the villagers had helped the remnants of the crushed [Temerian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Army "Temerian Army"), resolved to make them an example to others who might do so by attacking and killing all the villagers. However, they were ultimately stopped by [Ves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ves "Ves") and a reluctant Roche, along with [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"). 

### Map description

    _One of the oldest villages in the region. Owes its name to a certain undereducated botanist who could not discern one kind of tree from another and called them all mulberries._
