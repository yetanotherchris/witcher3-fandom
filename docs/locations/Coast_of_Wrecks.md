## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Coast of Wrecks** is a large beach in western [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") where it's appropriately covered in shipwrecks. To the east is [Forest Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forest_Hut "Forest Hut"), while the [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp") lies to the south. 

A large group of pirates has established itself in the area, robbing and killing any travelers wandering too close. Some time after they're cleared out, some peasants and a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Coast_of_Wrecks\) "Merchant \(Coast of Wrecks\)") will take up residence here. 

## Map description
