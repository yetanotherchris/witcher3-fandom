**Crookback Bog** is a large swamp area in southern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

## History

Crookback Bog is home to a variety of beasts, yet an [orphanage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Orphans "Orphans") for orphaned and unwanted children lies in the middle of the swamp despite the hazards, as does the grotto of a friendly [godling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Godling "Godling") named [Johnny](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Johnny "Johnny"). 

The [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones") of Crookback Bog comprise a triad of mysterious entities that are worshiped by the local residents. The inhabitants of the village of [Downwarre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren")[n](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren"), in particular, give great credence to the Crones, as do certain inhabitants of Velen residing further afield of the Bog. However, this worship isn't without cost: they readily participate in mutilating themselves and even send children to the orphanage, knowing they'll be killed and eaten by the Crones. 

The orphanage situated at the heart of the swamp is overseen by [Gran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gran "Gran"). There is a Pagan chapel where Gran can ritually communicate with the Crones by the Tapestry. 

[Drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") are a common sight in Crookback Bog, as in most aquatic areas, and [water hags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag") may also be encountered on occasion. On the mountainside beside Crookback Bog, [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") and a [wyvern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern") can be found.
