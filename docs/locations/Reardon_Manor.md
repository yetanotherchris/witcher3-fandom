## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Reardon Manor** is the former home of the Reardon family but hasn't been occupied in some time, resulting in [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") overrunning the estate. 

## Map description

    _Abandoned estate of the once prominent Reardon family, relatives to the royal La Louve dynasty._

## Notes

  * If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") let [Letho](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letho "Letho") live in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ , Letho will be using the place to hide from the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") hitmen and [bounty hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arnout_Vester "Arnout Vester") that are after him. There will also be no wraiths, as he took care of them, but traps will be set up around the estate.
  * If Geralt (or Letho) clears out the monsters here, [Dolores](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dolores_Reardon "Dolores Reardon") can later be found in one of the huts and will tell Geralt she takes pleasure in trying to restore the manor.
