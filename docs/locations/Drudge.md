## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Drudge** is a small coastal village in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). It is placed on a peninsula southeast of [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton") beside the island of the [Bandits' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandits%27_Camp "Bandits' Camp"). It has since been abandoned however, for unknown reasons. 

## Map description

    _This once-peaceful fisherman's settlement now stands almost completely empty. Road-weary travelers sometimes find shelter in its abandoned huts - besides that, not a soul is to be seen._
