### Games

**Novigrad Gate** is one of the two gates leading into the city of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). 

## Map description

    _During[Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")'s exam sessions, this gate would be closed, to spare the students from [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s temptations._
