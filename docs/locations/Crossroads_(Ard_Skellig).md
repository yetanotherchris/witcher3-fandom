## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **Crossroads** , found north of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig"), forks two ways: one towards [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") and the other towards the northeastern mountains. 

## Map description

    _Fork in the road leading to Kaer Trolde._

## Notes

  * To the west of it, nearby up a hill is a [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") that is rather unique in that once you kill the [wyvern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern") guarding it, [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") will appear very near. They won't technically be guarding the treasure (a very minor one), but are so close that it might appear so.
