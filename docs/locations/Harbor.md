# Harbor

[![Tw3 icon harbor](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d9/Tw3_icon_harbor.png/revision/latest?cb=20160823081017)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d9/Tw3_icon_harbor.png/revision/latest?cb=20160823081017) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw3_icon_harbor.png)

**Harbors** are used as fast-travel points in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are scattered about the regions and are functionally identical to a [Signpost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signpost "Signpost"), though they can only be utilized if the player is [sailing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sailing "Sailing") in a boat (i.e. if the player is just standing in a boat, harbors won't appear on the map).
