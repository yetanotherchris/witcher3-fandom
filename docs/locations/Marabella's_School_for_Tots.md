## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Marabella's School for Tots** is a minor primary school run by [Marabella](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marabella "Marabella") in [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") district in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Young children of all races can master the basics here, then continue to study in nearby [University of Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/University_of_Oxenfurt "University of Oxenfurt") if they wish. 

## [Advertisement posters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Posters "Posters") entry

where they will learn reading, writing and respect for the gods!

## Notes

  * If Geralt spares [the Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree"), the orphans under [Gran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gran "Gran")'s care find their way to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") where they are enrolled in Marabella's school. However, none of them show any recognition of Geralt when he arrives.
