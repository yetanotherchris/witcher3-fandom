## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Destroyed Bastion** is a location in southeastern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). Now in a state of disarray, it is home to a solitary [cyclops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops"). 

## Map description

    _Bastion built during the reign of[King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Gardic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gardic "Gardic") and destroyed during the [First Nilfgaardian War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/First_Nilfgaard_War "First Nilfgaard War")._

## Notes

  * There are three other small ruins nearby, with switches on their walls. When all are activated, an [earth elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_elemental "Earth elemental") appears roughly in the middle between them. After killing it, a portal will appear on a nearby hill, transporting [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to the top of the destroyed bastion. After descending into the underground, Geralt is attacked by eight [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") that cannot be killed (only by [Piercing Cold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Piercing_Cold "Piercing Cold")). The only escape is by activating two crystals with [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard"), which will trigger another portal. Nothing else is found here, except for a strange-looking symbol on one of the walls in the main underground room, which may refer to the vampires in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") or the _Cyberpunk 2077_ game. However, the meaning has not yet been revealed by the developers.
