### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This longhouse tavern is situated in a village named [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik") on the [Skellige island](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall").

## Bugs

  * In rare occasions, a horse could spawn right in the entrance, blocking it from entering the tavern. The horse will still be there even if you meditate or travel far away and come back. You can use [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard") or [Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni") to scare the horse away so you can enter, but by doing so you have the chance to make the guards hostile.
