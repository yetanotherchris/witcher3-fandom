## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Books and Scrolls** is a two story bookstore in the northern part of [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"), in the city center of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and likely the largest source of books in the city, if not the whole region. The establishment is protected by [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") with its owner and operator being [Marcus Thaddeus Knut Hodgson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Marcus_T.K._Hodgson "Marcus T.K. Hodgson").

## Notes

  * The ancient library discovered during the quest [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never") is fittingly located directly underneath the shop.
  * This is one of the only places to sell books for a reasonable amount of coin.
  * Unusually for a specialist retailer, the store will also purchase crafting goods at rates comparable to merchants.
