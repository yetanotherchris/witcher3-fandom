**Wolven Glade** is a landmark located northeast of [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). The [pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar") informs [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") if they go here that none dare mention the place's name because in the past terrible things happened here. 

If Geralt decides to kill the [botchling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Botchling "Botchling") during [Family Matters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Family_Matters "Family Matters"), this is the place where the pellar will conduct his ritual while the witcher protects him from [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"). 

## Map description

    _A long, long time ago, when this land was ruled by forest spirits and ancient gods, the living would come here to pay their respects to the dead in the way their holy tome commanded "Walk thee in darkness, on a path of blood, standing under bare sky, naked before the gods and their messengers."_
