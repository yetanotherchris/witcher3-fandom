## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Olena's Grove** is a forested area located in southern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). Many alchemists and herbalists used to come here looking for rare ingredients, before it was overrun by [endregas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega "Endrega"). 

If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") liberates the site, 120 [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") will be awarded, and a [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Olena%27s_Grove\) "Herbalist \(Olena's Grove\)") will become available, while the peasants who return can be seen picking herbs. 

## Map description

    _Legends claim that a beautiful[nymph](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nymph "Nymph") named Olena once lived in this grove. She fell in love with a young hunter and the man swore to be true to the nymph, but later betrayed her. She decided to punish him by casting a spell on his spirit, which wanders the grove to this day._
