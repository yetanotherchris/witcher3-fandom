## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Herbalist's Hut** , found northeast of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), is home to the [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") herbalist, [Otto Bamber](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Otto_Bamber "Otto Bamber").[1]

## Map description

    _Home to a halfling herbalist who is a passionate devotee of innovative gardening methods and experimental herbal medecine._
