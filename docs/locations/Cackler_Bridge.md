## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Cackler Bridge** is a rickety closed bridge located in northeastern [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and is the shortest path in contrast to the windier path traversing through the south of the crossroads. 

## Map description

    _Bridge named in honor of a woman who went mad from unfulfilled love. After her heart snapped, she spent all her days running up and down this bridge while laughing hysterically._

## Notes

  * While the barricade gate is initially closed, it can be opened for convenience, however, it can only be done by reaching it from the west side.
  * Due to the burned [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") tent beside the gate, it can be inferred that the bridge was guarded by [Nilfgaardian troops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Army_of_Nilfgaard "Imperial Army of Nilfgaard") shortly after their victory until raided and ransacked by bandits. In fact, there is a bandit camp nearby to support this.
