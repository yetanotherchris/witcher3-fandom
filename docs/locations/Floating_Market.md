## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ (cut)

The **Floating Market** was to be a location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ but was cut during development.[1]

    _A market established on conjoined ships, only accessible with a[boat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sailing "Sailing"). Heirs of a once [large and proud clan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tordarroch "Clan Tordarroch"); cast out by the [mighty Ice Giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myrhyff "Myrhyff") from their [homeland](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"). The vowed that they shall never set foot on dry land other than home, and they are on a constant voyage to strengthen their ranks and become strong enough to vanquish their foe. It's constantly on the move on the eastern shores of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), in the shadow of mountains._
