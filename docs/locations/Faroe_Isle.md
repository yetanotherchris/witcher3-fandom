## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Faroe** is the southernmost main island in the [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") archipelago and traditionally belongs to [Clan Dimun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Dimun "Clan Dimun").

## Trivia

  * It is very likely named after the [Faroe Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Faroe_Islands) located between Scotland and Iceland.
  * The island resembles the southernmost island in the Faroe Islands, called "Suðuroy" (South Isle).
