## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Holmstein** is a village located halfway between [Kaer Muire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Muire "Kaer Muire") and [Holmstein's Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein%27s_Port "Holmstein's Port"), and serves as the capital of [Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond")'s lands.
