## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Heatherton** is a village to the north of [Blackbough](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbough "Blackbough") that was destroyed by the [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") when they came looking for the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") spy, [Hendrik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hendrik "Hendrik"). 

## Map description

    _The inhabitants of this village were relieved when they learned the path of the marching armies had shifted slightly and passed their village by. Then, one night... they changed their minds._
