## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Abandoned Sawmill** in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") has been abandoned for a long time. At first believed to be the work of the nearby [druids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid"), who were against the logging carried out in this area, the workers were instead all killed by a [leshen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshen "Leshen") that settled in the surrounding forest. It is located in the forest west-northwest of [Redgill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redgill "Redgill"), west-southwest of [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith"), and northeast of [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

## Map description

_Though located deep in the forest, an ideal place for lumber harvesting, the sawmill now lies abandoned and unused._
