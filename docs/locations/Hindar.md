# Hindar

**Hindar** is a sacred grove on the island of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"), in the [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") archipelago.

## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Freya's Garden** is a large sanctuary that was built by the locals of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") a long time ago. Recently, the priestesses were almost all butchered during one of their ceremonies by a local bandit named [Morkvarg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg "Morkvarg"). 

### Map description

    _Garden dedicated to the goddess Freya. Once beautiful and filled with thousands of fragrant blooms and herbs, today it lies abandoned and untended._
