# Monster den

[![Tw3 icon monster den](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/c/ce/Tw3_icon_monster_den.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/50?cb=20170901053624)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/c/ce/Tw3_icon_monster_den.png/revision/latest?cb=20170901053624)

A **monster den** is a type of marked treasure location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, where loot is hidden inside [monster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster")-infested dungeons (often caves, but not necessarily). Monster dens aren't as common as other marked treasures, but the locations themselves are usually more elaborate and/or associated with quests. 

Similarly to [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") and [bandit camps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp"), a monster den is considered cleared and its map marker greyed-out as soon as its designated treasure chest is opened, even if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") manages to do that without slaying any enemies. 

## Map description

    _Monster-infested location. A constant worry for those living nearby._

## Velen and Novigrad

4 locations: 

  * Mine northwest of [Hanged Man's Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hanged_Man%27s_Tree "Hanged Man's Tree") ([Nekkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker"), but they don't appear during [A Deadly Plot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Deadly_Plot "A Deadly Plot"))
  * [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto") ([Wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") and [ekimmara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekimmara "Ekimmara"))  
Visited for [Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear")
  * Cave east of [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten") (Nekkers and a rabid [rock troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rock_troll "Rock troll"))  
Visited for [Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 4](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear_Upgrade_Diagrams_-_Part_4 "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 4")
  * [Cavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cavern "Cavern"), north of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") ([Golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem"))  
Visited for [Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 3](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear_Upgrade_Diagrams_-_Part_3 "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 3")
