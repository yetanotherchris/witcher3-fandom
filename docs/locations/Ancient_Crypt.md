## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ancient Crypt** is an [elven ruin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elven_ruins "Elven ruins") found underneath the northern mountains of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), spanning from the northern island shores almost all the way to the village of [Rogne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rogne "Rogne"), where it has a second exit. 

Many treasure hunters have wanted to scavenge the crypt, but it was [magically](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") sealed from both ends, in order to protect the [Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sunstone "Sunstone") \- a powerful [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") relic, which [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") eventually managed to retrieve with the help of [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart"). 

## Map description

    _Though[Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") are famed for bravery bordering on madness, there are certain places which even they keep their distance from. This is one of them._
