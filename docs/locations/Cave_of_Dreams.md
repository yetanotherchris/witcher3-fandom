### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Cave of Dreams** is a mysterious cave in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") where it is said that anyone who enters can face his own fears by eating some hallucinogenic herbs.
