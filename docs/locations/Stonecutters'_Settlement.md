## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Stonecutters' Settlement** , also known as **Holloway** is a mostly-walled village in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), located just outside of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), that was under the protection of the [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") army. While villagers here mined stone, the army also brought in [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") prisoners of war to help with production. 

As Redanians later[2] moved out of the village, exploitation of the inhabitants labor ceased, but they were preyed on by local bandits. 

## Map description

    _Over twenty years ago a certain Bartolomeo, known as "Badger" on account of certain characteristics aspects of his coiffure, discovered a rich deposit of high-quality stone on this spot. He bought the land for a song, then leased it back to local peasants before heading off to[Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir"), where he lives the life of a rich and powerful townsman to this day._
