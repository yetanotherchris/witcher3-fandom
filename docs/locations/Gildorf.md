## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gildorf** is the more affluent district in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , located in the north of city center and connected with [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") via [St. Gregory's Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/St._Gregory%27s_Bridge "St. Gregory's Bridge").The place boasts the finest brothel in the area, bathhouse with thermal water, morgue, well, market with stalls, two shops and many residences. 

It is inhabited by the free city's social elite - nobles, wealthy merchants, townspeople, ambassadors and alike. Because of its importance, the place is protected by [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") and [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter"). There are four entrances to the city's sewers. One of the steel swords, [Gildorf sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf_sword "Gildorf sword"), is named after this district.
