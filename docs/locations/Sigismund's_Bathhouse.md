**Sigismund's Bathhouse** is south of [St. Gregory's Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/St._Gregory%27s_Bridge "St. Gregory's Bridge"), in the [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), is one of the prominent places during _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. This large bathhouse is run by one-time spy and now Big Four crime lord, [Sigismund Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra"), and his eunuch, [Happen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Happen_the_Eunuch "Happen the Eunuch"). It also has an entrance to the [sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_sewers "Novigrad sewers") system.
