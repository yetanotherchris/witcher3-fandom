## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **arena** , together with the [nearby casino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_casino "Whoreson Junior's casino"), were two of the most profitable source of revenue of the crime lord [Cyprian Wiley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley"), also known as "_Whoreson Junior"._ However, when Cyprian was taking care of business elsewhere, the arena was under the control of [Igor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igor_the_Hook "Igor the Hook"), better known as "_the Hook"_ , Cyprian's right-hand man. He administered the fights and bets, choosing who could fight and who couldn't. 

The arena is frequented by all kinds of competitors: [mercenaries](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mercenary "Mercenary"), [wild dogs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dog "Dog"), warriors from [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), [wyverns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern"), [endregas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega "Endrega"), and last but not least, the [bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") [Boris](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boris_\(bear\) "Boris \(bear\)").
