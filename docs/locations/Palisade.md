## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Inn palisade in the Outskirts](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/95/Places_Inn_Palisades.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20081104181150)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/95/Places_Inn_Palisades.png/revision/latest?cb=20081104181150 "Inn palisade in the Outskirts")Inn palisade in the Outskirts

In the [Outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts "Outskirts"), the [inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts_Inn "Outskirts Inn") is surrounded by a palisade. Inside, there are a number of [interesting people](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts_Inn "Outskirts Inn") to chat with. The [Old Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Manor "Old Manor") is also within a palisade.

## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

There is a location in the third game called **Palisade** on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

### Map description

    _The remnants of the palisade that once marked the border between the territories of[Clan Drummond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Drummond "Clan Drummond") and [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite")._
