## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Vegelbud Residence** is the large estate located north of [Carsten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carsten "Carsten") and east of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") that serves as home to the [Vegelbud family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_family "Vegelbud family"), one of Novigrad's prominent noble houses. 

As a rich family, Vegelbuds mainly organize two events here: first are [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") races in the name of [Erasmus Vegelbud](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erasmus_Vegelbud "Erasmus Vegelbud") and the second come lavish banquets famed in the entire region. 

## Map description

    _Domicile of a prominent Novigrad family whose line can be traced back to the times when the first[human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") settlers [came to these lands](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/First_Landing "First Landing")._

## Notes

  * Access to the main part of the estate is restricted and you cannot enter under most circumstances. The only times access is granted to the grounds is during the two secondary quests [A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)") and [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins"), the interior only being explorable during Carnal Sins.
