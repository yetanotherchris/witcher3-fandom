## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **battlefield** is a location within [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). It is where [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") and [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") troops clashed in the [Battle of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_White_Orchard "Battle of White Orchard") during the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III"). 

The territory is now covered with corpses and infested by [necrophages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage "Necrophage") such as [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") and [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul").
