### Games

**Triss' house** in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") is a three-story house situated near [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

## History

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), when the [witch hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunts "Witch hunts") erupted within [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"), [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") was forced to flee from here and take shelter in another [house](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss%27_hideout "Triss' hideout") in [the Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits"). When [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") visited Novigrad, the house had already been abandoned and most of the valuable objects of the [sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") were stolen by looters.
