## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Farcorners** is [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")'s largest district by land size, as it makes up the small communities and farmland outside the city's walls. Many [non-humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman") live here as well as several hiding [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage"). The district is under the control of [Carlo Varese](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese") and his gang, the [Crownsplitters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crownsplitters "Crownsplitters").

## Notes

  * Interestingly, in the _Prima Guide_ , Farcorners is supposed to be just the island to the east of [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane") and south of [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits"). However, in game, the district extends almost to [Cunny of the Goose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cunny_of_the_Goose "Cunny of the Goose") on [Grassy Knoll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grassy_Knoll "Grassy Knoll"), the island to the south of Novigrad.
