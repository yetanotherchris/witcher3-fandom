## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Honeyfill Meadworks** is a bee farm and estate owned by [Holofernes Meiersdorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holofernes_Meiersdorf "Holofernes Meiersdorf") and his sons, a clan of rich [halflings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling"), and is located on the outskirts of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and west of [Dancing Windmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dancing_Windmill "Dancing Windmill"). 

It is said that whoever has not tasted their mead has never known true pleasure. This beverage is proudly served in all the best guest houses of the [North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/North "North") and a [priest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priest "Priest") is said to have once called upon it in a theological dispute, arguing that if vile halflings can create a beverage this divine, it must mean good can, in fact, be born of evil. 

They had trouble with a strange creature, the one they dubbed [The Apiarian Phantom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Apiarian_Phantom "The Apiarian Phantom"), who destroyed bee farms and killed a farmhand in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games").
