## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Sawmill** , located in western [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), is providing wood for the whole region. This location was abandoned and filled with [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul") and [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul"), but if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") clears them out the people will return. 

Aside from being a place for cutting wood, it also has a house with a broken roof, a pen with no livestock, a well that has a [beehive](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Honeycomb "Honeycomb"), and one [grindstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grindstone "Grindstone"). To the north lies the [garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison"), nearby east lies [Tomira](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tomira "Tomira")'s hut, south lies a small Nilfgaardian camp, and southwest the road to [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima").
