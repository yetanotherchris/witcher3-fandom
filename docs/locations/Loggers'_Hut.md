## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Loggers' Hut** is an abandoned cabin located just south of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and was once inhabited by several woodcutters before a [powerful leshen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kernun "Kernun") killed them. 

## Map description

    _A lone cabin deep in the Novigrad Forest - an ideal base for woodcutters._

## Associated quest

  * [Contract: Lord of the Wood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Lord_of_the_Wood "Contract: Lord of the Wood")

## Notes

  * Once [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") deals with the [leshen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshen "Leshen"), the place will become inhabited once more by workers.
