## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Blackbough** is a village in western [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and located quite closely to [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). [The Pellar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Pellar "The Pellar")'s cottage can be found in the north of the village. This small settlement has not only one, but two [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") offering various items such as [crafting components](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") or animal hides. 

## Map description

    _This village takes its name from the unwanted limbs loggers used to bring here to burn, leaving stacks of charred logs behind. The locals, however, prefer the old legend which holds that their village was founded by a prominent member of an ancient[race](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Race "Race") of tree people._
