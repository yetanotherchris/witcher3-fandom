## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Temple Isle** is the name for a small island connected to the north side of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") by [St. Gregory's Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/St._Gregory%27s_Bridge "St. Gregory's Bridge") and is the location of the [Great Temple](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Temple_of_the_Eternal_Fire "Great Temple of the Eternal Fire").
