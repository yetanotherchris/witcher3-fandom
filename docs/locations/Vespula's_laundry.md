This **laundry** located in the [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") belonged to [Vespula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vespula "Vespula"). 

## History

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley")'s [men](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cutups "Cutups") came to claim their 200 [crown](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") tax for offering protection to Vespula and to avoid dramatic accidents to the laundry. [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") arrived at the same time to ask her about [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion"), and ended up solving her problem with the thugs.
