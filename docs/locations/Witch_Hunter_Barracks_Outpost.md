## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Witch Hunter Barracks Outpost** are the headquarters of the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") in the city of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), who are allied to the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") and [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V"), and responsible for finding and bringing [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage"), and to a lesser extent [nonhumans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nonhuman "Nonhuman"), before the yoke of oppression. Those found guilty of sorcery are then burned at the stake in [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square").
