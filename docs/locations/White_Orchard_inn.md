## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**White Orchard inn** is the most prominent building in the [village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and was run by [Elsa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elsa "Elsa"). Because of war and moving armies, some very interesting people, like [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") scholar [Aldert Geert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aldert_Geert "Aldert Geert"), stayed here for a time. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") passed through and stayed at the inn in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") while looking for [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg").
