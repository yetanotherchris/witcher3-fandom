### Games

**Strange Cottage** is an abandoned cottage in the center of the [Isle of Mists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isle_of_Mists "Isle of Mists"). It is now home to a quartet of [dwarves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") who have lost their way. They refuse to allow [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") into this abode until he finds their friends.
