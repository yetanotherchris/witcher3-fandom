## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Blandare** is a village located in the middle of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

## Map description

_Unusually for a[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") village, Blandare is located inland, far from any shore. Its inhabitants scratch out a living through mining and shepherding._

## Notes

  * There's a [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") inside a crypt in the village's cemetery (to the north) that always contains a random relic item. It is guarded by [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") that ambush you once you're inside.
