## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Caesar Bilzen's house** is a three-story building just south of [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and belongs to [Caesar Bilzen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caesar_Bilzen "Caesar Bilzen"), the accountant of [Carlo Varese](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carlo_Varese "Carlo Varese"), one of the [Big Four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four") of the city. Together with his endless fishing trophies, the [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") used to collect a great amount of [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") memorabilia. 

_If Geralt helps Caesar:_ When Cleaver sent some of his [men](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crownsplitters "Crownsplitters") to capture Caesar to punish him for some discrepancies in the ledger, [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") helped Caesar to beat them, after which the dwarf packed and left the city.
