## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The living quarters of [Cyprian Willey](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley"), also known as "Whoreson Junior," the fourth of the [Big Four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four") crime lords of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and businessman with a sickening depravity for flesh. 

It is a wonder the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") turn a blind eye to this deviant individual, but Whoreson seems to have friends in high places (on [galleons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/HMS_Oxenfurt-Tretogor "HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor") moored off [Oxenfurt harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Harbor "Oxenfurt Harbor"), for example).
