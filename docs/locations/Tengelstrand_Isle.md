## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Tengelstrand Isle** is a small island in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), located between [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") and [Ingdalen Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ingdalen_Isle "Ingdalen Isle") and is home to a small group of [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren").
