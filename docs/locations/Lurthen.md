## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lurthen** is one of the three villages located on the island of [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall"). It was recently abandoned after a flock of [harpies](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") and [erynias](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia "Erynia") came to afflict the settlement. 

## Map description

    _Lurthen's most famous inhabitant was a certain Peter Pijus, known for the fact that he was able to drink an entire barrel of mead in one go and remain standing._
