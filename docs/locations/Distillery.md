## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Distillery** , located high in the peaks of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), is home to a [cyclops](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops") who has taken over this mysterious place. 

The approach to the distillery is populated by a number of [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul").
