## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**His Majesty's Ship _Oxenfurt-Tretogor_** is a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") royal ship and among the best [military vessels](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") in the [Northern Kingdoms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"); largely funded by donations from the noblewomen of the realm, it was commanded by [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") during the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III").
