## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Bay Of Winds** is a small abandoned harbor situated on a bay on the southwestern shores of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"). Several [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") now prowl the beach there and from time to time, the shoreline floods. A usable boat is also moored in the bay. 

## Map description

    _Three generations ago this bay was a popular meeting spot for the local youth. Then one night a terrible storm broke out and the sea pounded into the beach, swallowing up several merry-makers and dragging them out to a watery grave._
