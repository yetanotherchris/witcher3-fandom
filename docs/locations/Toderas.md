## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Toderas** is an abandoned village located in eastern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). Most of the peasants in this village are dead, having been killed off by several small groups of bandits. 

When first passing through, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can come across two groups of people arguing over who gets to loot the bodies, having both helped in killing the people. Geralt can either choose to stay out of it, in which case the groups will fight each other, or insult them and thus the two groups will attack Geralt. 

Later on, the village will be overrun by [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul"). 

## Map description

    _Village founded by[King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_of_Temeria "Griffin of Temeria") of [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria"), the husband of [Clarissa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clarissa_of_Toussaint "Clarissa of Toussaint") of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"). The king had planned to turn Toderas into a large, bustling university city, a sort of Temerian alternative to [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), but, as any visitor can quickly attest, his efforts failed utterly._
