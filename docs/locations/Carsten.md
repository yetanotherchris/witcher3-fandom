### Games

**Carsten** is a village in the [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") region of [Gustfields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gustfields "Gustfields"). It is located southwest of [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence"). 

## Map description

    _A village named after a baker whose exquisite goods gained him fame, as well as the privilege of supplying bread to the table of the hierarch of the[Church of the Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). Following his death, none proved capable of recreating his recipes for his delicious and deeply aromatic breads, so these days Carsten is known chiefly for its trade in grain and flour._
