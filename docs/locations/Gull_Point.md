## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gull Point** is a landmark located on the eastern shores of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It's a small peninsula blocked off from the rest of the island by snowy, mountainous terrain. The only thing of note is a [signpost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signpost "Signpost") made of stacked rocks. 

## Map description

    _Sea fowl come from miles around to congregate on this scrap of barren land, mate and lay eggs._
