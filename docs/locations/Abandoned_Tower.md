## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Abandoned Tower** is located west of the village of [Heatherton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heatherton "Heatherton") within [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). 

The tower is mostly inaccessible, but it is possible to climb up on a paraphet when attempting it from the northwestern corner. There are two chests at the top of the tower. 

## History

During [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri")'s stay with the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), they raced each other to this tower, where they awoke a powerful [basilisk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk") who was resting atop it. 

## Map description

    _Legend has it a beleaguered traveler once stood at this tower's gates. He begged shelter for the night, claiming he'd been injured, but the[baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baron "Baron") living inside was afraid the traveler was a [spy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spy "Spy") and sent him away. Little did he know the traveler was a powerful [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage"), who cast a [curse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Curse "Curse") on the tower, its inhospitable owner and all who dwelled with him. Soon thereafter the baron and all his retinue died in mysterious circumstances, and the tower fell into ruin._
