## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ (cut)

**Dalvik** was to be a village in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). However, this location was removed during the development of _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and thus was only seen in the game's [VGX trailer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:The_Witcher_3_Wild_Hunt_-_VGX_Trailer "File:The Witcher 3 Wild Hunt - VGX Trailer").
