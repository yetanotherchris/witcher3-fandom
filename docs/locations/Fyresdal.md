## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Fyresdal** is a village located on the southern shore of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") under Snow Giant's Arm. The elders of this village claimed to have seen a [dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Dragon_of_Fyresdal "The Dragon of Fyresdal") but upon further investigation with the help of a certain [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"), it was discovered that it was only a very large and powerful [Forktail](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forktail "Forktail").

## Trivia

  * Fyresdal is the name of a municipality in eastern Norway, in the county of Telemark. It translates to "valley of the lake Fyrir," though the lake it refers to is now known as Fyresvatn.
