### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Mudplough** is a region of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is Velen's smallest region, constituting of just an island that's mostly uninhabited.
