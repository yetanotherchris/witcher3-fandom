## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **crossroads** leads travelers out of the [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") region. There is a bandit camp quite close to the south of this place and also a [Person in Distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_Distress "Person\(s\) in Distress") in the forest, due east.
