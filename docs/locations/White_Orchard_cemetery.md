## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**White Orchard cemetery** is north of the [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). There is a [Place of Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Place_of_Power "Place of Power") and [Melitele](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Melitele "Melitele") statues are located in the nearby chapel. 

## History

In the [1260s](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1260 "Timeline/Games"), the Verrieres family crypt was infested by [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"); Lord [Ignatius](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignatius_Verrieres "Ignatius Verrieres") demanded [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Kolgrim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kolgrim "Kolgrim") of the [School of the Viper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Viper "School of the Viper"), face a [trial by ordeal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trial_by_ordeal "Trial by ordeal") for the alleged kidnapping of a [local beekeeper's son](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitty "Vitty"). This trial involved clearing the crypt, however he was beheaded by the villagers and left to rot there.[1]

Sometime after, Ignatius discovered his son [Florian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Florian_Verrieres "Florian Verrieres")'s homosexual relationship with the hunter [Mislav](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mislav "Mislav"), and so Florian hung himself, later being buried at the crypt, with a gravestone stating that he died of "apoplexie", masking the true nature of his death. Finally after his father's subsequent execution, and the [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") occupation of White Orchard, the crypt fell into disrepair. 

_If Geralt chose to investigate:_ In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), after visiting the graveyard, [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") found Kolgrim's remains and slew the wraiths.

## Notes

  * After the wraiths are gone, grave robbers may appear in the graveyard after midnight, though they have no visible label such as "bandit" or "deserter".
  * During the empress ending of the [Something Ends, Something Begins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Something_Ends,_Something_Begins_\(quest\) "Something Ends, Something Begins \(quest\)") quest, a world border protects this area from being accessed. However, if mods without collision are used to clip through this border, a red deleted-object placeholder box can be found in the cemetery basement, and the place of power will be missing.
