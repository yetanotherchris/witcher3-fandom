## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Oxenfurt Chess Club** , one of the [academics](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy")' pastimes, a society for the advancement of game where paupers and kings are on equal footing. 

That is, until King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") took an interest. Reports of him executing those that bettered him in a game are unsubstantiated and unlikable, though he does frequent this locale.[1]
