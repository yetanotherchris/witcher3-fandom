## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Castle Tuirseach** on [Mount Aardeklove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mount_Aardeklove "Mount Aardeklove") was once a great fortress rivaling [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") in both size and prestige. Yet, all that remains of the fortress by present events is a ruin on the north coast of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"). For many centuries, the castle served as the seat of [Clan Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tuirseach "Clan Tuirseach"). It was destroyed after bandits and Nilfgaardian bounty hunters paid by [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") besieged the castle with catapults intending to kill Jarl [Torgeir the Red](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torgeir_the_Red "Torgeir the Red"). Castle Tuirseach crumbled and fell into ruin bringing down with it the jarl along with his men and the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Gerd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gerd_\(witcher\) "Gerd \(witcher\)").
