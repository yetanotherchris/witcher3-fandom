## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Bird Island** is a small island in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") located southeast of the [Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grotto_\(Velen\) "Grotto \(Velen\)"). 

The island itself is uninhabited by people but is home to a few [foglets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foglet "Foglet"), causing the island to become shrouded in an unnatural fog. There is also a minor hidden treasure cache here with no associated quest.
