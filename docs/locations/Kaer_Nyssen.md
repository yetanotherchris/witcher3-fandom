## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Kaer Nyssen** is an old destroyed castle situated up a hill overlooking the destroyed village of [Boxholm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boxholm "Boxholm") and the nearby river. It lies east of [Fayrlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fayrlund "Fayrlund") and southeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig") in central [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

Around [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") it was home to a powerful [fiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend"), [Morvudd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvudd "Morvudd").

## Trivia

  * Kaer or Kêr means "city" in [Breton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Breton_language), while Ker means "city" or "fortress" in [Cornish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cornish_language). Caer means the same in [Welsh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Welsh_language).
