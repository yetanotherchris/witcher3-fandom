## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Bandits' Camp** , located southeast of [Boatmakers' Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boatmakers%27_Hut "Boatmakers' Hut"), is inhabited by renegades. There is also a [person in distress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Person\(s\)_in_distress "Person\(s\) in distress") here. Once rescued, the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Claywich\) "Merchant \(Claywich\)") will go to [Claywich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claywich "Claywich"), where you can then trade with him.
