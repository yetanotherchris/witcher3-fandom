## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **abandoned site** is located at a fishermen's hut west of [Hangman's Alley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hangman%27s_Alley "Hangman's Alley"). The camp is initially occupied by pirates. If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") liberates the camp, its inhabitants will return, including a [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Herbalist \(Spitfire Bluff\)"). 150[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") is awarded for liberating this site. 

## Notes

  * Before the site is liberated, a [letter from a pirate leader](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_from_a_pirate_leader "Letter from a pirate leader") can be found inside the hut, which however is quite hard to get due to the pirates constantly keeping Geralt in combat mode. It is replaced by a different [letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_\(The_Witcher_3\)#Letter_\(Hangman's_Alley\) "Letter \(The Witcher 3\)") when peaceful inhabitants return. 
    * The easiest way to get the pirates' letter version is to kill all but one of them, lure that last man inside the hut and then quickly loot the letter just as he dies and the liberation scene phases in.
