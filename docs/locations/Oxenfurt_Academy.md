**Oxenfurt Academy** is a renowned higher education facility located in the [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") city of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). Founded by [Nicodemus de Boot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nicodemus_de_Boot "Nicodemus de Boot"), its main academic rival is the [Imperial Academy in](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Academy "Imperial Academy") [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). 

Among its more famous alumni are [Shani](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shani "Shani") and [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion"). The former went on to become the dean of the Faculty of Medicine and Herbology, while the latter studied the seven liberal arts, graduating with _summa cum laude_ honors. Dandelion went on to lecture at the institution many years after his graduation. The [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") also attended some lectures at the university, such as astronomy.[1]

One of the academy's more notable achievements was the renovated [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") aqueduct, which served as an experimental sewage treatment plant, of dubious reputation. The [Philosophers' Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philosophers%27_Gate "Philosophers' Gate") guards the entrance, requiring one to sign into a guest book before entry.[2]

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

Even though it's one of most dominating sights of the city, the University does not happen to be a place visited in a secondary or main quest. This however changes with the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion. Also a number of professors from different faculties make their appearance. 

During the course of the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War") the University was closed, and most of its faculty and students conscripted into the ranks of the Redanian Army. Those students and professors involved with the Faculty of Medicine and Herbology were instead 'invited' by King [Radovid V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") to join the medics accompanying the army. The building is protected by [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") and soldiers.

## Trivia

  * It would appear that the inspiration for the Academy of Oxenfurt may be derived from the United Kingdom's oldest (medieval) universities: [Oxford](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Oxford) (c. 1096) and [Cambridge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Cambridge) (c. 1209), with both universities often referred together and abbreviated as "Oxbridge". Additionally, the coat of arms bear striking similarities to those of the University of Cambridge.
