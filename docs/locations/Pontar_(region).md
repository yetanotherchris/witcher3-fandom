# Pontar (region)

[![Map from ](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/a7/Map_Velen_HoS.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/293?cb=20171108054644)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/a7/Map_Velen_HoS.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20171108054644) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Map_Velen_HoS.jpg)

Map from _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

In _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , the **Pontar** refers to lands that cover the region of the [Pontar Delta](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_Delta "Pontar Delta"). The world map consists of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), the independent city of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), and southern parts of [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"), including the city of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). The game's rendition of the region excludes some locations from the [Witcher saga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_saga "Witcher saga"), namely [Acorn Bay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Acorn_Bay "Acorn Bay"), [Crane Islet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crane_Islet "Crane Islet"), the city of [Foam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foam "Foam"), and the settlement of [White Bridge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Bridge "White Bridge") (originally intended to appear). Although the in-game map labels two major regions as "Velen and Novigrad", both are contained on the same map in addition to portions of southern Redania. Its actually a super region consisting of these three.

## [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")

The former [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") province of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") (also known as No Man's Land) - currently under [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") occupation - also constitutes the largest part of the Pontar region. Situated south of Novigrad and west of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), its provisional capital is [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). The prevailing terrain consists of forests and swamps.
