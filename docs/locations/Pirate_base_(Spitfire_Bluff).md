## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This unmarked fortress ruin, located on the peninsula southwest of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp") and northwest of [Condyle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Condyle "Condyle") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), is the main base for pirates in the [Spitfire Bluff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spitfire_Bluff "Spitfire Bluff") area and their [leader](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate_Leader_\(Spitfire_Bluff\) "Pirate Leader \(Spitfire Bluff\)") resides here along with approximately 10 other pirates. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can find [Diagram: Griffin steel sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Griffin_steel_sword_-_enhanced "Diagram: Griffin steel sword - enhanced") here and he has to defeat the pirates and clear their [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") before a woman can appear at the nearby [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") site, in order to start the the [Love's Cruel Snares](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Love%27s_Cruel_Snares "Love's Cruel Snares") quest. 

After clearing the camp and some time, a couple of students will appear here to excavate the site.

## Notes

  * When killed, the Pirate Leader at this location drops [pirate leader's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate_leader%27s_notes "Pirate leader's notes").
  * Though the extent of the intended effects to clearing this location are unclear, it was meant to at least weaken the influence of pirates in the area, similarly to hanse bases in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. However, the concept wasn't fully developed for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ base game release.
