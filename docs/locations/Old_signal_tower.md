## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Old signal tower** is an abandoned tower in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is just south of [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") and contains a portal to a cave below it. Long abandoned, it's now home to [erynias](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia "Erynia").
