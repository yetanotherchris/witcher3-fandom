## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Isolated Shack** is a small, abandoned, hunting hut located to the west of [Heatherton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heatherton "Heatherton"), belonging to a hunter named [Niellen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Niellen "Niellen"). A [werewolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") has made its lair beneath the house away from civilization. 

## Map description

    _Small hut constructed by a famous sculptor who, having garnered every laurel possible for his trade, abandoned his[Koviri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir") residence and moved here in order to find inspiration in solitude and reflect on what to make of the rest of his life._
