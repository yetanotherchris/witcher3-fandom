**Nilfgaardian Garrison** is a location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## History

It once belonged to [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard")'s baronet, [Ignatius Verrieres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignatius_Verrieres "Ignatius Verrieres"), but when the baronet learned his son, [Florian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Florian_Verrieres "Florian Verrieres"), was a homosexual and in a relationship with [Mislav](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mislav "Mislav"), Ignatius banished Mislav from the castle. Upset at this, Florian then committed suicide and Ignatius turned to drinking, letting the fortress fall into a state of ruin. 

It was later used as a fortification by the [Temerian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Army "Temerian Army") to guard the [Ismena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ismena "Ismena") river and the road running from [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") to [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), but it fell to the [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") after their victory at the [battle of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_White_Orchard "Battle of White Orchard") in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). The garrison then served as an outpost for the Nilfgaardian commanding officer, [Captain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Captain "Captain") [Peter Saar Gwynleve](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peter_Saar_Gwynleve "Peter Saar Gwynleve").

## Notes:

  * There's a locked door that you can find the [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scratched_key#Scratched_key_\(White_Orchard\) "Scratched key") to on the ledge pictured. To get there, turn left inside the gate and go up until you see a ledge, drop off it and there will be a wall to Aard. Going through here will allow you to get to the hanging point and check a corpse for the key. Although you can unlock and go through the door, running of the ledge lands you in water near another loot chest.
  * Before being pointed here during the story, the gates to the garrison are locked and the quest-related characters haven't appeared yet. However, you can simply jump inside over a low wall near the staircase and still deal with the quartermaster.
