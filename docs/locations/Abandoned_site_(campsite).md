## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **abandoned site** is situated at a crossroads west of [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch") and is overrun by renegades. When liberated, 120[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") \+ 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") is awarded, and some refugees will move in along with a [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(campsite\) "Herbalist \(campsite\)") and [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(campsite\) "Merchant \(campsite\)"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Ghosts of the Past](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghosts_of_the_Past "Ghosts of the Past")

## Notes

  * This is the location where [Letho](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letho "Letho") will lead [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to during the [secondary](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") quest Ghosts of the Past. Regardless of its previous state, for the duration of the quest the place is occupied by [Louis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Louis_\(bandit\) "Louis \(bandit\)") and his band.
