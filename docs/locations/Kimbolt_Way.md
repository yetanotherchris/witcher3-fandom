**Kimbolt Way** is a road located in southeastern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") leading to the [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp"). 

## Map description

    _Road built on orders of Baron[Kimbolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ravanen_Kimbolt "Ravanen Kimbolt"), meant to act as a safe escape route in case his plans to take power after King [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest")'s death went awry._
