There are 19 temples, and many **shrines** devoted to the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire") located throughout [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad").[1] Places that are protected by the [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") and when the sun sets, the beggars tend to warm themselves at around the shrines.[2]
