## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Amavet Fortress Ruins** is an unmarked location in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") that sits atop a small plateau overlooking the [village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ransacked_Village "Ransacked Village") below. 

The ruins can only be entered by climbing and jumping onto rocky cliffs and building parts of the fortress. 

## History

It was used by a local lord called [Ignatius Verrieres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignatius_Verrieres "Ignatius Verrieres") who detained and interrogated the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Kolgrim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kolgrim "Kolgrim") of the [Viper School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_School "Viper School") over an alleged kidnapping of a [boy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitty "Vitty"). For unknown reasons, it was left neglected and is now in ruins. A group of bandits have later made [camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") there.
