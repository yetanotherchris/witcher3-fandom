**Rosemary and Thyme** is a brothel located in the [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane") district of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

It was originally owned and run by [Alonso Wiley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alonso_Wiley "Alonso Wiley"), better known as Whoreson Senior. Having been a patron of the arts and a fan of [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion")'s, Whoreson Senior left the business to the bard when he died. Dandelion then invited his friend [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") to run it together as a tavern. 

After Dandelion is saved from the [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard") by the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher"), he renames the establishment to the **Chameleon** and refurbishes it into a cabaret and tavern. The end result is determined by the player through earlier dialogue choices.

## Trivia

  * "Rosemary and thyme" is part of the lyrics of a traditional English ballad, Scarborough Fair.

## Notes

  * Dandelion claims that the name "Rosemary and Thyme" evokes images of [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") cuisine.
  * As of [Patch 1.07](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.07_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.07 \(The Witcher 3\)"), a [stash](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stash "Stash") can be found next to the front entrance.
