## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**The Kingfisher Inn** is the largest tavern establishment in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and is located in the northeast corner of [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square"), the center of the big city. [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla"), also known as Callonetta, is a popular performer there. 

Run by [Olivier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olivier "Olivier"), it offers [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"), fine wines, and an atmosphere devoid of talk of the current [pogrom](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pogrom "Pogrom") against sorcery. The [sewer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_sewers "Novigrad sewers") tunnels beneath the structure also make it the perfect place for extricating [mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage") from the city. It is named after a piece of [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion")'s poetry, as mentioned by Olivier.
