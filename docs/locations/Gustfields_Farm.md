## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gustfields Farm** is a farmstead in the [Gustfields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gustfields "Gustfields") region of [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"), located just north of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and west of the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut"). 

## Map description

    _Farmstead founded years ago by an eccentric flaxen-haired painter named Cunigund de Cabbrae, who came here seeking peace, quiet and fresh country air._
