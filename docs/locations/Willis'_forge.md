**Willis' forge** was the home and workshop owned and operated by the [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") [Willis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Willis "Willis") in the village of [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

## History

During the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III"), the forge was forcefully contracted to supply armor to [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") forces led by [Captain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Captain "Captain") [Peter Saar Gwynleve](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peter_Saar_Gwynleve "Peter Saar Gwynleve"), who had conquered the region. 

One night in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), a [drunk local](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Napp "Napp") set fire to the workshop, burning it to the ground. Willis lost most of his equipment, managing only to salvage an anvil, and some basic tools. He sought assistance with the local Nilfgaardian command, for whom the arson was a major strategic blow, and so supplies were ordered to rebuild the workshop. Meanwhile, Willis also asked [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") for help in tracking down the arsonist. The forge was eventually rebuilt.
