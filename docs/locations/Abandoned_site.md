# Abandoned site

[![Tw3 icon abandoned site](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/74/Tw3_icon_abandoned_site.svg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/50?cb=20220713040434)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/74/Tw3_icon_abandoned_site.svg/revision/latest?cb=20220713040434)

There are a number of **abandoned sites** scattered about the various regions in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , and its expansions, _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion and _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion. In general, abandoned sites are initially populated by hostile creatures or bandits, though once all enemies in a site are killed, it becomes repopulated by civilians. 

Liberating abandoned sites is rewarded usually with 120-170[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"), depending on the area, and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown")​. When a site is cleared of enemies, it will also get populated and, more often than not, some type of [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") becomes available at the location as well.

## Notes

  * All abandoned sites in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion are parts of [quests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_quests "Blood and Wine quests"), and liberating them doesn't yield [![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") or coin rewards.
  * When liberating an abandoned site in Toussaint, a different animation plays than in any other location of the game.

## Bugs

  * The animation that plays when liberating an abandoned site in Toussaint always has [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") sheathing his [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword"), even if he didn't use it during the fight.
