## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ancient Oak** is a tree located south of [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). The area surrounding it is feared by most of the local populace due to its history of dark rituals. 

The Ancient Oak is a centuries-old oak tree growing on a small hill, under which lies a system of caves that is the resting place of [the Ghost in the Tree](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Ghost_in_the_Tree "The Ghost in the Tree"). Many winding paths lead to the Oak from all directions, and the tree is somewhat separated from the rest of the surrounding forest by a clearing. 

The hillock on which the Oak grows is colloquially known as the Whispering Hillock due to the Ghost in the Tree continuously emitting a heartbeat-like sound from underneath it.
