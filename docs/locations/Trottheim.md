## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Trottheim** is one of the two villages located on the island of [Faroe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe "Faroe"). The local pirate captain [Hammond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hammond "Hammond") has established his gang here. 

## Map description

    _This village was once famed across all of[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") for the fact that it was inhabited exclusively by women, all their men having died during one raid or another._

## Notes

  * Freeing the slaves and killing all the pirates including Hammond and his guards will have the area repopulated by villagers and [Clan Dimun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Dimun "Clan Dimun") warriors.

## Trivia

  * The name is most likely taken from the name [Trondheim](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trondheim "wikipedia:Trondheim"), a large Norwegian city.
