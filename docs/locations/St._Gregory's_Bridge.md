## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**St. Gregory's Bridge** is a large, populated bridge linking [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") to the rest of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). 

## Map description

    _Bridge named after the[hero of Novigrad who saved the city](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Saint_Gregory "Saint Gregory") from a horrible famine three hundred years ago by sacrificing half his fortune to import food from [Nazair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nazair "Nazair"). After this, he was declared a saint, something even the jurors of the [Church of the Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Church_of_the_Eternal_Fire "Church of the Eternal Fire") were unable to change._
