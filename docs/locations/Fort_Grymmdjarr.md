## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Fort Grymmdjarr** is an old ruined fortress southwest of [Fyresdal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyresdal "Fyresdal") in southern [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") and named after [Grymmdjarr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grymmdjarr "Grymmdjarr"), the founder of [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

It is now home to a [harpy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy "Harpy") nest, though it's also the temporary camp for thieves during [The Family Blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Family_Blade "The Family Blade"), during which the harpies won't appear, but the nest can still be destroyed.
