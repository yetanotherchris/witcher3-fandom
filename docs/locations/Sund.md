## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Sund** is a village located on the cliffs overlooking the western bay of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") between [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn") and [Fayrlund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fayrlund "Fayrlund"). 

## Map description

    _Small village whose inhabitants are for the most part shepherds._

## Trivia

  * Sund means "Strait" in all of the North Germanic languages.
  * In real life, there is a village called [Sund](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sund,_Faroe_Islands) in the [Faroe Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Faroe_Islands).
