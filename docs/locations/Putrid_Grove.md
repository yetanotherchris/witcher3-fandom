**Putrid Grove** is an enclave within the district of [Lacehalls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lacehalls "Lacehalls") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). It is ruled by the King of Beggars, [Francis Bedlam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Francis_Bedlam "Francis Bedlam"), and serves as the headquarters for his gang, the [Blindeyes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blindeyes "Blindeyes"). There, magic users have found sanctuary from the ongoing witch hunt perpetrated by the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") and the [Temple Guard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Guard "Temple Guard").
