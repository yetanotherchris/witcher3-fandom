## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ransacked Village** is a burned down settlement in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

## Map description

    _A band of[soldiers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") attacked this village early one morning. They slaughtered most of the villagers in their beds, and the lucky few who fled to the woods in time had nothing left to return to._

## Notes

  * At the press demo shown at E3 2013, the village was in the midst of burning down upon [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir")'s first arrival during the main quest [Lilac and Gooseberries](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lilac_and_Gooseberries "Lilac and Gooseberries"). Even then the developers admitted they would probably change the village later.
