## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Martin Feuille's Farmstead** is a large plantation it is located at the end of the road which forks to the south, east of [Winespring Grange](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Winespring_Grange "Winespring Grange"). It is the northeasternmost notable location around [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") if you don't have the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion. 

It also includes two pastures for about 30 [sheep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sheep "Sheep") and 16 [cows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cow "Cow"). Another two cows can be seen standing in a shelter below, but they cannot be killed. 

## Map description

    _Founded by Lord Martin Feuille, this vast plantation was until not so long ago the largest producer of alfalfa in the region. Sadly, when the war broke out the lord fled to his winter residence in[Kovir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir "Kovir"), leaving his land to be administered by an ill-suited steward who squandered his liege's fertile fields._

## Notes

  * This is one of very few notable places in the game without any related quests, points of interest, enemies or even a small note to read and learn more from. Only a lot of beer and wines in the cellar.
