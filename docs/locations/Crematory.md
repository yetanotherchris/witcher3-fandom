## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Crematory** is a building north of the [Oxenfurt Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Gate "Oxenfurt Gate") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and is used by the city as a location for burning corpses infected by the [Catriona plague](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Catriona_plague "Catriona plague"). A [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") [alchemist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist_\(Crematory\) "Alchemist \(Crematory\)") set up shop there in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). He is a [gwent player](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent_players "Gwent players"), using the [Nilfgaardian Empire deck](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire_Gwent_deck "Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck").
