## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Midcopse** is a small village, that consists of eight buildings, located in the western area of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). It [houses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz%27s_hut "Keira Metz's hut") a witch that helps cures ailments and the woes of the villagers. 

## Map description

    _Typical farming settlement which the worst of the fighting has left untouched - but which famine now grips all the same. One of the larger villages in this region._
