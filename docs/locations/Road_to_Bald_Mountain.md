## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Road to Bald Mountain** is both a village and a long and treacherous path leading to the infamous [Bald Mountain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(location\) "Bald Mountain \(location\)"). 

If visited before the [main story line](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(quest\) "Bald Mountain \(quest\)"), many [foglets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foglet "Foglet") can be found prowling the area. Once the story quest is reached, however, it will be a regular village with services available to Geralt. 

## Map description

    _The peasants of Velen believe the summit of Bald Mountain is home to witches,[werebbubbs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werebbubb "Werebbubb") and [wights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wight_\(creature\) "Wight \(creature\)")._
