## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Condyle** is a small, ransacked village in western [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). It is accessible via the road leading southwest from [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"), and also has a [fast travel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fast_travel "Fast travel") marker. 

The village and its surrounding area is inhabited by cannibals, [feared even by pirates to the north](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unsent_letter#Unsent_letter_\(boatyard_pirates\) "Unsent letter"), and they've established multiple [bandit camps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") in the area, with their main base just east of the village. 

After clearing the village and some time, villagers include a local herbalist will return. 

## Map description

    _This village has been completely and utterly destroyed. Rumors claim its inhabitants perished in a gruesome massacre._
