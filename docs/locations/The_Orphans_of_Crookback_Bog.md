## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**The Orphans of Crookback Bog** is the location of a small village in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , located in the middle of [Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crookback_Bog "Crookback Bog") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"). It houses: the powerful [Ladies of the Wood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ladies_of_the_Wood "Ladies of the Wood"), [Phillip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s [wife](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Anna_Strenger "Anna Strenger"), and many orphaned children. 

## Map description

_Orphaned and unwanted children from nearby villages find a roof over their heads and a bowl of warm food here._
