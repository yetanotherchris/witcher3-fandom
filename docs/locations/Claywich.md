**Claywich** is a small village in southwest [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") situated to the north of [Oreton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oreton "Oreton"). 

## Map description

    _Every year at[Belleteyn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Belleteyn "Belleteyn"), a great feast is held in Claywich accompanied by games, song and dance. On that night villagers from far and wide come to celebrate, with passing travelers welcome as well. Shortly before midnight the youth in attendance race deep into the forest in search of a fern flower, and though no one has as yet found one, many have found their other halves, or at least a night of moonlight passion._
