### Games

**Lighthouse** located to the southwest of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), is a location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## Map description

    _Many years ago a horrible accident took place here: a ship carrying the cousin of[King Radovid IV the Bald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_IV "Radovid IV") wrecked against the rocks during a storm. The king ordered a lighthouse erected on that spot in order to warn other seafarers of its deadly dangers._
