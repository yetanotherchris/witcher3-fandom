## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gedyneith** is a sacred oak tree within a grove located in the northeastern forests of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). Most [druids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") live here, where they worship [Freya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Freya "Freya") and practice their crafts in peace. 

It's northeast of the [Abandoned Sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Sawmill_\(location\) "Abandoned Sawmill \(location\)") and southwest of the [Whale Graveyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale_Graveyard "Whale Graveyard"), and has two signposts located in the eastern and western sides of the grove. 

The grove hosts a number of grottoes tunneled on the side of the hill where the tree stands to serve as dwellings for the druids. The place is also the headquarters of [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")'s [hierophant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierophant "Hierophant"). 

## Map description

    _An oak which is sacred to all Skelligers, as it is worshiped both by druids and Freya's disciples. The Isle's most important ceremonies are held here, including weddings and royal coronations._
