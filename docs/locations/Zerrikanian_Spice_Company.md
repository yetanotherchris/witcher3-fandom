# Zerrikanian Spice Company

[![Tw3 zerrikanian spice company](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/4c/Tw3_zerrikanian_spice_company.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20170829234551)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/4c/Tw3_zerrikanian_spice_company.png/revision/latest?cb=20170829234551) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw3_zerrikanian_spice_company.png)

**Zerrikanian Spice Company** is one of the biggest importer of spices from [Zerrikania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikania "Zerrikania"), a wild and far land situated beyond the [Korath desert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Korath_desert "Korath desert"). The company's headquarters is situated in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad").
