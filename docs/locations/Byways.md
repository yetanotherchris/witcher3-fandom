## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Byways** is a mostly abandoned village in southwestern [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") which is now overrun by high-level [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") and [alghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul"). 

## Map description

    _Most of this area's residents have fled north or died of[plague](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Catriona_plague "Catriona plague"). In better times they busied themselves making prize-winning bricks._
