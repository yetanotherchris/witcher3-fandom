## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Temerian Partisan Hideout** is the headquarters of what remains of [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria")'s army and is led by [Vernon Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernon_Roche "Vernon Roche"). It is an open ceiling cave located just southeast of the [Vegelbud Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vegelbud_Residence "Vegelbud Residence") and north-northwest of the [Herbalist's Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist%27s_Hut "Herbalist's Hut"). 

## Map description

    _Though the[Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") thought Temeria died along with [King Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_Foltest "King Foltest"), Temerian guerillas still hide in the woods, prepared to give their lives at a moment's notice in their fight for independence_
