## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Honorton** is an unmarked village in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and a location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is a village situated to the east of [Claywich](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claywich "Claywich") and west of the [Boatmakers' Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Boatmakers%27_Hut "Boatmakers' Hut").
