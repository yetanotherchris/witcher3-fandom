## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Palace of Awakening** , formerly the **Moon Palace** , is a royal palace and the headquarters of [Viceroy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viceroy "Viceroy") [Ge'els](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ge%27els "Ge'els"). It resides in the core of the [Aen Elle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Elle "Aen Elle") world within its capital, [Tir ná Lia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tir_n%C3%A1_Lia "Tir ná Lia"), overlooking the [Easnadh](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Easnadh "Easnadh") river.
