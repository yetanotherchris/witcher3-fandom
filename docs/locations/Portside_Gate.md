## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Portside Gate** is one of six gates in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), this one named due to its proximity to the [Novigrad Docks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad_Docks "Novigrad Docks"). It connects the [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") district to [Harborside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harborside "Harborside"). 

## Map description

    _Though not the most stately of gates, this one's location near the bustling port has made it the calling card of the city._
