## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Yngvar's Fang** , also known as **Mount Aardeklove** , is the name of the highest mountain found on the island of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"). It is the location of one of the [Place of Power](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Place_of_Power "Place of Power") dedicated to [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen"). A glorious fortress [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach") once stood on way to peak watching over [Clan Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tuirseach "Clan Tuirseach")'s dominion but [Nilfgaardians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") eventually destroyed it. 

## Map description

    _This mountain peak was named after the[mythical bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yngvar "Yngvar") which, according to legend, was defeated by [Tyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tyr "Tyr"), the heroic founder of Clan Tuirsearch._
