## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Mowshurst** was a destroyed and now abandoned village in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), northeast from [Lurtch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lurtch "Lurtch"). It has no [signpost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signpost "Signpost") or unique map description, though it does have a hidden treasure marker.
