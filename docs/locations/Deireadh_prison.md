## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Deireadh prison** is a very well guarded prison in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). It is rumored that no one sentenced to Deireadh gets out alive. 

The prison appears for the first time during the quest when [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") and (optionally) [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") will help you to rescue [Margarita](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margarita_Laux-Antille "Margarita Laux-Antille"). During that time the [Prison Warden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prison_Warden "Prison Warden") and garrison here is made primarily of [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") and a few [Redanians](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army"). [Sheala de Tancarville](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sheala_de_Tancarville "Sheala de Tancarville") is also incarcerated in this prison. 

## Description

_An infamous jail, said to be built atop an ancient[elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") catacombs, and now the main garrison for the [Redanian soldiers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army") in Oxenfurt. [Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s rivals are said to be kept in squalid conditions below ground, including sorceresses he wishes to keep alive, rather than impale._[1]
