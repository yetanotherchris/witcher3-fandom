## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Tretogor Gate** is one of the six gates leading into [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), this one being named after the capital city of [Redania](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania"), [Tretogor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tretogor "Tretogor"). It's one of two gates that connects the [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") district to [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits"). 

## Map description

    _Gate erected with funds from the[Redanian royal family](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redanian_Dynasty "Redanian Dynasty"), who, wanting to earn favor with Novigrad merchants and the [hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch "Hierarch"), dedicated a significant amount of coin to its construction, as well as some no-less-valuable (wo)manpower in the form of the master architect Countess Anna Yaye-Pinkovitz and her skilled crew._
