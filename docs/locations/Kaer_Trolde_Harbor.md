## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Kaer Trolde Harbor** is a sprawling hub of trade in [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig") which hosts merchants from Nordling [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), as well as the [Kovir and Poviss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kovir_and_Poviss "Kovir and Poviss") and the other Islanders from rest of archipelago. Place also serves as dockyard for [Jarl of Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jarl_of_Skellige "Jarl of Skellige")'s fleet with many boatwrights working day and night on longships. 

## Map description

    _One of the busiest ports in Skellige. Goods from every corner of the world are brought here to be sold and traded._
