## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Marlin Coast** is a small abandoned settlement located on the northern shores of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"). It was later used as a makeshift camp by the [Nilfgaardian Empire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") during their attack on [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). 

## Map description

_Until quite recently this beach was frequented by fishermen come to fish marlins out of the nearby waters._

  * During the opening stages of [On Thin Ice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/On_Thin_Ice "On Thin Ice"), a quartermaster capable of buying and selling goods and crafting weapons is present. Due to a bug, the icon for the quartermaster will remain in place after the end of the game, even though he's no longer available.
