### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ford** is a river crossing located in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") that crosses two small tributaries of the [Ismena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ismena "Ismena") river, forming part of the road west of the village. 

## History

Upon their arrival to the region, [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") first encountered the [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Griffin "Royal Griffin") terrorizing White Orchard here in May [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"). As they approached, the griffin was devouring a [horse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse "Horse") that belonged to [Bram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bram "Bram"), whose cart had been attacked a short time prior but thankfully the [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") successfully drove the monster off before it found Bram. 

Bram then thanked the witchers for their help. However, unable to fix his broken cart, he abandoned it and took what he could carry to White Orchard village.
