## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Forest Hut** is a small abandoned cabin located north of [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). Several [insectoids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid "Insectoid") are living close to the hut. 

Its former inhabitants all died in mysterious ways.
