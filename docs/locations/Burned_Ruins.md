## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Burned Ruins** can be found just east of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). 

The powerful [Shrieker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shrieker "Shrieker") has established its lair inside a cave located near this place. It is susceptible to the [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") sign, but is still very deadly. You can find the Enhanced Griffin School Trousers Diagram in the building, under some rubble. 

## Map description

## Notes

  * It is possible to climb into the smaller enclosed room in the ruins, however there is no way to climb out. In this enclosed space you will find a hole in the ground from which it's presumed Shrieker exited the cave during the contract, however it is not possible to enter the cave from this hole.
