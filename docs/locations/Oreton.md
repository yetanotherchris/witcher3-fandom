## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Oreton** is a small harbor village situated in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and is under [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") control. 

## Map description

    _Village founded by[Count](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count "Count") [Primislavus don Stessa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Primisalvus_don_Stessa "Primisalvus don Stessa"), distant cousin to [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest") of [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria"). The count was known for his passion for racing chariots down winding forest paths and narrow country roads. This spectacle delighted onlookers, won the hearts of highborn ladies and aroused hatred in his rivals._
