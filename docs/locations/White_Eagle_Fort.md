## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**White Eagle Fort** is a makeshift camp built by [Trollololo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trollololo "Trollololo"), a "recruited" [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldier and [troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll "Troll"). 

## Map description

    _The grand name might seem in ill-fitting with this place, but the troll that lives here - a Redanian patriot and military aficionado - goes to great lengths to make his beloved King[Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V") proud._
