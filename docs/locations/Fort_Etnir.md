### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Fort Etnir** is an old ruined fortress north of [Rogne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rogne "Rogne") and east of [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") in northern [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). It's currently home to an [ice elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_Elemental "Ice Elemental") and some [gargoyles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gargoyle "Gargoyle"). 

## History

The once-proud fortress used to be a steadfast guardian of the northern expanses of Ard Skellig before falling to ruin and is presumably the final resting place of Chalimir's body after the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Gerd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gerd_\(witcher\) "Gerd \(witcher\)") brought what was once the ruins' roof down on them. 

The ruined fort also used to be the lair of a [striga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Striga "Striga") before Gerd slew it on orders of [Torgeir the Red](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Torgeir_the_Red "Torgeir the Red"), the Jarl of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig") at the time.
