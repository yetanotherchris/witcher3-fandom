# Place of Power

See: [Intersection](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Intersection "Intersection")

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

[![Place of Power](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/4d/Place_of_Power.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20080407134941)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/4/4d/Place_of_Power.png/revision/latest?cb=20080407134941 "Place of Power")Place of Power

In _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ , **Places of Power** are (nearly) invisible spots where magic is concentrated. They are _not_ [Circles of Elements](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Circle_of_Elements "Circle of Elements"). Unlike those, Places of Power are practically invisible until [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") approaches them. There is an audible roaring sound when he gets close enough to them which is also a clue to finding them. And finally, if the [witcher medallion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_medallion "Witcher medallion") is set to detect magic, it will vibrate near the Places of Power. 

    [![map marker](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/73/Marker_Place_of_Power.png/revision/latest?cb=20081115175705)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/73/Marker_Place_of_Power.png/revision/latest?cb=20081115175705 "map marker") These places of power are indicated on maps with a blue dot.

### Mechanics:

  * Left-click on the blue light source to activate a Place of Power.
  * Choose the action you want to perform from the list of available actions, initially there is only the _Ritual of Magic_.

There are Places of power in all locations of the game, except [Kaer Morhen keep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), in the Prologue. 

### Locations:

  * [Outskirts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Outskirts "Outskirts"): outside the crypt, one inside the crypt and a third between the Salamandra hideout and the southern cave.
  * [Sewers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima_sewers "Vizima sewers"): outside the fisstech lab, one where Gellert Bleinheim waits and in the tunnel leading to the elven ruins.
  * [Swamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp "Swamp"): on a small island just east of Wyvern Island, on a small island just west of the brickmakers' village, and at the foot of Melitele's shrine.
  * [Lakeside](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lakeside "Lakeside"): one near the lakeside south of the Fisher King's hut, another at western exit from the Lakeside to the Fields.
  * [Murky Waters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Murky_Waters "Murky Waters"): one outside, east of the Country Inn, up a small hill.
  * [Fields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fields "Fields"): north of the hermit's compound.
  * [Swamp cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_cemetery "Swamp cemetery"): not far from the old mine, and also in Saint Gregory's tomb chamber.
  * [Old Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Manor "Old Manor"): by the shipwreck on the way to the landing.
  * _[The Price of Neutrality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Price_of_Neutrality "The Price of Neutrality")_ premium module: one near the wyverns and one northwest of the camp.

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

[![Tw3 icon place of power](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/8/82/Tw3_icon_place_of_power.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/29?cb=20170901043602)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/8/82/Tw3_icon_place_of_power.png/revision/latest?cb=20170901043602) [![Tw3 icon place of power](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/8/82/Tw3_icon_place_of_power.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/29?cb=20170901043602)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/8/82/Tw3_icon_place_of_power.png/revision/latest?cb=20170901043602)

In the third game places of power return and will grant [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") a Skill Point if their locations are found and then activated. It is important to find these as there are a limited number of Skill Points in the game. Each Place of Power is tied to one of the 5 [Signs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Signs "Signs") and will increase that Sign's intensity when drawn from. 

### Locations

[White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") (Six Places) 

  * East of the [Abandoned Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_Village_\(White_Orchard\) "Abandoned Village \(White Orchard\)"), a [Bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") awaits nearby (Yrden)
  * Southwest of the Abandoned Village (Quen)
  * Northeast of the [Sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(White_Orchard\) "Sawmill \(White Orchard\)") (Axii)
  * North of the [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill_\(White_Orchard\) "Mill \(White Orchard\)"), in front of a crypt, a [Wraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith") will spawn upon first time approaching (Igni)
  * Even further north from the Place of Power by the crypt (Aard)
  * North of the [Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossroads_\(White_Orchard\) "Crossroads \(White Orchard\)"), just next to a [ghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") nest (Quen)

[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")/[Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") (Eight Places) 

  * Just southeast of [Alness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alness "Alness"). (Axii)
  * Near the summit of Bald Mountain, by a path beneath the giant tree. This Place of Power is only accessible during or after a [quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bald_Mountain_\(quest\) "Bald Mountain \(quest\)"). The marker on map is northwest of [Destroyed Bastion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Destroyed_Bastion "Destroyed Bastion"). (Quen)
  * South of [Frischlow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Frischlow "Frischlow"), near the coast. (Aard)
  * Just west of the [Ancient Oak](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Oak "Ancient Oak"). (Igni)
  * In a path to a treasure room to the left after finishing the 4 guardians puzzle during the [Magic Lamp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Lamp_\(quest\) "Magic Lamp \(quest\)") side quest. Keira cannot follow you here. To access it later, go to the Boat icon on the map northeast of [Byways](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Byways "Byways"), to find the cave entrance. (Yrden)
  * [Lornruk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lornruk "Lornruk"), at the base of the fort by the waters, guarded by several [Drowner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner")s. (Quen)
  * Northeast of [Electors' Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Electors%27_Square "Electors' Square") signpost in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), within the temple, near the main brazier by cliff's edge. (Igni)
  * Northwest of [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren"), atop a hill that mounds above [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto"). (Yrden)

[Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") (Nine Places) 

  * Southwest of Crossroads, in the water. (Axii)
  * By the mountain path northwest of [Kaer Muire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Muire "Kaer Muire") (Axii)
  * Inside the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt"), only accessible during or after [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone") main quest. (Igni)
  * In a tunnel connected to [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde") cellar. The marker on the map is northwest of [Bridge to Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bridge_to_Kaer_Trolde "Bridge to Kaer Trolde") signpost. This particular Place of Power is only accessible when and if player help [Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite") during a certain [quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"). (Quen)
  * Easternmost road of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), on the second level of a ruined building. Closest marker on the map is [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith") to the west. (Aard)
  * Slightly north of [Druids Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_Camp "Druids' Camp") (Yrden)
  * At the summit of [Yngvar's Fang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yngvar%27s_Fang "Yngvar's Fang") (Quen)
  * West of [Harviken](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harviken "Harviken"), by an altar. (Yrden)
  * Northwest [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog"), near Udalryk's Family Home. Players will have to remove some debris inside one of the rooms on the main floor and jump to the other side through a destroyed wall. (Igni)

[Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen")

  * Encountered during the quest [The Final Trial](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Final_Trial "The Final Trial"), by a pathway that stretch from the exit of the first cavern (the one housing [Old Speartip](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Speartip "Old Speartip")) towards [Circle of Elements](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Circle_of_Elements "Circle of Elements"). Because there's no notice board in Kaer Morhen, this Place of Power cannot be marked on the map until discovered. (Igni)

[Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint") (five places) 

  * Inside Albertus Grotto (entrance is North of [Rioux-Cannes Outpost](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rioux-Cannes_Outpost "Rioux-Cannes Outpost")), at the end of a path that branches to the cavern's southeastern edge (Axii)
  * Southern shore of Seidhe Llygad (the lake southwest of [Beauclair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beauclair "Beauclair")). The closest signpost is [Hortense Vineyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hortense_Vineyard "Hortense Vineyard") to the east. (Aard)
  * North of [Arthach Palace Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arthach_Palace_Ruins "Arthach Palace Ruins"), guarded by a relatively strong necrophage. (Yrden)
  * In a hollowed space of the rocky hills in which [Fort Ussar Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fort_Ussar_Ruins "Fort Ussar Ruins") lies on top. This spot can be reached from the western entry path to the ruins. (Igni)
  * Behind a cracked wall in a hidden chamber of [Termes Palace Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Termes_Palace_Ruins "Termes Palace Ruins"). The chamber lies south from the entrance to the dungeon where diagrams for Grandmaster Wolven gear are kept. (Quen)

### Other

  * In the quest [Through Time and Space](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Through_Time_and_Space "Through Time and Space") there is a missable Aard place of power in the world of vapors. If it is missed, the ability point granted by it is lost.

### Notes

  * Ability points gained from places of power carry over to [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") while the places of power reset. Thus, you can gain more ability points from them on another playthrough.
