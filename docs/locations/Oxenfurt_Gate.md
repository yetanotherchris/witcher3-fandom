**Oxenfurt Gate** is one of six gates leading into the city of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). It is named after another [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") city: [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). The gate is the most northward one located on the east side of the city. 

## Map description

    _In the times when Novigrad and Oxenfurt were embroiled in fierce neighborly disputes, this gate went through several completely different names, the Gate of Harlots and the Gate of Bloodsuckers being two of the longer lived examples. Its current name was chosen when this conflict was finally put to rest._
