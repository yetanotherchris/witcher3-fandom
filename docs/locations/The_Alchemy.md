## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_  
 _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion

**The Alchemy** is an inn located in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. The innkeeper, [Stjepan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stjepan "Stjepan"), will play [Gwent](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwent "Gwent"). 

During _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion, the [Redanian Free Company](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redanian_Free_Company "Redanian Free Company") (also known as the Wild Ones) reside here for a time with their [ataman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ataman "Ataman") [Olgierd von Everec](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olgierd_von_Everec "Olgierd von Everec").
