## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gustfields**[1] is a [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") region next to [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and [Pontar Delta](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_Delta "Pontar Delta"). It seems that famous and rich families like Garins, [Everecs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/House_of_Everec "House of Everec") or Moldavians, have their estates here. The land is characterized by swaying wheat fields and lazy villages, it looks like it came out of a painting. In the distance the walls and smog of Novigrad are visible. South of Gustfields lies [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") province of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), which is a stark contrast with its bleak landscape.
