**Ddiddiwedht** is a desert that forms part of an arid [world](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Multiverse#Known_worlds "Multiverse") inhabited by hardy shrubs, [sandcrabs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sandcrab "Sandcrab") and other desert creatures capable of surviving months without water. 

## History

Eons ago, Ddiddiwedht used to be a seabed of a sapient ocean. According to [Avallac'h](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Avallac%27h "Avallac'h"), while the universe teems with life, even lifeforms much more intelligent than [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") can exhaust all natural resources of a planet to the last drop, which is what eventually occured on Ddiddiwedht. 

At one point in Ddiddiwedht's history, [elves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") erected structures there, including a [portal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Portal "Portal") to other worlds. 

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), while attempting to reach the world of the [Aen Elle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aen_Elle "Aen Elle"), [Geralt of Rivia](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") and Avallac'h accidently landed in the desert before being attacked by sandcrabs. Avallac'h then quickly activated the portal and the pair continued on.
