## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Cavern** is a location in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , being just northeast of [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle"), and is inhabited by a powerful [golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem"). 

One can also use [The Eye of Nehaleni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Eye_of_Nehaleni "The Eye of Nehaleni") to find a hidden room with the remains of an alchemist and his assistant, as well as the [alchemist's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist%27s_notes "Alchemist's notes"). 

## Map description

## Notes

  * The hidden room contains a second hidden alcove that can be seen on the map, but its entrance doesn't glow using [Witcher Senses](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_Senses "Witcher Senses"). An [Aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard") symbol is depicted on the wall beside where the opening is concealed and a wall must be destroyed there. Behind it are a few more containers on of which has random diagrams.
  * A set of Tracker's [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Tracker%27s_boots "Diagram: Tracker's boots"), [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Tracker%27s_gauntlets "Diagram: Tracker's gauntlets"), [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Tracker%27s_trousers "Diagram: Tracker's trousers") and [Shiadhal's armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Shiadhal%27s_armor "Diagram: Shiadhal's armor") diagrams were all supposed to be found as themed loot in one of the two iron chests inside the hidden room, but both have been randomized instead.
