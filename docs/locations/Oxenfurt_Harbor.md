**Oxenfurt Harbor** is the dock area of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"). 

## History

In the [1260s](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1260 "Timeline"), [Myhrman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myhrman "Myhrman") ran an illegal store in the docks, selling medical items to people such as [Shani](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shani "Shani") and [Rience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rience "Rience"). It ended in [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1267 "Timeline"), when [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart") tortured him for information.[1]

In [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), [King Radovid](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Radovid_V "Radovid V")'s [galley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/HMS_Oxenfurt-Tretogor "HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor") docked there, heavily guarded by his men. Two other ships were also moored at its gangplanks.[2]
