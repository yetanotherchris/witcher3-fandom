## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Southern Gate** is one of six gates in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). In particular, this one is located between two districts: [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") and [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners"). 

## Map description

    _In truth demarcating the eastern and not southern edge of the city, the Southern Gate was given its inappropriate name by a one-time city planner who knew nothing about architecture and could not read a map, but had in his favor the fact that he was the mayor's cousin and thew lavish parties. Though confusingly incorrect, the name stuck and now the city's residents never think twice about its illogical appellation._
