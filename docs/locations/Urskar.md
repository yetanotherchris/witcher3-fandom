## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Urskar** is one of the three villages located on the island of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"), on the shores of a big lake at the center of the island. It was destroyed after the [ice giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_giant "Ice giant") [Myrhyff](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myrhyff "Myrhyff") appeared.[1] Southeast of this village is the [Clan Tordarroch Forge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tordarroch_Forge "Clan Tordarroch Forge").
