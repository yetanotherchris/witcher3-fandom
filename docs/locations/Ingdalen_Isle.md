## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ingdalen Isle** is an island split in two by a perfectly circular crater. 

Both islets are forested and the atoll-like strait in between them is home to a host of [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren") and [ekhidnae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna "Ekhidna"). The strait itself is a large undersea crater formed from the effects of [teleportation](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Teleportation "Teleportation"). A ship wreck also lies amidst its seafloor which could very much be the ship of the famed [djinn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Djinn "Djinn") specialist, Amos var Ypsis.
