**Ruined Watchtower** is an abandoned tower in the [Kaer Morhen valley](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen_valley "Kaer Morhen valley"), located north of the [main keep](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), next to a large lake. 

## History

It once housed a workshop, where [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") gear diagrams were drawn up, by two men named Chird and his master Hieronymus. One day however, the tower was stormed by a group of peasants armed with farm tools, and the gates were barricaded, though the peasants broke through and killed the two. 

## Map description

    _This lakeside watchtower was once the pride of the[Wolf School](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School "Wolf School") witchers, yet today lies in ruins, the only sound the wind whistling past its stones._

## Notes

  * The road north from the tower leads up to an impassable blockade of [Old Speartip](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_Speartip "Old Speartip")'s cave. There's nothing to do there, except hunt [wolves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_\(creature\) "Wolf \(creature\)") and [nekkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker") in the surrounding woods.
  * Far south from the tower, there is a guarded treasure location that always gives a random relic item, guarded by a [Leshen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshy "Leshy").
