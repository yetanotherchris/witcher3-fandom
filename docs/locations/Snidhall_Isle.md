## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Snidhall Isle** is a small island north of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

This island is home to a powerful [archgriffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin "Archgriffin"), guarding a hidden treasure for the [treasure hunt quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_treasure_hunts "The Witcher 3 treasure hunts"), [Unlucky's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unlucky%27s_Treasure "Unlucky's Treasure"). It is also where [Ivar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ivar "Ivar") from the quest, [Free Spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Free_Spirit "Free Spirit"), can be found. 

## Associated quests

  * [Unlucky's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unlucky%27s_Treasure "Unlucky's Treasure")

## Notes

  * The archgriffin here is considered to be the highest level enemy in the base game.
