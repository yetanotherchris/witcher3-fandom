## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Electors' Square** is a popular square located in the middle of [Temple Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temple_Isle "Temple Isle") near [Grand Picket](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grand_Picket "Grand Picket") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and named in honor of the [Electoral College](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Electoral_College "Electoral College"), who select a new [hierarch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch "Hierarch") from amongst them. 

## Map description

    _Square named after a group of Novigrad reformers who enacted bold transformations that led to the city's rapid growth, enriching its residents considerably and ushering in the city's golden age._
