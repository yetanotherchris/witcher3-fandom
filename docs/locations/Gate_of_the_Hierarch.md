## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Gate of the Hierarch** is one of six gates in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and named after the [leader](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch "Hierarch") of the [Eternal Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Fire "Eternal Fire"). It's one of two gates that connect the [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") district with [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane"). 

## Map description

    _This gate is named in honor of Novigrad's own son, the hierarch of the Eternal Fire. Supposedly this name was given to it upon popular request, though no one can be found who remembers requesting any such thing._
