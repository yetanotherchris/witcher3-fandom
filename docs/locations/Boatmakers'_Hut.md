## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Boatmakers' Hut** is a small abandoned site southeast of [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch") and is currently overrun by [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul"). After liberating it, villagers will re-populate the area and a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Boatmakers%27_Hut\) "Merchant \(Boatmakers' Hut\)") arrives with them as well. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") receives 120[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") and 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") for liberating the site, in addition to 20[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") for destroying the [monster nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and a possible 75[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") for killings its "boss" monster. 

## Map description

    _Though nothing about this small domicile is particularly eye-catching, a family of the best shipwrights in[Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") has lived here for generations, crafting the finest skiffs and dinghies north of the [Yaruga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yaruga "Yaruga")._
