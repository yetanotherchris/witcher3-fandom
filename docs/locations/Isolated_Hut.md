## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Isolated Hut** , located east of [Yantra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yantra "Yantra"), is home to a group comprising [dwarf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") and [halfling](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Halfling "Halfling") counterfeit painters. 

## Map description
