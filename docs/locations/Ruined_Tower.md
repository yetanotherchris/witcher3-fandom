## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Ruined Tower** has been abandoned for years and is located south of [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren") in [Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crookback_Bog "Crookback Bog"). It is now home to various wandering [elementals](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa "Elementa"). 

In the east corner of the ruins there is a chest that contains the diagram for [Ursine steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_steel_sword_-_superior "Ursine steel sword - superior"). 

## Map description

    _Five centuries ago King[Geddes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geddes "Geddes") sent his most loyal knight, [Martin of Oakdale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Martin_of_Oakdale "Martin of Oakdale"), to watch over the inhabitants of this troubled land. He also sent his least-loyal knight, to scrub Martin's latrines._
