## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Glory Gate** is one of six gates in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") and one of two that connects [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") district to [Glory Lane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glory_Lane "Glory Lane"). 

## Map description

    _Toughs and hooligans often end a night of drunken escapades under this gate after being thrown out of the nearby taverns._
