**Crow's Perch** is a palisade hold in the [Temerian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria") province of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen"), formerly owned by the [Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baron "Baron") [Vserad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vserad "Vserad") and presently occupied by [Phillip Strenger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger") (known as the Bloody Baron), and his men. 

It is a small community of maybe 50 peasants, who eked out a living before the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_War_III "Northern War III") enveloped the [North](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdom "Northern Kingdom"), and have only just clung on after. As former Baron Vserad's seat, the village itself benefits from the protection of a low moat and palisade wall, while one can only gain access to the town itself by riding or walking across a shoddily constructed wooden bridge (built in the proudest Velen style). 

Vserad and his own kin enjoyed a modest keep. Built mostly in red brick and situated at the very top of the hill, it afforded the baron a picturesque view of his lands in happier times and ample warning in more dangerous ones. 

## Map description

    _After Vserad, its previous owner, panicked at the news that armies were approaching and fled to[Fyke Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fyke_Isle "Fyke Isle"), this castle became home to Phillip Strenger, alias the Bloody Baron, along with his family and entourage._
