**Ursten** is a village located northwest of the [Border Post](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Border_Post "Border Post"). 

## Map description

    _War has caused countless refugees to flee[Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria"). With the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)") blockaded, they have tended to flood villages which, like Ursten, are located close to river crossings._
