## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Abandoned Village** , located in the northern part of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"), northwest of [Urskar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Urskar "Urskar"), has been ravaged by the [Ice Giant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Myrhyff "Myrhyff") after he woke from his slumber. 

## Map description

    _This village's residents were forced to abandon it in a hurry when the Ice Giant unexpectedly awoke and decided to make known his wrath._
