## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**The Bits** is a [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") district located in the eastern part of the city, bordered by the city's walls to the east and south, [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") to the northwest, and [Hierarch Square](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hierarch_Square "Hierarch Square") to the west. It is the poorest district in the city as well as the most crowded. Fugitive mages like [Triss Merigold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") made their home here for a time, as well as several nonhumans. The district is under the control of two criminal gangs: the [Cutups](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cutups "Cutups") and the [Blindeyes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blindeyes "Blindeyes").
