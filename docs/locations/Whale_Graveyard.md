## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The **Whale Graveyard** is a coastline filled with [whale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale "Whale") carcasses found on the eastern shores of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). A local group of pirates have established their [camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") here, looking for valuable fossils to sell. According to [Skelligers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), whales came to die here ever since the time of [Hemdall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hemdall "Hemdall") when the king of whales died here. If the [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") cleared the nearby pirate camp, [druids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druid "Druid") can be seen camping there and tending the graveyard.
