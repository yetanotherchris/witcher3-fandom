## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **farm** is located just east of [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") village and belonged to the Vildenvert family in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games"), whose last living members were [Dune](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dune_Vildenvert "Dune Vildenvert") and his brother [Bastien](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bastien_Vildenvert "Bastien Vildenvert"). 

When the [Third Northern War](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Third_Northern_War "Third Northern War") started however, Bastien enlisted in the [Temerian Army](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Army "Temerian Army"), leaving the farm only to Dune and his wife, [Liesje](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Liesje "Liesje"), as Dune had cut off some of his fingers to avoid being drafted. 

_If[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") defends [Rhosyn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rhosyn "Rhosyn") during [Missing in Action](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Missing_in_Action "Missing in Action"):_ after Bastien went missing in the [Battle of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_White_Orchard "Battle of White Orchard"), Geralt helped Dune and the family dog [Hussar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hussar "Hussar") to find his brother, along with a [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire") soldier, Rhosyn. The [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") convinced the brothers to allow Rhosyn to disguise himself and live at their farm.
