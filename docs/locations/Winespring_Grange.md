## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Winespring Grange** is a vineyard located northeast of [Moldavie Residence](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moldavie_Residence "Moldavie Residence"). It has its own mercenary guard and its whispered that a fabulous beaujolais, that could compete with even the wines of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), is produced here. 

## Map description

    _Years ago, an eccentric[count](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Count "Count") named Jacobus Ruth of the Rieslings settled here. The count could not stand the pomposity of court life but loved good vine. He thus planted a vineyard here which produces a fabulous beaujolais prized on both sides of the [Pontar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pontar_\(river\) "Pontar \(river\)")._
