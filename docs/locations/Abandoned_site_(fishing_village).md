## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

This **abandoned site** is located at a small fishing village on the coast directly east of [Stonecutters' Settlement](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stonecutters%27_Settlement "Stonecutters' Settlement") and west of [Oxenfurt Academy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Academy "Oxenfurt Academy"). Initially overrun by [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner"), after they're killed off, 120[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") \+ 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") is awarded, and a [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(fishing_village\) "Merchant \(fishing village\)") will arrive, along with a few peasants and [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") [soldiers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_Redanian_Army "Royal Redanian Army").
