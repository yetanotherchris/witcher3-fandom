## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Refugees' Camp** is a location northwest of [Midcopse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Midcopse "Midcopse"). It consists of a circle of tents around a winged statue and is surrounded by forest on three sides, while to the west lies a clearing which leads to the [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks"). 

Initially, the camp is marked as an [abandoned site](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site "Abandoned site") as it is home to a horde of bandits. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") may kill the bandits to liberate the location and restore it to its original purpose. From then on, the camp will be home to refugees displaced by the war between the [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Realms "Northern Realms") and [Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). A [herbalist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist_\(Refugees%27_Camp\) "Herbalist \(Refugees' Camp\)") will also move in and become available for trade. 120[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level") \+ 50 [![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown") is awarded for liberating the site. 

## Map description

    _The members of this small community have erected a large, winged statue - evidence of people turning to old gods and ancient cults in this time of war and famine._
