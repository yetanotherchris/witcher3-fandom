## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Crippled Kate's** is one of the less prosperous [brothels](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brothel "Brothel") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"). It is governed by [an unnamed madame](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Madame_\(Crippled_Kate%27s\) "Madame \(Crippled Kate's\)") and has around ten [prostitutes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Prostitute "Prostitute").
