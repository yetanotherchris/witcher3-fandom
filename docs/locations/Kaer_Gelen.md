## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Kaer Gelen** is a ruined fortress found in the northern mountains of [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), in [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") territory. It is now home to a few [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith"), and at the top level, a [katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan") who guards two chests with random relic gear. 

## Map description

    _This old fort's ruins attract the sort whom one would not want to run into in a dark alley._

## Bugs

  * After pulling the lever to open the main entrance, the portcullis can be seen slowly clipping through the stone arch above instead of sliding into a proper slit, a minor missing detail.
  * It is possible to enter through the closed gate by galloping into it on a horse, then dismounting just as Geralt collides with it.

## Notes

  * Two [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") books, [Fridebjorg the Bloody](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fridebjorg_the_Bloody "Fridebjorg the Bloody") and [Highwayman's letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Highwayman%27s_letter "Highwayman's letter"), were supposed to be found at this location, and are related to an encounter with a "Blood Countess" character, who is likely the katakan at the top - evidently a female who once assumed a [human](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") form and called herself "Fridebjorg".
  * One of the chests in the katakan's room was at some point meant to include [Diagram: Bounty hunter's gambeson](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Bounty_hunter%27s_gambeson "Diagram: Bounty hunter's gambeson"), [Formula: Vitriol](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Formula:_Vitriol "Formula: Vitriol") and [Assassin's gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Assassin%27s_gauntlets "Assassin's gauntlets"), and the other [Assassin's trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Assassin%27s_trousers "Assassin's trousers"). It's unclear if that's been changed on purpose or not, but they give random loot instead.

## Trivia

  * Kaer or Kêr means "city" in [Breton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Breton_language), while Ker means "city" or "fortress" in [Cornish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cornish_language). Caer means the same in [Welsh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Welsh_language).
  * _Gelen_ means leech in [Welsh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Welsh_language).
