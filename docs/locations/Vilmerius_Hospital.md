## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Vilmerius Hospital** is named after an unidentified Vilmerius[1] and is a three-story building located in the northeastern part of [The Bits](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Bits "The Bits") district 

The chief surgeon is [Joachim von Gratz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Joachim_von_Gratz "Joachim von Gratz"). Due to recent conflicts, this hospital has had to turn away many of the wounded, as the richer and more influential families receive treatments before the general populace.[2]

It's a place where [Priscilla](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Priscilla "Priscilla") convalesces after she is attacked and Geralt can also bring here an injured [witch hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") for treatment.
