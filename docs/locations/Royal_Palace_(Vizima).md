The **Royal Palace** in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima") is a castle that once served as the seat of power for [Temeria](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temeria "Temeria")'s [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King "King") [Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foltest "Foltest") and his court. It was built after the abandonment of the [previous seat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_manor_house "Old manor house") of [Temerian monarchs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Temerian_Dynasty "Temerian Dynasty") due to problems with a [striga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Striga "Striga"). 

## _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_

### Journal entry

_The castle was completed around the time when King Foltest's previous seat was taken over by a striga, the product of the ruler's incestuous affair with his[own sister](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Adda_of_Temeria "Adda of Temeria"). Spacious stone halls, stained glass windows and vaulted ceilings indicate that [dwarven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") engineering was employed here. King Foltest is a very active ruler and often travels, though when in Vizima he usually occupies his chambers and generally remains at the castle with his modest court. In the throne room, the King grants audiences to his subjects, be they petitioners or trusted advisors._

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

The Royal Palace is a region in the third game but limited to one location, the interior of the palace itself and the current [Emperor of Nilfgaard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperator "Imperator"), [Emhyr var Emreis](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Emhyr_var_Emreis "Emhyr var Emreis"), who now occupies it. The courtyard has a secret room containing an [old letter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_letter "Old letter").
