## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Spitfire Bluff** is a region of [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It's located on the western edge of Velen, between [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch_\(region\) "Crow's Perch \(region\)") and [The Mire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Mire "The Mire").
