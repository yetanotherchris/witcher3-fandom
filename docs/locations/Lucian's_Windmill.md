## Appearance(s)

### Games

_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Lucian's Windmill** is a landmark located south of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), just outside of the [Farcorners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Farcorners "Farcorners") district. 

## Map description

    _Lucian le Foix, the famous[Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") sculptor and architect, bought this windmill several years ago and made it into his country retreat. Sadly the enormous popularity of the great Lucian's designs means he spends little time in his fortress of solitude and has entrusted its care to a steward._
