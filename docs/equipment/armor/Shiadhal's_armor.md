# Shiadhal's armor

**Shiadhal's armor** is a body armor in both _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ and _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Its name suggests a connection with [Shiadhal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shiadhal "Shiadhal").

## Item ID

Shiadhal armor

This version can be found as random loot. 

One place where it often spawns (though not guaranteed) is the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") east of [Gedyneith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gedyneith "Gedyneith"), guarded by an [Earth Elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_Elemental "Earth Elemental"). It is also be pretty much a sure find in at least one of two spots near the [Cavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cavern "Cavern") north from [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") \- deep in the cavern itself, behind the illusion there, or from the guarded treasure ([armored arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armored_arachas "Armored arachas")) above.
