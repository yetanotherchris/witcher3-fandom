Heavy gloves 04 Netflix

One of the best gauntlets in the game. They can be found fairly often as random loot or, starting at level 31, be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")

Since [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), a special Netflix pair can reliably be obtained very early, along with a [Nilfgaardian guardsman armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_guardsman_armor "Nilfgaardian guardsman armor"), from a chest inside [House of Respite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/House_of_Respite "House of Respite"), next to the [Nilfgaardian Helmet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Helmet "Nilfgaardian Helmet"). They have the exact same stats as the regular versions, but are the only pieces to change appearance with the _Alternate Nilfgaardian Armor_ option enabled.
