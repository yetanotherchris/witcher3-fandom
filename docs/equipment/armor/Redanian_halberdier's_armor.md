# Redanian halberdier's armor

**Redanian halberdier's armor** is a [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in the following forms: 

  * Redanian halberdier's armor (relic)
  * Redanian halberdier's armor (crafted)
  * Redanian halberdier's armor (basic)
