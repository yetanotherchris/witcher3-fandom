# Eternal Fire executioner's armor

**Eternal Fire executioner's armor** is a [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in two forms: 

  * Eternal Fire executioner's armor (relic)
  * Eternal Fire executioner's armor (basic)

## Item ID

Medium armor 11

One is always found in the left of 3 reward chests for [The Witchers' Forge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witchers%27_Forge "The Witchers' Forge") quest. It can also be found as random loot or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
