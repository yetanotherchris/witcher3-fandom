**Superior Ursine boots** are craftable [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") and are part of the [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are needed to craft [mastercrafted Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Ursine_boots "Mastercrafted Ursine boots").
