## Item ID

Lynx Pants 1

**Legendary Feline trousers** are craftable [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") and are part of the [Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. They are needed to craft [enhanced legendary Feline trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_legendary_Feline_trousers "Enhanced legendary Feline trousers").
