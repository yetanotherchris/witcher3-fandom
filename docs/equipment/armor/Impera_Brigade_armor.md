# Impera Brigade armor

**Impera Brigade armor** is a [heavy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ named after the [Impera Brigade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Impera_Brigade "Impera Brigade"). It comes in two forms: 

  * Impera Brigade armor (crafted)
  * Impera Brigade armor (relic)
