# Mag Deira cuirass

**Mag Deira cuirass** is a [heavy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in three forms: 

  * Mag Deira cuirass (relic)
  * Mag Deira cuirass (crafted)
  * Mag Deira cuirass (basic)

## Item ID

Heavy armor 01

One is received as a reward for completing the secondary quest [The Path of Warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Path_of_Warriors "The Path of Warriors") and another is in the reward chest for [Out of the Frying Pan, into the Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Out_of_the_Frying_Pan,_into_the_Fire "Out of the Frying Pan, into the Fire"). It can also often found as random loot and can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
