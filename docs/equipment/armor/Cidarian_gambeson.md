# Cidarian gambeson

**Cidarian gambeson** is a [light](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in 3 different forms: 

  * Cidarian gambeson (relic)
  * Cidarian gambeson (crafted)
  * Cidarian gambeson (common)
