# Angrenian cuirass

**Angrenian cuirass** is [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in three forms: 

  * Angrenian cuirass (relic)
  * Angrenian cuirass (crafted)
  * Angrenian cuirass (basic)
