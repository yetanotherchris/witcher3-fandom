**Ciri's armor** is [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ but only worn by [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"). 

This armor can only be obtained via the console. It has no inventory icon, and equipping it on [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has no visual effect.
