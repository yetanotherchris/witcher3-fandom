## Item ID

Skellige Casual Suit 01

**Elegant Skellige shirt** is a [light](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found in [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room at [The New Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port") inn at [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor"). It is also sold by [Elihal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elihal "Elihal"), [Sjusta](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sjusta "Sjusta") and the [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Kaer_Trolde_keep\) "Armorer \(Kaer Trolde keep\)"). 

[Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") recommends [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to wear it when they both attend a banquet honoring [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_the_Skellige_Isles "King of the Skellige Isles") [Bran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bran_Tuirseach "Bran Tuirseach")'s death. She also recommends he wear [Skellige breeches](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_breeches "Skellige breeches") and [Skellige festive slippers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_festive_slippers "Skellige festive slippers").
