**Forgotten Wolven armor** is a craftable [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") and is part of the [Forgotten Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_Wolf_School_Gear "Forgotten Wolf School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. The gear was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)").
