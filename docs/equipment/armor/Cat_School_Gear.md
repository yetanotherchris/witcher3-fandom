# Cat School Gear

[![Full mastercrafted set](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/aa/SchoolOfTheCat.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/306?cb=20160112074849)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/aa/SchoolOfTheCat.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160112074849) [![Full mastercrafted set](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/aa/SchoolOfTheCat.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/306?cb=20160112074849)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/a/aa/SchoolOfTheCat.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160112074849) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:SchoolOfTheCat.jpg)

Full mastercrafted set

**Cat** or **Feline School Gear** is a [witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") armor and swords set inspired by the [School of the Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Cat "School of the Cat") for witchers and is one of the [Scavenger Hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. The set includes chest armor, steel and silver swords, crossbow, gauntlets, boots, and trousers, all of which are upgradeable except the crossbow. Also, there is not a [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") version of the crossbow. 

The armor set is worn by [Gaetan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gaetan "Gaetan") in the DLC secondary quest [Where the Cat and Wolf Play...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Where_the_Cat_and_Wolf_Play... "Where the Cat and Wolf Play..."), as well as presumably being worn by [Kiyan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kiyan "Kiyan") before he was experimented on by a [mage](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mage "Mage"). According to said mage's notes, the gear used by witchers of the School of the Cat is heavily inspired by [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") weapons and armor.[1]

  * **Bonuses for 3 pieces:** Strong Attack increase Fast Attack damage for 5 seconds by 10% for each piece of the set.
  * **Bonuses for 6 pieces:** Rear attacks deal 50% more damage, and also stun the opponent at the cost of 1 Adrenaline Point(s).

_Note:_ all legendary gear are stronger versions of base game gear in New Game +.
