## Item ID

Gryphon Boots 2  
NGP Gryphon Boots 2 ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"))

**Enhanced Griffin boots** are craftable [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") and are part of the [Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are needed to craft [superior Griffin boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Griffin_boots "Superior Griffin boots").
