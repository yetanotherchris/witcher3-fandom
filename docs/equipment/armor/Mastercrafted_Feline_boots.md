## Item ID

Lynx Boots 4

**Mastercrafted Feline boots** are craftable [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") and are part of the [Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are needed to craft [grandmaster Feline boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Feline_boots "Grandmaster Feline boots") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion.
