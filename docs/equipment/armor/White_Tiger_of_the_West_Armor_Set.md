# White Tiger of the West Armor Set

**White Tiger of the West Armor Set** was introduced as a reward with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), for owning or upgrading to _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ \- Complete Edition. 

It appears alongside the [Dol Blathanna Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna_Armor_Set "Dol Blathanna Armor Set") set and the Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen in [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), in a chest under a window.

**What update introduced the White Tiger of the West Armor Set in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt introduced the White Tiger of the West Armor Set on December 14, 2022, as part of the next-gen update, Patch 4.0. This armor set is accessible to players who own or upgrade to The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Complete Edition. The complete set is located in Yennefer's room in Vizima, stored in a chest under a window.

**How does the White Tiger of the West Armor Set compare to other armors in The Witcher 3?** ![toggle section](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/36002e46-06fe-4633-9d99-27d96cf5bcdc)

The White Tiger of the West Armor Set in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Complete Edition is a medium armor with an 85 rating. It provides +75 vitality and +5% resistance to piercing and other damages. This armor set, released in Patch 4.0, can be located in Yennefer's room in Vizima, along with the Dol Blathanna Armor Set and the Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen.
