# Cavalry boots

**Cavalry boots** are [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that come in the following forms: 

  * Cavalry boots (relic)
  * Cavalry boots (crafted)
  * Cavalry boots (basic)
