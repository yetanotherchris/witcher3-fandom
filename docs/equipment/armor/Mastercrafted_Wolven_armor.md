## Item ID

Wolf Armor 3

**Mastercrafted Wolven armor** is a craftable [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") and part of the [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is needed to craft [Grandmaster Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Wolven_armor "Grandmaster Wolven armor"). 

It is also the only piece of [witcher gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_witcher_gear "Category:The Witcher 3 witcher gear") that can be both crafted and found as loot, in the [Iron Mine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_Mine "Iron Mine") near [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen") during the secondary quest [The Witchers' Forge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witchers%27_Forge "The Witchers' Forge").
