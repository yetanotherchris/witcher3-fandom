## Item ID

Geralt Shirt

A **shirt** is a basic piece of [light](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_armor "Feline armor")
  * [Legendary Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_armor "Legendary Feline armor")
  * [Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_armor "Griffin armor")
  * [Legendary Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Griffin_armor "Legendary Griffin armor")
  * [Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_armor "Ursine armor")
  * [Legendary Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_armor "Legendary Ursine armor")
  * [Legendary Manticore armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Manticore_armor "Legendary Manticore armor") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_armor "Wolven armor")
  * [Legendary Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Wolven_armor "Legendary Wolven armor")
  * [Forgotten Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_Wolven_armor "Forgotten Wolven armor")
  * [Legendary forgotten Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_forgotten_Wolven_armor "Legendary forgotten Wolven armor")
  * [Toussaint Color Guardsman's armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_Color_Guardsman%27s_armor "Toussaint Color Guardsman's armor") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Toussaint Ducal Guardsman's armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_Ducal_Guardsman%27s_armor "Toussaint Ducal Guardsman's armor") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") except [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay")

  * Armors [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")

Geralt Shirt No Knife
