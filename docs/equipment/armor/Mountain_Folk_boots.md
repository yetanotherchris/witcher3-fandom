# Mountain Folk boots

**Mountain Folk boots** are [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that come in two forms: 

  * Mountain Folk boots (crafted)
  * Mountain Folk boots (relic)

## Item ID

Boots 07

One pair is dropped by the [Imp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Janne "Janne"), if he is killed during [Contract: An Elusive Thief](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_An_Elusive_Thief "Contract: An Elusive Thief"), and another can be accepted as reward from [Eskel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eskel "Eskel") during [To Bait a Forktail...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/To_Bait_a_Forktail... "To Bait a Forktail..."), if you beat him in the [horse race](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse_Races "Horse Races") to [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"). 

Can also be found as random loot or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
