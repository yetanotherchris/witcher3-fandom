# Witch hunter's armor

**Witch hunter's armor** is a [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is named after the [witch hunters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter"), the police organization whose purpose is to hunt down sorcerers and sorceresses). Most witch hunters encountered in the game don this armor. 

It comes in the following forms: 

  * Witch hunter's armor (relic)
  * Witch hunter's armor (basic)
  * Witch hunter's armor (quest)
