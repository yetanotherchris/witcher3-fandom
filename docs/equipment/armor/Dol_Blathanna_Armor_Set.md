# Dol Blathanna Armor Set

[![Tw3 thousand flowers armor](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/9f/Tw3_thousand_flowers_armor.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/187?cb=20221214045751)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/9f/Tw3_thousand_flowers_armor.png/revision/latest?cb=20221214045751) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw3_thousand_flowers_armor.png)

**Dol Blathanna Armor Set** was introduced as a reward with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), for owning or upgrading to _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ \- Complete Edition. 

It appears alongside the [White Tiger of the West Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Tiger_of_the_West_Armor_Set "White Tiger of the West Armor Set") and the [Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swords_of_the_Nine-Tailed_Vixen "Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen") in [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), in a chest under a window.
