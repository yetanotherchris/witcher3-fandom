## Item ID

Starting Armor

**Kaer Morhen armor** is a [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [chest armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is the basic, starting armor, which is equipped by [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") at the beginning of the game. It is needed to craft the [warrior's leather jacket](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Warrior%27s_leather_jacket "Warrior's leather jacket"), a visually identical, but statistically superior chest armor piece.
