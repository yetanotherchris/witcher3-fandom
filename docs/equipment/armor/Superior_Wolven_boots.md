## Item ID

Wolf Boots 3

**Superior Wolven boots** are craftable [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") and are part of the [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are needed to craft [mastercrafted Wolven boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Wolven_boots "Mastercrafted Wolven boots").
