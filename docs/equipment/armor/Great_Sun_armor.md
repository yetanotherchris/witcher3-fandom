## Item ID

Shadaal armor

**Great Sun armor** is [heavy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , named after the major religion (the cult of the [Great Sun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Sun "Great Sun")) in the [Nilfgaardian Empire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire"). 

It may be found as random loot from the many underwater treasures in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and some _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion treasure chests.
