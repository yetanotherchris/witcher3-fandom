## Item ID

Nilfgaardian casual shoes

**Elegant Nilfgaardian shoes** are [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") shoes in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can acquire 3 pairs during the beginning of [Imperial Audience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Audience "Imperial Audience"). If kept, they can later be used for attending banquets during other quests: 

  * [A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_3\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 3\)")
  * [The King is Dead – Long Live the King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_King_is_Dead_%E2%80%93_Long_Live_the_King "The King is Dead – Long Live the King")
  * [The Man from Cintra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Man_from_Cintra "The Man from Cintra") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

They're also sold by [Elihal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elihal "Elihal"), and in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion by [Pierre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pierre "Pierre") as well.
