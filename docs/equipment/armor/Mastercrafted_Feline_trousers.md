NGP Lynx Pants 4 ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"))

**Mastercrafted Feline trousers** are craftable [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") and are part of the [Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are needed to craft [grandmaster Feline trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Feline_trousers "Grandmaster Feline trousers") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion.
