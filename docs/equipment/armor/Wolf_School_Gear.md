As part of week [5's DLC](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , we now have a [Scavenger Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Wolf_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Wolf School Gear") for the **Wolf School Gear** , released on the 18th of June 2015. It was what many players of the game wished to see introduced, as all other [witcher schools](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_schools "Witcher schools") got their own armor set. The set includes chest armor, steel and silver swords, gauntlets, boots, and trousers, all of which are upgradeable, and all legendary gear are stronger versions of base game gear in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

The armor style is very similar to that of [Eskel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eskel "Eskel")'s and that of other [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") of the [School of the Wolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Wolf "School of the Wolf") \- layered jackets made of leather and reinforced with metal studs/rivets. The mastercrafted and grandmaster versions further reinforce the outer jacket with panels of mail armor and metal spaulders on the shoulders. 

Prior to [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), the grandmaster equipment added in _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion didn't require the mastercrafted versions for it to be made like the [Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Griffin "School of the Griffin"), [Bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Bear "School of the Bear"), and [Cat](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Cat "School of the Cat") school grandmaster equipment. However, this has since been fixed with the update. 

  * **Bonus for 3 pieces** : Each instance of Bleeding applied to enemies increases sword damage by 1% for each piece of the set.[1]
  * **Bonus for 6 pieces** : Each Adrenaline Point increases the possible number of Bleeding effects that can be applied to a single enemy.

## Notes

  * With the exception of grandmaster-level gear, unlike other witcher school sets, the legendary versions of Wolven equipment have distinct global item IDs. Thus, while only obtainable via console commands or mods, they aren't aren't technically exclusive to [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +").
