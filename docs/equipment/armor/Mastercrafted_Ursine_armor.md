## Item ID

Bear Armor 3  
NGP Bear Armor 3 ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"))

**Mastercrafted Ursine armor** is a craftable [heavy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") and is part of the [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is needed to craft [Grandmaster Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Ursine_armor "Grandmaster Ursine armor") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion.
