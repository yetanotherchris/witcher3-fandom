## Item ID

Bear Pants 1

**Legendary Ursine trousers** are craftable [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") and are part of the [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. They are needed to craft [Enhanced Legendary Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Legendary_Ursine_trousers "Enhanced Legendary Ursine trousers").
