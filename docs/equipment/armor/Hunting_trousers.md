# Hunting trousers

**Hunting trousers** are [light](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") or medium armor [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that come in the following forms: 

  * Hunting trousers (starter)
  * Hunting trousers (crafted)
  * Hunting trousers (master)
  * Hunting trousers (common)

## Item ID

Starting Pants

These unique trousers are classed as [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor"), unlike all other hunting trousers, which are light. 

One pair is automatically equipped on [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") at the start of a new game, and another is later acquired and worn during the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion quest [Dead Man's Party](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dead_Man%27s_Party "Dead Man's Party").
