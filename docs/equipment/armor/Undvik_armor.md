**Undvik armor** is a [heavy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") and part of the [Skellige Armor set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_Armor_set "Skellige Armor set") [free DLC](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , released on June 3, 2015. It can be bought from the [Armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Kaer_Trolde_keep\) "Armorer \(Kaer Trolde keep\)") in [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") and cannot be dismantled. The armor's design elements are strongly influenced from [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") craftsmanship. 

Whether due to a bug or intentionally, the upgraded [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") version can be purchased in a regular game as well. At level 41, it will replace the level 16 version in the armorer's inventory. Similarly, it cannot be purchased before reaching level 41 even in a New Game +.
