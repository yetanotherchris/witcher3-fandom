## Item ID

NGP Gryphon Armor 3 ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"))

**Mastercrafted Griffin armor** is a craftable [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") and is part of the [Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is needed to craft [grandmaster Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Griffin_armor "Grandmaster Griffin armor") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion.
