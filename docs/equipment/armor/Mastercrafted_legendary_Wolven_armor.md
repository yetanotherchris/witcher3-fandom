## Item ID

NGP Wolf Armor 3

**Mastercrafted legendary Wolven armor** is a craftable [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") and is part of the [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. It is needed to craft [Grandmaster legendary Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_Wolven_armor "Grandmaster legendary Wolven armor").
