# Nilfgaardian Armor set

[![Tw nilfgaardian-armor-dlc](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/5e/Tw_nilfgaardian-armor-dlc.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20160503185425)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/5e/Tw_nilfgaardian-armor-dlc.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20160503185425) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Tw_nilfgaardian-armor-dlc.jpg)

**Nilfgaardian Armor set** is one of the 16 [free DLCs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that was released on June 3, 2015. The DLC adds four [Nilfgaardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Empire "Nilfgaardian Empire")-themed armor items, as well as three pieces of horse equipment, all of which can be bought from the [quartermaster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quartermaster_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Quartermaster \(Crow's Perch\)") at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch"). The most basic version of the armor set is equippable at level 10 or above, while the second version becomes available at level 35. 

Wearing part of the Nilfgaardian armor set grants one access into the [House of Respite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/House_of_Respite "House of Respite").
