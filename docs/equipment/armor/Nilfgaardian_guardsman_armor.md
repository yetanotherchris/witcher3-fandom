# Nilfgaardian guardsman armor

**Nilfgaardian guardsman armor** is [heavy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in the following forms: 

  * Nilfgaardian guardsman armor (relic)
  * Nilfgaardian guardsman armor (crafted)
  * Nilfgaardian guardsman armor (basic)
  * Nilfgaardian guardsman armor (quest)

## Item ID

Heavy armor 03r  
Heavy armor 03 Netflix

Can be found as random loot after reaching level 16, quite often from [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure"), smuggler's caches and other similar points of interest. 

Since [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), a special Netflix version can be found at the [House of Respite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/House_of_Respite "House of Respite"), along with [Nilfgaardian guardsman's gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_guardsman%27s_gauntlets "Nilfgaardian guardsman's gauntlets"), in a chest next to the [Nilfgaardian Helmet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Helmet "Nilfgaardian Helmet"). They have the exact same stats as the regular versions, but are the only pieces to change appearance with the _Alternate Nilfgaardian Armor_ option enabled.
