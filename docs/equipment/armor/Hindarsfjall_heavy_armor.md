# Hindarsfjall heavy armor

**Hindarsfjall heavy armor** is a [heavy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in the following forms: 

  * Hindarsfjall heavy armor (relic)
  * Hindarsfjall heavy armor (crafted)
  * Hindarsfjall heavy armor (basic)
