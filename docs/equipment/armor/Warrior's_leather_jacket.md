## Item ID

Starting Armor 1

**Warrior's leather jacket** is a [medium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is obtainable only via crafting and the diagram for it is part of the starting gear. It is visually identical to the [Kaer Morhen armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen_armor "Kaer Morhen armor") but has better stats.
