## Item ID

White Tiger Pants

**White Tiger of the West armor** are [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") and part of the [White Tiger of the West Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Tiger_of_the_West_Armor_Set "White Tiger of the West Armor Set") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It was released on December 14, 2022 as part of [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), the next-gen update, for owning or upgrading to _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ \- Complete Edition. 

The entire set can be found in [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), in a chest under a window, along with the [Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swords_of_the_Nine-Tailed_Vixen "Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen") and the [Dol Blathanna Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna_Armor_Set "Dol Blathanna Armor Set").
