# Assault gauntlets

**Assault Gauntlets** are [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_gauntlets "The Witcher 3 gauntlets") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that come in two forms: 

  * Assault gauntlets (crafted)
  * Assault gauntlets (basic)

## Item ID

Heavy gloves 03

One pair is always found north from [Kaer Gelen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Gelen "Kaer Gelen"), on a body behind the stones where the skeleton with a [Poem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Poem "Poem") is. They can also be found as random loot or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
