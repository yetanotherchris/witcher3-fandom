## Item ID

Bear Gloves 4

**Mastercrafted legendary Ursine gauntlets** are craftable [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_gauntlets "The Witcher 3 gauntlets") and are part of the [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. They are needed to craft [Grandmaster legendary Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_Ursine_gauntlets "Grandmaster legendary Ursine gauntlets") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion.
