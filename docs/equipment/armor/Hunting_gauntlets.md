# Hunting gauntlets

**Hunting gauntlets** are [light](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") or medium armor [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_gauntlets "The Witcher 3 gauntlets") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They come in the following forms: 

  * Hunting gauntlets (starter)
  * Hunting gauntlets (crafted)
  * Hunting gauntlets (master)
  * Hunting gauntlets (common)

## Item ID

Starting Gloves

This unique version of the gauntlets is automatically equipped on [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") at the start of a new game. They are classed as [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor"), unlike all other hunting gauntlets, which are light.
