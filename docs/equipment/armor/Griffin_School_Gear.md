# Griffin School Gear

[![Grifffin](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0b/Grifffin.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20151009154415)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0b/Grifffin.png/revision/latest?cb=20151009154415) [![Grifffin](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0b/Grifffin.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20151009154415)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/0/0b/Grifffin.png/revision/latest?cb=20151009154415) [](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/File:Grifffin.png)

**Griffin School Gear** is a medium armor set of witcher gear inspired by the [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") of the [School of the Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/School_of_the_Griffin "School of the Griffin"). The set includes chest armor, steel and silver swords, gauntlets, boots, and trousers, all of which are upgradeable. It's one of the [Scavenger Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear") quests in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , and all legendary gear are stronger versions of base game gear in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"). 

This set was based upon armor presumably used by the legendary witcher [George of Kagen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/George_of_Kagen "George of Kagen") (slayer of the [green dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Green_dragon "Green dragon") in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen")) in times past. 

  * **Bonuses for 3 pieces:** After using Stamina to cast a [Sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") in its standard mode, the next Sign cast within 3 seconds will be cast in standard mode without using Stamina.
  * **Bonuses for 6 pieces:** The size of [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") traps is increased by 40%. While you are within an Yrden trap, Stamina regeneration is increased by 5/s and Sign Intensity by 100%, and damage is reduced by 20%.
