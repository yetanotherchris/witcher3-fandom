## Item ID

Yennefers Dress

**Yennefer's dress** is a pair of patterned trousers that are [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [chest armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") puts these on when he, [Eskel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eskel "Eskel"), and [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert") decide to cross-dress and use [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s [megascope](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Megascope "Megascope") to call other sorceresses of the [Lodge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lodge_of_Sorceresses "Lodge of Sorceresses") to join their drunken party at [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"). 

## Notes

Despite the armor showing as a pair of [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers"), this item is actually chest armor.
