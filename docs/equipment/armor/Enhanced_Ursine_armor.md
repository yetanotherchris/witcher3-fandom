## Item ID

Bear Armor 1  
NGP Bear Armor 1 ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"))

**Enhanced Ursine armor** is a craftable [heavy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor") and is part of the [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is needed to craft [superior Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Ursine_armor "Superior Ursine armor").
