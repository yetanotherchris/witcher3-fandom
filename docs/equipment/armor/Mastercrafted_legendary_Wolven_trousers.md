## Item ID

NGP Wolf Pants 4

**Mastercrafted legendary Wolven trousers** are craftable [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") and are part of the [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. It is needed to craft [Grandmaster legendary Wolven trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_Wolven_trousers "Grandmaster legendary Wolven trousers").
