# Lyrian hardened leather trousers

**Lyrian hardened leather trousers** are [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that come in two forms: 

  * Lyrian hardened leather trousers (crafted)
  * Lyrian hardened leather trousers (basic)
