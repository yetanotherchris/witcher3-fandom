**Griffin trousers** are craftable [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") and are part of the [Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. They are needed to craft [enhanced Griffin trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Griffin_trousers "Enhanced Griffin trousers").
