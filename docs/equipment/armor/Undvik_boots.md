**Undvik boots** are [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") and part of [Skellige Armor set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_Armor_set "Skellige Armor set") [free DLC](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ on June 3, 2015. They can be bought from [Armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Kaer_Trolde_keep\) "Armorer \(Kaer Trolde keep\)") in [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") and cannot be dismantled. In the various elements of the armor, favored by Geralt during his treks across the unforgiving tundra of [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), strong influence of [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik") mastercraft can be seen. 

Whether due to a bug or intentionally, the upgraded [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") version can be purchased in a regular game as well. At level 41, it will replace the level 16 version in the armorer's inventory. Similarly, it cannot be purchased before reaching level 41 even in a New Game +.
