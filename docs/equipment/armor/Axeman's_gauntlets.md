# Axeman's gauntlets

**Axeman's gauntlets** are [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor") [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_gauntlets "The Witcher 3 gauntlets") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that come in two forms: 

  * Axeman's gauntlets (crafted)
  * Axeman's gauntlets (basic)
