# Hunting boots

**Hunting boots** are [light](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") or [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that come in the following forms: 

  * Hunting boots (starter)
  * Hunting boots (relic)
  * Hunting boots (crafted)
  * Hunting boots (master)
  * Hunting boots (common)

## Item ID

Boots 01 q2

It can always be found in the big chest next to the [grindstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grindstone "Grindstone") in the [Nilfgaardian Garrison](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Garrison "Nilfgaardian Garrison"), although the soldiers there will attack Geralt for stealing it. It is also received as part of the reward for completing [Ladies of the Wood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ladies_of_the_Wood "Ladies of the Wood"), often found as random loot, and can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All base game [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") except [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay")
  * Armors [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(boatyard\) "Merchant \(boatyard\)") in the [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Coast_of_Wrecks\) "Merchant \(Coast of Wrecks\)") at [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks")
