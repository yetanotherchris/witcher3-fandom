## Item ID

Skellige casual shoes

**Skellige festive slippers** are [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") footwear in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be found in [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room at [The New Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port") inn at [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor"). They are also sold by [Elihal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elihal "Elihal"), [Sjusta](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sjusta "Sjusta") and the [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Kaer_Trolde_keep\) "Armorer \(Kaer Trolde keep\)"). 

[Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg") recommends [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to wear them in [1272](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline/Games#1272 "Timeline/Games") when they both attended a banquet honoring [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_the_Skellige_Isles "King of the Skellige Isles") [Bran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bran_Tuirseach "Bran Tuirseach")'s death. She also recommends he wear [Skellige breeches](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige_breeches "Skellige breeches") and an [Elegant Skellige shirt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elegant_Skellige_shirt "Elegant Skellige shirt").
