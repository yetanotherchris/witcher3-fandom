## Item ID

Bath Towel Pants 01

**Bath towel** is a unique [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor") [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers") item in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

After being shaved during [Imperial Audience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Audience "Imperial Audience"), and visiting the [Big Four](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Big_Four "Big Four") in [Sigismund's Bathhouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund%27s_Bathhouse "Sigismund's Bathhouse") during [Get Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Get_Junior "Get Junior"), you will automatically equip this. 

## Notes

  * It can be kept in the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") if you drop it before it gets removed from the inventory. 
    * It can then be placed on an armor stand in [Corvo Bianco](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Corvo_Bianco "Corvo Bianco") during the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, though this leads to an unusual graphical glitch where Geralt's legs are still attached and clip into the stand.
