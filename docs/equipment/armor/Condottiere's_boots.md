# Condottiere's boots

**Condoittiere's boots** are [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor") [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that come in two forms: 

  * Condottiere's boots (crafted)
  * Condottiere's boots (relic)

## Item ID

Heavy boots 07

One pair is always found after solving the statues puzzle inside the [monster den](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_den_\(Trottheim\) "Monster den \(Trottheim\)") south of [Trottheim](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trottheim "Trottheim") on [Faroe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe "Faroe") (where the armor pieces are found during [Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 4](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Cat_School_Gear_Upgrade_Diagrams_-_Part_4 "Scavenger Hunt: Cat School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 4")). Another is in a chest up on a ledge where the [gargoyles](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gargoyle "Gargoyle") are during [The Path of Warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Path_of_Warriors "The Path of Warriors"). Oddly, a pair can also be dropped by one of the soldiers during [An Eye for an Eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Eye_for_an_Eye "An Eye for an Eye"). 

Also available as random loot and, starting at level 31, can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
