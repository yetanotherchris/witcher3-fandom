# The Witcher 3 armor

**Armor** serves as protective covering to mitigate damage in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It comes in 3 main categories: [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor"), [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor"), and [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor"); and consists of four pieces: [chest armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_chest_armor "The Witcher 3 chest armor"), [gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_gauntlets "The Witcher 3 gauntlets"), [trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trousers "The Witcher 3 trousers"), and [boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_boots "The Witcher 3 boots"). 

There are sets of unique witcher armor that can be collected by stumbling across hidden witcher caches or by completing [Scavenger Hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_scavenger_hunts "The Witcher 3 scavenger hunts"). 

_For a list of witcher armor, see[The Witcher 3 witcher gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_witcher_gear "The Witcher 3 witcher gear")._

_For all other armor, see[Category:The Witcher 3 armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_armor "Category:The Witcher 3 armor")._

The weight level of armor can be changed in the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion, using the following [glyphwords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyphwords "Glyphwords"): 

  * [Levity](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Levity "Levity") (changes into light armor)
  * [Balance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Balance "Balance") (changes into medium armor)
  * [Heft](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heft "Heft") (changes into heavy armor)

## Armor Crafting

_For the main article, see[Crafting](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crafting "Crafting")._

Armor can be crafted using materials gathered through exploring the world, dismantling other Armor, or purchasing components from Vendors. 

Diagrams for the pieces of Armor are needed before Geralt can craft. These can be found by purchasing them from Vendors, or finding them throughout the world. Once a Diagram is acquired, Geralt must take these to an Armorer in order to build them.
