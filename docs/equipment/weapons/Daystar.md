## Item ID

Daystar

**Daystar** is a unique [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found as random loot in many places, but also guaranteed in the following: 

  * from [Abaya](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abaya "Abaya") during [Contract: Muire D'yaeblen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Muire_D%27yaeblen "Contract: Muire D'yaeblen")
  * on a corpse in the chamber where the [Nightwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith") is during [Peace Disturbed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Peace_Disturbed "Peace Disturbed")
  * on a corpse near, but not the actual guarded treasure on an island northwest of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"), guarded by a [Basilisk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk")
  * on a corpse between the two eastern circles southeast of [Arinbjorn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arinbjorn "Arinbjorn"), near the crypt where [Diagram: Wolven silver sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Wolven_silver_sword_-_superior "Diagram: Wolven silver sword - superior") is found
  * on a corpse in one of the jail cells at [Castle Tuirseach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Castle_Tuirseach "Castle Tuirseach") during [Scavenger Hunt: Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Bear_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Bear School Gear")

Generally speaking, if you see a sitting corpse somewhere on [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), it likely has the sword.
