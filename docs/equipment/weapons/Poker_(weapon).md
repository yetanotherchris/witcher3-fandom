# Poker (weapon)

**Poker** is a secondary weapon in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in the following forms: 

  * Poker (basic)
  * Poker (quest)
  * Poker (unknown)

## Item ID

W_Poker

Surprisingly rare as loot for a basic weapon, usually only found in mines, a few caves in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), and military garrisons if one is brave enough to steal from them. However, it can be purchased from all blacksmiths outside of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), and pretty much any other merchant who sells generic weapons.

## Item ID

Iron Poker

This unique Poker is carried by [Nathaniel Pastodi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nathaniel_Pastodi "Nathaniel Pastodi") and can only be obtained by killing him at [Crippled Kate's](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crippled_Kate%27s "Crippled Kate's") during the quest [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins"), either immediately or after speaking to him. Unlike the basic one, its tip glows and is much longer in appearance. 

### Notes

  * In early versions of the game, this one was classified as a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") instead of a secondary weapon, and was also a relic.
