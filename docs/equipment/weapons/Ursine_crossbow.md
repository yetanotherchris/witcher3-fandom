## Item ID

Bear School Crossbow

**Ursine crossbow** is a craftable [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") and is part of the [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Unlike other school gear weapons and armor, the crossbow can not be upgraded and doesn't have a "legendary" version in [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +").
