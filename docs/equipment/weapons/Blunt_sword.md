## Item ID

sq303_blunt_sword

**Blunt sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_sword "Steel sword") and [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") receives this sword from [Irina Renarde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Irina_Renarde "Irina Renarde") and uses it for the faked duel with [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") during the side quest [Cabaret](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cabaret "Cabaret"). Despite it having no other purpose, it can also be purchased at any point from the [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Silverton\) "Blacksmith \(Silverton\)") in [Silverton](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silverton "Silverton").
