## Item ID

Inis

**Inis** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found south of [Eldberg Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eldberg_Lighthouse "Eldberg Lighthouse") as part of the [treasure hunt quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_treasure_hunts "The Witcher 3 treasure hunts"), [Pearls of the Coast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pearls_of_the_Coast "Pearls of the Coast").
