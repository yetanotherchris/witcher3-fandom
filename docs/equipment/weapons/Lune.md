## Item ID

Lune

**Lune** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which an only be found as random loot, after reaching level 11 from [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure"), and after reaching level 31 from some caves in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). 

## Notes

  * This sword was initially supposed to be found near [Harrisi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harrisi "Harrisi") on the soldier's corpse that has [Gwyhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwyhyr "Gwyhyr"). The container's internal name actually still has "lune" in it.
