## Item ID

Robustswordofdolblathanna

**Superior Dol Blathanna sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be purchased from the [elven merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael\)") located between the [Loggers' Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loggers%27_Hut "Loggers' Hut") and [Lucian's Windmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lucian%27s_Windmill "Lucian's Windmill"). It is also available as random loot, and is known to spawn more often in the reward chest for the treasure hunt quest [Lost Goods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lost_Goods "Lost Goods").
