## Item ID

Bear School steel sword 1

**Legendary Ursine steel sword - enhanced** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. It is needed to craft [Legendary Ursine steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_steel_sword_-_superior "Legendary Ursine steel sword - superior").
