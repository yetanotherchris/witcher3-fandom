## Item ID

Torlara

**Tor Lara** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

This sword can only be found as random loot and the chance for it to spawn it is very low. One place where it is known to appear is the guarded treasure west of [Olena's Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olena%27s_Grove "Olena's Grove"), guarded by an [Alghoul](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul "Alghoul").
