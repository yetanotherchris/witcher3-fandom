## Item ID

mq2038_headsman_sword

**Horned Hoskuld's sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and possible [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During [Iron Maiden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_Maiden "Iron Maiden"), [Jutta](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jutta_an_Dimun "Jutta an Dimun") may send [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") out to retrieve this sword to prove he's worthy of fighting her. If so, it can be found on the western shore of [Faroe Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle"), in a sunken chest. 

## Associated quest

  * [Iron Maiden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_Maiden "Iron Maiden")

## Notes

  * There may be a bug where if you equip this sword into your steel sword slot, without first removing your current steel sword, the sword already in that slot will be replaced by this one, and will be missing from your [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory").
  * If you acquired the sword, but don't present it to Jutta during the quest, it will remain forever as a quest item in your inventory, and it is considerably heavier than most other quest items. The only way to remove it then is on PC via the console command removeitem('mq2038_headsman_sword')
