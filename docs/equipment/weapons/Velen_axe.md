**Velen axe** is a secondary weapon in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found as random loot, often in military camps, or purchased from almost any [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith").
