## Item ID

Wild Hunt sword 3

**Wild Hunt warrior's sword - superior** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") that was [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't appear during normal gameplay. 

It was supposed to be dropped by [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") warriors during [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen").
