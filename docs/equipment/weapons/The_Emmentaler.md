## Item ID

Cheesecutter

**The Emmentaler** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can only be obtained through the secondary quest [Of Dairy and Darkness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Dairy_and_Darkness "Of Dairy and Darkness"). 

## Associated quest

  * [Of Dairy and Darkness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Dairy_and_Darkness "Of Dairy and Darkness")

## Notes

  * Since [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), dismantling this sword also produces [cheese](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cheese_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Cheese \(The Witcher 3\)").

## Trivia

  * Emmentaler, more often known as [emmental](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emmental_cheese) or Swiss cheese, is a type of cheese that originated from around the Emmental region in Switzerland.
