## Item ID

Cleaver

**Cleaver hood** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the body of [Therazane](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Therazane "Therazane") or as random loot.
