# Ard'aenye

The name of this blade is [Elder Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Speech "Elder Speech") and translates as _mountain fire_ or _greatest fire_. It was quite likely inspired by the real world family of [flame-bladed swords](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flame-bladed_sword "wikipedia:Flame-bladed sword"): _flambard_ s, _flammard_ s and _flamberge_ s.

**Ard'aenye** is a sword which _used to be_ found during [Chapter IV](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chapters "Chapters") of _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ , the first time [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") entered the [fields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fields "Fields"). It is **no longer available** in the game, but does appear to be the same model as the sword used by the [Grand Master](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grand_Master "Grand Master") of the [Order of the Flaming Rose](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Order_of_the_Flaming_Rose "Order of the Flaming Rose"). It closely resembles the description of a [gnomish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnome "Gnome") [gwyhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gwyhyr "Gwyhyr") in the novels. 

In the [Enhanced Edition](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher:_Enhanced_Edition "The Witcher: Enhanced Edition"), **Ard'aenye** was once obtainable and used to be found during [Chapter V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chapters "Chapters") of _[The Witcher](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_\(game\) "The Witcher \(game\)")_ , inside [Raven's crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Raven%27s_crypt "Raven's crypt"), in the [swamp cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swamp_cemetery "Swamp cemetery"). It was found inside one of the graves of **graf Corbus de Ruyter** with the [Remains of Raven's Armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Remains_of_Raven%27s_Armor "Remains of Raven's Armor"). After the last patch update, **Ard'aenye** was replaced by a [meteorite sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_meteorite_swords "The Witcher meteorite swords"), regardless of the description in the [Enhanced Edition](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher:_Enhanced_Edition "The Witcher: Enhanced Edition")'s official Game Guide book on page 105. 

There are actually two steel weapons called Ard'aenye that Geralt can use on the first game, one is the copy of the sword used by the Grand Master, and the other is the heavy mace used by the class of mutant known as [Greater brother](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_brother "Greater brother"), however the Ard'aenye heavy mace can only be equipped using a Save Editing Tool and unfortunately is not possible to tranfer the weapon to the second game using the save import system.
