## Item ID

Viper School steel sword  
NGP Viper School steel sword ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"))

**Viper steel sword** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Viper School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_School_Gear "Viper School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. The diagram can be found in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), in the [Amavet Fortress Ruins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Amavet_Fortress_Ruins "Amavet Fortress Ruins").
