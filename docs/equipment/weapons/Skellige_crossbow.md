## Item ID

DLC13 Skellige Crossbow

**Skellige crossbow** is one of three [crossbows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") added to _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ by the [Elite Crossbow set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elite_Crossbow_set "Elite Crossbow set") [free DLC](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC"). It can be purchased from the [blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yanne "Yanne") in [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel").
