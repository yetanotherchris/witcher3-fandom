## Item ID

Caroline

**Caroline** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be found on [Wham-a-Wham](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wham-a-Wham "Wham-a-Wham")'s corpse, if he is killed, and relatively often as random loot.
