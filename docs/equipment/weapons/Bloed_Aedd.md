## Item ID

Bloedeaedd

**Bloed Aedd** is a relic [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It may be obtained as random loot, on [Sarasti](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarasti "Sarasti")'s corpse during [Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Mystery_of_the_Byways_Murders "Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders"), or from the treasure hunt [Shortcut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shortcut "Shortcut").
