**Dwarven axe** returns in the third game as a secondary weapon and can be found as random loot or dropped from the [Shrieker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shrieker "Shrieker"). It can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") from the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall") (after the area is liberated)
  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Blacksmith") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
