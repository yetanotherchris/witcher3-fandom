## Item ID

Lynx School Crossbow

**Feline crossbow** is a craftable [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") and is part of the [Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Unlike other school gear weapons and armor, the crossbow can not be upgraded and doesn't have a "legendary" version.
