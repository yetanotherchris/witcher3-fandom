# Black Unicorn

**Black Unicorn** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_sword "Steel sword") in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ and _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

## _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_

## Black Unicorn

[ ![Tw2 weapon Darkdifficultysteelsworda2](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/eb/Tw2_weapon_Darkdifficultysteelsworda2.png/revision/latest?cb=20111007114859) ](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/eb/Tw2_weapon_Darkdifficultysteelsworda2.png/revision/latest?cb=20111007114859 "Tw2 weapon Darkdifficultysteelsworda2") In combat, drains foes of vitality while increasing the witcher's.
