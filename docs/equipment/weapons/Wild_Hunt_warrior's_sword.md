## Item ID

Wild Hunt sword 1

**Wild Hunt warrior's sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , available exclusively as a drop from [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") warriors, whether they appear in person or through a spectral projection. Those enemies only appear during four main quests: 

  * [Nithral](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nithral "Nithral") during [Wandering in the Dark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_in_the_Dark "Wandering in the Dark")
  * In person warriors during [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen")
  * Ghosts inside the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt") during [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone")
  * In person warriors during [On Thin Ice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/On_Thin_Ice "On Thin Ice") (though usually ignored and hard to loot due to the constant action)
