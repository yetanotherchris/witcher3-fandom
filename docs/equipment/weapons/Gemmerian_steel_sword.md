# Gemmerian steel sword

**Gemmerian steel sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It comes in the following forms: 

  * Gemmerian steel sword (crafted)
  * Gemmerian steel sword (basic)

## Item ID

Nilfgaardian sword 4

This version is found quite rarely as random loot, but is notably dropped by [Nilfgaardian soldiers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army") killed during the secondary quest [An Eye for an Eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Eye_for_an_Eye "An Eye for an Eye"). It can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
