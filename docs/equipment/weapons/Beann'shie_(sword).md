The Beann'shie is the most powerful and one of the best [steel swords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_steel_swords "The Witcher 2 steel swords") in _The Witcher 2_. The [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Beann%27shie "Diagram: Beann'shie") to craft this weapon can be found in the ruins of the asylum in Chapter I as random loot, but it is extremely rare. You can find a crafted Beann'shie in the same place but it is even more rare. The diagram can also be found randomly in Chapter III in the Nekker Cave. 

### Notes

  * This weapon was removed in the PC version of _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings - Enhanced Edition](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings_-_Enhanced_Edition "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings - Enhanced Edition")_.

Beannshie

While technically only available as random loot, due to a randomization bug, **Beann'shie** is guaranteed to appear in the following places after reaching a high enough level: 

  * [Guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") on an island northwest of [An Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Skellig "An Skellig"), guarded by a [basilisk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk").
  * Guarded treasure on a small island southwest of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad") (near the [Feline crossbow diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Feline_crossbow "Diagram: Feline crossbow")), guarded by a basilisk.
  * Guarded treasure northeast of [Harpy Feeding Ground](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy_Feeding_Ground "Harpy Feeding Ground"), guarded by a basilisk.
  * Guarded treasure in the middle (and just a bit to the west) between [Inn at the Crossroads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads "Inn at the Crossroads") and [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten"), guarded by a [Bilge hag](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag "Water hag").
