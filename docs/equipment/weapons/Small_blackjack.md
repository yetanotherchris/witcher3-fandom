This weapon is among the first that Geralt can find when he begins his escape from the [La Valette dungeons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dungeons_of_La_Valette_Castle "Dungeons of La Valette Castle"). Afterwards, it can occasionally be found in loot throughout the game. Strangely the blackjack appears to work similar to an iron sword with a range longer than the weapon and unsheathing sheathing and hit noises used by the iron sword.
