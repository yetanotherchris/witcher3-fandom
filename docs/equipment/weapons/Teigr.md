## Item ID

mq1058_cat_school_sword

**Teigr** is a [witcher gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_witcher_gear "The Witcher 3 witcher gear") steel sword in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can either be obtained during [Where the Cat and Wolf Play...](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Where_the_Cat_and_Wolf_Play... "Where the Cat and Wolf Play...") as loot from [Gaetan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gaetan "Gaetan"), or during [Take What You Want](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Take_What_You_Want "Take What You Want") if he's spared. 

## Bugs

  * Sometimes Gaetan doesn't have Teigr on him when he dies. Waiting until after Geralt comments about needing to go find the little girl to loot his body might correct this. If he still doesn't have it at that point, one must reload from a prior save (the game provides a checkpoint save file just before you enter combat with him).

## Notes

  * Teigr has identical pommel to Feline steel sword, but the scabbard's color pattern and design is different from regular [Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") gear.
  * Teigr, along with [Viper venomous silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_venomous_silver_sword "Viper venomous silver sword"), are the only Witcher gears that can't be reproduced due to lack of in-game diagrams.

## Trivia

  * Teigr is a Welsh word for tiger.
