## Item ID

q402 Skellige sword 3

**Winter's Blade** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is said to have been forged in [Mahakam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mahakam "Mahakam") and tempered in [dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon "Dragon") fire. It once belonged to [King](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_the_Skellige_Isles "King of the Skellige Isles") [Erlend Stonefist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erlend_Stonefist "Erlend Stonefist") and has remained in [Clan an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite") for centuries. 

It is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Crach an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite") as his way of helping for [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen"), but is unfortunately not useful there as [Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wild_Hunt "Wild Hunt") warriors are susceptible to [silver swords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword"). 

As of [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), the damage of this weapon scales with the character's level and it has a third rune slot. In order to receive the level up damage increase, Geralt has to carry the sword, at least in the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory").
