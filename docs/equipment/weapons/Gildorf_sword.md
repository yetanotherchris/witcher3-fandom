# Gildorf sword

**Gildorf sword** , so named in honor of [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_.

  * Gildorf sword (crafted)

## Item ID

Novigraadan sword 2

Aside from random loot, one such sword can be found on a [witch hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter")'s corpse inside the [White Orchard cemetery](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard_cemetery "White Orchard cemetery") chapel. Somewhat oddly, it is also dropped by [Fallen Knights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fallen_Knight "Fallen Knight") and thugs during the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion main quest [Open Sesame!](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Open_Sesame!_\(Hearts_of_Stone\) "Open Sesame! \(Hearts of Stone\)"). 

It can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
