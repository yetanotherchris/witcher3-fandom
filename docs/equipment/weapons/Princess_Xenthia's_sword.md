**Princess Xenthia's sword** can be won in a game of dice poker with [Skalen Burdon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skalen_Burdon "Skalen Burdon"), after completing the quest "[The Spear of Destiny](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Spear_of_Destiny "The Spear of Destiny")". Likewise on [Iorveth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iorveth "Iorveth")'s path it can be obtained from the same source anytime during Chapter II. Prior to Version 2.0, a [rusty steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rusty_steel_sword "Rusty steel sword") was won instead. 

### Notes

  * As of the Enhanced Edition, this sword no longer looks like an Elven blade.
  * On the Xbox 360, a bug may occur where Skalen tells you that you won the Princess Xenthia Sword. However, you get a [Robust Kaedweni sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Robust_Kaedweni_sword "Robust Kaedweni sword") or a [rusty steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rusty_steel_sword "Rusty steel sword") instead. You may also even get nothing at all (as of June 6, 2012).

## Item ID

Princessxenthiasword

**Princess Xenthia's sword** can be found as random loot, somewhat often at the following locations: 

  * [Kaer Gelen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Gelen "Kaer Gelen") in a chest guarded by a [Katakan](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan")
  * in a chest on top of the battlements of the [Bloody Baron](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Phillip_Strenger "Phillip Strenger")'s castle at [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch")
  * [Whoreson Junior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyprian_Wiley "Cyprian Wiley")'s [casino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whoreson_Junior%27s_casino "Whoreson Junior's casino") in the interrogation room's chest

### Notes

  * The sign intensity rolls aren't independent and will always be the same for both Igni and Axii.

## Trivia

  * As the name suggests, this sword refers to [Xena](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xena "wikipedia:Xena"), a fictional character of a TV series.
