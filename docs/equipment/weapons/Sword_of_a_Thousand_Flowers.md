**Sword of a Thousand Flowers** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and part of the [Dol Blathanna Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dol_Blathanna_Armor_Set "Dol Blathanna Armor Set") for _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It was released on December 14, 2022 as part of [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), the next-gen update, for owning or upgrading to _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ \- Complete Edition. 

The entire set can be found in [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s room in [Vizima](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vizima "Vizima"), in a chest under a window, along with the [White Tiger of the West Armor Set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Tiger_of_the_West_Armor_Set "White Tiger of the West Armor Set") and the [Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Swords_of_the_Nine-Tailed_Vixen "Swords of the Nine-Tailed Vixen"). 

## Notes

  * When placed on a weapon rack in [Corvo Bianco](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Corvo_Bianco "Corvo Bianco") with the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, this and its counterpart will incorrectly bug and display the scabbard of the sword floating above the rack.
  * This sword has two unused item descriptions, possibly intended to alternate depending on whether they appear in a regular playthrough or [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"): 
    * _Forged by elves for killing humans._
    * _A blade given to the commanders of elven commandos._
