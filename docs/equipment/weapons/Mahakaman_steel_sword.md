# Mahakaman steel sword

**Mahakaman steel sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in two variants: 

  * Mahakaman steel sword (crafted)
  * Mahakaman steel sword (basic)
