**Harvall** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_steel_swords "The Witcher steel swords") which can be found: 

  * In Chapter II, or III, in the swamp on a decomposing body on [Wyvern Island](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern_Island "Wyvern Island").
  * Or in Chapter IV on a corpse near the wyverns in the [Fields](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fields "Fields"), north of the [Small farm](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Small_farm "Small farm") (though it seems it is there only the first time you visit the area). It doesn't seem to appear if you already looted it from the corpse in the swamps.
  * It can also be obtained by completing [Sabrina Glevissig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sabrina_Glevissig "Sabrina Glevissig")'s mission in _[The Price of Neutrality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Price_of_Neutrality "The Price of Neutrality")_ premium module.

It is a much better blade than the default [witcher's steel blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_sword "Steel sword"), and probably better than any [meteorite sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Meteorite_swords "Meteorite swords") you may have at that point in the game. (You should compare the [stats](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_swords "Steel swords") just to be sure.) Regardless, if you find the sword in Chapter II, definitely compare it to your current steel blade and sell whichever one has the worst stats, keeping the better one for yourself.

It is likely that Geralt would have had to side with the Order in the first game and import.

## Item ID

Harvall

**Harvall** returns in the third game and can only be found in four quest-related locations. 

The first and easiest to access is during the [treasure hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_treasure_hunts "The Witcher 3 treasure hunts") quest [An Unfortunate Turn of Events](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Unfortunate_Turn_of_Events "An Unfortunate Turn of Events"), and the second is another treasure hunt, [A Plea Ignored](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Plea_Ignored "A Plea Ignored"), which however involves a much higher level enemy. 

The other two chests unfortunately require [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") to have reached level 30 before Harvall will appear in them (but it is guaranteed at that point), even though the locations are usually visited much earlier, so you may want to leave them to loot later: 

  * Up in the lighthouse at [Lornruk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lornruk "Lornruk"), next to [Dobromir's journal](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dobromir%27s_journal "Dobromir's journal") from [Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear").
  * Behind a breakable wall in the sewers underneath [The Kingfisher Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Kingfisher_Inn "The Kingfisher Inn"). You will gain access to this area during [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), and the spot in question is just before reaching the underground library.
