**Griffin steel sword - enhanced** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is needed to craft [Griffin steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_steel_sword_-_superior "Griffin steel sword - superior").
