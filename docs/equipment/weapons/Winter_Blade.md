**Winter Blade** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can only be found as random loot, usually around [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). One place where it spawns relatively often is [Yustianna's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna%27s_Grotto "Yustianna's Grotto"), in the chamber where [Yustianna Disturbed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna_Disturbed "Yustianna Disturbed") takes place.
