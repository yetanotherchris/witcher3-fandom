## Item ID

W_Axe01

**Blunt axe** is a secondary weapon in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be purchased from all [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in the [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"). It can also be found as random loot, particularly often on battlefield corpses.
