**Wild Hunt warrior axe** is a secondary weapon in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Known sources: 

  * On [Djenge Frett](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Djenge_Frett "Djenge Frett")'s body if he dies during [The Sad Tale of the Grossbart Brothers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sad_Tale_of_the_Grossbart_Brothers "The Sad Tale of the Grossbart Brothers").
  * On one of [Ves](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ves "Ves")' two Blue Stripe comrades who die during [An Eye for an Eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Eye_for_an_Eye "An Eye for an Eye").

## Notes

  * Unlike other Relic items with randomized level requirements, this axe may spawn at a level much higher than Geralt's, often as high as 10 above.
  * This axe was at some point intended to be found at the [guarded treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") in an inlet northwest of [Elverum Lighthouse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elverum_Lighthouse "Elverum Lighthouse") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"), but its container definition, though still in the game files, is unused and the chest there is randomized instead.
