## Item ID

Rusty Novigraadan sword

**Rusty Novigrad sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_sword "Steel sword") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is dropped by bandits in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") and found on battlefield corpses throughout the game. Early on, since the [Missing in Action](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Missing_in_Action "Missing in Action") quest takes place on a [battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_\(White_Orchard\) "Battlefield \(White Orchard\)"), many can be found there.
