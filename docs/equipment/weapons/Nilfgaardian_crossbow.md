## Item ID

DLC13 Nilfgaardian Crossbow

**Nilfgaardian crossbow** is one of three [crossbows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") added to _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ by the [Elite Crossbow set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elite_Crossbow_set "Elite Crossbow set") [free DLC](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC"). It can be purchased from the [quartermaster](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quartermaster_\(Crow%27s_Perch\) "Quartermaster \(Crow's Perch\)") in [Crow's Perch](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crow%27s_Perch "Crow's Perch").
