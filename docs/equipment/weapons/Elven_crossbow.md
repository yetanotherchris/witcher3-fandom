## Item ID

DLC13 Elven Crossbow

**Elven crossbow** is one of three [crossbows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") added to _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ by the [Elite Crossbow set](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elite_Crossbow_set "Elite Crossbow set") [free DLC](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_DLC "Category:The Witcher 3 DLC"). It can be purchased from the [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Gildorf\) "Merchant \(Gildorf\)") in [Gildorf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gildorf "Gildorf") district [shop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shop_\(Gildorf\) "Shop \(Gildorf\)").
