## Item ID

NGP Wolf School steel sword 2

**Legendary Wolven steel sword - superior** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. It is needed to craft [legendary Wolven steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Wolven_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Legendary Wolven steel sword - mastercrafted").
