## Item ID

Gryphon School steel sword 3

**Legendary Griffin steel sword - mastercrafted** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. 

It is needed to craft [grandmaster Legendary Griffin steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Legendary_Griffin_steel_sword "Grandmaster Legendary Griffin steel sword") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion.
