**Barber-surgeon** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_steel_swords "The Witcher 2 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_. It cannot be found, but crafted after finding the [corresponding diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Barber-surgeon "Diagram: Barber-surgeon"), which can be found in a chest located on the ground floor of the watchtower in [Flotsam](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Flotsam "Flotsam"), where [Bernard Loredo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bernard_Loredo "Bernard Loredo") attempted to burn some [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") women alive. 

### Notes

  * This weapon was removed in the PC version of _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings - Enhanced Edition](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings_-_Enhanced_Edition "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings - Enhanced Edition")_.

**Barber-Surgeon** returns as a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in the third game. It only be found as random loot inside caves in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), and from [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure"). 

### Notes

  * The [aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard") [sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") intensity roll will always match with chance to dismember, and the bonus experience and gold rolls will match with one another too.
