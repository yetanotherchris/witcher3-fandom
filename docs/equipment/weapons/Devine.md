## Item ID

Devine

**Devine** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found on the corpse of [Hagubman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hagubman "Hagubman") during [Contract: Strange Beast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Strange_Beast "Contract: Strange Beast") or as random loot, though the latter is very uncommon. 

## Associated quest

  * [Contract: Strange Beast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Strange_Beast "Contract: Strange Beast")

## Notes

  * At some point, this sword was also supposed to be received along with [Bleidd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bleidd "Bleidd"), instead of the [Rose of Shaerrawed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rose_of_Shaerrawed "Rose of Shaerrawed"), if one chooses to clear the armory during the main quest [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen").
