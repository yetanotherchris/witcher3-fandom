## Item ID

Nilfgaardian sword 3

**Vicovaro blade** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found as random loot or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") and [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
