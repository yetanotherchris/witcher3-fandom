## Item ID

Crossbow q206

**Death From Above** is a [crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be received as a reward from [Crach an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite"), if [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") chooses to help [Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite") or [Hjalmar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar_an_Craite "Hjalmar an Craite") after the massacre at the feast during [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit").
