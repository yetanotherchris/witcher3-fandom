# Dancer

**Dancer** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steel_sword "Steel sword") in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ and _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_.

## Regular Value

### Sell

80 [![Tw2 icon oren](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7d/Tw2_icon_oren.svg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/12?cb=20110601231431)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren")

Added by the _[Enhanced Edition](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Enhanced_Edition "The Witcher 2: Enhanced Edition")_ update. It can be found in the [nekker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker "Nekker") cave while escorting [Brigida](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brigida_Papebrock "Brigida Papebrock") through the [forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne_forest "Loc Muinne forest") during the [Crown Witness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown_Witness "Crown Witness") quest. Along with [Forgotten vran sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_vran_sword "Forgotten vran sword"), [Caerme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caerme "Caerme"), and [Mourner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mourner "Mourner"), it is one of the best steel swords in the game. It is only accessible in chapter 3. 

It is also possible to obtain a crafting diagram for Dancer in the [Burned down hospital](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Burned_down_hospital "Burned down hospital") during the quest [In the Claws of Madness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Claws_of_Madness "In the Claws of Madness") in chapter I. Albeit extremely rare, it can be part of the loot in the chest behind the fake wall after the room where you fight the second pair of wraiths.
