## Item ID

Wolf School steel sword 3

**Wolven steel sword - mastercrafted** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is needed to craft [Grandmaster Wolven steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Wolven_steel_sword "Grandmaster Wolven steel sword").
