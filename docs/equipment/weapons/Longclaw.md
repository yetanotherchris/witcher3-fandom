# Longclaw

**Longclaw** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in 2 forms: 

  * Longclaw (crafted)
  * Longclaw (relic)

**Longclaw** can be found on the body of [Morvudd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvudd "Morvudd"), in the reward chest for [Unlucky's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unlucky%27s_Treasure "Unlucky's Treasure"), or as random loot.

  * [Contract: Missing Son](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Missing_Son "Contract: Missing Son")

## Notes

  * This sword has its own unique, but unused image in the game files. Many other craftable swords in the game use the starter [Witcher's steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher%27s_steel_sword#Witcher's_steel_sword_\(starter\) "Witcher's steel sword") inventory icon, likely due to oversight, but Longclaw is the only one where both of its versions use the incorrect one.

## Trivia

  * This sword is named after the [bastard sword of the same name](https://awoiaf.westeros.org/index.php/Longclaw) from the _[A Song of Ice and Fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire "wikipedia:A Song of Ice and Fire")_ series by [George R. R. Martin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_R._R._Martin "wikipedia:George R. R. Martin").
