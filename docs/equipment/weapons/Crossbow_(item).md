# Crossbow (item)

![Disambig icon](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/5/5e/Disambig_icon.svg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/42?cb=20230420235840) | _This article is about items named "Crossbow". For a general description of crossbows in_[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt") _, see[Crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow")._

There are 7 homonymously named [crossbows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crossbow "Crossbow") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , all effectively versions of the same weapon, with linearly growing power, level requirements and rarity as loot. All except the common one can be purchased or found as random loot, but each stronger version will be available in fewer places and after reaching a higher level threshold. For simplicity, they are presented here grouped by item iters.

## Item ID

Crossbow 1

The most basic crossbow, received from [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), just before fighting the [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)") during the main quest [The Beast of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beast_of_White_Orchard "The Beast of White Orchard"). 

If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has no crossbow on him, it may also be auto-equipped for free during other quests, when shooting a crossbow is required. The one exception being the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion quest [Turn and Face the Strange](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Turn_and_Face_the_Strange "Turn and Face the Strange"), where it is strategically placed as loot instead.
