# Gwyhyr

A **gwyhyr** is a splendid [gnomish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnome "Gnome") sword, regarded as the best in the world. The gnomes had ceased production of these weapons more than two hundred years before the events of the [saga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher_saga "Witcher saga"). Gwyhyrs were forged with "dark iron" which made the blades light and sharp as a razor. They were also characterized by their undulating edge and [ray](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ray "Ray") shagreen covered hilts. 

In late [1267](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Timeline#1267 "Timeline"), [Leo Bonhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leo_Bonhart "Leo Bonhart") takes his prisoner [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri") (using the alias Falka) to the swordsmith [Esterhazy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Esterhazy "Esterhazy") to give her a sword to fight with in the [Claremont](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Claremont "Claremont") [arena](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arena_in_Claremont "Arena in Claremont"). Esterhazy reveals an expensive gwyhyr he had in storage and gives it to Ciri as a gift, who thus names it [Zireael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zireael_\(sword\) "Zireael \(sword\)").

Gwyhyr appears in _The Witcher 2_ as a rare steel sword. It is the best steel sword available in Act 2. Geralt obtains the sword by completing the [Baltimore's Nightmare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baltimore%27s_Nightmare "Baltimore's Nightmare") quest in Chapter 2 if he sides with [Iorveth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iorveth "Iorveth"). To obtain it, he must keep Master [Baltimore's notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Baltimore%27s_notes "Baltimore's notes") from Thorak (i.e. he cannot show him the notes!). this ultimately leads to the witcher being forced to kill [Thorak](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Thorak "Thorak") after which he returns to Alderman [Cecil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cecil_Burdon "Cecil Burdon") and is rewarded with the sword. 

Alternatively, showing Thorak the notes but rejecting his offer to let him search the workshop first will turn Thorak and his team hostile. This has the same result as not showing Baltimore's notes to him in the first place.

**Gwyhyr** returns as a steel sword in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

One can be found on a [Nilfgaardian soldier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army "Nilfgaardian Army")'s corpse inside the cave where [Harrisi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harrisi "Harrisi") has her nest during the quest [Contract: Missing Brother](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Missing_Brother "Contract: Missing Brother"), in one of the alcoves in the final chamber. It is otherwise only obtainable as random loot - from [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") after reaching level 15, and in some caves in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") after reaching level 25.
