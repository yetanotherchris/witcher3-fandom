# Tir Tochair blade

**Tir Tochair blade** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in 2 different forms: 

  * Tir Tochair blade (crafted)
  * Tir Tochair blade (relic)
