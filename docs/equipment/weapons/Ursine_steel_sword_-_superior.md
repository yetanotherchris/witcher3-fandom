## Item ID

Bear School steel sword 2  
NGP Bear School steel sword 2 ([New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +"))

**Ursine steel sword - superior** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is needed to craft [Ursine steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Ursine steel sword - mastercrafted").
