## Item ID

Skellige sword 3_crafted

**Clan Hindar sword** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't appear during gameplay. 

As implied by its item ID, this was intended to be a craftable sword, but the diagram for it doesn't exist. Unlike other similar swords such as the [Faroe blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_blade "Faroe blade"), there's no non-crafted version of it either, although some remnants of such do exist in the game files and evidently it has simply been deleted. 

Interestingly, in spite of being named after [Clan Hindar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Hindar "Clan Hindar") and not [an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_an_Craite "Clan an Craite"), its base template was used to create [Hjalmar's steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar%27s_steel_sword "Hjalmar's steel sword").
