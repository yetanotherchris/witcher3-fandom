**Caerme** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_steel_swords "The Witcher 2 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ and can be crafted after obtaining [its diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Caerme "Diagram: Caerme") as a reward for the quest [An Encrypted Manuscript](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/An_Encrypted_Manuscript "An Encrypted Manuscript"). Along with the [Forgotten vran sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_vran_sword "Forgotten vran sword"), [Mourner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mourner "Mourner") (Dark Mode only), and [Dancer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dancer "Dancer"), it is one of the best steel swords in the game.

## Item ID

Caerme

**Caerme** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , available as random loot inside caves in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), and from guarded treasures. 

One location where it can often be found (if not guaranteed) is the guarded treasure inside a crypt at [Blandare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blandare "Blandare")'s cemetery (north from the village), guarded by 3 [wraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wraith "Wraith").
