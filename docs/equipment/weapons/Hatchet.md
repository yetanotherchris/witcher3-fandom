**Hatchet** returns in the third game as a secondary weapon and is always found in the big chest near the [Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mill "Mill") in [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"), as well as being purchasable from all [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Blacksmith") in the [Northern Realms](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Northern_Kingdoms "Northern Kingdoms"). It can also be found as random loot, particularly often on battlefield corpses.
