# Bremervoord blade

**Bremervoord blade** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") available in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in the following forms: 

  * Bremervoord blade (crafted)
  * Bremervoord blade (basic)

## Item ID

No Mans Land sword 4

Can rarely be found as random loot, but one is always dropped by a bandit during [A Dog's Life](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dog%27s_Life "A Dog's Life"), and it can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")

### Associated quest

  * [A Dog's Life](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Dog%27s_Life "A Dog's Life")
