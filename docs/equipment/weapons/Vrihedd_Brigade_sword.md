# Vrihedd Brigade sword

**Vrihedd Brigade sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_.

## Item ID

Scoiatael sword 1

In addition to regular random loot, at least one can be picked up during [Contract: Woodland Beast](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Woodland_Beast "Contract: Woodland Beast"), where regardless of choices made, one is propped up at the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") camp. All [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") warriors, [Vernossiel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vernossiel "Vernossiel") and her band included, will drop the sword if killed as well, but there are only a couple of other secondary quests where they appear. 

It can also be purchased from the [Scoia'tael merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael_Crossroads\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael Crossroads\)") in the forest east of [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt"), after he is rescued.
