# Wild Hunt warrior's sword - enhanced

**Wild Hunt warrior's sword - enhanced** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") that was [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Both of its versions can be obtained through console commands, but don't appear during normal gameplay: 

  * Wild Hunt warrior's sword - enhanced (relic)
  * Wild Hunt warrior's sword - enhanced (basic)
