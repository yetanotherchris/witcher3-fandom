## Regular Value

### Sell

118 [![Tw2 icon oren](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7d/Tw2_icon_oren.svg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/12?cb=20110601231431)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren")

This sword can be found in a locked safe in [Dethmold](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dethmold "Dethmold")'s room beneath the [Ruined library](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_library "Ruined library") in [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne") during the quest "[Of His Blood and Bone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_His_Blood_and_Bone "Of His Blood and Bone")" on [Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roche "Roche")'s path. The [key](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dethmold%27s_safe_key "Dethmold's safe key") to the safe can be looted from Dethmold's body. Along with [Caerme](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Caerme "Caerme"), [Mourner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mourner "Mourner") (Dark Mode only), and [Dancer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dancer "Dancer"), it is one of the best steel swords in the game. 

As of the [Enhanced Edition](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Enhanced_Edition "The Witcher 2: Enhanced Edition"), this sword is now available on [Iorveth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Iorveth "Iorveth")'s path as well. It is located within the [Crypt of Mages](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crypt_of_Mages "Crypt of Mages") in the same tower as the quest [The Secrets of Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Secrets_of_Loc_Muinne "The Secrets of Loc Muinne"). The player must cast Aard or Igni at the appropriate crystals to open a hidden door. Inside the next room is the sword. Taking it activates a boss fight with [The Guardian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Guardian "The Guardian").
