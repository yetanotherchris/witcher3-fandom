## Item ID

Hjalmar_Short_Steel_Sword

**Hjalmar's steel sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

[Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can win this sword from [Hjalmar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar_an_Craite "Hjalmar an Craite") if he defeats a [Vildkaarl](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vildkaarl "Vildkaarl") in a fistfight at the banquet in the [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel") during the quest [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"). This fistfight must be done before you complete the first quest objective by collecting your reward from [Crach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite").
