# Blade from the Bits

**Blade from the Bits** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that comes in 3 different forms: 

  * Blade from the Bits (relic)
  * Blade from the Bits (crafted)
  * Blade from the Bits (basic)

## Item ID

sq304 Novigraadan sword 4

This version is received from [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori") once [Of Swords and Dumplings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Swords_and_Dumplings "Of Swords and Dumplings") is completed. 

As of [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), the damage of this weapon scales with the character's level and it has three rune slots. In order to receive the level up damage increase, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") has to carry the sword, at least in the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory"). 

### Associated quest

  * [Of Swords and Dumplings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Of_Swords_and_Dumplings "Of Swords and Dumplings")
