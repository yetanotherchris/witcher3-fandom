## Item ID

Lynx School steel sword

**Legendary Feline steel sword** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. It is needed to craft [legendary Feline steel sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_steel_sword_-_enhanced "Legendary Feline steel sword - enhanced").
