**Witcher's Steel Blade** is one of the steel swords that [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") receives at the beginning of the game, during the Prologue (the very first being a [rusty sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rusty_sword "Rusty sword")). Has the general two-handed longsword model. 

Although it is used primarily against [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human") and reportedly has little effect on monsters, Geralt is able to fight off most monsters during the early stages of the game using only this sword. 

In the Prologue, [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") mentions that witchers have good cold steel. The best improvement for this sword is to have a [meteorite sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_meteorite_swords "The Witcher meteorite swords") forged.
