**D'yaebl** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_steel_swords "The Witcher steel swords") given to Geralt by [Velerad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velerad "Velerad") as a gift from [King Foltest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King_Foltest "King Foltest") at the end of [Her Highness the Striga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Her_Highness_the_Striga "Her Highness the Striga") in Chapter V if he cures [Princess Adda](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Adda_the_White "Adda the White") a second time. It is used primarily against [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Humans "Humans") and has little effect on [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_bestiary "Category:The Witcher bestiary"). The sword is supposedly coated in dragon blood. Also carried by [Azar Javed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Azar_Javed "Azar Javed") during the end of Chapter II. The sword penetrates armor though it isn't mentioned in the description.
