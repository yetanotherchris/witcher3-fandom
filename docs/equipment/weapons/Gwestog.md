## Item ID

Gwestog

**Gwestog** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is received as a reward for defeating the [bear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") [Olaf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olaf_\(bear\) "Olaf \(bear\)") during [Fists of Fury: Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fists_of_Fury:_Skellige "Fists of Fury: Skellige"). 

In addition to regular random loot from treasure chests, it is also known to drop randomly from Witch Hunters during the quest [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never").
