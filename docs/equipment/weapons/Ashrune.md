## Item ID

Ashrune

**Ashrune** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

Can be found in a chest right beside the [Yngvar's Fang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yngvar%27s_Fang "Yngvar's Fang") signpost or as random loot.
