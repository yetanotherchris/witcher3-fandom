# Red meteorite steel sword

**Red meteorite steel sword** is a [The Witcher 3 steel swords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") that was [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Both of its versions can be obtained through console commands, but don't appear during normal gameplay: 

  * Red meteorite steel sword (crafted)
  * Red meteorite steel sword (basic)
