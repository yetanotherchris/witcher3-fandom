# Maribor sword

**Maribor sword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in the following forms: 

  * Maribor sword (crafted)
  * Maribor sword (basic)

## Item ID

No Mans Land sword 3

Can be found as random loot or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad"), [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")

One is also always dropped by one of the bandits encountered during the [secondary quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_secondary_quests "The Witcher 3 secondary quests") [Death By Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Death_By_Fire "Death By Fire"), and it is scripted to appear on some dead soldier bodies, particularly near [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure"): 

  * Two corpses at the [drowners](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") guarded treasure west of the [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks") signpost, on the shore around a dead whale
  * Near the [armored arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armored_arachas "Armored arachas") guarded treasure above the [Cavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cavern "Cavern") north of [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")
  * At the [Earth Elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_Elemental "Earth Elemental") guarded treasure in a stone circle east of [Kimbolt Way](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kimbolt_Way "Kimbolt Way")
