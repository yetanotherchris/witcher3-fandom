## Item ID

Wooden sword

**Wooden sword** is a prop weapon and a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is only used during a couple of quests where lethal force isn't desired, like sparring with [Rosa var Attre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosa_var_Attre "Rosa var Attre"). The one obtained from [Open Sesame!](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Open_Sesame!_\(Hearts_of_Stone\) "Open Sesame! \(Hearts of Stone\)") can be kept, although it's also possible to re-acquire the other one by returning to the [Var Attre Villa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Var_Attre_Villa "Var Attre Villa").
