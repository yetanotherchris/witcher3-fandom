# Velen longsword

**Velen longsword** is a [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in 3 different forms: 

  * Velen longsword (crafted)
  * Velen longsword (common)
  * Velen longsword (master)

It can be found as random loot, and is a very common drop from human enemies in the [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") area. It can also be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All base game [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Calveit_Watchtower\) "Merchant \(Calveit Watchtower\)") at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(battlefield\) "Merchant \(battlefield\)") by the road west of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort")
  * Weapons [merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
  * [Zoltan Chivay](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zoltan_Chivay "Zoltan Chivay") during [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen") (dependent)

## Item ID

No Mans Land sword 1 q2

This version of the sword can also be found often as random loot or enemy drops, but it has three specific locations where it is guaranteed: 

  * On a dead soldier's body to the left of the [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard") [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)")'s nest.
  * On a charred body in the burned down building near the broken road gate west of the village in White Orchard.
  * On a burned body in [Mowshurst](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mowshurst "Mowshurst") (north of [Reardon Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Reardon_Manor "Reardon Manor")), where [Out of the Frying Pan, into the Fire](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Out_of_the_Frying_Pan,_into_the_Fire "Out of the Frying Pan, into the Fire") takes place.
  * On a dead soldier's body near a shrine north of [The Orphans of Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Orphans_of_Crookback_Bog "The Orphans of Crookback Bog"), just south of a nearby ruined house.

It can also be purchased from all merchants who sell the Common version.
