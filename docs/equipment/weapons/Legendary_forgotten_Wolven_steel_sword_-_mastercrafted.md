## Item ID

Netflix steel sword 1

**Legendary forgotten Wolven steel sword - mastercrafted** is a craftable [steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_steel_swords "The Witcher 3 steel swords") and is part of the [Forgotten Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_Wolf_School_Gear "Forgotten Wolf School Gear") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ with the [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +") option. The gear was introduced with the next-gen update, [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)").
