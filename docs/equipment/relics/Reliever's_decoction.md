## Tips

  * The description can be misleading, although the effect will prevent specters from turning immaterial again, the first strike must be dealt after forcing it to become material with [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") and/or [Moon Dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Moon_Dust "Moon Dust"), after which it will be unable to become immaterial again as long as the potion is active, thus removing the need to use Yrden or Moon Dust for the remainder of the fight. 
    * Although it's possible to strike a specter while it's immaterial and deal minor damage the potion will not take effect.
  * The effect is particularly useful when fighting [noonwraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith "Noonwraith") and [nightwraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith").
  * The effect to disable other abilities is no joke as it renders specters practically unable to fight back. 
    * It will not prevent noonwraiths or nightwraiths from spliting into 3 smaller versions to regenerate, but it will still prevent them from becoming immaterial once they reappear.
