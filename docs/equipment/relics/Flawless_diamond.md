## Item ID

Diamond flawless

**Flawless diamond** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is not needed to craft anything but can be dismantled into [diamond](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diamond "Diamond"). 

It can be found as random loot, dismantled from [Toussaint steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint_steel_sword#Toussaint_steel_sword_\(magic\) "Toussaint steel sword") (magic), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(boatyard\) "Merchant \(boatyard\)") in the [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp")
