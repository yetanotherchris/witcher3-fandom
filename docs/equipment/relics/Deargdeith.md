## Item ID

Deargdeith

**Deargdeith** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be looted from [Gael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gael "Gael") and [Hubert Rejk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk"), as well as found as random loot, though the latter is very uncommon.
