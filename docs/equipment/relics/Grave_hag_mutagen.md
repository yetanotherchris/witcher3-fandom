## Item ID

Grave Hag mutagen

**Grave hag mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to craft [grave hag decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grave_hag_decoction "Grave hag decoction"). They act like [lesser green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_green_mutagen "Lesser green mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster. [Grave hags](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grave_hag "Grave hag") in the wild are quite rare and high level, so the earliest and most reliable way to obtain this mutagen is by completing [Contract: The Merry Widow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Merry_Widow "Contract: The Merry Widow"), during which [Mourntart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mourntart "Mourntart") is guaranteed to drop it. 

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
