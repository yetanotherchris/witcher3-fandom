## Item ID

Mutagen 27

**Griffin decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that raises resistances by 1% each time [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") is hit during combat, up to a maximum of 25%. The effect lasts until the end of battle. 

Drinking a Griffin decoction by itself will not raise any resistance; only after you take your first hit in battle will resistances increase.

## Tips

  * The resistance will stack directly those from any other sources. 
    * This includes [Protective Coating](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Protective_Coating "Protective Coating"), which alone will provide up to 50% resistance against all 5 types of damage without any armor.
  * Some armor sets like high tier [Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") may provide +75% of resistance to a damage type ([elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa "Elementa")), in which case the resistance can reach 100%. While damage will not be reduced to 0 it shall become so minute that it can be pretty much ignored even on Death March difficulty.
  * Successful parries or "blocks" from [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") shield will not raise resistance. Damage taken per hit does not influence increase in resistance and does not influence armor.
