**Succubus trophy** is a [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

During the quest [Contract: Deadly Delights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Deadly_Delights "Contract: Deadly Delights"), it is either given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Salma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Salma "Salma"), or looted off her remains if he chooses to kill her.
