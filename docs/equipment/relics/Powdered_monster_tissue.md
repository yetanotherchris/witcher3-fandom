## Item ID

Monstrous dust

**Powdered monster tissue** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft many items. 

It is a low chance random drop from certain [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary"), [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") outside of [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), and can be dismantled from a few other monster drops.
