It can be found as random loot in the base game, guaranteed loot in [bandit camps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp"), [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") and [treasure hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_quests#Treasure_hunts "Blood and Wine quests") in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(roadside_camp\) "Merchant \(roadside camp\)") in the roadside camp south of [Kaer Trolde](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde "Kaer Trolde")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Kaer_Trolde_Harbor\) "Merchant \(Kaer Trolde Harbor\)") in [Kaer Trolde Harbor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_Harbor "Kaer Trolde Harbor")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Drummond_sawmill\) "Merchant \(Drummond sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(Drummond\) "Sawmill \(Drummond\)") south of [Sund](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sund "Sund")
  * [Wandering merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_merchant_\(Skellige\) "Wandering merchant \(Skellige\)") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Hauteville\) "Armorer \(Hauteville\)") in [Hauteville](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hauteville "Hauteville") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Belgaard_Vineyard\) "Blacksmith \(Belgaard Vineyard\)") in [Belgaard Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Belgaard_Manor "Belgaard Manor"), after he is rescued ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Lazare Lafargue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lazare_Lafargue "Lazare Lafargue") in his [workshop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lazare_Lafargue%27s_workshop "Lazare Lafargue's workshop") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Trentin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trentin "Trentin") in his [workshop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trentin%27s_workshop "Trentin's workshop") in Hauteville ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

Dimeritium ingots may also be obtained by dismantling [dimeritium plates](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dimeritium_plate "Dimeritium plate"), [Great Sun armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Sun_armor "Great Sun armor"), many Relic swords, all [Viper](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_School_Gear "Viper School Gear") venomous swords, superior/mastercrafted level [Feline](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear"), [Griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_School_Gear "Griffin School Gear") and [Ursine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") chest armors, and mastercrafted/grandmaster level [forgotten Wolven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_Wolf_School_Gear "Forgotten Wolf School Gear") steel swords.
