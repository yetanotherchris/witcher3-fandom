**Wraith decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that will automatically cast a free [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") shield whenever a single hit takes more than 30% of [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia")'s health.

## Tips

  * Very useful on higher difficulty settings, as pretty much any attack that hits Geralt can meet the criteria.
  * The applied Quen shield will have special effects from skills such as [Exploding Shield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Exploding_Shield "Exploding Shield").
