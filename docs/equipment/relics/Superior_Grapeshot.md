## Item ID

Grapeshot 3

**Superior Grapeshot** is the highest level of the [Grapeshot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grapeshot "Grapeshot") bomb in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can carry 4 of these at a time. 

Aside from the effects in description, the Superior Grapeshot's effectiveness in chipping shielded opponent's Stamina has improved enough that a single shell is likely sufficient to make any foe drop their shield.
