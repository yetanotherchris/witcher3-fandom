## Tips

  * The decoction only takes effect at exactly 6:00 PM and 6:00 AM. It will do absolutely nothing if it is drank at 6:XX. So it's best to drink it prior. 
    * An easy way to use it is by meditating to 6:00 and then drinking it directly from the inventory without exiting the menu.
  * Once the potion takes effect it will last for 6 in-game hours i.e. 6:00 AM - 12:00 PM or 6:00 PM - 12:00 AM
  * Just as the description implies one of the 5 signs will randomly receive a boost. 
    * Although overall sign intensity will also be increased in statistics it will have no effect on the other signs. This is due to the fact that the overall intensity takes the average of all signs into account, so increasing the power of one sign will increase it too.
