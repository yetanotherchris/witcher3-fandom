## Item ID

Clearing Potion

**Potion of Clearance** is a special [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that allows the player to redistribute their [ability](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_abilities "The Witcher 3 abilities") points when consumed (except those used for [mutations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mutations_\(Blood_and_Wine\) "Mutations \(Blood and Wine\)")). 

Early on, it can only be bought from merchants, though it is relatively expensive with a price tag of 1,000[![Oren3](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/e/e9/Oren3.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20160608063044)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown "Crown"). After reaching level 30, it may also be found as random loot in some chests otherwise reserved for high-quality [alchemy ingredients](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients"), but that is extremely rare. One can be received for free at the start of a [New Game +](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/New_Game_%2B "New Game +").
