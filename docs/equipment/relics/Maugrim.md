## Item ID

Aerondight

**Maugrim** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can only be obtained during a few quests noted below. 

## Associated quests

  * [A Costly Mistake](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Costly_Mistake "A Costly Mistake")
  * [Contract: Deadly Delights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Deadly_Delights "Contract: Deadly Delights") (reward from [Salma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Salma "Salma") for sparing her)
  * [The Tower Outta Nowheres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Tower_Outta_Nowheres "The Tower Outta Nowheres") (chest in the final room)
  * [Wandering in the Dark](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wandering_in_the_Dark "Wandering in the Dark") (behind an illusion near where [Nithral](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nithral "Nithral") is fought, guarded by a [foglet](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foglet "Foglet"))

## Bug

  * Sometimes this sword's name will revert to its debug name "Aerondight" when given as a reward during the contract, but this is not the same [Aerondight](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aerondight "Aerondight") sword that's given by the [Lady of the Lake](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lady_of_the_Lake_\(nymph\) "Lady of the Lake \(nymph\)") in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion.

## Notes

  * The [sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") intensity rolls aren't independent and will always be the same for both [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") and [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden").
  * The sword was originally called Aerondight, which is evidenced by its debug name.
  * Maugrim is a legendary silver sword named after a [werewolf](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werewolf "Werewolf") controlled by [Eira Frostsinger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eira_Frostsinger "Eira Frostsinger"). The sword was forged by the witcher [Gerring of Kharkiv](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gerring_of_Kharkiv "Gerring of Kharkiv"), who slew the monster. Its current whereabouts are unknown.[1]
