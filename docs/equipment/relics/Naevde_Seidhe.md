## Item ID

Naevdeseidhe

**Naevde Seidhe** returns as a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in the third game. 

At least one is guaranteed inside the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt"), where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") goes with [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart") during [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone"). After encountering the [golem](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem"), instead of following Philippa take the opposite direction into a small room to find the sword in a chest there. Another one is usually found in one of the many other treasure chests ahead in the crypt, but it might be random. 

Outside of the Ancient Crypt, it can only be found as random loot. 

### Notes

  * The [Igni](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Igni "Igni") and [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") [sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") intensity rolls will always match.
