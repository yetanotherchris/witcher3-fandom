White Raffards Decoction 3

**Superior White Raffard's Decoction** is the most advanced form of the [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Three of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot.

## Notes

  * Unlike most other potions that have five charges when upgraded, this potion only has three charges at the superior version.
  * The game description of the effect is rather misleading; instead of granting full immunity from damage, the potion actually nulls a threshold of [health](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitality#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "Vitality") loss for a limited time. If [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") takes a large enough amount of damage before the duration ends, the immunity effect will be cancelled.
