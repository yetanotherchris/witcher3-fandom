## Item ID

q105_ritual_dagger

**Crones' dagger** is a main [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

After progressing far enough in the main story line, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") talks to the [Crones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crones "Crones") through [Gran](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gran "Gran"), who task him with helping [Downwarren](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Downwarren "Downwarren")'s [ealdorman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ealdorman_\(Downwarren\) "Ealdorman \(Downwarren\)") before they'll reveal what they know about [Ciri](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ciri "Ciri"). They then order Gran to give Geralt this dagger to show to the ealdorman, so he knows that Geralt was sent by the Crones. 

Later, after Geralt completed the task, the ealdorman will then take this dagger and cut off [his left ear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ealdorman_of_Downwarren%27s_ear "Ealdorman of Downwarren's ear") with it: his payment to the Crones for "helping" the village.
