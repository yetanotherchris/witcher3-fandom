## Item ID

Full Moon 3

**Superior Full Moon** is the most advanced form of the [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Five of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot.

## Tips

  * The healing appears to be around 0.85 HP per each point of toxicity, excluding it's own. Meaning if the potion is drank while toxicity is at 0 it will have no effect.
  * The effect does stack with [Acquired Tolerance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Acquired_Tolerance "Acquired Tolerance"), although even after drinking 3 decoctions it will only regenerate around 180 HP.
