## Item ID

Tlareg

**Tlareg** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which notably is named "Geralt" spelled backwards. 

It can be found in a small sack at an unmarked shrine on a cliff just north of [Larvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Larvik "Larvik")'s harbor. Otherwise it is available as random loot inside caves in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), and from guarded treasures.
