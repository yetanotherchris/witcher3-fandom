## Item ID

Siren vocal cords

**Siren vocal cords** , while placed under the [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") tab, is actually a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Griffin steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Griffin steel sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Legendary Griffin steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Griffin_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Legendary Griffin steel sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Legendary Wolven steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Wolven_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Legendary Wolven steel sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Wolven steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Wolven steel sword - mastercrafted")

They are a random drop from [sirens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Siren "Siren"), their [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and the [ekhidnae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekhidna "Ekhidna") who guard the nests. Somewhat oddly, siren vocal cords can also be found as regular random loot in the [bandit camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") at the [Whale Graveyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Whale_Graveyard "Whale Graveyard") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig"). 

## Notes

  * While categorized as a crafting component, [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants") will treat this item as an alchemy ingredient. It is not only priced as such, but also [alchemists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist "Alchemist") and [herbalists](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Herbalist "Herbalist") will buy it, while [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith") will not. This is beneficial, as herbalists offer best prices for alchemy ingredients. 
    * This is likely the result of a coding error, although it's unknown which of the two categorizations was intended as the correct one.
