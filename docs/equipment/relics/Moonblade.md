# Moonblade

**Moonblade** , or **Moon Blade** , is a legendary silver sword. According to the tales, it was forged by smiths from [Ellander](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ellander "Ellander") for Wolf Witcher [Olach of Ban Gleán](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Olach_of_Ban_Gle%C3%A1n "Olach of Ban Gleán") who was hired to get rid of a [demon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Demon "Demon") tormenting the duchy. It was anointed by one of the few remaining local priestesses of [Lilvani](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lilvani "Lilvani"), Goddess of the Moon. Folk say that Lilvani herself guided the witcher's blade when he confronted the demon.[1]

**Moonblade** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") of [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") design. This deadly silver sword is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by the [Royal huntsman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_huntsman "Royal huntsman") in [Chapter V](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_storyline "The Witcher storyline") as a possible reward (the other possible reward being [G'valchir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/G%27valchir "G'valchir")) for bringing in all ten [trophy monster heads](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_trophy_quests "The Witcher trophy quests"). Merely killing the monsters is not sufficient, their heads must be returned for the quests to count. It is used against [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_bestiary "Category:The Witcher bestiary") and has little effect on [humans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Humans "Humans"). 

Described by the [Royal huntsman](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_huntsman "Royal huntsman") as _"splendid"_ , and it is. _Can neither be bought nor sold._

It is the highest damage silver sword in the game, the equivalent steel sword being [G'valchir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/G%27valchir "G'valchir").
