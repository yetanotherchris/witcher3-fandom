### Effect(s)

## Item ID

Troll mutagen

**Troll mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is used to craft [troll decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll_decoction "Troll decoction"). They act like [lesser green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_green_mutagen "Lesser green mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster. Almost any [troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Troll "Troll") will do, though the drop rate is quite low and only [Wham-a-Wham](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wham-a-Wham "Wham-a-Wham") is guaranteed to drop it. 

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
  * A common strategy is to find a lone rabid rock troll, like the one near [Trail to Yngvar's Fang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trail_to_Yngvar%27s_Fang "Trail to Yngvar's Fang") signpost, then save before killing it and reload until it drops.
  * The [ice troll](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ice_troll "Ice troll") encountered at [Clan Tordarroch Forge](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Clan_Tordarroch_Forge "Clan Tordarroch Forge") erroneously drops other loot, while [Bart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bart "Bart") (if killed during [Blindingly Obvious](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blindingly_Obvious "Blindingly Obvious")) drops no loot at all - they are the only known exceptions who can be killed, but can't drop the mutagen.
