**Noonwraith trophy** is a [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is the earliest possible trophy one can get in the game, during [Contract: Devil by the Well](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Devil_by_the_Well "Contract: Devil by the Well"). Another, slightly more valuable version can be found later during [Contract: The White Lady](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_White_Lady "Contract: The White Lady").
