## Item ID

Monstrous plate

**Monster carapace** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that can be dismantled into [cured draconid leather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cured_draconid_leather "Cured draconid leather") and [chitinous shell](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chitinous_shell "Chitinous shell"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Mastercrafted Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Ursine_armor "Mastercrafted Ursine armor")
  * [Mastercrafted legendary Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_legendary_Ursine_armor "Mastercrafted legendary Ursine armor")

It can be dismantled from a few other components or randomly dropped by certain monsters:

## Item ID

Monstrous carapace

**Monster carapace** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_crafting_components "Blood and Wine crafting components") that, unlike the one found in the base game, is only a loot item, cannot be dismantled and is not needed for anything. 

It is a guaranteed drop from [Pale Widows](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pale_Widow "Pale Widow") and [shaelmaars](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shaelmaar "Shaelmaar"), and random drop from [giant centipedes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Giant_centipede "Giant centipede"), [kikimore warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kikimore_warrior "Kikimore warrior") and [workers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kikimore_worker "Kikimore worker"), as well as [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") of all the listed monsters.
