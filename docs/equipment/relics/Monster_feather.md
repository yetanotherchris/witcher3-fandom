## Item ID

Monstrous feather

**Monster feather** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

**Other items:**

  * [Beann'shie](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beann%27shie_\(sword\) "Beann'shie \(sword\)")
  * [Bolt with bait](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolt_with_bait "Bolt with bait")
  * [Longclaw](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Longclaw "Longclaw")
  * [Negotiator](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Negotiator "Negotiator")
  * [Precision bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Precision_bolt "Precision bolt")
  * [Tor'haerne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tor%27haerne#Tor'haerne_\(crafted_relic\) "Tor'haerne") (crafted relic)
  * [Tracking bolt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tracking_bolt "Tracking bolt")

**Witcher gear:**

  * [Enhanced Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Griffin_armor "Enhanced Griffin armor")
  * [Enhanced legendary Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_legendary_Griffin_armor "Enhanced legendary Griffin armor")
  * [Griffin silver sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Griffin silver sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Legendary Griffin silver sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Griffin_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Legendary Griffin silver sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Griffin steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_steel_sword_-_superior "Griffin steel sword - superior")
  * [Legendary Griffin steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Griffin_steel_sword_-_superior "Legendary Griffin steel sword - superior")
  * [Enhanced Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Wolven_armor "Enhanced Wolven armor")
  * [Enhanced legendary Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_legendary_Wolven_armor "Enhanced legendary Wolven armor")
  * [Wolven silver sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Wolven silver sword - mastercrafted")

  * [Legendary Wolven steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Wolven_steel_sword_-_superior "Legendary Wolven steel sword - superior")

It is a random drop from [cockatrices](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice "Cockatrice"), [erynias](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia "Erynia"), all base game [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and can be dismantled from the following items: 

  * [Erynia eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia_eye "Erynia eye")
