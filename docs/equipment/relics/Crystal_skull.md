**Crystal skull** is a [quest item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_quest_items "The Witcher 3 quest items") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is the crystallized skull of [Yennefer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yennefer_of_Vengerberg "Yennefer of Vengerberg")'s magic kestrel, which accompanied the [sorceress](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sorceress "Sorceress") during her flight across the [battlefield](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battlefield_\(White_Orchard\) "Battlefield \(White Orchard\)") of [White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Orchard "White Orchard"). 

After defeating the [ghouls](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul "Ghoul") with [Vesemir](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vesemir "Vesemir") at the start of the quest [Lilac and Gooseberries](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lilac_and_Gooseberries "Lilac and Gooseberries"), [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can pick up the skull near where the [witchers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witcher "Witcher") made camp for the night. He comments that it was most likely left behind by Yennefer. Picking it up grants 50[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level"). If it is not picked up, the skull disappears from the ground after completion of the quest [The Beast of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beast_of_White_Orchard "The Beast of White Orchard"). 

The skull can later be given to Yennefer after completing the secondary quest [The Last Wish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Last_Wish_\(quest\) "The Last Wish \(quest\)"). To do this, Geralt must speak to Yennefer at her room in [The New Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Port "The New Port") before leaving, as once the room is left she teleports away too (available only if you didn't break with her, otherwise she leaving place instantly). 

The second opportunity is available in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), after [breaking the curse](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Va_Fail,_Elaine "Va Fail, Elaine"). She will sit near dinner table. 

The third opportunity is available at the [Dandelion's tavern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rosemary_and_Thyme "Rosemary and Thyme") after completing [The Great Escape](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Great_Escape "The Great Escape") quest. Yennefer will be taking care of [Margarita](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Margarita_Laux-Antille "Margarita Laux-Antille") (and [Philippa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart"), if Geralt and [Triss](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Triss_Merigold "Triss Merigold") have [taken her](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blindingly_Obvious "Blindingly Obvious") from [Dijkstra](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sigismund_Dijkstra "Sigismund Dijkstra")) in a room on the second floor of the building, second door on the right. 

The next and last opportunity to give the skull to Yennefer is during [Battle Preparations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_Preparations "Battle Preparations"), as once that quest completes and the ship sails to Skellige, she can no longer be talked to outside of scripted events. Giving Yennefer the skull grants 50[![XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/f7/Tw3_icon_xp.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/25?cb=20170901051043)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP earned based on the Story and Swords! difficulty level").
