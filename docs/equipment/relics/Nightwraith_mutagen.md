## Item ID

Nightwraith mutagen

**Nightwraith mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is used to craft [nightwraith decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith_decoction "Nightwraith decoction"). They act like [lesser green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_green_mutagen "Lesser green mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster, but this mutagen is also a guaranteed drop from any [nightwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nightwraith "Nightwraith"). Additionally, any regular [noonwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith "Noonwraith") can be swapped by its counterpart at night, so one can pretty much choose any such target. However, all are higher level than [Jenny o' the Woods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Jenny_o%27_the_Woods "Jenny o' the Woods"), so it's easiest to just do her [quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Jenny_o%27_the_Woods "Contract: Jenny o' the Woods"). 

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
