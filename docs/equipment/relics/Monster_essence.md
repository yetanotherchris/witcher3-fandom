## Item ID

Monstrous essence

**Monster essence** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft many items. 

It is a random drop from certain [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary") and all base game [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and can be dismantled from other monster drops:
