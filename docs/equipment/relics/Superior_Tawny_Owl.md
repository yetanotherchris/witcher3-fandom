## Tips

  * The potion has infinite duration between 6 pm - 7 am. Thus it is a good idea to always drink it between these hours.
  * The infinite duration will take effect even if the potion was drank prior to 6 pm but did not expire before passing it. Meaning if it had 5 sec duration left when the clock hit 6 pm it will remain at 5 sec duration until 7 am.
  * The regeneration is affected by Geralt's armor weight class and will only be at default 10 when wearing medium gear. 
    * When wearing heavy gear the regeneration will be 7.5
    * When wearing light gear the regeneration will be 12.5
  * Using [glyphwords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enchanting#Glyphwords "Enchanting") that change an armors' weight class will change the regeneration accordingly. Thus wearing [Levity](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Levity "Levity")-enchanted armor will grant the full 12.5 regeneration.
