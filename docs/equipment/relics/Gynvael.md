## Item ID

Gynvael

**Gynvael** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can only be found at [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") or as random loot inside caves in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"). 

## Notes

  * Gynvael translates from [Elder Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Speech "Elder Speech") to "ice".
