## Item ID

Monstrous heart

**Monster heart** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is a low chance random drop from most non-[specter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"), [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"), and can also be dismantled from the following items: 

  * [Endrega heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_heart "Endrega heart")
  * [Gargoyle heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gargoyle_heart "Gargoyle heart")
  * [Golem's heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem%27s_heart "Golem's heart")
  * [Nekker heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_heart "Nekker heart")
  * [Shaelmaar hair](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shaelmaar_hair "Shaelmaar hair") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
