## Item ID

Monstrous egg

**Monster egg** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft many [witcher gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_witcher_gear "The Witcher 3 witcher gear") items. 

It can be found in base game [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") or dismantled from a few monster drops: 

  * [Cockatrice egg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice_egg "Cockatrice egg")
  * [Endrega embryo](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_embryo "Endrega embryo")
  * [Griffin's egg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin%27s_egg "Griffin's egg")
  * [Harpy eggs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy_egg "Harpy egg")
  * [Wyvern egg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern_egg "Wyvern egg")

Somewhat oddly, it can also be dismantled from some [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") boots and trousers (those listed below), even though none of the boots are crafted with it.
