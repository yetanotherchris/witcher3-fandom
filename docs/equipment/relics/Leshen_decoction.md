## Tips

  * Unlike most "punishment" effects, the potion will do the exact same damage regardless of how much damage [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") receives when struck by an enemy. So even if he is hit while under the effects of [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen"), the enemy will still receive all the reflected damage despite Geralt not taking any damage himself. 
    * As of [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)") this only appears to apply to the 10 damage effect, while the 10% will only be returned if Geralt takes damage accordingly.
