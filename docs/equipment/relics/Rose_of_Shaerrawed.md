## Item ID

Roseofaelirenn

**Rose of Shaerrawed** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is given to [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") by [Lambert](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lambert "Lambert") during the quest [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen") if the player chose to clear the armory instead of patching holes in the walls.
