# Exploding bolt

**Exploding bolt** is an [ammunition item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bolts "Bolts") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in 2 different forms: 

  * Exploding bolt (magic)
  * Exploding bolt (relic)

Note that these bolts show damage as "fire."

A stack of 10-20 can be found in a chest at the [Scoia'tael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scoia%27tael "Scoia'tael") camp during the [Spooked Mare](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spooked_Mare "Spooked Mare") quest. Stacks of up to 10 have a 50% chance to appear on some soldier corpses (all may also have [tracking bolts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tracking_bolt "Tracking bolt")): 

  * South of [Lindenvale](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lindenvale "Lindenvale")'s cemetery, under the northern side of the bridge
  * In the middle of some rocks on the fields between the two converging roads southeast of [Benek](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Benek "Benek")
  * In [Ignis Fatuus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignis_Fatuus "Ignis Fatuus")' cave, right next to the chest with [Diagram: Ursine silver sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Ursine_silver_sword_-_superior "Diagram: Ursine silver sword - superior")
  * Under a tall flag in the marsh northeast of the [Seven Cats Inn](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Seven_Cats_Inn "Seven Cats Inn")
  * In the woods far north of [Est Tayiar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Est_Tayiar "Est Tayiar"), edge of the base game map, but shortly west of [Vikk Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vikk_Watchtower "Vikk Watchtower") with the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion
  * In the ruins right in the middle between [Holmstein's Port](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Holmstein%27s_Port "Holmstein's Port") and the [Cave of Dreams](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cave_of_Dreams "Cave of Dreams")

### Bugs

  * The crafting screen will show these to have level 19 required and 0.01 ![Tw3 weight brown](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/98/Tw3_weight_brown.svg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/13?cb=20180420180200), but any crafted exploding bolts will have a level requirement of 13 and 0.11 ![Tw3 weight brown](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/98/Tw3_weight_brown.svg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/13?cb=20180420180200). While not erroneous per se, "+12 Fire damage" will also be shown as an effect instead of the inherent base damage on the bolts, which actually ends up as "11-13 Fire damage".

Starting at level 25, the Relic version can be purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yanne "Yanne") at [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel")
  * [Éibhear Hattori](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/%C3%89ibhear_Hattori "Éibhear Hattori") in [Novigrad](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Novigrad "Novigrad")

A stack of 20 can be found in [Arnvald](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arnvald "Arnvald")'s locked chest in the servants' room at [Kaer Trolde citadel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Trolde_citadel "Kaer Trolde citadel"), which however is only lootable if he dies during [King's Gambit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/King%27s_Gambit "King's Gambit"). Small stacks of 3-6 may be found in a few containers in the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion.
