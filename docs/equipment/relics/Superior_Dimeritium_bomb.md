## Attributes

### Effect(s)

Releases a cloud of [dimeritium](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dimeritium "Dimeritium") slivers that block [magic](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Magic "Magic") and [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster")' magic abilities. The blockade survives for a short time after the cloud dissipates.  
30s Effect duration
