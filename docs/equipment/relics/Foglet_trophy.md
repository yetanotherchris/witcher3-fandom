**Foglet trophy** is a [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that adds +5% bonus gold. 

It can be acquired from killing [Ignis Fatuus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ignis_Fatuus "Ignis Fatuus") during [Contract: Swamp Thing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Swamp_Thing "Contract: Swamp Thing").
