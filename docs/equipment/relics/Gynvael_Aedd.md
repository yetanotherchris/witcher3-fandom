# Gynvael Aedd

**Gynvael Aedd** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2_silver_swords "The Witcher 2 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_2:_Assassins_of_Kings "The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings")_ and _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_.

It can be bought from [Falas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Falas "Falas"), a merchant in chapter III at the [Loc Muinne amphitheatre](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne_amphitheatre "Loc Muinne amphitheatre"). The sword can also be found on a corpse in the Sewers during [The Secrets of Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Secrets_of_Loc_Muinne "The Secrets of Loc Muinne"). It is of [elven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elf "Elf") origin, as its name means _ice shard_ in [Elder Speech](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Speech "Elder Speech"), which is similar to [Aedd Gynvael](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aedd_Gynvael "Aedd Gynvael"), the name of a city in [Narok](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Narok "Narok").
