## Item ID

Venom extract

**Venom extract** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

**Witcher gear:**

  * [Viper silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_silver_sword "Viper silver sword")
  * [Viper steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Viper_steel_sword "Viper steel sword")
  * [Legendary Viper silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Viper_silver_sword "Legendary Viper silver sword")
  * [Legendary Viper steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Viper_steel_sword "Legendary Viper steel sword")

**Other items:**

  * [Faramond's blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faramond%27s_blade "Faramond's blade") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Fen'aeth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fen%27aeth "Fen'aeth") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)

It can be received as a reward for completing [On Death's Bed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/On_Death%27s_Bed "On Death's Bed"), dropped by some [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"), dismantled from a few items, or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Alchemist](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemist_\(Crematory\) "Alchemist \(Crematory\)") in the [Crematory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crematory "Crematory") near [Oxenfurt Gate](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt_Gate "Oxenfurt Gate")
  * [Keira Metz](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Keira_Metz "Keira Metz") (cheapest)
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Putrid_Grove\) "Merchant \(Putrid Grove\)") in [Putrid Grove](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Putrid_Grove "Putrid Grove")
