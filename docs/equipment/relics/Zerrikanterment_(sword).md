## Regular Value

### Base

1004 [![Tw2 icon oren](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/7/7d/Tw2_icon_oren.svg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/12?cb=20110601231431)](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oren "Oren")

This sword is named after the [dragon](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zerrikanterment_\(creature\) "Zerrikanterment \(creature\)") and may be crafted from a [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Zerrikanterment "Diagram: Zerrikanterment"), which can be bought in Chapter III in [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne") from [Bras of Ban Ard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bras_of_Ban_Ard "Bras of Ban Ard") in the courtyard. 

The [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Zerrikanterment "Diagram: Zerrikanterment") can rarely be found as general random loot in chests as early as in Flotsam. (Confirmed on pc, found in the Toll Collector's House.)
