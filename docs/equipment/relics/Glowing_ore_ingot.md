## Item ID

Glowing ingot

**Glowing ore ingot** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [dimeritium ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dimeritium_ingot "Dimeritium ingot"). 

**Crafting Requirements** ![Tw2 icon crafting](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/9/9e/Tw2_icon_crafting.svg/revision/latest?cb=20110531194409)  

**Min. Required Level:** Journeyman [armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer")/[blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith")  

[![Tw3 glowing ore](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/ff/Tw3_glowing_ore.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/40?cb=20170216074337)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/ff/Tw3_glowing_ore.png/revision/latest?cb=20170216074337)

× 2

[![Tw3 glowing ore](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/ff/Tw3_glowing_ore.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/40?cb=20170216074337)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/f/ff/Tw3_glowing_ore.png/revision/latest?cb=20170216074337) [Glowing ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glowing_ore "Glowing ore") × 2

In the base game it must be crafted, but with the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion it can also be found as random loot in [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * [Armorer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer_\(Hauteville\) "Armorer \(Hauteville\)") in [Hauteville](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hauteville "Hauteville")
  * [Blacksmith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith_\(Belgaard_Vineyard\) "Blacksmith \(Belgaard Vineyard\)") in [Belgaard Manor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Belgaard_Manor "Belgaard Manor"), after he is rescued
  * [Lazare Lafargue](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lazare_Lafargue "Lazare Lafargue") in his [workshop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lazare_Lafargue%27s_workshop "Lazare Lafargue's workshop")
  * [Trentin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trentin "Trentin") in his [workshop](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Trentin%27s_workshop "Trentin's workshop") in Hauteville

It can also be obtained by dismantling most base game craftable Relic swords, superior/mastercrafted level [witcher gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_witcher_gear "The Witcher 3 witcher gear") swords and chest armors, [enriched dimeritium ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enriched_dimeritium_ore "Enriched dimeritium ore"), and the following lootable items: 

  * [Dwarven blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarven_blade "Dwarven blade")
  * [Faustino](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faustino "Faustino")
  * [Gnomish Gwyhyr](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnomish_Gwyhyr "Gnomish Gwyhyr")
  * [Impera Brigade armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Impera_Brigade_armor "Impera Brigade armor")
  * [Pang of Conscience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pang_of_Conscience "Pang of Conscience")
  * [Steiger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Steiger "Steiger")
  * [The Digger](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Digger "The Digger")
  * [The Striga](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Striga "The Striga")
  * [The Tamer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Tamer "The Tamer")
  * [Tir Tochair blade](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tir_Tochair_blade "Tir Tochair blade")
  * [Zireael armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Zireael_armor "Zireael armor")
