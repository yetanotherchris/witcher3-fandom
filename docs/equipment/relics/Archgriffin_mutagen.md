## Attributes

### Effect(s)

+5% Attack power (then +5% for every red ability connected to it for a max of +20%)

## Item ID

Volcanic Gryphon mutagen

**Archgriffin mutagens** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to craft [archgriffin decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin_decoction "Archgriffin decoction"). They act like [lesser red mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_red_mutagen "Lesser red mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster, of which there are a few: 

  * [Opinicus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Opinicus "Opinicus") during [Contract: The Creature from Oxenfurt Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Creature_from_Oxenfurt_Forest "Contract: The Creature from Oxenfurt Forest") (guaranteed)
  * The [archgriffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin "Archgriffin") on [Snidhall Isle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Snidhall_Isle "Snidhall Isle") during [Unlucky's Treasure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Unlucky%27s_Treasure "Unlucky's Treasure")
  * The archgriffin during the quest [Contract: The Griffin from the Highlands](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Griffin_from_the_Highlands "Contract: The Griffin from the Highlands")
  * The archgriffin in [A Surprise Inheritance](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Surprise_Inheritance "A Surprise Inheritance") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
