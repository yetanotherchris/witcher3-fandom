**Arachas decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It reduces damage received by a maximum of 20%, with the value determined by the current percentage of maximum carry weight. For example, carrying 20/100 weight will provide 80% of the bonus. Carrying 50/100 weight will provide 50% of the bonus.

## Tips

The damage reduction based on armor weight appears to be inverted, as the damage is reduced more the heavier it is instead of lighter: 

  * 20% while wearing [light armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_light_armor "The Witcher 3 light armor")
  * 33% while wearing [medium armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_medium_armor "The Witcher 3 medium armor")
  * 50% while wearing [heavy armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_heavy_armor "The Witcher 3 heavy armor")

The effect also ignores [glyphwords](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyphwords "Glyphwords") which change the weightclass of the worn gear. Meaning wearing [Bear School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear_School_Gear "Bear School Gear") enchanted with [Levity](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Levity "Levity") which changes it into light armor will still give 50% damage reduction, while wearing [Cat School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cat_School_Gear "Cat School Gear") enchanted with [Heft](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heft "Heft") will still only give 20% reduction.
