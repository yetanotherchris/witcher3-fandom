# Fate

**Fate** is a legendary [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") of unknown whereabouts. It was forged by dwarven swordsman Vladov Varga.[1]

## Item ID

Fate

**Fate** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") that be received from [Crach an Craite](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crach_an_Craite "Crach an Craite") after helping [Cerys](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cerys_an_Craite "Cerys an Craite") and [Hjalmar](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hjalmar_an_Craite "Hjalmar an Craite") on [Spikeroog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Spikeroog "Spikeroog") and [Undvik](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Undvik "Undvik"). It also often appears as random loot.
