## Item ID

mh206_fiend_trophy

**Fiend Trophy** is acquired from the corpse of the fiend [Morvudd](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morvudd "Morvudd"). This [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") can be attached to your [horse saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse_equipment "Horse equipment") to give you a bonus.
