## Item ID

Mutagen 20

**Earth elemental decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that increases resistance to poison, bleeding and burning effects applied by enemies. 

Each reasistance increased by 10% and according to comments in the game files is supposed to increase by that much every second. However, it reportedly only applies a one time 10% increase, despite what the description says.
