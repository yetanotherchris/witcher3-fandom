## Item ID

Cat 3

**Superior Cat** is the most advanced form of the [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Five of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot.

## Notes

  * Since the "next-gen" [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), the potion also increases critical hit chance.
  * Prior to the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion release (unofficially patch 1.21), it had a four times longer duration of 720 seconds.
  * Superior Cat was originally also meant to not increase Toxicity and this effect description still exists in the game files.
