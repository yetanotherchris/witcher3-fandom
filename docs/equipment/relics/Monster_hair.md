## Item ID

Monstrous hair

**Monster hair** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Superior Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Feline_armor "Superior Feline armor")
  * [Superior legendary Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_legendary_Feline_armor "Superior legendary Feline armor")
  * [Feline crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_crossbow "Feline crossbow")
  * [Feline silver sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_silver_sword_-_enhanced "Feline silver sword - enhanced")
  * [Legendary Feline silver sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_silver_sword_-_enhanced "Legendary Feline silver sword - enhanced")
  * [Manticore gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manticore_gauntlets "Manticore gauntlets") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Legendary Manticore silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Manticore_silver_sword "Legendary Manticore silver sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Enhanced Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Ursine_armor "Enhanced Ursine armor")
  * [Enhanced legendary Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_legendary_Ursine_armor "Enhanced legendary Ursine armor")
  * [Grandmaster Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Ursine_trousers "Grandmaster Ursine trousers") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Grandmaster legendary Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_Ursine_trousers "Grandmaster legendary Ursine trousers") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Ursine crossbow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_crossbow "Ursine crossbow")
  * [Ursine silver sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_silver_sword_-_superior "Ursine silver sword - superior")
  * [Legendary Ursine silver sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_silver_sword_-_superior "Legendary Ursine silver sword - superior")
  * [Ursine steel sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_steel_sword_-_enhanced "Ursine steel sword - enhanced")
  * [Legendary Ursine steel sword - enhanced](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_steel_sword_-_enhanced "Legendary Ursine steel sword - enhanced")

It is a low chance random drop from many monsters ([specters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") and [elementa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa "Elementa") are notable exceptions), all base game [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"), and can be dismantled from the following items:
