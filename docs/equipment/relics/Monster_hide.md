## Item ID

Monstrous hide

**Monster hide** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [hardened leather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hardened_leather "Hardened leather") and [monster blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_blood "Monster blood"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Mastercrafted Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Feline_armor "Mastercrafted Feline armor")
  * [Mastercrafted legendary Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_legendary_Feline_armor "Mastercrafted legendary Feline armor")
  * [Superior Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Griffin_armor "Superior Griffin armor")
  * [Superior legendary Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_legendary_Griffin_armor "Superior legendary Griffin armor")
  * [Superior Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Ursine_armor "Superior Ursine armor")
  * [Superior legendary Ursine armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_legendary_Ursine_armor "Superior legendary Ursine armor")
  * [Superior Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Wolven_armor "Superior Wolven armor")
  * [Superior legendary Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_legendary_Wolven_armor "Superior legendary Wolven armor")
  * [Forgotten Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_Wolven_armor "Forgotten Wolven armor")
  * [Legendary forgotten Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_forgotten_Wolven_armor "Legendary forgotten Wolven armor")
  * [Grandmaster forgotten Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_forgotten_Wolven_armor "Grandmaster forgotten Wolven armor")
  * [Grandmaster legendary forgotten Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_forgotten_Wolven_armor "Grandmaster legendary forgotten Wolven armor")

It is a low chance random drop from some [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"), all base game [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and can be dismantled from a few other hides dropped by monsters.
