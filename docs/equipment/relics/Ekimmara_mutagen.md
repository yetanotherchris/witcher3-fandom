## Item ID

Ekimma mutagen

**Ekimmara mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is used to craft [ekimmara decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekimmara_decoction "Ekimmara decoction"). They act like [lesser green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_green_mutagen "Lesser green mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding creature, though there are only three [ekimmara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ekimma "Ekimma") in the game, all encountered during quests: 

  * In the mill during the secondary quest [Following the Thread](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Following_the_Thread_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Following the Thread \(The Witcher 3\)")
  * In [Dragonslayer's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dragonslayer%27s_Grotto "Dragonslayer's Grotto"), where you'd go for [Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear")
  * [Sarasti](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarasti "Sarasti") during [Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Mystery_of_the_Byways_Murders "Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders") (always drops it, but also the highest level)

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
