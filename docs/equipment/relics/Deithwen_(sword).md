# Deithwen (sword)

The sword **Deithwen** can be looted from one of the skeletons near a ruined wagon, a short distance past the entrance to the caves east of [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne"). It is guarded by two [arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas "Arachas"). 

In [Roche](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roche "Roche")'s path of Chapter III, a second copy of the sword appears in a cave by the [forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne_forest "Loc Muinne forest") path where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can escort [Brigida](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Brigida_Papebrock "Brigida Papebrock") through during the quest [Crown Witness](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Crown_Witness "Crown Witness").

## Item ID

Deithwen

**Deithwen** returns as a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in the third game. 

It can be found as random loot, during a few quests noted below, or in a chest tucked behind the first [aard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Aard "Aard")-able obstacle during [The Final Trial](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Final_Trial "The Final Trial") (but not really part of the quest). 

### Associated quests

  * [Sunken Chest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sunken_Chest "Sunken Chest")
  * [In Wolf's Clothing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_Wolf%27s_Clothing "In Wolf's Clothing") (reward for lifting the curse off [Morkvarg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Morkvarg "Morkvarg"))
  * [Contract: The White Lady](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_White_Lady "Contract: The White Lady") (on the remains of [The White Lady](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_White_Lady "The White Lady"))
  * [Master Armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Armorers "Master Armorers") (in the chest with [high-quality smithing tools](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/High-quality_smithing_tools "High-quality smithing tools"))
