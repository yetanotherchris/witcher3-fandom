## Item ID

Monstrous liver

**Monster liver** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Grandmaster Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Feline_armor "Grandmaster Feline armor") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Grandmaster legendary Feline armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_Feline_armor "Grandmaster legendary Feline armor") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursine_trousers "Ursine trousers")
  * [Enhanced Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Ursine_trousers "Enhanced Ursine trousers")
  * [Legendary Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_trousers "Legendary Ursine trousers")
  * [Enhanced Legendary Ursine trousers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Legendary_Ursine_trousers "Enhanced Legendary Ursine trousers")

It is a low chance random drop from most [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") ([specters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost"), [elementa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa "Elementa") and [insectoids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid "Insectoid") are notable exceptions), [rotfiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend "Rotfiend") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"), and can be dismantled from the following items: 

  * [Cave troll liver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cave_troll_liver "Cave troll liver")
  * [Nekker warrior's liver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior%27s_liver "Nekker warrior's liver")
