A **griffin trophy** is a [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. This item may be obtained by looting it from a [griffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_\(creature\) "Griffin \(creature\)")'s corpse. 

One opportunity to obtain this trophy presents itself in the main quest [The Beast of White Orchard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Beast_of_White_Orchard "The Beast of White Orchard") once the griffin is defeated. In this case, looting the trophy is required to progress the quest. 

Another one can be looted from the remains of [Opinicus](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Opinicus "Opinicus") during [Contract: The Creature from Oxenfurt Forest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Creature_from_Oxenfurt_Forest "Contract: The Creature from Oxenfurt Forest"), despite it actually being an [Archgriffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin "Archgriffin"). 

In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it may be looted from the griffin encountered during the quest [A Portrait of the Witcher as an Old Man](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Portrait_of_the_Witcher_as_an_Old_Man "A Portrait of the Witcher as an Old Man"). 

## Notes

  * The effect given by this trophy is also given by the [archgriffin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archgriffin_trophy "Archgriffin trophy"), [cockatrice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice_trophy "Cockatrice trophy"), [earth elemental](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_elemental_trophy "Earth elemental trophy"), and [slyzard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Slyzard_trophy "Slyzard trophy") trophies.
  * When strapped to a saddle, the trophy features dynamic hair that is affected by Nvidia HairWorks.
