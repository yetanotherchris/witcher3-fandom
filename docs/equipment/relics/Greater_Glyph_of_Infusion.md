## Item ID

Glyph infusion greater

**Greater Glyph of Infusion** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which presumably was originally intended to be used for crafting greater [glyphs](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glyph "Glyph"). It still exists in the game files, and technically can even be crafted, but doesn't appear during normal gameplay and neither does its [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Greater_Glyph_of_Infusion "Diagram: Greater Glyph of Infusion"). Thus, it cannot be obtained without the use of console commands.
