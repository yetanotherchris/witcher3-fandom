## Attributes

### Effect(s)

+5% [Sign](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sign "Sign") intensity (then +5% for every blue ability connected to it for a max of +20%)

## Item ID

Dao mutagen

**Earth elemental mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is used to craft [earth elemental decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_elemental_decoction "Earth elemental decoction"). Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their [corresponding creature](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_Elemental "Earth Elemental"). They act like [lesser blue mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_blue_mutagen "Lesser blue mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
  * There are 2 [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") each in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige") guarded by earth elementals. However, they're all higher level monsters than what one would encounter during quests.
