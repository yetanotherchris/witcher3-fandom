**Wyvern trophy** is a [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that when equipped adds +5% bonus experience from [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary"). 

It can be looted from the remains of specific [wyverns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern") during the quests listed below. 

## Associated quest
