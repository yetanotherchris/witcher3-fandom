## Item ID

Monstrous tooth

**Monster tooth** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Feline gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_gauntlets "Feline gauntlets")
  * [Enhanced Feline gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_gauntlets "Enhanced Feline gauntlets")
  * [Legendary Feline gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_gauntlets "Legendary Feline gauntlets")
  * [Enhanced legendary Feline gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_legendary_Feline_gauntlets "Enhanced legendary Feline gauntlets")
  * [Grandmaster Feline silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Feline_silver_sword "Grandmaster Feline silver sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Feline steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_steel_sword_-_superior "Feline steel sword - superior")
  * [Legendary Feline steel sword - superior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_steel_sword_-_superior "Legendary Feline steel sword - superior")
  * [Grandmaster Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_Ursine_boots "Grandmaster Ursine boots") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Grandmaster legendary Ursine boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_Ursine_boots "Grandmaster legendary Ursine boots") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Superior Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Superior_Ursine_gauntlets "Superior Ursine gauntlets")
  * [Mastercrafted Ursine gauntlets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mastercrafted_Ursine_gauntlets "Mastercrafted Ursine gauntlets")

  * [Legendary Ursine steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Ursine_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Legendary Ursine steel sword - mastercrafted")

It is a low chance random drop from most [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"), [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"), and can also be dismantled from the following items: 

  * [Foglet teeth](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Foglet_teeth "Foglet teeth")
  * [Monstrous fang](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monstrous_fang "Monstrous fang")
