## Item ID

Monstrous ear

A **monster ear** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Manticore armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manticore_armor "Manticore armor"). 

It is a low chance random drop from many [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") ([specters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost"), [elementa](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Elementa "Elementa") and [insectoids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid "Insectoid") are notable exceptions), [drowner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"). In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it can also be dismantled from [wight ear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wight_ear "Wight ear").
