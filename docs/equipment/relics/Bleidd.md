## Item ID

WitcherSilverWolf

**Bleidd** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") that was [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It exists in the game files and can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't appear during normal gameplay. 

The game files also contain a different unique [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") icon for it than the one it appears with if obtained, which is for a steel counterpart to it. 

Likely an early version of a [Wolf School Gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolf_School_Gear "Wolf School Gear") sword, it was at some point supposed to be received (along with [Devine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devine "Devine")) instead of the [Rose of Shaerrawed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rose_of_Shaerrawed "Rose of Shaerrawed") during [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen") if one chooses to clear the armory.
