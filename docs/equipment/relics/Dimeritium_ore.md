## Item ID

Dwimeryte ore

**Dimeritium ore** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [glowing ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glowing_ore "Glowing ore"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Dimeritium ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dimeritium_ingot "Dimeritium ingot")

Dimeritium ore can also be obtained by dismantling many swords, particularly those of [dwarven](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") and [gnomish](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Gnome "Gnome") origins, the rarer ones with more unique-sounding names, as well as most superior+ level [witcher gear](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_witcher_gear "The Witcher 3 witcher gear") swords and gauntlets. 

## Tips

Dimeritium ore is required to craft enriched dimeritium ore which is required to craft [grandmaster witcher gear sets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Master_Master_Master! "Master Master Master Master!"). 

  * Some higher tier swords contain dimeritium ingots which can be dismantled into ore. However, since dimeritium ingots are required for lower tier witcher sets, they should only be dismantled as a last resort.
