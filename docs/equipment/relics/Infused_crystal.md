## Item ID

Infused crystal

**Infused crystal** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [infused shard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Infused_shard "Infused shard"). It is needed to craft:

In the _[Blood and Wine](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine "Blood and Wine")_ expansion, it is also often found during [treasure hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_and_Wine_quests#Treasure_hunt "Blood and Wine quests"), and in many [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure") and [bandit camps](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bandit_camp "Bandit camp") around [Toussaint](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Toussaint "Toussaint"). 

## Tips

Infused crystals are required to craft [infused slyzard hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Infused_slyzard_hide "Infused slyzard hide"), which are required for [grandmaster witcher gear sets](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Master_Master_Master_Master! "Master Master Master Master!"). 

  * Two [infused dust](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Infused_dust "Infused dust") can be used to craft an [infused shard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Infused_shard "Infused shard"), and two infused shards can be used to craft an infused crystal.
