## Attributes

### Effect(s)

Each foe slain accelerates [Vitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitality "Vitality") regeneration for the duration of the battle.  
**Since[Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"):**  
10 Vitality regeneration per enemy killed during combat  
**Before[Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"):**  
5 Vitality regeneration during combat

## Item ID

Mutagen 18

**Grave hag decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that increases [Vitality](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Vitality "Vitality") regeneration for each enemy killed during combat. The effect has no upper limit, but only lasts until the end of battle.
