## Item ID

Monstrous brain

**Monster brain** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be dismantled from [drowner brain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner_brain "Drowner brain"), and is a low chance random drop from most non-[specter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"), all base game [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), and white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear").
