## Item ID

Killer Whale 1

**Killer Whale** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Three of these potions can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. Unlike most other potions in the game, Killer Whale cannot be upgraded to an enhanced or superior version.

## Notes

  * Enhanced and superior versions of Killer Whale were originally intended to exist, but were eventually [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") during development. The potions themselves don't exist in the game files, but their effect definitions do - 75% breath for the enhanced version, and a whopping 200% for the superior one. 
    * All three versions also have a "swimming stamina" property, which also got cut from the game. It is likely the removal of this and other water-based mechanics that has made enhanced and superior versions superfluous, rather than the apparent power balancing issues, which are trivial to correct.
  * The icon for this potion was originally created for and then repurposed from [Tiara](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Tiara "Tiara"), which got [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from the game. It is still named as such in the game files.
