**Blade of Ys** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") that was [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It can be obtained through console commands, but doesn't appear during normal gameplay.
