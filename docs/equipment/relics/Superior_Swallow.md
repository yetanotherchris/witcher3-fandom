## Item ID

Swallow 3

**Superior Swallow** is the most advanced form of the [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. Five of these can be crafted at any one time and placed in the consumables slot. 

One batch can be received from a choice during [The Battle of Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Battle_of_Kaer_Morhen "The Battle of Kaer Morhen"). If you have a lesser version of the potion at that point, it effectively allows for more charge uses between replenishments.
