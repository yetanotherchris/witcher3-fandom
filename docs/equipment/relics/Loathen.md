## Item ID

Loathen

**Loathen** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can only be found as random loot. 

It is more likely to spawn in cave locations in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), outside of which it only appears at guarded treasures. 

One place where this sword spawns frequently, though not guaranteed, is the guarded treasure ([Noonwraith](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith "Noonwraith")) in the mine cave just south of [Frischlow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Frischlow "Frischlow"), where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can go for the [gauntlets diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Enhanced_Griffin_gauntlets "Diagram: Enhanced Griffin gauntlets") during [Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear/Part 2](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Scavenger_Hunt:_Griffin_School_Gear/Part_2 "Scavenger Hunt: Griffin School Gear/Part 2"). 

Another location where it is known to appear is [Yustianna's Grotto](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna%27s_Grotto "Yustianna's Grotto"), in a chest on the platform exactly where the bandits during [Yustianna Disturbed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yustianna_Disturbed "Yustianna Disturbed") are.
