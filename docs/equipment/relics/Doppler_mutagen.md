## Item ID

Doppler mutagen

**Doppler mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to craft [doppler decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler_decoction "Doppler decoction"). They act like [lesser red mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_red_mutagen "Lesser red mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

There is only one opportunity to obtain this mutagen: by choosing to kill [Imp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Janne "Janne") during the quest [Contract: An Elusive Thief](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_An_Elusive_Thief "Contract: An Elusive Thief"). 

## Notes

  * The player only has one chance to receive this mutagen: during the An Elusive Thief contract. However, if the player leans towards being merciful, they can miss this chance and must either forego making the decoction, use one playthrough to kill the [doppler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Doppler "Doppler"), or use console commands to get the mutagen.
  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
