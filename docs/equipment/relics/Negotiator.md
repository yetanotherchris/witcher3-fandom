One way to obtain **Negotiator** is from [Serrit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Serrit "Serrit")'s corpse along with [his notes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Serrit%27s_notes "Serrit's notes") during the quest "[The Assassins of Kings](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Assassins_of_Kings "The Assassins of Kings")". 

In Iorveth's Path, it is given as a reward for gathering the necessary items to disperse the mist of wraiths or for gathering the necessary items to complete [A Matter of Life and Death](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Matter_of_Life_and_Death_\(The_Witcher_2\) "A Matter of Life and Death \(The Witcher 2\)"). 

## _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_

**Negotiator** returns as a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in 2 different forms: 

  * Negotiator (crafted)
  * Negotiator (relic)
