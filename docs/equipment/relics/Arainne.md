## Item ID

Arainne

**Arainne** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can only be found as random loot inside caves in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), and from [guarded treasures](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Guarded_treasure "Guarded treasure").
