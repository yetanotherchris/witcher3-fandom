## Item ID

Anathema

**Anathema** returns as a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in the third game. 

It can be looted from the [Royal wyvern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_wyvern "Royal wyvern") during [Contract: Phantom of the Trade Route](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Phantom_of_the_Trade_Route "Contract: Phantom of the Trade Route") or found as random loot, but that is extremely rare.
