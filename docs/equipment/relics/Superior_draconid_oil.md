## Item ID

Draconide Oil 3

**Superior draconid oil** is an [oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_oils "The Witcher 3 oils") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that adds +50% attack power against [draconids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Draconid "Draconid"). It is needed to craft [enhanced draconid oil](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_draconid_oil "Enhanced draconid oil").
