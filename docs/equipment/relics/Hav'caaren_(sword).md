## Item ID

Havcaaren

**Hav'caaren** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. It is awarded after defeating [Eredin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Eredin_Br%C3%A9acc_Glas "Eredin Bréacc Glas") at the end of the main quest [On Thin Ice](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/On_Thin_Ice "On Thin Ice").
