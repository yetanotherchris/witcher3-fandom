## Item ID

**Nekker warrior decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that grants immunity to panic for any mount used by [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") (i.e. not limited to [Roach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roach "Roach")) and adds 50% attack power in mounted combat.
