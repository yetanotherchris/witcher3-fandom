**Addan Deith** can be looted from a chest in the northwestern rune room during the quest [The Gargoyle Contract](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Gargoyle_Contract "The Gargoyle Contract") in [Loc Muinne](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Loc_Muinne "Loc Muinne") during [Chapter III](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chapters "Chapters"). 

After obtaining the weapon, [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") starts reading a message from its previous owner: 

    _You hold the sword of Aramil, who defied his king's rule. The Lord of the Wild Hunt desires the gene of the Elder Blood. He seeks to fling open the gates between the worlds, so that terror and destruction may reign._
    _The King of the Hunt ordered me slain, and the wraiths born of his command assumed living form, pursuing me through the voids between the worlds for centuries._
    _I have arrived at Loc Muinne. Though tired, I know I will not rest. The riders of the Hunt draw near. On my back I can feel their deathly breath._

## Item ID

Addandeith

Can be found on the remains of [Mourntart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mourntart "Mourntart") during [Contract: The Merry Widow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Merry_Widow "Contract: The Merry Widow"), or purchased from the [Scoia'tael merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Scoia%27tael_Crossroads\) "Merchant \(Scoia'tael Crossroads\)") in [Oxenfurt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Oxenfurt "Oxenfurt") forest, after he is rescued. 

It also appears fairly often as random loot, and one is almost always found in one of the many treasure chests inside the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt"), where [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") goes with [Philippa Eilhart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Philippa_Eilhart "Philippa Eilhart") during [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone").
