**Diagram: Impera Brigade armor** is a [crafting diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Category:The_Witcher_3_crafting_diagrams "Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft [Impera Brigade armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Impera_Brigade_armor "Impera Brigade armor"). 

It can be found in the reward chest for the [treasure hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_treasure_hunts "The Witcher 3 treasure hunts") quest [A Plea Ignored](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Plea_Ignored "A Plea Ignored"), at [random diagram locations](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_diagram_locations "The Witcher 3 diagram locations"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") in [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
