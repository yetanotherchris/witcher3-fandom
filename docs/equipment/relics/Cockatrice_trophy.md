## Item ID

mh101_cockatrice_trophy

**Cockatrice trophy** is a [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is a reward from killing the [Shrieker](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Shrieker "Shrieker") and gives 10% chance to dismember enemies. Like any other trophy, it can be attached to [Roach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roach "Roach")'s saddle.
