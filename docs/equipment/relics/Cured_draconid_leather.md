## Item ID

Draconide leather

**Cured draconid leather** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [hardened leather](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hardened_leather "Hardened leather") and [chitinous shell](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Chitinous_shell "Chitinous shell"). It is needed to craft many high level items, almost all of which are armors.

## Tips

Cured draconid leather can be used to craft Infused slyzard hide, which you need for Grandmaster armor sets. It also required for lower tier sets, thus it's best to gather as many as you need.
