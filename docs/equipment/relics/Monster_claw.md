## Item ID

Monstrous claw

**Monster claw** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It is a random drop from many [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") ([specters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") and [insectoids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid "Insectoid") are notable exceptions), all base game [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"), and can be dismantled from a few other monster drops: 

  * [Alghoul claw](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul_claw "Alghoul claw")
  * [Harpy talons](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Harpy_talons "Harpy talons")
  * [Nekker claw](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_claw "Nekker claw")
  * [Wyvern egg](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern_egg "Wyvern egg")
