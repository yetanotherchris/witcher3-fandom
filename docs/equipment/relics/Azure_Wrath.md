## Item ID

Azurewrath

**Azure Wrath** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Silver_sword "Silver sword") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which can be looted from the [Howler](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Howler "Howler") during [Contract: Mysterious Tracks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Mysterious_Tracks "Contract: Mysterious Tracks"). It is also available, though very rare, as random loot. 

## Notes

## Trivia

  * This sword's name is quite popular in the video game industry, appearing as a sword (and sometimes even a rifle) in many other games. The likely original such occurence (and one with significant lore) is in [Diablo II](https://diablo.fandom.com/wiki/Diablo_II "diablowiki:Diablo II"), where two different swords have carried the name _Azurewrath_.
