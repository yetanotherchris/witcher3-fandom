**Trophies** are a special type of loot [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can collect from certain [contracts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_contracts "The Witcher 3 contracts") and equip to [Roach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Roach "Roach")'s [saddle](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Horse_equipment "Horse equipment"). Each trophy has a certain effect but only one can be equipped at a time. They can also be sold off if the player has no use for it. 

Trophies that are part of an expansion have been marked.
