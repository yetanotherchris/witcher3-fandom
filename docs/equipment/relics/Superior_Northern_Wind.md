**Superior Northern Wind** is a [bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that freezes foes with a chance of instant killing. Up to four of these can be carried in the [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") at one time.

## Bugs

  * The crafting screen will incorrectly display 4s effect duration instead of the 10 properly shown on the actual bomb. This is caused by how the item is defined in the game files, with 4s listed directly on the item and 6s added by its effect definition, the latter of which is not read by the crafting screen.
