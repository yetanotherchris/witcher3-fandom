## Item ID

Runestone greater

**Greater runestone** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , which presumably was originally intended to be used for crafting greater [runestones](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Runestone "Runestone"). It still exists in the game files, and technically can even be crafted, but doesn't appear during normal gameplay and neither does its [diagram](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Diagram:_Greater_runestone "Diagram: Greater runestone"). Thus, it cannot be obtained without the use of console commands.
