## Item ID

Pheromone Potion Bear 1

**Bear pheromones** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that stops [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") from being hostile. The effect only works on regular brown bears, and not their white variation or [berserkers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Werebear "Werebear"). 

The potion and its [formula](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manuscript_page:_Bear_pheromones "Manuscript page: Bear pheromones") exist in the game files and are fully functional, but don't appear during gameplay and cannot be obtained without the use of console commands or mods.
