## Item ID

Mutagen 7

**Ekimmara decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that grants 10% life leech. 

The damage does not need to be directly dealt by [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia"). Inflicting enemies with damaging conditions like bleeding and burning will also give health regeneration. Causing these conditions to multiple enemies at once can result in astonishing rates of health regeneration.
