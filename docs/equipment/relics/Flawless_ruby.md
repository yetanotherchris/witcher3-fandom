## Item ID

Ruby flawless

**Flawless ruby** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that can be dismantled into [ruby](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruby "Ruby"). It is needed to craft the following items: 

  * [Feline silver sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Feline silver sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Legendary Feline silver sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_silver_sword_-_mastercrafted "Legendary Feline silver sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Grandmaster legendary Feline silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_Feline_silver_sword "Grandmaster legendary Feline silver sword") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Feline steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Feline steel sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Legendary Feline steel sword - mastercrafted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_steel_sword_-_mastercrafted "Legendary Feline steel sword - mastercrafted")
  * [Forgotten Wolven silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Forgotten_Wolven_silver_sword "Forgotten Wolven silver sword")
  * [Legendary forgotten Wolven silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_forgotten_Wolven_silver_sword "Legendary forgotten Wolven silver sword")
  * [Grandmaster forgotten Wolven steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_forgotten_Wolven_steel_sword "Grandmaster forgotten Wolven steel sword")
  * [Grandmaster legendary forgotten Wolven steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Grandmaster_legendary_forgotten_Wolven_steel_sword "Grandmaster legendary forgotten Wolven steel sword")

It can be found as random loot, dropped by the [witch hunter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunter "Witch hunter") who holds [Dandelion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dandelion "Dandelion") during [A Poet Under Pressure](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Poet_Under_Pressure "A Poet Under Pressure"), or purchased from the following [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_merchants "The Witcher 3 merchants"): 

  * All [armorers](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Armorer "Armorer") and [blacksmiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Blacksmith "Blacksmith"), except the one at [Ardaiso Quarry](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ardaiso_Quarry "Ardaiso Quarry")
  * Armors and weapons [merchants](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp\) "Merchant \(Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp\)") in [Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nilfgaardian_Army_Group_%27Center%27_Camp "Nilfgaardian Army Group 'Center' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(boatyard\) "Merchant \(boatyard\)") in the [boatyard](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Abandoned_site_\(boatyard\) "Abandoned site \(boatyard\)") south of [Refugees' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Refugees%27_Camp "Refugees' Camp")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Calveit_Watchtower\) "Merchant \(Calveit Watchtower\)") at [Calveit Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Calveit_Watchtower "Calveit Watchtower")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Coast_of_Wrecks\) "Merchant \(Coast of Wrecks\)") at [Coast of Wrecks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Coast_of_Wrecks "Coast of Wrecks")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Ursten\) "Merchant \(Ursten\)") in [Ursten](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ursten "Ursten")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(battlefield\) "Merchant \(battlefield\)") by the road west of [White Eagle Fort](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/White_Eagle_Fort "White Eagle Fort")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(an_Craite_sawmill\) "Merchant \(an Craite sawmill\)") at the [sawmill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sawmill_\(an_Craite\) "Sawmill \(an Craite\)") northeast of [Rannvaig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rannvaig "Rannvaig")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Isolated_Hut\) "Merchant \(Isolated Hut\)") at the [Isolated Hut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Isolated_Hut_\(Hindarsfjall\) "Isolated Hut \(Hindarsfjall\)") on [Hindarsfjall](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hindarsfjall "Hindarsfjall")
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Mohrin_Village\) "Merchant \(Mohrin Village\)") in [Mohrin Village](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Mohrin_Village "Mohrin Village") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant_\(Stacheier\) "Merchant \(Stacheier\)") in [Stacheier](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Stacheier "Stacheier") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
  * [Ofieri merchant](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Dulla_kh%27Amanni "Dulla kh'Amanni") at [Upper Mill](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Upper_Mill "Upper Mill") ![82?cb=20160710035615](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/d/d8/HOS_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/82?cb=20160710035615)
