## Item ID

Mutagen 8

**Chort decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that grants [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") immunity to the regular stagger effect and downgrades knockdowns to stagger. 

It can be useful against big monsters that have a habit of staggering Geralt, like [endrega warriors](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega_warrior "Endrega warrior") and [golems](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Golem "Golem").
