## Item ID

Mutagen 10

**Wyvern decoction** is a [potion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_potions "The Witcher 3 potions") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that gradually increases attack power, 1% at a time, until the end of battle or non-toxicity damage is taken. There is no upper limit, but the rate of increases is yet unknown.

## Tips

  * It works well with the [Quen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Quen "Quen") sign, as attacks absorbed by the shield will not cause the damage boost to reset.
  * Since the boost requires avoiding damage it can also synergizes well with [water hag decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Water_hag_decoction "Water hag decoction") and [Invigoration](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Invigoration "Invigoration") as long as [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") remains at full health.
