## Item ID

Monstrous eye

**Monster eye** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft the following items: 

**Witcher gear:**

  * [Feline boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_boots "Feline boots")
  * [Enhanced Feline boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_Feline_boots "Enhanced Feline boots")
  * [Feline silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Feline_silver_sword "Feline silver sword")
  * [Griffin armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_armor "Griffin armor")
  * [Griffin steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Griffin_steel_sword "Griffin steel sword")
  * [Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_armor "Wolven armor")
  * [Wolven steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wolven_steel_sword "Wolven steel sword")
  * [Manticore armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Manticore_armor "Manticore armor") ![79?cb=20160527184953](https://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/witcher/images/3/3b/BAW_English_logo.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/79?cb=20160527184953)
  * [Legendary Feline boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_boots "Legendary Feline boots")
  * [Enhanced legendary Feline boots](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Enhanced_legendary_Feline_boots "Enhanced legendary Feline boots")
  * [Legendary Feline silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Feline_silver_sword "Legendary Feline silver sword")

  * [Legendary Wolven armor](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Wolven_armor "Legendary Wolven armor")
  * [Legendary Wolven steel sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Wolven_steel_sword "Legendary Wolven steel sword")

It is a low chance random drop from many [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster") ([spectres](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") and [insectoids](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Insectoid "Insectoid") are notable exceptions), [drowner](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner "Drowner") [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest"), white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"), and can be dismantled from the following items: 

  * [Arachas eyes](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas_eyes "Arachas eyes")
  * [Cyclops' eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops%27_eye "Cyclops' eye")
  * [Erynia eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Erynia_eye "Erynia eye")
  * [Fiend's eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend%27s_eye "Fiend's eye")
