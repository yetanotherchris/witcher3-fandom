## Item ID

Nekker Warrior mutagen

**Nekker warrior mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to craft [nekker warrior decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior_decoction "Nekker warrior decoction"). They act like [lesser red mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_red_mutagen "Lesser red mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster, but in this case there's a small catch - [monster nest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") bosses don't count. Any other [nekker warrior](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior "Nekker warrior") will do, although the drop rate is a bit low. 

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
  * A common strategy is to kill off the regular nekkers then run away and save before fighting the nekker warriors alone. Then keep reloading the game until the mutagen drops.
