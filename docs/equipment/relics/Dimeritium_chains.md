## Item ID

Dwimeritium chains

**Dimeritium chains** is a [cut content](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") [junk item](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_junk "The Witcher 3 junk") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ , still present in the game files, but only obtainable through console commands. 

Like the similarly named "dimeritium shackles", it was likely meant to appear during content related to [witch hunts](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Witch_hunts "Witch hunts"). It also has the exact same item properties, except it dismantled into a [glowing ore ingot](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glowing_ore_ingot "Glowing ore ingot") instead of [glowing ore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Glowing_ore "Glowing ore").
