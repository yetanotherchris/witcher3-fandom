## Notes

  * Unlike other potions, the superior version increases to five charges from two charges of the enhanced version. 
    * This is most likely because, unlike all other potions, White Honey works exactly the same regardless of which version is used. Also all other Superior versions of potions gain additional effects, thus the only thing the Superior version of White Honey can do is greatly increase the number of available charges.
  * Superior White Honey was originally intended to "renew the duration of all active potion effects" instead of canceling (yes, on top of clearing toxicity). To say that would've been overpowered is an understatement, and hence why it was [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") during development.
