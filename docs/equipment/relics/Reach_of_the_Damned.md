## Item ID

Reachofthedamned

**Reach of the Damned** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can only be found as random loot inside caves in [Velen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Velen "Velen") and [Skellige](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Skellige "Skellige"), and from guarded treasures. One guarded treasure location known to spawn it (although it only guarantees a relic item and not this particular sword) is south of the [Ruined Watchtower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ruined_Watchtower "Ruined Watchtower") in [Kaer Morhen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kaer_Morhen "Kaer Morhen"), guarded by a [Leshen](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshy "Leshy").
