## Item ID

Silver Dust Bomb 3

**Superior Moon Dust** is a [bomb](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bombs "The Witcher 3 bombs") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. [Geralt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Geralt_of_Rivia "Geralt of Rivia") can have up to four of these in [inventory](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_inventory "The Witcher 3 inventory") at one time. 

It will prevent certain monsters from using special abilities, and since the "next-gen" [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), it also from regenerating health.

## Tips

  * The bomb will also prevent specters such as [noonwraiths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Noonwraith "Noonwraith") from becoming immaterial, which is far more convenient than luring them into [Yrden](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Yrden "Yrden") traps. The superior version in particular causes Yrden to be redundant while dealing with such foes as its effect is permanent.
