## Item ID

Arachas mutagen

**Arachas mutagens** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to make [arachas decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas_decoction "Arachas decoction"). They act like [lesser green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_green_mutagen "Lesser green mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monsters, which (counterintuitively) in this case are [venomous arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Venomous_arachas "Venomous arachas"). There are a few of them spread throughout the world: 

  * Northwest of the [Druids' Camp](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Druids%27_Camp "Druids' Camp") on [Ard Skellig](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ard_Skellig "Ard Skellig")
  * Harrisi during [Contract: Missing Brother](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Missing_Brother "Contract: Missing Brother")
  * In a side room inside the [Ancient Crypt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ancient_Crypt "Ancient Crypt") during the main quest [The Sunstone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sunstone "The Sunstone")
  * The "arachas" encountered during [Contract: Skellige's Most Wanted](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Skellige%27s_Most_Wanted "Contract: Skellige's Most Wanted") is also venomous, but mislabeled
  * In the cave where the treasure hunt quest [Hidden in the Depths](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hidden_in_the_Depths "Hidden in the Depths") takes place on [Faroe](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Faroe_Isle "Faroe Isle")
  * In the _[Hearts of Stone](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hearts_of_Stone "Hearts of Stone")_ expansion, one is above the [Bloodrot Pit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bloodrot_Pit "Bloodrot Pit"), and all three [arachasae](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas "Arachas") variations can be encountered near [Heddel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Heddel "Heddel")'s cemetery

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
