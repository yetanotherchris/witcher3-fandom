## Item ID

DLC10_Remorse

**Pang of Conscience** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_. 

It can be found at the [old signal tower](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Old_signal_tower "Old signal tower") on a skeleton, along with the [letter of apology](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Letter_of_apology "Letter of apology"). 

## Notes

  * Prior to [Patch 4.0](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_4.0_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 4.0 \(The Witcher 3\)"), this sword's level was static and didn't scale at the moment it spawned.

## Bugs

  * Despite being a unique item, the Pang of Conscience will sometimes respawn after loading a save, allowing the player to farm these swords infinitely and sell them for a high profit.
