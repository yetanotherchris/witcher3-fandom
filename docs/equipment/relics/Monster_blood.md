## Item ID

Monstrous blood

**Monster blood** is a [crafting component](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_crafting_components "The Witcher 3 crafting components") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that is needed to craft many items. 

It is a random drop from most non-[specter](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost "Ghost") [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"), almost all [monster nests](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_nest "Monster nest") (except [archespore](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Archespore "Archespore") ones), white [bears](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Bear "Bear"), and can be dismantled from the following items: 

  * [Alghoul bone marrow](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Alghoul_bone_marrow "Alghoul bone marrow")
  * [Cave troll liver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cave_troll_liver "Cave troll liver")
  * [Cyclops' eye](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclops%27_eye "Cyclops' eye")
  * [Devourer's blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Devourer%27s_blood "Devourer's blood")
  * [Drowner brain](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Drowner_brain "Drowner brain")
  * [Ghoul's blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul%27s_blood "Ghoul's blood")
  * [Leshen resin](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshen_resin "Leshen resin")
  * [Lesser Glyph of Infusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_Glyph_of_Infusion "Lesser Glyph of Infusion")
  * [Monster hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster_hide "Monster hide")
  * [Necrophage hide](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Necrophage_hide "Necrophage hide")
  * [Nekker blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_blood "Nekker blood")
  * [Nekker heart](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_heart "Nekker heart")
  * [Nekker warrior's liver](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Nekker_warrior%27s_liver "Nekker warrior's liver")
  * [Rotfiend blood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Rotfiend_blood "Rotfiend blood")

## Notes

  * Prior to [Patch 1.20](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.20_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.20 \(The Witcher 3\)"), monster blood could also be dismantled from [cockatrice stomach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrice_stomach "Cockatrice stomach").
  * Monster blood can also be dismantled from [wight stomach](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wight_stomach "Wight stomach") and [Greater Glyph of Infusion](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Greater_Glyph_of_Infusion "Greater Glyph of Infusion"), and is needed to craft the latter. However, both of those items have been [cut](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_cut_content "The Witcher 3 cut content") from the game.
