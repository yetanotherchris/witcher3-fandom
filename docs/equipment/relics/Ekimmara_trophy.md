## Item ID

mh104_ekimma_trophy

**Ekimmara trophy** is a [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that adds +5% bonus experience from killing [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Monster "Monster"). 

It can be acquired by killing [Sarasti](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Sarasti "Sarasti") during the quest, [Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_The_Mystery_of_the_Byways_Murders "Contract: The Mystery of the Byways Murders").
