## Item ID

Succubus mutagen

**Succubus mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to craft [succubus decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Succubus_decoction "Succubus decoction"). They act like [lesser red mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_red_mutagen "Lesser red mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster, and there are only two [succubi](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Succubus "Succubus") in the game: [Salma](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Salma "Salma") during [Contract: Deadly Delights](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Deadly_Delights "Contract: Deadly Delights"), and the [one](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Succubus_\(Ard_Skellig\) "Succubus \(Ard Skellig\)") who has [pimpernel](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Pimpernel "Pimpernel") during [Practicum in Advanced Alchemy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Practicum_in_Advanced_Alchemy "Practicum in Advanced Alchemy"). 

## Notes

  * If the player leans towards being merciful, they can miss both chances and must either forego making the decoction, use one playthrough to kill one of the succubi, or use console commands to get the mutagen.
  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
