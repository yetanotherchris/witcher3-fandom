**Leshen trophy** is a [trophy](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_trophies "The Witcher 3 trophies") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ that adds +5% bonus [experience](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/XP#The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "XP") from [monsters](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_bestiary "The Witcher 3 bestiary"). 

It can be acquired from the following [leshens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Leshen "Leshen"): 

  * [Kernun](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Kernun "Kernun") during [Contract: Lord of the Wood](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Lord_of_the_Wood "Contract: Lord of the Wood")
  * [Woodland Spirit](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Woodland_Spirit "Woodland Spirit") during [In the Heart of the Woods](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/In_the_Heart_of_the_Woods "In the Heart of the Woods")
