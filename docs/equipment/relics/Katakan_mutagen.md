## Attributes

### Effect(s)

## Item ID

Katakan mutagen

**Katakan mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is used to craft [katakan decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan_decoction "Katakan decoction"). They act like [lesser red mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_red_mutagen "Lesser red mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster. While not obvious due to claims about [Higher Vampires](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Higher_Vampire "Higher Vampire") in the base game, a guaranteed source of this mutagen is [Hubert Rejk](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Hubert_Rejk "Hubert Rejk") during [Carnal Sins](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Carnal_Sins "Carnal Sins"). Other [katakans](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Katakan "Katakan") that can be encountered perhaps slightly earlier appear during the quests [A Tome Entombed](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/A_Tome_Entombed "A Tome Entombed") and [Now or Never](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Now_or_Never "Now or Never"), but those won't always drop the mutagen. 

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
