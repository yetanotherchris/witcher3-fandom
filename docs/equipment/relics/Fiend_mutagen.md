## Item ID

Fiend mutagen

**Fiend mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") and is used to craft [fiend decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend_decoction "Fiend decoction"). They act like [lesser green mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_green_mutagen "Lesser green mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster. The earliest opportunity to get this mutagen is during the quest [Return to Crookback Bog](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Return_to_Crookback_Bog "Return to Crookback Bog"), where the final enemy is a [fiend](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Fiend "Fiend"). All other fiends in the game are of much higher levels. 

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
  * A level 16 fiend can be farmed south of Crookback Bog, though there are many [endregas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Endrega "Endrega") and an [arachas](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Arachas "Arachas") on the way to it.
