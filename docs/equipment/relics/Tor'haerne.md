# Tor'haerne

**Tor'haerne** is a [silver sword](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_silver_swords "The Witcher 3 silver swords") available in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and comes in the following forms: 

  * Tor'haerne (crafted relic)
  * Tor'haerne (crafted magic)
  * Tor'haerne (basic)
