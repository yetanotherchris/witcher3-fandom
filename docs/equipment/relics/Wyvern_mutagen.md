## Item ID

Wyvern mutagen

**Wyvern mutagen** is an [alchemy ingredient](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_alchemy_ingredients "The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients") in _[The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3:_Wild_Hunt "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt")_ and is used to craft [wyvern decoction](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern_decoction "Wyvern decoction"). They act like [lesser red mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Lesser_red_mutagen "Lesser red mutagen") when placed in the diamond-shaped slots in the character panel. 

Like all [special mutagens](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_mutagens "The Witcher 3 mutagens") they can only be dropped by their corresponding monster, and like most has a guaranteed source: the [royal wyvern](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Royal_wyvern "Royal wyvern") during [Contract: Phantom of the Trade Route](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Phantom_of_the_Trade_Route "Contract: Phantom of the Trade Route"), although that is a relatively high level quest. All other [wyverns](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern "Wyvern") may also drop the mutagen, but their chances are rather low. 

## Notes

  * In an undocumented change for [Patch 1.08](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.08_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.08 \(The Witcher 3\)"), all mutagen icons were changed. However, there was still a bug for those on Steam and Origin that didn't reflect this and was later fixed in [Patch 1.22](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Patch_1.22_\(The_Witcher_3\) "Patch 1.22 \(The Witcher 3\)"). Despite this, some may still see the original icons instead, even with a fully patched game.
  * The lowest level quest where a wyvern is encountered is [Contract: Patrol Gone Missing](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Contract:_Patrol_Gone_Missing "Contract: Patrol Gone Missing"). However, that is also the only [contract quest](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Witcher_3_contracts "The Witcher 3 contracts") where the "boss" monster can, but isn't scripted to always drop a mutagen.
  * Another easy alternative is [The Most Truest of Basilisks](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/The_Most_Truest_of_Basilisks "The Most Truest of Basilisks"), as not only is the wyvern injured from the start but you will also have [Redanian](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Redania "Redania") soldiers for support. Just be sure to save before talking to the [Beast Tamer](https://witcher.fandom.com/wiki/Beast_Tamer "Beast Tamer") in case the mutagen won't drop.
